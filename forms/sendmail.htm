 Maggie the Jackcat loves fan mail! (And who wouldn't.) Please use the form below for feedback, questions, suggestions, effusive praise, or just to say howdy! Please indicate if you would be willing to have your letter published in the Letters Section.

The following ground rules will apply to all published letters:

Letters may or may not be published. Maggie the Jackcat will make all selections about which letters are chosen for inclusion in the Letters Section.

Letters that duplicate questions already answered may be eliminated. The purpose of published letters is to make fun reading, so good letters may be dropped if they are similar to others. It doesn't mean that I don't love you! I will do my best to give a personal response, but due to the volume of mail I receive, I cannot promise one.

Letters may be edited for length, clarity or directness. Errors in grammar and spelling will be corrected, if I catch them. I will try to preserve the intent or purpose of the letter. If it's a nasty letter, I will not attempt to make it less nasty. If it's complimentary, I will not buff the good parts. Much. Letters that have no publishing preference checked will be assumed to be publishable.

Letters will be "signed" with your in-game name and your server. If there is no signature of that sort, then the part of the email address *before* the “@” sign will be used, without the domain part. So, for example, URDEAD@playerkiller.com becomes simply “URDEAD”.
