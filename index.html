Maggie the Jackcat's Fabulous AC Guide!

  
Welcome to Maggie the Jackcat's kick-ass fabulous Asheron's Call web site! Chock full of spoiler information and totally useless and pointless information, such as the stats on an opal dinner plate -- gleaned from my own travels and the generous contributions of visitors to this site.
 

As many of you know, after many years, Asherson's Call will be shutting down on January 31, 2017. With the generous assistance of Jungle Souljah of Leafcull, we've brought the site back online for people to enjoy and relive adventures of earlier days.

It's bittersweet logging on during these final days, but I'm glad to have the opportunity to enjoy Dereth one last time to say goodbye. I'd also like to give a huge thank you to everyone involved in the game, especially to all the players who made my time so enjoyable and for helping to make this site what it was.

Functionality may not be fully restored, but you can find the old Kick-Ass Fabulous site here.

--Maggie the Jackcat
