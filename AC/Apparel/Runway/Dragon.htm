The Runway

 Dragon Warrior has the honor of being the first person on the Runway to sport an item of dyed clothing! In addition to his Dho Life Master Robe, he also owns a Faran Robe with Hood, dyed a rich shade of red!

We arranged to meet in Nanto, so we could take pictures in front of the Blue Ghost Falls. They made a great backdrop for his Dho Robe, picking up the blue accents. Usually, Dragon Warrior fights with his Olthoi Helm, but he put on his blue "social hat" for me. He even has a pair of plain purple gloves, just like the ones Vilegoroth gave me, to wear when he's not engaged in combat! 

    

Speaking of Vilegoroth, he joined us at the falls to hang out for a bit and to watch the Runway shoot. When I asked Dragon Warrior if this was the outfit he wanted featured, he responded, "Yes," then pulled out the red robe, stating "Or I can do this." I suggested we do both. When he added that he could also unmule a Matty Coat outfit, Vilegoroth chimed in with a lighting quick, "No, do those!" Haha. (Ok, ok. Maggie can take a hint. I will ease up on the Matty outfits on the Runway! :)

We had a splendid time discussing Composite Bows, hunting grounds, and playing strategies. Right now, Dragon Warrior fights near Ayan Baqur, trying to find more Ravener Guts for his second attempt at stringing his bow. In addition to bow, he also has staff and lockpick trained and uses two schools of magic: life and item. 

As we were nearing the end of our evening, Dragon Warrior suddenly let out a long sign, telling us his vassal, Natasha Mandragoran, had just tanked her first granite golem. When I asked why the sigh, he said, "Brings a tear to my eye :) They grow up so quickly...."

Yes, I know what he means!

Thanks for posing Dragon Warrior! It was as much fun meeting you in-game as it was meeting you in person at the Player Luncheon in Boston!
