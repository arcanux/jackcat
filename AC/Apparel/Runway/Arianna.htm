The Runway

 When I wrote back to Aazhian telling him that I'd love to have him and his girlfriend, Arianna Bloodthorn, pose on the Runway, he responding saying that when he told Arianna, she was so excited she started practicing her poses right there! I can understand why. Anyone would be delighted to show off such a gorgeous collection of armor!

Arianna was nice enough to sneak out during the middle of her allegiance meeting so we could take some pics. I enjoyed listening in for a while, hearing her ideas about their group, listening to all the arguing and banter. The perils of simply powerleveling and the resultant burnout. The drudgery of corpse recovery, rescuing over and over again those playing out of their league. When Arianna had the floor, she suggested that instead of sitting around waiting for Turbine to entertain them each month, they should create their own purpose and goals. Fascinating discussion, to be sure, but I wanted to see her clothes!

    

And she wanted to show me! She brought out her mule and started pulling out some beautiful pieces. While I ID'd her main black and gold outfit, she slipped on the Amuli suit above. Very nice! It goes particularly well with her coloring, I think. Then she put on her Greater Shadow Amuli set. (Pre-July Patch, since it's taken me so long to get their pages up!) I must say that it's even more attractive now than it was the first time I saw it! ;-)

The last outfit we decided to go with was this great gold Yoroi/Koujia set. I was torn trying to decide which outfit to put as the main picture, since they were all so nice. I finally went with the black and gold because it just looked so sharp, especially when she was standing with Aazhian in his red. 

It was obvious to me from the way they treat each other that Aazhian and Arianna are deeply devoted to each other, even if I hadn't seen all the romantic inscriptions on their favorite pieces. But that doesn't stop them from mercilessly teasing each other. I mentioned that I didn't see how people managed allegiance; I had enough trouble keeping my one needy vassal supplied with mana stones! Aazhian exclaimed, "omfg! You want to see a real mana junkie?" Then he pointed straight at Arianna. She denied it at first, but then fessed up, but blamed him on getting her hooked. He says she's worth every C note.

Thanks once again for posing, Arianna, and for sharing your stuff with the rest of the world. Aazhian was right. Your armor *is* pretty!
