The Runway

 Hi Maggie -
I am the leader of a small monarchy on Harvestgain, but more importantly... I have one of the best looking outfits in the game.  *casts ego boost self VI* It is a nice morbid outfit: black, black, a little gold, and more black.  I am a sword fighter, so I usually carry my Atlan sword, or the SoLL for looks.  I have been complimented many times for my armor, and I'm quite proud of that.  I've also been told I'm a sick person, well... because I am.  You'll find that out if ya meet me.  Hope to meet you sometime... gimmie a yell.

-Det, the crazed lord commander of shiny things.


It took me weeks and weeks to meet up with Detriment, but the delay worked out in one big way: he's the first person on the Runway to get the benefit of my spiffy new graphics card! I am still amazed at how gorgeous the game is in 3D! Look at that sky, that face, that armor, that cool glow from the Ravenous Sword, the smoldering and the stinging! I must have taken a gajillion screen shots of Detriment as we did the Runway shoot.

    


    

In-game, Detriment is what some might call a d00d! He was even tied to the Subway in Arwic. After we met up, he tried to shock me with his bad-ass self: 

He also shared a book with me that he and some of his buddies pass around and contribute to when the mood strikes. I read through a bit of it, but can't offer any of it here. It certainly wasn't suited to a general audience. He's right! He's a total sicko!!! ;-) 

I wondered briefly why he wasn't playing on Darktide. I had always heard that Darktide was full of anti-social sicko maniacal d00dz. (Just kidding!!) The subject did sorta come up as we were hanging out with some of his buddies.
