Where Are They Now?

 While I was in Glendon Wood the other night, I bumped into Mu'allim Danier and stopped to admire his stats. He graciously agreed to pose for the Runway in this much coveted Hoary Mattekar Robe. (The Hoary Mattekar was hunted to extinction during the Sudden Season.)

I made him take off his hat so I could see if he was as good-looking as he claimed. (Actually, he didn't claim to be good-looking at all. However, when I asked him if there was anything else he wanted folks to know about him, he said, "Yes, I'm a very good kisser, except for the bad breath." Maybe Danier should lay off all that spicy kimchi!)

Then he started boasting that his loyalty stat was higher than my vassal's. And before I knew it, he was hauling out all his mules to show off all his cool stuff. (He has a lot of cool stuff!) Before our serendipitous meeting ended, he was offering to show me around the BDC (Black Death Catacombs) some evening. I don't know... I think he was flirting with me!

          

                

His vassals claim that there's a strong silly side to this powerful player. However, there's nothing silly about the impressive store of weapons and items he has earned, fighting the Olthoi in the Black Death Catacombs. Both the Crossbow and the Sapphire Staff came from Olthoi Nobles.
