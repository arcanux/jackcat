The Runway

 Ok, I will admit it. I let Gheres Reficul cut in line. I swore him to secrecy, but am confessing anyway! Gheres Reficul is a frequent trader. When I told him there were several people ahead of him in line for the Runway, he expressed dismay! He had acquired a handsome blue Amuli suit with gold accent pieces and was afraid the ensemble would never make it until his turn.

I was meeting him anyway, to accept a purple girth he thought I might like, so it seemed silly not to just go ahead and shoot some pics. The weather was beautfiul, the armor was shiny and clean, and he was excited to show off his new Atlan Sword! I must agree that the suit looks especially nice with the Atlan Sword! It would have been a shame to miss this classy look. (I've since met up with the others who were ahead of him for their features, so everyone is back on schedule! :)

I was introduced to Gheres origanlly when he sent some pics of a few things he discovered while travelling through Dereth -- an outpost outside of Zaikhal, The Advocate Dungeon. I've taken one of the screenshots he sent of the Advocate Dungeon and put it up on a new page in the Odds & Ends section here!

Gheres Reficul has also attempted to teach me the fine art of sitting on rooftops. He took me into Qalaba'r and showed me how to jump on the ledge, then make running jumps across the rooftops. I was determined to succeed, but unfortunately, I appear to be jumping impaired. Somehow, I kept ending up on the ground with skinned knees and 4 points of damage!

In addition the the pants pictured above, I also got the stats of Gheres's spare pants, the Gromnie Amuli Leggings. It's always nice to have some back up mana-free duds. Other pieces of note are the Shore Spine, one of many from a favorite hunting area, and the Bracelet of Fealty, which he wears out of admiration for his Patron Talandra under the Monarch Vrumor. Thanks once again for posing, Gheres Reficul! It's been a pleasure to get to know you!
