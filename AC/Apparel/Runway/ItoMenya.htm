The Runway

 Ito Menya of Frostfell poses for the Runway in this sharp looking black suit. A blademaster who dabbles in the art of Item Enchantment, he carries the fabled Sword of Lost Light. It's pretty cool looking!

Ito Menya belongs to the Clan Epik and has a web site of his own with a cool slide show of some of his adventures. Check it out here! I especially like the link he has to my site! :) You can also see an even nicer picture of the shot above on his site, which we tried to recreate for the Runway. Although I am an excellent photographer, my camera equipment could be better (Ito's got a better graphics card).

         

I was able to snap a shot of Ito Menya while he was casting True Value on some of his equipment with the aid of his Carnelian Wand. Since I don't normally play on Frostfell, I was on with Daughter of Jackcat, a new low level ID-ing character I created just to meet him. After Ito was done buffing items, he then handed me his armor and equipment so I could get shots of each piece. When I told him that I had snapped a pic of him nekkid, he wailed, "Waaahh. My first modeling job and it's porn!" I added the last picture of Ito Menya in repose because I thought he looked neat, sitting on the edge of the hillside, overlooking the water.
