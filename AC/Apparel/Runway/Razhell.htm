The Runway

 Greetings Maggie!!!
I've visited your site and it's really nice. I'd like to be on the Runway section because I have red iron celdon leggings that look like magma and I'd like to show them off!

Thank you,
Razhell

By the time I finally met up with Razhell, he had traded in his Celdon legs for a set of Amuli. But he still favored red. It's his color, he tells me. His monarch gave him the suit because he knew how much Razhell liked the color. I told him he looked very festive! Like a Christmas tree in his red and green! All he needed was some sparkling lights! He complied by doing a few bars of Dereth's favorite hit: YMCA!

    

He was a little shy about his bald head and at first didn't want to take off his hat, but I convinced him. I don't know why he was shy about it; his head is very nicely shaped. Soon, however, he was completely at ease. I even got a peek at his underwear!! (See Velvet Tunic below :)

    

Next we took a few pics of him in his Master Robe, again a lovely deep shade of red. The orb he's carrying was given to him by another player after he helped kill Shadows one day. 

The final picture is a shot of some of his old pieces of armor -- Steel Scale Sleeves and Iron Celdon leggings. They *do* look like magma. Razhell tells me the two pieces are for sale, so if you're interested, give him a yell at overcrash87@hotmail.com. (Thistledown)

Thanks again for posing Razhell! It was a pleasure to meet you in person!
