The Runway

 "LOOK! It's The Green Flash!"  "The Green Flash?!"  "Yes, The Green Flash!" "Wow! He saved my life once." These are the words I overheard as The Green Flash streaked through town.

And I know why! He saved my life too. I was having a little trouble taking down a Lich when a second one suddenly piled on. Magic Resist always seem to fail just when you need it, doesn't it? A few good wallops of Whirling Blade later, I was three bodies down, my gloves were out of mana, and I had enough vitae to make killing them just out of reach for me. I realized I was beginning the old downward spiral of death, when the Green Flash, a good friend of Vilegoroth, @telled me a hello.

He immediately offered to help as he was just a quick portal hop away. I gladly welcomed the assistance. My gorgeous purple leggies were at risk! Whack, whack, whack. Those Liches didn't know what hit them!

After the excitement was over, we headed back to the surface and I got to take a closer look at him. The man is GREEN!! Even his underwear is green! And he sports a crop of bright red hair. I mean, really, is this Runway material, or is this Runway material?!

"It's The Green Flash!"

Yes, it's the Green Flash! He was happy to let me snap some pics of his great look, even one in his underwear!

He was an excellent model, when I could get him to be still for a moment. The guy must have one amazing jump skill! I'd lose track of him for a second, then look up to see him about 100 feet in the air above me. He's The Green Flash!

       

      

While I was handing him back his head, I accidentally dropped it on the ground. (I have got to map that "give to selected" key!)  It looked pretty cool sitting on the ground in front of him, but then we decided it was just a little creepy! He then fooled around wtih some of the chat poses helping me get some nice pics. The last one up there is *surrender*. But I bet he doesn't have to use that when he's armed with his Shivering Atlan Sword, or his powerful Scimitar (that is a nizzze sword).

"It's The Green Flash!"

Yes, it's The Green Flash! My hero!
