The Runway

 It took a long time for us to coordinate a meeting time to snap pics of his outfits (He was level 23 at the time) and even longer for me to get them up on the Runway. (He's currently at level 30!) With no further delay, may I present Roberto-Eduardo of Morningthaw and one of his well-dressed mules, Cragstone Robertoson!

He originally sent me mail about his Green Mire Cuirass and wanted to show it off because of the difficulty it took to get it. Here he is, in a matching pyreal crown and some fancy Yoroi sleeves. I thought the white boots were a nice touch. I have a fondness for them, myself. His secondary characters, which also include a finely dressed fellow named Hebian-to Robertoson, look a lot more pulled together than my own. Mine are usually a total mish-mash of whatever I happen to have tucked away.

Roberto-Eduardo has a web page where he recounts his adventures with his Allegiance, the Orion Hunters. Click here for his journal. I read with nostalgia his quest to reach level 35 in order to train Item Enchantment. I remember Ned Cleversun's single-minded attempts to do the same. It will happen, Roberto! Congratulations on your Atlan sword! Thanks for posing for everyone and good luck on your journeys!
