The Attractive Yet Practical Ensemble

I am wearing what I like to call my attractive yet practical ensemble. No, it's not fancy Celdon. No, it's not a Green Mire Curiass that screams out to every beast in the vicinity: "Come attack me!" I'm 15. Gimme a break! Any level 15 can procure an ensemble such as this. It's subtle; it's classy.

Part of the beauty of this outfit is its versatility. All I need for an entirely new look is to change my shoes to pair of sollarettes, or to switch to a new breastplate and leggings. A lovely jade green that says, "I am fun, I am alive!" Or blood red that says, "I am in a vicious mood, and if you cross me I will ream you through with my gold long sword (incidentally, with BD III and a difficulty of 12, sword skill over 126 and hand-crafted for Aluvians) and poke your buggy eyeballs out the back of your head!"

Ladies, accessorize! Don't forget your swords and wands! This particular ensemble is heavenly with snowflakes fluttering from my frost takuba, drifting carressingly across my back. I always wield it while running for that extra flair!
