Where Are They Now?

 Aazhian, a Creature, Item, Life Mage (with a bow), wrote to me saying that he would like to be on the Runway, and he'd especially like to see his girlfriend Arianna featured; she had some pretty armor. So did Aazhian, it turns out. This  set of Red Yoroi is one of the boldest, most arresting looks I've seen! Although shy about disrobing at first, Aazhian was soon reciting the history of each piece: Legs off an Umbris, Arms off a Noble, Guants off a Noble, Helm off an Unbris, Yumi off an Umbris, Orb off a Virindi Master.

When we first met up, Aazhian only had a short while to pose. The monarch of his allegiance had stopped playing, so there was a meeting planned to discuss restructuring. We quickly did a few outdoor shots with his bow and Gertarh's Dagger. 

Aazhian discussed his recommendations for character creation: Don't specialize and have stats at 55 across the board. Train tons of skills.  It must have worked for him because he had three schools of magic, a Bow skill of 294, Melee Defense at 298 and Dagger at 312. That's a well rounded character! He joked with me about my vassal's piddly death stat and said it was nothing. His was 747 and counting.

     

Arianna came online just when it was time for the Allegiance meeting. I suggested that I sit in for a short while, so that she didn't miss the discussion. Besides, I had never been to an allegiance meeting before. (Unless you count me and Ned hanging out while he tells me what kind of jewelry he needs next!) I met the gang: Ts'i, Diamonnd, Coor, Phoenix Ghimson, Sui Cheng, Tepshen Orashi, Kaneda Jubei, and Cain Dark. I didn't have to be there long to decide that I was glad I had such a small allegiance. :) So and so was unhappy; someone else needed a purpose; another fellow needed powerleveling. I gained new respect for monarchs everywhre as I watched Perilous (Aazhian's alter-ego) placate the crowd and address their concerns. Eventually, Aazhian was elected to be the new monarch! But I missed the whole thing. I had sneaked out the back with Arianna long before they got to that point! 

When I congratulated him later after he joined back up with us, he laughed and said, "just pass the aspirin." Then he dragged out his mule, a real Fashion No No! Yikes! Arianna calls the look "AC meets Tyrion's Imanewbie." Hahaha. But he had lots of cool stuff, including his pack of Sturdy Iron Keys which he insisted would be more full if someone (Arianna) didn't love using them so much. They also showed me the Diamond Ring he had given Arianna. As a fashion photographer, I am privileged to read the many inscriptions people have on their most cherished items. You might not believe it, but Dereth is just full of romantics! Thanks for posing Aazhian. You and Arianna make a dashing couple!
