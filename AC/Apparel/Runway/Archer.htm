The Runway

 I met Archer From Hell when I was on Frostfell taking pics of Ito Menya's armor. He was hanging out in Zaikhal, where Ito and I had arranged to meet. I complimented him on his outfit and he complimented me on my website, pulling out one quest item after another that my site had helped him obtain. One thing let to another and I asked him if he'd like to be up on the Runway as well. He said he'd not be opposed to being a fashion model!

He joined us for the run to Cragstone, where we planned to take pictures overlooking the water. I got this great shot of Archer From Hell with his bow below. It's my favorite picture of an archer so far! Isn't that cool looking?

From among his loot, he showed me his Hamud's Pyreal Katar, his Impious Staff and his Sparkling Atlan Claw, pictured below. I think it looks nice with his sleeves!

Because we had not made arrangements ahead of time by email to meet, Archer From Hell was extremely hesitant to hand me his loot for screenshots and IDing. He armor was very hard won, and he wasn't going to hand it to some level 1 mule he had just met, who *claimed* to be Maggie the Jackcat from Thistledown! I understood his caution; however, that meant getting pics was quite the ordeal!

We tried email, but some of the files were too large at first and didn't come through. Then he suggested meeting in Glendon Wood. I was dubious, thinking I would never survive the trip, but was willing to give it a try. This was, as you can imagine, unsuccessful. My poor level 1 body made several trips to the lifestone before giving up! So, I waited for Archer to resend pics of the missing armor and equipment. Finally, the shots arrived!! Yay! When I was putting the page up, I noticed that somehow, we missed his breastplate. I decided close enough! Thanks Archer from Hell for posing! Sorry for the extremely long delay (he's probably level gajillion by now)! And thanks for the pics of the Shadow Armor pieces you sent earlier. Nice bows!
