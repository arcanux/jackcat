The Runway

 You don't think Sara fit all those outfits on one set of mules do you?! Meet Stiletto, Sara Truflight's in-game fiancé, who offers her not only love and devotion, but extra closet space!

Stiletto joined Sara and Maggie the Jackcat in Mayoi, where we were taking pictures of Sara's Shadow Armor. He had several other sets of her armor stored on his mules and was bringing her some changes of clothes. Suddenly, however, Sara's computer disconnected and she disappeared. While we were waiting, Stiletto was nice enough to pose himself in his beautiful Amuli suit! He also brought Stil on, one of his secondary characters, and gave me a preview of some of Sara's fancy outfits. Here he is, striking a pose in Sara's blue Yoroi!

      

We had a nice conversation about his role in their allegiance, The Illuminati. Stiletto is High Councilor of the Dark Marauders

You say, "So what do the Dark Marauders do?"
Stil says, "We are the number one Rescue service in Morningthaw. We specialize in Corpse retrievals."
You say, "Ahh. Too bad you're not on Thistledown :)"
Stil says, "Our Motto, Anytime-Anywhere!"
Stil says, "Our most creative rescue was in the Acid Pit of the Nexus"
You say, "Yikes"
Stil says, "Had two corpses to retrieve, and were able to pull it off without a hitch"
You say, "How did you do that?"
Stil's death is preceded by a sharp, stabbing pain, courtesy of Mosswart Feeder!

Oops! I guess he forgot for a second that he was hanging out with a very   overburdened level 1 character! Stiletto, level 43, then reappeared to secure the area and to threaten the local Mosswart community with extermination!

Stil, unfortunately, was tied to the Nanto lifestone! Sara logged back on and we decided to wait the body to decay to retrieve the Yoroi pauldrons on the corpse, and then to make the run to Nanto ourselves, rather than trying to get Stil back to us.

Here's a picture of Stil and Sara in Nanto, picking out some outfits for her to wear. We finally settled on the white Koujia for Sara. As Sara posed, and I took pics, Stiletto really started to enjoy the process. He gave us a little background music to contribute to the shoot!

After Sara was squared away, Stiletto got called on to oil some strings for the Composite Bow. As a high level alchemist for his allegiance, he's pretty popular these days! He brought out his own bow to show me, but unfortunately, it had started to rain and the quality of the picture I took was quite poor. You can see the stats below.

Thanks for posing, Stiletto, and for sacrificing yourself in Mayoi to save me from the sharp, stabbing pains of the Mosswart Feeder. I just *know* you threw yourself in the line of fire to protect me! :) Sara Truflight is indeed lucky to have such a brave fighter by her side!
