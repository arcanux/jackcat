The Runway

 I have to say, this is one of the prettiest outfits I've seen! Meet Nocta of Leafcull, another one of Dereth's snazziest dressers. She sent me a simple email, saying she'd like to be a fashion model: "Form over function... always." With a note like that, I knew I wouldn't be disappointed!

We met up in Qalaba'r and walked a short distance from town to do the shoot. One Maggie the Jackcat fan, The Unforgiven II, followed us and ran excitedly around in circles, realizing he was witnessing the process in action! I kept having to shoo him out of the way, so I could get that nice shot of Nocta with the Livnak Range behind her. What a great backdrop!

Nocta has a fondness for Green Garnet (goes nice with her eyes!) and has a several pieces of equipment made with it, including a super-cool Rapier, pictured below. Is that a snazzy outfit, or what?! She tells me that when she wears this ensemble, people just come up to her and give her stuff!

             

After we were done in Qalaba'r, we took a run to Shoushi where Nocta had her Item Master Robe stored. When I mentioned outside that I'd like to take the pictures inside the spire, it created quite a stir until the bystanders figured out I meant Shoushi's Spire of Serenity!

We thought it'd be nice to get a shot with some spell effects surrounding her. However, we quickly realized that all her jewelry and equipment were out of mana! It gave us a good laugh though. Looks like everybody in Dereth is addicted to mana stones! Thanks again for volunteering, Nocta. Love the clothes!!
