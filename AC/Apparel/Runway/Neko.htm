The Runway

 "I've finally shed my drab gray Matty Coat and oddly purple Olthoi Helm, and donned a snazzy suit of armor befitting my rank.  Why not feature me on your splendid Runway site?  I can show up almost anywhere at any time, for such is my craving for attention."

- Neko Bijin
http://hsk.acsage.com
Harvestgain Lifemage

I knew I'd like Neko Bijin before we even met. Previously she had written me under the name "Nitpicker" about the proper pronunciation of vitae. I am always well-disposed towards anyone who gives me a chance to show off my knowledge of Classical Latin. It is seldom that one is presented the opportunity! Bijin, of course, means pretty woman, literally "beautiful  person" in Japanese. (Yes, I am just pretending to know Japanese. Neko Bijin told me that. :)

Neko Bijin is one of the rarest creatures in Dereth: a Life Mage right out of the box! She says she dropped alchemy though, because she couldn't figure it out. That was, of course, "back in the day, when all was new and wondrous," before all these awful spoiler sites popped up on the web. ;-)

I recently had a request from a visitor to add people's stats to the Runway page, so others could get an idea about how they crafted their character. Neko Bijin graciously. She says there are no trade secrets to her template. She specialized Mana Conversion and Arcane Lore, and kept Assess Creature, Assess Person, and Healing trained. She may be one of the last mages in Dereth with Item or Creature, but she loves her character too much to start over.

Since she's Sho, she has Unarmed as a freebie skill. Below she's posing with her Hamud's Pyreal Katar and a gorgeous Gold Yoroi set. Her skill is actually high enough to use the weapon. It's very effective, she says, after a nice Imperil VI. She also has the Impious Staff in her possession, but it's only for show.

    

Neko Bijin tells me that she wasn't always monarch of her allegiance. Her Patron actually swore to her when she leveled past him. I almost didn't mention that because I don't want Ned Cleversun getting any silly ideas. ;-) I asked her a little more about her allegiance. Was there anything about them she'd like to mention? Yes, she said. They're the coolest group on Harvestgain. And the smartest, most polite, most humble, etc. etc.... :)

At one point while we were hanging out, fooling around, Neko Bijin exclaimned about some comment or other, "Dereth is full of wonders!" Made me laugh. That's one of her appealing characteristics. She makes them laugh and then they swear allegiance to her. It's certainly as good a system as I've ever heard! She says she does her best to bring mirth to the dour world of powerleveling.

As a Life Mage, powerleveling others is her specialty. 

Neko Bijin tells you, "I've raised up a bunch of scrubs into the powerful army now at my command!"
Neko Bijin thunders.

I was appropriately impressed!

Some other items Neko Bijin offered to show me were some of her orbs and her Fez collection. She's just a few points shy of using her Life V Silver Orb. The Velvet Gloves are the ones she's been using since she lost her Unarm Vs on the Obsidian Plain. We were having a great time, passing stories back and forth about this and that. Soon, however, it was time to stop the Show and Tell and go have dinner.

You tell Neko Bijin, "Thanks for posing! You will make a good page!"
Neko Bijin tells you, "Best compliment I've ever gotten in the game."
Neko Bijin tells you, "Except of course for, Hey Sexy!  Wanna be my vassal!?!"

Well, it sure beats, "u ned a patron?" Thanks again, Neko Bijin. You *did* make a good page! Congratulations on attaining Rank 5!!!
