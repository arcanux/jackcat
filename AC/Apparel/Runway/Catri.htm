The Runway

 When I first spoke with Catri she suggested something a little different to add to the variety on the Runway: a selection of outfits she had chosen for her mules.

"I have 2 mules and a mage that I've dressed in some nice looking, but rather low level armor combinations. Being vain I went for looks over function in all chars but my main. The lines are simple, the materials generally tight fitting leather and cloth, and the AL ranging from 20 to 60. Pale blue and white, deep blue and black, and bright orange and grey are my colors of choice. I like their look, even if they aren't as awe inspiring as that beautiful gem armor."

By the time we met in person, she had completed her set of Greater Shadow Amuli 2 days before and was days away from completing her Composite Bow! The picture above is taken in Holtburg. The view is great, isn't i? Although I wanted her main pic to be one of her main character, I really was delighted to see the sampling of outfits she had available on her mules! The first outfit is a slick looking grey and black yoroi set. Like me, she loves leggings for their slimming effect. I especially like the leather outfits too! The orange coat with the grey leggings is really sharp. She switched the orange to a Matty coat and claimed it was her spacesuit look from a B rated movie, all silver grey. I told her I couldn't put another Matty coat up on the Runway; people would rebel!


Luckily, we had some other choices! Catri pulled out a nice set of green chain, and an unfinished orange set on her mule Static, or as she call him, her token male. ;) But my most favorite of all was the white and light blue outfit she dresses her alchemist in: simple business wear for a working professional! :) After the fact we realized that we should have had her pose near the mists of the Holtburg menhir ring to really show the outfit off.

As we were making our arrangements to meet via email, she mentioned that she might have some of the dyed robes for me to see: "not necessarily in the colors that indicate success, but ahh, I like pink :)" Seems her alchemist is pretty handy! Pictured below, in addition to the Dho Robe with Vest and the Suikan Robe is a green dyed Faran Robe! 

As usual, I asked her what sort of things she'd like me to mention on the page. She insisted that I mention that she is devastatingly beautiful, and that her husband, Silverfrost, is the most devilishly handsome fellow you've ever seen. I said, of course! I was going to mention that anyway! Then she pulled out some purple gloves for my special Harvestgain Maggie! They were so cool! I liked them a lot. 

As if that wasn't all -- cool outfits, fun conversation, new gloves -- she also taught me another neato trick: how to run with flaming streamers behind you. It's fun! You do the *YMCA* emote and jump and run in the middle. Her first try was unsuccessful, because her alchemist/fletcher was overburdened with a gajillion arrowheads! My first attempt was also poor because, well, I am jumping impaired! :) But we finally got our discoing selves going and showed Holtburg what it means to have a good time!

Finally, after we had been hanging out half the evening, I realized I didn't even know what her main skill was for her main character Catri. "Gossip," she replied. "My main skill is most definitely gossip!" ;-)

Thanks for posing Catri! Sorry it took me so long to get you posted on the site! And tell that devilishly handsome fellow of yours I said hi!
