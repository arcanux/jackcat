The Runway

 Sara Truflight, an Archer on Morningthaw, has got to have one of the best collections of armor in the game! She wrote to me, telling me about some of the many outfits she had put together, and some she still has in the works. She runs around in rags, she says, to keep people from asking about her armor all the time! I loved her outfits so much that I decided to feature several of them on the site. Believe it or not, this is just a fraction of her overall collection!

Sara Truflight wanted to pose in her Gold Yoroi ensemble (below), as it meets all her criteria for a good suit of armor: form, function, and beauty! After developing my film and editing the pictures, I overruled her (I can do that; it's my site!) and put the Celdon Shadow Armor in the main spotlight. Really, it's because I like that picture of Sara above so much. I think she looks positively regal.

         

But she's right. This gold suit is really snazzy. I am partial to Yoroi Leggings, myself. They're very slimming. I had her pose with her bow drawn, to give a taste of Sara Truflight in action.

Since I was on Morningthaw, a world I don't usually play on, I was only level 2 and my stats were still very low. Sara met me in Nanto and buffed me up for the run to Mayoi, where her servants were housed. In addition to being an accomplished Archer, Sara also has Item and Creature Magic. She periodically cast Focus V on me and True Value on her items, so I could read the stats. To give you an idea of how much fun I was having, checking out all her fancy clothes, I leveled to 3 before we were done! :)

Eventually, we ended back in Nanto for a few more pictures. These last two suits are really pretty! I had never seen the white Koujia before. It's quite striking in my opinion.

    

Even Sara's mules are decked out! Check out Arrow Boy, Id by Illuminati, and Saras Purse below. When I complimented her on the orange outfit, she said, "I just kinda threw this one together!" I was having so much fun riffling through her closets that time was just flying by. Sara brought out You Can Cook who gave us some cookies to snack on while we took a small break. He's not pictured here, but you can trust me when I say that he had a nice outfit too! :)

Sometimes it's the details that really make the outfit. Sara Truflight has some exceptional accessories, including this gorgeous handbag and these Beautiful shoes. ;-)

   
Saras Purse

I would be remiss if I didn't mention that Sara Truflight is a High Council Member for The Illuminati. Periodically, they'll have armor give-aways for their Allegiance members. Sara is, I would guess, very popular at such events! You can read Sara's bio by visiting their web site here.

Thanks once again for posing, Sara Truflight, and for showing off your beautiful clothes. I can't wait to see what you come up with next!
