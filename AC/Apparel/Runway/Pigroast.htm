The Runway

 Pigroast II and I finally met in person the other day when he generously offered me a spare Mattie Coat for my needy vassal, Ned Cleversun. Pigroast II was valiantly trying to save me from the evil of eBay; I had suggested I might need to resort to the auction site to finally shut Ned up (j/k Ned)! When we met in Hebian-to, he was waiting for me on top of the lifestone. I suggested that he pose for the Runway, in his own Mattekar Coat. As you can see, he agreed!

He brought out his mule to get the spare fur, and showed me a festive girth and some pants he had hanging in the closet. He claimed they, "just weren't him." Pigroast II tends to prefer the subtler fashion shade of grey with just a hint of blue. Becuase I always like to see what people look like with their hats off, I snapped this pic below of Pigrost II in his nice green tunic. It matches his eyes!

     

     

Alas, even Pigroast II couldn't resist asking Maggie the Jackcat if she had ever thought about getting a Patron, as he passed over the much coveted coat. Knowing that I was a Sword wielder, he even brought out his great collection of Swords to entice me. Unfortunately, I had to tell him that I just didn't think I could deal with a Monarch named, "Squishy." ;-)
