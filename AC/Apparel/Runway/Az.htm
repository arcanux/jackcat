 You've read the column, now meet him in person! Here he is... The One... The Only... Azeraphel Lorekeeper! Yee!

Azeraphel Lorekeeper was kind enough to take time from his Patronage duties at his home base in Lytlethorpe (Thistledown), to pose for a few shots for the Runway. In addition to the everyday wear-about-town Crown he is wearing, he also owns a large selection of other head gear, including a very cool Gold Crown of Leadership and this fighting helmet, pictured below.

The Azurite Orb he is holding is an incredible color match for the trim on the Faran War Master Robe. The charming inscription reads "Nerfball of the Winter Sky." Azeraphel Lorekeeper also has some pieces befitting his station as the leader of a sizable following. For special occasions he might wear the beautiful silk shirt listed below. Have you ever seen such fine quality clothing? Thanks, Azeraphel, for participating on the Runway! You look mahvelous! And thank you also for those cool new sleeves!
