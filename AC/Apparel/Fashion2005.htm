Where Are They Now?

Announcement on the Turbine Site for Fashion Show (held March 5th & 6th.)

Well now is the time to show the world just what they have been missing, and to dazzle all before you and become the Fashion King or Queen. Let all the others despair and and hide in your shadow as you bask in the glory and earn fame and fortune.

Categories to be judged:

1. Ugliest assemble that somehow looks good
2. Best use of Pinks and Oranges
3. Best matching set of armour
4. Best use of visible accessories. (Hats, Crowns, shoes, shields, swords, etc)

The competition will be judged by an independent panel of judges, their decision is final. The show will be held in Yaraq Meeting Hall, all are welcome to either enter or just watch.

Chimaera
Jolt Live Ops Manager - USA
 

The Fashion Show is about to start in Yaraq Meeting Hall, come and show of your best designer clothes

With spring arriving, it's the perfect time to take a walk down the Runway! As most of you know, I just love Dereth's fashions! I was thrilled to be asked to be one of the guest judges for Dereth's Fashion Show of March 2005, along with Jolt representatives and judges from several other AC fan sites. (Click here to view some prior Runway models.)

We met in Yaraq's meeting hall and waited for the participants to gather. +LeM Chimaera introduced the show and gave an overview of the categories to be judged. As each category was called, the contestants lined up on the wall while the judges walked past them, examining their togs. We'd each score our favorites, then Chimaera would tally the totals. To pass the time while the winners were being tabulated, Chimaera add a little mayhem to the proceedings by spawning a few high level creatures! I saw the lifestone a few times myself, that's for sure, as I was only level 1 on most of the servers. (Oh, the lengths I'm willing to go for fashion!) ;-)

The runners-up received a Writ and a Black Luster Pearl. The winner of each category was awarded with 2 Writs, 2 Black Luster Pearls and arguably one of the most desirable titles in the game: Fashion King or Fashion Queen. Almost makes me wish I wasn't judging! :)

Black Luster Pearl
A large black pearl that appears to come from an unknown creature. Wisps of dark energy flail out from the pearl, compelling you to hold it in your hands.
