 So many fashion choices, so little time!

Maggie the Jackcat used to have a super cool purple girth before she lost it, on an ill-fated jaunt to a POI north of Zaikhal. Next she moved to a Gold Scalemail shirt, courtesy of Tear. She paired the shirt with Yoroi leggings; chainmail bracers, coif and gauntlets; and some sporty white studded leather boots (worn to temporarily replace the Sollerets given to her vassal Ned Cleversun. Maggie liked wearing the boots because they reminded her of the white go-go boots she coveted as a child, but her mother would not let her have).

Update! See Maggie's new togs on the Runway! (The classic Matty Coat & sexy purple yoroi leggings days.)

The next upgrade was the gorgeous Chainmail Hauberk (seen on the main page) and a pair of protective platemail leggings with steel toed boots, all in a rich shade of deep purple. A classis Olthoi Helm topped the ensemble!

Then, thanks to the generosity of Gheres Reficul, Maggie the Jackcat obtained a set of Celdon Shadow Armor!

In earlier days, the craftsmanship of an item meant diddly in terms of protection or damage and only affected value. With the introduction of Tinkering, the craftsmanship became extremely important.

In each armor section you'll see all the quest and crafted items, plus store-bought gear. I also include a sampling of armor from the loot generator, so that people can see some of the varieties of colors and styles available in the game.
