 One of the coolest things about Asheron's Call is not only the fabulous character creation choices, but also the wide array of outerwear choices. It is possible to look totally unique! Of course if you're Ned Cleversun, fashion isn't your main concern; utility is the name of the game. If he's not running around in a Mattekar Coat and Olthoi Helm, he's sporting a colorful dyed Faran Robe!

In this section you will see a thorough sampling of Ned Cleversun's Clothing and Armor. In the Armor Section you can see him wearing a Matty Coat. In the Formal Wear section, he's modeling a Wedding Gown. On the Runway, what else, but a fabulous party dress.
