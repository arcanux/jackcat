Where Are They Now?

 One of the coolest things about Asheron's Call, besides the fabulous character creation choices, is the wide array of outerwear. With all the selections available, it is possible for the players to look totally unique! Of course you are going to see scads of folk running around in trendy favorites (Mattekar coats and Olthoi Helms were the fad in my youth), but you will also see tons of variety! Check out my spring portrait to the right! I'm quite a babe, if I do say so myself!

In this section you will see a thorough sampling of the Clothing and Armor in AC. Each main section is broken down into Quest & Crafted items, then store bought items. Finally, most sections have a sampling of random loot generated wear so you can get an idea of the sorts of spells you might find.

The Formal Wear section has an overview of Dresses and Wedding Raiment, and the Designer Wear section shows you where to buy particular colors of basic off the shelf clothing. You can also visit the Clothing page in the Bargains section for information on where to buy various Robes.

That's all well and good, you say. But what does it look like on?! The Runway section showcases various players modeling some of their favorite fashions.
