Archivist Kera

NPC ID: 1255  •  Location: Dereth Archives

Archivist Kera is a scholar who tends to the historical records of Dereth. She can be found cataloging tomes and offering lore about local threats and defenses.

Background

Kera has dedicated her life to preserving histories and warning local settlements about emerging threats. She occasionally provides quests that involve recovering lost documents.
