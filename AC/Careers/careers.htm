 In Dereth, it is vital to plan your development carefully. You might find that you will scrap your first few attempts as you discover mistakes you made early in character creation.

The Guides section gives links to various essays and templates for suggestions on creating  your character.

The Professions section outlines the pre-made newbie templates (updated during the Castling event). If you don't quite feel up to creating one on your own yet, you might want to experiment with one of these.

The Skills page gives description and formulas for the Attributes and Skills available in game.
