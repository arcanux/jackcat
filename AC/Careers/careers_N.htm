Ned Cleversun's Job Openings

  

Home

 Here are a few of the job openings currently available from Ned Cleversun:

Whiner Secretary
If Ned Cleversun were not determined, he could easily spend all of his time in game answering tells from people complaining about him being wherever it is he happens to be. Everyone wants Ned Cleversun to be somewhere else! "Level 80 in BSD?! It's disgraceful! I can't get any experience! Have you tried Aerlinthe? Have you tried the Martinate Holding?" The Whiner Secretary's job is to maintain a running dialog with all these whiners so that Ned can concentrate on killing Tuskers.

Imperiler
While Danier, the Monarch of DaMonarchy on TD, frequently performs this function for Ned in BSD, he is sometimes not available. After all, Danier has 2,200 followers and has many things on his plate, so to speak. Your job as Imperiler is to help Ned get the optimal amount of experience per hour. Be part of a growing team of professionals. Must have Life Magic and good tanking skills.
