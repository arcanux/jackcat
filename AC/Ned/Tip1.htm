TIP FOR ORGANIZING SPELL COMPONENTS

Players with magic: when buying comps, put the comps in the second half (the bottom half, below the scroll line) of your main pack. If you do this, then:

-- Comps that you loot from monsters will auto stack with previously established stack of same comp. (see next tip)

-- Comps that you buy from mage shop appear at top of pack and can be stacked by highlighting the comp and pressing the "F" key. This eliminates the need to move comps to sub-packs.
