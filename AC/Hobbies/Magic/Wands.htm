Random Loot Generated Wands & Scepters
In addition to the many quest and crafted items listed above, many creatures drop wands as random loot. The value of these wands varies greatly, depending on quality and depending on what spells they may have. Because of the low burden, wands are often desirable to pick up to sell for cash.
