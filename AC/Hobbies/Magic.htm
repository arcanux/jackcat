 Even the staunchest of melee characters has a hard time resisting the pull of magic in AC. This section lists the various spells in the game as well as the scrolls used to learn them and the wands used to cast them.

Visit the Scrolls page for a list of Scriveners who sell scrolls for level 1-6 spells. You'll also find information on Foci, and a list of quest scrolls. The last section details which creatures drop which level scrolls (to help fill out this section, send me an email with info).

The Item, Creature, Life and War sections list the various levels 1-6 spells for each school. (Spell durations have not been updated yet.)

Visit the Level VII Spells page to find out which Level VII spell name corresponds to the more familiar common spell name.

The Orbs, Staffs and Wands pages, like the weapon pages in the Sports section, break down the various casting items into the quest and crafted casting items, store bought casting items, and a sampling of the random loot generated ones.

Finally, the Spell Research page is provided as a historical reference.
