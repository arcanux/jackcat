Toys & Games

 From the Festivus event.

There hasn't been a trophy collection task as disgusting as this since the Composite Bow! But being fashionable for the Halloween holiday season is worth carrying around a few miscellaneous appendages.

To create your creature guise, you first need to collect 2 Arms, 2 Legs, and a Torso from one of the following creature types: Mu-Miyah, Sclavus, Skeleton or Undead. It doesn't matter which level creature you hunt; they all have a chance of dropping body parts. The following dungeons list some of the locations where these various creatures may be found.

Mu-Miyah
Mountain Keep - 54.7N, 32.9E
Mines of Despair - 75.4S, 57.3E (14+)
Sand Shallow - 11.5S, 0.7E

Sclavus
Sclavus Cathedral - 42.3S, 52.6W; 33.5S, 80.1W; 17.9S, 89.6W
Empyrean Foundry - 25.1S, 54.1E
Swamp Temple- 23.4S, 47.9E (10+)
Arcanum Storehouse - 54.8S, 74.4E
Lower (45+); Mid (30+); Upper (15+)

Skeletons
Catacombs of the Forgotten - 17.3N, 32.8E (15+)
Krau Li's Labyrinth - 35.2S, 23.8E (25+)
 Frest Greelving's Dungeon - 6.4N, 43.9E
Mines of Despair- 75.4S, 57.3E (14+)

Undead
Serac Vault - 75.9S, 29.0E
Halls of the Helm - 18.7N, 1.5E (10+)
 Frest Greelving's Dungeon - 6.4N, 43.9E
Mines of Despair - 75.4S, 57.3E (14+)

Once you have all your parts, combine them then bring them to one of the Mask Makers:

Alexander the Deft in Eastham - 17.6N, 63.4E
Janda Sulifiya in Al-Jalima - 7.4N, 4.7E
Tsua Kagemata in Sawato - 28.7S, 59.3E

They will hand you a Creature Guise in return! Sclavus, Skeletons and Undead also drop heads which can be made into Masks. To tidy things up, there are two versions of the Mu-Miyah Guise: one with a head (made with a Mu-Miyah Torso with a Head) and one without (made with Mu-Miyah Torso). The Mu-Miyah Guises also come in different colors, corresponding to the colors of the various real Mu-Miyah in Dereth.

Andreea of MT reports that the full Mu-Miyah Guise can be displayed on a wall hook, if you don't mind it facing the wall!

New guises were introduced during The Calm event: the Armored Skeleton Guise, the Armored Undead Guise and the Scarecrow Guise! Assassins also began dropping Pirate Hooks. Combined with Peg Legs from other high level humans and an Eye Patch, you can now have a complete pirate costume as well!

Note: after the Festival Season is over, creatures may no longer drop parts, so gets yours while they're available!
