 From Lonely In the World

Pack Toys were originally introduced as a gift to make up for a 36 hour bug-correcting roll-back. When players logged on, they got the following message and found a random pack doll in their inventory:

For a moment, you feel as if a wee little mousey has scampered up your leg...

These beloved little items became hot collectibles by many!

During A Reign of Stone, newer players were given a chance to get a pack doll of their own. Three casinos opened, a few selected dolls were available to win as prizes:

Aluvian  - Pack Scarecrow
Gharu'ndim - Pack Golem
Sho - Pack Drudge

You could also choose to give your Pack Doll to the owner of the casino for a chance to win one of the new Golden Gomnie Pack Toys, with hip hopping motion!

Pack Dolls became hookable during The First Strike. If you're like me, you hvae your very own little butt-scratching Plush Tusker guarding your front door!

The Visions in the Darkness event introduced yet another Pack Doll. Turn in an Insidious Monouga Idol to an Ivory Crafter for your very own Tremendous Monouga Pack Doll!

You give Ivory Crafter Insidious Monouga Idol.
Ivory Crafter tells you, "This is an excellent find. My friend is working on a collection and has given me an exclusive special offer. You qualify for the special collector's edition Tremendous Monouga Pack Doll. Supplies are limited, so only one per person, per month."
Ivory Crafter gives you Tremendous Monouga Pack Doll.

Note: you can only receive one per month. If you turn in a 2nd one too soon, you'll receive xp and a trade note.

You give Ivory Crafter Insidious Monouga Idol.
Ivory Crafter tells you, "I have already given you one of the nice stuffed Monouga's. But I can still reward you with a little something. There's a little extra money in there for you."
You've earned 8,000 experience.
Ivory Crafter gives you Trade Note (50,000).

During From the Darkness Born, previously retired Pack Dolls once again became available. Complete Bleeargh's Gratitude quest for 6 Mosswart Thanking Stones. They can be gambled at any of the 3 Casinos for a chance to win one of these treasured prizes!
