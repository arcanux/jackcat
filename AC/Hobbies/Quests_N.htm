Quests





 Ned Cleversun is not much of a quest guy. One of the few times I *was* able to drag him on a quest (PK Altar), he totally freaked. Instead, he's embarked on a few real life quests to improve his life in Dereth.

Quest #1: Ned goes to the player luncheon in Boston, gets drunk with the devs, whines and nags and gets them to make him a special 4X damage bow.

Quest #2: Ned crawls around in the dark at dev's group rental in Boston, finds keg of hard cider, chugs 63 ounces in less than 1 minute. Wins +100 natural armor increase.
