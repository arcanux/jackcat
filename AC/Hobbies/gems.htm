Special Uses




 Alchemy Gems were introduced during the Into the Darkness event and offer an alternative to Life Magic buffs. Pyreal Motes combined with Vials of Concentrated Aqua Incanta produce a new substance called Aqua Vitae. By using a crucible to mix common herbs, potions and powders with the aqua vitae, alchemists can create a full set of life magic protections gems from levels III to VI. Rengeration, Rejuvenation and Mana Renewal may also be produced!

To receive a guide, visit Ciandra, the Arcanum Alchemist, in the Xarabuydun Consortium.

Shortcut: To get to Xarabydun, take the Monument Sands settlement portal from Al-Arqas (33.6S, 132.E). It will drop you at 37.9S, 16.0E. From there it's a short run to the Consortium.

Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist tells you, "I haven't been this excited since we discovered the Temples of Enlightenment and Forgetfullness! I was able to get some of my thoughts on paper, but the most important pieces were in front of us all along. Pyreal motes and Aqua Incanta -- who would have ever known?"
Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist gives you The New Alchemy.
Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist tells you, "In this book you'll learn the very basics. Thanks to Hiro supplying the crucible, a discovery that he simply grumbles about, we're now able to brew potent magical concoctions that can be turned into solid forms, much like what the Lugians are able to do. Visit Zarri ibn Khaybi to buy your crucible. Good luck!"

Text: The New Alchemy

The crucible can be purchased from Zarri ibn Khaybi in Xarabaydun at 41.9S, 16.2E.

Vendors: Alchemy Supplies
Vendors: Spell Components

The basic steps to make the gems are outlined below:

1. Concentrated Aqua Incanta + Pyreal Mote = (5) Aqua Vitae

+ =

2. Crucible + Potion = Crucible with Potion
You add the potion to the crucible.

+ =

3. Crucible with Potion + Herb = Potion and Herb Crucible
You add the [herb] to the mixture, successfully predicting the correct timing.

+ =

4. Aqua Vitae + Potion and Herb Crucible = Treated Potion and Herb Crucible
You add the aqua vitae to the mix, the formula bubbles and appears ready for application to a crushed powder.

+ =

5. Treated Potion and Herb Crucible + Powdered Gem = Spell Gem
The powder congeals into a gem-like substance. The process is a success!

+ =

Note: To make more Aqua Vitae at a time, you can use combined pyreal.

Pyreal Sliver = 10 Aqua Vitae
Pyreal Nugget = 25-30 Aqua Vitae
Pyreal Bar = 50-55 Aqua Vitae
High Quality Ingot = 125 Aqua Vitae

The herb used determines the level of the spell you will produce:

Level III gems - Eyebright
Level IV gems - Henbane
Level V gems - Amaranth
Level VI gems - Frankincense

The Potions & Powders determine the spell type:

Stibnite + Onyx = Armor
Quicksilver + Hematite = Piercing
Verdigris + Turquoise = Bludgeoning
Cadmia + Moonstone = Blade
Brimstone + Malachite = Acid
Colcothar + Quartz = Cold
Turpeth + Carnelian = Fire
Cobalt + Agate = Lightning
Vitriol + Bloodstone = Regeneration
Cinnabar + Amber = Rejuvenation
Gypsum + Lapis Lazuli = Mana Renewal

If you fail the skill check, your mixture will be destroyed!

You fail to time the cibation correctly and fail the process.

You fail to surmise the correct time for cibation and ruin the mixture.
