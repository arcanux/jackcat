Pyreal Bars
Alchemy is used to make Pyreal Bars into Pyreal Ingots. Pyreal Bars are used in the Altan Weapons Quest and Isparian Arms. They may also be turned into an Agent of the Arcanum for a Writ of Refuge. The higher the quality of bar, the greater your chance of receiving a peerless or perfect weapon!

Isparian Weapons
Alchemy is used in several steps when making an Isparian Weapon: when using a Decanter of Essence on Diamond Powder to make the oil, using the oil on a Pyreal Ingot to make a Diamond Infused Pyreal Ingot, and when making the Oil and treating the Diamond Heart for the Modifying Tool. See the quest write-up for details. Originally, the skill checks were very high, but they have subsequently been reduced, most recently, during the Heart of Woe event.

Making Slag for Impious Staff
To make Slag for the Impious Staff quest you must be trained in Alchemy (no skill check). Harlune the Misanthrope will give you the Smelting Pot in exchange for the broken staff. The Iron Ore is found in Colier Mine. See the quest write-up for details!

Oiled String for Composite Weapons

Alchemy is used in the Composite Bow/Crossbow quest to oil the sting with Bloodseeker Oil. The string determines the weapon's damage modifier. The string can be made from either 3 Braided Sclavus Tongues or 3 Braided Ravener Guts. The best string is made from a successful application of oil to Braided Ravener Guts!

Making Dyes

Alchemy (as well as Cooking) is used in Making Dyes. Crush your plant using the Mortar & Pestle, then use Neutral Balm to create a vial of dye. If you fail the skill check, you will destroy the plant.
