Special Uses




 The Alchemy Skill allows you to create gems, potions and oils. The skill costs 6 skill credits to train and 6 to specialize.

Formula: (Coordination + Focus) / 3

Use the links to the left for detailed information on alchemical formulas, making potions -- health, mana and chorizite -- and making spell gems.

In addition to these basic uses, the alchemy skill is also used in dyeing and in certain quests. Visit the Special Uses link for more information on using Alchemy for Pyreal Ingots in the Atlan and Isparian Arms quest, for slag in the Impious Staff quest, oiling a string for composite weapons, making dye and stamping an Alchemy Flag!

Text: Alchemy Guide
Text: The New Alchemy
Vendors: Alchemy Supplies



Tip: If you are making health potions, make sure you don't have the exact amount of health needed for the potion or you will DIE! If you have less health, you will get an error message.
