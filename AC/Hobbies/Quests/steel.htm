From A Swelling Tide.

Sturdy Steel Chests are opened with Sturdy Steel Keys and yield 1-5 items of high craftsmanship. They chests may also drop Level VII spells, double-spell jewerly or clothing, and occasionally Majors!

The chests are found in the Direlands outside of the VoD. They've often been spotted around Ayan Baqur (especially near the lifestone) and the Nexus.

The following dungeons also contain Sturdy Steel Chests:

Tainted Grotto
17.8S, 50.6E - all
Map: Tainted Grotto
Creatures: Burun Ruuk Fiend, Burun Ruuk Lout, Burun Ruuk Seer, Burun Ruuk Soothsayer

Directions: Chest is found in the lowest level. This dungeon also contains a Sturdy Iron Key Chest.

Mountain Citadel
5.4N, 71.8W - all
Map: Mountain Citadel
Creatures: Extas Raider, Gotrok Juggernaut, Gotrok Tiatus

Directions: Keep right from the entrance and go through 2 rooms. You'll reach a 3rd room after the passage begins heading south. Take the east passage from the room and head down the ramp. The chest is in a small room at the end of the passage.
