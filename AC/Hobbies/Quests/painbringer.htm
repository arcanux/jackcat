Gandalf Zu'l Zoran of SC reports finding a corpse at 1.5N , 88.0E on Aphus Lassel.

You search the corpse...
You examine the ramains closely, and find that this poor soul had nothing of value...
...except for a dirty scrap of paper.
You take the note and think to yourself, "What was he doing on the shore, this far north?"

You give Brighteyes, the Tailor Dirty scrap of paper.
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "Where did you find this?"
You point to the west, towards the place where you found the body.
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "Along the shore you say? Let me examine this note a little more closely."
Brighteyes studies the scrap of paper given to him.
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "You are lucky I have learned about their strange methods of communication, otherwise I don't think there'd be an Isparian alive who could translate this."
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "It seems this particular fellow was taken "below" and given...uh...great power."
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "And somehow it...ah...opened his mind -- see here, it is indicated with the symbol they use for light."
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "And here, the symbol for...fighting or war, I think. It is repeated over and over - I am not sure why."
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "I'm afraid I can't figure out much else -- however I am quite concerned with this "enlightened" sycophant. A revolution among the enslaved masses of these islands would be my dream come true!"
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "It would seem as though he was escaping from somewhere "below," on the large island to the west."
Brighteyes, the Tailor tells you, "Possibly the Tusker Assault lair? I have heard that subterranean complex is teeming with the beasts and their thralls."

Lucifer's Hand of TD reports: "Just wanted to give ya some info on the new quest on Aphus Lassel. First you need to find a Sychophant corpse on the western part of Bobo's Island (Same island Kleeoh is on). Loot the corpse for a piece of paper, then take it to Brighteyes the Tailor in town. He will then instruct you to head to the Tusker Assault dungeon. Once in, you will encounter Deranged Psychophants, Obsidian Chitticks, Assault Guards, and Enraged Female and Male Tuskers. When you get to the end of the dungeon, you will find a new tusker called The Painbringer. Kill him then loot the Head off of the corpse. Take the head back to Brighteyes and he will give you the Staff of the Painbringer." Thanks also to Revenants Soul of LC!

Dungeon: Tusker Assault - 3.9S, 84.2E
Level: 85+
