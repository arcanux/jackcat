 The Soul Staff is a trophy from a Lugian Warlord, a level 46 Lugian introduced in the Keep Your Enemies Closer event. During A Perfect Paradox, the staff was updated and is now a more powerful weapon! The approximate drop rate for the staff is about one in four.

Lugian Warlords are found in the Linvak range between Qalaba'r and Kara. (Click on the pic to the left for a map of the spawns.)  They are usually accompanied by several Gigas Lugians. Like all the creature lords, they can be scarce and difficult to find and are harder to defeat than their regular counterparts. For best results, use lightning attack.

The staff is extra cool and creepy looking, covered with the faces of defeated foes. 

During the Come What Follows event, Belinda du Loc in Stonehold developed the ability to enhance numerous trophy items. Hand your staff to her and she will return the enhanced version. Original pre-patch weapons cannot be enhanced.

NPC: Belinda du Loc - 68.9N, 21.6W (Stonehold)

Soul Staff
A bleached staff with strange faces carved on it. Some seem to be Lugian, some Isparian, and others simply have blackened out faces. The staff seems to be a collection of the faces of the foes the former owner defeated in combat. Lightning crackles among the mouths and eyes of the carved faces.
