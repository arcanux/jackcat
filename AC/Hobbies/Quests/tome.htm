From the Friend and Foe event.

Fizzy Magoo of WE reports: "Hey there Maggie. I think I have discovered a new quest. I took the Kivik Lir portal in the basement of our mansion. I ran around for a bit and found a dungeon at 42.7S 46.6W. An NPC named Ssavashka the Devoted was beside the portal. I talked to him and he asked me to complete a quest for him. I entered the dungeon and discovered it full of mid lvl Revs. The dungeon itself is fairly linear. If you come to a dead end it isn’t far to backtrack and try a different way. Just keeping working your way down. Eventually you will come to a room full of lvl 120ish Revs and Demilichs. In their midst is an undead boss. Kill it and loot a “Crypt of Adhorix Gem” The respawn on the boss is about ten minutes or so. I grabbed three gems just to be sure. I gathered a full fellow of friends and opened the portal the gem produces. Inside is a Blackmire looking room full of lvl 120’sh Revs again. In the middle of the room is Adhorix’s Tomb. Around the tomb are three giant pillars. Our fellow destroyed the pillars first, then went to work on the tomb. I’m not sure if you have to kill the pillars first or not but we did it in that order. Once the Tomb is destroyed it dropped some “rubble” We hit the surface portal and came out at the original dungeon. We all gave the rubble to Ssavashka the Devoted. In return he gave us a hookable book that increases the users focus by 10 as well as some xp. Pics included. Keep up the good work. :0)"

From Darius of the Nexus - HG

Ssavashka the Devoted tells you, "Help me, man-thing. Help me. My master's tomb lies defiled within that portal, profaned by the Dericostian sorcerer-dog known as the Eternal Harvester. Day and night, sleep and wake, I hear the voice of Master Adhorix. He is in pain because of the Dericostian's desecration."
Ssavashka the Devoted tells you, "End my torment. Go into this portal, defeat the Harvester, and destroy the tomb of Adhorix. It is the only way we will have peace. Bring me rubble from the tomb once you have done this, and we will reward you."
Ssavashka the Devoted says, "Why have the Old Ones abandoned us to the foul leeches of Dericost?"

Dungeon is called Font of the Enternal Harvester (lvl 80+)

The gem dungeon is very small and has creatures on a 30 second spawn.

I just tanked damage and destroyed one pillar and then the tomb and got my rubble.

You give Ssavashka the Devoted Tomb Rubble.
Ssavashka the Devoted tells you, "At last, my master can rest in peace. At last, his voice no longer wails in my mind. I thank you, man-thing. Perhaps there is a way I can give you to speak with my Master yourself, that he may show you his gratitude..."
You've earned 2,300,000 experience.
Ssavashka the Devoted gives you Tome of Blood and Bone.

Thanks also to TheKnight of SC!

The Gerbil King of HG sends this additional information: "Figured I would just let you know that the pillars themselves control the spawn in the final room. It is not needed to destroy the pillars in order to get the rubble. "
