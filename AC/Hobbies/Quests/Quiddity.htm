Doogens Mage says, "Two of our members were at Black Hill on TOP of the plateau with the 2 portals and the tower. A server message appeared... and then a spawn. Ordinarily that is a safe area."

Doogens Mage says, "They cleared it, and after the mess of Dark Marionettes, Tuskers and Virindi Masters, they found Quiddity Ingots. The ingots say right on them where to turn them in. We are trying to find out how to get the baddies to spawn."

Zonata says, "and they are givable Maggie, both the ingots and the weapons."

Bring the Quiddity Ingot to the Virindi Crafters in the North (32.0N, 43.2W). ID the Virindi to determine which item they craft. Give the Ingot to the Virindi Crafter and you will receive the corresponding Quiddity Weapon in return.

Crafter of Orbs tells you, "You are made of flesh, and are therefore the one whom I seek. Grant me the ore delivered by the Quiddity, and I will make what you desire."

Crafter of Orbs tells you, "Odd... you are not as the master described. Your epidermis is fair and supple."

Several people claim that they have received off-the-record confirmation from the Devs that the raids were Admin triggered. In a recent interview (January 30, 2002), Troop had this to say: "The Quiddity raids are not currently going on in game. Quiddity Ingots will probably reappear in Dereth at some point, though."

During the Persuasion event, Quiddity Ingots were made available again as a quest reward. Click here to find out how to get a Quiddity Ingot of your own!
