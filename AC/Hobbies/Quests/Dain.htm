Here's a little walkthrough for the Gareth Dain Quest. First of all talk to Talira in Candeth keep, he asks you to search for his husband, that was last seen west of the city.

Map: Sclavus Temple

Go to the sclavus temple (82.5S 75W), and enter the dungeon. Take the path directly in front of you down to thh next level. Clear the sclavus form the room and find a passage down . From there, take the left hand passage and proceed through some rooms filled with sclavus. In the 3rd or 4th room you'll notice there is a path to your left, take it and meet the strange sclavus. Talking to him, he'll ask you to leave him alone. Here you seem to have a choice, kill him or not. I chose to kill him (nothing in his corpse).

Strange Sclavus yells, "Death to the intruders!"
Strange Sclavus's seared corpse smolders before you!
Strange Sclavus falls. It hisses with its final breath, "Tell my wife I love her very much."

Proceed off the path you were on, continuing down passed two doors, until you reach a room with Falatacot giant stone heads. In the next room over (passed a door with diff 325 after vagabond gift, there is another large room. On your left is another passage, with a door of 450 diff after vagabond's gift. Get through it, clear the area of sclavus (important), and then attack the sarcophagus at the end (vulnerable to fire). when destroyed, it'll spawn a Falatacot plus a full respawn of the room. On the Falatacot is a scrap of paper, with a single word, "Akri"

Go back to the room next to the giant falatacot heads, and this time proceed straight. You'll eventually reach a surface portal, DON'T TAKE IT. Take the passage to the left, through another door (325 diff), and in a room with more sclavus and two useable torches that light the room. use them and continue, until you reach a room with 3 chomu sclavus lords and 4 sarcophagus. Kill the sclavus and then use all the torches in the room, and a passage will pop out. Follow the passage till you get to a room with a book inside it. Loot the book.

Return to Candeth Keep, and let Talira read the book, she will ask you if you had vanquished whoever had turned her husband to a sclavus (that was the Falatacot Sister in the sarcophagus). If you did kill him, you'll receive the Polestar in return (pic enclosed)

It seems there is another possible outcome to this quest, as Dalius is none too keen on the outcome of the events. Both NPC's reacted to the scrap of paper (before meeting the strange sclavus) indifferently (Dalius) or casting a war spell on you (Talila)

Hope this helps

Hi Maggie,

Just ran the Gareth Dain quest with a few people.  Start by talking to Talira Dain and Dalius Kendmar in the northwest tower of Candeth Keep, then run to 82.5s, 75.1w to the Sclavus Temple.  Outside are regular and Lord Sclavi, fairly easy to pick through them and get to the portal inside.

Once in the dungeon, you'll face exclusively Lord Sclavi of varying
types.  Make your way downward to the 2nd level, and there will be a small opening in one of the walls that leads downward into an area that looks EXACTLY like the Ancient Temple where the Living Tome is.  At the bottom, you'll see a large room with a Sarcophagus at the opposite end.  The Sarcophagus IDs as an item, but is attackable and debuffable.  It has about 20,000 hp or so, but there's no real way to tell.  Once it's killed, it spawns about 30 uber Sclavi and an undead Falatacot called Sister Vitriaka.  Once you kill her, you can loot a Scrap of Paper from her corpse.  The only thing on the paper is the work 'Akri'.

There will also be a torch at the bottom of the stairs that opens a hidden door in the large room.  Go through the door and down some spiral stairs and you'll find a small room with an unreadable text called Falatacot Tome, which you translate in Hebian-to.  There's also a book called Book of Memories to be found behind another hidden passage in the halls before the big room with the Falatacot.

When you go back to Candeth Keep, you can give the Book of Memories to Talira Dain for the Polestar -- OR -- you can give the translated version of the Falatacot Tome to Dalius Kendmar for the Staff of Coercion.  Do not give the books to the wrong person, do not give them the Scrap of Paper, and do not try to get both items or the second attempt will result in them destroying the book you give them and calling you a liar.

No idea yet what the Scrap of Paper is for.

Hope this helps!
Confucius of FF

In the write ups for the new quest this month, it was mentioned a couple times about needing to pull a torch to get the unreadable text.

To give more specifics on it, since the current writes contradict themselves.

Upon entry of the room, it is the second torch on the right. *Important* you need to have the note from the Sister on you to be able to open it, as the word on the note is translated into "open". If you don't have it on you, the torch will flare and suck away all your mana and stamina.

You will know you are successful if you torch flares. The hidden door is on the opposite wall from the torch.
