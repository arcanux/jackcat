From A Swelling Tide.

Rumor: Renegade Note

Oopa of MT sends this reports on General Garsh: "We saw this outside during a raid on Fort Tethana. It disappeared when it was near death though. There is an outpost nearby to the west that seems to be thier home base. And they do respawn apparently .. in ranks all around Teth."

Fort Tethana Sentry tells you, "You may pass through the gate."
Fort Tethana Sentry says, "Hurry, lest the Renegades gain entry!"

Raider Juggernaut says, "Gotrok, to my side! The Isparians have begun to fight back."

With the last of his strength, the Commander cries out for his troops to fall back.

 Note: The Renegade Bow and Crossbow were intended to have wield requirements of 290 Bow and Crossbow base skill. Next month this will be corrected (if your skill is not 290, you will no longer be able to wield it). Bow may be given.

From Visions in the Darkness
Montezuma's Revenge of HG reports:  the Tumerok Generals are down to 5,000HP and easier than VoD Tummies. The zillion hollow Lugians and vuln-casting Tumeroks can make for a hassle, though. Both times, they dropped the Hunting Brace, but I couldn't pick up the second one - there's a one-week timer. I don't know if that's new or not.
