During the Come What Follows event, a new NPC arrived in Stonehold. Kill 10 Fallen creatures for an xp reward and a new title!

NPC: Belinda du Loc - 68.9N, 21.6W (Stonehold)
Rumor: List of Hunted Creatures

Belinda du Loc tells you, "Hello, I am Belinda a hunder of rare creatures. There's nothing like the hunt to let one know they are alive."
Belinda du Loc tells you, "I would like you to experience the hunting of the elusive banished creatures. Return to me when you have located and killed 10 of them."
Belinda du Loc tells you, "I would like you to experience the hunting of the elusive fallen creatures. Return to me when you have located and killed 10 of them."
Belinda du Loc tells you, "I would like you to experience the hunting of some of this lands rarest and most elusive creatures."
Belinda du Loc tells you, "Use that list as a reference. Kill 50 of the creatures in the "Rare Game" section of that list and I will reward you greatly."
Belinda du Loc gives you List of Hunted Creatures.

Belinda du Loc tells you, "Excellent, you are now an experienced hunted of fallen creatures. If you have any trophies from these creatures you don't need I will accept them and reward you alternatively."
