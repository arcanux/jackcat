From the Throne of Destiny

After completing the Knights of Karlun quest, you can return to Grand Mother in Silyun for a 3rd task.

To reach Silyun, take the portal outside of Sanamar at 76.6N, 58.7W.

NPC: Grand Mother - 87.4N, 70.5W (Silyun)

Grand Mother tells you, "You, who have done so much for the people of Silyun, I now request a boon from you."
Grand Mother tells you, "Not for the people. Not for the cause. But for ME!"
Grand Mother tells you, "I seek...no demand vengeance. It is my right and I have been denied it for far too long."
Grand Mother tells you, "For you see, not long after we arrived on this wretched land, my favorite grandson was taken from me."
Grand Mother tells you, "One of those vile creatures we now call Eaters attacked our camp and brutally killed and ate him as we we all watched in stunned horror."
Grand Mother tells you, "The short of the tale is that thing escaped and all attempts to kill it have met with failure."
Grand Mother tells you, "You shall be my blade. My truth. My justice. My revenge!"
Grand Mother tells you, "Seek this creature out and kill it."
Grand Mother tells you, "Bring me the jaws of this Eater so that they can cause no more sorrow to others."

Marauder's Lair - 83.9N, 66.4W
--Levels: 1+
--Map: Marauder's Lair
--Creatures: Truffle Thrunguss, Engorged Eater, Eater, Marauder

Keep west from the entrance until you reach a ramp down. Keep left and you'll go down a ramp, then up one around a corner. At the dead end, jump. Go south and jump again. Head up the stairs to the west and jump into the back of the dungeon to the west in the area with the surface portal. The Marauder is by the portal, accompanied by a few Eaters. Kill the Marauder and return the Jaw to Silyun.

Timer: 7 days

You give Grand Mother Marauder's Jaw.
Grand Mother tells you, "This is from the beast that killed my grandson?"
The fire in Grand Mother's eyes slowly fades replaced by tears of sorrow.
Grand Mother tells you, "For so long I have waited for this moment."
Grand Mother tells you, "All the old pain and memories flood back into my soul when I look upon this jaw."
Grand Mother tells you, "Bless you soldier of light. You will forever have my gratitude."
You've earned 360,000 experience.
Grand Mother gives you Grand Mother's Medallion.
Grand Mother gives you Hero of Silyun Token.

You give Grand Mother Hero of Silyun Token.
Grand Mother tells you, "For your deeds and service to Silyun I shall bestow the title Hero of Silyun upon you. Kneel before me."
Let it be known that [player's name] has won the approval and support of the people and shall henceforth be called, Hero of Silyun!
Grand Mother tells you, "You have done much for Silyun. And for that, we are grateful. Go in peace, my friend."
Grand Mother tells you, "You have done much for Silyun. And for that, we are grateful. Go in peace, my friend."
