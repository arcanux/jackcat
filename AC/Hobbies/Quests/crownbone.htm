 From The Madness of Men.

Rumor: Skeleton Raids

Note: This rumor is believed to be for the Crown of Bone introduced during The Madness of Men. Unknown when introduced into the game.

A report from Trieze of FF: "Hiya magie, check out the castle NE of Shoushi at 24.3S, 76.6E. It now has skeleton bone lords, skeleton warriors, fleshless warriors, and the baron himself. I have included pictures of him, stats, and how his little crown looks."  (5 min spawn)

Baron Nuvillus's Castle - 24.3S, 76.8E.
Shortcut: Take Mosswart Place Cottages settlement portal from Shoushi at 31.3S 72.6E.

This Castle was updated during the Madness of Men event and now presents a formidable challenge. Home to the Spinning Wheel used in the Flag quest, the castle has gone from a previously vacant dwelling to the home of numerous skeletons!! To reach the Spinning Wheel on the upper floor you must now fight through an plethora of creatures: Fleshless Warrior, Sewer Rat, Silver Rat, Skeleton Bone Knight, Skeleton Bone Lord, Skeleton Captain, Skeleton Lord, Skeleton Wraith.

In a small room on the upper level, you can find a Runed Chest guarded by Baron Nuvillus. The Baron himself also drops the Crown of Bone (one week timer).
