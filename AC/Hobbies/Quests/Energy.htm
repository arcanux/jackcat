 The Energy Crown is a trophy from The Pure One, a new Doll introduced in the Keep Your Enemies Closer event. The approximate drop rate for the crown is about one in four.

Pure Ones can be found west of Yanshi. Like all the new boss creatures, they can be scarce and difficult to find. Midoriiro of WE reported his find at 9.1S,  35.9E. Sek the Wise and Kriesen of MT report that the Pure Ones spawn in one set Zone, between 9-13 South and 30-37 East. 

They suggest tying at the Abandoned Arena (11.3S, 37.6E) first and wearing Bludgeoning and Acid protections, plus Magic Resistance.

Pure Ones are usually found with 2-3 other Dolls, Innocent and Innocuous. When you kill the Pure One, it may also drop an Obsidian Shard in addition to the crown!! 

During the Come What Follows event, Belinda du Loc in Stonehold developed the ability to enhance numerous trophy items. Hand your crown to her and she will return the enhanced version. Original pre-patch crowns cannot be enhanced.

NPC: Belinda du Loc - 68.9N, 21.6W (Stonehold)
