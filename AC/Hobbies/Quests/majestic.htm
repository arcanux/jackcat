NPC: Majestic Pumpkin - 30.7N, 27.8E

Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "Do not fear me friend. I am a spirit of the earth and I would have you help me."
Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "A few days ago I witnessed a terrible evil while visiting one of the growing places. A monstrous form was moving in the patch. It's shape was that of a huge man, but it's body was made of my kin. I do not wish to speak of what I found in the patch after destroying the abomination. Know that it fills me with a great sadness and rage.."
Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "I will not abide with these perversions of my kin. Find 3 of these Pumpkin Lords, as they call themselves, and destroy them. Return to me when your task is complete and I will reward you as best I can."


 

Pack Pumpkin Lord
The vile and naughty Pumpkin Lord. He was grown from a bad seed.
Value 10
Burden 10

 

Golden Pumpkin
A beautiful golden pumpkin.
This item is used in cooking.
Value 10
Burden 70

 

Festival Lights
A small reflective pumpkin bauble with dancing colored lights around it. Don't drop it unless you want to lost it. This item will quickly disappear if dropped on the ground -- it will even disappear from inside a pack, if that pack is dropped on the ground.
This item can be used on ceiling and wall hooks.
Value 5000
Burden 50

 

Pumpkin Backpack
A hollowed out pumpkin with some straps so it can be used to carry things.
Can hold up to 24 items.
Value 250
Burden 1
