During the Repercussions event, Flags got an update when new Flag Stamps were introduced as creature drops. The first stamps were the Academy, Alchemy, Bael'Zharon, Cooking, Fish, Fletcher, Herald, Moon, Olthoi and Virindi stamps.

Use a stamp on a flag to decorate it. If you want the symbol to be a color other than white, you can first dye the stamp! Stamping a flag may require a special skill or have other requirements. View the stamp descriptions to the right for more information.

If your skill is not high enough and you fail the skill check, you will botch your flag!

Note: You can turn stamped flags in to Verdentine Morgan and receive a clean white unstamped flag in return.

See the Flags quest for details.

During the Festivus event three more stamps became availalbe. In addition to the Skull Stamp which is dropped by creatures, the Chess Stamp and the Wi Stamp may be purchased from one of the three furniture vendors:

Cragstone - 24.6N, 47.1E
Hebian-to - 39.4S, 82.8E
Zaikhal - 13.2N, 0.3E

Another stamp was introduced during The Iron Coast event. The Royal Coat of Arms stamp is a reward from the Emissary of Asheron for the defeat of the Harbinger!

Emissary of Asheron tells you, "I see that you have stood against the beast that threatens to tear this world asunder. Remember that our efforts against this creature will continue forward."
Emissary of Asheron tells you, "You have done well to defend the land. In time we may well find a way to destroy this being but until then we must remain ever vigilant."
Emissary of Asheron tells you, "For your efforts my master and the High Queen wishes to reward you."
Emissary of Asheron gives you Harbinger Arm Token.
Emissary of Asheron gives you Royal Coat of Arms Stamp.
Emissary of Asheron tells you, "If you return that to me I will reward you with experience and a new title. As there was much more to learn within than met the eye."

See the Harbinger quest for details.

Another store bought stamp arrived during the Discoveries event. You can purchase a Mite Stamp from the Lost Wish vendors outside of Arwic (take the Mt. Shortcut portal from Arwic at 34.9N, 56.0E).

The town stamps are the latest introductions. During the Heart of Woe event, a New Town Stamp could be received in exchange for salvage.

Aun Teverea tells you, "Thank you friend. It will take many skins to build the tents and lean-to's we are planning for the new stronghold."
Aun Teverea gives you New Town Stamp.
Aun Teverea tells you, "You have given one-half the resources of which the alliance is seeking from each member. It is people like you who will be considered the true founders."

During A Swelling Tide, the New Town Stamp was renamed the Candeth Keep Stamp as a result of the town naming contest!

Caelis Renning tells you, "You have given one-half the resources of which the alliance is seeking from each other. It is people like you who will be considered the true founders of Candeth Keep. These alliance has allocated a symbolic gift for all of those who show dedication to the project."
Caelis Renning give you Candeth Keep Stamp.

See the Salvage Titles quest for details.

Flag Stamps may also be used to decorate select items, such as the Shield of Power and the Nefane Shield. See The Madness of Men patch page for details!

Botched Flag
A fine flag made from grievver silk.
You can use this flag on roof and yard hooks. It can be dyed using the cooking skill. It can be stamped with a stamp symbol. Give this flag to the Grievver Scholar, Verdentine Magnan, in return for a fresh white unstamped flag.

Value

 500
Burden 50
