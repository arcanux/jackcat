Tekapuapuh
To see the outcast Tumerok and his pet Putiputi for yourself, head west to 46.4N, 91.5W. You'll find Tekapuapuh standing next to a small fenced yard, complete with lawn gnome and an Olthoi Grub! When you speak to him, you'll find that his grub is hungry for some special food, the "onga" that can be found in the dungeon where Tekapuapuh discovered the grub. Bring back lunch for Putiputipuh and Tekapuapuh will reward you by crafting a bow from various artifacts retrieved from the dungeon.

Secret Putiputipuh Place
As Tekapuapuh's note instructs, head to the Secret Putiputi Place at 46.3N, 82.1W. The dungeon is restricted to levels 20+.

If you are not high enough in level you'll get the following response:
Tekapuapuh tells you," If only you where a little stronger you could help me and my putiputipuh!"

Inside you'll fight through 4 sections, retrieving 3 Mysterious Artifacts from Olthoi Cisterns, and a message from a 4th Cistern. After each section, you'll need to destroy glowing pustules until you trigger the one which opens a portal into the next section.

Section 1
 Map: Secret Putiputi Place - Parts 1 & 2

When you enter the first section, you'll be in a room with several Olthoi Grubs (Level 18) which are feeding on a dead Olthoi corpse. As you follow the corridor, you'll fight your way past Olthoi Nymphs (L32), Workers (L53), Drones (L35) and Soldiers (L61). All the Olthoi in the dungeon are a creepy pale white color. You'll eventually come to a room with Grubs, Workers and about 9 blue sac-like Olthoi Cisterns. One of the cisterns contains the first Mysterious Tumerok Artifact.

Note: You can use your examine key to identify which cistern is "Unlocked."

Using the Cisterns triggers more Olthoi to spawn. You'll receive the following messages:
You feel around in the warm slime for a bit. This one seems to be empty.
 Something slimy grips your hand and then quickly releases it.

Once you have your first artifact, continue in the dungeon until you find a room with Glowing Pustules. When you use a pustule, you'll get a message that hints at what you need to do:

A glow emits from the top of a cistern on the other side of the room then quickly fades.

Destroy the pustules until you trigger the portal at the opposite side of the room. Jump through it to the second section. The portal spawning pustules will always trigger the portal each time you kill it.

A section of the floor boils up producing a large glowing pustule.
The pustule explodes, spraying you with it's rank fluids.
The Pustule shudders, then bursts. You have opened a gateway portal!

Section 2
Map: Secret Putiputi Place - Parts 1 & 2

From the entrance of part 2, keep to the left until you go through a series of toxic green doorways into the cistern room. From section two forward, numerous floor triggers spawn Olthoi throughout. In addition to the earlier Olthoi, you'll also be fighting Harvesters. Lag can be brutal. Turn down your graphics to help.

Toxic air singes your lungs for 2 points of damage!

Again kill the Olthoi and find the cistern which contains the second Mysterious Tumerok Artifact. Head back through the doorways and keep left again until you find the pustule room. Kill them until you spawn the portal.

Section 3
Map: Secret Putiputi Place - Part 3

For section three, take either passage and head east until you find the large open room. Head down and look for the opening at the bottom of the pillar. Follow the ramp, keeping straight. Bear south and then up a ramp into the big open cistern room. The far east pit contains the cistern with the third Mysterious Tumerok Artifact. After you're done fighting in this room, it will look like a giant can of RAID exploded!

The pustule room for this section is on the same level as the cisterns. The pustule you need is in a second pustule room, adjacent to the first. The portal will spawn in the room to the north.

Section 4
Map: Secret Putiputi Place - Part 4
The cisterns in the fourth section are in a large open space below a ledge riddled with passages. Unlike the previous three cistern room, you are not looking for an artifact. Instead, you are looking for a cistern which gives you the following message when you open it:

As you reach into the cistern, your hand begins to itch. The sensation dies down after a moment.

This message flags you to be able to feed Tekapuapuh's grub. Once you've received the message, you need to find Putiputipuh's onga, which are apples lying on the ground next to a backpack.

Head back up to the ledge and take the west passage, then south. You should find the apples on the ground in a room with some Olthoi Grubs. Once you have your apples, return to Tekapuapuh. In one of the passages near the apple room you will find a portal which takes you directly to him!!
