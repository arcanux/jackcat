 From Pillars Made of Sand.

Renegade Camp: 10.2S, 59.0E (or nearby)
Rumor: Map to Burun Lair

Neros of TD reports: "I discovered a camp of Renegade Tumeroks and Lugians at 10.2S, 59.4E (East of Yanshi, West of Dryreach). In the camp, there's a few maps on the ground as well as a campfire (6400bu) that can be picked up."

Dungeon: Burun Cavern - 12.0S, 50.0E (near Yanshi - no level restriction)
Creatures: Burun Luuk Routs & Seers
Rewards: Carved Gemstone Lockpick, Renegade Lugian Ring, 2 Texts
Text: Gems
Text: A Message from Morgluuk

A report from Deven-Kenyon of SC: "The drop is safe to buff, take the west passage at the T and keep to the left side of the wall all the way down. There's a key at the bottom. Portal recall and take the Right passage this time, keep on the right side of the wall all the way down. This side deviates in that there's a hole in the floor. Keep going down to the chest and use the key. Inside there's a ring, lockpick, and lore. One note is called gems, one's a fetid note that needs to be brought to the Aun in Arwic." Thanks also to Thor Azoroth of TD!

You give Translator Aun Laokhe A Fetid muck-covered Note.
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, “Foul beasts. They mean to make war on us all and overwelm this world for what appears to be Wharu. They claim they have begun to change the burnt lands, the desert I think? Be wary of this foe. I fear that they will arrive in great waves.”
Translator Aun Laokhe gives you A Message from Morgluuk.
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, “Farewell budhi.”
