From A Perfect Paradox

The Skeletal Atlatl is dropped by a Lord of Decay, a level 25 Skeleton introduced in the Keep Your Enemies Closer event. It wasn't until A Perfect Paradox, however, that the creature began to drop the Atlatl.

Lords of Decay have been spotted west of Yanshi, south of Uziz. Occasionally, they hvae been seen on the beaches near Eastham. Davidge of SC reports finding his at 2.3N, 7.8E SE of Al-Jalima. Wizo of MT found one N of Hebian-To on the beach. (Click on the pic to the left for a map of the spawns.) They are usually accompanied by 3 Skeleton Warriors. Like all the creature lords, Lords of Decay can be scarce and difficult to find. The approximate drop rate for the atlatl is one in four.

During the Come What Follows event, Belinda du Loc in Stonehold developed the ability to enhance numerous trophy items. Hand your atlatl to her and she will return the enhanced version. Original pre-patch weapons cannot be enhanced.

NPC: Belinda du Loc - 68.9N, 21.6W (Stonehold)
