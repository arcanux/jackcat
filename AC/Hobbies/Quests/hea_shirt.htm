From the Friend and Foe event.

Town Crier tells you, "I have heard that the Hea town of Ahurenga is being plagued by thefts..."

First the Drudges, then the Thrungus and now the Mosswarts! Dereth is overrun by thieves! Speak to Hea Kassoka in Ahurenga to find out how you can help stop the madness!

NPC: Hea Kassoka - 47.3N, 90.1W

To reach Ahurenga, take the Northern Ahurenga Plains portal from Redspire at 41.0N, 83.5W, or take the Ahurenga Portal directly from Stonehold at 68.6N 20.7W.

Hea Kassoka tells you, "Much as it shames me, human, I would ask for your help. A group of filthy, smelly creatures known as Mosswarts have made a home on my island and are raiding my supplies. I am but one warrior and cannot attend to my duties and guard my supplies all the time."

Hea Kassoka tells you, "I would like for you to go into Hagra's Holdfast at 48.2 N, 88.5 W and confront their chief. Whether you must negotiate with him or just kill him, put an end to their thievery."

Hea Kassoka tells you, "We have discussed this, have we not? Go into Hagra's Holdfast at 48.2 N, 88.5 W and confront their chief. Put an end to their theft of my supplies, one way or the other."

Hagra's Holdfast - 48.2N, 88.5W
--Levels: 35+
--Map: Hagra's Holdfast
--Creatures: Mosswart Clinger, Mosswart Mire-Witch, Big Chief Hagra

The drop is safe and the dungeon is fairly straightforward. You'll nead south initially until you reach a room full of cooking vats and giant mushrooms. Head through the room and the dungeon will eventually bear north. Pass through 2 more of the large room on your way to the final room where Big Chief Hagra awaits.

If you remember, Hea Kassoka said you could either negotiate or kill Big Chief Hagra in order to stop the theft.

If you speak with him, he'll give you a treaty to take back to Hea Kassoka.

Big Chief Hagra tells you, "You! Human help Hagra! Big 'Rok with spear in 'Rok village, very angry with Hagra! Hagra have no choice, Hagra's people very hungry! We escape bad things on bigger land, need food to live and make more Mosswarts! You take this to Big 'Rok with spear, tell him we be friends!"
Big Chief Hagra gives you Hagra's Treaty.

Note: Hagra's Treaty

If you kill him, you'll find his Big Head on the corpse (2 week timer).

Take the surface portal and head back to Ahurenga. Hea Kassoka will reward you for either the Treaty or the Head. If you're a person of peace, you'll get a larger xp reward!

You give Hea Kassoka Head of Big Chief Hagra.
Hea Kassoka tells you, "Ah, the ugly little beast's head. This will do. It is fair and right that I reward you, human. This is an armored shirt of my own making."
You've earned 50,000 experience.
Hea Kassoka gives you Hea Bone and Hide Shirt

Alternate reward:

You give Hea Kassoka Hagra's Treaty.
Hea Kassoka tells you, "So the little beggar would trade peacefully with me, eh? Still, it is an interesting proposal, and I think they may prove useful to me..."
Hea Kassoka tells you, "Very well. I thank you for resolving this situation without undue bloodshed. It does not surprise me that you weakling humans always seek peaceful solutions. I will reward you with an armored shirt of my own making..."
You've earned 75,000 experience.
Hea Kassoka gives you Hea Bone and Hide Shirt.

If you're conflicted and first negotiated, then killed Hagra anyway, you get scolded by Hea Kassoka if you try to turn in both:
You give Hea Kassoka Head of Big Chief Hagra.
Hea Kassoka tells you, "You brought me Hagra's treaty, and I was well pleased. The situation was resolved without bloodshed. Why, then, would you bring me his head? While I appreciate a good beheading as much as the next Hea, this seems... wasteful. You will get no additional reward from me. Begone."

If you speak to Hea Kassoka before your timer is up, you'll receive the following message:

Hea Kassoka tells you, "You have helped me resolve my problem with the Mosswarts, human, and I thank you. I do not think we have any other business, outlander."
