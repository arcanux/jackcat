From the Friend and Foe event.

When these hunting quests were introduced, repetitive hunting gained some structure! Speak to various NPCs and they'll set you on a task to kill a certain number of specific creatures. Your reward is an experience bonus, a housing plaque and a title token! Let's hear it for goal oriented hunting!

Both the tables to the right and list below give details for each kill task: the town, NPC, creature, title and xp reward! Below, you'll also see the text spoken by each NPC. You can click on the creature type for more information on where to hunt. If you are too low level to enter the dungeons where specific creatures are found, the NPC will warn you to come back when you are stronger! Kill tasks are on a 2 week timer.

Additional Marae Lassel kill tasks were added during Under Cover of Night and Come What Follows. They are also included below. For the Vissidal Isle kill tasks, go here!
