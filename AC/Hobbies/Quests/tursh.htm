From the Assault event.

Deep inside one of Queen Elysa's Hidden Vaults lies an artifact of great power, the Tursh Totem. Speak to Royal Guard Tirenia in Holtburg and learn that creatures have attempted to steal this item! Retrieve it, and return to her for a reward.

Royal Guard Tirenia - 42.1N, 33.7E

Tirenia tells you, "Please, the Queen is in dire need of your assistance! A group of creatures recently assaulted one of the Queen's hidden vaults. The few guards who were able to escape told me that these creatures were after some sort of artifact."

Tirenia tells you, "These vaults house numerous objects of immense power. Whoever these invaders are, we cannot allow them to obtain that which they seek. I need you to infiltrate the vault and repel the interlopers. If you happen upon the object they are after, return it to me."
Tirenia tells you, "These creatures cut off surface access to the vaults. You will have to enter the sewers beneath in order to break in. Access to the sewers can be found in an old temple at 77.2N, 33.0E."
 
The temples can be reached by headed to Plateau Village and taking the Zatara settlement portal. The temple is a short run from there. There are four level restricted dungeons to enter, however I only have information for the level 100 or greater version.
 
The Defiled Temple Asylum is filled with Restless Spirits and Pestilence Rats, both level 135. Be prepared, as the spirits will cast Blade Vulnerability VI and Imperil VI very often, and attack with a salvo of heavy slashing spells (including Whirling Blade VI and VII, as well as blade walls). Make your way to the center of the temple and jump down onto the bridge, from here you can either jump into the pit (if you dare) or follow the corridors down. When you reach the pit, kill the Restless Spirits there until you get a message...
 
Restless Spirit's ethereal energies wash over you as its form dissipates into nothingness. You feel changed somehow.
 
You will now be flagged to enter the portal at the back of the dungeon. Portal Recall will take you to the top of the temple, run past the pit and to the back of the temple to find the Sewers (not exact name) portal.
 
Inside this dungeon you will face Pestilence Rats, Diseased Phyntos Wasps, Ripper Grievvers, and acid elementals called Virulence.
 
You will need a large group for this dungeon, as the spawn rates are quite fast and there are more than a few corridors that are literally road blocked with a mass of creatures.
 
When you reach the end of the sewer you will find the portal to the Fortified Royal Vault. Upon enter the vault you will be attacked by two Sewer Rats (level 58). In the rooms after the drop you will find several Royal Guards.
 
Royal Guard tells you, "The Queen dispatched a force of Guards to explore the shores of Northern Osteth. It was then that the Shadows struck. They took us completely by surprise. We are mostly new recruits here. I fear we are not quite up to this challenge."
 
Royal Guard tells you, "You bear the stench of the sewers on you. Perhaps your stink will drive them off!"
 
Royal Guard tells you, "They're after the Tursh Totem! You have to get in there and take it from them. The Queen would rather the totem be destroyed than it fall into the hands of the Shadow."
 
Royal Guard tells you, "Where did you come from? I thought all access to the surface was cut off."
 
After that you will encounter Drudge Bloodletters, Peerless Drudges, Drudge Cabalists, Virindi Consuls, Virindi Desecrators, Shadow Nightmares, and Olthoi Brood Matrons (you will not fight the Olthoi, apparently Queen Elysa has been keeping several of them in a caged pit).
 
At the end of the vault you will find a hostile Lugian named Kerrak. Upon slaying him you will find a large number of Tursh Totem Shards. Return the shards to Tirenia in Holtburg.
 
You give Tirenia Tursh Totem Shard.
Tirenia tells you, "The Tursh Totem. If they had escaped with this object..."
Tirenia tells you, "You have done our Queen a great service. I am only sorry that the totem had to be destroyed in the process. Still, that is better than the alternative."
You've earned 11,549,313 experience.   (Percentage reward, not sure on the exact amount. I'm level 103)
Tirenia tells you, "You deserve a reward for your heroic actions. Please take this replica as a show of our gratitude."
Tirenia gives you Replica of a Tursh Totem.
Tirenia gives you Royal Oil.
 
The Replica of a Tursh Totem is a mansion hookable portal that sends you to the Defiled Temple. The Royal Oil is a full arm (upper + lower) paint that casts Focus VI, Mana Renewal VI, Minor Willpower, Moderate Focus, Willpower VI, and Piercing Protection VI.
 
-- Arvid of Frostfell

Zulu of TD reports on the Lower Wing:

"I did the lower version of the Tursh Totem quest and here's some details.

First, there are 4 level restricted Temple Portals as follows:

Defiled Temple Lower Wing (40-59)
Defiled Temple Middle Wing (60-79)
Defiled Temple Upper Wing (80-99)
Defiled Temple Asylum (100+)

There are three dungeons to each wing, the temple, the sewers, and the royal vault.
I completed the 40-59 version solo with a level 59 Spear Character (with life magic).
The temple area was full of Spirits and Sewer Rats. A couple of Specters were there too. Killing a Spirit in the 'pit area' flags one to enter sewer portal. Bring a stack of dispel gems for this area..Very fast spawn! The sewers contained Gold and White Phyntos wasps, Sewer Rats, Spiked Grievvers, and Apozims...again fast spawn but pretty straight forward to last portal. The last area was the Royal Vault and it contained some Royal Guard NPC's then Panumbris Shadows, Drudge Raveners, Augmented Drudges, Virindi Observers and Councillors, and eventually the boss Lugian, Shoktok, a level 70 Lugian with around 5000 hp. I died in boss room first time through but after rebuffing I could recall directly to Royal Vault. This time I was able to land an Imperil V on Shoktok and take him down. He dropped a totem shard that I turned in to the Holtburg NPC for 15% to level and the lowbie version of Royal Oil. I attached Pics of Shoktok and Royal Oil lowbie style.

Thanks for putting up the best AC site!"

Dear Maggie,

At around 4:00 PM EST Deadlybow II, The Hill People, and I (Wild Hunt) went and *tried* to do the tursh totem for levels 80-99. At first it seemed like a breeze but further down it got worse. (NOTE: respawn timers are lightning fast, and them rats are irritating.)

Once you get into the level 80-99 Defiled Temple, you run down the corridor and through the archways till you come to a big open room with a BIG pit. There is a bridge in the middle of the pit. Kill all the Poltergeists than shift jump on to the bridge. Kill the rats real fast. Find the room with a Runed Chest and run down the corridor. Stick to the left walls and go down again. If you're not fast enough the rats will respawn. When you run through the corridor you will end up at the bottom of the pit. There are 5 or 6 special Poltergeists. Each person in the group has to be flagged by killing a Poltergeist.

Once everyone is flagged. Do a *Regular* Portal Recall. Then run through the corridors and archways and run around the pit opening and through the second set of corridors and archways and run into the Sewers Portal.

Now the sewers are pretty straightforward. You will encounter Bane Greivvers, Giant Phyntos Wasps, Infested Rats, and Miasmas. Run through the halls until you run across a big room with 5 bridges in different locations and higher up. Run through the halls until you cross each of the 5 bridges (It will feel like you are running in circles but your not). Each time you cross a bridge make sure you're up one higher than the last. After you cross the last bridge you will come to a 3 way forked ramp. Run forward. You will end up with a portal. Go in.

Now you should rebuff. People that can't buff theirselves can go to a mansion or hometown and *regular* portal recall back. Once everyone regroups and is ready to go. You can Explore the Guards outpost until you're ready to go. Go through the doors (it's easy enough to find) and run down a ramp. You will encounter about 20 Unconquered Drudges on the first level. Once you kill all those, find the next down ramp, but be careful: you will trigger a Virindi attack. After you kill off those V's and drudges, find your way down to the 3rd level, with Shadows.

We're too tired to keep playing so we're taking a break. We're logged off inside the 3rd dungeon. In the morning we'll send in more info.

Wild Hunt
