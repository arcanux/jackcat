From the Friend and Foe event.

NPC: Surloshen Realaidain - 99.7S, 96.7W (Caul)

NPC: Xireri - 94.4S 96.6W

Tunnel of Love - 94.4S, 96.6W
--Levels: 120+
--Map: Tunnel of Love
--Creatures:

Galia of LC with A Princes off LC reports: "The fellow I was with found a tower with a golem named Xireri (lvl 85) on it, and a portal below called Tunnel of Love. It's located at 94.4S 96.6W. It's 120+ to enter and you need to give Xireri a Butterscotch Suckling to get flagged for entrance.

You give Xireri Butterscotch Suckling.
Xireri bows deeply, then wiggles its hand and does a little pirouette. You surmise that such actions mean the Golem is thankful for the Butterscotch Suckling, and has now granted you access to the Tunnel of Love.

From Dark Savior of TD:

You give Surloshen Realaidain A Letter From the Heart.
Surloshen Realaidain tells you, "This... this... this tells me nothing."
Surloshen Realaidain tells you, "I see that look on your face. You think me in denial, don't you? You believe that my mother was crazy, that she obsessed over a man who did not return her affections. But don't you see? This letter proves nothing! Surely he simply refuses to take responsibility for his actions! Asheron is my father! And there is nothing you can say that will make me believe otherwise!"
Surloshen Realaidain closes his eyes and slowly exhales. The shaking in his body subsides as he calms himself down.
Surloshen Realaidain tells you, "I apologize. I should not have raised my voice. You have done me a great service, and for that I should reward you. Please take this helm. It is too heavy for me. My neck hurts when I wear it."
You've earned 42,000,000 experience.
Surloshen Realaidain gives you Surloshen's Helm.
Surloshen Realaidain slaps himself in the face.
Surloshen Realaidain says, "I told you so!"

In order to do the quest you must go to 94.4S, 96.6W on Caul island and talk to the golem who tells you to go to the East side of Caul and kill a certain margul. Once you get the item from the Margul, you enter the Tunnels of Love located at 94.4, 96.6. Once in I found my way to the top where there is an iron chest with a note from the heart within it, Once you get the note, return to the new NPC claiming to be Asheron's son and you get a new helmet with mod magic def and major acid ward with a 375 melee base wield req.

From somewhere up above, you hear the grinding of stone on stone as Xireri grows angry. You feel that perhaps you will not be welcome in these tunnels again. That is, until you appease the golem with some more of that tasty Butterscotch Suckling.

Thanks also to Tricky' of LC, Gasoline of LC!

From TheKnight of SC:

Levels: 120+
Location: Caul Island
Special Note: You must have lockpick in order to do this quest, or /pklite through the door with a friend.

Flagging: You must get a Butterscotch Suckling from a creeping margul. It is a rare drop and might take you awhile to get. A great location for a static spawn for these is at coords: 94.3S, 93.2W as 4 of them spawn there consistantly.

Getting Inside The Dungeon: Once you have your piggy, head to coords 94.4S, 96.6W and hand your piggy to Xireri the Golem.

Inside The Dungeon: Once inside it's pretty straightforward, as you'll be going up higher and higher as you go across the many bridges.

The Black Marrow Chest: Once you pass the locked doors you'll see a Black Marrow Chest that you can loot.

Towards The End: Towards the end you will find the exit portal and a chest which you will be looting for the note. Once you grab it you'll get this message:
"From somewhere up above, you hear the grinding of stone on stone as Xireri grows angry. You feel that perhaps you will not be welcome in these tunnels again. That is, until you appease the golem with some more of that tasty Butterscotch Suckling"

Procede forward: Now go to coords 99.7S, 96.7W and give Surloshen Realaidain the note for Surloshen's Helmet.
