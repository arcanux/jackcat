Monarch House of Elder, MT:

Hea Temenua Level 111 Hea Tumerok. He is accompanied by Level 111 Hea Kanokeh and Level 111 Hea Karenua.

Hea Temenua drops the two pieces for the Exquisite Elari Wood Bow, and a Hea Totem. 

Aun Harelthah 44.2N, 78.1W
The Hea Tumerk Tenepuh drops totems than you give to Harelthah. Apparently, he only accepts certain tokens at separate times, so you may have to keep checking back to redeem yours. You will receive either a Stave of Palenqual, A melee Weapon or a Ranged Weapon.

A report by Shadymilkman:

First off let me say this quest is almost an exact replica of the "assembling" steps of the SoCS. Like how there is a base and gems, but you can only assimilate 3 gems to the SoCS.

Alright -- your first step is to find a Hea totem. The totem is found off a random spawning tumerok named Hea Temenua. When you kill him you will find a Greenwood haft, Gromnie Sinew Bowstring and Hea Totem.

After obtaining his death items return to the Aun village and talk to Aun Hareltah.

He says the following: 
Aun Hareltah cocks his head to the side, as if listening to something inaudible.
"Wind has brought tidings of your deeds, friend. She whispers, '[Player Name] has slain the brutal Temepuh, who calls himself Temenua as he hunts your people on the upland. Temenua, who sheds your xuta's blood at the behest of the soulless atua ngamaru.'"
"This deed is a fiery blaze upon your keh, Isparian, even if you know it not. Should you desire the Stave of Palenqual, give me Temepuh's totem. Now we are pressed, and it is time for us to release the magic of Tonk."
"But if you do not desire this magical stave, if you would rather have the ranged kalindan or the fighting staff called hoeroa, hold your hand for now. 
I will only reward you once for the butcher's death."

So this says if you want to give him the totem now, he will give you the base magic item: Stave of Palenqual. However, if you are melee, or bow you may want to wait.

After you receive the base staff you must find 3 out of these 5 totems that you want to use on the base staff. Each totem will add different spells on the staff.

Now if you decide NOT to turn the Hea totem in for the Stave of Palenqual, you can re-click on him to ask him to make either a staff or a crossbow.

Just like a cycle, first time you click he asks if you want to redeem the totem for the Stave of Palenqual. Click on him again and he will ask if you want Hoeroa of Palenqual.

If you choose the melee staff, he does this:
Aun Hareltah gives you Hoeroa of Palenqual.
Be your eyes and mind open, though, for only three of these five boon-totems may be added to the totem of Palenqual.

The crossbow stuff is still unknown, so any info would be great.

Hope this helps!
-Shadymilkman mage
Level 85 Sorcerer of Solclaim
