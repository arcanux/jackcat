From the Repercussions event. This quest became repeatable during A Perfect Paradox.

[Player's name] has slain Dranith Menacet. As the Hollow Minion bleeds its last bit of life onto the ground, it calls to its farther in a child-like voice. "Father, I have failed you. They will find the way to your home." In Rithwic, a new portal appears.
Dranith Menacet says, "Father...Father... My body crumbles to dust, am I forgotten. I am dead. I am sorry father I have failed you and will rise no longer."

After Dranith Menacet was killed, a permanent portal to Martine's Retreat appeared in Rithwic (10.6N, 58.3E). Inside the retreat is a chest containing a Prismatic Stone which can be added to Isparian Weapons for added elemental damage!

Map: Martine's Retreat

To begin, find Josef. You'll find him east of the entrance in a small alcove. When you speak to him, he'll give you Josef's Wish List. As you hand him each item on the list, he tells you the full blown tale of Martine, Gaerlan and Asheron. If you give him a Singularity Key, he will hand you Gaerlan's Key.

When you have your key, head to the south wall of the Retreat and follow the ramps until you reach the invisible bridge. Jump up at the end to go through the secret passage. You'll be in a room with a Singularity Chest. Gaerlan's Diary spawns on the floor.

Text: Gaerlan's Diary

Use the key on the Singularity Chest and retrieve An Unfashioned Prismatic Stone (no-drop) from inside. Use an Intricate Carving Tool on the Stone (no skill check required) for a Major Prismatic Stone!

Use the Prismatic Stone on any Isparian Weapon to create a Shimmering Isparian Weapon for added elemental damage!

The shard of living crystal fall away fashioning a perfect prism.

For a chart of available Isparian Weapons click here.

Note: The chest contains numerous Stones, so you only need one Singularity Key for a group of people to complete the quest.
