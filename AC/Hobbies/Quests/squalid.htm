From the Friend and Foe event.

Issabow of HG reports: "So I got this hint off the barkeep in Silyun. There's a new Ruschk Camp located at 83.1N, 54.8W. When you arrive, clear the camp and talk to the npc inside. He sends you to 2 other camps:

--north - 85.3N, 55.0W
--northeast - 84.5N ,53.2W

Kill both camp leaders and loot 2 keys. Bring keys back to to original camp and open the chests in the NPC tent. Each key has 4 uses. There is a 29 day timer on looting items, but you can open chest with key before timer is up so don't waste your pulls like I accidentally did =(.  I recieved 2,400,000 xp and a shield from npc when I handed him both items from chest."
