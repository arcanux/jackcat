Thrungus Hole 94.2N, 43.0W - Level restriction unknown (180+ ?)

NPC: Hermit - 94.2N, 43.0W

Receive lifestone chips from hermit. Find 6 Baby Thrungus in dungeon. Give chips to Baby Thrungus and fight the creatures that spawn. At 6th, you will be dusted.

Return to Hermit and you'll receive a certificate for a Thrungus Reaper Title, 4 Trade Notes (250,000) and 59,000,000 xp.
