 On July 23rd, 2003 during A Swelling Tide, a new global was heard, heralding the opening of Asheron's Island near Eastham!

The ground trembles, casting stones from cliff faces and snow showering from the highest peaks.
The quake originates near the city of Eastham, but the shockwave can be felt as far away as Marae Lassel.
A piercing wail echoes across the ocean waters near Eastham, and a groundswell of Olthoi can be seen pouring over the Island to the east of the city.

As players explored, they discovered four level restricted portals to the island.

--Nanto Portal - 52.6S, 81.9E
(Asheron's Island South - no restriction)

--Khayyaban Portal - 47.4S, 24.9E (Asheron's Island North - levels 40+)

--Uziz Portal - 25.0S 28.1E
(Asheron's Island West - levels 60+)

--Baishi Portal - 49.7S, 62.2E
(Asheron's Island East - levels 80+)

The island was teeming with Olthoi! To the north, Asheron's castle could be seen in the distance. People soon found that they were unable to enter Asheron's Sanctum until they completed a quest. Enter one of the four Hives to be flagged to enter the Sanctum.

An easy task, you may say! That is, until you actually try to reach the entrance. They are heavily guarded by Olthoi and death awaits you!

Matron Hives
--South - 15.9N, 73.7E, Levels 20+
--North - 23.4N,69.4E, Levels 40+
--West - 19.3N, 72.6E, Levels 60+
--East - 18.4N, 69.3E, Levels 80+

Asheron's Sanctum  - 21.2N, 69.1E
Asheron's Sanctum Portal - 20.8N, 68.6E

Once you've entered one of the hives, you can then enter Asheron's Sanctum.

Note: If you enter through a summoned portal, you will not be flagged.

Darius Falc of WE reports:
Asheron's tower can be reached via the portal in the Matron's Hive on south Island. From the Nanto portal drop, run left with the shore on your left and you should find it. The portal to West island is at the bottom of the Matron's Hive, but it isn't for the weak hearted...Olhoi swarm eviscerators, mutilators, guardians, warriors and nobles abound.

When you get to West Island head for the big mountain thing, Asheron's Tower is on the top. This run is *very* scary...imagine your worst nightmare then add Jerry Springer.

When you get to the base of the mountain the only thing you can do is run around it till you find a way up....with half the Olthoi in the world trying to stop you.

There is a plateau half way up where you can rest that is relatively Olthoi free...well compared to everywhere else.

When you get to Asheron's tower you will find a portal to the Inner Sanctum...take it...you'll find Asheron and his Guardians...(Don't forget to take pics...great eye candy)...talk to asheron and he'll tell you that some 800 year old hive matron has returned and give you a book.

The book is 30 pages long so I haven't read it yet...if you want to know what's inside, go talk to Asheron :).

Have fun, and remember...be careful out there :).

DF.

Note: It's hookable!

Those who choose to explore the hives will find an Olthoi Brood Matron at the bottom. Eventually, after fighting the Matron again and again, players will receive a global message. After three globals over a period of 12 or so hours, another Brood Matron with lower health stats will spawn. Kill her to receive a 4th and final global. Once she is dead, you'll find a trophy on her corpse. From this point on, she spawns approximately every 5 minutes and drops the trophy each time she is killed. The portal on the mainland also changes so that players are deposited directly into the dungeon instead of the island.

Note: At this time the exact mechanics of the quest are still unknown. There's been some speculation that the mobs on the surface must also be killed in order to spawn the globals.

To reach the Matron, go straight from the entrance down the ramp then head to the left and down again. Head north until you reach the acid pit, then it's a straight run to the Matron.

The Brood Matrons from each of the four dungeons drop a different trophy. When they are turned into the Olthoi Hunter in Arwic at 30.9N, 56.3E, different levels of Asheron's Raiment are given in return with various random spells (see stats to the right)! Asheron's Undies are buffable and may be dyed.

There is a three week time on picking up the trophies.
