The Callous Heart Entry
When you use the portal gem, you will be transported into the Callous Heart Entry area, an open room with Bright Silver Keys scattered on the floor. Pick one up for use inside the dungeon (you can pick up as many as you want). There are several hallways from this main room leading to a series of portals. The North and West passages both lead to The Callous Heart High Grounds Portal, restricted to Levels 45+. The East passage leads to The Callous Heart Left Wing - Levels 30+, and the South passage leads to the Right Wing - Levels 15+. The portals cannot be tied to, recalled or summoned.

The Callous Heart Right Wing - 15+
The Callous Heart Left Wing - 30+
The Callous Heart High Grounds - 45+

The Callous Heart Right Wing
The Right Wing, restricted to Levels 15+, is the easiest, but that's not saying it's easy! When you enter, you'll fall a short distance, taking a few points damage. The initial room has a Harnessed Scintilla (Level 48) and a Controlled Flamma (Level 48). They'll hit you with Lightning and Fire damage, as well as Lightning Bolt III and Flame Bolt III.

If you manage to make it past these two foes, you'll see a ramp on the opposite side of the room. Head down... into a room with about 10 more of them! Your goal is to reach the chest across the room. Use your Bright Silver Key on the chest for your reward: An Encrusted Bloodstone Jewel (worn on the neck) with the spell Essence Spike (increases maximum health by 20 points). The chest contains numerous Jewels, but you will only be able to pick up one.

The Callous Heart Left Wing
The Left Wing is restricted to Levels 30+. Again, you'll take a small bit of damage on a fall at the entry. This dungeon is full of Obedient Obsidian Golems and Controlled Frosts (Level 51). The reward in the chest at the end is also An Encrusted Bloodstone Jewel, but the spell is Essence Sluice (increases max health by 25 points).

The Callous Heart High Grounds
The most dangerous dungeon is the High Grounds, restricted to Levels 45+. The inside is full of Bound Crystal Golems (Level 200) and Bound Coral Golems (Level 341), all on a very quick respawn. These creatures are just like their unbound compatriots, but do not drop loot (although they're 30K xp a pop!). After a short fall on entry, you'll be greeted by about a dozen of them. A small walk-way leads down into another pillared room, but there's no safety there, only another large group of them!

Head up another ramp and bear NW to find the chest. Be careful not to get trapped against a wall; if you do, you're a goner. Large groups have been known to be wiped out completely in this dungeon. Prepare for massive Bludgeoning damage! The chest at the end contains An Encrusted Bloodstone Jewel with the spell Essence Glutton (increases max health by 30 points).

It is possible to solo these dungeons by using Impen, heavy Banes and Protections, and boosting your health by any means possible (Elysa's Favor, Endurance, Health Elixirs, etc.) then running through to the end (shields recommended). Consider turning down your graphics to reduce lag.

Note: if you log out in the dungeon, you will be at the lifestone when you log back in.

The Encrusted Bloodstone Jewels are no-drop, no-give. The spells may not be layered with Life Giver from Elysa's Favor.
