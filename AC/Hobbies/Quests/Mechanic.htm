From the Reign of Terror event.

During a troubled time when the Olthoi menaced the land, a new NPC appeared seeking parts to a machine used to create poison. Speak to him and he'll request a list of parts needing replacement due to the corrosive properties of the poison itself. The Undead Mechanic is found on Marae Lassel at 42.1N, 79.0W.

Undead Mechanic tells you, "Although we are much more powerful than your kind, Isparian, we admit some trepidation about this incursion of Olthoi. Should they burrow too deeply near our sanctuaries ..."
The undead looks down for a moment, avoiding your eyes.
Undead Mechanic tells you, "So. We offer you our aid in repelling these creatures. We have at our disposal the recipe for a potent poison, a poison which can weaken even the Ancient Queen herself. But the poison damages the machine in which it is concocted, and to distill more of this poison we must constantly replace many parts of the machine."
Undead Mechanic gives you Missing Mechanisms.
Undead Mechanic tells you, "This text details the parts we will need. At one time there were stores of these parts cached about the land, but now, with the chaos of the times and your meddling, intrusive ways, Isparian, who knows where the parts may lie?"
Undead Mechanic tells you, "But find them, and bring them to me, and I will reward you with a dose of the poison."

Rumor: Missing Mechanisms

There are 20 alloy pieces scattered in various dungeons throughout Dereth (30 day timer). Originally, when you gave the Undead Mechanic one of the alloy pieces, you would receive Poison in return. This poison was used to ruin the Olthoi Food Stores when Nuhmudira led the attack against the Ancient Queen.

(See the Preparation for the Ritus and the Ancient Olthoi Queen quests.)

You give Undead Mechanic Alloy Engine.
Undead Mechanic tells you, "Ah, yes, one of our mechanisms. Very good. I have always maintained that Isparians must be good for something."
Undead Mechanic gives you Poison.
Undead Mechanic tells you, "Here is your reward. Use it most cautiously, and do not squander it!"

You allow Agent of the Arcanum to examine your Alloy Fan.
Agent of the Arcanum tells you, "What an interesting ... umm ... mechanism! I have no idea what it's for! But you know, those Arcanum Researchers, they've been working with odd metals like this -- you should ask one of them. You can find them in the libraries in Cragstone, Hebian-To, or Zaikhal."

You allow Arcanum Researcher to examine your Alloy Utensil.
Arcanum Researcher tells you, "Well, look at that. Where did you say you found this?"
The Arcanum Researcher studies the mechanism carefully. She rubs it, tries to dent it with a fingernail, even sniffs at the metal.
Arcanum Researcher tells you, "No, I've never seen anything like this. Although ..."
She tilts her head to the side as she thinks.
Arcanum Researcher tells you, "No, I think I do remember something about a metal like this. Something the undead were working on. Hmmm ..."
Arcanum Researcher tells you, "Why don't you ask the Undead Mechanic, by the waterfall on Marae Lassel? Maybe he can tell you something about this ... umm ... mechanism."

After the poison was used on the olthoi food stores and the Ancient Olthoi Queen was defeated, the Undead Mechanic began giving new rewards for the alloy pieces.

The bands, shins, and toes of the Walking Boots change color. The bands take the color of your undershirt. The shins take the color of highlight color of your lower body armor (bands on amuli), and the toes are the base color of your armor. The Walking Boots may be dyed.

Following are detailed directions to each of the alloy pieces. Note that several dungeons require lockpicking.

1. Alloy Device
--Dungeon: Filo's Doom 12.0N, 62.2E
--Closest Town: Rithwic
--Creatures: Russet and White Rat, Drudge Skulker and Slinker, Young Banderling, Gnawer and Carrion Shreth, Skeleton.

--To Alloy: South from the entrance and right down the ramp. Use the lever to open the door to the lower level. At the bottom of the ramp make a left at the T. Head down another ramp, and straight past the room with the exit portal. Keep to the right until you reach the Alloy Device.

2. Alloy Apparatus

--Dungeon: Empyrean Garrison 54.2S, 81.8E
--Closest Town: Nanto
--Shortcut: Subway to Shoushi, Shoushi to Nanto at 32.3S, 71.7E.
--Requirements: Lockpick
--Creatures: Drudge Prowler, Slave (12), Robber; Black and Red Rat; Blood and Hunter Shreth.

--To Alloy: Keep left from the entrance, skipping the first intersection, until you reach the ramp down. At the bottom of the ramp go left at the split, then immediately right. Use the levers to open the door to the east, then go through the locked door (lockpick required). Keep going down until you reach the Shreth area. The alloy is guarded by a Hunter Shreth.

3. Alloy Instrument

--Dungeon: Bone Lair 29.4S, 7.0E
--Closest Town: Al-Arqas
--Shortcut: Subway to Yaraq, Yaraq to Al-Arqas at 22.8S, 0.7W.
--Creatures: Skeleton Warrior and Captain

--To Alloy: Pick the lock of the right door in the large room. Use the lever in the small room to the right to open the door to the upper level. Follow the passage to the end and pull the large lever in the end room. Jump back down to the large room and go through the now open middle door. Go left at the split to the final room with the alloy.

4. Alloy Tool

--Dungeon: Glenden Wood Dungeon 29.9N, 26.4E
--Closest Town: Glenden Wood
--Creatures: Drudge Slave (12 & 14), Banderling Blade and Ogre, Shore Armoredillo, Lich, Silver Rat, Granite Golem.

--To Alloy: Use the Platemail Hauberk quest to find your way through the dungeon. When you reach the Granite Golem/Glenden Prison portal area, take the NE passage from the room to reach the alloy.

5. Alloy Contraption

--Dungeon: Tomb of the Dead 18.3S, 9.3E
--Closest Town: Tufa
--Shortcut: Take Subway to Yaraq, then take the Al-Arqas portal at 22.8S, 0.7W. Take the Bleached Skull Wastes Settlement portal at 33.6S, 13.2E which will drop you at 14.5S, 9.6E
--Creatures: Risen Soldier and Knight, Dark Wisp.

--To Alloy: Button in the middle room (west of entrance) opens door to lever room to the south. Pull all the levers, then head to the room north of the entrance (jump down). Take the now open door on the east wall then follow the passage to the final room with the alloy.

6. Alloy Implement

--Dungeon: Saadia's Retreat 53.6S, 76.6E
--Closest Town: Lin
--Shortcut: Tou-Tou portal from Arwic at 33.8S, 56.4E, Lin portal from Tou-Tou at 28.0S, 95.4E
--Creatures: Mosswart Fanatic, Idolator and Zealot, Silver Rat.

--To Alloy: Jump and head down until you reach the throne room. Make a left, then follow the passage until you're heading up. Take the steep wooden ramp down, then go up the stairs around to your left. The alloy is in the hallway above.

7. Alloy Utensil

--Dungeon: Enkindled Souls 49.4S, 26.9E
--Closest Town: Khayyaban
--Shortcut: Al-Jalima portal from Arwic at 33.6N, 57.1E, Khayyaban portal from Al-Jalima at 7.5N, 5.1E
--Creatures: Skeleton Wraith, Lich Lord

--To Alloy: From the entrance take the left split, then keep to the right until you reach the lever room. Pull the levers to open the doors to the ledge area (keep heading right from the lever room). Take the NW door around to the next level, then keep to the left through the 2nd part until you reach the alloy.

8. Alloy Mechanism

--Dungeon: Crypt of Ashen Tears 14.6N, 3.5E
--Closest Town: Zaikhal
--Creatures: Banderling Striker and Rogue, Lich Lord, Gold Wasp, Sewer and Black Rat.

--To Alloy: Keep left all the way down. The Alloy is found in the final Lich Lord room.

9. Alloy Cogwheel

--Dungeon: Theives' Den 17.7N, 65.0E
--Closest Town: Eastham
--Creatures: Banderling Striker, Bandit and Rogue, Drudge Stalker and Ravener, Skeleton Bone Knight.

--To Alloy: Go right at the sign, then take the ramp down behind the exit portal. Go left through the door and press the button behind the barrels in the corner. Go through the now open door down the north passage and jump to the level below (a on map). Keep to the left and take the 2nd available ledge jump down (c on map). You'll be in a small room with passages on all 4 sides. Take the north passage and jump again (b on map). Jump one more time (d on map) and take the north passage to the alloy.

10. Alloy Contrivance

--Dungeon: Smuggler's Hideaway 29.5S, 91.4E
--Closest Town: Tou-Tou
--Creatures: Banderling Striker and Rogue

--To Alloy: Take the bunker portal to enter the fort. Keep right all the way to the first lever. Head down the ramp and go left through 2 door into the big room. Exit south, go west at the 4 way intersection, the make a left at the split to the final room with the alloy.

11. Alloy Sprocket

--Dungeon: Mountain Keep 54.7N, 32.9E
--Closest Town: Colier
--Creatures: Dread Mu-Miyah
--Shortcut: Take the Mountain Keep Cottages Settlement Portal from Holotburg at 40.2 N, 35.1 E. You'll exit at 50.3N, 35.3E

--To Alloy: Jump from the entrance, then take the east passage and jump all the way to the bottom. Take the passage out of the room at the bottom to the north, then keep right, all the way up to the room with alloy.

12. Alloy Cylinder

--Dungeon: Dungeon of Tatters 62.2S, 40.1E
--Closest Town: Khayyaban
--Shortcut: Take the portal to Baishi from the Atrium Residential portal in Glenden Woods. Take the Tattered Ridge settlement from Baishi at 50.1S, 63.5E which drops you at 62.1S, 40.1E.
--Creatures: Shadow Lieutenant, Panumbris Shadow, Mist Golem

--To Alloy: From the entrance, keep left until you reach the ledge. Don't jump down; instead, jump kitty corner across to your right. Follow the ramps, going up, then back down. When you reach a small room, take the ramp to the north, then hug the left wall all the way down to the Mist Golem level to find the alloy.

13. Alloy Dynamo

--Dungeon: Dungeon Nye 6.2N, 34.4E
--Closest Town: Lytelthorpe
--Shortcut: Take the Lytelthorpe portal from Rithwic at 9.5N, 59.9E. Take the Enchanter's Meadow settlement portal from Lytelthorpe at 2.2N, 50.4E. You'll exit at 5.7N, 36.9E
--Creatures: Drudge Raveners, High Mu-Miyahs

--To Alloy: Entrance portal is in the lower level of a small bunker guarded by Dark Revenants. Surrounding area may be dangerous. From the entrance, take the ramp heading up near the surface portal. Keep left all the way down until you reach the exit portal. Face away from the portal and walk straight until you reach the wall, then move to the left until you find the secret passage. The alloy is down a passage guarded by 4 High Mu-Miyah.

14. Alloy Piston

--Dungeon: Old Mine 79.2S, 27.0E
--Closest Town: Qalaba'r
--Requirements: Lockpick
--Creatures: Lugian Montok, Wasteland Rat, Revenant, Dark Magus

--To Alloy: The entrance portal is guarded by Gigas Lugian. Once inside keep to the left going down a few ramps until you reach the ramp heading up to the west. Take the south passage from the room at the top of the ramp and follow it until you dead end in an alcove. Take the passage to the east and follow it until you reach the grated doors. Go through the 2nd set of grated doors and head down to the Revenant level. Follow the passage straight until you reach the locked door (lockpick required). Once through the locked door, go north ignoring all alcoves, then east ignoring all rooms and alcoves, then south until you reach the final room. The alloy is guarded by several Revenant and one Dark Magus.

15. Alloy Fan

--Dungeon: Crater Caves 66.7N, 12.5E
--Closest Town: Crater Village
--Shortcut: From Glenden Woods, take the Highland Manors Settlement Portal at 27.9N, 29.5E. From your exit at 63.2N, 16.9E run to Crater Pathway at 60.5N, 15.7E.
--Creatures: Cursed Wisp, Nubilous and Mist Golem, Fire Elementals, Magma Golem

--To Alloy: Make the first right from the entrance and head down to the room with 2 Cursed Wisps. Take the middle passage at the split, then head down the ramps into the big Golem room. Exit the room from the north. Go down the ramp into a small round room, then head east until you go down another ramp. At the 4 way intersection, go south, then make a left down the ramp. From there, just keep down the ramp to the magma pit. The alloy is back behind the pyreal bellows area.

16. Alloy Pinion

--Dungeon: Small Ice Cave 67.5N, 50.3E
--Closest Town: Bandit Castle
--Requirements: Karwin's Key. Give Karwin the Lunatic at Bandit Castle a bowl of Carol's Carrot Soup.
--Shortcut: Take the Mountain Shortcut from Arwic at 34.9N, 56.0E. Take the Bandit Castle portal at 42,4N, 60.5E. Run to Bandit Castle at 66.5 N, 50 E for key, then run to Ice Cave from Bandit Castle.
--Creatures: Wasteland Rat, Ebon Gromnie, Nubilous Golem, Risen Lord

--To Alloy: Keep left until the pit. Jump down, then take the ramp down to the east. Keep to the left, then cross the bridge. Use the key on the door (difficulty: 9999) to retrieve the alloy.

17. Alloy Engine

--Dungeon: Lost Garden Ruins 65.8S, 59.3E
--Closest Town: Kara
--Shortcut: Take the High Mountain Valley portal outside of Arwic at 26.3N, 63.9E
--Creatures: Cursed Bones, Pandemonium and Paroxysm Shadow

--To Alloy: Keep left from the entrance until the pit. Exit the pit room to the southeast then go west at the 4 way intersection. Look for the stairs on the south wall, then go up to the west. At the T go south, then east, then south to the alloy room.

18. Alloy Motor

--Dungeon: Inner Dungeon 43.5N, 43.4W
--Closest Town: Plateau Village
--Shortcut: Buy Plateau Invitation from a Wedding Planner
--Requirements: Lockpick - difficulty 350 (or purchase key from Windmill vendor at 40.6N, 41.6W)
--Creatures: Unruly and Terrible Monouga, Guardian and Nomad Mu-Miyah

--To Alloy: Unlock either door from the entrance and follow the corridor to the first room. Go through several rooms, keeping to the left at all times (you will need to unlock one more door) all the way down to the bottom room with the alloy.

19. Alloy Generator

--Dungeon: Mount Naipenset 5.6N, 56.2W
--Closest Town: Fort Tethana
--Shortcut: Take the Collosus Foundry portal to Teth from Ayan Baqur at 62.0S 51.3W, then make the long run to the dungeon. Or reach Teth by taking the South Dires portal from Mayoi at 62.2S, 77.1E, then taking the Slaughterhouse portal at 80.6S, 37.4W.
--Creatures: Gold Golem, Small Sandstone Golem

--To Alloy: The run itself is fairly safe, but the dungeon entrance is heavily guarded by creatures such as Coral Golems, Banderling Enforcers and Corrosion Wisps, etc. From the entrance, keep left all the way to the pit. Jump down, then use the pressure plates to open either door (plate is in front of opposite door). Follow either passage to a ledge, then just keep jumping down until you reach the ramp to the final room. The alloy is guarded by a level 100 Small Sandstone Golem! Cute, but watch your shins!

20. Alloy Turbine

--Dungeon: Phyntos Menace 68.1N, 20.1W
--Closest Town: Stonehold
--Shortcut: Take the Azaroth Settlement Portal from Qalaba'r at 74.8S, 17.8E, then take the Stonehold portal at 85.8N, 27.6W.
--Creatures: White Wasp, Harrower Grievver

--To Alloy: From the entrance, go left, then work your way east until you reach the center room. Take the stairs up to the north then turn right, going through 2 smaller rooms until you can take stairs heading up to the west. The alloy is in the south room at the top.
