From the Reprisals event.

These quest notes are provided as a courtesy until I can do a complete write-up.

Ophar Kabitaki of FF reports: "The Huge lump of coal found on Abominable Snowmen, when taken to the Blind Snowman, gives you a Snowy Cabin Snowglobe instead of the Snowman Snowglobe. No Spells, just a decoration."

Cotho's Mage of WE reports that the Huge Lump of Coal appears to be on a timer. When given a 2nd lump, the snowman responds:

Blind Snowman tells you, "Ah, more coal? You are very kind, but I have all I need to refashion my eyes. It may take some time, but you have helped me enough. Here, have this back in case you lose your eyes."
Blind Snowman gives you A Huge Lump of Coal.

Miroku Mage of TD reports: "On my way to one of the new dungeons, I saw a very large snowman. He was a level 125 Abominable Snowman. I started shooting him with a fire rend. Big mistake! He vulned me for cold and starting shooting walls and streaks (hitting me for 100+ each time). I finally switched to a CS and it took me about 5 mins to take him down. He dropped some poofy snowballs and a huge lump of coal. I ran to the snowman village and the mayor told me to give it to Elijah.

You give Elijah A Huge Lump of Coal.
Elijah tells you, "I see you have found my escaped creation. I was attempting to build a powerful defender for Snow Haven, but something went awry. Then there was the smashing and the stomping, oy the big teeth, then came the unbearable pain."
Elijah tells you, "With this Huge Lump of Coal I will attempt to reproduce the experiment in order to figure out what went so very very wrong. Don't worry I have things completely under control. Take this as a small reward for your help."
Elijah gives you Winter Orb.
