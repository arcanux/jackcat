From the Mirror, Mirror event.

The Doll's Eye was introduced when the Singularity Caul was updated during the Mirror, Mirror event. To reach the Caul you must be flagged by killing the Guardian of the Singularity, a level 100 Virindi found at the end of the Singularity Bore (see the Quintessence Sickle quest for details).
The dungeon is restricted to levels 50+. You can also use the Singularity Caul Recall spell.

Note: The Caul is the island Bael'zharon called players to run "The Guantlet", an invitation only quest to receive an Atlan Shadow Stone and the title of Dark Master. A group of Virindi led by Levistras chose the Caul as the location to build a new Singularity before they were defeated.

The ring is dropped by a Level 145 Doll, the Befouled Doll (rare drop). The Befouled Doll is found on the eastern and western area of the Singularity Caul. Watch out for both magic and melee assaults and bane for lighting. Use Slashing and Cold attacks for best results.
