From the Reprisals event.

Town Crier tells you, "The Ambassador in Silyun is seeking brave adventurers for some mysterious task..."

This quick and easy quest rewards you with experience and a pair of Whispering Blade Gloves in exchange for the Head of Baron Entemarre, a Viamontian Commander.

To begin, speak to the Ambassador Hashina in Silyun. To reach Silyun, head to Sanamar from Shoushi at 34.3S, 73.4E then take the Silyun portal at 76.6N, 58.7W. You can also reach Silyun via portal gems purchased from Luressi di Maki in Sanamar.

NPC: Hashina - 87.3N, 70.5W (Silyun)

Hashina tells you, "Queen Elysa has sent me here to establish a formal relationship with the good people of Silyun, who have rebelled against Varicci's tyranny. In order to aid them in their fight, I would ask you to journey into the Direlands near Ayan Baqur. The Viamontians have built a fortress at 62.7S, 82.5W. Bring me the head of Baron Entemarre, the garrison commander. The people of Silyun seek vengeance upon Entemarre for his brutal tactics in the earlier rebellion."

Hashina tells you, "Have you found Baron Entemarre at his encampment in the Direlands yet?"

Baron Entemarre's Fortress is a short run from Ayan Baqur (catch someone with a tie to Panopticon if possible). You'll see a guard tower just outside the fortress, and the fortress itself is guarded by various Viamontians.

Fortress Guard Tower  - 61.8S, 82.7W
--Creatures: Viamontian Knight,
 Viamontian War Wizard

Baron Entemarre's Fortress - 62.7S, 82.5W
--Creatures: Royal Thaumaturge, Viamontian Commander, Viamontian Counselor, Viamontian Knight, Viamontian War Wizard

Fight your way into the fortress to reach the portal to Camp Entemarre, a dungeon under the fortress where Baron Entemarre waits.

Camp Entemarre - 63.0S, 82.5W
--Levels: 80+
--Map: Camp Entemarre
 --Creatures: Voracious Eater, Viamontian Knight, Viamontian Commander, Viamontian War Wizard, Baron Entemarre

The dungeon is simple to navigate. Just keep straight to the final room where you'll find Baron Entemarre. Kill him (5 minute re-spawn) and return with his head to Silyun.

Timer: 13 days

Give Hashina Entemarre's Head for you reward: 1,500,000 experience (shares in fellowship) and the Whispering Blade Gloves!

Hashina tells you, "You have slain Entemarre and taken his head? Excellent. Please give it to me now."
You give Hashina Entemarre's Head.
Hashina tells you, "Yes, at last... This wretch Entemarre was responsible for great suffering among the people of Silyun. You must realize that there can be no official acknowledgement of your deed. However, I can reward you through my own resources..."
You've earned 1,500,000 experience.
Hashina gives you Whispering Blade Gloves.

Hashina tells you, "You have already helped me by slaying Baron Entemarre. There is nothing else I would ask of you now, but you have my continued gratitude."
