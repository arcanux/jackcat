From the Shining Runes and Shattered Hands event.

The following notes are provided as a courtesy until I can research a full write-up.

Town Crier tells you, "I've heard stories about adventurers hunting down and killing a large "Mukkir." I have no idea what a "Mukkir" is, but it sounds scary...."

Town Crier tells you, "Adventurers have reported that they've found Gems that will open a gateway to a nest of Mukkir, whatever they are."

Portal gems drop from Royal Inquisitor (Commander's Quarters in North Glenden Wood Prison).

Kill the Barbaric Mukkir Nest-Lord and loot its head (3 minute spawn). Give head to Qath al-Haddash in Qalaba'r for reward!

You give Qath al-Haddash Barbaric Mukkir Nest-Lord's Head.
Qath al-Haddash tells you, "Amazing..."
Qath al-Haddash tells you, "This is a valuable find. With this, we may be able to learn more about these "Mukkir" and predict what the Viamontians will seek next. Please, allow me to reward you."
You've earned 14,587,990 experience.
Qath al-Haddash bestows upon you the title of "Mukkir Masher."

An additional Mukkir Nest was added during the Shattering the Dark event.

Hi, Elizabetha Lazulae from Harvestgain again :). A few more pics here to round off the evening. It seems that pits full of low-level mukkir have appeared near the starter towns. These pictures were taken across the river from Shoushi (31.3S, 74.8E) and show Small Fledgeling Mukkir in their new home. The dungeon is basically just a series of caves and twisty passages and doesn't appear to contain any quest items or higher level creatures, although it's filled with swirling black clouds that make it hard to navigate. No level restrictions, just walk in. There are a few Mukkir around the entrance, so you can be sure when you've found it. Hope this is useful to you."

Yaraq Nest - 20.3S, 0.2W

Royal Guard tells you, "I have been sent by Queen Elysa to seek the aid of those willing to give it. There is a small nest of those vile Mukkir just northeast of here. If you will go thin out their number, say, kill 15 of the Small Mukkir Fledglings, I will reward you for your efforts."

Royal Guard tells you, "Excellent! The Queen will be most pleased. With their numbers thinned, we should be able to keep the local infestation of Mukkir in check for a time. Here, you've earned this reward."
You've earned 10,000 experience.
The Royal Guard grants you the "Small Mukkir Squasher" Title.

 

 

 

 

Mukkir Nest Portal Gem
A Viamontian crafted portal gem, keyed to open a Gateway to a hidden nest of Mukkir. Perhaps sommething of value can be gathered fromm this nest.
Use this gem to summon a short-lived portal to the Mukkir Nest. You must be at least 150th level to enter this portal. This gem works best if used outside in a relatively flat area.

Value

 1000
Burden 10
Spell Summon Primary Portal I
Spellcraft 210
Mana 50/50

 

Barbaric Mukkir Nest-lord's Head
The decapitated head of the Barbaric Mukkir Nest-lord. Qath al-Haddash in Qalaba'r may find this specimen of great interest.

Value

 0
Burden 150
Special Props Attuned, Bonded
