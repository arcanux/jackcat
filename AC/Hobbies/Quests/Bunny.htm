 Updated during The Madness of Men.

The regular titles not special enough for you? How about becoming a Bunny Master! Find Larry the Rabbit Master (2.0N, 41.6E) outside Lytelthorpe. He'll tell you a sad tale about ferocious rabbits which have taken over his subterranean gardens!

Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "My glorious garden! Ruined, it's ruined! It's those terrible rabbits!"
Larry wrings his hands.
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "When they first showed up, I thought it would be simple to get rid of them. But nothing worked! I kept trying and trying -- I recruited every adventurer in Dereth to help me -- but they wouldn't go away! Then I gave up and left my little farm behind ... But farming's in my blood, you see, and I just can't let the rabbits win. So I came back. ... And do you know what I found? They've been breeding! There are more of them then ever!"
Larry shakes his fist.
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "Go and see for yourself! See if you can do anything about those vermin, why don't you! Bring me back some sign that you killed one of the big ones and I'll make sure that everyone knows your rabbit-killing prowess."
Larry sighs, wistfully.
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "But I don't expect you can even make a dent. ... Oh, umm ...do you think you might spare a pyreal? I can portal you into my garden for a pyreal. The cost of maintaining this farm would amaze you."

You give Larry the Rabbit Master Pyreal.
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "Thank you, friend. This will help tide me through these hard times. And please, clear the angry rabbits from my lovely garden and I'll be forever thankful."
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "Just be careful, those critters know how to fight back!"

They must be related to that White Rabbit!! ;-)

Note: The Purchasing Order spawning on the floor of the hut nearby (15 minute spawn) is no longer necessary. Simply give a pyreal to Larry and he'll portal you (only you) to a dungeon of fierce pink, green and purple bunnies!

Map: Larry's Garden

When you enter the garden, you'll have 30 minutes to defeat the 6-7 Rabbits within. They will only attack if they are attacked and won't swarm, so you can take them one at a time. Although the name and color of the bunnies may vary, they will all be level 44 with the big Boss Bunnies being level 50. Kill the Boss Rabbits for a Lucky Rabbit's Foot. If you're lucky, you may even get Marshmallow Eeps as loot! (Not required to receive your title.)

After you've defeated the terrible beasts speak to Larry for your new title of Bunny Master! Yay!

You give Larry the Rabbit Master Lucky Rabbit's Foot.
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "Ah, you got one of the big ones! That's one pesky rabbit that won't bother my lovely garden anymore!"
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "You are truly the master of bunnies! And everyone should know it!"
Larry grants you the title of Bunny Master

You give Larry the Rabbit Master Pink Marshmallow Eep.
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "Yeah, these come off the little ones."
Larry the Rabbit Master tells you, "Golly! Tastes like chicken!"

During the Shining Runes and Shadowed Hands event (April 2006) another use for Eeps was introduced.

Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "Got any eeps?"

You give Ulgrim the Unpleasant Green Marshmallow Eep.
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "A Green Eep! Thanks! For some reason I've been craving these things."
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "So delicious and sugary..."
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "Ugh, I feel sick now."

You give Ulgrim the Unpleasant Pink Marshmallow Eep.
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "A Pink Eep! Thanks! Well... it's not my color, but..."
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "Oh, it's stale."
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "No, no, I love them crunchy too!"
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "Ugh, I feel sick now."

You give Ulgrim the Unpleasant Purple Marshmallow Eep.
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "A Purple Eep! Thanks! These taste the best of all the Marshmallow Eeps!"
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "You can taste the purple."
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "That hit the spot. Here, have this in honor of your beneficence."
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "You'll always be one of my Eeps."

When you give Ulgrim a Purple Eep, Ulgrim grants you the title of "Ulgrim's Eep! You'll receive no other indication that you've received the title.

Thanks to Rima of LC and Skeksis of VT for details on the new title!
