Epona Macha of TD sends the following details:

Tanada Master Quest (80+)

This is a one time only quest.

In Shoushi, talk to Sayuji Jina.
Go to the Kara portal, then to the Yukibazi settlement portal at 82.9s/48.1e. This drops you at 71.8s/47.3e. From there, run to the Tanada House of Storms dungeon portal at 68.1s/45.4e.

The drop is safe into the dungeon. This is a very easy and linear dungeon. Take a right at the first chance and then just follow to the end. There is one ramp going up to the last room.
Kill the Master and loot the medallion. He only drops one medallion and there is a 5 minute respawn.

Go back to the beginning and head toward the portal. There is a small shrine on the floor in front of the portal. Give your medallion to the shrine to be flagged for the portal.

Repeat the above for the next 2 dungeons.

The 4th dungeon is laid out a bit differently but still linear and easy. The end room has 4 acolytes with the Master. These also cast spells. Once you have your last medallion, return to Sayuji Jina in Shoushi.

Give her your medallion.
Reward:
4.8million xp, 3 Gems of Balance (+10 to coord), and The Rise and Fall of the Tanada (lore book).
