From the Friend and Foe event.

During the Friend and Foe event, Dirty Old Crates were spotted on the landscape, guarded by groups of Tumeroks. Halves of old weapons were found inside. Find two halves of a weapon and combine them to make a Worn or Battered Weapon!

Parts for the Worn Weapons have been found in the Direlands near Fort Tethana and the Obsidian Plains. Parts of Battered Weapons have been found scattered across Osteth, near Glenden Wood, Eastham, and Tou-Tou to name a few areas.
