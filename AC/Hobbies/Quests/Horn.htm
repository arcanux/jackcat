 Town Crier tells you, "Are you thinking of getting a Horn of Vigilance? Then go see Coron Usgin in Zaikhal. Has he got a deal for you! And you can only hear about it here, through Town Criers! Go see him today, won't you?"

Town Crier tells you, "Hear that? Of course not, you are not an Olthoi. That sound you don't hear is my new Horn of Vigilance keeping the vermin away. At least I think it is... Anyway, the deal was too good to resist and it's available only from Coron Usgin in Zaikhal!"

As the Town Criers suggest, start in Zaikhal by talking to Coron Usgin (above Jeweler)!

Coron Usgin tells you, "Hello harried traveler. Is thy residence threatened by Olthoi or other vermin? Then thou art in need of the Horn of Vigilance! I have been
commissioned by the famed Isparian Researcher Telk the Addlepated to sell instructions for this device that he has spent decades developing. The merest breath of wind blowing through this instrument produces a sound which is not discernible by human ears, yet repels all manner of vermin! It also makes an exquisite decoration for any residence!"

Coron Usgin tells you, "Such a fine instrument could easily be sold for 999,999,999 Pyreals, however I will provide you with everything you need to craft it for a mere jar of Sunflower Oil! For one single jar of Sunflower Oil I will provide thee with a beautiful, leather bound, instruction book describing the easy to follow crafting steps for making the astounding Horn of Vigilance."

Coron Usgin tells you, "But hark! There is more!"

Coron Usgin tells you, "For no extra fee at all I will give thee an extremely useful carving knife which thou may use to craft this item! This knife alone has a thousand
and one uses! It can carve arrowshafts, cut meat and chicken, chop cabbage, filet fish, slice mushrooms and carrots, cut bread and even carve a pumpkin! Is that not extraordinary?!"

Coron Usgin tells you, "Thus, I strongly urge thee to hurry, pick a Sunflower and bring it to Lilly of Glenden Wood. She will make thee some Sunflower Oil which
thou should bring back to me. It is just that effortless! One may find Sunflowers in forested areas of North and South Osteth."
Coron Usgin tells you, "When you return I will tell you about other items required. Good hunting!"

Hmm. Decades in development? Slicing and Dicing? All for one low price? Maybe if we're lucky, he'll thrown in a George Foreman Grill as well! ;-)

First bring your Sunflower to Lilly of Glenden Wood and have her make you some oil. (For more information on where Sunflowers may be found, visit the Interior Decorating page.) Lilly can be found at 32.0N, 27.2E

Lilly of Glenden Wood tells you, "I use Auroch horns in the making of my potions. I discovered how to use Mattekar horns to produce health elixirs and Sunflowers to make Sunflower oil."
Lilly of Glenden Wood tells you, "If you give me a horn, I'll give you some of my potion in return. If you give me a Sunflower I'll give you some Sunflower oil."
 You give Lilly of Glenden Wood Sunflower.
Lilly of Glenden Wood tells you, "Thank you. A lovely Sunflower."
Lilly of Glenden Wood crushes the Sunflower and mixes it into a vat of liquid.
Lilly of Glenden Wood tells you, "There you go! A nice supply of Sunflower oil."
Lilly of Glenden Wood gives you Sunflower Oil.

Return to Zaikhal and give Coron Usgin the Sunflower Oil to receive further instructions.

You give Coron Usgin Sunflower Oil.
Coron Usgin tells you, "Thank you for the Sunflower Oil! Thou hast made a wise purchase! Here is thy instruction book explaining how to craft the Horn of Vigilance and thy free carving knife! Be certain to read the instruction book carefully. There are a mere four items you will have to loot from creatures I am certain you will easily defeat. Then you must carve them into the appropriate components."

Coron Usgin tells you, "You may begin by getting a large Niffis Shell. It is needed for the base of the instrument. Not a small Niffis shell, mind you, but a large one-you are not in the business of crafting a mere kazoo!"

Coron Usgin gives you Crafting the Horn of Vigilance.
Coron Usgin gives you Carving Knife.

Text: Crafting the Horn of Vigilance (complete with disclaimer notice!)

Use your Carving Knife (actually, any Carving Knife will do) on a large Niffis Shell for your base. The three pipes are made from the Femur Bone of a Lich Lord, a Tibia from a Virulent Grievver, and a Tusk from a Tusker Slave. Mount your finished Horn on a floor hook and amaze your friends!

Suggested Hunting Grounds:
Sephal Niffis - Northern Shores
Lich Lord - Serac Vault
Tusker Slave - BSD
Virulent Grievver - N Shores or Bael'Zharon's Lair!
