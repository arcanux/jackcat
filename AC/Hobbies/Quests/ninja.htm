Master Liao Oazaka tells you, "It has been told to me that the Tanada clan of the Nanjou Shou-jen have come through the portals from Ispar to Dereth! I bear a great grudge against them - my clan on Ispar was slain by them, leaving me with only a sister left alive. I desire revenge against them."
Master Liao Oazaka tells you, "I understand they hone their skills in the Egg Orchard, the Dark Design, the Path of the Blind, the Feeding Tubes, and the Forgotten Chasm now. Seek them there and slay them for me. Each bears upon themselves a golden shuriken bestowed upon them by their clan leader. If you give me sixteen of these shuriken to prove that you have slain many of their number, I shall give you a replica of the gear that the Nanjou Shou-jen wear. I understand that this appearance may be of interest to some."
