 The original Siraluun Headdress and Dress were introduced during the Dark Majesty expansion. This simple quest required you to hunt Feathers from the Kithless Siraluun (Level 88) found on the island of Marae Lassel.

During The Calm event, new versions of the dress were introduced. Now feathers may be collected from any of the Siraluun!

Town Crier tells you, "Yesterday, my daughter and I were walking through the marketplace when she saw what she claimed to be the most beautiful dress ever. Now I have to figure out how to make it for her... kids... sheesh!"

One feather will create a headdress. Five feathers collected in a special bag will create a dress. The lower level feathers may be turned in to Aun Arenura in Bluespire. Kithless, Badlands, and Untamed Siralunn Feathers go to Timaru!

NPC: Aun Arenura - 39.2N, 75.0W
NPC: Aun Whethura - 43.9N, 78.1W

Aun Arenura the Crafter tells you, "Greetings! My father, Aun Whethura in Timaru has tasked me with helping the younger members of the xuta, Ispar. He says that we can both learn much from each other."
Aun Arenura the Crafter tells you, "My father's fondness for the Siraluun has also rubbed off onto me. Should you find any trophies, such as Siraluun Feathers or Claws, I will gladly take them and reward you with a weapon."
Aun Arenura the Crafter tells you, "Also, I am learning the art of tailoring as well. If you should bring me this bag..."
Aun Arenura the Crafter gives you Embroidered Bag.
Aun Arenura the Crafter tells you, "...full of feathers, I will craft you a gown of feathers. I should warn you though. I am not skilled enough, nor am I worthy of working with the feathers of the more powerful Siraluun. You should bring those feathers to my father in Timaru."
Aun Arenura the Crafter tells you, "One final note, unlike my father and the others who reside within the walls of Timaru, I bear no grudges to those who may have acted against the Tonk. My father would say that I am foolish for this belief."

Bluespire can be reached from a portal at 21.7S, 1.3W in the Social Center of Yaraq.

Aun Whethura the Crafter tells you, "How I miss the birds of my home world! My brother Ihipura once taught me to invoke their animas for aid. If you bring me a small bundle of feathers from the Kithless Siraluun that roam the island, I will ask Siraluun to help me make a special headdress for you. Even better, if you combine five of the smaller bundles into a large one, I will make a lovely dress. Here, add the bundles to this special bag, it will keep them from getting rumpled.
Aun Whethura the Crafter gives you Embroidered Bag.

Timaru is located atop a mesa on the Marescent Plateau of Marae Lassel. To reach it head to Bluespire from Yaraq at 21.7S, 1.3W, then run to the Marescent Plateau portal at 41.9N, 77.6W (restricted to levels 35+).

The drop rate on the feathers is extremely rare, and the birds themselves are feisty and difficult to kill! But it will all be worth it when you return to the crafters to collect your reward. Hand in one feather for the Headdress or a bag with five feathers for the stunning Siraluun Dress! You will also receive an experience reward (see the Marae Lassel Crafters & Collectors page for amounts).

You give Aun Arenura the Crafter Small Bundle of Marsh Siraluun Feathers.
Aun Arenura the Crafter tells you, "Ah...Siraluun feathers. Wait just a moment, and I will make something special for you."
Aun Arenura mumurs a prayer to the Siraluun.
Aun Arenura the Crafter gives you Marsh Siraluun Headdress.
You've earned 1,000 experience.

The dress, the headdress and the embroidered bag may be given.

Visit the Shortcuts page for a list of portals to the island of Marae Lassel.

Note: During the Repercussions event, the spells on the original Kithless Siraluun Headdress changed to reflect the removal of the appraisal skills and the introduction of Tinkering.
