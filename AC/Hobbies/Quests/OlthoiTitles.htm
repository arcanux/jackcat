 From Dark Majesty.

The Olthoi Pincer quests are a great way to boost your xp and to stock up on treated healing kits. Begin by speaking with Behdo Yii, a researcher in Redspire on Marae Lasse. While exploring an Abaondoned Tumerok Outpost, Behdo Yii of Redspire was chased away by Olthoi. Help him rid the area of Olthoi and he will reward you for your efforts! As part of your reward, you will also receive a Token which can be exchanged for a new title.

You can also purchase a rumor for this quest from Micon Stuvis in Redspire (introduced during Pillars Made of Sand).

Rumor: Behdo Yii

There are five Olthoi dungeons in all, each of increasing difficulty. One particular Olthoi in each of the dungeons drops the pincer you need as proof for Behdo Yii. The Pincers are attuned and bonded and may not be given. There is a three week timer on each of pincers.

To reach Behdo Yii on Marae Lassel, take one of the three Respire portals. You'll find him at 40.8N, 83.3W.

Redspire Portal - 42.5N, 31.1E (Holtburg)
Redspire Portal - 3.5S, 22.4E (Samsur)
Redspire Portal - 15.9S, 46.7E (Yanshi)

Behdo Yii says, "I can't believe I have this wonderful tumerok site to explore, and I am forced to loiter around here due to these pesky Olthoi. If you head up to forty-three point eight degrees north, and eighty-three point four degrees west, you can help me with this problem!"

Behdo Yii tells you, "Hello there stranger! I am wondering if you can help me in a bit of a predicament I find myself in. I was busy exploring a wonderfully well-preserved abandoned Tumerok outpost, when I was beset upon by a number of Olthoi!"
Behdo Yii tells you, "I had never seen these fearsome creatures that close before, and the fact that I escaped with my life leads me to believe that these Olthoi aren't quite so tough as their mainland counterparts. If you could journey to this tumerok outpost an rid it of these troublesome beasts I would be most grateful."
Behdo Yii tells you, "If you could bring me back some proof that you have conquered them, such as a pincer, I would reward you well. The portal into the dungeon is straight north of here, the Abandoned Tumerok Site."

The five dungeons for this quest are Olthoi only. Buff and bane with Piercing and Acid Protection.

Abandoned Tumerok Site - 42.0N, 82.2W
Map: Abandoned Tumerok Site
Levels: No restrictions
Olthoi: Olthoi Harvester (Level 15)
Reward: 5,000xp, 5 Treated Healing Kits, C Note, Harvester Token
Title: Bugstomper

Shortcut: Dungeon is due North of Redspire. Reach Redspire from any of the following portals:
Redspire Portal - 42.5N, 31.1E (Holtburg)
Redspire Portal - 3.5S, 22.4E (Samsur)
Redspire Portal - 15.9S, 46.7E (Yanshi)

Hug the left wall all the way down to the final room. The Olthoi which drop the pincer is at the bottom of the wide ramp. Once you have it, return to Behdo Yii for your reward, and instructions for the next pincer dungeon!

Behdo Yii tells you, "Ahh many thanks! You are a true hero! Here is a reward for you."
You're earned 5000 experience.
Behdo Yii gives you Treated Trade Note.
Behdo Yii gives you Treated Healing Kit.

Behdo Yii gives you Treated Healing Kit.
Behdo Yii tells you, "And in recognition of your skill, I bestow you with this token, please return it to me if you wish for me to change your title to something appropriate of your valor."
 Behdo Yii gives you a Harvester Token.

Behdo Yii tells you, "Finally, I am glad that you saw fit to help me with this little matter, but I now have a new problem. It seems that while I was busying occupying myself with this Tumerok archaeological site, a whole new host of bugs have taken up my previous exploration site, a dungeon on the other side of the island, named the Dark Lair. I tried to show those bugs what's what, but I'm afraid I wasn't able to make much of a dent in their numbers. If you could go there and teach them a lesson or two, I would be very appreciative."

Olthoi Soldier's Nest - 45.2N, 76.3W
Map: Olthoi Soldier Nest
Levels: 30+ (Plateau is 35+)
Olthoi: Olthoi Soldier (Level 61)
Reward: 100,000xp, 5 Treated Healing Kits, D Note, Bracelet of Piercing Protection, Soldier Token
Title: Soldier Slaughterer

Shortcut: Dungeon is on the Plateau, just east of Timaru. From Redspire, run to the Marescent Plateau Portal at 41.9N, 77.6W

Be careful as you approach the Othoi Soldier's Nest. The entrance is often surrounded by tumeroks and lightning elementals. The dungeon is a little more dangerous as well! You'll have to cross deadly acid pits to reach the Olthoi Soldier which drops the pincer. From the entrance stay left until you reach a room with two ramps down. Take the west leading ramp down to the next level. Next you'll come to a surface portal next to an acid pit. You must run across the pit and take the passage to the northwest. The room you enter will have a reddish hue. Continue up the wide ramp and through the corridor until you reach a larger room with gas vents and a blue hue. The Olthoi Soldier is in this room. If you reach the 2nd acid pit, you've gene too far.

Alternatively, from the entrance you can take the north ramp instead of the west ramp. Keep to the left until you reach the open room with a vent. On the opposite side of the room is a short ramp up to an acid pit. If you stand at the edge of the pit, a Soldier will come attack you. Once you attack back, the others will join him. The pincer soldier should come across the pit with the others.

You give Behdo Yii A Soldier Pincer.
Behdo Yii tells you, "I am most impressed! You have accomplished much in a short time, traveler."
You've earned 100000 experience.
Behdo Yii gives you Trade Note.
Behdo Yii gives you Treated Healing Kit.
Behdo Yii gives you Treated Healing Kit.
Behdo Yii gives you Treated Healing Kit.
Behdo Yii gives you Treated Healing Kit.
Behdo Yii gives you Treated Healing Kit.

Behdo Yii tells you, "You might find that bracelet useful, I dare say. And you deserve this token, please return it to me if you wish for me to change your title to something appropriate of your valor."
Behdo Yii gives you A Soldier Token.

Behdo Yii tells you, "I am somewhat embarrassed to bring it up, but it seems as though the townspeople have thought of me as the renowned Olthoi Slayer. I have tried to explain to them that you are the one doing all the killing, but they don't seem to understand that. Now they expect me to go kill off a nest of Olthoi supersoldiers! We call them Olthoi Legionaries, and they are quite terrifying, from what I am given to understand. Well, not so terrifying to one such as you, eh? They have taken over an ancient Empyrean grotto, kill them, return a pincer as proof to me, and I shall shower you with riches."

Note: Bracelet is bonded and cannot be given.

Ancient Empyrean Grotto - 52.7N, 73.1W
Map: Ancient Empyrean Grotto
Levels: 40+
Olthoi: Olthoi Legionary (Level 70)
Reward: 500,000xp, 5 Treated Healing Kits, DCCL Trade Note (75K), 5 Gems of Purity, A Legionary Token
Title: Exterminator

Shortcut: Dungeon is located on the Plateau. From Redspire, run to the Marescent Plateau Portal at 41.9N, 77.6W

From the entrance, keep to your left until you reach a small down ramp. At the bottom of the ramp you'll come to a split. Take the right path heading east. At the the next intersection, turn left and keep to the left all the way until the final room. After retrieving the pincer from the Legionary, return to Behdo Yii for your reward!

Thanks to godor of FF (Magemerlin), Vangar of FF, Mufasa Al-Jibar of SC, Juliett of MT

Lair of the Eviscerators - 53.7N, 76.6W
Map: Lair of the Eviscerators
Levels: 50+
Olthoi: Olthoi Eviscerator (Level 85)
Reward: 3,000,000xp, M Note, 5 Treated Healing Kits, 5 Gems of Stillness, An Eviscerator Token
Title: Bug Butcher

Shortcut: Dungeon is located on the Plateau. From Redspire, run to the Marescent Plateau Portal at 41.9N, 77.6W

From the entrance keep to the left until you've gone down your 2nd ramp. Go through the door, then keep to the right until you're heading west down a ramp. From here you'll be hugging the left wall again all the way to the final room. The pincer is dropped by the Eviscerator that spawns in the SE corner of the final room. Return for the final time to Behdo Yii in Respire for your reward!
