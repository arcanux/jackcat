Runed chests first appeared during the Iron Coast event. Reports filtered in of chests spawning on the landscape filled with fabulous items and guarded by various creature spawns.

Some chests are unlocked, while others have varying  difficulties of resistance (from 100-400). To open the locked chests the lockpick skill is required. The higher the difficulty the chest, the better the loot within. The highest contains loot that rivals SIK (Sturdy Iron Key) chests!

Note: During The Hall of the Tusker King, more chests appeared, some in static locations. The timer on the randomly spawning chests was also reduced!

The runed chests which are static spawns have unique 7 day timers. Each category of randomly spawning runed chest is on a 24 hour timer (there are 7 categories total according to srand). For example, if you've unlocked a runed chest with 300 resistance, you cannot use another 300 runed chest for 24 hours. You can, however, open a 200 resistance chest within the same 24 hours.

Use the tables to the right for locations of runed chests in static locations! If you have locations I'm missing, please send me an email!
