From the Children of the Prodigal Lord event.

Town Crier tells you, "There is word that a Tumerok named Aun Teriona on Marae Lassel seeks the livers of infected Carenzi."

HMac furious of FF reports: "Hey there, This is regarding the new "Carenzi Plague Quest." To start talk to Aun Teriona at 41.3N, 75.3W near Bluespire. According to your level, he will tell you which Carenzi you should go kill. Once you start killing them, eventually some will drop livers right on the ground. You must pick one up and a timer starts from that point. You now have 2 hours to kill the amount of Carenzi needed to stop plague in area (you must keep picking up the livers, not just kill the Carenzi). Eevery time a liver is picked up, it will tell you your progress, like "you have killed over half the number you need to kill" or something like that. Nnow I would believe that any Carenzis drop livers. The highest ones according to the Tumerok are tainted Carenzi, but I would believe Ravagers are higher in level (all found on Caul). Once you kill enough, you will get xp right away without talking to Tumi again. I got 24 million xp for killing the third highest carenzi (I'm lvl 233). I'm sure the other ones are more xp. On a last note, when you do talk to Tumi again, he gives you a writ of refuge no matter which Carenzis you finished killing. Hope this helps, luv the site."
