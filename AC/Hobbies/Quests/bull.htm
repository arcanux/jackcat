From the Throne of Destiny Expansion.

After completing the Knights of Karlun quest, you can return to the Grand Knight of Sanamar for a third task.

NPC: Grand Knight - 70.0N, 62.5W (Sanamar)

Grand Knight tells you, "Well, it seems you may be worthy of royal service after all. Looking for more work, are you? There is one sensitive mission which you could undertake for us..."
Grand Knight tells you, "The Red Bull of Sanamar, which you see at the center of town, has been sick for some time. We don't know how to cure it! The only man who might is the bull's old caretaker, a man named Lascare. Well, he left town some time ago. All we know is that he lives off by himself on a lonely hilltop on the island to the northeast. Seek him out, please, and figure out a way to cure the bull!"

As the Grand Knight instructs, seek out Old Lascare, the Red Bull's former Livestock Handler to see if he can help cure the ailing bull.

Old Lascare 87.9N, 55.4W

Old Lascare says, "Enough blood on my hands already..."
Old Lascare tells you, "Oh, so the old Grand Knight sent you here, eh? I figured that brute would need my help eventually. That bull has an ugly secret - so ugly I dare not speak it, ha ha! The answer to your problems lies in the Cannibal Caverns, 88.0N, 58.5W. You can figure the rest out if you read my journal."

The Journal is lying on the floor inside Old Lascare's cottage. Reading it reveals a shocking secret! The Red Bull dines on the hearts of the Fiun. Yuck!

Text: Old Lascare's Journal

Old Lascare tells you, "What more do you want? I told you where to go. Read my journal for the rest of the story. I have literally washed my hands of this whole ugly business."

Make your way to the dungeon as directed and fight your way to the bottom to retrieve one of the Fiun's organs for the Bull's health.

Cannibal Caverns - 88.0N, 58.5W
--Levels: 50+
--Map: Cannibal Caverns
--Creatures: Demented Fuin, Deranged Fiun, Engorged Eater, Atavistic Fiun

From the entrance, go through the first room and down the south hall. At the next large room, go through and again and keep south. Follow the dungeon south until the texture changes, then keep straight until you dead end. Go east and head down and you'll find a journal page on the ground.

Note: Page from a Journal

When you reach the reddish glowing area of the dungeon, keep left until you find the Atavistic Fiuns. Kill them until you find a Maddened Fiun Heart. Return to the Grand Knight in Sanamar for your reward!

You give Grand Knight Maddened Fiun Heart.
Grand Knight tells you, "Ah, this... heart is the key to the bull's health? Very well. This gives me much to think on. I thank you for your service. You are not the first to bloody your hands in the King's service."
Grand Knight gives you Hero of Sanamar Token.
Grand Knight gives you Medallion of the Red Bull.
You've earned 600,000 experience.

You give Grand Knight Hero of Sanamar Token.
Grand Knight tells you, "Your dedication to our King is commendable, and greatly to be praised. I will make it known that you have served us well."
Grand Knight tells you, "You have done much for the glory of Sanamar. The King of Viamont salutes you."
