From the Down Twisting Paths event.

Details from Tlosk of HG:

1. Creepy Chambers dungeon, 1+, 80.0S 60.0W, northeast of Candeth Keep
(south of Obsidian Plains).

2. From drop, take second hall on the right and go south a long ways until
you come to a ramp leading north down into a large room. Stick left and you
will come to a large room with a lever in the middle that opens a door in a
side area on the north side. From there only one way to go.

3. Near the exit portal is an Amoredillo Key on the floor. There are five
chests (one in each of the large rooms), but only the one in the western,
octagonal room contains the Spinning Staff of Death. The chest is marked
"Quiet waterside contemplation, mother and pup together..."

4. Keep the staff if you like it, otherwise you can turn it in to Xanda Har
in Wei Jou for an xp reward (she does not return the staff). The reward is
20% of your next level but is capped at 3.5 million. The staff is currently
givable and the key is on a 6 day timer.

Town Crier tells you, "I was chatting with Xandra Har of Wai Jhou yesterday.
She's convinced that some bandits in the southern Direlands are developing
a new super-weapon. She's looking for people to retrieve it for her."

Xanda Har tells you, "Hello! Say, I don't suppose you could help me out with
a small favor? I've heard that there's this group of bandits out in the
wilderness, a bit south of the Obsidian Rim. Rumor has it that they're
stockpiling treasure and ... well, doing something strange with creatures.
Either breeding them or worshipping them -- no one is quite sure which."
Xanda Har tells you, "But it's the treasure that I'm interested in! And my
curiosity is just killing me! I even heard that they might have some sort of
super weapon!"
Xanda Har tells you, "Will you go and see what you can find out for me? I
think their lair is called the Creepy Chambers. The barkeeper here sells a
rumor with better directions than I can give you. And if you can find that
super-weapon, bring it back to me! I'll reward you!"

The Bandits of the Creepy Chambers (Rumor from Barkeep Goku Bai-Akane in Wei
Jou)
Bandits have established a base south of the Obsidian Rim, near 80S 60W, in
an old dungeon called the Creepy Chambers. Supposedly they are stockpiling
treasure there to finance some nefarious deeds. No one knows quite what they
are up to, but it seems to involve the creatures of Dereth, both native and
imported.
Xanda Har of Wai Jhou is very interested in learning more about these
bandits.

You give Xanda Har Spinning Staff of Death.
Xanda Har tells you, "What's this? You found this in the Creepy Chambers,
eh? Wait ... *this* is the bandit's super-weapon? Well shoot! No, no, it's
fine... it's just -- I'm not a staff warrior. I was hoping for a sword."
Xandar Har tells you, "Well, I did say I'd reward you. Thanks anyway, I
guess."
You've earned 543,291 experience. (Level 61)
You've earned 3,500,000 experience. (Level 244)

 

Spinning Staff of Death
The core of this staff is made of bone and the outside is plated with the skin of a Titanium Armoredillo. Despite its weight, it feels light -- as if it seeks to whirl away on its own, slashing the very air to ribbons.

Armoredillo Key
The handle of this key is fashioned in the shape of a spining armoredillo. This key was found in the Creepy Chambers and presumably unlocks a chest there. Scratched on the body of the key are the fragmentary works: "...careful choice ... only the Armoredillo chest contains ..."
