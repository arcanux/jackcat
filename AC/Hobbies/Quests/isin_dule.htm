Quest Details: Shield of Isin Dule
Rumor: Shadow Spire on the Caul
Text: Isin Dule's Orders
Timer: 13 days

Sidd's mage of SC reports:
"First you must kill multiple spawns on Caul below the spire. A portal will then open. You must go into the portal, and run up the stairs, kill the Captain, he opens another portal, go in. At the top is the Shadow boss, Shadow Cynn, I believe. He is really really hard, for he casts a Black madness spell that decreases all skills by 30%. The boss monster is Red, but you do not have to be PK to cast on him, so do not be fooled. Melees are advised to move away when he casts since he casts shadowy vortex which drains 180 life a second. As you begin to fight, re-spawns of the monsters below will spawn up top around the Boss. But once he dies, they all die. The coords are 92S, 92.9W for the spire. You must kill 4 or 5 spawns for the portal. The shield is buffable, droppable, and pure ownage!"

Hello Maggie,
My name's Grin, of Thistledown, and I'm here to report some good news. There's a new quest. But not just any new quest. This one will make your skin crawl with excitement and your toes curl with joy!! I'm talking about the quest located at the new Shadow Spire on Singularity Caul Island at about 91.9s, 92.6w.

We first arrived and noticed a red dot at the top of the tower. At first I thought maybe it was Bael Zharon incarnate? It's called Shadow Cyst and we couldn't ID it from the ground. The bottom of the tower is swarmed with Shadow Wraiths, Twisted Shadows, Marguls of all sorts, Panumbral Soldiers (the meanest of the bunch), among other mean creatures on that island. Eventually 3 Liutenant Shadows spawned. They appeared to be of high ranking in the shadow community. They all wielded a mighty shield and shadow sword. I think one of their names was Liutenant Shenran? Doesn't matter.. After they were dead, a portal summoned at the base of the spire.

The battle raged in the spire before I made it in there, but this was no easy fight, to be certain. Some of our companions died for our great cause and curious minds. Good people as well. Eventually after walking to the top of a very tall spiralling bone staircase, we fought with a Captain of the shadow forces. After he was defeated another portal was summoned. This time to the very top where the Shadow Cyst and his minions resided.

The Shadow Cyst had around 60000 hit points and was definitely NOT messing around. He casted a spell known as Black Madness that reduces all skills by 30%!! The fight raged on for a good while, but in the end, Fist-I got the kill with the combined efforts of Vizmir, Abdul al-Terran, and myself. The Shadow Cyst dropped about 8 or so? lumps on a 13 day timer used to open the chest right at the edge near the battle with the Cyst, overlooking the ground below the Spire. Inside was the Shield of Isin Dule and a fleshy lumpy thing, which I haven't taken to the translator yet, but I'm definitely in for a good read I think. =) Best of luck, adventurers. And be sure to bring back up!! (Quest is level 140+)
