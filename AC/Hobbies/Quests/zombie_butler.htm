From Dance of the Dead event.

The following notes are provided as a courtesy until I can research a full write-up.

Nastazio of TD reports: "This new patch brings Zombie Butlers from housing! How do you make one? It's quite easy! You go to lower levels areas, such as right outside hub, and run around till you find gravestones. You then double click them to make the "old Zombies" spawn. You kill, and loot body parts. You'll need 2 legs, 2 arms a Torso, and a head. Once you have all of these body parts, take your butt to Glenden Wood and hand them to Ulgrim in the tavern."

Thanks also to Magicpipe II of TD!

Here are several pics related to the Zombie Butler quest

Look for the old gravestones that randomly spawn. Click on it to spawn around 10 zombies (there are different level of zombies for different geographic regions). Kill the zombies and loot the fresh body parts (you need to get a Fresh Zombie Head, 2 Fresh Zombie Arms, 2 fresh Zombie Legs, and a Fresh Zombie Torso). Take them to Ungrim the Unpleasant Smelling in Glendon Wood. When you give him all the parts he will give you a Zombie Butler. Then go find a Large Mnemosynes and apply it to the butler to animate him.

A Simple Man/HG
