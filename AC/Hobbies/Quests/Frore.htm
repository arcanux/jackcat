 Frore Quest presented by special request

To begin the Frore quest you must collect three transcripts from the Standing Stones.

The first standing stone is locked. You need a Lich Seal -- a medallion with a skull imprint stating "use on a locked standing stone to unlock it"-- to open it. Seals can be gotten from Lich Lords or Revenants. You may have to kill a lot of Liches before you find a seal.

The standing stone is located east of the Bandit Castle (66.1N x 51.4E) near the peak of the large mountain. Stand in the gate of the Bandit Castle facing the mountains. Climb the steep cliff you see. You will find the stone atop the peak, where you have the best view of the prison.

Only the first Stone is locked. The second stone containing the second part of the transcript is located WSW of Nydisa Castle (69.2N x 15.3E), again near the peak of a mountain. Look for the low cliffs due west and climb them. Look again for cliffs and for an area that seems to have only one way up.

The last transcript is near Stonehold (72.0N x 22.0W). Head north of the Rat Tower to the peak of the closest mountain. The standing stone is atop, in a thick wooded area.

*IMPORTANT*
Combine the three pieces into one, using them on each other as you would in alchemy or fletching.

Take the newly combined transcript to the translator in Zaikhal. In return, the Translator will give you the Gelidite Transcript.

Take the translated Gelidite transcript to the lady at Plateau Village (44.2N x 43.3W). In exchange, she will give you a key to the chest at the end of Frore and will open a portal to Frore. (This portal cannot be summoned, but you can tie to it.) You will arrive at the top of a mountain peak, with sheer drops off all sides. You will take considerable damage on the jump down, so make sure your jump skill is decent. You must be level 20+ to enter.

Once in, be careful not to die, because you will be unable to get back inside.

You will need at least 2 people to open a set of doors on the way in, and you will need lockpick to open the remainder of the doors inside.

Frore is cold! You will take cold damage continually, so buff your cold protection, wear your matty coat and bring a healer or heal kits with you. The first part of Frore is like a maze that loops. Take lefts inside the cavern, and keep making lefts.

You will encounter: ice golems (right at the entrance),  undead, zombie-like Initiates, Mountain Rats, Gelidite Lords who cast high level magic, and more ice golems.

During the Sudden Season, Frore contained two level 80+ undead, and the Great Work, which has since been destroyed. Tremblant's Ivory Staff is also no longer available.

The chest is in the small building on the right wall, furtherst down at the end of Frore. The Ice Heaume, Hammer, Robe all spawn in the chest. The chest is unpickable and must be opened with the key (1 use). The Robe is a no-drop item, so make sure the right person uses the key on the chest. (The Robe, however, can be lost at death.)

Tip: To open the door  at the entrance of Frore, you will need to pick the lock of the first door (have a friend standing by to open it for you.)  Take the first portal and run over to the switches. When you've hit all three switches, run back, get through the door and you will see the last gate, and the portal to Frore Proper.

Tip: Make sure you have "Let other players give you items" checked in your Options panel during quests! They often involve exchanges.
