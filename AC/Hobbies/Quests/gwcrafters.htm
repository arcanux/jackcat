Glenden Wood - 29.6N, 27.2E
Copper Vanurp - Food Designer
Tiffany Comfore - Alchemical Artist
Wils Morrem - Artist in Wood and Feathers

Wils Morrem tells you, "The whistling wind, the wood, the biting metal ... these are the atoms of existence."
Wils Morrem tells you, "If you insist on putting motivations to our being, talk to Ms. Comfore. She's the political one."

Tiffany Comfore tells you, "Welcome! Here you see the beginnings -- the fragile embryo -- of the future art community of Dereth."
Tiffany Comfore tells you, "We are here, my two fine friends and I, to prepare for peace! After all the recent hubaloo, we believe that Dereth is in for a long and glorious Golden Age of Art, in which all Derethians everywhere will forsake their violent ways and instead take up the gentlest and most noble of sciences: alchemy, cooking, and fletching."
Tiffany Comfore tells you, "We are at the forefront of this artistic revolution, leading the way, dismantling the roadblocks, smoothing the path for the mass of humanity behind us! We live to serve!"
Tiffany Comfore tells you, "We're gathering converts every day, every minute! If you would like to join us, you can start by trying your hand at crafting. Just take an old, disused item that is of no worth to anyone -- for instance, gromnie teeth! -- and fashion it into a beautiful and useful object of art."
Tiffany Comfore tells you, "Then bring it to one of us, and we will reward your efforts."

Copper Vanurp tells you, "Food. Food is the basis of life. We are all food. You, you I see as a bountiful dessert, layered with chocolate and cinnamon, encrusted with rare fruits and sweetmeats in arcane patterns. You are a work of art! But cloyingly sweet ..."
Copper Vanurp tells you, "Our purpose here? We are here to ennoble the Derethian soul! To encourage it to rise to its full potential, like a fine, full loaf of the best bread in the oven of the gods!"
Copper Vanurp tells you, "Tiffany is our fearless leader, the great organizer. She can tell you of our efforts here."

Grind Gromnie Teeth with mortar and pestle for paste.

You pulp the tooth into paste.
You give Tiffany Comfore Ash Gromnie Tooth Paste.
You've earned 32291 experience in your Alchemy skill.

I have alchemy trained on the char I turned it in with, only at 150 base though. Hope this helps! --Tokosimi, WE

Ivory Toothpick - 5,000xp to your fletching skill.
Jade Toothpick - 30,000xp to fletching
Swamp Toothpick - 100,000xp to fletching
Ash Toothpick - 25% of xp needed to raise fletching skill 1 point

Using Carving Knife:
Ivory Toothbrush - 5,000xp to your cooking skill.
Jade Toothbrush - 30,000xp to cooking
Ash Toothbrush - 25% of xp needed to raise cooking skill 1 point
Swamp Toothbrush - 100,000xp to cooking

Using Mortar & Pestle:
Ivory Toothpaste - 5,000xp to your alchemy skill.
Jade Toothpaste - 30,000xp to alchemy
Ash Toothpaste - 25% of xp needed to raise alchemy skill 1 point
Swamp Toothpaste - 100,000xp to alchemy

1 week timer
