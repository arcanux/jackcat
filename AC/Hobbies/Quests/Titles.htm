 Tired of being an "Adventurer"?! Travel to one of the three Bestower Guild Outposts and solve a puzzle to get a new title. Representatives from the Guild of Bestowers can be found at the following locations:

17.0N, 63.3E - Eastham
53.9S, 74.4E - Lin
73.9S, 19.1E - Qalaba'r

To solve the puzzle, you'll need to combine a Puzzle Base Piece with a Skill Puzzle piece that can be purchased from one of the Representatives.

Bestower Representative Nerezi bin Risid tells you, "I sell instructions and puzzles pieces to applicants here at the Guild of Bestowers.

Read more in the Handbook of the Bestowers' Guild.

Choose your title wisely! You can only have a title once, so if you change in the future, you can never go back to your initial choice.

The Puzzle Base Piece spawns on the ground inside the tent. Purchase your Skill Puzzle piece of choice and combine. If your skill is high enough (approx. 250+), you will get the following message: 

Like most skill checks, there's a chance of failure even with a very high skill and a chance at success even with a lower skill. People have succeeded in the 170s and failed in the high 200s. If you fail, the timer is 24 hours before you can try again.

Give your solved puzzle to the Examiner for your new title! 

Advanced titles became available during the Lost in the New Horizon event. A much higher skill is needed to achieve the higher designation. Recommended skill level 300+.

If your Chef, Alchemist of Fletcher achieves success, you will also get a nifty new hat!
