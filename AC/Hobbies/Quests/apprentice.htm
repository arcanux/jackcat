 From Pillars Made of Sand. Additional apprentices were added during From the Darkness Born event.

Thanks to Vellus of MT, Mage of Light - LC, MHulan de Shampoo of WE!

New Apprentices give rewards for crafted items. Each reward is on a separate 1 week timer.

Apprentice Fletcher tells you, "Hello! My master in Glenden Wood, Wils Morrem, has sent me out into the world to participate in the great Mission - encouraging the young people of Dereth to embrace a life of artistry!"
Apprentice Fletcher tells you, "If you bring me an example of your craftsmanship - say, a lightning arrowhead - I will reward you with a token of encouragement."
The apprentice coughs quietly.
Apprentice Fletcher tells you, "Um, we're not rich yet, though, so I'm afraid I can only reward you once a week."

You give Apprentice Fletcher Bundle of Lightning Arrowheads.
Apprentice Fletcher tells you, "Excellent! Excellent! Look at that workmanship! Good job!"
Apprentice Fletcher gives you Trade Note (1,000).
You've earned 5,000 experience in your Fletching skill.
