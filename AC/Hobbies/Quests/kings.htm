From Phades of DT:

Ok. We went there on DT with about 6 people... hah. Reeshan laughed at us.

Every weapon type does hardly any damage. You'll need burun slayers...which still do garbage damage, but double the damage of non burun slayers.

So everyone do the Burun slayer quest or get double the people to make up for it.

6 of us were like 4 guys attempting to kill a tremendous monouga when they first came out...

Go to BM5. Drop down and talk to the Watcher of the dead(looks like a ghost).

When you drop down, jump down to the lowest lvl. Take passage on the EAST wall that leads up.

Take the first Left that you can heading West. Button on the wall to first flood room. Big Heads at SW.. Farthest SW big head has a pressure switch under it. Step on the plate and go through the door that opens just next to the head on the SW wall corner.

Next flood room, make your way to the SW corner again. Button on wall will open the next door.

Run down the hallway till you get to a split. Make a left at the button (push it to open passage way). Keep heading left till you get to the next flood room.

In the flood room on the EAST side is a doorway with a pressure switch in front of it. Open up that passage and pull the lever, run out the room and head left to the SOUTH opened passage. Follow upwards making lefts and till you get to a button and door just before the Gateway. Use the gateway to get to next section.

Next section has a test. 3 bags and a directions sign. Answer changes everytime. To get through, talk to the directions and then click on the left bag to get a marble. From left to right put your marbles in the bags: Mixed, Black, White.

If you didn't get the passing message, then talk to directions and do it over and over till you do. May take a few tries. when you get approved, use the exit gateway to get to the next section. (You only ever have to do this once. If you die and come back, you can just run to the exit portal.)

Last section is a HUGE MAZE. But.. really easy. Lots of dead ends but if you want to go through the fastest and easy way then... Go left, then left... then left.. then left... etc... From Deven-Kenyon of SC:

Hey there maggie!

Well, here it is, a lil walkthrough to help peeps along on this new quest. I'll post it on my blackmire site as well with pics

King Reeshan walkthrough. Thanks to Rare-to, Guil ty, and Counterspell for their imput and help.

Note: Bring at least a full fellow (made up of mostly archers and mages, although melee's are needed for killing burun through the doors, they have little impact on Reeshan himself at the end.)

This quest starts at the Temple of Izji Qo (Blackmire 5 temple located at 12.7S 58.8W) (You can either use the Izji Qo hooked portal you got on Blackmire 3, or you can run from Fort Tethana). The temple statue is still down inside, but there's now a new inhabitant, a Watcher of the Dead. Instead of using the statue, talk to the Watcher.

After you speak to the watcher of the dead...

Watcher of the Dead tells you, "My purpose is plain, I watch the dead that reside within the halls of this temple. I have stood this vigil since my sacrifice over thirty thousand years ago, when the world cooled and the darkness made war upon all. The weight of time has long crushed my bones to dust and left with me with this incorporeal form. Yet, I serve my purpose with the same vigor that I did in life."

Watcher of the Dead tells you, "A threat, not of this world grows within the belly of this temple. They have come from across the stars, across the divide between our worlds with one thought in their ravenous minds; destruction."

Watcher of the Dead tells you, "Their pilgrimage is for naught, there are no sisters left upon this world who draw breath, there are none who recall the glory of Golden Shores and the valley wherein Ualannan once held vigil watching over all our sisters. All is dust, but this will not deter these beasts from another world."

Watcher of the Dead tells you, "They come and they shall spread their rot to the core of this world and there it shall grow unfettered until all is consumed and destroyed. You must destroy the beasts within the deepest halls of the Izji Qo's temple. They have found passage within and slaughtered or consumed all souls that have stood against their trespass."

...you'll portal in and have a small drop to a platform below. face east and jump down. take the east ramp up to the first door. there's a pressure plate on the floor as well as a button, so be prepared. hit the button, head in door and go north then head west then north again. go west to another door where there's a big room of burun, fight through the door. The button is located father back on north wall. in this room you'll see two statue heads on the south wall, face them, then turn west. You'll want to take the door to your immediate left past the second head. There are two pressure plates under the heads.

The pressure plate for the South door is under the cloesest head to that South door, pressure plate for N wall door is under the East statue. Hit the plate under the head for the south door and go in. If anyone gets left inside, there's a button on inside wall to open same door. Now follow hallway to the T, take the west passage at the T. The button for this door is on the north wall. kill through the door

Once inside, you'll be in your second large room. there's one head in the n/w corner wall facing you. run south/west to the door on the south wall. follow the hallway to the door. There's a pressure plate towards north end of hall facing door on south wall. There's also a button on other side to reopen for friends.

Follow hall again north to the door in east hallway. There's a button and pressure plate. Follow around and up heading south to the lever room. The lever is in a room on west wall, it opens a door on south wall. if you're with a fellowship, kill the burun then go inside, if you're trying to make a solo run, you may have a problem, but it's doable. Circle the burun around so they don't all clog the doors, then head to west lever, there's a pressure plate right outside the door that'll open it. You’ll probably get stuck here killing lots of burun through the door. Kill them first, hit the lever, then get out of the

Room and head to the south door. Make your first right after door, go right again after that. Head east and up two ramps. You’ll be in the portal device room. Use the device

A violent hum sounds in your head and you are drawn unceremoniously into portal space.

This next area is the marble puzzle room. Talk to the sign in east alcove, you'll be handed three containers with marbles in them, one is black, one's white and the third is mixed. go to the three bags in the west alcove, use the bag with the black and white marbles on it. Now, depending on what marble you pull from the bag, hand it the same color. the two remaining go to the white and black marble bags. Note: black cannot go in black, and white cannot go in white. Once you receive a message you've completed it, you may now access the exit portal to the north. Also, once you complete the marble puzzle the first time, you do not need to re-due it again and you can either rebuff at the marbles or take the exit, the drop is safe

This next area is tricky. Head west into the large room and take the door on the south wall in that room and follow the hall and take the second right heading south. go east to wall and make a left south and then immediately east (another left). Take your next immediate left heading east. Follow that hall, passing rooms and over a ramp similar to g man where you can see through the floor heading through more halls and small rooms. You cannot get lost here. You'll head over another ramp and take your first immediate right after coming out of that hall. Keep north then turn right (east). Keep heading east to wall, take left (north) and go down ramp. You’ll be in an open room, head south and run all the way south to and up a ramp. Take a left and follow west. Take another left then an immediate right, then left again and head down the ramp west into another room. The portal device is in a room on the west wall. do not use it until everyone is ready. The drop is HOT. You'll be facing the same burun but an added bonus, King Reeshan himself! He's a cutie :D And he's very hungry. If you get swallowed by him, quickly move to one of the four sides of the stomach and attack it, eventually he'll spit you out. Mages have a harder time in here, but keep at it, you will get out.

Don't debuff him! If you do, he's that much harder to kill, and he starts throwing around worse debuffs at you. If your health is debuffed, I'd suggest getting away from him asap. The debuffs don't last long and you can jump right in and fight again. Use your melee's to keep him in line, your archers to do the most damage, and your mages to keep everyone alive. Also infusing them with stam when they ask wouldn’t hurt.

Once he's dead (this takes a very long time, make sure you're well prepared for this quest! Two plats is NOT going to cut it) he'll drop hides everywhere on the floor. They look like sacks. Grab one, collect any victims from the floor as well, and head to cragstone where you have a nice chat with Guard Ellyon for your reward, which has been noted as random. This is where you obtain the new mansion portals. This current one goes to one of the three bsd's (it's a random portal each time). You also get gems to imbue weapons and burun armor. If you plan on doing this quest more than once, then hand in the hide for a title of Reeshan Killer. If not, hold on to it, as there will be more hides to add to it for a reward later on.

Hope this guide has helped you, and feel free to ask for a personal run through if you're on SC :)
