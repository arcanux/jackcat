 From Pillars Made of Sand.

Throughout the month, Royal Guard Gabrielle in Rithwic (10.5N, 58.2E) would change messages giving news of new Renegade Strongholds. Explore A Small Complex and retrieve a key from Captain Waliknua. It opens a chest in the Burun Cathedral (formerly known as the Sclavus Cathedral). Inside you'll find a bracelet -- the Renegade Stone Clasp, 10 Renegade Herbal Kits and a piece of lore.

Royal Guard Gabrielle tells you, "Your arrival is most fortunate! I've just been informed that the Renegades and the Burun are meeting as we speak. Our spies have found a location on the shore of the Prosper river between Lytelthorpe and Dryreach. There, in a makeshift camp, they have summoned a portal to a secluded citadel. We need you to go investigate this location and stop them from signing any treaties. Who knows what these Burun would be capable of with the Renegades assisting them."
Royal Guard Gabrielle tells you, "Before you run off! Remember that there are still Renegade activities near Stonehold, in the Direlands swamp, near Fadsahil's shop in the A'mun desert, east of Yanshi, and on the southern border of the Blackmire Swamp. These areas still need to be secured."

To reach the Small Complex take the Siege Road Settlement portal from Rithwic at 8.7N, 60.9E. Next head to the E Dires Swamp portal at 8.1S, 74.5E. You'll drop a short distance from the Small Complex located at 39.1S, 53.4W.

Note: you can also use a Blackmire Mansion portal if available.

Map: Small Complex
Levels: 40+
Creatures: Gotrok Tiatus, Gotrok Fortress Guard, Murk Drudge, Tumerok High Priest, Tumerok Champion, Captain Waliknua

Take the north passage from the drop and keep to the left until you reach the final room. The Tumerok Captain Waliknua carries a half dozen Strong Iron Keys (7 day timer).

Next head to the Burun Cathedral nearby at 42.3S, 52.6W. This dungeon was previously known as the East Dires Sclavus Cathedral. As you approach, you'll see why it has now been renamed; sclavus lie dead all around the entrance and it has been overtaken by Burun inside!

Map: Burun Cathedral
Levels: all (lockpick required)
Creatures: Burun Ruuk Lout, Seer, Fiend, Soothsayer

From the drop, head directly north until you reach a locked door (resistance 124). Once through the door, keep right until you reach the big open sloping room. Take the south passage from this room and keep to the left until the final room. The Burun spawn in the dungeon is intensely fast, so be prepared and have your frost weapon handy!

Use the Strong Iron Key on the Renegade Chest for your reward: the Renegade Stone Clasp bracelet, 10 Renegade Herbal Kits and lore "Correspondence."

Text: Correspondence

Note: ID the chest before opening it and make sure that the burden is at 6475 before you use your key. The Herbal Kits respawn slowly in the chest and if you use your key too soon, you won't get the full 10 kits.
