From the Mirror, Mirror event.

Scourge's Hide Leggings were introduced when the Singularity Caul was updated during the Mirror, Mirror event. To reach the Caul you must be flagged by killing the Guardian of the Singularity, a level 100 Virindi found at the end of the Singularity Bore (see the Quintessence Sickle quest for details). The dungeon is restricted to levels 50+. You can also use the Singularity Caul Recall spell.

Note: The Caul is the island Bael'zharon called players to run "The Guantlet", an invitation only quest to receive an Atlan Shadow Stone and the title of Dark Master. A group of Virindi led by Levistras chose the Caul as the location to build a new Singularity before they were defeated.

The leggings are made from Scourge's Hide dropped by a Level 161 Rat (rare drop). Scourge is found on the eastern part of the Caul. Best attack unknown. Scourge casts a spell called... Scourge which cuts your stamina in half for 30 seconds!

Give the hide to a Leather Crafter in exchange for Scourge's Hide Leggings.
