From the Mirror, Mirror event.

Snarl's Jerkin was introduced when the Singularity Caul was updated during the Mirror, Mirror event. To reach the Caul you must be flagged by killing the Guardian of the Singularity, a level 100 Virindi found at the end of the Singularity Bore (see the Quintessence Sickle quest for details). The dungeon is restricted to levels 50+. You can also use the Singularity Caul Recall spell.

Note: The Caul is the island Bael'zharon called players to run "The Guantlet", an invitation only quest to receive an Atlan Shadow Stone and the title of Dark Master. A group of Virindi led by Levistras chose the Caul as the location to build a new Singularity before they were defeated.

The jerkin is made from Snarl's Hide, dropped by a Level 161 Carenzi, Snarl (rare drop). Snarl is found on the eastern part of the Caul (reported coords: 95.8S, 90.4W and 95.5S, 92.0W). Use Bludgeon or Cold attacks for best results and don't forget to bane you legs! Snarl casts a spell called Depletion which cuts your mana in half for 30 seconds!

Give the hide to a Leather Crafter in exchange for Snarl's Jerkin.

If you have a pic of Snarl, please send me an email!
