From the Throne of Destiny Expansion.

As you travel through the Halaetan Isles, you'll notice 2 kinds of Fiun: pleasant enterprising and otherwise normal Fiun, and crazed zombie-ish sort of Fiun. Stop by the bar in Sanamar and you can purchase a rumor, telling you how you can help this second kind.

Rumor: The Fiun Healing Machine

To reach Scorus at Silyun, take the portal from Sanamar at 76.6N, 58.6W. You can also buy a portal gem gem from Luressi di Maki for an I note.

Scorus - 87.5N, 70.3W (Silyun)

Scorus tells you, "You come to Silyun, Isparian? Are you no friend of the Slayer?"
Scorus tells you, "The Slayer has unleashed the Eaters upon us. They hunt us as they did in the land before. Those they do not kill, they madden instead."
Scorus tells you, "But there may be a cure. The mages who graced this land prior crafted marvelous artifacts - items of great power. Find these items. Create the machine. Perhaps we may cure our maddened kin."
Scorus gives you Healing Machine Recipe.
Scorus gives you A Message from Scorus.
Scorus tells you, "Luck to you and your mission friend."

Text: A Message from Scorus
Text: Healing Machine Recipe

Scorus gives you a list of dungeons where various parts of the healing machine may be found. He also gives you instructions to assemble the parts.

Note: You'll need Sturdy Iron Keys or Lockpick to complete this quest.

Small Empyrean Vault - 80.4N, 74.0W  
--Levels: 30+
--Map: Small Empyrean Vault
--Item: Lavus
--Creatures: Famished Eater, Crazed Fiun, Raving Fiun

From the drop keep left until you go down a ramp. Take your first right and you'll come to a room overlooking a hallway below. Jump down and retrieve the Small Empyrean Key. Recall back to the entrance and this time go right. Use the key on the Iron Door at the end of the hall. Head north and look for the Old Chest near the surface portal. Use a Sturdy Iron Key on the chest (or use Lockpick) and retrieve the Lavus from inside.

The Old Chest has been unlocked.
Your key is used up.

Remote Empyrean Vault - 81.7N, 71.2W   
--Levels: 40+
--Map: Remote Empyrean Vault
--Item: Tihn
--Creatures: Raving Fiun, Famished Eater, Eater, Engorged Eater

To reach the chest with the Tihn, take the ramp south from the entrance and keep left. You'll circle around, going down a ramp to the north, then another to the south. Follow the corridor to the a closed door. The Tihn is in a chest behind the door, guarded by two Fiun. Again, use a Sturdy Iron Key or lockpick to open it.

Note: This dungeon also contains the Brewmaster's Front Cover from the Brewmaster quest and an Engorged Eater Jaw in the lower level.

Hidden City - 94.4N, 70.0W  
--Levels: 50+
--Map: Hidden City
--Item: Healing Machine Pedestal
--Creatures: Vile Ruschk, Cursed Bones, Phantom, Dark Leech

Navigating the Hidden City is straightforward. Simply keep straight throughout, skipping all side room. Initially you'll be going south, then after a ramp, you'll be heading straight east. The Pedestal is in a chest near the surface portal (use a SIK or lockpick).

Frozen Library - 90.7N, 56.4W
--Levels: 1+
--Map: Frozen Library
--Item: Ruschk Iceberg Key
--Creatures: Rabid Eater, Abhorent Eater, Demented Fuin, Deranged Fiun, Frenzied Fuin

To reach the Frozen Library, take the Plains of Ruin portal out of Silyun at 87.9N, 69.7W. You'll exit at 90.7N, 51.2W, a short run from the library. This dungeon doesn't contain a component of the healing machine; however, it does contain a key necessary in the next dungeon.

From the drop take the west passage and keep heading north in general. Once you go through a large room with a ledge above, keep to the right and look for a pit. Jump down into the pit and you'll find the key spawning on the floor near the exit portal.

Ruschk Iceberg - 96.3N, 60.0W
--Levels: 70+
--Map: Ruschk Iceberg
--Item: Healing Machine Hook
--Creatures: Ruschk Warlord

You'll have to jump onto the Iceberg and run up the side to enter the portal. Inside is thick with Ruschk Warlords (vulnerable to bludgeoning)! Follow the passage north until you reach a 4 way intersection. Ignore the ramps to the north and east and take the passage to the west. At the next 4 way intersection, take the ramp down to the north and keep to the right until you reach the locked door. Use the Ruschk Iceberg Key on the door, then follow the passage to the pit.

Jump into the pit and again keep right. You'll come to a ledge. Jump down to the next level, then take the ramp to the east. You'll be on another ledge overlooking the surface portal. Jump down (shift jump so you don't hit the portal) and get the hook from the chest before exiting.

K'nath Lair - 49.7S, 56.3W
--Levels: 1+
--Map: K'nath Lair
--Creatures: Granite Golem, K'nath D'Nob, K'nath N'gell, K'nath Nosaj, K'nath S'that

Greater K'nath Lair
--Levels: 50+
--Item: Runic Skull
--Creatures: K'nath Ban'ry, K'nath Di'vda, K'nath K'ram, K'nath Ri'shc, K'nath D'dot, K'nath Dyn'ra, K'nath Ra'dnas, K'nath Son'ja

The K'nath Lair is an old Dereth dungeon. Now, however, it has a lower level full of more powerful K'naths. The layout of the two levels is the same, so if you know the easier top level, you can run through the bottom to retrieve the skull.

To reach the dungeon, head to Dryreach from Holtburg at 42.2N, 34.2E. Take the E Dires Swamp portal at 8.1S, 74.5E, then run to the dungeon.

From the entrance, pick the door to the left open, then keep to the left until you reach the portal to the Greater K'nath Lair. Follow the same route to the Runic Skull. You'll find it resting on the floor by a stone pillar near the surface portal.

Undead Temple - 90.9N, 43.2W
--Levels: 1+
--Map: Undead Temple
--Item: Healing Machine Orb
--Creatures: Specter, Haunt, Poltergeist, Phantasm, Dark Master, Armored Skeleton, Skeletal Champion, Great Skeleton, Skeleton Bone Knight, Primeval Skeleton

From the drop, keep to the right and go down the ramp. Go through the coffin room, then jump into the pit. Keep to the right and head up the small ramp. Jump down the level below and take the passage up to the left. Keep to the left until you find the Runic Door Guardian. Hand the skull to the Guardian to gain passage to the final room. The chest it on a platform at the top of the ramps.

The only decipherable runes on the statue read "Runic Skull".
You allow Runic Door Guardian to examine your Runic Skull.
A voice from everywhere and yet nowhere bids you enter.

Once you have all the components, return to Scorus in Silyun. If you allow him to examine the parts, he instructs you to put it together per his instructions.

You allow Scorus to examine your Runic Skull.
Scorus tells you, "An odd artifact."
Scorus tells you, "Look. These runes can be translated to "Undead Temple". Perhaps this object will be useful there."

You allow Scorus to examine your Healing Machine Orb.
Scorus tells you, "Did I give you the recipe prior? Make the machine whole and return to me."
Follow the recipe in the note he gave you to make the Healing Machine.

You attach the Lavus to the Tihn and create a Lavuhn.
You attach the Lavuhn to the Pedestal and create a Healing Machine Base.
You attach the Healing Machine Base to the Hook and create a Hooked Base.
You attach the Hooked Base to the Orb and create the Fiun Healing Machine.

Once it's assembled, give the Healing Machie to Scorus for your reward!

You give Scorus Fiun Healing Machine.
Scorus gives you Helm of Gratitude.
Scorus tells you, "You have assembled the machine! This day is great for us. Perhaps maddened kin will be maddened no more. We owe you much, Isparian."
You've earned 2,000,000 experience.
Scorus gives you K'nath Lair Portal.
