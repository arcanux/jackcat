Collector (Sanamar) - 72.0N, 61.0W

Collector tells you, "I'm interested in monster trophies from common rats, red phyntos wasps, armoredillos, Drudges, Mosswarts, Banderlings, penguins, and Ruschk. If you give me such an item, I will reward you with money or an item. With regret, I don't deal in hides."

You give Collector Penguin Beak.
Collector tells you, "Ah, a beak from the most feared and troublesome penguin. Wave one of these at Lorca Sammel. I promise you, it's a good time."
You've earned 500 experience.
Collector gives you 500 Pyreals.
