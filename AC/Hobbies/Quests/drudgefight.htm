 From the Mired Hearts event.

Town Crier tells you, "Strange gems are being carried by some creatures. They seem to lead to secret locations that we haven't heard of before. Go figure."

The Drudge Fight gem is one of the rare drop portal gems introduced during the Mired Hearts event. It drops off either a Drudge Lurker or Drudge Stalker. These common drudges can by found in numerous dungeons throughout Dereth. Click on the links above for a list of places where they can be found. My favorite is the Gredaline Consulate outside of Qalaba'r. To reach the dungeon Gredaline Villas portal from Qalaba'r at 74.8S, 17.8E. When you use the gem, you'll be portaled inside a dungeon, the Drudge Fight!

Note: make sure you're outside on a flat area before using your rare gem! You may wish to test the area with a starter town gem before using your Drudge Fight quest gem!

Map: Drudge Fight

The dungeon is impossible to get lost in. It's really just one large room at the end of the passage. It's full of numerous Drudge Lurkes and Drudge Stalkers. When you reach the end of the dungeon, you'll find 2 special Drudges with stats and appearance similar to Raveners: Baktak, the Human Slayer and Kerthump, the Ear Taker. Each carries a Drudge Championship Belt!

Timer: 7 Days

The final room also contains a Runed Chest.

The belt may be dropped or given. Wear it with pride. But remember, do not talk about Drudge Fight!

Drudge Fight
Yes, drudge brother, you are invited to the biggest event of the year! The final Knock-Out fight between Kerthump, the Ear Taker, and Baktak the Human Slayer. A no claws death-match for the prized Drudge Championship Belt. Do not talk about Drudge Fight.
This portal summoning gem works best if used outside in a relatively flat area.
