From the Reprisals event.

These quest notes are provided as a courtesy until I can do a complete write-up.

Alchanar of LC reports on a new Royal Guard in Cragstone!

NPC: Royal Guard Qumasha - 26.0N, 49.1E
Text: Count Dardante's Notes
Dungeon: Viamontian Esper Mine - 72.2N, 3.0E (Levels 100+)
Dungeon: Viamontian Direlands Mine - 8.6N, 76.4W (Levels 100+)
Dungeon: Dardante's Keep (Levels 100+)
Location: Count Dardante's Island - 91.0N, 38.4W (Levels 100+)

Royal Guard Qumasha tells you, "Ah! Perhaps you can help me. The Queen has asked me to find out all I can about how Varicci crafted that terrible weapon, Heartbreaker. Regardless of your background or political affiliation, I hope you can appreciate how much of a menace Varicci poses. Please journey to the Viamontian Esper Mine at 72.2 N, 3.0 E and see if you can get the gem that the mine's commander carries. I should be able to use that to forge a portal gem into the heart of Varicci's research operation."

Response to level 92 player:
Royal Guard Qumasha tells you, "I'm sorry, I don't think you can help me."

Padre-Adamo reports: "Hello there Maggie! Padre-Adamo, Minnie the Minx, and Dark Kaito have just completed the Elemental Necklace Quest from the Guard in Cragstone. Attached are pictures taken from the quest. It was pretty cool and good fun. Straightforward as well. On the quest we faced Adepts of Lightning, Fire, Ice, and Acid. We also faced Viamontian Tribune, Abhorrent Eaters, and Viamontian Commanders."

Quest details courtesy of TheKnight of SC:

Note: Before you start this quest make sure and bring plenty of dispel potions/gems (level 7's). You're going to need them!

1.Start in Cragstone and talk to Qumasha located by the Cragstone Meeting Hall. She will tell you to go to coords: 72.2N X 3.0E. To get there take a portal to crater and then take the Anadil settlement portal at 69.5N, 4.0W which will drop you a short distance from the the portal.

2. Enter the portal located at 72.2N X 3.0E (Restricted to levels 100+) If you like tie once inside to the portal as it is tieable and recallable, as you will be recalling here a lot as it's a tough dungeon.

3. Proceed straight and then straight again under the overpass ignoring the left/right passageways, then take a left at the end around and keep right all the way to the end to the boss. If you get lost portal recall, or first/secondary if you tied. This place is a massive maze so expect to get lost a lot.

4. After you have your gem from the boss, return to Cragstone and hand it to the NPC again. This time you'll be told to go to coords: 8.6N X 76.4W near Fort Teth. Either lifestone recall to Ayan, or if your not lifestoned there, go to Shousi and take the Kara portal and then the Kara Statue portal to Fort Teth.

5.Enter the portal located at 8.6N X 76.4W (Restricted to levels 100+) If you like tie to this one now as it is also tieable and recallable. Expect to be recalling here a lot also as it's a tough dungeon as well.

6. Again this one is a maze like area and you're sure to get lost some. The only advice I have here is look for a hallway right around the middle of one of the passageways and follow that through to the end to the boss. This one is totally different layout wise from the first, so the same route will not work here.

7. Again return to Cragstone and give the gem to the NPC and she will hand you pack a portal gem in return and ask that you retrieve some notes from this area. It is a summonable portal, but you must have done the first two steps in order to use it. People can't just join on this midway through as this flags you down as "You must complete a quest before using this portal". The portal is non-recallable/non-summon/non-tieable. This is VERY important because if you die and you're doing this one without a fellow, you're not going to be able to recover without someone else using their gem so you can get back there.

8. After using the gem and you go through, you are on a small island off the coast of the Halatian Islands to the east shore. On the island is a giant fort which you are tossed into the middle of, with a villa towards the rear. You will battle your way past the various Viamonts to the inside of the villa and down until you run into the Doorkeeper Knight. Kill him and loot the key to use on the door. Or you can just use lockpick on the door (it's 400 resist) and bypass the Doorkeeper Knight; however, he is blocking the way so you might as well use the key on his body.

9. After you enter the portal you will be underground and fighting more Viamonts; however, this area is even more deadly then all the other areas previously combined. You will be fighting all 4 Elemental Aspect types so watch yourself! Head straight and then to the right past the doors, just keep following the doors until you get to a hallway that branches out into a large room. Now stop! There are all 4 elemental aspects in this room as well as a Viamont, which are on very quick respawn timers.

There is a desk here that you can bust open (or you can skip this part if you have lockpick see note below) in order to get the key. Attack low as high/med you'll just miss. Once broken grab the key and head to the opposite side of the room and up the stairs. Use the key on the chest and loot the note inside. Note *If you have lockpick you can run straight to the other side of this dangerous room and just lockpick the chest open* You'll notice a Mana Barrier here which you can't attack.

10. Once you have the note, it's back to Cragstone for a final time. Hand the note to the NPC for your reward of 9,647,500 XP (unknown if this is a % based number or just a flat number for now) and your Necklace Of Elemental Adepts. If you don't want the Necklace. you can turn it back in for an XP amount.

You give Royal Guard Qumasha Esper Mine Commander's Gem.
Royal Guard Qumasha tells you, "Ah, how disappointing. It appears that the Esper Mine Commander's gem only has part of the information I need to access Count Dardante's Keep. There is a second installation, in the Direlands... at 8.6N, 76.4W. The commander of that mine should have a gem, similar to this one, that will allow me to forge a portal gem."

You give Royal Guard Qumasha Direlands Mine Commander's Gem.
Royal Guard Qumasha tells you, "Yes, the Direlands Mine commander's gem. This is what I needed. Allow me to fuse and enchant the gem..."
Royal Guard Qumasha chants softly over the stones, magically fusing the two stones into one.
Royal Guard Qumasha gives you Count Dardante's Island Portal Gem.
Royal Guard Qumasha tells you, "The gem I have given to you should create a portal to take you into the stronghold of Count Dardante, who I believe was instrumental in the creation of Heartbreaker. I have heard that Dardante keeps his notes in a locked chest in his office. If you cannot pick the lock, you will have to ransack his desk to find his key."

You give Royal Guard Qumasha Count Dardante's Notes.
Royal Guard Qumasha tells you, "This is valuable information. We should be able to use this to take measures to keep Varicci from creating more of those fiendish blades. I thank you."
You've earned 9,647,500 experience.
Royal Guard Qumasha gives you Necklace of the Elemental Adepts.
Royal Guard Qumasha tells you, "This necklace may be useful to you, should you have to encounter any more of those Viamontian Elemental Adepts. If you do not find it useful, give it to me and I shall reward you with knowledge to improve yourself in whatever way you see fit."
