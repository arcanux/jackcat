 From Pillars Made of Sand.

Throughout the month, Royal Guard Gabrielle in Rithwic (10.5N, 58.2E) would change messages giving news of new Renegade Strongholds. When she reported that Muldaveus and Niraltah had been spotted entering a stronhold near Stonehold, adventurers raced to explore. Muldaveus and Niraltah have long since departed, but inside you'll find Ollar, Renegade Captain. Defeat him and you'll recover a key that can be used on a Renegade Chest containing 100 Distilled Mana Potions, a Coarse Hide Shirt and a piece of lore.

Royal Guard Gabrielle tells you, "Hail! You've arrived just in time! I've received word that Muldaveus and Niarltah have been spotted entering a stronghold in the north near Stonehold. We've sent some agents up to the location as a precautionary measure, but we need all the assistance that we can get."
Royal Guard Gabrielle tells you, "There have also been sightings of possible contact between the Burun and Renegades in Direlands swamp, near Fadsahil's shop in the A'mun desert, east of Yanshi heading toward Dryreach and the southern edge of the Blackmire Swamp."

To reach A Renegade Stronghold, take the Azaroth Cottages settlement portal from Qalaba'r at 74.8S, 17.8E, then take the Stonehold portal at 85.8N, 27.6W. Run to the dungeon located at 71.4N, 28.6W.

Map: A Renegade Stronghold

Creatures: Ascendant Tumerok, Tumerok Champion, Tumerok High Priest, Gotrok Tiatus, Gotrok Extas, Gotrok Fortress Guard, Ollar, Renegade Captain

To reach Ollar, Renegade Captain, keep to the right from the entrance until you dead end in an open room. On his corpse you'll find a half dozen Strong Iron Keys (7 day timer). When you have your key return to the entrance (portal recall or backtrack your step).

Gotrok Fortress Guard tells you, "Their edges are barbed and worn! Strike hard and fast my brothers!"

This time keep to your left. Once through the door, head north until you find A Renegade Chest in the final room. Unlock the chest for 100 Distilled Mana Potions, a Coarse Hide Shirt and the text, Mana Oil!
