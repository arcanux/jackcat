 The Diamond Shield is a trophy from a Diamond Lord, a special Diamond Golem introduced in the Keep Your Enemies Closer event.

Diamond Lords can be found on the Obsidian Plains and in the South Direlands. (The one Danier is fighting in the pic was located at 68.7S, 68.2W.) They appear pink in color and are usually found accompanied by a few Diamond Golems. And if you thought Diamond Golems were hard to kill, you should give this bad boy a try! That is, if you can find one! ;-) The new boss creatures can be scarce.

Your perseverance will be well worth it, as these shields not only have great protections, but are a gorgeous rainbow hue to boot!
