January 28, 2005

Aloha Maggie.

Allow me to first say that I love your site. I'm sorry I don't have a chat log or a very clear story, but it was a very hectic time getting this quest done. I hope that I can accurately relate the events enough though.

Thursday night, around 11 PM eastern, some friends and I portalled to Aphus Lassel to kill Mowen. In the town, we noticed a drudge wandering around. His name was "Curmudgeon the Drudge" and he was an admin/+Leo/Dev roleplaying with us. In perfect and unbroken drudge speak (which is similar to a young child's speech, see the inscriptions on the items in the screenshots that I am sending), he asked us for help and protection. It turns out that he wanted to get a Doll Leader's Charm for his wife as a gift, but took the wrong portal (He never said the name of the item, but I was able to figure it out) and had ended up at Aphus Lassel. After listening to his story about being afraid of the "Shiny faces" (Dolls) and the "Ebul Drudge wif Shiny face" (virindi altered drudges, such as Raveners), I started to run to get a portal tie for him.

Well, just as I got the nearest tie to the Singular Pyreal Repository, someone came along and killed Curmudgeon. He was not accepting @tell messages, so we thought all was lost. However, about 10 minutes later, Curmudgeon gives me an @tell saying that a "Bad man cause me much hurt, make go through swirly". I reply to him that I've got a ride for him and inquire to his location. He doesn't know the name of the town he is in (Hey, drudges can't read town signs), but he is in a beach town. I check Eastham and Mayoi, but no Curmudgeon. I ask him if there's anything else he can tell me, and he mentions a tasty apple pie. This tips me off that he's in Yaraq, so I meet him. My fellowship (Badangel, Osato Cin, Thengil, Tanyia and I), guide him along the run, killing everything in our path to protect him. We get to the dungeon and it turns out the he actually needs us to take him through the entire quest. This gets a bit hairy, but we get him to the end, and he gets the bracelet for his wife. He then portals us back to Yaraq, thanks us many times, and just before he leaves, he offers to give us rewards. He gave everyone in my fellow a Drudge Board with Nail that he inscribed with a personal thank you for each person. He then says that since I led him so well that he has a special gift for me. He gave me a "Huggy Drudge Doll". It looks different that other pack drudges (see screenshot), has a special description and an inscription from him. Also, just before he left, he offered to tell the world what we had done. Rather than a global message, he decided to give all of us Unique titles (see screenshot) of "Curmudgeon's Friend".

I hope I've relayed the story to you well enough, I'm no expert writer or storyteller. If you have any questions, feel free to reply.

--Xantis of Leafcull
