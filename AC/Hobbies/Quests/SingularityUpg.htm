Rumors updated during Pillars Made of Sand. Quest updated during Treaties in Stone. You must complete the Singularity and Bound Singularity Weapons quests before upgrading your weapon to an ultimate one.

Drunken Asian Man from SC reports: "Martine's dungeon on ML has changed too. There are plated and silver tuskers, also there are the new virindi, Martinate Virindi Inquisitors. I think they did away with the Martinate Hollow minions because I didn't see one."

Rumor: Virindi Weapons

To upgrade your Singularity Weapon, you'll need to take it to a Virindi at the bottom of the Martinate Holding, a dungeon on Marae Lassel (must have Dark Majesty).

To reach the dungeon, head to Ziakhal and take the portal to Danby's Outpost at 16.7N, 1.6W. From the outpost, head NE out of the settlement to 24.1N, 28.1W. You may want to hit the lifestone there before taking the portal to the Central Tuatara Plains.

At the drop on Marae Lassal you may encounter Carenzi,  Azrael Zefirs, Vapor Golems and Virindi. Head NW to the Martinate Holding at 59.6N, 77.1W.

The entrance is safe for buffing. Inside you'll find Executors, Martinate Hollow Minions, Armored Tuskers, Silver Tuskers, Tusker Guards and Slaves, and Virindi Servants and Masters.

The dungeon itself if pretty linear. There's a dispel trap near the entry -- hug the wall to avoid it, and one mana trap near end. Both are jumpable. 

The first room contains about 6 Tusker guards and 3 Virindi Servents. Once you've cleared the room, continue South until you reach a room with Silver Tuskers. Continue SW, following the wall and you'll fight a Virindi Master, then 2 Martinate Hollow Minions to the south. The next large open room will be populated with Armored Tuskers, Virindi Eexecutors and more Hollows.

You may want to just run through this room and exit via the south hallway. Follow the corridor until you reach the final room. You'll find 3 or 4 Virindi Executors and the NPC Virindi. Give it your Singularity (blue -- destroyed on death) in return for the upgraded version (green - no drop, no give). The upgraded weapon has a higher base damage and adds +5% Attack and +5% Melee Defense.

Executor Assistant tells you, "The Master is pleased to see you have made it so far, little one. You haven't lost your pretty Singularity weapon now, did you? Give it to me, and we shall embark on the next step on this journey...yes? Yessssss."
Executor Assistant gives you A Journal by Martine.

You give Executor Assistant Singularity Sword.
Executor Assistant tells you, "Slicing and dicing is fun. I used to be able to do it so much more before the Master came to me. But I am not complaining! You must not tell Master I was complaining!"
Executor Assistant gives you Singularity Sword.

You give Executor Assistant Singularity Dagger.
Executor Assistant tells you, "The Master once told me that his people had a saying, 'If you prick me, do I not bleed?' But I was watching him during one of his 'presentations.' He did not bleed at all."
Executor Assistant gives you Singularity Dagger.
