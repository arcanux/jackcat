From Shadowcouncil of MT:

Holy bajeezus.

OK we made it through the new dungeon 4 times or so getting bodies then a buddy died recalling out so we figured we should take a party back in to get his body ..

HAHAHAHA

We started this journey at 10 pm est, and decided to give it up at 4:30 am est.

Anyway, I haven't had this much fun in years, in AC. I was actually challenged by the game tonight, overly challenged. And I could care less if there is a reward for doing it. If you feel you can do anything, bring one fellow of 9 people and try to make it to the bottom of the drudge side of the new dungeon. Just don't bring anything that you can't afford to lose =D.

There are 2 sides to the dungeon and tons of ways to do it.

When you are trying to get there, use the VOD back door portal and run north until the clearing opens then head west and sw until you can run up the mountain. When you run up you will head North again and jump off into more VoD critters and haul butt to the new dungeon

37.9, 65.6

When you enter the drop will be clear. Don't be tempted to walk into that hallway.

It will draw level 160 drudges and level 160 banderlings.

Bane everything but light and acid and buff everything else.

Ok to do the Drudge side (since I know it so well now).
You will need to hug left side of wall. Once you get to the flat level, you will hug the left wall. Move maybe 1 body length forward and stop, then jump to the right (see 1st pic) or you will hit a trap and dispell all your level 7's.

There is a pile of rocks there (in picture) do not touch them or they will spawn a massive spawn of 4 or 5 level 150 drudges.

Then you will go to the right but stop there. You will need to hug the left side of the wall so you don't set of the rocks in front of you.

This will lead you to an unlocked door with 3 seraphs and 1 mystic in it.

OK you need to start searching the mystics for a glittering key to open the sealed door.  This is also a really darn good place to buff since only 1 mystic and 2 seraphs spawn here.

This will lead you to the room with a Virindi Statue. After you kill the 8 or so drudges you will need to go straight across and straight down this will lead you to another room with the exact same look and a door behind a virindi statue.

Open that door and go through.

There will be a few drudges in that hall then it curves and you will see Scrum.

Not to sure what to do with this guy. There are 8 or so drudges in this hall/room, but you can hit them through the windows with bow or spells easily. You will know what I'm talking about when you get there.

I just had a blast doing it, even though we aren't quite sure what we are supposed to do when we finish it.
