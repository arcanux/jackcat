From the Dance of the Dead event.

The following notes are provided as a courtesy until I can research a full write-up.

Dakmor Kavu of HG sends these details: "Hey Maggie! Here are some more goodies from the patch that I thought you and your fans might enjoy :-P

Torgash & Kalakh (NPCs)  - 94.0N by 45.9W

Talk to Torgash to Recieve the message:
A chill passes through your body, numbing you to the core. For a single moment, you feel like you're going to die from the sensation alone. The Ruschk opens his mouth; his speech comes out in but a whisper... "[player name]"
In your mind, the following information becomes apparent to you. The Ruchk standing before you wishes you to kill 30 of the Viamontian Knights in the valley below. Once this is complete, he wishes you to retrieve a vial from the westernmost Knight Encampment in the valley, near 93.5N, 51.4W. You get the impression that the vial is in a small building, toward the back of the encampment.

Upon completion of the task:
The Ruschk looks greatly pleased. He presses his right fist into his left hand and bows to you. The Rushk then looks at you quizzically, seeming to wonder if you wished to hunt more knights.
You have earned 8,700,000 experience. (Level 247)

Timer: none

Talk to Kalakh to recieve the message:
The Ruschk looks you up and down, like he is gauging your strength. With a nod of approval, he stares into your eyes, and with a wash of deathly cold, the following information becomes apparent in your mind:
The Ruschk wishes you to enter the Temple of the Black Spear, located at 95.5N, 51.8W, and retrieve a small totem, important to the followers of Grael. Once you bring the totem back to him, he will reward you for your strength.

The dungeon is 130+ to enter, neither tieable nor recall able. Its filled with Mukkir and Rushk, which get progressively tougher, with Shadows appearing later on. When you get down to the room with the surface portal, keep going down past. Room with totem is a little ways on.

You give Kalakh Black Spear Ice Totem.
The Ruschk looks at the Totem with obvious contempt, and drops it unceremoniously in his pouch. Then, with a look which seems to beam with pride, he claps you on the back. With obvious effort, he speaks in a barely audible whisper.
Kalakh tells you, "Well... Done."
The Ruschk, Kalakh, awards you the title of "Friend to the Frostglaive."
You've earned 29,175,980 experience

Much thanks again to Yeti Ginsu for the company and much appreciated firepower on the quest!"

Ninavie and Zegeger of HG report:

Run from eastwatch, two npc's at 93.2N 48.2W.

Arialle du Bellenesse tells you, "Greetings. If you have the time, I have a mission that you may be interested in. To the northwest, at 95.5N, 51.8W , there is a Temple to that accursed Grael. Inside, the Ruschk seem to have created a Totem capable of empowering Grael himself."
Arialle du Bellenesse tells you, "This Totem cannot be allowed to stay in their possession. They could possibly even use such a thing to overcome the splitting of Grael into Aspects, and we'd have to battle a fully empowered Grael, all over again."
Arialle du Bellenesse tells you, "Retrieve this Totem for me, so we can seek a way to prevent these from aiding Grael, as well as removing it from their possession, and I will reward you for your efforts.

Temple of the Black Spear, restricted to level 130+

Commander Rylane di Cinghalle tells you, "Greetings. If you have the time, I have a set of tasks you could aid me with. I'm here to help thin out the number of Ruschk in the valley below, and to gain one of their Totems for further study."
Commander Rylane di Cinghalle tells you, "If you were to kill, say... 30 of the Ruschk in the valley, and then acquire one of their smaller Ice Totems from the encampment at 95.5N, 42.3W, I'd be more than happy to reward you for your efforts."
Commander Rylane di Cinghalle tells you, "I'd kill the Ruschk first. That should throw them into enough disarray that you should be able to grab the Totem unnoticed."

Black Spear Ice Totem
A small Ruschk totem, seemingly formed completely out of blackened ice. There is a palpable aura of menace and cold flowing from the totem at all times.
This item cannot be sold.
Value 0
Burden 200
Special Props Attuned, Bonded
