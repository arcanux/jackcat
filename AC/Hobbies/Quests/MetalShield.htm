The beaches of Eastham used to run wild with newbies. Those tiring of the ocean view would pop into the conveniently located Eastham Sewers for a change of pace. How many realized back in the day that in addition to the spawning Drudges, the Eastham Sewers also hid a Metal Round Shield locked tight within a chest!

The rumor to the dungeon can be purchased in Eastham from Degel Parson the Scrivener at 71.5N, 63.5E.

Directions to Eastham Sewer
An old sewer is located north of town from Farnum's  shop. It's not far from here and within sight of the shore.

Bandits and vagabonds once used the sewer as a place of refuge. Recently, the sewer has become home to drudges and shreths.

The Sewers are located at 18.7N, 63.4E, just outside of town (no level restrictions). Inside you'll find low level Drudges (Slinkers, Sneakers, Prowlers and Robbers) and Shreth (Gnawers & Carrion and one Hunter).

Map: Eastham Sewers

To begin the quest head down the ramp from the entry room and keep to the left. When you go around the corner, you'll see a ramp straight ahead heading down. At the bottom is a large open room with a mushroom ring growing on the floor.

Take the south passage out of the room. Bypass the ramp to the east and continue bearing north. You're going to hug the left wall through a series of small rooms until you reach one with a pile of straw on the floor and a Skulker and a Sneaker inside (about the 5th room). The Gold Key is on the Drudge Skulker.

Head back the way you came hugging the right wall until you get to the ramp just past the mushroom ring. Follow the ramp down until you reach the locked door. You'll need lockpick to open it (resistance 82). Next you'll go through two more doors heading north. As you enter this next section, you'll be fighting Drudge Prowlers and Robbers.

At the first three way intersection, head north into the room with a chest. From there, head west past the ramp to your right and through a room with several drudges. The hall will curve around to the north and you'll enter a room with three chests on the floor.

Take the ramp up, head east through a few doors, then take another ramp up. Go through a small room and take yet another ramp until you end at a "T".

Take the left passage heading south in order to retrieve the 2nd key. At the top of the passage is a large open room with a few bookcases and other furnishings. Three Sneakers and a Prowler lay in wait! The Key to the final chest is on one of the Drudge Sneakers.

Go back to the "T" and take the other passage heading north. You'll go through a large open room then across a catwalk. When you get to the room with Strength Fountain, you're almost there! At the split, keep to the right and you'll reach the final room. A Hunter Shreth guards the chests inside. Use the Gold Key to open the door and your other Key to open the chest on the right. Inside you'll find the Metal Round Shield!

To exit, take the left split past the strength fountain or jump from the catwalk above to reach the surface portal.
