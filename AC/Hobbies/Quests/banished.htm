From the Infiltration event.

Banished Weapons are a set wield requirement trophy weapons dropped from Banished creatures. Banished creatures are found scattered on the Southern Landbridge. (Use the Land Bridge Villas portal from Qalaba'r at 74.8S, 17.8E.)

They have also been spotted in northern Osteth near Celdiseth's Cottage and NE of the Fire Propylaeum. The trophy is always found on the creature's corpse and there is no timer. Below is a list of which Banished Creature drops which weapon:

Banished Grievver - Atlatl
Banished Lugian - Axe
Banished Banderling - Blade (Sword)
 Banished Shadow - Bow
 Banished Phyntos Wasp - Crossbow
 Banished Olthoi - Mace
Banished Monouga - Nekode
Banished Mu-Miyah - Orb
 Banished Tusker - Point (Dagger)
Banished Tumerok - Spear
 Banished Drudge - Staff

In addition to the Banished Weapon, the creature may also drop other trophies. For example, the Grievver may drop Silk and the Drudge may drop a Drudge Head!

During the Come What Follows event, a new NPC arrived in Stonehold. Kill 10 Banished creatures for an xp reward and a new title (reward details still needed)!

NPC: Belinda du Loc - 68.9N, 21.6W (Stonehold)
