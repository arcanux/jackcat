From Whack-a-mole of FF: Ulgrims Recall now drops you on.. *drumroll* Ulgrims Island [an island below the southern landbridge] where beer is free in a well outside. No monsters apart from lvl 1 mice in the basement.

If you get him Ulgrims Homebrew from down in the basement, he gives you a Casting Beer Stein. It casts stam2mana4. And generally looks like a beer stein :)

The island is located at 94.8S, 9.7W. If you don't have the recall spell, give the Drunken Madman outside Baishi (53.0S, 61.0E) Winter Lager from the barkeep in Nanto, and he'll summon Ulgrim’s house.

Ulgrim's House:
Ulgrim's House. Be Respectful!

Walk in and you'll see Fish Boots on a hook just inside door. They're scaly, yet comfortable! If you talk to Ulgrim, he'll send you to the basement to fetch him some Home Brew.

Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "Hey, since you're already bothering me you could at least make yourself useful and fetch me some of my home brewed stout from the basement."
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "Just take the portal to the lower basement and look for the glowing stout, you can't miss it. And don't go messing around with any of my things!"

Take the portal to the basement and get a mug of Home Brew (14 day timer). While you're there, check out the curiosity, the Holy Handgrenade of Antioch, on a hook in the first room.

You can also kill the brown and white mice until one drops Ulgrim's Golden Key (no timer).

There are two storage chests in the basement. One contains Stout, a Stein and Winter Lager. The other contains an Ust, Peppermint Sticks, a Nanto portal gem, and a Hangover Cure (random - you may need to camp the chest for a short bit).

Behind the barrels, a Gharu'ndim wall tapestry hides the seekrit Treasure Room Door! Go behind the tapestry (walk through it) and use the Golden Key on the Treasure Room Door (resistance 100, may be picked).

Prepare to be dazzled!! The treasure room is full of... treasure! Heaping piles of it! You'll also see pyreals arranged to form Ulgrim's initials. The treasure is on a 28 day timer. It may be hooked and given, or it can be salvaged for 8-10 units of Salvaged Gold (workmanship 10.00).

You'll also find a ring (from a pickable lockbox - 28 day timer, also opens with Ulgrim's Key) and Ulgrim's pack toy, a Golden Gromnie (no timer)!

Recall and return to Ulgrim with the Brew. But don't drink it!!  Rosso of LC reports that he accidentally drank Ulgrim's Brew and now can't get his casting stein for 2 weeks!
