 From Pillars Made of Sand.

Throughout the month, Royal Guard Gabrielle in Rithwic (10.5N, 58.2E) would change messages giving news of new Renegade Strongholds. She asked players to head to the shore of the Prosper River near Lytelthorpe to prevent a treaty from being signed.

Royal Guard Gabrielle tells you, "Your arrival is most fortunate! I've just been informed that the Renegades and the Burun are meeting as we speak. Our spies have found a location on the shore of the Prosper river between Lytelthorpe and Dryreach. There, in a makeshift camp, they have summoned a portal to a secluded citadel. We need you to go investigate this location and stop them from them from signing any treaties. Who knows what these Burun would be capable of with the Renegades assisting them."
Royal Guard Gabrielle tells you, "Before you run off! Remember that there are still Renegade activities near Stonehold, in the Direlands swamp, near Fadsahil's shop in the A'mun desert, east of Yanshi, and on the southern border of the Blackmire Swamp. These areas still need to be secured."

Renegade Stronghold - 2.4S, 63.7E
Map: Renegade Stronghold
Timer: 28 Days

Creatures: Burun Ruuk Adherent, Burun Ruuk Fiend, Burun Ruuk Lout, Burun Ruuk Ruffian, Burun Ruuk Savage, Burun Ruuk Seer, Burun Ruuk Shaman, Burun Ruuk Soothsayer, General Fostok, Gigas Raider, Gotrok Extas, Gotrok Fortress Guard, Gotrok Juggernaut, Gotrok Montok, Gotrok Tiatus, Gotrok Titan, Tumerok Gladiator, Tumerok High Priest, Tumerok Priest, Tumerok Taskmaster, Tumerok Trooper, Tumerok War Monger, War Chief Amanua

Original Creatures: Lord Muldaveus, Morgluuk, Niraltah, Torgluuk

To reach the dungeon, take the Riverbend Cottages settlement portal from Lytelthorpe at 2.2N, 50.4E.

Inside the dungeon were the lore bosses -- the Lugian Lord Muldaveus, Niraltah theTumerok and the Buruns Torglukk and Morgluuk. The Burun were impossible to fell; however, Muldaveus (or Niraltah on other servers) was eventually defeated. As he collapsed, the following global went out:

Muldaveus' voice thunders over the land, "Niarltah, we must flee. The Isparians have overrun our position. Come Morgluuk and Torgluuk, we will take you to the safety of our hidden fortress. Fostok, Amanua, get in here and clean up this tripe!"

Niarltah's voice echoes over the land, "Muldaveus, we must flee. The Isparians have overrun our position. Come Morgluuk and Torgluuk, we will take you to the safety of our hidden fortress. Amanua, Fostok, get in here and clean up this tripe!"

Once they were defeated, 2 more lore bosses spawned: War Chief Amanua and General Fostok. When killed, they drop the Ornate Tumerok Breastplate and the Horned Lugian Helm.

The dungeon is straightforward and symmetrical throughout. Keep to the right, or keep to the left, all the way to the final room. Be careful not to pulled into the surface portal when you reach that section. The creatures get more dangerous as you go deeper. When you reach the final room, you may want to keep a wall of melees in the hallway and have the mages attack from behind. You'll be swarmed by Burun, Lugian and Tumeroks, and finally War Chief Amanua and General Fostok. The two boss creatures are difficult to kill, so bring some firepower with you. You'll especially want a few mages, as melee attacks have little effect.

War Chief Amanua says, "You should have thought of the consequences of this action, Isparian."
War Chief Amanua says, "Mighty spirits watch over my soul. What hope have you to penetrate their protection?"

Gotrok Titan says, "You have come to a crossroads where your life shall come to an end!"
General Fostok says, "This end by my fists and weapons. Your body shall crumple like the walls of Linvak Tukal!"

[Player] has struck a mortal blow to Fostok. As the Lugian General falls to the ground motionless, his helmet falls from his head.

[Player] has struck a mortal blow to Amanua. As the Tumerok War Chief falls to the ground motionless, his armor snaps free.

Once General Fostok and War Chief Amanua have been killed, retrieve the Horned Lugian Helm and Ornate Breastplate from their corspes!
