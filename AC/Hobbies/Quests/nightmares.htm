From the Friend and Foe event.

What is the Order of the Raven Hand doing on the Singularity Caul? What hideous servants of the shadows will they unleash? Characters over level 100 can investigate and try to put an end to their latest plot!

Vitae Boy of MT reports: "To start the Cult of the Raven Hand quest, go to 99.7, 96.7 on caul. Talk to Surloshen Realaidain. Go to 96.5, 95. Kill Xaa Xiil for the Twilight Fragment. Kill Archon Traesis for the Caliginous Aegis. Go to 94.3, 93.4. Kill Greater Void Lord for Raven Fragment. Kill Archon Greis for Circle of Raven Might. Go to 95, 98.2. Kill Greater Panumbral Horror for Iniquitous Fragment. Kill Archon Ingfall for Mitts of the Hunter. Put the Fragments together, and turn them in to Sarkin Killcrane in Wai Jhou for 10% (I think) xp. There's more, but it's restricted 150+ and I'm 106, so I couldn't go :(

Sarkin Killcrane tells you, "So many adventurers have brought me pieces of this item, and it is only now that I see if completed that I remember where I've seen it before."

Sarkin Killcrane tells you, "With this lock in our hands, we can assume that one such gate exists on the Singularity Caul, likely in an incomplete state."
Sarkin Killcrane tells you, "We know that these gates guarded some of the scattered pieces of the Crystal Array that were used to contain the essence of the Hopeslayer. It is not so far fetched to think that other similar arrays were created to contain other horrors.
Sarkin Killcrane tells you, "I thank you for your effort, and you should be appropriately rewarded. That is the decree of our Queen."

A report from Darius of the Nexus - HG:

Goal at the end is to get 2 books. An Unreadable Dericost Tome and a Journal of High Archon Kraest.

You turn them it to Sarkin Killcrane in Wei Jhou. He gives you 10% XP for the Tome (which has to be Translated at Zachial) and he gives you the Journal Back.

The Raven Hand are gonna release some creature trapped in a Crystal Prison. Sound Familiar???

Group was Zenoth, Darius of the Nexus, Lithea Sarmad, and Tlosk.

Tlosk got the kill on the High Archon, which dropped nothing significant.

The 2 books are the things to get for this quest.

You give Sarkin Kilcrane Translated Dericost Tome.
Kilcrane gently unlocks the sealed translation and quickly skims the bulk of Kuyiza bint Zayi's translation.
Sarkin Kilcrane tells you, "A Dericost text... Kuyiza always manages to come through on these types of artifacts."
Sarkin Kilcrane tells you, "Judging from his translation, this appears to be an account of some creature. Something powerful and driven by the darkness. The text doesn't do very well to describe it physically, merely stating that this creature could bring out the fear on those it fought."
Sarkin Kilcrane tells you, "It was a beast of grand stature. Those it confronted were felled in the conflict or driven mad with fear."
Sarkin Kilcrane tells you, "If the Raven Hand has managed to release something, or weaken the bonds that hold it, I fear we must act quickly."
Sarkin Kilcrane tells you, "For now, it appears my search is at a halt. Whatever the goal of the Raven Hand on the Singularity Caul, it seems as though they have achieved it."
You've earned 113,283,860 experience.
Sarkin Killcrane gives you Account of the Spear of Mukkir.
Sarkin Killcrane gives you 3 Trade Notes (250,000).

Text: Journal of High Archon Kraest
Text: Account of the Spear of Kukkir
