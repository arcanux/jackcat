 From Mired Hearts.

Throughout the month's event Bleeargh, a dev controlled mosswart, would appear in towns seeking to trade for salvage. His appearance would usually precede one of the Burun attacks. If you spoke with Bleeargh and traded with him, you could receive a Mosswart Thanking Stone along with other random items he may have been carrying. If you then gave the Thanking Stone to Aun Loakhe, you would receive a Diplomat Token for a title!

After completing your trade, Bleeargh would become agitated and warn about the "smellies" coming. Shortly thereafter, a burun invasion would spawn!

If you attacked and killed Bleeargh instead, a global message would spawn: "[town name] is the target of the next burun attack" a few minutes after his death.

Sadly, Bleeargh seldom survived his visits to town or the attacks of the "Smellies". A second stone, the Mosswart Mourning Stone, could be found on his corpse.

A third type of trophy, a Burun Idol, dropped from the leader of the Burun attacks. Its corpse would contain up to 5 of the idols. When given to Aun Loakhe, the idols lost their Dropped on Death property. Currently, their only known use is as a hooked housing item. (All three stones become giveable after they have been turned in to Aun Loakhe.)

Below are two tales of encounters with Bleeargh, plus several stories of the Burun attacks. Those who defeated the leaders of the attacks received a global for their bravery!
