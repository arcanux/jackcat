Arcanum Research Facility

The Arcanum Research Facility is located at 5.8N, 6.3E near Al-Jalima. You may not enter the portal unless you have done the previous steps. Inside you'll encounter Martinate Simulacrum (level 40) and Martinate Simulacrum Magicians (level 44). The Magicians cast level V Vulns and Drain Health IV. Take the door closest to you on the right when you enter, and follow the right wall through a few doors to the final room (3 Simulacrum and 2 Magicians). Collect 10 of the Enchanted Decanters that are lying on the ground. They appear pink in color. Decanters are no drop/no give and do not stack.

After you have 10 Decanters, return to the Emissary of Asheron. When you speak to him, he will give you a text.

Emissary of Asheron tells you, "Yes, Martine has proven to be a formidable foe, when in truth Lord Asheron only wishes to aid him. But there is little that can be done to help him now. Though my Lord has not lost all hope for his redemption."
The being looks you over, and you feel a warm glow surround you briefly.
Emissary of Asheron tells you, "I see that you have collected ten decanters. You have performed most admirable in the face of danger you are a true asset to your Queen. Please, take this."
Emissary of Asheron gives you Treatise on Essence.
Emissary of Asheron tells you, "The information within is too large for me to explain to you. It is better that you are given a hard bound copy so that you may refer to it at later times if the need should arise. Know this, the place that Asheron has opened should be free of danger, though it is not easy to navigate. Yet, if Martine, has found this place it is possible that there will be enemies within. There are forces at work that even Asheron has yet to find."

Essence Font - Filling the Decanters

Next head to the Fathomless Chasm at 36.9S, 63.4E (restricted to levels 20+) to fill your decanters. A quick route is to take the Shou-Zin settlement portal in Shoushi (31.3S, 72.6E). You'll exit at 36.7S, 68.5E. The dungeon is separated into 5 different areas, each level restricted:

Each section has an Essence Font at the bottom where you will fill your decanters. The fonts are guarded by a Boss Golem in each section.

Fathomless Chasm Devex 20-29
--Thanks to Lone Bow of FF for details.

Map: Fathomless Chasm Devex

When you enter the portal, you'll take about 30 or so points of damage on a drop. At the bottom is a safe room. This level consist of Ice Golems, Glacial Golems, and Frosts. Make a right out of the initial room. Follow this to the first hard Left which will lead you down to another room. Go either way out of this room and you'll come to a jump. This jump takes you to the room before the mana wall. After the jump you'll encounter be 2 Frosts, 2 Glacial Golems, and a Shivver. Finally, you'll come to a whole army of Frosts and the boss Golem. The exit is out the middle passage (not the hard left). You'll curve around and around until you reach a surface portal. There are 2 Frosts guarding the exit. Recommend: Magic Resistance, Cold and Bludgeon protects.

Fathomless Chasm Clough 30-39
--Thanks to waferness of LC and Pcmorris of MT for details.

When you enter the portal, you'll take some damage on a fall. Two Fire Elementals await you. You'll come to another jump. At the junction you have the choice of jumping again to the left, or taking a surface portal on the right. Again you'll take damage on the jump. At the bottom you'll find 4 Fire Elementals and about a dozen Magma Golems. Run north, past more Magma Golems, and keep heading down. You'll pass even more Fire Elementals. The Essence Font is in the last room and is guarded by a Magma Golem Exarch. Recommend: Bludgeon, Armor and Fire protects.

Fathomless Chasm Plummet 40-49
--Thanks to Shalone Bloodtide and dsteeldemon of SC for details.

When you enter the portal, you'll take about 40 pts of damage on the fall. Waiting for you are 2 Caustics (Acid Elementals) and an Obsidian Golem. Run past them down the hall, then take your first right. Go through the large room and follow the wall to a ledge. When you jump from the ledge, you'll encounter 4 more Caustics and 2 Obsidian Golems. Once again, exit the room, and take the first right. Follow the curve of the hall and take the first left you see (you'll begin to hear a humming). When you come to the 3rd and final ledge, be prepared before you jump. The room below is filled with about 20-30 Caustics, 2 Obsidians and a Coral Golem Viceroy. The mana stream, a giant blue pillar of energy, is behind the Viceroy. When you kill the Viceroy, all the elementals will die with it. Recommend: Acid, Bludgeon and Armor protects.

Fathomless Chasm Descent 50-70
--Thanks to Lynchet of DT, Shukar Jhusen of TD, Veto of WE and the Southern Cross of DT for details.

Like the lower level sections, you'll take damage on entry from a fall. Ahead of you'll see a doorway. Don't take it, but instead take the doorway to the right. Follow the wall to the left. (This route is longer than necessary, but it's easier to follow and remember.) Before reaching the Essence Font at the bottom, you'll have to take 2 more jumps. The dungeon is filled with Electric Elementals of all types. The boss Golem at the bottom is a level 885 Diamond Golem Suzerian. One tactic is to hit him with Magic Yield / Bafflement / Feeblemind from above and then drain. He can then be Imperiled and Vulned for the attack. Once the boss golem is dead, the elementals will die also. Recommend: Armor, Lightning protects.

Fathomless Chasm Abyss Levels 70+ -- Thanks to Gary V. for details.

Map: Fathomless Chasm Abyss

From the entrance head down the west hallway sticking to the right until you find the abyss portal. After the initial drop, head east, sticking to the right. Take your first jump then head east. Follow the hallway until you come to a split. To the right is a surface portal. To the left is a hallway. Take the hallway passage until you come to your 2nd jump. Continue to your third jump which takes you to the final room. The surface portal is in the SE corner. The Mana pool is directly to the south. The font is guarded by a Crystal Golem Imperator. Recommend: Banes and protects for all elementals, bludgeon and impen.

You may get the message that "Not all the ingredients are present." That means there are too many people trying to fill their decanters at the same time. Just try again until you have collected your mana. Once the Decanters are full, they may be stacked and dropped.

Diamond Infused Ingots

Add Diamond Powder (dropped from Diamond Golems found in the Direlands, Chalimere, Aerlinthe and elsewhere) to the Decanter of Essence for Diamond Oil. Skill check approx: 425-450. Thin Oil is a failure. Thick Oil is a success.

You skillfully add the diamond powder to the essence. Swirling the mixture causes it to become thick.

Use the oil on Pyreal Ingots to make Diamond Infused Ingots. (See the Atlan Weapons quest for details on how to make a Pyreal Ingot.) The Alchemy skill check for the highest quality Ingot is very high. Thick Diamond Oil to the HQ Ingot - 425

A success with Thick Oil will give you a Perfect Ingot. Failure will yield a Superb Ingot. Thin oil success gives a Good Ingot and failure, a Quality Ingot.

[Research on the various skill checks is still on-going. If you have any information to add, please send me an email!]

You apply the diamond oil to the ingot, making a perfect diamond infused ingot.

Head to the Crater (see Shortcuts) and purchase one of the New Glyphs from Silencia. Use the glyph on your ingot then give to Silencia's Magma Golem for your weapon. Buff up first! The Melee skill check is rumored to be 400+ and the Ranged weapons are 350+. The Wand relies on Mana Conversion skill check of 375. Each type of Ingot has a different skill check when you stamp it. For example, the 375 Mana Conversion skill check is for a "Good Ingot", while for a perfect ingot, the check is only 275.

It is possible to get a Perfect Weapon, even without a Perfect Ingot. See the notes from the State of the Code below for more details.

Finished Weapons may not be dropped or given away, and they may not be buffed (a bug is allowing Bows & Crossbows to be buffed until the June '02 patch). The Town Criers give hints to their special properties: they are very effective against elementals.

Town Crier tells you, "Have you seen those new crystalline weapons? My cousin had one. He said that he didn't care for it too much when fighting without an elemental stone."

Town Crier tells you, "My cousin said that the new weapons made from pyreal were nothing spectacular. But then he put a fire stone in his sword and waded in amongst some Shivvers.  Scary, scary stuff.  No ordinary fire sword could have caused that carnage."

View the Isparian Weapons stats page for a list of the weapons and the additional spells given by Atlan Stones. (Black Fire and Shadow Stones may not be added.)

To get the text Isparian Arms, return to Ciandra after filling your decanters. She'll ask you to hand her a full one to verify you have completed the quest and will tell you about the 3 week timer on the quest.

Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist tells you, "I see that you have succeeded in finding the essences and have more than likely crafted yourself the new weapons of Isparian make. Thanks to Asheron and Nuhmudira for their efforts. Unfortunately I cannot allow you passage into the research facility yet. Please wait until you three weeks transpire before you collect your next set of decanters."

Isparian Weapons Modifying Tool

The Modifying Tool is used to remove Altan Stones from the Isparian Weapons.

Combine a Decanter of Essence with Medium Ground Chorizite (see the Potions page) to get Nullification Oil  -- skill check 350.

You skillfully add the chorizite powder to the essence. The mixture bubbles briefly, and settles into a deep purple hue.

Add the Nullification Oil to a Diamond Heart for a Treated Diamond Heart -- skill check 150.

Use an Intricate Carving Tool (see Lockpicks) on the Treated Diamond Heart to make the Modifying Tool -- Lockpick skill check 275.

The final tool may be dropped or given away (corrected in the hot fix). The Intricate Carving Tool is destroyed on use. Stone and Modifying Tool appear to have a high breaking rate. Make sure you buff your Focus before attempting to remove an Atlan Stone.

From the State of the Code:

What are the crafting skill checks needed to create the new Isparian Weapons?

The crafting checks are based off of achievable stats at level 50. They take into consideration buffing as well. There has been some player discussion that places the skill checks well beyond the range of what the average player of Asheron's Call could achieve before level 100. This is simply not the case.

With these weapons we introduced a new dynamic in crafting that would allow a player who obtains the lowest quality initial item, in this case a Pyreal Infused Ingot, to have the chance to create the Perfect final version. The skill checks for those interactions are extremely high.

True Alchemists will be best at making the highest quality of Ingot, as has always been the case, and the skill check is very high. But again, the "best" version of the items is meant to be difficult to obtain.
