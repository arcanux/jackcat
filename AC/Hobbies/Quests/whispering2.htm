From the A Change in Tactics event.

These quest notes are provided as a courtesy until I can do a complete write-up.

Thanks to Death by Fire of WE for the following report: "Hi Maggie, Like to say Hi to my 2 Officers Devil's Angel and AI. I helped the King in the past and started this quest in Sanamar. Basically go from Sanamar to Silyun to Dungeon. Pwn everything. Get Key off Chamberlain (no timer) and open door...run to the Commander, kill him take note and head back to Sanamar."

Garmasi tells you, "Aha! You helped me in the delicate matter the bull, did you? His Majesty has need of your services again. We seek to infiltrate an organization known as the Whispering Blade, which seems to be some gathering of assassins and spies cobbled together by the desperate followers off the Bloodless Queen."
Garmasi tells you, "Bring this note to a Gharu'ndim named Hashina, in the rebel town of Silyun... It is a forgery that should gain you entrance to their base of operations..."
Garmasi tells you, "I want you to enter their secret lair and, by whatever means necessary, obtain their commaner's notes."
Garmasi gives you Whispering Blade Introduction.

You give Hashina Whispering Blade Introduction.
Hashina tells you, "Interesting... Very well, allow me to direct you to the nearest Chapterhouse. It isnot far from here, at 85.4N, 70.4W. Simply present yourself to Commander Jin Zo for further orders."

Dungeon: Silyun Chapterhouse - 85.4N, 70.4W (Levels 80+)
Text: Whispering Blade Introduction
Text: Letter to Commander Jin Zo

You give Garmasi Letter to Commander Jin Zo.
Garmasi tells you, "Splendid work. But you were attacked as you entered the Chapterhouse? Ah, I see, these notes explain much... A fine job, then, of battling through an operation gone wrong. Yes, I do believe you possess the skills and loyalty required to join us..."
You've earned 1,500,000 experience.
Garmasi gives you Rossu Morta Boots.
