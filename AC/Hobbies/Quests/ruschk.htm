From the Throne of Destiny Expansion.

This collection quest allows you to receive a massive amount of experience if you can defeat the final embodiment of the Ruschk god!

To begin, speak to the Ruschk Challenger (92.4N, 47.5W) atop a ridge near the deadly eater valley. From Silyun, you can take the Plains of Ruin portal at 87.9N, 69.7W or run from the Fiun Outpost. You're looking for the broken shadow spire at 93.4N, 44.6W. Behind the spire lies the entrance to the valley at approximately 92.7N, 47.1W. Once through, you'll need to be fast on your feet! You'll want to climb the ridge from the west side at around 93.4N, 49.0W. At the top you'll find the Ruschk Challenger and a Ruschk Totem. Speak to the Challenger and you'll receive the Ruschk Trials.

You feel an icy grip on your heart, as though the Ruschk is reaching inside of your very chest with ethereal hands. The Ruschk opens his mouth; his speech comes out in but a whisper...

You'll be instructed to collect 5 Ruschk Scalps from the high level Ruschk. Ruschk are vulnerable to Bludgeoning and they cast level VI and VII Cold and Piercing vulnerabilities.
