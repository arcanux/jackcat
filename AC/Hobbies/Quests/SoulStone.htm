Begin by speaking to Antius Blackmoor in the Arwic Pub.

Antius Blackmoor tells you, "I am here against my better judgment. I had hoped that we would find an answer to the growing Burun problem, but as of yet, the Arcanum's scholars are not able to unlock the magic necessary to imbue weapons with slaying properties. Thus I am here at the request of High Queen Elysa with a proposition."

Antius Blackmoor tells you, "Nuhmudira was removed from the council some months ago for conduct that was not befitting of her position. No public charges were levied, but she understood that there was an unspoken accusation. I am here to see that she is given a message. That message is one that I must support"

Antius Blackmoor tells you, "Elysa is willing to grant her clemency in return for her aid. We need you to seek her out and obtain the devices necessary for finishing the Noble Weapons. They require the addition of a complex gem. Once that gem is set in place they should become Burun slaying weapons. Nuhmudira was last known to be residing in a remote cottage outside of Uziz near an oasis on the rim of the A'mun desert."
Antius' demeanor changes slightly.
Antius Blackmoor tells you, "Please, if she gives you the opportunity once you have obtained what you will need, ask her a simple question. Ask her if she let Oswald into the castle the night that Elysa was attacked. If you get an answer give that information to one of these guardians: Guard Chandler Hammett in Lytelthorpe, Guard Gabrielle in Rithwic, or Emissary Ydnew in Yanshi. They are three of the four members of the guard that I can absolutely trust. They will reward you for your service. Take great care on your journey and watch out for that monster."

Directions from Sanddh of WE:

Door up ramps again, straight this time. You really NEED 2 people here. Lever to go through door & portal.

At first intersection, go right and immediately left up a ramp. Doors, buttons everywhere. Room. another ramp, left, go up ramp, big room.  Here's the trick:

Before you enter the room, there are two buttons. Open the door to the room, let someone go to NNW corner room, push other button, person 1 goes in, opens door for person 2, and runs to NEE corner room. Person 2 runs to dead end, there's a trigger plate there to open door NEE. Always check if door is opened, otherwise you can start all over.

So: person 1 stands in front to-be-open-door, steps in, opens door for person 2, goes to next door. Person 2 runs to trigger plate, opens next door, runs back to big room. Rinse repeat.
Door sequence: 1. NNW; 2. ENE, 3. WSW, 4. NNE, 5. SSW, 6. WNW, 7. ESE, 8. SSW.

Last trigger plate will open the door in the middle of S wall. Person 1 gets in. To open door for person 2, the button is UP.
To get up, you need to shift jump 3 stepping stones that fade in and out vision the whole time. No worries, they are there all the time. Up there, person 1 opens door for person 2.

Fight your way to the portal. On the other side, over a half-invisible catwalk, is the urn.

Return urn to Nummie, if you never made a choice, you're done. If you chose to damn her, you have one more dungeon.
