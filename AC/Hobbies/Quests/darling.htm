From the Friend and Foe event.

Town Crier tells you, "Do yourself a favor; if you run across a woman named Xsao Lann, stay away. That lady's crazy."

Aw, she's not crazy! Who wouldn't like a level 250 Ursuin for a pet! Xsao Lann can be found at Fort Tethana.

To reach Fort Teth, head to Shoushi and take the Kara portal at 33.1S, 73.2E. Take the Foundry portal at 83.3S, 47.1E. To enter the Fort, talk to the Sentry standing guard inside the gate.

When you visit the Shopkeeper Yoshi Deshi, you'll find that Xsao Lann is offering a reward for her lost pet.

Rumor: Lost Pet

**REWARD**

Lost pet. Responds to the name Darling. If you see her please contact Xsao Lann at 1.5N, 71.6W.

NPC: Xsao Lann - 1.5N, 71.6W

Xsao Lann tells you, "Have you seen my pet? I was walking around 4.6N 89.5W when she disappeared."
Xsao Lann tells you, "Her name is Darling and I would be ever so grateful if you could bring her home to me."
Xsao Lann tells you, "Before you go, I suggest you find some others to help you. I saw lots of scary monsters around there."

Creepy Canyons - 4.6N, 89.5W
--Map: Creepy Canyons
--Levels:180+
--Creatures: Stomper, Darling

The Creepy Canyons is a short run from Fort Tethanan. You'll be fighting Tusker Stompers the entire way through. From the drop, jump below. Follow the corridor until you reach a 2nd drop. Jump below and head SE. You'll find a hidden passage behind a tree in the SE corner that will take you directly to Darling. Darling is a level 250 Ursuin with 5,000 health points and high regen. Kill her and retrieve her Collar.

Return the collar to Xsao Lann in Fort Tethana for 3,7000,000 xp, Darling's Collar and an Uruins Chew Toy!

You give Xsao Lann Darling's Collar.
Xsao Lann tells you, "Oh no! This is horrible. My cherished Darling is dead."
Xsao Lann begins to sob quietly.
Xsao Lann tells you, "I loved her so very, very much."
Xsao Lann suddenly looks up at you.
Xsao Lann tells you, "You said there were lots of Tuskers down there?"

You've earned 3,700,000 experience.
Xsao Lann gives you Darling's Collar.
Xsao Lann gives you Ursuin Toy.
