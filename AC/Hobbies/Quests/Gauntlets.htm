 The Swamp King (a level 90 Mosswart) drops the Gauntlets of Marksmanship as a trophy. Like the other Creature Lords introduced in the Keep Your Enemies Closer event, Swamp Kings can be difficult to find and hard to kill. The approximate drop rate for the gauntlets is about one in four.

Swamp Kings can be found in Vesayen Isles (near Jungle Shadows - the new subway) and in the swampy areas of the Direlands. Swamp Kings are usually accompanied by several other Mosswarts. Don't mistake them for another creature introduced at the same time, the level 81 Mosswart Swamp Lord.

During the Come What Follows event, Belinda du Loc in Stonehold developed the ability to enhance numerous trophy items. Hand your gauntlets to her and she will return the enhanced version. Original pre-patch gauntlets cannot be enhanced.

NPC: Belinda du Loc - 68.9N, 21.6W (Stonehold)
