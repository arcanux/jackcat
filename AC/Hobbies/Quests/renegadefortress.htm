 Renegade Fortress: 28.5S, 65.5W (VoD)
Timer: 28 days
Levels: 60+

Gawa Ibn Edwas of SC reports: "The new fort in the dires features a door which can easily be killed. After killing the door, one will face a horde of tumeroks and lugian. They've set up their defenses pretty tight. There are walls with spikes and catapults to help them. After you entered the fort, you can jump down to the buildings inside. I didn't see a lot of the inside, as I accidentally hit a portal. The dungeon is 60+ restricted and that's where I am writing this email now."

I thought a send you a lil walkthrough to the Renegade Fort Dungeon since the ones I have seen so far around the net are a bit vague and doesn’t help much to find the way in the dungeon (that can be a bit confusing as our fellowship found out the first time we where there). Hope it helps!

Walkthrough, Renegade Fort Dungeon:

Run to the Drunken Mad Man (53S,61E) outside Baishi and give him sake so that he open the Walled Portal. If you have around 400 in jump you can jump over the wall here and run to the Renegade Fort (28.3S, 65.5W) otherwise take the Walled Fort Portal and run from there instead.

Arriving to the Renegade Fort do NOT go close to the walls, they throw tons of nasty stuff on you, so my suggestion is that archers and mages takes out the door to the fort while the melees just stand around and look cute for awhile J

When the door falls run straight in and jump down a little cliff and run in to the portal on the other side of the court. The drop is somewhat safe if no one has taken down the door at the drop. If not, kill the door and take to the right (actually it doesn’t matter if you take left or right here because it will end up at another door).

After you have destroyed the door, head down the little ramp and take a left, follow the corridor and take the ramp directly around the corner when the corridor ends (180 degrees turn) run straight down the little ramp and up on the next one. There take the second ramp down to the right.

Follow it all the way down where you will end up in a room with 2 Elite Guards (18k health and hollow rocks), 1 Tumerok Commander (2k health) and some reavers and mattys.

The commander drops one half of a ring (there is about 6-7 of them on the corpse so all can grab those directly), the green elite guard drop the Renegade Hauberk and the blue one drops the Renegade Leggings. (Only one piece on each lugian). The re-spawn rate is fast so the wait is not long at all.

In the middle of the room is also a table that you can double click on, do that and you will receive 3 notes that will be turned in for exp (1.5M each when turned in to Translator Aun Laokhe in Arwic)

/Greystaff @ TD

As you shuffle through the collection of papers a few documents catch your eye. Perhaps if you showed these to Aun Laokhe he could pass the information on to the High Queen.

Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "They wished to ally with Aerbax? Going back to the atua ngamaru that began all the problems for the Tonk... I am sorry for my brothers. I fear they may be beyond salvation. I will see that Ispar Elysatah is informed of this discovery."
You've earned 1,500,000 experience.

You give Translator Aun Laokhe Orders to Contact the Burun.
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "Orders to establish contact with the race from beyond this world. What plans will our once brothers derive next? I will see that Ispar Elysatah receives this report. Thank you."
