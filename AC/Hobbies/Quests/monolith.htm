Hi Maggie,

Just finished Dark Caverns Quest. Need large group. Gem to dungeon is found on Ravens around Caul. Dungeon is split into 2 two parts. Groups split at start and take the 2 opposing paths. Fairly straight forward. One side is full of Virindi's and Shadows, the other is full of Raven Conscripts. Pillars abound and will hurt you bad. At end of each side is a door, operated from a lever by the other group in thier door room. Pull levers and meet up. Follow around and kill a door and follow path to room with boss. 70k in hp's I think, but he goes down to frost and or slash. We did not try anything else. In center of final room is a platform that has the Monolith and token is from boss's corpse. Monolith spells cast +3 to focus, quick, coord and endurance and lasts for 2 hours.

Pics from the new quest from the gem that the ravens drop. Dark Monolith Cavers.is the name of the quest. Pretty straight forward quest. Gotta split up into two groups at the beginning with the right side being V and shadows, an the left being raven hands. The two groups end up joining up at the end so no worries with the boss. The end boss is a huge fire elemental where slashing and bldg worked out well on him (cold not so well desipte what you woudl think).

Kill him and your fellow gets flagged to use the monolith in the center platform. The loot on him is a title token. If you take the dark monolith shard to ulgrim he will give you 5 million exp. Or you can hook it on a house hook and it is a usable item. It casts 4 spells that last 2 hours each and increase your quick, coord, will, and end by 3 pts.

First to complete it on VT: Rak'than Lograthsun, Minnie the Minx, Ares Blackheart, Some Mage, Powerlite, Compulation, Eagleheart, Morgwyn, Skekis, Talonblade.

Hi Maggie, I just wanted to submit a new quest from his patch.

Its for the Dark Monolith

You start by looking for a Dark Monolith Caverns gem. The Raven creatures at caul drop them.

Afterwards you use them, I'd make sure you have a decent sized fellow there are allot of high level virindi and shadows inside. As well as a few Crystals.

When you come in at the drop the fellow will need to split up into 2 groups, each taking a different hall, I'd suggest killing as you go, otherwise you will get trapped by the critters following you. Each fellow will follow their halls till you come to a door that can only be opened with a lever. The leaver at each opposite door opens the other door. Afterwards follow the hall and the 2 groups will meet up, afterwards it doesn't matter which hall you follow as both come out at the same place. You will come out at a large room filled with magma it will have small magma golems and a Guardian of the Dark monolith, I'd suggest focusing on him, when he dies the smaller golems die also.

Once you've cleared the room there is a Dark Monolith in the center of the room. Everyone needs to double click on it, it will give you a Dark monolith that needs to be hooked to use.

It casts Dark Persistence: +3 Endurance
Dark Reflex +3 Focus
Dark Equilibrium +3 Coord
Dark Purpose +3 Quick

Those that did the quest and need to be included are Fallen God, Ralph II, Brutal-Force jr, Jarbo, Kool-aide, Anarkist, Uck Me, Orahn, Pink-i, Redstar, Dread', and Cps All of the Fallen God monarchy on Darktide

Thank you
