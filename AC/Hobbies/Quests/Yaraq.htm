 The Yaraq Starter quests were updated during The Calm event. The easiest of the starter quests, it involves running from outpost to outpost to deliver notes for some of Yaraq's residents.

There's romance in the air! Nasun ibn Tifar of the North Yaraq Outpost (18.7S, 1.6W) asks your help delivering a note of Congratulations to Mara al-Luq, daughter of Lubziklan. His flowery words betray that his interest might be more than casual. After getting an additional signature from Ahyara at the East Yaraq Outpost (21.4S, 1.2E) and delivering the note to Mara, return a thank you note to Nasun for a small reward. 

Note: To shorten your runs, tie to the lifestone in town, which is halfway between the two outposts!

Nasun ibn Tifar tells you, "My friend Mara al-Luq, the flower of Yaraq, has just received some happy news, and I want to congratulate her."
Nasun ibn Tifar gives you Congratulations.
Nasun ibn Tifar tells you, "Would you have the time to bring this to Ahyara in the East Yaraq Outpost? She is also a friend of Mara's, and would wish to sign this small note as well."

Rumor: Congratulations

Lovely Mara,

This quest certainly gives you plenty of opportunity to see the local countryside as you travel to the East Yaraq Outpost (21.4S, 1.2E) to find Ahyara. (Recall to the lifestone first to shorten your run.) Ahyara gives you back the signed congratulations and asks you to deliver it to Mara in Yaraq proper. She also give you her own congratulations which needs an additional signature before delivery.

Ahyara tells you, "My friend Mara al-Luq in Yaraq has just received some happy news, and I want to congratulate her. Could you rush this to Nasun in the North Yaraq Outpost? He is a bit smitten with Mara, and would wish to sign it first."
Ahyara gives you Congratulations.

Mara al-Luq has moved from her father's residence and now resides in Yaraq at the Cerulian Cove tavern - 21.7S, 1.6W. Mara is also the Yaraq's Society Agent for the Exploration Society's Letter Quests. Recall to the lifestone, then give her the congratulations; she hands you a Thank You note to return to Nasun.

Note: If you return a note before it is signed by both parties, Mara prods you to get the 2nd signature.

You allow Mara al-Luq to examine your Congratulations.
Mara al-Luq tells you, "Oh, how sweet of Nasun! Thank you for bringing this to me! But didn't he as you to take this to Ahyara first?"

You'll really get your exercise completing your deliveries, but it's worth it to see Nasun ibn Tifar's pleasure at hearing from Mara. After you run back to the North Outpost and hand Nasun ibn Tifar the note from Mara, he leaps for joy! You will too when you receive your reward!

Yaraq was formed when the wizard Raqur al-Hayra led a group of Gharu'ndim across the A'mun Desert to a beautiful cove along the coast of the Inner Sea. He plunged his staff into the earth and fresh water burst forth. Alas, Raqur was killed by a Mu-miyah archmage before the town was completed, so he never had the chance to see this charming oasis develop into the thriving town it is today.
