 During The Widening Gyre event, it was discovered that the Virindi were gathering to create a New Singularity. Ecorto the Lost Director, a Virindi who defected from the plan due to a disagreement in method (he'd like a tidier eradication of humans!!), led players to  Levistras, the Speaker of the New Singularity, located in the Seat of the New Singularity. Levistras was defeated, and the plans for a New Singularity thwarted. (Note: Levistras dropped a Shroud of Levistras which is now retired.)

You give Ecorto the Lost Director Fragment of the Singularity.
Ecorto the Lost Director tells you, "This is what is required... I will make you the portal to enter the Seat of the New Singularity. Seek out Levistras and put an end to the renegade faction's disastrous plan. Levistras is vulnerable, because in his ambition he has severed his connection to the home Singularity, before the completion of the New Singularity! Stand back, lest you interfere with my casting..."
Ecorto the Lost Director tesll you, "Strike now, human!"

[Player name] has struck down Levistras, the Speaker of the New Directive! All across Dereth, Virindi recoil as the leader of their movement falls, severed from the Singularity. The other members of the Virindi inner circle go into hiding...
"No! The New Singularity was so close to completion! This is not an acceptable outcome!"

New Singularity Director tells you, "Your kind slew Levistras, meatling, but we will continue to build our New Singularity, against all resistance!"

Others have risen to take Levistras' place. Speak with Ecorto in Ziakhal for instructions on how to gain access to the Seat of the New Singularity. You'll need to create a Fragment of the New Singularity, made by combining three slivers of Singular material obtained by using a chisel on three pillars inside the three Singular Repository dungeons.

Ecorto the Lost Director is found outside the mage shop in Zaikahl at 13.7N, 0.1E. When you speak with him, he will hand you a chisel which you'll use to chip the slivers from the pillars.

Ecorto the Lost Director tells you, "Greetings, human.  Call me Ecorto.  I have disjoined myself from those who plan your eradication because I disagree with their methods.  Their plan to construct a New Singularity in this realm is madness.  Even now they gather and imbue material to construct their monstrous dream.  Find the repositories of the New Singularity in the desert, the southern mountains and the Obsidian Plains, human."
Ecorto the Lost Director gives you Ecorto's Chisel.
Ecorto the Lost Director tells you, "Take this chisel, scrape slivers from the pillars they assemble, and bring the joined elements back to me... Be careful, however.  The Singular material is quite strong, and could break the chisel."

Ecorto the Lost Director says, "I was with the renegades... no use trying to hide that... but I disagree with some of the things they are doing..."
Ecorto the Lost Director says, "If we cannot think of anything quieter and tidier than that... We are not any better than these humans..."
Ecorto the Lost Director says, "Oh, we're not united any more than you are."

Ecorto the Lost Director tells you, "I have given you the instrument already, human. Hurry now!"

Singular Pyreal Repository

4.2S, 8.9E - Levels 24+
Map: Singular Pyreal Repository

--Drudge Raveners, Innocent, Innocuous and Pristine Dolls, plus a Singular Doll

To reach the dungeon, your best bet is to take the west road out of Samsur and follow it through the mountain pass. The portal cannot be summoned. From the entrance, keep heading north. The dungeon is very straightforward. At the bottom of your fourth ramp, you'll hit a T. Turn right, and follow the passage until the final room. Use the chisel to chip some Slivers of Singular Pyreal from the pillar. Note: you will be able to get 1-4 slivers before the chisel will break.

You scrape a sliver of Singular Pyreal from the pillar.

You'll also find a Singular Doll spawning near the pillar. Kill it to receive the Doll Leader's Charm (30 day timer) and a Singular Pyreal Message Shard.

Singular Chorizite Repository

77.3S, 51.6E - Levels 35+
 Map: Singular Chorizite Repository

--Singular Gigas, Extas & Titus Lugian Raiders and a Singular Lugian Warlord, Singular Virindi Servants and Masters.

The second dungeon is a little more difficult. The creatures inside do hollow damage. To reach the Singular Chirizite Repository, the quickest way is from the North Adjamaer Cottages settlement portal in Kara at 83.0S, 48.0E. You'll exit at 76.0S, 53.6E, just a click or two away! The portal cannot be summoned.

Again, the dungeon is extremely easy to navigate. Simply go... north! Use the chisel on the pillars in the final room to retrieve your Slivers of Singular Chorizite.

You scrape a sliver of Singular Chorizite from the pillar.

You can also kill the Singular Lugian Warlord on the landing prior to the final room to receive a Lugian Warlord's Scepter and a Singular Chorizite Message Shard.

Singular Lugian Warlord tells you, "Human weakling! Have you come to plunder our stocks of minerals? You will not leave here alive!"

Singular Obsidian Repository

55.9S, 64.8W - Levels 45+
 Map: Singular Obsidian Repository
Cannot be summoned

--Tumedion Hollow Minions, Tusker Guards and Rampagers, Virindi Puppets, Servants and Masters, plus a Singular Virindi Director.

The third singular dungeon is on the Obsidian Plains. The portal cannot be summoned, so you'll likely be running from Sotiris (68.0S, 69.2W) or BSD (56.6S, 86.0W). Don't try this one alone!! You'll eventually come to a room with a super fast spawn of Rampagers, Virindi and Tumedion Hollow Minions! There's so many of them you won't be able to take a step without trodding on Tusker guts! Turn down your graphics settings to avoid lag.

Like the first two dungeons, the route is not complicated. The rule is keep straight! From the entrance, follow the corridor south. After heading down the first ramp, continue south until the passage turns west. Head down the ramp, then follow the hallway until you're heading north. When you hit the fast spawn room, look for the Singular Virindi Director. It drops the Obsidian Director's Mask and a Singular Obsidian Message Shard.

New Singularity Director tells you, "Your kind slew Levistras, meatling, but we will continue to build our New Singularity, against all resistance!""

The following room is the pillar room; you'll see them on a ledge above you. To reach them, go through the room until you reach the 4 way intersection. Take either the east or west passage and head up the ramp. Use the chisel on the pillars to retrieve your slivers.

You scrape a sliver of Singular Obisidian from the pillar.

The exit portal is further down the hallway north in the room to the west.

Seat of the New Singularity

Once you have all three slivers, you can combine them first into a Singularity Calyx, then next into the Fragment of the New Singularity.

You coil together the slivers of Singular Chorizite and Singular Pyreal.
You coil the sliver of Singular Obsidian about the Singularity Calyx.

When your party is ready, give the Fragment to Ecorto the Lost Director in Zaikhal and he will open a portal to the Seat of the New Singularity (cannot be summoned).

Levels 55+
Map: Seat of the New Singularity

--Terebrous & Tumideon Hollow Minions, Tusker Guards and Rampagers, Virindi Masters, Directors and Executors, plus the New Singularity Director.

Like the Repository dungeons, the Seat of the New Singularity is mostly linear. The initial drop is safe for buffing. You'll be heading north, north, then north again! Initially, you'll encounter Tusker Guards and Virindi Masters. As you go deeper in, you'll start fighting Rampagers and Directors. Eventually you'll come to a ledge with a pit below containing more Rampagers, Directors and Tumideon and Terebrous Hollow Minions.

Jump into the pit, then continue north over the lava until you reach the final room. The New Singularity Director is surrounded by more Hollows and Rampagers, as well as Executors. The New Singularity Director drops the Virindi Shroud.

Return to Zaikhal to have your message shards translated by Diyas al-Yat.

Note: Using the Fragment of the Singularity is an excellent shortcut to complete the Caul Recall Scroll quest!
