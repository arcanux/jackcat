Fairouz of MT sends the following:
Fight your way through the renegade Tumeroks and Lugians in the dungeon maze, heading east and south. When you reach a hallway heading southeast, you'll come upon a room with signs of a fresh slaughter. Tumerok, Lugian and Olthoi corpses litter the floor, accompanied by a few live bugs.

A Reinforced Door blocks the path out of the room. Attack the door with melee, missile or magical attacks. Unlike the Gaerlan's Quest door, the Reinforced Door doesn't seem to have much ability to regenerate and will go down after approximately 2000 points of damage.

You flatten Reinforced Door's body with the force of your assault!

Beyond the door, continue fighting through Olthoi. Eventually, you will encounter Swarm Olthoi, including a Royal Olthoi Guardian around a locked Olthoi Door (Resistance: 999). The Royal Olthoi Guardian carries a Smelly Olthoi Gland, which may be used to unlock the Olthoi Door.

After clearing the ramp behind the Olthoi Door, you can head either north or south at the intersection to reach a pit. Jump down and head through the passageway. Eventually, you will encounter a second pit.

After hopping into the second pit, you'll eventually encounter an intersection heading to the northeast and northwest. Take either direction to find more bugs, including a pair of Olthoi Keepers and a Young Olthoi Queen. Defeating the Queen opens a door.

The Young Olthoi Queen screams and falls. A acrid smell fills the room and the door on the far wall squelches open.
The thunder of crushing Young Olthoi Queen is followed by the deafening silence of death!

Before moving on, make sure to loot an Olthoi Chitin from her corpse (28 day timer). She carries 15 sets of Chitin on her enough for an entire fellowship.

Head through the door, down a ramp and kill a few grubs in the final room. At first glance, an acid pool dominates the room. Upon further investigation, the pool is actually a Pool of Goo. Use the pool.

Notes from Andon of MT:

The dungeon starts off looking like the Lugian Citadel. Lots of Lugians and Tumeroks in little alcoves. Once you reach the Reinforced Door, you begin to see Olthoi (and casualties on both sides). Past that point, the dungeon becomes a sandstone walled passage filled with weaker Olthoi (drones, soldiers, few nobles). At the end of this portion, you kill the Royal Olthoi Guardian and grab its Smelly Olthoi Gland to open a green "olthoi door" - use it just like a key.

Past this point the scenery changes into the standard Olthoi decor, with Swarm Olthoi (Evicerators, Nobles, and Warriors) all over the place. Every couple of rooms would be filled with 20-30 Swarm olthoi. I didn't have much difficulty navigating the way since there weren't many dead ends beyond all the small side rooms. After killing a rather large swarm, I ended up in a vast purple room with a pit in the center. Jump down the pit and head down the only passageway. Later you'll end up in another pit room - again, jump down and continue. The room with the Young Queen was a V shape, effectively trapping up the olthoi while I replenished myself. She had two Olthoi Keepers and a handful of other protectors. I ended up finding a Sturdy Steel Key on one of the Keepers.

Grab the Olthoi Chitin off the body of the Young Queen (There were more than a dozen of them). At this point you'll see the message that the door she was guarding has opened. Through the door are several grubs guarding a dead olthoi body, which shows up as "Pool of Goo". Double click it to receive Royal Olthoi Jelly. You can then Portal Recall to return to the beginning of the dungeon and give Maloth the chitin for the XP (5% of your level cost), Mace, and Pendant. With the Pendant worn, you will not have to pay an M note to enter the dungeon any further.

Note: Since Nuhmudira is already gone, I didn't have a use for the Jelly. Sadly, I didn't not think to take a picture of the chitin or try to pick up more than one.

Hope it helps,
- Andon of MT
