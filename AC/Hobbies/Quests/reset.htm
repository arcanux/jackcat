From the Down Twisting Paths event.

The following notes are provided as a courtesy until I can research a full write-up.

NPC: Giordonni, Plumber - 72.1N, 61.4W
NPC: Lydda - 59.7S, 88.0W

Absolution of FF reports: "Hi Maggie. Seems there are 2 NPC's that will allow you to reset your loyalty. Lydda in Ayan if you are aligned with King Varricci and Giordonni if you are aligned with Silyun or Elysa. Not sure if the dungeons are the same for both as I was aligned with Silyun. I wanted to reset so I could go back and get my titles =).

The dungeon Giordonni sends you to is at 34.1N 50.6W level 60+. There are sclavus all over from Faisi to a few Lords. There are 2 pieces of a crucible you need just head down til you see the port then go right for one piece and back left for the other. There are 2 bosses who each drop a key to their specific chest. Combine the Crucible pieces and give to Giordonni and your loyalty is reset =)"

Skeksis of VT reports:

Talk to Lydda in Ayan Baqur. She is by Ulgrim.

Lydda tells you, "I see that you have been bold in your service for King Varicci. If you regret what you have done, and wish to take service under Queen Elysa, my associates and I can help you. We can arrange to have all evidence of you... transgressions disappear."
Lydda tells you, "But first you must do something for me. I seek an artifact, a Ruschk funerary fetish, from the Ruschk burial mound at 91.5 N, 68.8 W. Bring me the artifact, and your record will be wiped clean."
Lydda tells you, "Be warned, this is a very difficult and risky operation, so neither I nor my associates will do this for you again. We will only help you change your allegiance once."

Run from Silyun to the coords Lydda gave you. Level restriction is 60+. Inside you will encounter Vile Ruschk, Ruschk Warlords and the Ruschk Crypt Guardian. The Crypt Guardian drops an Inner Burial Chamber Portal Sending Gem. Using the gem sends you to the next area.

Inside the Inner Burial Chamber (very small dungeon), you will encounter Ruschk Warlords and the Ruschk Grave Keeper. Kill him and loot the Ruschk Funerary Fetish.

Return to Lydda in Ayan to finish the quest.

You give Lydda Ruschk Funerary Fetish.
Lydda tells you, "Excellent work. I will make sure that the royal archives no longer have any trace of your cooperation with King Varicci. However, this does not mean the Queen's agents or the Grand Mother will trust you. You will have to earn their trust."
