 From Pillars Made of Sand.

Throughout the month, Royal Guard Gabrielle in Rithwic (10.5N, 58.2E) would change messages giving news of new Renegade Strongholds. A third Renegade Chest was discovered near Sawato, opened by a key located in a new Renegade Stronghold outside of Shoushi. It yields a Chorizite Veined Shield as a reward!

Royal Guard Gabrielle tells you, "Greetings. You're arrival is providence. I've been informed of a cluster of Renegades that have found their way to southern edge of the Blackmire Swamp. We need to keep them away from the Burun. If the items that Aun Laokhe found last month are any indication, the Renegades mean to form an alliance with these Burun invaders."
Royal Guard Gabrielle tells you, "If you're also up to the task, there have been reports of Renegades in the swamp east of Yanshi heading toward Dryreach, near Fadsahil's shop in the A'mun desert and in the Direlands swamp."

To reach A Renegade Stronghold, take the Shou-Zin settlement portal from Shoushi at 31.3S, 72.6E and run to the dungeon at 34.4S, 64.8E. The entrance is inside one of the buildings of the Sho Outpost.

Map: A Renegade Stronghold

Creatures: Ascendant Tumerok, Tumerok Champion, Tumerok High Priest, Gotrok Extas, Gotrok Fortress Guard, Gotrok Tiatus

From the drop, keep to the left all the way down. When you reach the room with the structure, take the passage down to the final room. a Strong Iron Key rests on a table (7 day timer). The inhabitants of the dungeon use Hollow Weapons so wear your armor!

Gotrok Fortress Guard says, "Sound the alarm! We're under attack!"
Gotrok Fortress Guard tells you, "The ridicule of this death will never end. Why could you not be a drudge instead?"

Once you have your key, recall to the entrance and run to 31.3S 58.9E (or near by). You'll find a Renegade Chest guarded by Gotrok Montok, Gotrok Gigas and a Tumerok Major.

Use the key on the chest to open it. Be careful! The chest casts Flame Bolt VI when unlocked! Inside the chest you'll find a Chorizite Veined Shield, a Refined Chorizite Chunk (use a mortar and pestle to create 20 chunks to make potions), and a note titled Chorizite Ore.

Text: Chorizite Ore
