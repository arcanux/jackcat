From the Remembering the Past event.

The following notes are provided as a courtesy until I can research a full write-up.

Thanks to Angeni of HG for details below:

Town Crier tells you, "I have heard that there is some mysterious entity in the swamps of Blackmire called the Soul Hunter who may be involved with Asheron's long absence from Dereth... There is some kind of tracker in Sawato who is investigating that."

Hasin Lin can be found in Sawato

Hasin Lin tells you, "I would ask of you a favor, friend. I have received information that the one who protects us all is in some mortal peril himself. I'd rather not say more now. I'd just ask that you head into Blackmire Swamp and see if there is any strange Falatacot-related activity in the vicinity of 28 S, 56 E. Please go there, seek out a being called the Soul Hunter, and bring me back anything you find."

Soul Hunters Lair level 60+ at 27.7S, 55.8E.
Faisi Deciples in it (sclavus)
Go North

Pick up sacrifical knife and goblet then use knife on goblet.

You have cut yourself with the sacrificial knife and filled the goblet with your own blood.
You give Altar of Urdhinivix Filled Sacrificial Goblet.
The altar is pleased with your offering, and you feel an unsettling sense of cold infusing your heart. You sink to your knees, compelled by a force you cannot name.

Portal 60+ Soul Hutner's Chambers.

Kill the Soul Hunter and loot the Soul Hunter's Untranslater Orders on him.. go North then East.

You allow Hasin Lin to examine your Soul Hunter's Untranslated Orders.
Hasin Lin tells you, "I can't do anything with this. The cuneiform is certainly of the Falatacot script, but I haven't learned to read that yet. Please get it translated for me. I believe Fanzen San, the translator based near Hebian-To, would know what to make of this."

You give Fanzen San the Translator Soul Hunter's Untranslated Orders.
Fanzen San the Translator tells you, "What's this now? Oh mercy! What terrible things this note portends! Where did you get this? Never mind, it's not my purpose... Here, take this translation, and bring it to the proper authorities!"
Fanzen San the Translator gives you Soul Hunter's Orders.

Hasin Lin tells you, "Oh my. Just as we feared. I will have to report back to the Queen with this dreadful news. Thank you for your help."
You've earned 2,078,355 experience.
Hasin Lin gives you Copy of Soul Hunter's Orders.

Idyll of FF sends these details:

Hiya Maggie!
The Soul Hunter quest begins at Sawato - 28,9E / 59,3E to be exact - by talking to Hasin Lin. No talking to him = no flagging for the dungeon! :P

The Soul Hunter's Lair is at 27,8S / 55,7E and lvl 60+. From the entrance, keep going straight until you hit an altar. There are 2 ways of getting to the Soul Hunter:

1. Pick up the sacrificial knife, pick up the goblet, use the knife on the goblet and give it to the altar (using doesn't work). By doing so you sacrifice some blood of yourself and are flagged to enter the portal, which is a shortcut straight to the Hunter. Or, you take the long route

2. and head through the other corridors in the room with the altar (longer route)
either way, you end up at the Soul Hunter. Give the untranslated orders to fran sen (sp?), the translator at hebian, and bring them back to Hasin Lin to receive 2,xxx,xxx xp.

Thats it :P I guess the quest flags you for a quest added next month. No clue yet inhowfar sacrificing / not sacrificing affects the future.

Sacrificial Goblet

Collect ritual blood with this goblet.
