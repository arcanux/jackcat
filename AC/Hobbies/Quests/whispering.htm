From the A Change in Tactics event.

These quest notes are provided as a courtesy until I can do a complete write-up.

Thanks to Ophar Kabitaki for the following report: "This Quest is for 80+ and apparently you had to have aided the Silyun rebels by completing the Grand Mother quest to be assigned it. Talk to Mehkmet the miner in AB to start it.  Dungeon is a short run south of AB. Running through appears to just be stay left. Kill an Ore Collector to take his hammer, run on to end and use hammer on the deposit to get the ore. Take it to Mehkmet to get 1.5 mill xp and the boots. :)"

Thanks also to Yenohp of TD!
