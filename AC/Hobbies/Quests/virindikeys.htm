From Spring's Sorrow.

Text: Carving Keys and Keyring

This element of Lockpicking love was introduced during the Spring's Sorrow event. Three types of keys began dropping from various Virindi and Hollow Minions. Those trained in lockpicking were able to carve the keys using an Intricate Carving Tool. Buff your skill before attempting to the carve the key. The higher your skill, the better your chance at success. If you fail, the key is destroyed! (See the tables to the right for difficulty). Once carved, keys may be given or placed on Keyrings.

Each type of key opens a different type of chest. The Master Key opens a Master's Holding; the Directive Key opens a Director's Cache; and the most valuable key, the Singularity Key, opens a Singularity Trove. All three types of chests spawn in the Direlands, often near the beaches. The rewards have been compared to SIK loot, and loot from the Singularity Trove is considered to be the best loot in the game!
