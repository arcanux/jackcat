Text: Carving Keys and Keyring

Keyrings can now be carved out of Golem Hearts to hold different types of keys. Yay for more pack space! Use an Intricate Carving Tool on a Golem Heart to create the keyrings (see skill difficulty and heart type at tables to the right).

You carve the golem heart into a crude keyring.

Double-click a keyring and use it on a key to add a key to the ring. Use an Intricate Carving Tool on the keyring to pop a key off again. Keyrings may be given.

Note: Although keyrings have 50 uses, they can only hold 24 keys at a time.

 

Intricate Carving Tool
A multipurpose tool used to sculpt golem hearts and virindi artifacts.
Value 10000
Burden 40

Master Keyring
A crude keyring roughly carved out of a [sic] iron golem heart.
Use this ring on a master key to add the key to the ring. Use an intricate carving took on the keyring to pop a key off again. Adding a key uses up one of the ring's remaining uses, but removing a key does not.
Value 10
