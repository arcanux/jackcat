From the Hidden Vein event.

Originally thought to be a gift from the Arcanum, the Bronze Statues proved not to be protective, but a threat! Nuhmudira's sacrifices had given them life! Once they gained sentience, they embarked on a series of deadly town attacks.

During the Hidden Vein event Bhab, the Arcanum Tinkerer, spoke of how the statues could be defeated. He suggested looking underneath them for the statue workshop and destroying the native cast -- the mold from which they were being created. Bhab provided an Etheric Seal to assist in gaining access.

Bhab, Arcanum Tinkerer - 41.6S, 15.8E

Bhab can be found at Xarabuydun. To reach the Consortium, take the Al-Arqas portal from Yaraq at 22.8S, 0.7W then take the Monument Sands settlement portal at 33.6Sm 13.2E.

Bhab, Arcanum Tinkerer tells you, "The Arcanum's been quite busy studying all the bronze weaponry found on those crazy statues. We were hoping that if we found out how they were made, we'd be able to figure out how to destroy them. And we were in quite a pickle, I tell you! Turns out we were barking up the wrong tree all along. Asheron stopped by our workshop one day and gave us a little talking to. Told us a little bit about Empyrean transmutation magic from the old days of yore! Here's the secret--Asheron taught us how to create this thinga-ma-bob called an Etheric Seal. You plant it in a statue's pedestal, and it'll do a number on the stone--try it and you'll see!"
Bhab, Arcanum Tinkerer gives you Etheric Seal.
The Arcanum Tinkerer wiggles his eyebrows at you.
Bhab, Arcanum Tinkerer tells you, "I'll bet there's more than meets the eye underneath those statues--maybe it'll even lead to the place where they're being created! What's more, I've got a little theory I've been working on. These statues...well, there must be an original from which they've been molded, right? If you found the native cast and managed to destroy it...I'll bet you it'll have some kind of effect on the rest. If you find anything in there, bring it to me for a looksee--I can't get enough of tinkering!"

Sure enough, as the townspeople fought the statue menace, they discovered a weak spot at the base of the statue pedestals. By using the Etheric Seal on the weak spot, players gained access to the workshops below!

The dungeons were divided into 4 different foundry types, each with their own level restrictions. As the lowest level of Native Cast was defeated, the next level foundry would open up in retaliation. In addition the most difficult foundry, the Colossus Foundry, required players to be a Player Killer in order to enter. Once players found and killed the Native Casts, they received a global announcing the nullification of the statue! A bronze statue mechanism was also found on its corpse.

Currently, there are no level restrictions on the four Foundries and an etheric seal is no longer required. Simply double click the Pedestal Weak Spot to gain access to the Foundry portal. Anyone can go inside and fight the Bronze Statues within to obtain a Bronze Mechanism. When you turn them into Arcanum Researchers, you'll receive an xp reward. Turning them into Bhab, Arcanum Tinkerer yields a small Decorative Statue, a miniature of the one-time Commerative Statues. Each Bronze part is on a 24 hour timer. There's a 7 day timer on getting duplicate decorative statues.

Note: The timer applies to the bronze statue type, not to the name of the bronze mechanism. For example, you can pick up a Bronze Gear from the Drudge and a Bronze Gear from a Gromnie within the same 24 hour period, but you cannot pick up 2 Bronze Gears from Drudges.

Most of the Statues are vulnerable to Acid attacks. You'll also be hit with War Attacks in the higher level foundries, so be prepared!

The Castling event brought changes to the Bronze Statues. Time and the elements did their work and all Bronze Statues became oxidized, appearing a blue-green in color.
