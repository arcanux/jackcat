Town Crier tells you, "I've heard tell that a group of adventurers unearthed a temple filled with mu-miyah and other undead."

The Resting Place gem is another of the rare drop portal gems. This quest was introduced during the Treaties in Stone event. The gems are dropped by Phantasms.

When you use the gem, you'll open a portal to The Resting Place (no level restriction, but recommended for levels 60-80). Inside you'll be fighting Mu-Miyah -- Guardian, Nomad, Imperial, Royal and Dessicated; and Undead -- Great Revenants, Dark Masters, Phantasms, and the Ward of the Retreat.

The Ward of the Retreat drops 5 Grave Robber Title Tokens which can be exchanged for the Grave Robber Title. You can also retrieve a hookable coffin and a hookable Map of Auberean.

The dungeon itself contains several levers. Lepomis of SC sends directions:

The first lever is in one of the middle rooms. Follow the main hallway north about halfway, then turn left. The first lever opens the door in the SW corner. Behind that door is a path down to the second lever which opens the north door. Behind the north door is the skull and coffin room and the third lever. The third lever opens the door in the SE corner. Behind that door is the path down to the map room.

Ward of the Retreat says, "It cannot be! The interlopers invade our halls, disturbing our sleep. I have failed..."

You'll also find a Runed Chest (no lockpick required) with a 7 day timer. The Map of Auberean spawns on the floor and is on a 14 day timer. When you open your hooked coffin, it emits a billow of smoke and skull images.
