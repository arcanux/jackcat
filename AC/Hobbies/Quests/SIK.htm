Sturdy Iron Keys, or SIKs, are dropped by all kinds of high level creatures: Golems, Drudges, Lugians, Wasps, Elementals, Olthoi, Shadows, Skeletons, Rats, Virindi and more! (See the Trophies section for a full list.)

The keys are used to open Steel Chests found throughout Dereth. Permanent SIK chests are found in dungeons. The chests contain valuable loot of varying types and degrees. Other chests are found randomly spawning on the Obsidian Plain and hold Level VII spell scrolls. (See Ker-Lin's Guide to Finding War Scrolls for some helpful tips.)

Outside of dungeons, the chests are found in the Direlands outside of the VoD. You'll usually find them near Virindi spawns and Tusker Slaves and Guards spawns. Among the reported locations are the South Dires Landbridge portal drop, near the Beach Fort, and around the Black Hills.

During the Mired Hearts event, an NPC in the Tusker Assault dungeon on Aphus Lassel (3.9S, 84.2E) began giving rewards in exchange for SIKs. See the Staff of the Painbringer quest for details.

Below are a list of dungeons with SIK chests.

Click on the dungeon name for more information, including a link to the dungeon shortcut!
 

Black Spawn Den
Ayan Baqur - 56.7S, 86.0W
Wai Jhou - 60.0S, 57.7W
Fort Tethana - 13.7N, 79.9W
Levels: 20+
Map: Black Spawn Den
Creatures: Drudge Ravener, Drudge Stalker, Tusker Guard, Tusker Slave, Virindi Master, Virindi Servant

Directions: Head north then take the east ramp down. Continue straight ahead south until you reach the chest room.
 

Crater Caves
66.7N, 12.5E - Levels all
Map: Crater Caves
Creatures: Cursed Wisp, Flamma, Flare, Inferno, Magma Golem, Mist Golem, Nubilous Golem, Smithing Golem (NPC)

Directions: From the drop take any of the ramps down to the first room. Take the north passage and make a left at the T. Head north until you go down a ramp and enter a small circular room. Follow the passage east until the 4 way intersection. Take a right down the ramp then go left at the T heading north down a ramp. Keep to the right until the final room. The chest is in the south part of the final room past the lava pit. Be careful: the heat will do fire damage! The SIK chest is the middle of the 3 chests in the room.
 

Desert Mine
4.6S, 26.6E - Levels All
Map: Desert Mine
Creatures: Gray Rat, Lich, Skeleton, Skeleton Captain, Skeleton Lord, Skeleton Warrior

Directions: From the entrance go right heading south then right again down a ramp. At the bottom, go left through the locked door (dir 124), down a ramp and through another locked door (dif 162), then east to another locked door (dif 122). To the north is the final locked door (dif 402). The chest is to the left on the other side of the door.
 

Direlands Temple
80.5S, 71.4W
Creatures: Primeval Skeleton, Skeletal Champion, Skeletal Hero

Disaster Maze
61.3S, 51.0W - Levels 20+
Map: Disaster Maze
Creatures: Drudge Lurker, Drudge Prowler, Drudge Ravener, Drudge Sneaker, Drudge Stalker, Female Tusker, Goldenback Tusker, Male Tusker, Mountain Rat, Silver Rat, Tusker, Crimsonback, Tusker Guard, Tusker Slave, Virindi Director, Virindi Master, Virindi Puppet, Virindi Servant

Directions: 1) From the drop head west and go up and around the ramp. Take a right a the the T and go through the locked door (dif 124). Head west and take the 2nd corridor on the right. The SIK chest is in the far NW corner. 2) & 3) From the entrance, find the door on the south wall (guarded by magic trap). Once through the door keep to the south until you find the locked door (dif 400). Hug the left wall until the final room. Two SIK chest are in the NE corner next to a Slimy Note.
 

Dungeon of Corpses
56.1S, 49.6W - Levels 25+
Map: Dungeon of Corpses
Creatures: Drudge Lurker, Drudge Ravener, Drudge Stalker, Virindi Servant

Directions: 1) & 2)

Halls of Metos
Ayan Baqur - 56.0S, 89.2W
Tufa - 12.9S, 5.8E
Wai Jhou - 61.3S, 46.7W
Levels 15+
Map: Halls of Metos
Creatures: Granite Golem, Iron Golem, Lich, Lich Lord, Obsidian Golem, Revenant, Zombie

Directions: Go north from the entrance and make a left at the T. Take the first right and jump across the acid pit. The SIK chest is in a room at the end of the passage guarded by golems.

Heart of Innocence
around 34.0N, 39.0 - Levels 60+
Map: Heart of Innocence
Creatures: Adroth Salson, Bandit Lord, Bandit Mage, Den-Ru Chang, Itala the Knife, Li Fanli, Mercenary, Rogue, Rogue Mage, Thief, Torgrym the Magnificent

Directions: The SIK chest is to the right at the top of the ramp in the room with Jaleh's corpse. See the Heart of Innocence quest write-up for details.

Leikotha's Crypt
10.2S, 31.3E - Levels all
Creatures: Greater Mu-Miyah, Leikotha (NPC)

Shortcut: Take the Samsur portal from Yaraq at 23.0S, 0.2W, then take the Yinar settlement portal at 0.8S, 18.9E. You'll exit 9.5S, 33.3E, a short run from the crypt.

Lich Tower
52.6S, 68.6E - Levels all
Creatures: Lich, Lich Lord, Obsidian Golem, Revenant

Shortcut: Take the Baishi Foundry portal from Arwic at 33.6N, 56.7E, then take the Lin Kiln Park settlement portal at 50.2S, 63.4E. Run to the Lich Tower at 52.6S, 68.6E. You may have to jump down to the tower landing from the side of the mountain.

Lightless Catacombs
35.9S, 47.3W - Levels 14+
Map: Lightless Catacombs
Creatures: Harrower Grievver, Pandemonium Shadow, Panumbris Shadow, Tenebrous Shadow, Umbris Shadow

Directions: The SIK chest is in the final room along with the chest for the 2nd half of the Staff of the Nomads. See the quest write-up for details.
 

Lugian Citadels
Hills Citadel - 56.6S, 66.9E
Level: 15+
Map: Hills Citadel
Creatures: Gotrok Gigas, Gotrok Lithos, Gotrok Obeloth

Wilderness Citadel - 67.9S, 62.5E
Level: all
Map: Wilderness Citadel
Creatures: Extas Raider, Gotrok Extas, Gotrok Montok, Gotrok Tiatus

Ridge Citadel - 70.2S, 24.2E
Levels: 40+
Map: Ridge Citadel
Creatures: Extas Raider, Gotrok Extas, Gotrok Montok, Gotrok Tiatus

Directions: From the drop, keep to your right until you reach your third open room. There will be a ramp to the west heading up and a ramp to the east heading down. Take the ramp down to the east and the chest is in an alcove in a room at the bottom.

Mage Academy
74.7N, 18.5E - Levels 8+
Map: Mage Academy
Creatures: Banderling Breeder, Banderling Captain, Banderling Guard, Banderling Raider, Banderling Raver, Banderling Scout, Copper Golem, Dark Wisp, Ghost Wisp, Granite Golem, Ice Golem, Iron Golem, Jibrit Zefir, K'nath N'gell, K'nath Nosaj, Kilif Zefir, Lich Lord, Lord Rytheran, Obsidian Golem, Sandstone Golem, Shadow, Shadow Child, Skeleton Captain, Skeleton Warrior, Swamp Rat, Water Golem, Wood Golem, Young Banderling, Zofrit Zefir

Directions: The SIK chest is in Lord Rytheran's Library. See the Mage Academy quest write-up for details.

Mines of Despair
74.7N, 18.5E - Levels 14+
Map: Mines of Despair
Map: Beyond the Mines of Despair
Creatures: Accursed Miner, Ethereal Wisp, Greater Mu-Miyah, Lesser Mu-Miyah, Lich, Lich Lord, Lich Overseer, Pile O' Bones, Skeleton Captain, Skeleton Warrior, Undead, Decaying Warrior, Fleshless Warrior, Iron Golem, Obsidian Golem, Skeleton Captain, Skeleton Lord, Unstable Magma Golem

Directions: The SIK chests are across the bridge in the Beyond the Mines of Despair section. See the Focusing Stone quest write-up for details.

Mount Lethe Magma Tubes
33.9S, 85.2W - Levels 20+
Map: Mount Lethe Magma Tubes
Creatures: Altered Drudge, Augmented Drudge, Charred Miner, Ember, Foreman Brelax, Hellfire, Inferno, Lord Cambarth, Magma Golem, Obsidian Golem, Seared Guard, Shadow Wisp, Virindi Observer, Virindi Overseer

Directions: The SIK chest is guarded by a Magma Golem next to a lever. See the Mount Lethe quest write-up for details.

Nevius Passage
45.6S, 21.6E - Levels 8+
Map: Nevius Passage
Creatures: Adult Reedshark, Reedshark Elder, Reedshark Pup, Silver Rat, Swamp Rat, Veteran Reedshark

Directions: From the drop head west and go through the door. Take an immediate left and follow the corridor to the T. Make a right and at the next T make a left through the door. Follow the corridor and head west through the open room. Make a left at the split and head up the ramp. When you reach the large room, look for the door on the west wall. Once on the other side, jump into the pit. The SIK chest is at the bottom guarded by rats and a fire mist. You'll also find a portal to the North Desert Edge.

Olthoi Horde Nest
Colier - 55.8N, 45.3E
Bandit Castle - 64.3N, 52.8E
Mountain Retreat - 48.9N, 12.9W
Levels 30+
Map: Olthoi Horde Nest
Creatures: Olthoi Eviscerator, Olthoi Noble, Olthoi Soldier, Olthoi Worker

Directions: Head north from the drop.

The Pit
64.3N, 23.4W - Levels 60+
Map: The Pit
Creatures: Armored Skeleton, Bloodthirsty Monouga, Direland Rat, Lich, Merciless Monouga, Phantasm, Pugnacious Monouga, Revenant, Skeletal Hero

Directions: 1) Take the ramp down from the east corridor. Hug the left wall all the way down to the bottom of the pit. Look for the locked door in one of the small rooms to the SW (dif 332). The first SIK chest is behind the door. 2) The second SIK chest is almost to the bottom of the dungeon on the level above the exit portal. It's in a room to the NW guarded by undead.

Swamp Temple
23.4S, 47.9E - Levels 10+
Map: Swamp Temple
Creatures: Aste Sclavus, Barker Mosswart, Essa Sclavus, Limestone Golem, Mosswart Feeder, Mosswart Shaman, Mudlurk Mosswart, Sandstone Golem, Se Sclavus

Directions: First get the Grotesquely Carved Key from the Swamp Temple near Yanshi 23.4S, 47.9E to unlock the door to the east (or dif 500). The chest in the final room among some unlocked chests.

Tainted Grotto
17.8S, 50.6E - all
Map: Tainted Grotto
Creatures: Burun Ruuk Fiend, Burun Ruuk Lout, Burun Ruuk Seer, Burun Ruuk Soothsayer

Directions: Take the passage to the north directly after going down the second sloping ramp in the large center room. Keep to the right until you reach a small room with 2 chests. This dungeon also contains a Sturdy Steel Chest.

80.0N, 45.6W - Levels all
Map: Trial of the Vagabond
Creatures: N/A
