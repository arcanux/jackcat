They're coming to get you!! That's what we've been told and the changes to the Olthoi dungeons during the Heart of Woe patch may mean it will be sooner than we think! The Olthoi of Dereth have been busy underground. Digging deeper through the earth, they've connected the 7 Olthoi Dungeons with a series of Deeper Catacombs. Beyond the Deeper Catacombs you'll find that all the subterranean passages connect into an even more dangerous spawn area, the Catacombs of Opposition.

Nearby, a group of Isparians have made their home after becoming trapped long ago during a hunting expedition. They fight the Olthoi while surviving under primitive conditions.

Hunt in the passages of the Catacombs of Opposition for 4 new Olthoi Trophies. Bring them back to the Olthoi Town and the inhabitants will craft 4 different weapons for you: a Katar, an Atlatl, an Axe or a Spear. At the entrance to each of the 7 Deeper Catacombs, you'll find a Note from a Scout. A final Note can be obtained in the Olthoi Town. Bring these back to the Royal Guard in Cragstone for a reward of xp!

To begin, speak to the Royal Guard In Cragstone at 25.6N, 48.8E. He'll tell you about the scouts who went missing after investigating the Olthoi activities.

Royal Guard tells you, "Hail, adventurer! There are dire events unfolding beneath the soil of Dereth and Queen Elysa requires your aid in uncovering the details. This is of great import and has delayed the Alliance she has strived to form. The Queen had dispatched scouts to a number of Olthoi lairs but they have gone missing. If you were able to go investigate these lairs you would have the Queen's gratitude."
Royal Guard tells you, "The scouts were ordered to leave documentation of their findings as they progressed- if they have been captured we might yet find these. If you should come upon any documents or notes the scouts had left please bring them to me and I shall reward you."

You can start the quest from any of the 7 Olthoi Dungeons. Each scout note from the various dungeons will yield a different amount of xp. All 7 dungeons have their own Deeper Catacombs; the layout will vary from one to the next. After passing through the Deeper Catacombs, you'll take the portal to the Catacombs of Opposition. Here you'll find the Olthoi Town and the hunting areas for your 4 Olthoi Trophies. All 7 dungeons end up in the same Olthoi Town.

You'll be fighting Olthoi exclusively the entire way down, so buff appropriately and bane for Acid and Piercing!

Note: In order to make 2 of the 4 Olthoi Weapons, you'll need to have a pair of plain leather gauntlets and a quarterstaff. You can purchase them in any nexus or starter town before leaving, or you can simply loot them from the Olthoi as you hunt.

Once you've reached the Catacombs of Opposition, the first inhabitant you see is Ripper.

The big man does not speak, only looks at you with eyes that seem no longer human.

Surrounding the area are olthoi impaled on spikes, intended to scare off the dangerous Olthoi below. You'll see several passages leading down from Ripper's area. Follow the passage directly behind Ripper and you'll see Rat Nose. Next to Rat Nose is a pit, with more Olthoi Tunnels (Do this last. Once you jump in the pit, you can only get back out by fighting your way up through the olthoi below). Up a level from Rat Nose are more of the Olthoi Hunters.

If you want all 4 weapons, you will need a total of 5 Olthoi Trophies, a Femur, a Stab Claw, 2 Crests and a Long Claw, as well as the leather guantlets and a quarterstaff. The following Olthoi drop the trophies:

--Olthoi Femur: Olthoi Soldier
--Olthoi Stab Claw - Olthoi Guard
--Olthoi Crest - Olthoi Advance Guard
--Olthoi Long Claw - Olthoi Vanquisher

The trophies are all bonded (no-give) and are on a 7 day timer, with the exception of the Crest which is not on a timer.


Olthoi Atlatl

Trophies: Olthoi Femur & Stab Claw
Olthoi: Soldier & Guard
NPC: Pincer Danta

To make the Olthoi Atlatl, you need an Olthoi Femur form an Olthoi Soldier, and an Olthoi Stab Claw from an Olthoi Guard. Take the east or the south tunnel from Ripper's room to find both Guards and Soldiers.

Note: There have been reports that the Soldier on Marae Lassel that spawn during the Queen quest also drop the Femur.

Map: Catacombs of Opposition

Creatures: Olthoi Advance Guard, Olthoi Eviscerator, Olthoi Grub, Olthoi Guard, Olthoi Mutilator, Olthoi Noble, Olthoi Soldier, Olthoi Swarm Eviscerator, Olthoi Swarm Guardian, Olthoi Swarm Soldier, Olthoi Vanquisher, Olthoi Warrior

Also need an Olthoi femur. That's the top part of the leg, in case you don't know. You been around Olthoi as much as us, you get to know a lot about 'em. Femur and a stabbing claw, that's what I need."
Pincer Danta tells you, "Say, got any grub bites?"

You give Pincer Danta Olthoi Femur.
Pincer Danta tells you, "This should work."

Olthoi Spear

Trophies: Olthoi Long Claw
Olthoi: Olthoi Vanquisher
NPC: Teela

To make the Olthoi Spear, you need an Olthoi Long Claw from an Olthoi Vanquisher. Head to Rat Nose and jump into the pit. Warning: Unless you have an insanely high jump skill, you must fight your way through this section to return to the Olthoi Town. Take the west passage straight head and keep to the right all the way through to find the ramp back up. Deeper in past the Swarm Guardians the passages are full of Vanquishers, Swarm Eviscerators and Mutilators.

Map: Catacombs of Opposition

Teela tells you, "Well, honey, it sure is nice to know there's others fighting the Olthoi with us here. Can you really kill 'em with that? I use a nice spear myself. Goes through bug shell like butter. Of course, it's been years since I've tasted butter... Get me a bug's long claw and I might make you one. Make sure the long claw is nice and pointy. And freshly killed."
You give Teela Olthoi Long Claw.
Teela tells you, "This should work."
Teela makes a spear out of the Olthoi long claw.
Teela gives you Olthoi Spear.

Teela tells you, "Honey, have you tasted Olthoi fungus? We eat it steamed, fried, roasted and burnt. Sure is good. Why, I tell myself every day that I sure can't wait for another meal of Olthoi fungus. And grub bites. Mmmm. Hey, you should use that nice spear I made you and kill some bugs so we can get more fungus and grub bites."

Olthoi Weapons may be given.

You can also find Olthoi Grubs in this section. Bring them back to Pincer Dante for some tasty snacks, or use them on housing hooks to decorate your home!!

You give Pincer Danta Small Olthoi Grub.

Pincer Danta gives you Grub Bite.
Pincer Danta tells you, "Want a grub bite? They're tasty and about all we get to eat. Okay, maybe not so tasty."
