From The Calm event.

Many know the Beach Fort only from its reputation as a chorizite refinery. Others, however, have discovered the restorative properties of the brisk sea air!

Suzuhara Sheshumi, a former resident of Stonehold, has made the Beach Fort his new home in an attempt to treat his ailing lungs. Recently, his brother attempted to deliver a care package to him, but lost it in The Pit. Retrieve the package and bring it to the Beach Fort for a reward!

To begin the quest, visit the brother, Suzuhara Baijin, in Stonehold at 68.9N, 21.8W. You can also purchase a rumor from Vivaina, Shopkeep of Stonehold at 68.6N, 21.7W.

Rumor: Suzuhara Baijin's Delivery

To reach Stonehold, take the Azaroth Settlement portal from Qalaba'r at 74.8S, 17.8E. You'll exit at 84.1N, 31.1W, a short run from a portal to Stonehold located at 85.8N, 27.6W. You can also take the portal from inside Umbrall Hall on the Vesayan Isles.

Suzuhara Baijin tells you, "I fear I am not strong enough to re-enter the Pit. But perhaps you could do it! Go to the Pit at 64N, 23W. Find the package, then take it to my brother at the Beach Fort at 76N, 49W!"

The Pit is located a short distance away at 64.3N, 23.4W (Levels 60+).

Map: The Pit
Creatures: Bloodthirsty Monouga, Pugnacious Monouga, Merciless Monouga, Skeletal Hero, Armored Skeleton, Direland Rat, Lich, Revenant, Phantasm

It's not The Pit of old! The Monougas have been upgraded, so buff accordingly! Bring your cold and fire weapons. You'll also need lockpick if you want to reach the SIK chest inside the dungeon.

The route through the dungeon is fairly simple; keep going down!

Caution: do not jump into the pit! Jumping from top could result in death. Jumping from a lower level will make you bypass the area with the care package and you'll need to return to the top via a surface portal and try again. On your way down you'll see a few rooms with chests behind locked doors guarded by Skeletons.

Eventually, you'll reach a ledge with a ramp below that leads up to a tower in the middle of the room. Jump down from the ledge onto the ramp to enter the tower. From the tower, jump to the passageway below from the north ledge. Follow the corridor and fight past the undead to a large open room with 3 chests. One of the chests is actually the care package and may be picked up!

Timer: 7 days

Once everyone has a care package, retrace your steps to the tower area and jump below to the Beach Fort portal.

You'll find Suzuhara Sheshumi at 76.0N, 49.1W standing on the Beach Fort wall, enjoying the breeze.
