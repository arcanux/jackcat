 Here's the down and dirty guide to becoming a PK (Player Killer) as hinted to in the Bloodshed Rumor.

Rumor: Bloodshed Rumor

Several choices:

1) Darktide -- automatic PK Status.

2) Old Talisman - 2.3S, 85.6W

Find the Broken Sigil in Dungeon Nye 6.2N x 34.4E. Take the south passage up the ramp and hug the left wall until you reach a door opening into a small room. The northern exit from the room ends in a T. Turn right and head up the ramp. Head through the grated door and go north to the T. Make a left heading down, the follow the corridor across the bridge-like structure which leads to a room with the Sigil. It will spawn on the floor. You'll be fighting Drudge Ravener and High Mu-Miyah.

 Map: Dungeon Nye
Map: Old Talisman

Take the Sigil to the Hermit North of the dungeon at 11.8N x 33.7E and he will open a portal to Old Talismann in the Dires. There you can find the Altar of Bael'Zharon. Be careful! You will encounter the following creatures: Black Rat, Blue Phyntos Wasp, Brown Rat, Lich, Undead, Water Golem, Zombie.

3) Accursed Halls - 24.3S, 57.8W

An easier route to the life of a Player Killer is at the Accursed Halls via the Drunken madman near Baishi 53.0S, 61.0E. Give him some Sake and he will open a portal to the Walled Portals 21.5S, 74.6W. From there, take the portal to the Accursed Halls. You will encounter mostly Banderlings inside. Some have even gone PK at level 1 at the Accursed Halls by zipping right past the creatures. You may possibly encounter a Vorous Shreth, so be careful!

4) Mount Lethe - 33.9S, 85.2W

Visit the Water of Mount Lethe quest write-up for directions to the Altar.

5) Mage Academy - 74.7N, 18.5E

Visit the Mage Academy quest write-up for directions to the Altar.

6) Vesayan Altar at the Catacombs of Ithaenc.
