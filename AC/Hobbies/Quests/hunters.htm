From the Mired Hearts event. Updated during Pillars Made of Sand.

Town Crier tells you, "Those three Tumerok brothers are rather strange. Who goes out and picks a fight with creatures that are so darned tough? Not me, I'll tell you. Give me a nice safe place like a town and I'm happy."

Seven Tumerok Brothers have left Marae Lassel to hunt Golems. Speak to a brother to be flagged, then after killing 5 of the specified golem, return to the Tumerok for a reward. If you kill an additional 5 golems, you will be rewarded with a title and experience as well!

Note: The XP reward and the title token are given out on the completion of either:

1) Killing 10 golems first time doing the quest
or
2) Doing quest for 2nd time (2 x 5 golems)

#2 recommended as you'll get 12 fragments in total instead of 6.

Begin the quest in Holtburg at 41.6N, 34.2E by speaking to Aun Kielerea.

Aun Kielerea tells you, "Greetings. I am Aun Kielerea, a hunter for the Aun Xuta. My brothers and I have ventured away from the land of Palenqual to seek out mightier prey than what can be found near our home."
Aun Kielerea tells you, "This does not mean we have forsaken Aun Hareltah, rather that we are looking for glorious battle against truly worthy opponents. And we believe we have found it!"
Aun Kielerea tells you, "My brothers have stationed themselves across this land, hunting the strongest of the creatures you call golems. They are not selfish, and during the times that they are at rest, they will be happy to share in the hunt and reward those who have taken up the same challenge as they. Please go visit them."
Aun Kielerea tells you, "May you always be brave in battle."

Aun Akuarea - 2.6S, 20.0E (near Samsur)

Golem: Mud Golem Sludge Lord
Reward: 6 Tasty Puddings
Experience: 100,000
Title: Mudslinger

Aun Akuarea tells you, "Greetings. I am Aun Akuarea. I am the youngest of the seven who have left Palenqual to hunt! As I am the youngest, I have chosen the Mud Golem Sludge Lord as my prey. They are slow, but powerful for one such as I."
Aun Akuarea tells you, "would you like to hunt them as well? Very well, scour this region and others like it and I am sure you will find the golem. Kill five of these golems, return to me and I will see that your battles are rewarded."

Aun Akuarea tells you, "Excellent! Your key burns brightly with victory! As I promised, a reward for you! It isn't much, simply some fixings I brought with me from Timaru! Here, feast with me as we celebrate your achievement!"
Aun Akuarea gives you 6 Tasty Puddings.

Aun Tahuirea - 24.0N, 72.0W (Fort Tethana)
Shortcut: Head to the South Direlands portal at 62.2S, 77.1Efrom Mayoi. Go to Slaughterhouse portal at 80.6S, 37.4W, then run to 24.0N, 72.0W.

Golem: Crystal Lord
Reward: Tahuirea's Key
Experience: ???
Title: ???

Aun Tahuirea tells you, "Greetings. I am Aun Tahuirea. My seven sons have left the land of Palenqual in search of glorious hunts. No doubt trying to impress me. They are each talented hunters in their own right, but none of them are yet ready to hunt what I do."
Aun Tahuirea tells you, "What is it that I hunt, you ask? I keep my harpoon sharp for the rare Crystal Lords. They are a dangerous type of golem for ones such as you, so be sure to bring others of your xuta to aid you in the battle. Should you and your fellows come across and defeat one, please come speak with me. I would enjoy hearing the story of your battles."
Aun Tahuirea tells you, "Also, if you and your fellows succeed in defeating one, I will be pleased to share with you the bounties I have recovered from my previous victories."

Your killing blow nearly turns Crystal Lord inside-out!
Your fellowship is now locked. You may not recruit new members. If you leave the fellowship, you have 15 minutes to be recruited back into the fellowship.
[Fellowship Name] is now a closed fellowship.

You tell Tahuirea the tale of the battle you and your fellows waged against the Crystal Lord and its minions. The Elder Tumerok smiles throughout the entire story. As you finish, Tahuirea unclips a key from his belt and hands it to you.
Aun Tahuirea tells you, "Your tale was truly one of triumph! I only wish I could have been there to see the battle for the tale is one of incredible glory. Use this key to take some of the bounty I have amassed in my hunting of the Crystal Lords."
Aun Tahuirea gives you Tahuirea's Key.

Use the Key on the chest next to Aun Tahuirea for your reward (Sing quality loot)!
