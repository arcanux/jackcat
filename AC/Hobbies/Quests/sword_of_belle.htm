Hello Maggie,

TCers mentioned the 3 factions looking for a stolen item. Talked to the Yaraq guy, since I'll stick with Elysa for now.

I like it when NPCs give coords, so I tried the Bellenesse Sword Quest.After running on the Viamontian Islands and bumping into the sea all the time (and I still cant swim), I figured out from the ingame map it might be near Beach Fort.

Took Lady Miala's Estate (sp) portal from Mayoi and ran to coords 71.1N 50.2W like the guy said. No portal, but a dungeon anyway, just like the Vulcano one on Tusk Island. So no level restrict I guess ;).

Inside Bane, and deeper down Ripper Grievers. Place is quite a maze, and only way to know I wasn't going in circles was making a trail of dead spiders ;).
I sorta kept going E, and down. When there were 3 ways, I tried the middle one, backtracked when I found a dead end. Found a big room with a ramp, where Rippers lived, from there I took the exit on the downside, and with another ramp down, there was a small room with a ramp in the middle.At first, I went right hand rule from there, bumped into a lava pit, backtracked, and found the "Escaped Thief" that screamed for help from his eaters. Level 125 btw. On his corpse was the missing Sword of Bellenesse.

Timer is 27 days for another pickup.

I returned to Yaraq, and handed the sword to the new NPC. Got 3,5 million xps, and the Replica of the Sword of Bellenesse. Special properties included Viamontian Knight Slayer. Wonder what the Sanamar NPC would give instead... Sword was 370 weild.
When I returned the sword to him in hope for another weapon, I got another 2,5 million xps instead.. and no Viamontian Slayer bow or so :(.

See attached pics for stats of Thief, Sword, and the NPC in Yaraq.

Love the Site, hope to see the rewards from the other factions there!

From Tlosk of HG:

As one of the three factions for the Sword of Bellenesse quest, if you return the sword recovered from the Escaped Thief to Lucari di Bellenesse in Silyun (87.3N 70.5W) he rewards you with the Shield of Silyun.

Lucari di Bellenesse tells you, "We have not the resources to confirm this rumor, but, if you would be interested in checking out this rumor for Silyun, I can provide something in reward for this."

Lucari di Bellenesse tells you, "Go to the cave, find this thief. If he has the blade on him, and you'll know it if you see it, it is hard to miss, bring it to me. If this is truly the Sword of Bellenesse, all Silyun will be in your debt."

Lucari di Bellenesse tells you, "Be warned, agents of Varicci will also be seeking to return this blade to the Vaults of Sanamar. Also, I have heard rumor that someone in Yaraq also seeks the blade. The blade is the rightful property of the Duke Bellenesse. I hope, if you manage to find it before others do so, that you will return it to Silyun, so it may be returned to its rightful owner."

You give Lucari di Bellenesse The Sword of Bellenesse.
Lucari carefully examines the blade you have given him. With a very sad expression, he sets the weapon gently aside and looks to you.

Lucari di Bellenesse tells you, "Thank you... This is indeed the sword that was lost to us. All in Silyun thank you for returning this to us. It is a noble thing you have done."

Lucari di Bellenesse tells you, "Please, accept this token as a gift from us, and know that you have our gratitude for the valor and nobility you have shown to us this day."

Lucari di Bellenesse gives you Shield of Silyun.
You've earned 3,500,000 experience.

Lucari di Bellenesse tells you, "If you do not wish the shield, return it to me, and I will find another way to reward your kindness."

You give Lucari di Bellenesse Shield of Silyun.
Lucari di Bellenesse tells you, "As you wish."
You've earned 2,500,000 experience.

The Viamontian faction reward for the Sword of Bellenesse quest is the Shield of Sanamar, the NPC is Turien Di Furza at 71.8N 61.2W in Sanamar.

Turien Di Furza tells you, "I have been authorized to offer rewards to anyone who can return the blade that was stolen from the Vaults of Sanamar.
The blade is gold in color, and bears the markings of the Bellenesse family."

Turien Di Furza tells you, "The craven thief was last sighted near a cave at 71.1 N, 50.2 W and is believed to be hiding within. Find the thief, do justice upon him, and return this blade that was wrongly taken from this Kingdom."

Turien Di Furza tells you, "Bring the blade to me, and your loyalty will be rewarded. Be warned, however. I have heard that both Silyun and some Bloodless in Yaraq also seek this blade. They must not be allowed to gain that which is rightfully Sanamar's."

You give Turien Di Furza The Sword of Bellenesse.
Turien Di Furza tells you, "Ahh, yes. This is the blade that was stolen.
Allow me to reward you for your valor and loyalty to Sanamar and King Varicci."

Turien Di Furza gives you Shield of Sanamar.
You've earned 3,500,000 experience.
Turien Di Furza tells you, "If this shield is of no use to you, return it to me, and I will reward you in other ways."

The Sword of Bellenesse
