From Del Cabon:
Tusker Lucura: Level 100+.  Its full of Tusker Devastators, 20+ at a time in some spots. At the end you can kill a Tusker, for a horn. Horn gave 20ish million xp (level 125), a Title Token (Paragon of Death), and a gem to the island.

From Avail of LC:
Recomended fellow: 2 mages 410+ buffed life, 2 archers, 2 melee. at the least have these many in fellow. Bring: health elixers (lots)

Jobs: archers/melees kill the devastators. 1 mage with a healers heart healing, and 1 mage imperiling.

Ok, this is the fun part. You designate someone to lure the tuskers around in the second room. The lurer runs around the room in circles (ahead of everyone) dragging all the tuskers. The rest of the fellow waits a minute then charges in and they head south and in the middle of room is a ramp leading DOWN. They head down the ramp and around the corner. When they get there and are safe, the lurer runs down the ramp and the fellow kills the tuskers that are remaining.

After the place is clear head down the passage to a bigger tusker room. Clear the room and head down a ramp to the right and you'll come into a little room with 4-5 tuskers in it. 2 of the 5 tuskers drop tusks. Just hang out in a passageway while you wait for them to respawn so everyone can get a tusk. (respawn is about 5 mins)

I've done this quest 2 times already. First time we tried to clear the second room but it never worked out, couldn't kill them fast enough. The second time we did the lure method and it worked perfectly. Took about 15 mins to get everyone a tusk.

Have fun and stay alive.

Hey Maggie! Long time no see :)

Just wanted to provide a little info for people about the 100+ tusker Lacuna dungeon that was missing from the write-up on your site.

We had nearly 15 100+ people and couldn't clear the big room for a significant period of time. So the runner method is highly suggested. One important note... in the South end of the room, there are TWO ramps down. One is more south than the other (in the open room between two post) -- that is the incorrect one. The correct one is just north of that one, basically around the corner from the ramp you took into that room, going down into the very middle of the room.

Hope that clears things up -- we spent a good hour clearing our way in and out of the incorrect ramp....

-Zedjin of TD
