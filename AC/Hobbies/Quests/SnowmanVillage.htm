From the Waking from the Abyss event.

The Festivus season is upon us again, traditionally a time for fun and silly rewards. Give a Town Crier some hot chocolate to help them ward off the cold and they'll give you news of a Snowman Village!

Town Crier tells you, "The furniture vendors have a few new houshold items in stock now. I wish I could go home and enjoy all the things I've bought, but I have to stand out here in the cold just so you can get the latest news and information. No, no, don't worry about me, I'll be fine out here. All alone. Without something hot to drink to keep me warm..."
You give Town Crier Hot Chocolate.
Town Crier tells you, "Thank you! You are sooo nice!"
Town Crier tells you, "Hey, did you hear about the snowmen? Apparently they built a little town up in the mountains. You know what would make this hot chocolate even better? Marshmallows. Yup, sure wish I had some."

Shortcut: Take the Oasis Settlement Portal from Qalaba'r at 74.8S, 17.8E. You'll exit at 66.4S, 21.8E, just a short run from the Frost Haven portal.

Frost Haven - 68.6S, 25.4E

If you speak to The Mayor, you'll learn that a Magma Golem named Scold has been terrorizing the town. Journey to Mount Ingot (no level restriction) to the north and kill Scold for a cool reward!

The Mayor tells you, "Hello there, hello there!"
The Mayor tells you, "I'm Mayor of Frost Haven. Welcome to our humble little town."
The Mayor tells you, "Normally it isn't this quiet, but we've been having a bit of a problem."
The Mayor tells you, "Have you seen that mountain peak to the North? That's Mount Ingot, a homicidal Magma Golem named Scold lives up there. Every few weeks he emerges from his cave and marches down the mountain to stomp all over our town. Oh the snomanity, oh, it's awful!"
The Mayor tells you, "Oh! You have to help us. He must be stopped. If you don't help us I'm afraid we may be doomed. Doomed! Doomed!"
The Mayor tells you, "Umm, I'm sure I could dip into the slush fund for a reward if you stop him."

Map: Mount Ingot

Inside the dungeon you'll be fighting Limestone, Granite, Iron and then Copper Golems. When you reach the end (you can't get lost), fight Scold, the level 85 golem. When you kill him, he breaks into 4 Scold Chunks (level 65, smaller versions of Scold). Each Scold Chuck you kill breaks into two smaller Scold Lumps (level 45). Kill them to retrieve Scold's Heart (24 hour timer). Bring back the heart to the village and give it to one of snowmen for a reward!

You split Scold apart!
Scold says, "Oh, I'm just getting started!"
The Golem falls apart. Each segment reforms into a smaller version of the original.
