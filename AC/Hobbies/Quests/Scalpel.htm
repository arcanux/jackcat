 The following information is provided as a courtesy until I can research the quest for a full write-up.
 

The scalpel is found in the Habitat dungeon. A gem to the Habitat is obtained by killed a "Virindi Collector" which is a rare spawning mob, like a Diamond lord. It can spawn anywhere on the plateau, and is pretty easy to find because it calls your name out when you run by it.

The Collector's non-voice booms in your mind, "We have learned the art of intimidation from the finest of your kind. Words such as these they shout at us in the Maze. Why are you humored?"

 When the gem is used it opens up to a dungeon full of both Virindi and Olthoi. The drop point is safe. The dungeon is fairly small, and you will quickly reach a large room with Olthoi and Virindi Observers. Once of the Observers will summon a portal on it's death. It took our group about 10 minutes before one summoned, I believe it is random. The portal opens up to a large structure above the portal, in the same dungeon. If you look up from the room with the Virindi you can see it. The area there has about 2 executors and 4 observers. It's also very small, and you will quickly located one of two difficulty 400 chorizite threaded (resist all spells) chests. Lockpick these and the 2 halves to the dagger are inside. Combine them and you make the dagger. A high skill is needed to combine them and they are on a timer.

Good luck,
Giga of Harvestgain
