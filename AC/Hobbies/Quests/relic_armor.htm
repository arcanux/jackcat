Noble Relic Coat of Brilliance, work 0, AL 420, Anadil's Boon, MacNiall's Boon, Reenigne's Boon, Mi-Krauli's Boon, Inner Brilliance(+12 focus), Brogard's Defiance, Lore 220, Axe 150+ to wield, Value 20000, 1250 BU

Noble Relic Helm of Will, work 0, AL 420, Asmolum's Boon, Strathelar's Boon, Barnar's Boon, Inner Will (+12 to self ), Brogard's Defiance, Lore 200, Axe 150+ to wield, Value 20000, 350 BU
 

Abu Megan of Frostfell reports: "Hi Maggie, I've found the next piece of the Noble Relic Armor set. This one was on the 99th xp item turned in, though its possible there was a miscount making it 100."

Kitana Oazaka tells you, "Queen Elysa would have you accept this as a reward for your brave assault of this stronghold of evil."
Kitana Oazaka gives you Noble Relic Gauntlets of Strength.
 

Frax of SC poses in Ancient Relic Gauntlets below!

Aqua Tone of SC poses with Ancient Relic Armor!

Scotty the Handy Man reports: "Hiya Maggie- this is for a friend- Olthoi Master of LC experimented with the exact way to obtain a piece of Relic Armor. He managed exactly 100 of only lower-tier Dark Isle boss critter trophies to the Viamontian side, and he received the Relic Alduressa Gauntlets. So it’s 100 total of any trophy. Also to note for warning- don’t give your trophy to the NPC when another person is. The NPC might get distracted and you will lose your XP."
