Rebel Hideout - 43.9N, 73.9W

To reach the Hideout, head to Bluespire from Yaraq at 21.7S, 1.3W, then take the Marescent Plateau portal at 42.0N, 77.6W. Inside you'll find the rag tag crew of Carenzi Rebels who bear a significant resemblance to the cast of Red Dawn. ;-)

After you've talked to the rebels, head to the back tunnels.

Back Tunnels - 43.9N 73.9W
--No level restriction
--Map:
--Creatures: Restless Spirit, Royal Thaumaturge, Viamontian Tribune, Sewer Rat, Viamontian Seeker

If you go slowly through the tunnels, the spawns are small and easily defeated. Head west and keep to the right. You'll go down a ramp to the west, then go right. Take a left up a ramp to the east, make a left at the drop and down another ramp to the west. At the 3 way split go right down a ramp to the north and through the fern room. Take the north passage, then a left down a ramp to the west. Cross the wooden bridge and go left heading west until you reach the ramp up to the north (the east ramp leads to rats). Keep straight heading west at the 4 way and keep to the left. Jump up the crates to the next level up. The Viamontian Seeker is behind a door to your right. On its corpse you'll find 6 Grazer Bugs (2 week timer).

If you take the exit portal, you'll be dropped at 42.8N, 67.1W. It may be easier to portal recall back to Bluespire and return to the Hideout. Hand the bug to Dar Rell and he'll admit his guilt. Give it to Jadeth Eckhart for an xp reward (level dependent - caps at 7 million).

You've earned 3,234,914 experience.
 

Grazer Bug
Grazer Bugs are small parasitic creatures that live inside the bowels of warm blooded animals. They feed off the half-digested food of their host where they will reproduce with their life mate counterpart. In cases of accidental expulsion from its host, the Grazer Bug has the unique ability to track down the host animal to reunite itself with its mate.
Value 0
Burden 5
Special Props Attuned, Bonded
