Notes from Gali Nala of HG:

Black Marrow Reliquaries can drop one of 15 unique items that fall into 3 classes: gems, tokens and a pearl. All items but the pearl cast portal spells. Certain items can be turned in to Agents of the Arcanum for writs, while the spells on others will summon portals to otherwise unreachable Black Marrow Reliquaries.

It is suggested to wait 5 minutes between each pull for the best chance at pulling a unique item.

Non-summoning:
Black Luster Pearl - Increases all attributes by 2 for 10 minutes.

Red Gems (Cannot be turned in for writs):

Singularity Caul Asylum – Summons a portal to the Caul Asylum
Cavernous Olthoi Chasm – Summons a portal to the Cavernous Olthoi Chasm
The Orphanage – Summons a portal to the orphanage
Ancient Temple – Summons a portal to the Faltacot Temple

The remaining items can all be turned in for writs.

Items that summon portals to Black Marrow Reliquaries:

Shadow Cursed Token (token) – Burning Tower at 89.5s 95.8w - Drop is safe, chest is protected
Seething Skull (skull) – Floating Bridges at 94.4s 92.0w - Drop is hot
Corrupted Skull (skull) – Floating towers at 99.4s 97.6w – Drop is hot
Antiquated Compass (compass) – Oasis of the Sing Caul at 96.0s 91.3w – Chest & drop are safe
Items that summon portals:

Burning Veil (crown) – Center of the Sing Caul (Caulcano) at 94.4s 94.6w – Extremely dangerous drop.
Warriors Emblem (emblem) – Battlefield of Ayn Tayn at 41.3s 52.6e – This drop is on the mainland, an interesting place to explore.
Blood Fang Jewel (tooth) – Obsidan Rim at 48.0s 65.1w. Otherwise know as the VOD lifestone.
Black Glass Array (5 pieces of glass) – Obsidain Plains at 64.0s 65.3w. Within sight of the mushroom fields at the center of the Obsidian Plains.
Soul Chalice (green chalice) – Inside the Ponopticon dungeon (lvl 32 restriction) at 59.1s 84.1w. This is a shortcut to Ayan.
Desolate Seed (greenish black seeds) – Sing Caul at 98.0s 94.7w. This is the Sing Caul drop point.
