From the Toward Ancient Shores event.

The following notes are provided as a courtesy until I can research a full write-up.

Emissary of Asheron tells you, "What do I do? My lord's house is under attack! Talk to the Servant, he knows what to do."
Emissary of Asheron tells you, "Save my lord's house!"

Eastham - 17.9N, 64.3E

Give Servant Makkar's Eye or Shadow Fragment to enter portal to Asheron's Island.

You give Asheron's Servant Shadow Anchor Piece.
The Servant of Asheron carefully takes the Shadow Anchor Piece from your hands and swallows it. His white light flares blindingly for a moment and then returns to normal.
Asheron's Servant tells you, "Good, retrieve the other two peices and I will reward you."

You give Asheron's Servant Shadow Anchor Piece.
The Servant of Asheron carefully takes the Shadow Anchor Piece from your hands and swallows it. His white light flares blindingly for a moment and then returns to normal.
Asheron's Servant tells you, "Good, retrieve the last piece and I will reward you."

You give Asheron's Servant Shadow Anchor Piece.
The Servant of Asheron carefully takes the Shadow Anchor Piece from your hands and swallows it. His white light flares blindingly for a moment and then returns to normal.
Asheron's Servant tells you, "We have destroyed a small portion of the Shadow Anchor's strength. Here is the reward that I promised you. This once belonged to the young Queen Carraida of Haebrous, who died in Grael's Slave Rebellion. After her death the King could not stand to be reminded of his loss and gave her three favorite pieces of jewelry to my master of that age, Lord Alashorn. She was a proud Haebrean. I'm sure she would be pleased to know that they have been passed on to a noble hero. Perhaps you can, in some small way, take vengeance against Grael on her behalf. When all three pieces are worn together, their energies will combine to bestow an additional enchantment upon you. If you continue your efforts in weakening the Shadow Anchor, I will give you the other pieces."
Asheron's Servant gives you Pendant of Carraida.
Asheron's Servant tells you, "Return this piece jewelry to me if you would rather hone your skills, but I warn you, I will never offer it to you again."

You give Asheron's Servant Shadow Anchor Piece.
The Servant of Asheron carefully takes the Shadow Anchor Piece from your hands and swallows it. His white light flares blindingly for a moment and then returns to normal.
Asheron's Servant grants you the title Carraida's Avenger.
Asheron's Servant tells you, "The forces holding the Shadow Mouths here have been significantly weakened thanks to your heroic efforts. I am sure we will be able to defend the tower now. Here is Carraida's Bracelet. Remember her." Asheron's Servant gives you Bracelet of Carraida.
Asheron's Servant tells you, "Return this piece jewelry to me if you would rather hone your skills, but I warn you, I will never offer it to you again."
Asheron's Servant tells you, "If you wish to continue your efforts in weakening the Shadow Anchor I will reward you with a bit of knowledge for each piece you bring me. However, you must wait a short time before collecting more pieces. Exposing yourself to the Shadow Anchors energy so soon would be to great a risk. Event you might fall under Grael's control."
