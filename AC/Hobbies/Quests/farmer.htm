From the Friend and Foe event.

To begin the quest, purchase the rumor in Holtburg.

Rumor: A Farmer's Plea for Help

To reach the farm, take the Ianna settlement portal from Crater at 66.4N, 14.3E.

NPC: Farmer Kao - 51.4N, 7.5E

Farmer Kao tells you, "My crops! My livestock! Some vile creature has been ravaging my farm at night! Would you help me stop this terror? I have followed its tracks to a cave south of here, but I am too afraid to continue. Please help me."

Head south to The Fazenda Caves at 49.8N, 7.7E (Levels 40+).

--Levels: 40+
--Map: The Fazenda Caves
--Creatures: Oak Golem, Ferocious Ursuin, White Phyntos Wasp, Cave Ursuin, Grave Spirit, Dire Ursuin, Mighty Oak Golem, Black Phyntos Swarm, Fazenda Terror

The dungeon is easy to navigate. As you work your way through, you'll be picking up several items belonging to Farmer Kao.

The Feed Bag can be found in the first room. Take the right passage and you'll find a Shovel. If you head back and this time take the left passage, you'll head down a slope. Take the right at the bottom of the ramp and you'll find a water bucket. Go back and take the left passage from the bottom or the ramp and you'll find  a ramp down in the middle of the floor. At the bottom, take the right passage to find the gloves. Keep right and you'll come to another floor ramp down. The Fazenda Terror is in the final room guarded by 2 Black Phyntos Swarm and 3 Grave Spirits.

Timer: 7 days on items

Kill the Ursuin and bring the head back to Farmer Kao for your reward!

You give Farmer Kao Farmer Kao's Feed Bag.
Farmer Kao tells you, "There it is! My chickens will be happy that their feed bag has been returned. They were getting really hungry. Thank you!"
You've earned 75,000 experience.
You give Farmer Kao Farmer Kao's Shovel.
Farmer Kao tells you, "Hey! My missing shovel! This is great. Now I won't have to dig in the yard with my wife's wooden spoon. Thank you!"
You've earned 75,000 experience.
You give Farmer Kao Farmer Kao's Water Bucket.
Farmer Kao tells you, "My water bucket! Where did you find that? This will make trips to the well so much easier now. Thank you!"
You've earned 75,000 experience.
You give Farmer Kao Farmer Kao's Gardening Gloves.
Farmer Kao tells you, "There they are! I've been looking for those gloves. Maybe these blisters on my hands will heal now. Thank you!"
You've earned 75,000 experience.

You give Farmer Kao Head of Fazenda Terror.
You've earned 500,000 experience.
Farmer Kao tells you, "Excellent! This is the creature that has been causing all my grief! I am so relieved. Alas, I do not have much money to reward you with, but please let me craft you this trophy as a token of my appreciation."
Farmer Kao begins crafting an item...
Farmer Kao tells you, "This is some of my finest craftsmanship. I hope you enjoy it!"
Farmer Kao gives you Decorative Ursuin Head.
Farmer Kao tells you, "Thank you for saving my farm! The crops are growing, the flowers are blooming, and the chickens are happy now!"

When you hand your Decorative Ursuin Head on a wall hook, you'll get an eerie feeling that you're being watched!!

The eyes follow you around the room.
