COD:

Hea Temenua, Hea Karenua, and Hea Kanokeh are found together on the Marescent Plateau. They are Level 111 Hea Tumeroks, and they wield hollow weapons!





Hea Temenua (Elari Bow parts)
Hea Karenua (Awakener; Keys to Hea Cache)
Hea Kanokeh (controls other Lvl 50 Tumeroks)
Eventually, if you have good luck, you will encounter the three of them with some additional Hea Nualuan Tumeroks (Level 50).
If possible (!), dispatch the named Tumeroks first. If you can Kill Hea Kanokeh first, you hear this message:


"As the drum-speaker Hea Kanokeh falls, the Nualuan warriors of Temenua's party lose heart, and quickly leave the field of battle."
So the Hea Nualuan warriors are gone, leaving only Hea Karenua and Hea Temenua.

From Hea Temenua's corpse you get the basic Elari Bow (the Greenwood Haft) and the Gromnie Sinew Bowstring. You may also get a Hea Totem.

From Karenua you get the Awakener and several Hea Karenua Keys (you can only pick up one). This key opens the Hea Raider's Cache at (43.3N, 82.5W) in the southwest corner of the plateau. Singularity-level loot and an Ahunrenga Portal Gem.

There is a two week timer on getting the parts of the bow, regardless of your success at stringing it. To string it, buff your Bow Skill, and Coordination. [Note: Buffing Strength is not necessary (per a Dev on the Dark Majesty site). Our thanks to Johnny Muerte for this information! - Ed.]

Updated during Pillars Made of Sand:

--Elari Bow may be upgraded to a Sublime Elari. The sublime bow adds Biting Strike and a 250 wield requirement. Activation requirement is removed and Prey's Reflex is changed to BD VI.
