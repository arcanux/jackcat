 Alas, poor Gertarh has been assassinated. His fallen corpse is all that remains of the great Dagger quest. And this mysterious note:

Farewell Gertarh. Its a shame I had to kill you, but the price on your head was too great and my purse too light. - Oswald

There are two places to get the dagger.
You can buy it directly from Gertarh himself in the Thieve's Den in Eastham. Take the first left, use lockpick to open the door and purchase the dagger for 480p.

You can also get the dagger at the Ancient Lighthouse in the Direlands near Fort Tethana. Go down three levels and take a passage to the left. Use a lever to open a door to a room with a cell. Use lockpick on the cell and the dagger will by lying on the floor.

Gertarh's Dagger
Value 300
Burden 135
Damage 8-9
Type Slashing/Piercing
Speed 20


Tip: Gertarh is a Banderling. Don't attack him by accident!
