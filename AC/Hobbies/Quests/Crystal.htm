 The Crystal Sword is a trophy from a Sentient Fragment, a special Crystal Fragment introduced in the Keep Your Enemies Closer event. The approximate drop rate for the sword is about one in four.

Sentient Fragments can be found on the Obsidian Plains and in the South Direlands. Like all the new boss creatures, they can be scarce and difficult to find. (The one Gunko is fighting in the pic was located at 70.5S, 77.9W. Danier sent screens with a location of  61.7S, 46.1W. Shobolth found his Sentient Fragment near the Artifex Vault.) Jukee of TD reports he found a Sentient Fragment up north near the Creature Essense.

The Sentient Fragment is normally surrounded by 4 Dual Fragments. It appears to heal more rapidly than the normal Fragments. In addition to the sword, the Fragment may also drop a Small Shard!  

During the Come What Follows event, Belinda du Loc in Stonehold developed the ability to enhance numerous trophy items. Hand your sword to her and she will return the enhanced version. Original pre-patch weapons cannot be enhanced.

NPC: Belinda du Loc - 68.9N, 21.6W (Stonehold)
