During the Iron Coast event, Ned Cleversun's dreams came true with the opening of Tusker Island, also known as Aphus Lassel. The mad experience to be had was cause for joy, but there were yet other treasures to be revealed. With The Hall of the Tusker King new prizes became available from the mighty Bobo, King of the Tuskers. Retrieve the Fabled Tusker Paw from a challenging dungeon and exchange it for fabulous rewards!

To reach Aphus Lassel, look for a randomly spawning Tusker Temples to gain access. Or you can use one of the three permanent temples listed below. Another alternative is to hand a peppermint stick to the Blind Snowman for a chance at a Tusker Island Recall Gem. See the Snowglobe write-up for more information.

Permanent Tusker Temples:
10.5S, 65.6E - Levels 20 and lower
59.8N, 28.4E - Levels 20-40
0.7N, 68.1W - Levels 40+

Map: Tusker Temple

The dungeon is symmetrical in design. Generally, head east until you reach the final room with the portal to the Tusker Warriors Lair.

Ned Cleversun reports: "At the bottom of the first series of ramps is a stone tusker head. A ramp lead down from there to a large room. In that room are two Assailers and two Devastators and a few sycophants. Resist, resist, resist. Unreal. Good results ONLY when a vuln or imperil lands. Otherwise, no results. Bring a powerful mage with giant life magic. My 325 life magic skill allowed me to connect only rarely with vulns or imperils. Less than 1 time in 10. Meanwhile, Gossamer Flesh (Imperil 7) and other lev 7 vulns land on you."

There will be four main dungeon sections:

Tusker Warriors Lair
Tusker Food Storage
Legend of Tusker King
Throne of the Tusker King

Starfish of TD reports: "Inside are Assailers, Devestators, Annihilators (a few), Reapers, Obsidian Chittiks and Tusker Sycophants, not to mention dispel traps and Tuskers Gift traps. Many different sections on the way down."

Tusker Warriors Lair

Map: Tusker Warrior's Lair

From the entrance, head south, then make a right. Stay to your left until you head down a ramp. Continue left and follow the passage to the ramp. Head southeast through the columns to the next section.

Tusker Food Storage

Map: Tusker Food Storage

This section is more dangerous with Obsidian Chitticks and Reapers in addition to the Tuskers.

Head north from the entrance, turn right, left, right, then two more rights to the room of tuskers. The portal to the next section is in this room.

Legend of the Tusker King

Map: Legend of the Tusker King

Again you'll be fighting Tusker and Humans. Keep heading down until you reach the final portal.

You'll also pass Crude Tusker Paintings with disturbing descriptions!

A crude pitcure depicting a Virindi as it spreads open the skull of a Tusker to poke at the substance within its head.

A crude painting that shows a tusker eating one human, and speaking to other human.

A childlike painting that shows a Tusker with one hand missing fighting against an enormous Monouga.

A picture of a Tusker wearing a crown and munching on Chittick.

Throne of the Tusker King

Map: Throne of the Tusker King

Here is where King Bobo may be found. Speak to him and he portals you to a buff room with a portal to Arena of the Hero. Buff and bane first!!

Oolutanga, the Tusker King tells you, "I am OOlutanga the Tusker King. You are not worthy of speaking to me. You must fight and beat our hero Mowen Udaun before you can have my magic Tusker Paw.
Oolutanga, the Tusker King tells you, "Take your group and prepare for battle in the room where I will send you. Take your time and be ready to fight our champion who is very strong. Then go through the portal there and come to the isle of testing."
Oolutanga, the Tusker King tells you, "I will bring the other tuskers to watch you in your fight."
Oolutange the Tusker King tells you, "Win and I will reward you with my fabled Tusker paw, I will even make sure it has its magical powers courtesy of the other who speaks like you, Ketnan."
Oolutanga, the Tusker King tells you, "Lose and your body will rot in the arena. Sorry that is the way of things around here. Make sure you armor yourself well, because your magic will help very little against Mowen."

Arena of the Tusker Hero

Inside the Arena of the Hero you must battle Mowen Uduan, a level 900 Tusker. Starfish of TD recommends fire vulning, impen and fester. When he dies a portal will appear back to Bobo. Be ready!

When you return to Bobo, he will reward you with a paw and you may now enter the Idol of the Wishes portal.

Oolutanga, the Tusker King tells you, "Congratulations, you have bested the hero of the tusker people. So I,OOlutanga, giveyou now a reward so that you know that Oolutanga who is me, honors his word."
Oolutanga, the Tusker King gives you Fabled Tusker Paw.
Oolutanga, the Tusker King tells you, "Now walk through the portal to the temple of wishing. But do it before three days pass or you will lose your right to enter there."

Give you paw to one of the skill shrines for a reward. The fighter who did the most damage to Mowen Uduan will get three wishes. When they use the first wish, they will receive the paw back with two fingers left, then one finger left. The others will receive one wish. 

See the table to the right for a list of reported rewards from each statue. At this time it is unknown what determines which possible reward for each skill statue you will receive.
