Introduced during From the Darkness Born.

First, gather 6 items: Spear, Girth, Eye, Hand, Warpaint, and Necklace, to get 6 thanking stones (Casino) and the items activated. Second, get the Untranslated Note from the burun in swamp dires to have acces to Toad Idol Dungeon. You will get the 3 fellowship buffs and also the mossie statue. If you brought Bleeargh the 6 items, you will be able to use the Statue six times (mansion item only) to learn all Item Magic spells.

Thanks to Damin Sarin of FF, Blarrg of WE, Etana of WE, Masked Jackal of TD!

Julian's Touch of TD reports: "In diresland swamp, there are Guruk spawning, and the Burun Ruuk Seer drops a Untranslated Writing. Give it to the Aun in Arwic"

You give Translator Aun Laokhe Untranslated Writing.
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "A vile beast! What villainy lurks in the hearts of these creatures! There is no word in the Tonk language to describe the atrocity that these beasts can manage."
Translator Aun Laokhe gives you Note to Torgluuk.
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "It appears that the rumors that have been spreading through Elysatah's court could well be true."
Aun Laokhe sighs.
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "Apparently there has been some activity in the Direlands Swamp in an area once populated heavily by the Mosswarts. The Mosswarts have reached out and asked us for assistance, as you might know. Bleeargh, an emissary that sought out your people recently, went before Elysatah and asked for assistance. She told the Mosswart that they would afford him passage so long as he and his tribe remained peaceful."
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "I speak of this because many believe that the Mosswart exodus is related to the increase of the Burun, especially in the direlands. I think that it would be better for me to write this down for you."
Translator Aun Laokhe gives you Of the Burun.
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "Please, look into this matter for all our sakes."

Note: Untranslated Writing may also be found in dungeons and on other Buruk, including Burun Ruuk Fiends, Burun Ruuk Louts, Ruffians and Seers.

You succeed in covering your hands and arms in foul smelling muck.

First you have to take the hookable portal from blackmire 1, run around and find a large spawn of guruk's from boor's to crushers etc, with louts and seer's etc, one will drop a note. Turn that in to the aun in arwic and you get two notes and flagged to enter toad at 39.3S, 50.9W (close to bm 1 portal drop). after you have your translated notes, head back to toad and use it, you'll portal inside, where there are guruk's right on ya, so buff outside. we took the left hall first, backtracked a bit after we found each end room that had the scrolls on the floor before each statue
