 From Pillars Made of Sand.

Throughout the month, Royal Guard Gabrielle in Rithwic (10.5N, 58.2E) would change messages giving news of new Renegade Strongholds. A second Renegade Chest was discovered near Fadasil's old mage shop, opened by a key located in a new Renegade Stronghold outside of Tufa. Inside the chest you'll find a Renegade Bone Charm, Salted Meats and a piece of lore!

To reach A Renegade Stronghold, take the Sand Shallow Cottages settlement portal from Qalaba'r at 74.8S, 17.8E and run to the dungeon at 17.0S, 5.9E.

Map: A Renegade Stronghold

Creatures: Ascendant Tumerok, Tumerok Champion, Tumerok High Priest, Gotrok Extas, Gotrok Fortress Guard, Gotrok Tiatus

Gotrok Fortress Guard says, "Sound the alarm! We're under attack!"
Gotrok Fortress Guard tells you, "The ridicule of this death will never end. Why could you not be a drudge instead?"

To find the key go right at the entrance and keeping bearing right. The key spawns on a table in the 3rd large room.

Once you have your key, head to Samsur and take the Victory Residential Quarters portal to Khayyaban at 3.2S, 19.1E. From Khayyaban, take the Desert Vanguard Cottages portal at 49.4S, 26.0E then run to 34.7S, 12.1E (or nearby). You'll find a Renegade Chest guarded by Gotrok Extas, Tumerok Taskmasters and a Tumerok Major.

Use the key on the chest to open it. Be careful! The chest casts Flame Bolt VI when unlocked! Inside the chest you'll find a Renegade Bone Charm, 100 Tumerok Salted Meats, and a note titled Salted Meat. The Meats are heavy! You may just want to carry a few for emergencies and store the rest.

Text: Salted Meat
