 The following information is provided as a courtesy until I can research the quest for a full write-up.
 

Tadesha Xian of SC reports:

Look around the plateau for a Tumerok called Aun Ralirea. When you kill him, he drops a clay figurine (be careful, looks like a cheap copper amulet) some random monster hides (one char of mine got a large matty hide (old) and thick grommie hide, my other a small matty and thick grommie) and the Ebon Spine Harpoon and also a random number of Ebon Spine Harpoons (same name but thrown weapon, large damage variance (something like 24-36), looks same as the Spear version)

The best area to find Aun Railrea is around the dam up on the plateau, he doesn't spawn in the lowlands at all.

Once you have the Clay Figurine, take it to the Hea Tumerok Town (can't recall name of it) and give it to an NPC called Aun Arantah @ 47.1N 90.3W [correction - should be Hea Arantah].

He will talk for a bit then give you a skill point! (Yay!)

With regards to the spear, it cannot be enchanted, same as hollow weapons. I gave mine to a spear using friend, but we cannot confirm yet if it does hollow damage, but I would hope so considering it has no plusses.

Maggie,
I can confirm that the harpoon he uses does Hollow damage. My first encounter with Ralirea was, unfortunately, in a matty robe.

His first swing did 90 slashing damage in the chest.

I was fully level 7 baned and level 7 protected. It's definitely Hollow.

Hope this help,

Ebon Spine Harpoon
A hefty harpoon made from the spine of an ebon gromnie found on the Marcescent Plateau of Marae Lassel, and bound in bands of coldly glittering chorizite. This example can be used as a spear.
