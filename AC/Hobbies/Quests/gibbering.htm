 From the Mired Hearts event.

The Oubliette gem is one of the rare drop portal gems introduced during the Mired Hearts event. It drops either from a Virindi Consul or a Virindi Adjudicator. When you use the gem, you'll be portaled into the Oubliette dungeon.

At the bottom of the dungeon deep inside a pit you'll find The Gibbering, a creature that looks to be a Virindi experiment gone bad! This drudge-headed beast is a mish-mash of monster parts, including Sclavus, Moarsman and Banderling. It drops the Gibbering Claw.

Before using your portal gem, you'll want to buff and bane fully. If you die, you will be unable to return to the dungeon unless you have another of the very rare Oubliette gems.

Creatures: Drudge Bloodletter, Drudge Cabalist, Peerless Drudge, Ascendant Drudge, Assailer, Devastator, Telumiat Hollow Minion, Virindi Adjudicator, Virindi Consul, The Gibbering

To reach the Gibbering head north until you reach a pit. Jump down, then head north again. Eventually, you'll reach a ramp heading down. On this level you'll find a second pit. The Gibbering is at the bottom, along with a Runed Chest (no lockpick).

The Gibbering casts Level VII debuffs and is vulnerable to acid attacks. Kill him and retrieve the Gibbering Claw from its corpse. The Gibbering re-spawns every 3 minutes.
