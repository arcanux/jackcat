Olthoi Eggs are dropped as prizes on the corpse of the Olthoi Queen on the isle of Marae Lassel. Skilled cooks can make these eggs into fabulous delicacies with powerful magical properties. 

Recipes
(Recommended cooking skill 320+)

Olthoi Toast
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Olthoi Egg = Olthoi Batter
3. Olthoi Batter + Bread = Olthoi Toast 

Olthoi Cake
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Olthoi Egg = Olthoi Batter
3. Flour + Olthoi Batter = Olthoi Cake Batter
4. Baking Pan + Olthoi Cake Batter = Olthoi Cake 

Olthoi Carrot Cake
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Olthoi Egg = Olthoi Batter
3. Flour + Olthoi Batter = Olthoi Cake Batter
4. Olthoi Cake Batter + Carrot = Olthoi Carrot Cake Batter
5. Baking Pan + Olthoi Carrot Cake Batter = Olthoi Carrot Cake 

Chocolate Olthoi Cake
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Olthoi Egg = Olthoi Batter
3. Flour + Olthoi Batter = Olthoi Cake Batter
4. Olthoi Cake Batter + Chocolate Powder = Chocolate Olthoi Cake Batter
5. Baking Pan + Chocolate Olthoi Cake Batter = Chocolate Olthoi Cake 

Olthoi Pumpkin Pie
1. Baking Pan + Pumpkin = Cooked Pumpkin
2. Cooked Pumpkin + Milk = Liquid Pumpkin
3. Liquid Pumpkin + Honey = Sweetened Pumpkin
4. Sweetened Pumpkin + Jar of Cinnamon = Spiced Pumpkin
5. Spiced Pumpkin + Olthoi Egg = Olthoi Pumpkin Pie Filling
6. Olthoi Pumpkin Pie Filling + Dough = Olthoi Pumpkin Pie 

Pickled Olthoi Egg
1. Brine + Olthoi Egg = Pickled Olthoi Egg

Hard Boiled Olthoi Egg
1. Cooking Pot + Othoi Egg = Hard Boiled Olthoi Egg

Fried Olthoi Egg
1. Frying Pan + Othoi Egg = Fried Olthoi Egg

Vesayen Style Fried Olthoi Egg
1. Hot Sauce + Olthoi Egg = Marinated Olthoi Egg
2. Frying Pan + Marinated Olthoi Egg = Vesayen Style Fried Olthoi Egg
