From A New Threat.

The exploration of the Blackmire Temple resulted in the release of Burun into Dereth from another world. Queen Elysa seeks information about these new creatures, many of which have established a camp outside the temple at a Blackmire Encampment. Fight the Burun and retrieve texts, then turn them in to Translator Aun Laokhe in Arwic (33.5N, 56.8E) for rewards.

Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "Hail budhi, I come from the council chambers of Ispar Elysatah with a request. She bids that willing souls investigate the origin of the dark magics that spread across Osteth and Palenqual. She has heard that new anima have spread from the place where the kah took place. Should you find anything of note, I would like to see it. I will be calling on the keh of the Nuuani for aid to understand the things you find. Ispar Elysatah wishes to reach a peaceful accord with this new Xuta."
Translator Aun Laokhe tells you, "Should you find any odd writing that the other translators cannot decipher, perhaps the keh of the Nuuani will help decipher those as well."

To reach the Blackmire Encampment head to Yanshi from Shoushi at 35.0S, 74.4E. Once in Yanshi head to the Swamp Temple Place portal at 10.3S, 45.7E. The Burun Camp is located at 21.7S, 47.7E.

You'll be fighting Burun Ruuk Adepts,
Burun Ruuk Ruffians, and Burun Ruuk Scamps (bane for acid protection). The burun randomly drop 6 texts, each on a separate 7 day timer.

Once you have your texts, head to Arwic to receive your rewards. Translator Aun Laokhe is located atop a high tower in the middle of town at 33.5N, 56.8E.

Note: There are two levels of rewards. If your character is under level 35, you will receive jewelry with level 3 spells and a blue orb. If you are over level 35, your rewards will have level 4 spells and the orb will be green.
