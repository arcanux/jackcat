From the Shining Runes and Shattered Hands event.

The following notes are provided as a courtesy until I can research a full write-up.

Thanks to Tectonic Rifts, Absolution and Daawn of FF, Magicdef of HG, Fury's apprentice of TD, Rak'Than Lograthsun of VT, Minnie the Minx of VT, Reapist of TD, Sword Win of HG, Pharo of HG, Spaghetti Bender of TD!

The quest is simple. You basically kill the White Rabbit (thanks Tectonic for your sacrifice). Loot the gem and port to dungeon. There are several new tyoes of rabbit from Midnight, Twilight, and Dusk with bunnies. There are quite alot and without melee you would prolly get nipped to death. At the end is "Son of Pooky" . He is a little harder to kill than his pappy, but not too difficult. He drops several keys to the rabbit hutch which seems to have a random reward. I got the "Ring around the Rabbit" ring spell which has the same stats as a slashing ring spell with a cooler effect. Sends 8 rabbits shooting out in a ring. Tectonic got the rabbit girth. Not sure what else there might be...possibly a full rabbit suit?

Thanks to Daawn and Tectonic Rifts for the hippity hoppity fun quest.

Absolution

The quest was pretty straight forward. At the end there was the son of pookie and he droped 9 keys on him. Used the keys on the hay stack behind him and you got either a new war ring spell (40-80 pts slashing damage and the graphics for it are a bunch of bunnies shooting out in a ring), or a paw. The paw apparently you can attach to a melee weapon to decrease the variance on it by 20%.
