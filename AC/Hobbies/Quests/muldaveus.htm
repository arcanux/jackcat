From the Friend and Foe event.

Turbine quest teaser: "Ten months ago, the Burun named Torgluuk turned on Niarltah and Muldaveus, the leaders of the Tumerok and Lugian Renegades. Since that day, their whereabouts have been unknown. Level 80 characters and above are being recruited by a wanderer who seeks to follow the Lugian Warlord's trail!"

Note: Even though Turbine's teaser states the quest is restricted to Levels 80+, lower levels may complete this quest.

To begin the quest, speak with Mancanza the Wanderer in Silyun. You can reach Silyun via the portal from Sanamar at 76.5N, 58.6W or by purchasing a portal gem from Luressi di Maki for an V note (buy a few before you begin).

NPC: Mancanza the Wanderer - 87.0N, 70.4W

Mancanza the Wanderer gives you Muldaveus Sightings.
Mancanza the Wanderer tells you, "I have been wandering these lands in search of the Lugian Renegade Muldaveus. My journey has not gotten me very far. All I have found so far is a torn journal page, and various rumors that he may have survived his tangle with the Burun. I have written down several of these Muldaveus "sightings", and I was wondering if you could help me investigate. Go to these places. See if you can bring me any clues to his whereabouts. Good Luck!"

Rumor: Muldaveus Sightings

You'll be collecting 4 pages in various locations. Once you have them, combine them and return them to  Mancanza.

Page - 1

Tumerok Mine - 7.9N, 61.3W
--Levels: 15+
--Map: Tumerok Mine
--Creatures: Augmented Drudge, Lich, Tumerok Gladiator, Tumerok Major, Tumerok Taskmaster

To reach the Mine head to Kara from Shoushi at 33.1S, 73.2E and take the Fort Tethana portal inside the Foundry Statue at 83.3S, 47.1E. From Teth, run to the Mine at 7.9N, 61.3W. Once inside, simply keep left from the drop, down a ramp then up a ramp to a small room. You'll find a pack guarded by a few Tumeroks.

Page - 2

The second page is purchased from a Merchant N of Neydisa Castle for 1 pyreal (look under misc items rather than books and papers). Take the Neydisa Castle portal from Holtburg at 41.9N, 33.4E and run NW to reach the Merchant's cottage.

 Merchant Cottage - 75.5N, 10.6E

Merchant tells you, "Pssst. I hear you are looking for a fellow named Muldaveus. A man recently came through here claiming to have information on the Renegade's whereabouts. He sold me a piece of paper, but I can't make any sense of it. If you're interested, check my wares."
Merchant tells you, "An excellent purchase."

Page - 3

To reach the camp containing the 3rd note, take the Azaroth Cottages settlement portal from Qalaba'r at 74.8S, 17.8E then take the Stonehold portal at 85.8N, 27.6W. It's a short run to the Mountain Camp. You'll find another pack on the ground, guarded by various creatures.

Mountain Camp - 72.3N, 17.0W
--Creatures: Merciless Monouga, Banderling Thrasher, Banderling Enforcer, Gold Golem, Diamond Golem, Destroyer Grievver

Page - 4

The 4th page is found in a bookcase inside a Guard Tower near Plateau. Buy  a Plateau Wedding invitation from a Wedding Planner to reach the tower.

Plateau Watchtower - 42.9N, 42.3W

You successfully combine pages 1 and 2 of the journal.
You successfully combine pages 1 through 3 of the journal.
You successfully combine pages 1 through 4 of the journal.

Return to Silyun for further instructions.

You give Mancanza the Wanderer Incomplete Journal - Pages 1-4.
Mancanza the Wanderer tells you, "The missing pages of Muldaveus' journal! Let's see if we can decipher what old Muldaveus has to say."
Mancanza begins reading the pages...
Mancanza the Wanderer tells you, "His journal speaks of an icy dungeon he was exploring in the Halaetan Islands. Go there. See if you can find anymore clues to his survival. I have a feeling we are close now."
Mancanza the Wanderer gives you Last Known Location of Muldaveus.

Rumor: Last Known Location of Muldaveus

To reach the Icy Hideout, take the Plains of Ruin near Silyun at 87.9N,m 69.7W. You'll exit at 90.7N, 51.2W, a short run from the dungeon.

Icy Hideout - 89.5N, 56.3W
--Levels: all
--Map: Icy Hideout
--Frenzied Fiun, Frost, Gelid, Horripal, Copper Gromnie, Miasma

The dungeon is fairly straightforward. Head down then take a right at the big rock. Make a left down the ramp to the south and at the bottom, make a right at the T. Take the tunnel down near the blue crystals, then you'll reach the acid pit. go through the pit quickly to reduce damage taken. Be careful not to get trapped by the Miasmas. On the other side of the acid, take the stairs then the ramp up to the surface portal. You'll find an Insignia Badge on the ground nearby.

Again return to Silyun for your reward!

You give Mancanza the Wanderer Insignia Badge.
Mancanza the Wanderer tells you, "What have you got there? An insignia? This appears to be the insignia of Muldaveus! This is proof that he is alive! It's a shame that we still don't know where he is, but at least we know that we are on his trail. Sadly, I have no further missions for you at this time. I can only hope that we find him someday."
Mancanza the Wanderer tells you, "Please take this coat as a token of my appreciation."
You've earned 4,500,000 experience.
Mancanza the Wanderer gives you Squalid Coat.
Mancanza the Wanderer gives you 5 Platinum Scarabs.
Mancanza the Wanderer gives you 2 Trade Notes (250,000).
