Casinos were introduced in April 2001's A Reign of Stone event. Each Casino has three gambling tables: a low-stakes, medium-stakes and high-stakes. Buy tokens from one of the "dealers" and trade them in to the corresponding NPC for prizes. Guides are also available for purchase.

A Guide to Arshid's Den of Iniquity
A Guide to Gan-zo's Den of Iniquity
A Guide to Monty's Den of Iniquity

Note: the Casino workers used to refuse trade notes over a "C", but now they accept all trade notes (except the Olthoi Pincer quest note). There are also Pawn Shopkeepers who will buy back many of your unwanted prizes.

During the Discoveries event, gambling rewards were updated! As a result, the cost of tokens went up as well. Gambling Tokens can be purchased up to 100 at a time and be stacked up to 100 a pack slot.

Cost:
Low Stakes tokens - 5,000 py
Mid Stakes tokens - 25,000 py
High Stakes tokens - 50,000 py

Aluvian  - Pack Scarecrow
Gharu'ndim - Pack Golem
Sho - Pack Drudge

Pack Dolls can be given to the owner of each Casino for special rewards. You can win Tiny Shards, a Golden Key, Pyreal Peas, or a prized Golden Gromnie Pack Toy with hip hopping motion! Golden Gromnies were a part of the Mansion Purchase Price with the addition of the Dark Majesty expansion, but otherwise, they serve no other purpose than as a toy to amaze your friends!

The various trophies can be used in quests or turned in to various Crafters and Collectors for additional rewards.

The Golden Keys can be used on the Large Golden Chests for phat lewt (12-16 items -- SIK-like rewards)! During A Swelling Tide, the lewt was updated and became even phatter!

Note: During A Gathering of Colors, Casino portal gems became available as rewards from the Tumerok Banners quest. See the quest write-up and Rewards Table for more information.

--NPC Shosa Kiroji Matanui in Hebian-To for Gem Of Worth to Sho Casino.
--NPC Lieutenant Kenneth in Cragstone for Gem Of Worth to Aluvian Casino.
--NPC Ufet Profet Of Blades in Zhaikal for Gem of Worth to Gharu'ndim Casino.

Note: During The Madness of Men event, it became possible to hand a stack of tokens at the casino with only one at a time being used by the NPC!

During From the Darkness Born, previously retired Pack Dolls once again became available. Complete Bleeargh's Gratitude quest for 6 Mosswart Thanking Stones. They can be gambled at any of the 3 Casinos for a chance to win one of these treasured prizes!
