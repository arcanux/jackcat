From the Under Cover of Night event.

Enrico's Betrayal - Level 50 or higher players can help the commander of the Viamontian invasion cover up evidence of his various betrayals.

Town Crier tells you, "I have often wondered, do all of the Duke's relatives share his philosophies? Are they all rebels, or do some of them still follow their king?"

Retrieve three pieces of lore for Enrico di Bellenesse in Greenspire and he'll reward you with 400,00 xp, an MMD note and 10 philters for each!

NPC: Enrico di Bellenesse - 43.2N, 67.1W

To reach Greenspire, take the portal from Yaraq at 21.7S, 1.8W.

 Enrico di Bellenesse tells you, "I know not where your loyalties lie, but if you seek no more than coin, I have a task for you."
Enrico di Bellenesse tells you, "There are three letters scattered about this isle that once belonged to me. I would have them in my possession once again, but I am unable to task any of my soldiers with this mission. I must instead turn to mercenaries such as yourself."
Enrico di Bellenesse tells you, "The job is simple. Travel to the Lair of the Eviscerators, the Small Hive, and the Breached Hive. Within each of those dungeons will be a note. Find those notes and return them to me."
Enrico di Bellenesse tells you, "But you must exercise discretion in this task. These letters are dear to me. Do not read them, do you understand? Simply pick them up and bring them back to me."

Although Enrico instructs you not to read the letters, you won't be penalized if you sneak a peek! All three dungeons can be found on the Plateau of Marae Lassel. To reach the Plateau, you can take the Bluespire ports from Yaraq at 21.7S, 1.3W and take the Marescent Plateau portal at 42.0N, 77.6W. In each dungeon you'll need to find a Royal Chest.

Small Hive - Random Spawn, ML Plateau
--Levels: 40+
--Map: Small Hive
--Creatures: Olthoi Noble, Olthoi Nymph, Olthoi Soldier, Olthoi Worker
--Text: To My Dear Uncle

From the drop take the south passage. Keep left and head down the wide ramp. Take the west passage and you'll find the Royal Chest in the room above where the Rubble spawns.

Breached Hive - 49.8N, 73.1W
--Levels: 50+
--Map:
--Creatures: Olthoi Noble, Olthoi Piercer, Olthoi Eviscerator, Eater, Engorged Eater
--Text: A Warning

From the entrance, keep right, then go straight heading south at the 4 way. Keep south in general until you head down a wide ramp and enter the eater room. Exit the eater room to the south and follow the ramps up to the Royal Chest.

Lair of the Eviscerators - 53.6N, 76.6W
--Levels: 50+
--Map: Lair of the Eviscerators
--Creatures: Olthoi Eviscerator
--Text: Augmentation

You won't need to fight to reach the chest in the Lair of the Eviscerators. From the drop simply keep right until you enter a room. The chest is on the floor near some stone benches.

Return to Greenspire and turn in the Letters to Enrico di Bellenesse for you reward!
