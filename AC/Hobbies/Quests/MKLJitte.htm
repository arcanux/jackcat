 Mi Krau-Li's Jitte
Mi Krau-Li's Jitte is found deep within the Catacombs of the Forgotten (17.3N, 32.8E Restricted to Levels 15+). The best route to the dungeon is by running from either Cragstone or Glendon Wood. Inside, you can take either of the passageways as they will eventually lead you down to the same room. Initially, you'll pass through a series of chambers full of Skeletons. Missile Defense and Piercing Protection are a must! The chambers walls are spaced with recesses containing coffins. They're great places to duck out of the way to heal. 

After each chamber, you'll take a ramp down to the next level. As you make your way lower, you'll also begin to encounter Skeleton Warriors and Skeleton Captains. Eventually, you'll enter a room with three Skeleton Captains and with passageways leading in all four directions. If you choose the wrong passageway, you'll encounter a nasty spell casting Skeleton Lord. The path you seek should be directly across from the door you came through. Head up a short ramp and you'll be in a room containing Mi Krau-Li's Sarcophagus. Inside the sarcophagus you'll find a Note from Mi Krau-Li which will help you on the 2nd half of the quest.

To the right of the sarcophagus is a passageway with another ramp heading up. Continue bearing right through this section of the dungeon and you'll eventually come to a room with an altar. Spawning on the floor beside it is Mi Krau Li's Jitte. You'll recognize the room by its soft greenish glow. To locate the Jitte more easily, you can also use your ID keys on your keyboard: [ and ]. The area is populated by Skeleton Warriors and an occasional Skeleton Lord, so be careful! 

Mi Krau-Li's Improved Jitte
To improve the Jitte, you'll need to find Mi Krau Li, located inside Krau-Li's Labyrinth 35.1S, 23.8E (Restricted to 25+), North of Khayyaban or SW of Uziz. The inside is treacherous and confusing, with winding passageways, locked doors, and tricky lever puzzles. There are four switches inside that open a series of doors leading to Mi Krau Li. You'll face dangerous Skelton Captains, Skeleton Lords, and Skeleton Bone Knights. Deeper within, you may even encounter a few Bone Lords and a deadly Great Skeleton. You'll need to be quick as the doors don't stay open for long! 

First Trigger - Torch
From the entrance, head north (straight ahead). Take a left at the first intersection then steer to the right. You'll end up in a hall facing north containing a few Skeleton Captains and one Skeleton Lord. Kill them and head north again to a square room. Take the hallway north from there and follow it all the way until it heads right (east). Take the first north passage until you come to a room with a torch -- the first trigger. You'll have to kill three Lords in this room.

Second Trigger
Head back south the way you came until you hit the hallway from the very first intersection and go through the door the switch opened Stay to the right heading west. You'll go down a ramp. At the bottom you'll have to kill two Skeleton Lords, one to your right and one to your left. From there run straight heading south and kill the Bone Knight guarding the second switch. 

Third Trigger
After pulling the 2nd Lever, you can either head back to where you started or use the surface portal to get back to the entrance. From the switch: Run back the way you came (north). Then follow the right wall until you get to the first hallway, near the entrance. From the entrance, take the east branch of the first intersection of the Labyrinth and keep to your left at all times. It winds in circles like a maze. You'll end up in a small square room with three Lords in it. Head down, going east, and take the first left (north). Follow the north passage until it turns right (east) and you'll run right into the door. With luck (and quick feet!) it will still be open!

Fourth Trigger
After pulling the third lever, head back to the door and take a left (heading south). Follow the wall and you'll run into the third door, now open. You'll immediately have to kill a Skeleton Bone Knight and a few Captains. Stay to the right the whole way up until it branches. At that point, take a right. You'll have to kill a few more Skeleton Lords here. The next right will put you into the big room with the two Bone Knights and the Bone Lord who are guarding a lever -- the fourth trigger. You can run back the way you came, but an easier way is to take the surface portal nearby. If you're fast, you can actually run in and pull the lever and make a dash for the portal without having to stop and fight the deadly skeletons. 

Finding Mi Krau-Li
The final switch opens the door directly ahead of the entrance portal. Inside are three Captains and a Lord. After killing them, proceed down the ramp. You'll have to deal with four more Lords at the bottom -- two to the right, and two to the left. The room at the bottom of the ramp splits off to the left and right. The left (west) passage is bad; the right (east) passage is good. The left takes you to a room with nothing but two Bone Lords and a Great Skeleton. The right passage takes you to another Bone Knight, but past the Knight is a bridge and past the bridge is Mi Krau-Li.

Give Mi Krau Li the Jitte in your possession in return for Mi Krau Li's Improved Jitte.

To exit the dungeon, you can jump off the bridge; an exit portal lies below.

An exceiptionally well-balanced jitte, the weapon of Mi Krau-Li.

+5% Attack
