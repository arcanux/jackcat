From the Throne of Destiny Expansion.

Although many Eater jaws have specific quest purposes, they can also be turned in to Clemenza for xp rewards!

WARNING: If you plan to use your jaw for a quest, don't turn it in to Clemenza!

NPC: Clemenza 72.8N, 73.4W (Westwatch)

Timer: 3 weeks

Clemenza tells you, "I am looking for warriors of exceptional ability to test against the kingdom's weapons. There are five Eater dens that I wish you to infiltrate. Return to me with the jaws of the beasts therein and I will reward you appropriately."

Remote Empyrean Vault - 81.7N, 71.2W   
--Levels: 40+
--Map: Remote Empyrean Vault
--Item: Engorged Eater Jaw
--Title: Engorged Scourge
--Reward: 500,000 xp, Trade Note (75,000)
--Creatures: Raving Fiun, Famished Eater, Eater, Engorged Eater

From the drop keep to the right, heading down 2 levels until you get to the brewmaster's front cover. Take the passage to the west, directly across from the cover and head down the ramp. Keep to the left until you come to a pit. Jump below, then head North. At the T, turn left and take the ramp down to the west. Take the next ramp down to the east, then keep south until the final room. Kill the engorged eater for its jaw.

You give Clemenza Engorged Eater Jaw.
Clemenza tells you, "Very good. Though this jaw comes from the weakest of the King's weapons. If you wish for greater rewards than these, try your hand at a beast of a little more strength."
You've earned 500,000 experience.
Clemenza gives you Trade Note (75,000).
Clemenza gives you Engorged Scourge Token.

You give Clemenza Engorged Scourge Token.
Clemenza tells you, "Daughter of Viamont, I do now grant you the title of Engorged Scourge."
 

Ruined Empyrean Vault - 85.9N, 59.0W

--Map: Ruined Empyrean Vault
--Levels: 70+
--Item: Voracious Eater Jaw
--Title: Voracious Flayer
--Reward: 5,000,000 xp, Trade Note (100,000)
--Creatures: Voracious Eater

From the drop, keep left, going up two levels until you reach a large square open room. Take the NW passage out of the room and keep to the right until you reach a pit. Jump below, and again keep to the right until you reach a 2nd pit. Jump again, and kill the voracious eater to the NE to retrieve the jaw.

The force of your killing blow violently dislocates Voracious Eater's jaw!

You give Clemenza Voracious Eater Jaw.
Clemenza tells you, "Nice work. You have proven yourself a competent warrior. There are, however, many beasts of greater ability that await you in the world beyond."
You've earned 5,000,000 experience.
Clemenza gives you Trade Note (100,000).
Clemenza gives you Voracious Flayer Token.


Abhorrent Vault - 88.5N, 50.4W 

--Map: Abhorrent Vault
--Levels: 90+
--Item: Abhorent Eater Jaw
--Title: Abhorent Slayer
--Reward: 15,000,000 xp, MMD Trade Note
--Other quest uses: ToD Skill Credit
--Creatures: Abhorrent Eater

From the drop head west and jump into the pit. At the bottom of the pit, head east and jump again. Keep to the left, continuing down 3 more ramps until you see a ramp to the south. Follow the dungeon south and down to the final room.

The force of your killing blow violently dislocates Abhorrent Eater's jaw!

You give Clemenza Abhorrent Eater Jaw.
Clemenza tells you, "Very nice. You are indeed a warrior of much skill. We may well call upon your services in the future."
You've earned 15,000,000 experience.
Clemenza gives you Trade Note (250,000).
Clemenza gives you Abhorrent Warrior Token.


Ravenous Vault - 92.8N, 48.9W 

--Map: Ravenous Vault
--Levels: 110+
--Item: Ravenous Eater Jaw
--Title: Ravenous Slayer
--Reward: 35,000,000 xp, MMD Trade Note, D Note
--Other quest uses: ToD Skill Credit
--Creatures: Ravenous Eater

From the drop, hop down to the east (don't take the north pit!). Hop north down two more intervals. Take the east ramp down until you reach the passage south. Keep to the left, taking a ramp up, then down, circling around the hallway until you reach a split. Take a right at the split, then keep to the right all the way to the final room.

The force of your killing blow violently dislocates Ravenous Eater's jaw!

You give Clemenza Ravenous Eater Jaw.
Clemenza tells you, "Wonderful. You are a warrior of incredible ability. The Kingdom of Viamont would do well to enlist your aid in our continuing fight against the Bloodless."
You've earned 35,000,000 experience.
Clemenza gives you Trade Note (50,000).
Clemenza gives you Trade Note (250,000).
Clemenza gives you Ravenous Killer Token.

Insatiable Vault - 97.6N, 48.0W  

--Map: Insatiable Vault
--Levels: 125+
--Item: Insatiable Eater Jaw
--Title: Insatiable Slayer
--Reward: 40,000,000 xp, 2 MMD Trade Notes
--Other quest uses: Augmentation Gems, ToD Skill Credit
--Creatures: Insatiable Eater

From the drop, jump down and head west down a ramp. Keep to the left and head down another ramp. Jump below, and take the west passage, keeping to the left again until you reach another pit (the dungeon is symmetrical, so you can keep right as well). Jump again, and keep to the right til you reach an alcove with the eater.

Note: each of the 4 alcoves in the hallways above the exit portal contain jaw bearing eaters.

The force of your killing blow violently dislocates Insatiable Eater's jaw!

Note: Clemenza doesn't know what to do with the Marauder's Jaw used in the Faction quest.
