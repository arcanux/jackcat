Stormwaltz comments on a bug with the Awakener on CoD's Game Issues board. 

"Basically there's a problem with the specialized check. It works fine for enchantments, but it doesn't seem to work properly for endowments (that is, targeted spells attached to and cast through a wand, orb, or stave)."

Creature Enchantment must be specialized for it to work.

You can read the thread here.

(Thanks to Elder of MT and Erie of DT for pointing out the thread.)

The Awakener is a Tumerok Drum. It casts Nullify All Magic Other, has a Difficulty of 110, and you must have Creature Enchantment specialized to use it.
 

Update from A Gathering of Colors

Awakener is no longer PK only! From Rosso of LC: "And concerning the Awakener -- it casts Nullify other on PKs and NPKs, but only dispels negative enchantments! Woot! Talk about your best friend on dangerous quests. It's like having unlimited dispel gems, as long as you have a friend who has creature magic specialized and lots of spare mana stones ;) It uses 500 mana a shot and only stores 1000 mana :P

Hea Karenua's casting buadren. This is a fine old drum from the Tumerok world, rich with fetishes and power. The bones of centuries-dead drudges, mosswarts, and banderlings, rattle against one another in the wind. There is an inscription around the rim which reads, "To release one's brother from the clinging threads of a hateful dream."
