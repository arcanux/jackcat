From the Mirror, Mirror event.

The Doomshark Hide Coat was introduced when the Singularity Caul was updated during the Mirror, Mirror event. To reach the Caul you must be flagged by killing the Guardian of the Singularity, a level 100 Virindi found at the end of the Singularity Bore (see the Quintessence Sickle quest for details). The dungeon is restricted to levels 50+. You can also use the Singularity Caul Recall spell.

Note: The Caul is the island Bael'zharon called players to run "The Guantlet", an invitation only quest to receive an Atlan Shadow Stone and the title of Dark Master. A group of Virindi led by Levistras chose the Caul as the location to build a new Singularity before they were defeated.

The coat is made from the Doomshark Hide dropped by a Level 155 Reedshark, Doomshark (rare drop). Doomsharks are rumored to spawn after Umbral Rifts are killed. They are found on the western and northern parts of the Caul (reported coords: 94.6S, 96.7W). Use Frost and Pierce attacks for best results.

Give the hide to a Leather Crafter in exchange for the Doomshark Hide Coat.

You give Leather Crafter Doomshark Hide.
Leather Crafter tells you, "I've seen some interesting hides pass my way, but the hides that people have been bringing me most recently are the oddest."
Leather Crafter tells you, "A few more threads...and done!"
Leather Crafter gives you Doomshark Hide Coat.
Leather Crafter tells you, "Be careful out there."
