From the Betrayal event.

If you chose to save Nuhmudira in the Gelidite Library quest, you will be flagged to complete the Nuhmudira's Boon quest. The Boon quest allows you to craft magical gorgets imbued with helpful spells. The elixirs used to infuse the pyreal gorgets are made from Ruined Amulets found on various Dereth creatures.

Note: During A Swelling Tide, a new use was introduced for the Amulets. Use them (amulets, not the potions) on Sunstone Gauntlets to imbue them with a moderate aptitude spell for your skill!

After you appear at the Zaikhal lifestone after saving Nuhmudira, head into town and speak to the Agent of the Arcanum at 13.2N, 0.3E (you can actually speak to an agent in any town). You'll be given a letter to take to a Royal Guard outside of Lin.

Letter to Balthazar
Please allow this person to assist you in your task. They have demonstrated the kind of character that we are looking for in this endeavor.

Take the Letter to Balthazar, a Royal Guardian stationed just outside of Lin at 54.8S, 74.4E. (You can reach Lin by running from Nanto, or by taking the West Mayoi Mountain Villas settlement portal at 60.8S, 80.3E, exiting at 61.1S, 73.7E).

Ruined Amulets are no-drop, no-give; however the elixirs may be given. Elixirs may also be stacked in groups of 5.
