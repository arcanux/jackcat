 I made my Baudren drum, and the Aun guy says it was just a test to see if I'm a powerful warrior, yadda yadda, and tells me to see Aun Aulahke (everything he says is on your site already under the Baudren Walkthru).... I talk to Aulahke and he gives this story:

Aun Aulahkhe tells you, "Several moons ago, my father Shimauri summoned us to his accustomed story hour, to tell of a story never heard by my generation."

Aun Aulakhe tells you, "Long ago, in the combined pasts of the Hea and Aun, Aun Tanua was a nigh-legendary warrior who was beloved by our xuta. He left our gates to challenge the Puh in his mudflats of Ahurenga, never to return. We thought perhaps he had been captured or slain by the Hea, but now we have discovered the truth: when they were unable to conquer him, the Hea called upon the Atua ngamaru. Answering them, the floating demons bore down and plunged him down into the depths of the Lightning-Sea never to be heard of again."

Aun Aulakhe tells you, "My task is to find warriors brave enough to enter the Lightning-Sea, who can search for him and bring him back to us. My buhdi Tiakakhe tells me that you might be the person we are looking for."

Aun Aulakhe tells you, "When you find him, warm him with this ember of the akiekie fire, for there is no telling how the Aun Tanua's mind might have fared in his captivity."

Aun Aulakhe gives you Akiekie Ember.

Aun Aulakhe tells you, "My training, both in my homeland and with the Aualuan of your xuta, has taught me to summon the Burning Pools that shimmer upon the Lightning-Sea. I will open such a pool for you, that you may enter and find Aun Tanua."

Aun Aulakhe tells you, "A word of advice: to navigate the Shimmering Sea, the strong must rely upon the weak. Farewell, and Tanae speed you in your hunt for Aun Tanua."

Aun Aulakhe murmers a prayer to calm the waters of the Lightning-Sea. Slowly, a portal starts to shimmer in the depths of the akiekie pond.
