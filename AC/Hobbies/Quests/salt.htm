10 Black Pages of Salt and Ash
A page of an ancient book with runes that are unintelligible, but still seem to make sense to your eyes...
Use this page to infuse your wielded weapon with the Invocation of the Black Book, adding a 10% bonus to its Attack modifier. This spell stacks with other spells and cantrips.
