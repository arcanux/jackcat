From the Mirror, Mirror event.

The Puppeteer's Skull was introduced when the Singularity Caul was updated during the Mirror, Mirror event. To reach the Caul you must be flagged by killing the Guardian of the Singularity, a level 100 Virindi found at the end of the Singularity Bore (see the Quintessence Sickle quest for details). The dungeon is restricted to levels 50+. You can also use the Singularity Caul Recall spell.

Note: The Caul is the island Bael'zharon called players to run "The Guantlet", an invitation only quest to receive an Atlan Shadow Stone and the title of Dark Master. A group of Virindi led by Levistras chose the Caul as the location to build a new Singularity before they were defeated.

The skull is dropped by a Level 145 Marionette, the Puppeteer (rare drop). The Puppeteer is found on the NW part of the Caul (reported coords: 91.3S, 98.9W). Use Cold and Slashing for best results. The Puppeteer casts a spell called "Puppet Sting" which reduces all target's attributes by 25 for one minute.

Scully of FF sends this report: "The spell Wrath of the Puppeteer works different than volleys or blast spells in that it shoot the 3 bolts in succession toward the same target, hitting it 3 times." See pic to left for Lash of SC demonstrating the effect!
