 Asmolum's Throwing Daggers originally appeared during the Should the Stars Fall event. Asmolum the Worm--one time spymaster to Lord Inekkere (Level 277), was slain near the Catheral at Ithaenc on the Vesayen Isles. He was accompanied by Anadil of Shakrassekor (Level 516), one of the Great Sand Kings and leader of the Dericost forces in the campaign against the Hopeslayer and their protection of the soul stones. Although Anadil's remains, along with the splitered parts to his Staff, were placed in the Tomb of the Sand King, nothing remains of Asmolum and his hollow Throwing Daggers.
