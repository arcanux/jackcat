 The Serpent's Fang is a trophy from the Cold One, a special Sclavus introduced in the Keep Your Enemies Closer event. The approximate drop rate for the Spear is about one in four.

The Cold One can be found in the Direlands Swamps around the Obsidian Plains. They have also been found on the Vesayan Isles (the island with Jungle Shadows). Like all the new boss creatures, the Cold One can be scarce and difficult to find. The creature is also more difficult to defeat than its Sclavus counterparts. It has an exceptional ability to heal and is also well versed in the art of War Magic. That fang has quite the bite!

During the Come What Follows event, Belinda du Loc in Stonehold developed the ability to enhance numerous trophy items. Hand your fang to her and she will return the enhanced version. Original pre-patch weapons cannot be enhanced.

NPC: Belinda du Loc - 68.9N, 21.6W (Stonehold)
