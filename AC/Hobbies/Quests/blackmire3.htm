From the Treaties in Stone event.

A new NPC appeared in Candeth Keep and spoke of some undead he found near the south entrance of VoD. These undead guard the entrance of the Halls of Hizk Ri, the third Blackmire quest dungeon!

Finn Skigg tells you, "Good day! I see that you're an adventurous sort. So am I, my dear friend! I must admit that I have been in better spirits than I am at the moment, but the sight of another adventurer has caused a smile to spread across my face."
Finn Skigg tells you, "You see, I have just finished a jaunt across and all around Dereth. I managed to make the walk in about eighty days. I've only recently settled here in the pleasantly arid air of Candeth Keep. I had a particularly nasty run-in with some foul undead near the southern entrance of the Valley of Death."
Finn Skigg tells you, "While making my way into a valley, I was accosted by these foul looking undead beasts. They gave me such a fright that I lost a collection of rare plates I'd received from Baron Eli von Schreft back on Ispar. They're priceless, and I would certainly be pleased if someone were to bring them back to me. If you find them please bring them here."

The teamwork of a fellowship will be required to complete this quest. This blackmire has three level breaks. The sections are divided as follows: 60-79, 80-99, 100+. Bring several people for each level section you are completing. Stock up on Health Elixirs and Dispel Gems, and bring a Healer's Heart or, Attenuated Awakener.  You'll be fighting Undead and Guruk, so bring Fire or Slash to fight and a cold weapon.

Timer: 28 days

To reach the Halls of Hizk Ri, pop over to the VoD lifestone at 48.0S, 65.2W, or take the Obsidian Rim portal at 5.5N, 87.5W (take NW dires portal from the Aerlinthe drop). You'll exit at 48.0S, 65.0W, a short run from the temple.

Halls of Hizk Ri: 51.5S, 67.9W
Levels: 60+

Halls of Hizk Ri Entrance
Only one person in your fellowship should speak to the Guardian in the entrance room. You'll be given a riddle which must be answered to proceed. The rest of your party should stand by the south wall and wait for the door to be opened.

Guardian tells you, "You have entered the halls of Hizk Ri, the Matron of Pain. Your presence is unwelcome. You have disturbed a perfect order that has existed for eons. What right have you to enter these halls, cur?"
Guardian tells you, "Do not answer. Your presence marks a return of the ancient ways. You shall be tested and if not found wanting, you shall find your way to the seat of the Matron's power and wake her from her slumber."
Guardian tells you, "I pray that you fail, for power such as Hizk Ri's should never be loosed upon the world. We have suffered for millennia under her cruel wiles."
Guardian tells you, "The pillars scattered about the room are devoted to the bodies that hang in the sky above. A riddle seals their secret and bars entrance to the halls beyond. I shall issue this riddle after this final warning: return to me for judgment only when the riddle is solved. If you fail to do so you will be struck dead where you stand. If you return to me with only half the puzzle solved you will be struck dead where you stand. You have been warned."
Guardian tells you, "The riddle is as follows: Au, the light of this world, hangs in the east, opposed to star of portent. To the north, the moon run red with blood watches its twin in the south. Prove your worth, or die trying."

Four plates lie scattered on the floor with more pillars nearby. Your job is to give the correct plate to the correct pillar before returning to the Guardian. The answer is below:

North Pillar - Red Moon
South Pillar - Yellow Moon
East Pillar - Sun
West Pillar - Star

Once all four plates have been placed, return to the Guardian. When you speak to it a second time, the door to the south will slide open. Hurry to the door to catch up with your group!

Guardian tells you, "You may advance through the halls of Hizk Ri, Matron of Pain. Be warned, you dwell amongst the hopeless and cruel."

Down ramp are three choices of path. Choose the one that corresponds to your group's level. Follow the hallway and jump into the pit, then continue to a wide ramp fighting the undead.

Red: 100+ - Tormented Patriarch

Use the torch on the wall to open a door at the bottom of the ramp, then use the Guardian inside to be portalled to the next section.

When you speak to the Guardian at the drop, you'll be instructed to bring back a phial of bloody tears in order to continue.

Map: Well of Tears

Guardian tells you, "If you wish to meet Hizk Ri you shall need to garner her sister's tears for me. You will need to collect them in an ancient philter, lost somewhere in this crypt. Collect them from the well and return them to me and I shall then let you pass. Go now and return to me only when you can hand me the phial of bloody tears."

To reach the Phial, take the north passage and head up a ramp. The Ancient Enchanted Philter is a small blue bottle lying on the ground beside a table. Once you have the phial, continue down the passage, keeping to your right. After you go down three ramps, keep to your left until you reach the room with the Well of Tears, a stone structure full of blood. The eerie sound of a woman crying can be heard in the air. Use the phial on the well to fill it with tears.

As you dip the philter into the well it fills with blood tears.

Once everyone has the phial filled, run as fast as you can back the Guardian to avoid the respawn. Give the phial to the Guardian (one at a time as a precaution) to be portalled to the jumping section.

Halls of Hizk Ri - Jumping Section

Map: Jumping Section

This is the most challenging section of the quest, especially if you're chronically jumping impaired, as I am! You will need to jump from platform to platform, using four eyes that are mounted on the walls. The eyes glow with red, yellow and green hues. Each level section will use different eyes corresponding to the door they first entered: red for 100+, yellow for 80-99 and green for 60-79. As you use the eyes, you'll receive a debuff spell.

If your jump skill ranges from 330 - 430, you will be able to make your way around the room by using full, 1/2 and 1/4 shift jumps (keep the shift key held down for the entirety of the jump). Start to the right as you enter and work your way around, jumping to the platform behind the strange device after the first two eyes to get to the other side of the room. One of the debuffs, Chained to the Wall, is a jumping debuff! Use a dispel gem before continuing! Once you've used all four eyes, you'll be flagged to use the Strange Device. (To get back to the platform, you may need to use a full power jump, aiming for the back wall.)

If you fall, and it's likely someone in your party will (especially if you're questing with me!), you'll need to quickly run to the south, dodging the flying daggers from the Wailing Statues! To get back up to the jump start, you'll have to fight your way through the tormented undead. Jump into the pit then head up the east ramp. Make the first right, then an immediate left up another ramp. Take the west passage from the next room and use the button to open the door. From here there's only one route to the top. The final door is guarded by a wailing statue. Be careful as you use the button on wall to pass through.

A loud hum fills the room and for a moment you feel waves of mana washing over your body. You are certain that something has changed.

Hizk Ri Sewers

Map: Hizk Ri Sewers

The sewer section is full of Guruk, so pull out your frost weapon! Below are the different types of Guruk found in each section:

Hizk Ri Sewers - Levels 100+
--Creatures: Guruk Colossus, Guruk Behemoth, Guruk Gorefiend, Guruk Titan

Hizk Ri Sewers - Levels 80-99
--Creatures: Guruk Smasher, Guruk Beast, Guruk Brute, Guruk Destroyer, Guruk Hulk

Hizk Ri Sewers - Levels 60-79
--Creatures: unknown

The virulent air burns your lungs for 7 points of damage!

From the drop you can keep either left, or right the entire way through to reach the final room. Your route will be slightly longer than other routes through, but you won't get lost in the twists and turns. When you reach the final room, use the button on the SW wall to open the north door. Use the Guardian inside to be portaled to the boss room. Keep absolutely still as you come out of portal space!

Levels 100+:
Guardian teleports you with Patriarch Zixki.

Levels 80-99:
Guardian teleports you with Consort Hezhit.

Levels 60-79: unknown

Hizk Ri Boss Room

Map: Hizk Ri Boss Room

Have everyone stand still as they enter the boss room so you can coordinate your attack. When you move, undead will spawn and attack. Your goal is to kill the boss in order to open the door to the Crypt on the north wall. The following creatures are found in each sectioin:

Patriarch Zixki - Levels 100+
--Creatures: Tormented Patriarch, Zixki

Consort Hezhit - Levels 80-99
--Creatures: Tormented Consort, Hezhit

Unknown Dugeon - Levels 60-79
--Creatures: Tormented Attendant, unknown

When everyone has downloaded the room and is ready, run quickly to the NW hallway to fight in a controlled setting. The undead will follow you, casting debuffs. Use your dispel gems or you could find yourself in trouble! When the mob is under control, return to the large center room to attack the boss. It will randomly portal throughout the room to escape your attacks. It may also portal some in your group to one of 8 cells in the corners. If you get portalled to jail, use the torch inside the cell to open the hall behind you. The undead trapped down the hall has the key to escape. Be careful! The halls will hit you with damage unless you walk on the wood baseboards. If you're group is large enough, simply stay put and have your friend free you when the boss is dead.

Be prepared to have someone hurry to the north door as soon as the boss dies. The door only stays open for a short time. Once someone is through, they can open it from the other side after everyone is free from the cells. At the end of the north hallway is the final Guardian. Use it to enter the Crypt of Hizk Ri.

Guardian teleports you with Hizk Ri's Crypt.

Hizk Ri's Crypt

Map: Hizk Ri's Crypt

Like the other Blackmire quests, use the Sarcophagus first to receive Ancient Armored Gauntlets and Boots.

Sarcophagus of High Matriarch, Hizk Ri gives you Ancient Armored Gauntlets.
Sarcophagus of High Matriarch, Hizk Ri gives you Ancient Armored Long Boots.

You may also receive one additional prize, a portal to Izji Qo's Temple, a Crossbow or Mace, or a fellowship buff scroll. If you choose one of the fellowship spells, you will receive all four levels of that spell (4-7), no matter which section of the quest you went on. The weapons, however, have three different versions. Fellowship scrolls may be given.

Portal to Izji Qo's Temple gives you Portal to Izji Qo's Temple.

Note: You must remain in the fellowship until you receive both your rewards. If you are not in a fellowship, you'll receive this message:

You are not able to wrest the rewards from the cold grasp of the matron alone.

If you try and receive another reward before your timer is up, you will get the following message:

You feel the presence of the High Matriarch watching you and withdraw your hand. You are not beneath her notice it seems. Perhaps if you wait the cycle of the moons.
