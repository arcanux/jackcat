From the Throne of Destiny Expansion.

A little vitae got you down? The quest for Snow Lilies can help to ease your pain. To begin, speak to Lila Fiori in Silyun. To reach Silyun, you can take the portal outside of Sanamar at 76.6N, 58.7W or you can get portal gems to Silyun from Luressi di Maki in Sanamar in exchange for I trade notes.

NPC: Lila Fiori - 86.8N, 70.3W (Silyun)

Lila Fiori tells you, "What am I to do? The Grand Mother has placed an order for fresh Snow Lilies, but I am all out. Can you help me?"
Lila Fiori tells you, "Go to the Isle of Ruin and bring me back three fresh Snow Lilies. They are very rare so you will have to search for them. Be careful when picking them as they tend to attract hungry creatures."

Snow Lilies are found on the Isle of Ruin. You can reach it via the nearby portal to the Plains of Ruin at 87.9N, 69.7W. (You can also run from the Fiun Outpost or take the Eastwatch portal out of Sanamar at 71.2N, 61.3W.)

When you find a Snow Lily, your first inclination will be to pick it. But be careful! Snow Lilies work the same way as the Cultist Altars on Marae Lassel. When you use one (double-click), several creatures will spawn: two Bloated Eaters, an Insolent Penguin and a Mudwort Thrungus.

Run! When you're at a safe distance, head back and fight the creatures one by one. It's easiest to take them down with both a mage and a melee. When the creatures have been killed, check their corpses for snow lilies. When you have three, return to Lila Fiori for your xp reward and a Snow Flake Charm necklace!
