From the Reprisals event.

Town Crier tells you, "Another tragedy has befallen Fiun Gaya, who tends Lunnum's Pyre in the Halaetan Isles. He is seeking assistance from those who will go to help him."

Town Crier tells you, "Fiun Gaya is seeking assistance in trying to find something he, in his ineptitude, lost. Stupid Fiun. They really are good for nothing more than Eater-bait."

Note: You must first complete the Luminous Robe quest to be flagged for the Thief of Lunnum quest.

To begin, speak to Fiun Gaya. To reach Fiun Gaya, take the Sanamar portal from Shoushi at 34.3S, 73.4E then take the Eastwatch Outpost portal at 71.2N, 61.3W. Alternatively, have someone tie to the Path of the Blind surface portal to shorten travel time.

NPC: Fiun Gaya - 89.1N, 47.6W

Fiun Gaya tells you, "You have aided me with the light. I ask you help again, but this is a dark deed that you must uncover."
Fiun Gaya tells you, "In the night a thief crept to the Pyre while I was asleep. I awoke the moment the Pyre was disturbed, but quick and sneaky was the thief. It stayed in darkness, but managed to snatch Lunnum from the light. I raged at the thief, but alas I was too weak to stop it. I remember red eyes and then the fog of dreams."
Fiun Gaya tells you, "My need is to be here and keep the light. Your need is to catch the thief and return Lunnum. Please, find the thief, she must be returned to the light!"

Fiun Gaya tells you, "Hurry, you need to find the thief who stole Lunnum. She must be returned to the light! The thief was fairly large, perhaps it left some footprints nearby."

Follow the footsteps north and you'll find the deserted encampment of the thief. A Ruschk Totem rests on the ground near his pallet and a dye pot with a bit of blue.

If you try to pick up a 2nd totem you'll get the following message:
You have solved this quest too many times!

 Thief's Encampment - 90.4N, 47.6W

The description on the totem gives you a clue where to hunt next. Head to the Ruschk Camp at 83.1N, 54.8W and speak to the leader.

Ruschk Camp - 83.1N, 54.8W
--Creatures: Ruschk Warlord

NPC: Ruschk Camp Leader - 83.2N, 54.6W

Ruschk Camp Leader tells you, "As you look into the Leader's eyes you see an image in you mind. A small, funny looking Ruschk sneaks into the tent and takes a totem. Then the Ruschk attack the stranger, but it manages to summon a portal and escape."
You give Ruschk Camp Leader Ruschk Totem.
Ruschk Camp Leader tells you, "You look into the Ruschk Leader's eyes and he nods and gives you a piece of paper. You suddenly know that the thief dropped this when escaping the Ruschk Camp."
Ruschk Camp Leader gives you Thief's List.

Rumor: Thief's List

The items on the list comprise the elements of a Ruschk guise. Head to Mask Maker Janda Sulifiya in Al-Jalima for more information.

NPC: Janda Sulifiya - 7.4N, 4.6E

You give Janda Sulifiya Thief's List.
Janda Sulifiya tells you, "Hmmm, yes, I remember a Viamontian came into town looking for me to make a full Ruschk Guise for him. I've never made a full guise of a Ruschk and I must say it didn't turn out so well, but the man seemed pleased. He didn't say much, but I remember his face. Here, I'll make a sketch of him for you."
Janda Sulifiya gives you Sketch of a Viamontian.
Janda Sulifiya tells you, "I don't know if it's important, but I just remembered that he summoned a portal to Sanamar after he bought his guise. He made a big show of it too. Haha, As if I've never seen a portal summoning!"

Show the sketch around to see if you can get more clues to the thief's whereabouts.

You allow Fiun Gaya to examine your Sketch of a Viamontian.
Fiun Gaya tells you, "This is the thief? This would be the desecrator?"
Fiun Gaya tells you, "He must pay! He must give us back the Light of the Fiun! He must die! You must take Lunnum back from this foul snatcher of hopes."

NPC: Grand Mother - 87.4N, 70.5W

You allow Grand Mother to examine your Sketch of a Viamontian.
Grand Mother tells you, "Why would you show me this man's sketch?"
Grand Mother tells you, "No matter, I've never seen this shabby man before."

NPC: Grand Knight - 70.0N, 62.5W

You allow Grand Knight to examine your Sketch of a Viamontian.
Grand Knight tells you, "Yes, I have had dealings with this man. You say he stole a corpse?"
Grand Knight tells you, "Well, what people do in their spare time is none of my concern."

NPC: Fabian Strinjelli - 72.0N, 61.1W

You allow Fabian Strinjelli to examine your Sketch of a Viamontian.
Fabian Strinjelli tells you, "Yes, I have seen this man. He shops in town quite often. I believe his name is Andrilos. He lives somewhere in the Northern woods of Lotila Island."

Andrilos can be found at the bottom of the Thrungal Den, a dungeon SW of Silyun on Lotila Isle. (A tie to the Remote Empyrean Vault puts you very close by.)

Dungeon: Thrungal Den - 79.4N 71.6W
--Levels: 100+
--Map:
--Creatures: Enoki Thrungus, False Morel Thrungus, Andrilos

The dungeon is very easy to navigate. Simply keep right (of left) the entire way down. When you show Andrilos the sketch, he denies being the thief. You may even begin to suspect that he's been framed.

Andrilos tells you, "These fungal growths are facinating. Don't you agree? Collecting and categorizing the fungus in these caves is my hobby. It's important to have a hobby."
Andrilos tells you, "Did you want to ask me something?"
You allow Andrilos to examine your Sketch of a Viamontian.
Andrilos tells you, "Hmmm, I have to admit that this does look like me, but I was nowhere near Lunumm's Pyre when you say the theft took place. I... I was engaged in a private matter. I can't disclose all of the details or I might endanger other peoples lives, but I assure you that I am not your thief."

Even if you try and beat the truth out of him (use bludgeon), he'll proclaim his innocence!

Andrilos tells you, "I'm telling you, you have the wrong man. Why do you insist on attacking me."
Your final blow silences Andrilos and he slumps limply to the cave floor.

On his body you'll find a worn token inscribed "Hero of S..."

If you try and pick up a 2nd token you'll again get the following message:
You have solved this quest too many times!

Because of the faded inscription, it's not possible to tell if the token is for Sanamar of Silyun. Showing it to Grand Mother and Grand Knight doesn't help clear up the mystery of whether Andrilos was framed, nor is it known whether killing Andrilos will have any affect on future quests.

You allow Grand Mother to examine your Worn Token.
Grand Mother tells you, "No, I cannot say I know what this token means. I would think a hero wouldn't give an important token like this away..."
Grand Mother tells you, "Where did you find this?"
Grand Mother tells you, "If you are responsible for taking this token from a hero I wouldn't dare show it to anyone else. There might be... repercussions."

You allow Grand Knight to examine your Worn Token.
Grand Knight tells you, "A hero? A hero wouldn't let a symbol of honor become so damaged. It almost looks like it was worn down on purpose... Who did you say you took this from?"
Grand Knight tells you, "Hmmm, this bears further investigation. Thank you."

If you hand the token to Fiun Gaya, he'll reward you for killing the thief while expressing dismay that Lunnum is still missing.

You give Fiun Gaya Worn Token.
Fiun Gaya tells you, "The thief has been slain, but her light has not been found. I will die without her. You have done well in finding the snatcher of hope and you deserve what rewards I can give you."
You've earned 18,730,000 experience.
Fiun Gaya tells you, "Please, leave me now. I must find a way to find her."

Fiun Gaya tells you, "You have aided me in finding the thief. Please, do not return until I have need of you."

Shen Mara of LC sends these final thoughts:

P.S.: We really think that Andrilos was framed, the more we read over the quest notes. But we have no idea who framed him or where he was when it happened. This might be the first quest you can actually do wrong and fail....
