From The Calm event.

Town Crier tells you, "I heard that portals to Power Forges have been discovered on the shores of the Inner Sea. What mysteries like within their depths? Only time will tell."

During the Calm event, three Lugian Emissaries appeared in Yaraq, Rithwic, and Shoushi. They offered to build forges in town to enhance players' crafting skills if someone was brave enough to retrieve a Forge Essence from deep within the Power Forges on the shores of the Inner Sea. When the Essence was returned to the Emissary, 5 forges appeared. The player received a global for their efforts and an experience reward!

The Lugians, however, were only here for a short time. After their departure, the xp reward could no longer be obtained. And what of the essences? They can now be turned into an Agent of the Arcanum in exchange for Writs of Refuge or an experience reward!

The layout of the three power forges is identical. You'll fight through various creatures until you reach the Overseer. When killed, the Overseer drops a Forge Essence, a Ring, and a note.

Rumor: Inner Sea Directive

The route to each Overseer is the same in all three Power Forges. From the drop take a right, then go through the west door. Head up the ramp and then take another ramp to the south. Make a right (heading east) into a room with glowing blue crystals, then take the NE passage to exit the room. You'll reach a section where the floor is covered with portals. Walk over the portals and exit left (north), then take the ramp up to the east.

You'll reach another room with glowing blue stone. From there, exit to the north. You'll reach another ramp to the north, this time heading down. Follow the hallway to the next blue crystal room and take a right into the hall heading east.

Once through the hall you'll reach the Tusker room. Take a left heading north. Make a right at the immediate split keeping due north. Take the ramp up to the east, then keep to the right until you reach another ramp up to the north. One at the top of the ramp, keep to the left until you are facing a small alcove to the SW. The Overseer is found there, guarded by a fast spawn of dungeon creatures.

The Overseer has strong magic and elemental resistance. Several players have reported fire working well on the Overseer and others have suggested that mages use a Singularity Wand.

Each Overseer drops 3 Forge Essences (timer 30 days), the Inner Sea Directive and a Ring. Return to town and hand an Agent of the Arcanum the essence in exchange for a Writ of Refuge!

Note: See this post by meanbeard for important information about the forge XP reward.

"The Lugians are not giving level 126ers the appropriate XP reward because of a bug introduced during the final days of implementation. I would like to apologize to you for that bug.

I still want to give level 126ers the amount of XP you should have received for this quest. So, next month, take your Western Forge Essence to an Agent of the Arcanum. They will give you the appropriate XP reward. This reward will only be given once and will no longer be available once we publish the February event. If you have already given your Western Forge Essence to a Lugian, you will need to do finish this quest again. Of course, keep in mind that if you have already picked up a Western Forge Essence, you have to wait 30 days before you can pick up another one.

To be clear, the Agents will only reward XP for the Western Forge Essence, and they will only reward that XP to level 126ers."
