 From the Persuasion event.

Town Crier tells you, "Nuhmudira's mansion borders the swamp and desert. Be careful--my cousin warns that her Zharalim are guarding that place with their lives. You may be able to approach it from the front if you are very careful."

The discovery of Nuhmudira's Journal confirmed the rumors that had long been circulating: she had, indeed, been sacrificing Isparian lives in her mansion dungeon. An unwitting pawn or the Monster of the Labyrinth? This was the question we were left to face.

For more on Nuhmudira's background, read Brink of the Abyss, Homestead, and the teasers from The First Strike, Flesh and Blood, Betrayal and Persuasion.

Nuhmudira's Mansion is located outside Lytelthorpe at 4.1S, 36.0E.

To reach it, you can take the Asbel Domain settlement portal from Lytelthorpe at 2.2N, 50.4E. You'll exit at 0.9S, 39.9E, a short run from the mansion. Be cautious on approach; Zharalim roam the area.

Map: Nuhmudira's Dungeon

Nuhmudira's Journal is located deep within her dungeon. When you first go through the dungeon portal, you might not see anything amiss. Like many other residents of Dereth, Nuhmudira displays rare and valuable quest trophies on the walls.

The dungeon, however, contains secrets deep within. Head to the southwest room on the lower level. You'll see a few tomes displayed on lecterns and a set of bookcases against the wall. When you use (double-click) one of the tomes on the west wall, the bookcases move to reveal a hidden passageway!!

When you head down the ramp, you'll be attacked by Sclavus (Aste, Esse and Se). To find Nuhmudira's chambers, go left (east) at the 4-way intersection. The Journal spawns on the floor near her bed.

Text: Nuhmudira's Journal

If you think you can stomach it, take the south passage from the 4-way intersection and you'll find a bloody scene awaiting you: the prostrate body of a Sacrificed Zharalim.

To exit the dungeon, you can re-open the bookcases from the inside by pulling the candle on the wall.

See the Ancient Olthoi Queen quest for additional secrets within Nuhmudira's Dungeon!
