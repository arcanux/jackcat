 Got a little extra spare time on your hands? Looking for something to do on a rainy day? Why not pick up a hobby? Learn the secrets of alchemy to make special oils to treat foods and arrowheads. Take up fletching to craft neato arrows and quarrels. Pick up a new school of magic, or research new spells. Go on a quest. Play some games.How about a little arts and crafts. And of course you can always learn to cook. There's lots to do in Dereth besides chopping monsters in two!
