Tumedion Fortress (55.9S, 60.7W)
Recommended Levels: 35-45 (Restricted to levels 30+)

Creatures: Drudge Stalkers, Drudge Raveners, Virindi Servants, Tusker Guards, nd the mage's worst nightmare: the Tumideon Hollow Minions.

This is a great place to hunt. Just make sure you bring a fire weapon/arrows! On the first level there are Drudge Raveners/Stalkers in each of the 3 rooms down the hallway. If you attack one, they all come at you. The lower level contains Virindi Servants/Masters, Tusker Guards, and more Drudges. Make sure you don't rush into a room without inspecting it first because it is easy to be overcome by a lot of monsters. I wish you luck!

Brent Ua of MT
Level 44 / Unarmed Combat

Ok the dungeon is small and offers good loot averaging 2K. The Raveners drop SIK's and Guts now and again. Standing rules in the dungeon are these: 1) Keep the critters out of the entrance/buff room. 2) If you can't kill the Minions, leave them alone please. Tumideon Minions are the meanest of theire kind and inflict lots of damage. Regulars in the dungeon such as myself (been there since level 30) really find it difficult to play the dungeon when these Minions are loose and roaming the place. This is also a good place to Tie to if you're level 50+ and like to hunt the Obsidian Plains as it rests in the heart of this ever increasing place of danger. I suggest that your toon be able to cast level 6+ protection and cast level 5 Vulns and War spells as you will come up against as many as Five to eight Drudge Raveners and Stalkers at one time. Tuskers are maxed to 2 at one time, but you may find your self getting pummeled by three at a time on rare occasions.

Halls of Metos (4.4S, 0.6W / 56.0S, 89.2W / 61.4S, 46.6W))
Recommended Levels: 20-40 
Addtional recommendations: 20-40 for Archer / 30-45 for Mage / 40-45 for Melee

Creatures: Liches, Lich Lords, Revenants, Obsidian Golems, Iron Golems, Granite Golems.

Hunt the Liches (use Fire) until about 30 or so.  Then jump across the Acid Pit for some Irons (use Acid). When your attack skill (UA, sword, etc.) is buffable to 250 (level 32 in my case), go get the Revenants. They are 10K a pop but all life protects are needed.  Archers won't have as much of a problem killing the Revs as melees, so they may be able to take a Rev a little earlier.  If you're not sure if you can take a Rev and you don't want to risk dying, try taking the one in the subway. Obsidians can be VERY dangerous.  About a week ago I got hit for 140+ on a single melee attack with level 4 protects and 180+ armor, so be careful.

Leman Russ of FF
Level 27 UA/Item

At level 33, I found that killing level 90 Revenants gives me 10,000 xp each.  And the loot is awesome!!  Bring your fire weapons, as undead are weak against fire (and bludgeon).  Mages can drain here, but it's slow and hard to cast drains on a  level 90 magic user. Buff against everything, as Revenants cast all types of magic against you.  I currently kill one at a time, heal, then lure another one, kill, heal...repeat. This is with ALL level 4 life protects (acid, bludgeon, blade, fire, etc), and a Fire sword that does 15-30 dmg buffed, and a buffed sword skill of about 265(?). If you have Item magic, you can load up on goods (hauberks, peas, magic armor/weapons, the works) and just recall to a town and sell. Revenants also drop  SIKs, though I've yet to get one from them.  They also drop Large Mnemosynes, worth 15k in xp, or 45k in xp if you use an Obsidian Key on them. It's EASY to make 40k in about 20 minutes if you kill 4 or 5 Revenants here. A normal spawn produces 5 or 6 Revenants, but you don't have to face more than 2 at a time, tops... if you lure them.

Black Hills (62.2S, 50.5W)
Recommended Levels: 30+

Creatures: Drudge Raveners, Stalkers, etc.; Skeleton Bone Knight, Lord, Great Skeleton; Mosswart Mire Witch; Obsidian, Diamond, Iron Golems; various Lugians; Tusker Guards and Slaves, all kinds of Shadows.

Lotta high drudges. Vuln them, then fire away. Either that or drain. There's a house nearby that is 3 floors, has a healer, provisions, and mage shop.

Sometimes there are Lugians in the area. There are also high level skeletons and Mosswart Mire Witches. Nearby there are also various golems, including a Diamond once every so often.

Northern Quiddity Dungeon / Southern Quiddity Dungeon (Restricted to Levels 20-35 - opened by killing Black Claw Leaders)
Recommended Levels: 29-34

Creatures: Drudges: Lurker, Stalker, and Revener

Fire Weapons a must. Level IV protects required.

Recommended approach: fight from the starting portal to the Claw leader, then portal back to the start. Can typically be done in two buff cycles (using IVs).

These hunting grounds have the advantage of letting you stock up on Quiddity Orbs. You might not be able to use them now, but when you can activate them, it's sure nice to have one.

Gredaline Consulate ( 61.6S, 29.3E - Restricted to Levels 25+)
Recommended Levels: 25-40

Creatures: Lurkers, Stalkers, Raveners, Tusker Guards, Hollow Minions, Virindi of
many types.

I brought my LC mage here at level 4 with the trick of tying to a portal that surfaces you to an earlier part of the dungeon, but without the 25+ level restriction. The Lurkers and Stalkers in the front are great xp for her! Most people don't know about the second part, the envoy. It contains about 50 Raveners! This you can't enter until level 25. If you explore the envoy, you will find rooms with Hollows, Tuskers, and Virindi. More drudges too. If you keep to the right of the envoy upon entering, you will find yourself face to face with a character Virindi! He doesn't drop a spell; he drops a unique obsidian shard. Really worth a visit if you are a lore freak. If you are a more observant follower of directions, you may find yourself taking a right up a ramp, and onto a ledge. Two tuskers spawn below, as well as many Drudges and a Virindi Master. Great if you need a mask or guts.

Lugian Excavations (77.0S, 64.4E)
Recommended Levels: 35+

Creatures: Gigas and Extas Lugians - ALL USING HOLLOW WEAPONS

HIGH ORE Recommended level with group 35 + || Solo 40+. There are some traps on the way down to the actual mine. To avoid them stay on a wall when crossing into another room.

Once you get into the mine there are (with few exceptions) only Extas Lugians. They will either throw rocks (Bludg), go straight HtH (Bludg) or use axes (Slash).

Just follow corridors until you reach an ore deposit. Use it as cover when the Lug's throw and then take them in HtH. There is a reasonable spawn rate. I averaged 14 per 15 min buff and they are worth about 9K XP each.

Loot is weak but decent SIK drop rate on the Extas. Remember that you will only need Blade (Slash) and Bludgeoning protections. Hollow will bypass banes.
