 		 An archer without his bow is... a mage! But an archer with his bow is deadly! If you're Ned Cleversun, that is. With a trusty Yumi or a Composite bow, Ned Cleversun strikes fear in the hearts of Tuskers everywhere. His secret is Deadly Arrows (see Ned Cleversun's Guide to Fletching).

But Archery isn't the only activity Ned partakes in. He also enjoys making snow angels during the Derethian winter, as well as dragging hordes of tuskers up to the entry portal in BSD.
