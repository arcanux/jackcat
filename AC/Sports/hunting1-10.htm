Just West of Hebian-To
(near Hebian-To lifestone and Cragstone portal) 
Recommended Levels: 1-5

Creatures: low level Drudges, Shreths, Wasps, Mosswarts, Reedsharks.  If you venture a little further, you can find higher level varieties of each creature, as
well as Dolls, Banderlings, etc.

This is a great area for low-level hunting.  You can often find groups of low-level players to hunt with and help with corpse recovery.  Often mid-level characters are willing to help with corpse recovery if needed at this location.

Lytelthorpe Open Space Temple 2.8N 46.5E
Recommended Levels: 3 - 8

Creatures: Banderlings, Reedsharks, Drudges, Shreths

Run North from the West Lytlethorpe Outpost (1.0N, 48.4E) to the Ruined Cave (2.3N, 48.3E). Then turn left (West) to the temple. Once you run up the steps, you can attack the creatures with impunity. As long as you stay away from the edge, only missile or magic can hit you. There is even a pillar to dodge behind to avoid these attacks!

Ironbar of Frostfell
Axe/Bow

Near Bowyer, Grocer, and Lifestone at 3.9N, 70.5E
Recommended Levels: From 1 (if you're careful) to 12

Creatures: Plenty of sloooowww Aurochs, and fast-fast-fast Reedsharks, Hunter Shreths and 'dillos. Plus, the beach is about 3 clicks away to the east. The general
population there being Azure Gromnies, Mud and Water Golems, with an occasional Broken Fragment and Shadow Child.

It's a terrific leveling spot, particularly for archers (or at least folks with Bow trained). Quick spawn times, and flat terrain with decent escape routes for shoot-and-scoot.

Be careful, though. It's a treacherous run for characters below level 5, but doable from, say, Lytelthorpe (I was able to get a level 1 UA/Archer there with 5 Health...on the THIRD TRY!)

Also, the bowyer (who buys weapons only) sells Armor Piercing, Frog Crotch, and Blunt Arrows!

Yaraq Auroch Cow Spawns
Recommended for Levels 1-10

Creatures: Auroch Cows -- Level 10, very slow with 80 health.

Cows are very slow, so they are perfect for archers and mages.  Because
they hit hard, warriors will want at least 25 health and some decent armor
before trying these.

Jerem of  Solclaim
Crossbow - Level 19

South beach in Yaraq from 25S, 4.5W to about 26S, 0.0
Recommended Levels: 4-11 (Ranged attack levelsl 4-9, melee 7-11)

Creatures: Water Golems, Mud Golems, Shallows Lurkers, Azure Gromnies, Spiny Dillos and the occasional Shadow Child

Mossie Fort  (34.0S, 80.7E) 
(Called Fort Aimaru when looked up in AC Explorer)
Recommended Levels: 3 - 10

Creatures: Mosswarts, Drudges, couple of Brown Armoredillos, Azure Gronmies, Outcast Monouga, Banderlings, and Wasps spawn close outside the walls.

This is a great place to hunt in early levels. I normally come here at level 1-3 and stay here till level 10 or so. There is a LS next to a tower (viewable from the fort) NW of the fort, along with an Archmage inside.

Archers and war mages can get great XP from level 1 by perching on either the tower by the LS, or in the fort. When the critters in the fort do not offer enough XP, or it has a full spawn (hard to handle solo till 20's) the beach to the east is covered in Golems, Sharks, and the other normal beach creatures.

Level 36 Mage

Black Mire Swamps South of Yanshi
Recommended Levels: 1-20 Archer

Creatures: Undead, Zombies, Swamp Gromnies, Mud Golems, Water Golems, Mire Wasps, Mosswarts, Limestone Golems, Sandstone Golems and Sclavus up to level 40.

Undead and Zombies for the first ten levels. There are many around and they are easy to kill for an archer. At levels 7 or higher the Swamp Gromnies are good targets too. A sprinkle of Mud and Water Golems and Wasps makes life interesting. Stay clear of Mosswarts. Later, at level 13-15, the Swamp Temple has lots of Mosswarts, and further down Limestone Golems and Sandstone Golems, followed by Sclavi. Swamp Temple even has a lifestone for those with lock picking.

Tank Archer level 12

Fort Witshire (25.0N, 37.8E)
Recommended Levels: 1-10 (Archers and Mages)

Creatures: Armoredillos, Rats, Drudges, Shreth, Auroch (up to Bulls)

Comments:

This is a great spot for young archers and mages. There is a lifestone right inside the fort. You can stand on the walls and shoot at the monsters both inside and outside of the fort. It is a short run from Cragstone where you can get supplies.

Meki II of Frostfell
Level 56 Archer

Holtburg Dungeon (43.5 N, 33.0E)
Recommended Levels: 1-10

Creatures: Undead

Undead are level 15.... but they are great experience. I have had level 1 characters with 5 health and no super good stuff, just society weapons and some armor, who can solo this place from level one, if they are careful. If you have more health it is easier, but you can take it with 5 health if you are smart and cautious. They give great experience. If you successfully kill one with 5 health, then you get enough experience to raise your health to 8-10 or so, then kill another, etc.... I level here from 1 to 10 with ANY character I make. However, you need to get a lockpick mule or have your main to open a locked door, since that is the only place with undead.
