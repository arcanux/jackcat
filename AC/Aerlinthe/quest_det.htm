Discovering The Island

Ok, starting from the very beginning. Two Emissaries from Aerlinthe appear in Dereth. One in Chalicmere Fortress (17.7s 57.4w), and one at a group of temples in South Dires. (87.6s 65.6w).

First Steps

  1. Obtain a "Sluice Gate Bar" from Tenkarrdun Foundry dungeon.
  2. Deliver the "Sluice Gate Bar" to the Repair Golem in the second section of the Aerlinthe Reservoir dungeon.
  3. Obtain a "Pyreal Bellows" from Crater Caves dungeon at Mt. Esper.

Behemoth of Tenkarrdun

Locate and defeat Behemoth of Tenkarrdun at the center of the caldera and retrieve the black boulder as part of the quest sequence.
