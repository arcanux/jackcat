Aerlinthe — Directions

You can only gain access to Aerlinthe island via the two Emissaries that have been dispatched to Dereth.

Faladha the Emissary

Location: Chalicmere Fortress — 17.7s 57.4w (requires a C note to summon portal)

Faladha the Emissary (image)

Garaena the Emissary

Location: Group of temples in South Dires — 87.6s 65.6w (requires a D note to summon portal)

Garaena the Emissary (image)

Getting to Chalicmere Fortress

Best route: Go to Baishi. Buy a 'sake' from the food vendor there. Run south to the Drunken Madman's Tower (53.0s 61.0e). Hand the Drunken Madman the 'sake' and he will summon a portal. Take the portal and you will be at the 'walled portals' fort in the dires (21.5s 74.5w). Take the portal labeled "Accursed Halls" and exit the walls; run north to 17.7s 57.4w to reach Chalicmere Fortress.
