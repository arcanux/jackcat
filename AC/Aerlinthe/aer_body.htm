Quest item summary:


Reward for defeating Behemoth of Tenkarrdun - Level 579 Magma Golem in the caldera at Tenkkardun. When embedded into an Atlan weapon it casts Weapon Mastery 4,  Blood Drinker 6, Swift Killer 4, Cold Pro 4, and Curse Of Black Fire (-50 to Magic D). Diff 180. Weapon 250+. Stone is no-drop, no-give. Does not drop on death.

Reward for defeating Lady Aerfalle - Level 117 Dark Magus in her Keep. Casts Acid Streak 5, Leadership Mastery 5, Life Mastery 5, War Mastery 5. Diff 120. War Magic 230+. No-give. Drops on death.

Reward for defeating the Relic Bone known as "The Watchman" in the Abandoned Town. Casts Person Attunement 4 and Deception Mastery 4. Diff 150. Creature Enchantment 170+. Ring is giveable.
One of three infusions obtained by defeating the 'boss' Hellfires/Firestorms that invaded Tenkkardun, Espar and Lethe volcanos. Infusions used to upgrade the SoLL.Infusions are no-drop. No-give. Don't drop on death.

Reward found in the Lady Of Aerlinthe Chest which you open with the key found on Lady Aerfalle's corpse. AL 0. Casts Focus 5, Willpower 5, Arcane 6, Aerfalle's Ward (improves AL by 220 points). Diff 275. No-drop. No-give. Drops on death.
Another reward found in the Lady Of Aerlinthe Chest, inside Aerfalles Keep. Dmg: 15-30, Fire. Bns to Melee -4%. Bns to Attack +8%. Speed (30)
