Quest item summary

Reward for defeating Behemoth of Tenkarrdun — Level 579 Magma Golem in the caldera at Tenkkardun. When embedded into an Atlan weapon it casts Weapon Mastery 4, Blood Drinker 6, Swift Killer 4, Cold Pro 4, and Curse Of Black Fire (-50 to Magic D). Diff 180. Weapon 250+. Stone is no-drop.

Reward for defeating Lady Aerfalle — Level 117 Dark Magus in her Keep. Casts Acid Streak 5, Leadership Mastery 5, Life Mastery 5, War Mastery 5. Diff 120. War Magic 230+. No-give. Drops on death.
