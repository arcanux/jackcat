Aerlinthe — Quest Steps

Quest walkthrough (step‑by‑step)

  1. Obtain a "Sluice Gate Bar" from Tenkarrdun Foundry dungeon.
  2. Deliver the "Sluice Gate Bar" to the Repair Golem in the second section of the Aerlinthe Reservoir dungeon.
  3. Obtain a "Pyreal Bellows" from Crater Caves dungeon at Mt. Esper.
  4. Deliver the "Pyreal Bellows" to the Smith Golem in the forge at the bottom of the Tenkarrdun Foundry dungeon.
  5. Locate and defeat Behemoth of Tenkarrdun at the center of the Tenkarrdun caldera and retrieve the black boulder.
  6. Locate and defeat the boss Hellfire at the center of the Tenkarrdun caldera.
  7. Locate and defeat "The Watchman" Relic Bone in the Undead Town.
  8. Defeat Lady Aerfalle in the last room of Aerfalle Keep, and retrieve her key and staff.
