• Step 1 - Obtain a "Sluice Gate Bar" from Tenkarrdun Foundry dungeon.
  •  
  • Step 2 - Deliver the "Sluice Gate Bar" to the Repair Golem in the second section of the Aerlinthe Reservoir dungeon.
  •  
  • Step 3 - Obtain a "Pyreal Bellows" from Crater Caves dungeon at Mt. Esper.
  •  
  • Step 4 - Deliver the "Pyreal Bellows" to the Smith Golem in the forge at the bottem of the Tenkarrdun Foundry dungeon.
  •  
  • Step 5 - Locate and defeat Behemoth of Tenkarrdun at the center of the Tenkarrdun caldera and retrieve the black boulder.
  •  
  • Step 6 - Locate and defeat the boss Hellfire at the center of the Tenkarrdun caldera.
  •  
  • Step 7 - Locate and defeat "The Watchman" Relic Bone in the Undead Town.
  •  
  • Step 8 - Defeat Lady Aerfalle in the last room of Aerfalle Keep, and retrieve her key and staff.
  •  
  • Step 9 - Locate the "Lady Of Aerlinthe Chest" in Aerfalle Keep and use the key you retrieved from Lady Aerfalle to open it.

 

  • Addendums:
  • - When you deliver the Sluice Gate Bar to the Repair Golem, that will trigger the Smith Golem to spawn.
  • - When you deliver the Pyreal Bellows to the Repair Golem, a server-wide message will be broadcast and it will trigger the Behemoth of Tenkarrdun & the Tenkarrdun boss Hellfire to spawn.
  • - When you defeat the Tenkarrdun boss Hellfire, it will trigger The Watchman to spawn in the undead town. (It will also trigger the side-quest invasions of Mt Espar and Mt Lethe)
  • - When you defeat The Watchman it will trigger Lady Aerfalle to spawn and will also trigger the Repair Golem to respawn, thus resetting the quest.
