Cows seem to have a special place in Turbine's hearts. Even the most non-observant player is by now probably aware of the maps appearing on the sides of Dereth cows. You'll see Dereth on one, and the island of Marae Lassel on the other!

Cows may also be tipped! Push one (double-click) and it will fall over on its side, giving a startled moo!
