 Note: In February 2001 the Advocate & Sentinel programs were disbanded and replaced by paid employees. Special thanks to all the volunteers who kept the peace in Dereth for so long. Many of them are still continuing their good works on a less formal basis as Asheron's Helpers. You can find more information here!

Advocates are in-game volunteers, usually stationed at Starter Outposts or towns, who give general game information to newcomers. Advocates also carry the coolest looking shields in the game! To find out more, visit the Zone's Advocate page.

The turquoise shields are carried by fully trained Advocates. The orange shields are for Advocates in Training.

The Advocate Dungeon is located at 28.7S x 78.1E. Who know what secret parties and gatherings they have there? Thanks to Gheres Reficul for the screenshots!

Sentinels are another class of in-game helpers. They can be intervene in matters of abuse and can also relocate you if you happen to get stuck in a bugged area. Sentinels wear very tall hats.

Sentinels and Advocates will not help you recover your corpse. They will not spill secret game information. They cannot create bags of Diamond Scarabs or other rare and precious commodoties. Did I mention those cool shields?

  

Use the command @list [town name] to find out which Advocates are online nearby.
