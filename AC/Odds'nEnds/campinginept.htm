Easter Eggs

 

Hi Maggie -

This is Std I from Darktide. I've been a long time fan of your site and what you're doing, but anyway. I was doing the elemental weeping quest and I exited the dungeon and came across a tower. This tower had about 6 liches in it and at the top was a chest. I opened the chest and a dozen faces of this guy popped out and I was debuffed with Camping Ineptitude! I am sending some pics. I thought it was a pretty cool Easter Egg. Thanks maggie!

Many of you know the face in the Camping Mastery spell, but do you know who the visage of the Ineptitude spell is? It's Allen Grey, the former site manager of the old Crossroads of Dereth fan site. The picture was taken by Lum the Mad at E3 in 2000!

The chest can be found at the top of Lich Tower located at 82.7S, 48.8W. To reach the tower, take South Direlands portal from Mayoi at 62.2S, 77.1E and then make the run to the tower.

For the best effect, pan your camera out a bit, so you are looking at yourself opening the chest from a slight distance.
 


Chest casts Camping Ineptitude on Std I of DT!
