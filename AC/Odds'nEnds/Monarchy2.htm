If you'd like your fabulous allegiance to be featured in the Monarchies of Dereth, here is the following information you should send to me at jackcat@spacestar.net. Please select one person from your group to be the contact person for me to communicate with.

Information about the Monarch

Monarch's name
Monarch's level
Approximate members

General information about the monarchy

  • What server?

  • Location of Mansion or Villa

  • Web Site Address, if applicable

  • Please give a short description of your monarchy. You may wish to describe the philosophy/structure of the monarchy  

    (suggested questions below - please do not give one word answers)

    --    Do you have membership requirements?
    --Do you allow experience chains?
    --What do you focus on in-game? Quests? Hunting? PK?

Stories & Bios from members

  • You may also wish to include short bios of selected members. Do you have any members with special skills or strengths you'd like to tout, or who are important to your group for some other special reason?

  • Players may wish to describe personal experiences with the monarchy. What do you like about your monarchy? Why is it special to you? Why did you choose it? Is there a specific incident or story you can think of that exemplifies the experience of being in this particular monarchy to you?

Screenshots

  • Do you have a screenshot of your Monarch? Do you have a few good shots of your monarchy in action? On a quest? At an allegiance event? Hanging out being cool? All screenshots should be accompanied by a full description, included the names of the players in the picture.

All responses should include player's in-game name, level, type of player, and Patron's name.

As we all know, the ground-breaking allegiance system in AC is one of its greatest strengths! Show yourselves off!
