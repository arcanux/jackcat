Hey Maggie,

I got bored after hunting Hoaries and since I was in the area, I went over to Banderling Castle (66.3N, 2.2W). After fighting with a hoard of Banderling Guards, Captains, and other various low level bandies, some Tumerocks, and a couple of Skeletons, I came across a chest in the cave portion of the fort. When I opened the chest, it cast camping mastery on me! The spell description says increases the chances of finding phat loot. As the spell was cast it on me, devil mouse's face appeared in a funny way.

Calladyn the Axe

The rumors are all true! Inside a cave within the Banderling Fort lies a chest containing the visage of Turbine Dev Jesse (otherwise known as devil mouse or nei). There's no easy way to get to the Fort. It's an approximate 20 click run from Celdiseth's cottage. Or if you can get to Stonehold, you can take the the Lake Nemuel Settlement Portal at 69.6N, 20.2W which will drop you at 62.8N, 0.1W.

The fort is overrun by low level Banderlings (Raiders, Guards, and a few Captains, plus a Chief). The surrounding area can be more dangerous and you might encounter a few Strikers or Bezerkers. There's a lifestone right by the fort.

For the best effect, pan your camera out a bit, so you are looking at yourself opening the chest from a slight distance.
