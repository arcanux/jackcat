Easter Eggs

 

Most people visit the Drunken Madman at his tower outside of Baishi at 53.0S, 61.0E bearing gifts of sake. When you hand the madman the drink, he opens a portal to the Walled Portals (used as a route to the Accursed Halls for turning PK).

Drunken Madman tells you, "Have you read the note on the pedestal?"
Drunken Madman tells you, "If that is what you desire, then fetch me a good drink of sake and I shall grant you your wish. Otherwise, leave this place. Hee, hee!"
You give Drunken Madman Sake.
Drunken Madman says, "Ah, a drink! Hee, hee!"

What many people don't know, however, is if you hand the Drunken Madman an apple instead, he will disappear momentarily through portal space, then reappear! Plus he curses!! ;-)

You give Drunken Madman Apple.
Drunken Madman says, "Entering damn portal-space!"
