Tip: Overcrowding in towns causes portal storms, where you could be whisked away to a less crowded, and possibly more dangerous area. In earlier days, a bug enabled you to take shelter in a building to avoid being portal stormed out. This has since been fixed. To avoid portal storms currently, move away from crowded areas (and stay out of the hub ;-).
