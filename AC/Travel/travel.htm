 Dereth is HUGE!! But it will become manageable once you become familiar with the portal routes. As you travel, you will begin to recognize a few portal loops, such as the Cragstone, Hebian-to, Zaikhal, Qalaba'r loop--my personal favorite.

On the Portals, Etc. page, you will find Maggie the Jackcat's quick navigation table! You'll find coordinates for each town, as well as the local lifestones. Across the row from each town's listing, the coordinates for the nearby portals are included with the destination town and coordinates. I refer to this table again and again as I play.

The Settlement Portals page lists the housing portals that enable home owners to reach their settlements more easily.

Shortcuts will give you tips on how to get to out-of-the-way locations.


Note: Info is gathered from various resources. I have not personally verified all coordinates.
