 I absolutely love AC maps! Use the links to the left to find lots of useful map links. Sadly, many of my favorite maps, such as AC Heaven's town maps, no longer exist, but some great newer resources have come on the scene.

The Dereth Maps and General Maps page has links to larger overview maps of Dereth, maps for land masses, such as Aerlinthe, the Vesayen Isles, the Singularity Caul, and Marae Lassel. You'll also find portal maps, dungeon maps, some older beta and specialty maps, plus links to other great resources such as AC Spedia and AC Explorer, etc.

The Town Maps page has links to individual town and area maps for the various places in AC.



Tip: Using your Options Tab, you can display your coordinates under your radar instead of using full map view.

Tip: The small white and green dots on the in-game map are Points of Interest.
