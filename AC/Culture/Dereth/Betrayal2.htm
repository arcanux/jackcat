New Formulas for Health Potions!


My alchemist (lvl 21 with alch skill 191.) tried to make healing potions this morning. It seems that you can no longer use healing infusions on victual infusions to make healing potions. You now have to make health draughts by using a healing infusion on a natural balm. To make a healing potion you then have to use a victual infusion on it.

Azaryha

Aldron Do-nar of FF adds that elixirs are made by using another health/mana infusion on the potion.
