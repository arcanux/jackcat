Rumors
From the Turbine Site - January 18, 2004
 
If you haven’t read the press release yet or heard it from your buddies, fantastic news for the Asheron’s Call Throne of Destiny expansion pack:

Our distributor will be Sony Online Entertainment. We’ll be partnering with SOE to get the expansion pack on the retail shelves and to market it.

That’s right; come the spring of this year, the developer and publisher of EverQuest, Star Wars Galaxies and Planetside will be putting the ACTD expansion pack on a retailer’s shelf near you and will be helping us market the game. At first glance, this probably looks like we’ve lost our minds. I can hear the comments now: “Why would you have a competitor distribute the expansion pack? Are you mad? And can I have some of whatever you’re smoking?”

Well, we may be nuts, but there is a method to our madness:

1. SOE has very good reach into the retail world. They can get us good space in thousands of retail shops and that is critical to attracting more players into the game;

2. SOE also has a great marketing arm, one with years of experience at supporting retail launches of expansion packs, with proven results. We get to take advantage of that experience to give Throne of Destiny some extra oomph! in the marketplace.

Those are two nice advantages for a first time publisher to have, the equivalent to being dealt wired aces in Hold ‘Em. We plan to ride those aces for all their worth.

All in all, this is a great deal for the expansion pack. It gives us excellent reach in the retail space, allows us to stretch our marketing budget better while working with a group with proven success. I don’t think we could have done much better on this short of having God personally place the units on the shelf.

I’m sure there will be some speculation about what all this means or if there is some hidden agenda here, so before the rumors start:

Turbine is not being bought by SOE. This is strictly a retail distribution and marketing deal, nothing more;

We’re not moving the games to SOE’s The Station. You’ll still access them through Turbine;

The Illuminati had nothing to do with this deal. Really. No, really. I’m not kidding, man, you don’t want to go there…

To sum up, working with SOE on distribution and marketing is a great deal for all of us and a great opportunity to get Throne of Destiny in front of more eyeballs. We’re pretty excited about it and we hope you are, too.
__________________
Executive Producer
