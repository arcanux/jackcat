The Obsidian Span


The Gauntlet - Shadow Stone
The quest for the Shadow Stone was by invitation only. Candidates were Monarchs only, of around level 50 or so. A Sentinel appeared who challenged the Monarch to run the Gauntlet in under 30 minutes. They were charged with obtaining a Shadow Stone which lay atop a hill far in the distance. If the challenge was accepted, the Monarch was teleported to an un-chartered island in the SW corner of the map. Obstructing the challenger were mobs of Shadows, Shadow Lieutenants, Panumbris and Umbris. A strange creature called the Lag Beast was also reported.

For success, Monarchs needed a high run skill to outrun the Shadows, and a high jump skill (at least 250) in order to reach the stone. Those who were successful were given the title "Dark Master."
