Note: You get all the XP needed in your current level... Not just the amount you need to get to the next level! If you needed 1000 xp to get from level 1 to level 2, but you already had 500/1000, it would still give you 1000xp and you'd be 500 into the next level.

Thanks to Kain Aubec, An'iya and Zerono of HG, Olthoi Egg of VT!

Royal Guard Tabbeth tells you, "Several months ago, a device was discovered that, when attached to a suitable power source, would transport one to the eastern direland swamps. It seems as though the renegade Lugians and Tumeroks have shown increased activity in that area."
Royal Guard Tabbeth tells you, "I can only guess that their presence there has something to do with the alliance they are attempting to forge with the Burun."
Royal Guard Tabbeth tells you, "In addition to this, a group from the Royal Guard has been dispatched to the eastern direlands as well. Unfortunately, we have not received a correspondence from them in well over a fortnight. I am worried for their safety."
Royal Guard Tabbeth tells you, "If you happen across the group or, Pwyll forbid, their remains, please bring me any information they may have gathered."
Royal Guard Tabbeth tells you, "Best of luck to you."

Gorak tells you, "We've been stationed here to maintain the tenuous peace. We're simply here reinforcing these walls and providing a strong show of support for you and your people. But you and I know, we're warriors. If these Tumeroks ever got organized they could tear these walls down and slaughter all these folk before we could defeat them all. I'm hopeful though that our wayward brothers will find their way back to the right path. If they don't and they press these walls, you can be sure that my men and I will spill much blood, ours and theirs, before this town will fall. Of course, I could spill a lot more if I had remembered to pick up my axe before reporting to my post."
