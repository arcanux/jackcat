Following is Chain Lightning's account of the defeat of Black Ferah, originally published on the Triad site:

A Dream Day

OK first of all this is gonna sound like a load of B.S., but before logging on today I took a nap after class and I had a dream about playing the game. In the dream, I killed the shadow captain and experienced my 10 minutes of fame. As you all know tonight I had my dream come true!

Before logging on I went to the Triad message board and saw a post telling of the shadows moving in the obsidian plains. "Wow!" I thought. Could it be? Well I made it post haste to Weijou and sure enough shadows were everywhere but I didnt see Ferah and concentrated on an umbris (being the loot monkey that I am :) and saw the message come up that someone had been killed nearby by Dark Ferah. Whoa! here we go! I looked all around and saw him just as Spellweaver finished him off. Darn. Upon the message telling of them moving to the Linvak mountain range, I pursued my dream further to the mountain town of Kara, again arriving there just as Axen finished Ferah off. Now I was really frustrated. So close both times, yet so far. The third clue of the southernmost spire in a sho town had me thinking of Tou Tou but upon talking to Twostep he reminded me of a wilderness spire around Kara. So here we go again. Upon arriving there, a few people were already at the spire and we sat around discussing possible locations of the portal to get into this thing. Then Lady Luck came over and sat in my lap! Someone had discovered the portal and upon entering the spire either fell off or jumped out in a suicidal attempt to escape, and Ferah followed, almost landing on top of me! Unbuffed and completely surprised I just started draining. I'm not a Hero, although I was doing high 5's with my Dad who all the while was screaming "Go Chain!" (He gets into it as much as I do). I just happend to be extremely lucky, and was very proud that my 2 best buds Fleet and Mikain were there to share it with me. Score so far: Triad 2 Shadows 0. (Editor's note: The Shadows really should have a higher score, after all they have killed all of us a whole lot of times...)

The Nexus

The brave Dereth warriors continued to fight throughout the night, for evil never sleeps. Gathering up the scattered pieces of the Shadow Key from the various spires, they reassembled the key and made their assault on the Nexus. Players of all levels worked together to enter the restricted portals to the spires. Finally, in a mighty confrontation, they shattered the Nexus Crystal.

Reported Gateways: 64.5S, 82.8E; 19.7S, 8.9E
