New Deadly Arrows!
Here are the steps and pics for deadly arrows:

First create greater arrowheads.
Then use alembic on stibnite to create a bloodhunter infusion.
Then use infusion on aqua incanta to create bloodhunter oil.
Then use the oil on any greater arrowheads to make them deady.
Then use the deady arrowheads on shafts.

It appeared to be easier to create deadly arrows than it is to make greaters.

- Incoming of SC

Visit the Alchemy Formulas and Fletching Items and Fletching Steps for more information. Visit the Arrows & Quarrels page for stats.

Agent of the Arcanum tells you, "Hail! The Arcanum has asked us to inform all adventurers of an undertaking that you may perform to obtain Writs of Refuge. However, these places are not without their dangers and should not be ventured into alone.
