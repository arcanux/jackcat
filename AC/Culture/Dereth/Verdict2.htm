Updated Descriptions!
 
Imbuing

Salvaged Black Opal

 - 100 Chips of black opal material salvaged from old items.
Apply this material to a treasure-generated weapon or caster to imbue the target with Critical Strike. Critical Strike increases the chance that the item critically hits its opponent. The increase in chance depends on the attack skill of the wielder.

Salvaged Fire Opal

 - 100 Chips of fire opal material salvaged from old items.
Apply this material to a treasure-generated weapon or caster to imbue the target with Crippling Blow. Crippling Blow increases the amount of damage that the item does when it critically hits its opponent. The amount of extra damage depends on the attack skill of the wielder.

Salvaged Sunstone

 - 100 Chips of sunstone material salvaged from old items.
Apply this material to a treasure-generated weapon or caster to imbue the target with Armor Rending. Armor Rending gives the item the ability to ignore some of its opponent's armor. The amount of armor it ignores depends on the attack skill of the wielder.
