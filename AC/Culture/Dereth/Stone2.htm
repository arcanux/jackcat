New Lugian Trophies & Armor!
Speak to Aurutis at Linvak Tukal to receive the text: Arm, Mind, Heart. 

Aurutis tells you, "The raiders are striking hard now. They are fearful of our efforts to reach out to the Isparians. We have lost three of our most sacred totems due to their depredations. The Cloth of the Arm, the Scepter of the Mind, and the Blade of the Heart must be found to ensure the continued stability of Linvak Tukal."

Aurutis tells you, "Here, you may find this helpful in retrieving our totems. We have reports of a Raider Commander trapped in the Linvak Tukal Entryway. We can only hope that if you find him, you will find the totems. Strength. Focus. Purity." 

Vamp Hunter D poses in Lugian Armor dropped from the Commander at the end of the Linvak Tukal Dungeon at 76.7S 28.0E. Thanks to Rhydel for info and stats. You can also trade the Armor in to Master Ulkis for the Bastion of Tukal (same AL, a little heavier, and with a new nifty crested look). Thanks again to Rhydel for posing, and to Shaarak and Tarnellius of Thistledown for the stats. Killing each Commander triggers each successive spawn. This quest is heavily camped at this time and spawns are on a 1 hour timer (not confirmed).
