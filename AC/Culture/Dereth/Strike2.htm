Build Your Own Snowman!

 Combine a magic iceball (from Jolly Snowman) with a poofy snowball to get a torso. Combine the torso with another poofy snowball to get the body, then combine a strange stick with the body, and a carrot with that to get your complete snowman. (Thanks to Justin E. for recipe and to Sakura Kinomoto of MT for pics and stats!)
