New Island!
archergandolf provided instructions on how to get to the new island:

Head to the Obsidian Plains and find the Singularity Bore 64.1S, 63.8W (Restricted to Levels 50+, may be tied to). You'll find the new Rampager Tuskers & Terebrous Hollow Minions inside. 

Jump into the pit south of entrance. Beware of Lightning Traps. 

Take the west hallway and take continuous rights until you come to a room at a dead end.

You'll find 4 Augmented Drudges, 1 Minion and the Guardian of the Singularity.

Kill the Guardian of the Singularity for the Sickle (below) and to enter the new island.

The Virindi's cloak seems to boil, as if a thousand insects were seeking to escape before it collapses. The dying Virindi shares its final thoughts with you: "Your life is forfeit, human. You simply do not know it yet. In killing me, [player name], you have gained access to your doom."
As the Virindi falls to the ground, there is a bright flash of light!

You'll have 2 options for the Singularity Caul Portals (Restricted to Levels 55+) to get to the new island: 1) inside Bore Dungeon (run north) and 2) near BSD on the Obsidian Plain - 59.4S 57.8W.

New Shreth Poop!


Dev reports the following:

First off, seeds can be found off of any type of Shreth. I recieved one off a Hunter Shreth, and another seed off of a Blood Shreth. They look like green cocoa beans. (I've included screenshots of them in inventory and on the ground.)

Once you've gotten the seeds, you take them to the Swamp Gardens located outside Yanshi at 22.5S, 45.8E. Inside are a bunch of reedsharks ranging from Adults to Elders. At the bottom is an NPC character who you give the seeds to. He will in turn give you a plant back. (Although I've heard that he doesn't ALWAYS give back a plant). The color of the plant given will vary. I received a red plant (the one that's been extinct for a long time), and a blue plant (that just recently went extinct). 

From what I've heard, he only gives those plants that can no longer be found on Dereth, i.e. you can't get your purple plants this way.

I hope this helps.

Dev

Note: It's been confirmed that Terese will also give the new blue and purple plants for the seeds. Sometimes you may not receive any seeds at all, only a rude comment. Sometimes she will try to cast a spell on you. You may also get several seeds at once instead of just one.

Shallow Rift Opens!

A new dungeon has appeared near Rithwic (8.5N, 50.5E). Doogen sends this report & map from the trenches!

"Danier and I explored the dungeon last night -- many, many broken dolls, rifts and twists and turns later, we had navigated this LONG dungeon.  It's hard to get lost in it -- there are really no dead ends, just a few squares with one way in and one way out.  
 
Tonight Dirrich the Warden was spawning in the basement. I waited my turn and burned him!  He rewarded me with some odd gauntlets.... I have this funny feeling I cannot set them down. I also have an obsidian shard I'm gonig to take to have translated ASAP."

Note: the shard can be translated into Dirrich's Journal.
