--Dungeon: Ravaged Cathedral - 86.3S, 79.1W

Zerono, Cross-Slash and Duadhe Mahdi'in of HG report:
"This dungeon had Bandies and at the bottom where tummies just like the other dungeons. All 3 dungeons are identical. Here is a quick walkthrough

From drop there will be 2 mobs (specified in previous emails). You will take the east doorway in the main room you start. Take the north hall from there and follow it around into a big room with a hole in the floor. It has big wooden objects in the room you can't miss it. Fight the few mobs in that room (I suggest fighting from the hall and letting the mobs come to you that way the top mobs aren't on you all at once.) Jump down the pit and take the doorway to the north. Follow it and take either left or right at the split. There is a lever on both sides in the middle of the hall (also the middle door). Pull that lever and go back to the split and take the opposite way you went before. The lever on the right path opens the door on the left and vis versa. The gate door will open go down a ramp and take the doorway there. You will come too another split again, take which ever way they both lead too the same spot. Fight the mobs there. You will be at a weird little split. A ramp going down (north) a door behind you and a door to your left and right. Go down the ramp and pull the lever, it will then open the door to the west. Fight the few tummies and what ever the boss mob is (either a drudge, lugian or a tummie) and it will drop the respective item. Portal recall and run to the next dungeon. 7 day timer on all the quest timers. About a 3-5 min respawn."

"The drudge dungeon you will want fire, acid and slash buffed. Fire on the normal drudges acid on the mystics and slash on the tummies. Use renders. The lugian dungeon you will want slash and lightning buffed. Lightning for lugians and slash for tummies again. Finally for the Bandie dungeon lightning and slash as well. Lightning for bandies and slash for tummies again."
