From the Turbine Site - Nov. 4, 2004

Who are you?

I'm Steven Kaminsky.  Co-Executive Producer of the Throne of Destiny expansion pack with Jessica Mulligan.  I'm also Exec. Director of Production & Operations for Turbine's Santa Monica studio.

What do you actually do?

As you probably know, Producers wear a mess of different hats.  My focus is the scheduling, resourcing and coordination of the dev team effort for AC:TD.

On any given day I could be reviewing art concepts, working through the brain-numbing texture upgrade production process and related tech, turning out notes on our great new cinematic opening or grinding out an excel schedule with all the who what were whens of the multi-layered multi-tasking production process of producing an MMOG.

I'm also the front line go-to person for the dev team for most production issues.

Oh, and I manage the day-to-day operations of the LA studio which could be anything from working through IT issues to staffing new hires to new equipment to QA-testing the espresso machine :D

How did you end up here?

I was hanging from a limb up in a tree... just swinging by my tail when...

Urm, actually I was working in Hollywood producing the post production for a bunch of movies like "Dreamcatcher", "Spartan", "Thirteen Days", "Nurse Betty" and, back in the day, "Mortal Kombat" and "The Mask."   In between, I ran the game dev, engineering, graphics and live teams for Boxerjam.com for a few years.  Just your usual media-monkey jack-of-all-trades, at your service!

Jason Bell, the head of our new studio, plucked me from Hollyweird and pulled me back into the game world to work with Jess and hammer out the studio's launch..

Where did you get your nickname?

The editor on the Eminem movie "8 Mile" gave it to me, since I wasn't a 313 local and issued one at birth like the rest of the krew.
