Dungeon Updates!
--Lost Garden Ruins, 65.9S, 59.3E
Halaliel aka Unicron of Solclaim reports the dungeon has been upgraded to meet the surrounding area of the dungeon. Inside now has Ashen Bones and Cursed bones instead of Skeleton Warriors. The basement of the dungeon now has Pandemonium and Paroxysm Shadows instead of Shadows and Shadow children.

--Dungeon of Tatters, 62.1S, 40.1E
Sledgexbow of LC reports the dungeon now has Shadow Lieutenants, Panumbris Shadows and Mist Golems.

--Enkindled Souls, 49.4S, 26.9E
Tach Eyon of MT reports the dungeon now has Skeleton Wraiths, Lich Lords & a Revenant!

--North Glenden Prison, 31.8N, 25.5E
Flipz of FF reports: North Glenden Prison has been updated.....instead of various low level mites, it's filled with Mite Matrons, and instead of undead and zombies, there are Risen Soldiers now. No level restrictions on the portal.

--Thieves' Den, 17.8N, 65.0E
Lone the Wanderer of TD reports the Thieves' Den outside of Eastham now has Drudge Stalkers and Raveners, Banderling Strikers, Bandits and Rogues, plus Skeleton Bone Knights. Thanks also to Black Falcon of WE!

--Glenden Wood Dungeon, 29.9N, 26.3E
Hogann of TD reports the following: "Messing around patch day, my wife Kamelian and our friend Quaterstaff Pounder discovered that the monsters and the quest in the Glenden Wood Dungeon have been updated. Lots of Drudge Slaves, Banderling Blades, and Banderling Ogres <replacing the Guards>. The armor is still available, but is now colored. The one I got was purple, but we're camping now to see if the color is always purple, or if its random. Stats appear to the the same as before. UPDATE: After 2 spawns, it seems to be always purple."
For more details visit the Platemail Hauberk quest!

--Mount Naipenset, 5.6N, 56.2W
Gracchus of We reports the Caverns have been updated to Gold Golems with a Sandstone Golem spawn. The chests inside have also been upgraded!
