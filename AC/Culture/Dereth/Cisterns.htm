The Fire Cistern Core
On August 24th, a portal to the Fire Cistern Core (restricted to levels 30-60) opened in Stonehold.

Hello,
I came online and heard that a portal was opened in Stonehold so I quickly tried to run there by taking the azaroth settlement portal in qalabar. I died trying to get there thanks to all those annoying high level elementals now out in the wild but I eventually made it :). The inside of the Fire Cistern dungeon (level 30-60 only) had many fire elementals, magma golems and unstable magma golems. After battling my way up to the top I found the Fire Cistern Core.

There were already more than 30 people attacking it and no one had a clue how to destroy it. The lag was insane... I had to turn down graphics settings to be able to move and attack inside so fighting was pretty difficult. After 2 hours or more of battling and trying to find a way to kill it we took a break and sat outside as more and more people came.

Some people were saying that using magic on the core was healing it or that it was causing elementals to spawn...this was not true. The cistern had a huge regeneration rate of 1000hp every 4 seconds. With 1 million hp it would mean we'd have to do more than 200 damage a second to take it down. None of us had an idea of how to deal that much damage or how to get that many people in there to attack it... The high level elementals and unstable magma golems spawn frequently in the room causing even more lag and difficulty.

As we were losing hope and most people were giving up and saying that it was impossible to do and that turbine made it too hard, I decided to take a look at the other server boards at the vault. MT was mentioning that they found a way to kill it. Apparently since the core was so big, if you used a wall spell it would get hit by the entire wall doing an incredible amount of damage. So after organizing the remaining people we headed back inside. We had all the mages line the sides of the room firing shockwave wall spells at the core as the meeles swung at the core with whatever they had. It slowly lost health and after about 45 minutes the core fell and Martine appeared.
