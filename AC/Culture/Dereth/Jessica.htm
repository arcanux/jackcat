From the Turbine Site - Oct. 27, 2004

Who are you?

Jessica Mulligan, the Executive Producer for AC and the Creative Director on the Throne of Destiny Expansion Pack.

What do you actually do?

Depends on who you ask; it is widely rumored that I just pick the right people, put them in the right jobs and then surf pR0n all day while they make magic happen, :D.

Seriously, on a day-to-day basis, I read specs and meet with the leads to go over them, help keep track of the schedules with our production director, Steve Kaminsky (who is also the Executive Director of the studio here in LA), make adjustments where necessary, beg and weep for more people and money when necessary and in general make sure the dev team, the people who actually do the work, have everything they need to get the job done.

How did you end up here?

I had actually retired to the hills of Northwest San Diego to finish out the book I co-authored with Bridgette Patrovsky, Developing Online Games: An Insider's Guide.  One day in January 2003, Jeff Anderson gave me a call and asked if I'd be willing to take a short contract to do some work on AC2.  One thing led to another, they asked me to stay and manage the whole franchise and here I am.

What is your favorite quest in Asheron's Call and why?

Orion's Dungeon Noir.  It is simply one of the most creative pieces ever done in the game and I get a kick out of it.

What is your least favorite monster and why?

Those damn Olthoi.  My gimped archer has a hard time handling them before they aggro on me.  I think I hold the game record for most times died to an Olthoi.

Where did you get your nickname?

My nickname in the industry is Durga, one of the avatars of Kali, a Hindu death goddess.  I haven't used it much here, simply because I felt players should know me by name; it makes it so much easier when constructing voodoo dolls and I'm all about player convenience.
