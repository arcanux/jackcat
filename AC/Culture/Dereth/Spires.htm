Heard Recently in Dereth
The winds momentarily gust and howl, and through the blustering noise strange laughter may be discerned: high-pitched, thin, shrill, child-like, with an edge of cruel and twisted steel. A shadow seems to pass across the face of the sun. A presence has arrived in Dereth but soon the sensation passes. (March 10, 2000)

Tension seems to run like a current through the ground; the air tingles with sudden foreboding. Somewhere, a dark presence has arrived.

Like a silent black liquid, it flows up into Dereth, bringing the smell of decay and death. The impression fades quickly, and all is normal once again. (March 16, 2000)

If not for the slight change in the pressure of the air, the arrival of the third presence might not have been noticed. Yet the world seems just a little darker now. (March 24, 2000)

Menhir Rings
The Abduction Tales make mention of Menhir Rings, large stone circles, such as found on the top of Mt. Alphus, or outside Holtburg. The importance and significance of these rings has yet to be discovered.
