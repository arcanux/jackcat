Fans SECURE THEIR DESTINY TODAY;
Asheron's Call: Throne of Destiny
BONUS-filled Pre-orders NOW AVAILABLE

WESTWOOD, Mass. - April 11, 2005 - Turbine, Inc., a leading publisher of online subscription entertainment, announced today that players can now pre-order a copy of Asheron's Call: Throne of Destiny(tm), the newest expansion pack for the critically acclaimed Asheron's Call® series.  Fans can ensure that they receive the title on the first day it ships and limited edition bonus materials by ordering the game early at participating GameStop (U.S.), Electronics Boutique (U.S. and Canada) locations or by visiting the retailers online.

In addition to reserving a guaranteed copy of the exciting new game, fans who pick up pre-orders will also receive: 

  • An exclusive in-game Augmentation Gem that grants each avatar special abilities and  also effects the exclusive new characters created for the expansion pack
  • A detailed printed map of Dereth, which also features never before seen lands unveiled for the first time in Throne of Destiny
  • A special edition DVD featuring the game's opening cinematic
  • A $50 instant rebate from Alienware(tm)

More information about the limited time offer can be found by visiting http://www.turbine.com/actdpre.

In addition to GameStop and Electronics Boutique, the expansion pack pre-orders will be available at other select retailers up until the game launches.  Asheron's Call: Throne of Destiny, which will be distributed nationwide by Station Publishing, will be available at select major retail outlets across North America on May 11, 2005. 

About Asheron's Call: Throne of Destiny(tm)

Asheron's Call: Throne of Destiny(tm) is the third installment in the 3D massively multiplayer online role-playing game series Asheron's Call, first launched to critical acclaim in November 1999.  In the new expansion pack, players will enjoy a graphics upgrade, a new playable race, the Viamontians, new lands and a challenge to experienced gamers with a new high-level play system that rewards players with skill credits.

About Turbine, Inc.

Turbine, Inc. is a premier producer, publisher and operator of online subscription entertainment and is headquartered in Westwood, Mass.  With studios on two coasts, the Company is the largest privately-held online games company in North America.  Turbine's catalogue includes some of the most famous online entertainment brands, including Asheron's Call®, Dungeons & Dragons® Online and The Lord of the Rings(tm): Middle-Earth(tm) Online.  For more information on Turbine and its services, please visit www.turbine.com.

Heed the Call Early:
TURBINE OFFERS Asheron's Call 2: Legions
LIMITED EDITION PRE-ORDERS

WESTWOOD, Mass. - April 11, 2005 - Turbine, Inc., a leading publisher of online subscription entertainment, announced today that the highly anticipated massively multiplayer online game Asheron's Call 2: Legions(tm) is available to pre-order for a limited time.  Legions is the first expansion pack for the popular Asheron's Call 2: Fallen Kings® and will feature exclusive content, including new playable races, and builds on the exciting storyline of the original title.

Gamers can pick up their copies of the pre-order box at GameStop (U.S.) and Electronics Boutique (U.S. and Canada) store locations or by visiting the retailers online.  Fans who pre-order Asheron's Call 2: Legions early will receive: 

·         Exclusive early access to a bonus playable race - the Drudge!

·         A limited-edition CD-ROM featuring the game soundtrack, screenshots and other exciting art from the title

·         A set of six collectible Tarot-style cards from the world of Dereth

·         A $50 instant rebate from AlienwareTM

More information about the limited time offer can be found by visiting http://www.turbine.com/ac2pre. 

In addition to GameStop and Electronics Boutique, the expansion pack pre-orders will be available at other select retailers up until the game launches.  Asheron's Call 2: Legions, which will be distributed nationwide by Station Publishing, will be available at select major retail outlets across North America on May 4, 2005. 

About Asheron's Call 2: Legions(tm)

Asheron's Call 2: Legions(tm) is the first follow-up to the graphically ground-breaking MMORPG Asheron's Call 2: Fallen Kings®.  Legions will provide players with an ambitious new storyline complete with an extensive array of new content for hours and hours of additional online adventuring.  The expansion provides players with a new playable race, the mystical Empyreans, a massive new continent shrouded in mystery and an innovative new hero system.
