New Quests!
Collect the trophies dropped by the Tumerok Clans and create 6 different types of weapons. Banners are attached to Hafts (2 quailites: regular or reinforced) and topped with either a Spearheads, Symbol or Crest. Your final weapon will be a spear, a wand (magic staff) or a crested weapon. Weapons can be turned into the Recruiters for rewards. The higher level crested weapon can be turned in for an Assault Weapon (see quest details below).

New Quest -  Assault Weapons!
Quest Details: Assault Weapons

I met up with Enclave to get more information on how to turn a Banner into one of the new assault weapons. Here is the report!

In the Direlands between the Lightning Vault & AB Tumerok spawn: Lieutenants, Majors, Controllers, Commanders and Standard Bearers. The Standard Bearers drop Banners.

First he got a Mask Banner off a Standard Bearer.
Then a Perfect Haft off a Major.
Then a Mask Crest off a Tumerok Controller.

Use the Haft on the Banner for a Hafted Banner. 
Use the Crest on the Hafted Banner to get a Spear with 1 damage.

Give the weapon to one of the 3 Recruiters (info listed above) for a Staff Token. Give the token back to the NPC for the Assault Weapon.

Different types of Tumeroks spawn in different areas of Dereth and they drop parts from the 6 different Tumerok Tribes (see location information above).

Shreth = Assault Mace
Reedshark = Assault Axe
Mask = Assault Staff
Gromnie = Assault Spear

The Serpent and the Falcon do not give weapons. If you use a Symbol instead of a crest you will get a wand instead of a weapon, but you cannot then turn it in for a coin to make an assault weapon.

There's a 3 month timer on the tokens. The final weapon may be dropped or given away. Hafted Banners cannot be given.
