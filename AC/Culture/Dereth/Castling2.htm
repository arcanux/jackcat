Thanks to Dagle ibn Dos of FF

You give Bhab, Arcanum Tinkerer Bronze Coil from a Statue.
Bhab, Arcanum Tinkerer tells you, "A coil from one of those Bronze Ben Ten statues! Wait--somehow it doesn't look the same as the ones everyone was bringing me last month! Well, let's give it a try anyway, shall we?"
Chattering excitedly, the tinker upends his worksack and starts rummaging through its contents.
Bhab, Arcanum Tinkerer tells you, "Gears, pliers, metal plates, screws, nuts and bolts, A-ha! Here we go..."
The tinker swiftly snaps the gear into an assemblage of his spare parts and turns to you in surprise.

Queen Elysa Strathelar Appears!
One June 28th, the High Queen summoned her people to the Festival Stone of Thistledown near Cragstone Falls. Asheron and Martine were both present. After an exiting exchange, the Festival Stone lit the sky with brilliant fireworks.

Killer Angel of WE reports:
"Sheesh! Hello Jack Cat! I tell you...When it said High Quen Elsya Strathelar calls her people to the Thistledown Festival Stone...Hundreds of people were streaming through Subway (I was one of them) :-) running towards Cragstone where the festival stone was. When I got there (I was one of the first), there were already lots of people, but considering the amount that soon came afterwards....I was one of the first, so I finally got to see Asheron himself. He was the REALLY tall old looking guy in Empyrean robes and a hat. The robe and crown were white and blue. Elysa wasn't there, but Martine was. He was standing a little aways from Asheron. Asheron was addressing the people when Martine showed up and Asheron told him he was not welcome here. So I went over to Martine to take a screen shot and right when I was going to press the button, he casts a spell and a HUGE flame ring circles him, killing me and I don't know who else. So I didn't know what happened after that until I got back. When I got back, the whole radar map was COVERED with white. There weren't even dots...it was like all white...LOL.... But Asheron and Martine were already gone. I wasn't able to get screenshots of them, but I do have some screenshots of what the crowd looked like and what happened to my computer. ROLFMAO...haha...Anywayz...

Thank You...
Killer Angel of WE
