AC:TD Overview
What is Throne of Destiny?
What is Asheron's Call: Throne of Destiny?

It is the second expansion pack to the award-winning Massively Multiplayer Role-Playing Game (MMORPG) Asheron's Call.

[ back to top ]
When is the release date?
When will it be released?

We are currently targeting Q1 2005 for release.

[ back to top ]
Do I need to have Asheron's Call already?
Will I need to already own Asheron's Call in order to use Throne of Destiny?

Nope! Throne of Destiny will include both the original Asheron's Call as well as the first expansion pack, Asheron's Call Dark Majesty.

 

[ back to top ]
What will the min specs be?
What will the minimum requirements be to run Throne of Destiny?

We'll let you know once we've pinned down the final requirements. What we can tell you right now is that a 3D video acceleration card will be required.

[ back to top ]
What are the features?
What are some of the features of Throne of Destiny?

Throne of Destiny will include a graphics update, a new playable heritage group called the Viamontians, an increase to the character level cap, new rare items, and new monsters, items, and quests!

[ back to top ]
How do I play?
Is Asheron's Call: Throne of Destiny online-only? Is there a monthly fee?

Throne of Destiny is online-only as it is designed to be experienced by hundreds of players simultaneously. Playing the game does require a monthly subscription fee.

[ back to top ]
What about...
How much will the game cost? How do I get into the beta? Can you tell me more about the new features?

Watch the Throne of Destiny home page for frequent updates!

[ back to top ]
What about updates?
Will there be free content updates?

Throne of Destiny will continue the Asheron's Call tradition of regular content updates.
