Asheron's Call Press Event Invitation, Feb. 17, 2005
   
When?
February 17, 2005 from 6PM - 9PM

Where?
The exotic Shanghai 1930 restaurant and nightclub in San Francisco

What?
An exciting night of previews and live demos for Asheron’s Call®: Throne of Destiny™ and Asheron’s Call® 2: Legions™.

Who?
Meet Turbine executive producers and the development team behind these two great franchises.

Asheron's Call®: Throne of Destiny™
This expansion pack, the third installment of the original Asheron's Call, offers an all new graphics engine, a new playable race, and a new high-level play system aimed at advanced gamers.

 
Asheron's Call® 2: Legions™
The first expansion pack for Asheron's Call 2: Fallen Kings®! The Legions expansion features a new storyline, a new playable race, a new hero system, a massive new continent, over a dozen new creatures and powers along with challenging new quests.
