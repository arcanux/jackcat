From the Turbine Site - Oct. 18, 2004

On this page we'll be giving you examples of the Augmentation Gems that you'll be able to acquire and use in Throne of Destiny. For more information on the Augmentation Gems and how they work, check out the Raising the Level Cap article.

Please note that the information below, particularly the experience cost, may change before ship.

Oct. 18, 2004

Charmed Smith
Cost: 2 billion experience points

No matter how high your Tinkering skills get, your chance of successfully Imbuing an item can never go above 33%... except with this Augmentation! Once you use Charmed Smith, you receive a permanent +5% to your chance of success in an Imbue attempt. This can push your maximum chance of success up to 38%!

 

Enduring Enchantment
Cost: 4 billion experience points

Death has its consequences in Dereth... you gain vitae, you drop items, and the spells that are cast on you go away. Enduring Enchantment can help reduce that sting. Use this Augmentation and spells cast on you will no longer go away when you die!

Note that this effect does not apply to deaths in PK combat. A PK death will still cause you to lose your spells.

 

Might of the Seventh Mule
Cost: 1 billion experience points

Every Isparian on Dereth needs to consider burden when preparing for battle. The only way to carry heavier items is to increase your Strength attribute... or to use this Augmentation! Each use of Might of the Seventh Mule increases the carrying capacity of each point of Strength by 20%.

For example, let's say your character can carry 2000 Burden Units without becoming overburdened. After one use of this Augmentation, he'd be able to carry 2400 BU without becoming burdened!

This Augmentation can be used up to five times. Each time you use it, your pre-Augmentation capacity will be increased by 20%. After five Augmentations, your total burden capacity will have doubled.
