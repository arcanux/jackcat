Homecoming Pennant
This pennant is a gift from the High Queen and Ahseron to those who were present in Dereth during his homecoming. It is emblazoned with Asheron's seal - the sigil of the Realadain family and of the Lyceum at Knorr.
You can use this pennant on roof and yard hooks.

Value

 10,000
Burden 50

Jalina al-Hajj tells you, "The High Queen has commissioned me to create a pennant for all Isparians currently upon Dereth. Asheron himself has provided the device - the heraldry of his family. I am told that this is in honor of our continued bravery and heroics. He and the High Queen have decided that he no longer needs to watch over us, and now Asheron may return to his home."

Known Event Bugs
Posted by Ibn on the Turbine Forums:

  • We are investigating reports of lost items from the dungeon of the mansion at 47.1S, 79.4E on all worlds.
  • The Quintessence Sickle should have a 325 wield req, not a 325 activation req. This should be fixed in the February event.
  • The Sleek Dress may not look correct when dyed. This should be fixed in the February event.
  • If you receive a message over allegiance chat before you are finished logging in, it and further messages may appear in green text. This should be fixed in the February event.
  • Removing characters from house guest and storage lists is case-sensitive. If you are having trouble removing characters from these lists, make sure you are typing in the name exactly as shown. Adding characters to guest and storage lists works normally. We would like to have this fixed for February.
  • The Nefane Listris is dropping it's shell on every kill, this is not by design and should be fixed in February.
  • Dolls found on the Singularity Caul are casting level 1 Life Vulnerabilities. They should be casting higher level spells in February.
