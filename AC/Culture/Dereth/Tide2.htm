Imbuing - Rending

Salvaged Agate

 - 100 Chips of agate material salvaged from old items.
Apply this material to a piece of magical treasure-generated jewelry to imbue the target with Minor Focus. This will also raise the Arcane Lore difficulty and the Spellcraft of the piece by 25.

Salvaged Aquamarine

 - 100 Chips of aquamarine material salvaged from old items.
Apply this material to a treasure-generated weapon or magic-casting implement to imbue the target with Cold Rending. Cold Rending gives the weapon the ability to make its opponent vulnerable to cold attacks. The amount of vulnerability depends on the attack skill of the wielder. This effect does not stack with Cold Vulnerability spells.

A report from Darius Falc of WE:
Asheron's tower can be reached via the portal in the Matron's Hive on south Island. From the Nanto portal drop, run left with the shore on your left and you should find it. The portal to West island is at the bottom of the Matron's Hive, but it isn't for the weak hearted...Olhoi swarm eviscerators, mutilators, guardians, warriors and nobles abound.

When you get to West Island head for the big mountain thing, Asheron's Tower is on the top. This run is *very* scary...imagine your worst nightmare then add Jerry Springer.

When you get to the base of the mountain the only thing you can do is run around it till you find a way up....with half the Olthoi in the world trying to stop you.

There is a plateau half way up where you can rest that is relatively Olthoi free...well compared to everywhere else.

When you get to Asheron's tower you will find a portal to the Inner Sanctum...take it...you'll find Asheron and his Guardians...(Don't forget to take pics...great eye candy)...talk to asheron and he'll tell you that some 800 year old hive matron has returned and give you a book.

The book is 30 pages long so I haven't read it yet...if you want to know what's inside, go talk to Asheron :).

Have fun, and remember...be careful out there :).

DF.
