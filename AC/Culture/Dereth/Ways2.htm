Bunny Master!
The titles above not good enough? You can also attempt to become a Bunny Master! Find Larry the Rabbit Master (2.0N, 41.6E) and give him the Order that spawns in the hut nearby (15 minute spawn). He'll portal you (only you) to a dungeon of fierce pink bunnies! Defeat them for the proud title!! It's also rumored that these killer bunnies drop "eeps" as loot. Marshmallow eeps? ;-)

The full quest write-up can here: Bunny Master Title Quest.

New Outpost!

You can reach Danby's Outpost via a portal from Zaikhal at 16.6N, 1.6W. Inside you'll find a Leather Crafter, a few vendors, and Gunther Danby, a would-be meatball maker! Pack Love! 
Troop posted on the Vault boards discussing some of the reasoning behind the weightless pyreal. Here's an excerpt (sorry, I have misplaced the link):

"It still comes back to fun, with a healthy dash of common sense thrown in. Not being able to use M notes? That's silly. Having to mule or dump all your stuff to buy a 50k + item? That's silly. Was this the most elegant solution? Perhaps not, but we can sit around and wait forever for resources that may never appear, or we can act. Lately, we've been deciding to act. Yes, there are macroing and buy/sell issues to consider, but this merely makes the problem worse, it does not create a whole new problem. And we're working on that problem as well."

There you have it. The weightless pyreal combined with the higher stacking counts have succeeded in giving players what almost everyone in the game was clamoring for: more pack space! Yay!
