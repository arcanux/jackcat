From the Turbine Site - Oct. 20, 2004
 
Who are you?

Bryan. Lead Artist for the AC1 Expansion Pack development team. I lead the way into an abyss of texture recreation, object building and implementation, and artistic conceptualizing and file counting.

What do you actually do?

I create the art. The Designers create content and give it a description, then I interpret that description into the 3D model, textures and animations. The descriptions are also used to help me break down how big the content is and how many textures to use. I also need to decide how many polygons to use to describe an object, given the capabilities and limitations of the game engine. This is all done with the goal of making it pleasing to the eye.

How did you end up here?

I was lucky to have worked with AC2 artist Catfisch at a previous job. He let me know that Turbine was looking for artists for this project, I applied and here I am!

What is your favorite quest in Asheron's Call and why?

I really like anything that has to do with Ulgrim. He amuses me.

What is your least favorite monster and why?

I don't like K'naths. They look like they should jiggle but they don't.

Where did you get your nickname?

My mother, actually. It's short for "sugar pudding."
