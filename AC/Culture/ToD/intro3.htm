+Brute Warrior Defeated! (May 30, 2008)
Hi again Maggie, Airmail from Wintersebb back again with another live event to tell you about. We got a message today in a broadcast saying that the brute warrior was waiting for people in the PKL arena. A few of us rushed there to see what was going on, and there was a level 275 character there named +Brute Warrior, an admin controlled Player Killer Lite. He wore a suit that basically made him take little damage, but he didn't wear a breastplate, pauldrons, bracers or gaunts, so if you hit him in the mid-section, you would do full damage as a melee or missile character. His axe was brutal, does 750-1000 slashing damage, and even though the mods were low, he was hitting me for 40ish full speed, 100ish full power, and even with a +42% wand out, he was still hitting every attack. The battle went on for about 15 minutes altogether and I believe he had 100000 health, an attack from my CB spear was doing 60ish damage if I hit him any place he had armor, and 750ish if I hit the unarmored parts. I was critting him for approx 2750 points on a crit. He liked to jump spin to get away from the melees (and that is actually how I lived the 3 separate times he targeted me as well, I didn't die to him, heh heh). From what I can tell, he was immune (or super high magic D to life magic etc) because no one could land a debuff on him in any of the magic schools, just war damage was hitting him. In the end I got the kill as well as the last hit and he portaled back to the PKL arena and gave me his axe, which has a requirement of 10000 base Axe skill to wield, but it makes a cool trophy anyways. I will send a SS of him and the Axe as well, glad I could be of assistance again for a really fun live event. Keep up the great work on your site. =)
