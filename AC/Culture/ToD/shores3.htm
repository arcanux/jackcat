Asheron's Island - The Report from Leafcull

Hello Maggie,

A global went out earlier today on Leafcull :

The air resounds with a thunderous boom. A strong wind follows coming from the direction of Eastham. In Eastham a dark cloud can be seen forming over the island to the east of the city.

We then went to Qalab'r for some reason, which I don't know. I just followed the group ! We killed a Makkar there, which was spawned with a few Shadow Eddys and various other baddies. We made way to Eastham, where we found an Asheron's Servant, who asked for a Makkar eye. Just my luck, I happened to have one ! I handed him the eye and he instructed me to go to Asheron's Island using the portal behind him.

After making a grueling run up the mountain to Asheron's House, I died. Then, I went back and died again. On my third trip, I recovered my bodies as well as many of those in my allegiance who fell beside me. There are 3 Shadow Mouth's which you have to enter. Journey to the bottom to kill the Mukkir General. Small balls of light will spawn on the ground as well as 'shadow anchors' or something to that effect. Pick up the light only, then jump off the edge into the abyss to be portalled back up top. On the 3 corners of Asheron's house are lamps which you have to light. One ball of flame will light ONE lamp. Therefore you have to repeat this process for the other two shadow mouths.

Once this was done, we returned to Eastham and spoke to the Asheron's Servant again. He granted us access to the white tower, where Asheron usually overlooks all us folks on Dereth. When we returned to the Castle, we spoke to the Guardian (level 710 Golem) and he portalled us inside. To leave, you can speak to the Guardian again. Then, speaking to the Asheron's Servant (who is in BOTH places), he granted us permission to pick up the Shadow Anchor things which we came across in the first run through the Shadow Mouths. Go back, repeat the previous process of killing the Mukkir General, and you can pick up one of the anchors. Get all 3, then return them to the Servant for a Pendant of Carraida, which cats a few life spells on you. The Servant implies that there are 3 of these items, and that together they are very powerful. This is just the first piece.

You can also return the item for XP I believe. Note : The makkar eye and talking to the Asheron's Servant after lighting the lamps, will yield XP. I received about 13million and 49 million respectively at level 206.

Inside Asheron's Inner Sanctum, which is a portal which you must walk through inside his tower, there is a library. There is a note on the table as well saying that he's gone, but he's sure nothing will happen while he's away. He picked a good time to go on a vacation!
 

(click to enlarge)

To recap :

1) Retrieve a Makkar eye and give to the Asheron's Servant, east of Eastham near the LS and the windmill at 17.8N 64.3E.(might be optional, not sure)
2)Run up to Asheron's castle
3)Go into each of the Shadow Mouths and kill the general. Loot the flame.
4)Light all 3 of the lamps, accessible from the ramparts.
5)Return to the Servant in Eastham and talk to him
6)Speak to the Guardian in the Castle after returning to Asheron's Island
7)Speak to the Servant inside of the Tower
8)Return to the Shadow Mouths, kill the general, loot the anchors
9)Return the anchors to the Servant for a reward

Love the site Maggie, keep it up !

~Jiggi-watt of Leafcull
