New Quest - Tanada House of Waters!
Thanks to Ishmael of LC for details below. Thanks also to Devsie of SC, Flandamier of LC, Shogunz of TD!

If you've completed the Tanda quests by talking to Ben Ten's students in shoushi and Yanshi you're flagged for the new Tanada quest. Talk to the student in Shoushi. If you've helped her recently enough, she'll refer you to Meshenq in Zaikhal. Meshenq tells you that while tracking the Tanda to the Southern Landbridge, he was sent to his lifestone by a group of Tanada. He gives you a medallion and asks you to investigate the area, bringing back any relevant info on this new Tanada.

NPC: Sayuji Jina - 33.3S 72.8E in Shoushi
NPC: Meshenq - 13.8N, 0.6E in Zaikhal

Sayuji Jina tells you, "Ah yes, I remember your excellent work in the past on this ongoing Tanada investigation. I would ask you to tangle again with those renegade Nanjou Shou-Jen... My colleague Meshenq in Zaikhal had a run-in with some Tanada while he was exploring the southern land bridge. Please go to Zaikhal and see if there is some way you can help him."

Meshenq tells you, "Ah! Sayuji Jina told me of you! You helped her investigate the Tanada houses of Earth and Air, did you not? Excellent! I need your help now."
Meshenq gives you Tanada Gate Medallion.
Meshenq tells you, "I was exploring the southern land bridge when I was attacked by a pair of Tanada! But these were strange Tanada, not like the descriptions I've heard from Jina. They killed me and sent me back to my lifestone here in Zaikhal, but before I died I was able to tear this medallion off one of them..."
Meshenq tells you, "My camp was at 85.2 S, 4.4 W, and I think they came from the west. I would advise you to search the area and see if you can locate some trace of the Tanada presence... Please bring me back any evidence you might turn up of what has brought the Tanada to that area."

Dungeon: Tanada House of Water - 85.3S 5.8W (Level 80+)

You can reach the Southern Land Bridge via the settlement portals directly west of the drop in Qalabar. You're running mostly west to the coords he provides: 85.2S, 4.4W. You'll have to stay on the mountains, zig-zagging your way there. Don't jump down as you'll get stuck in a valley. You'll find an Abandoned camp site, apparently Meshenq's. Meshenq mentioned that he suspected the group came from the west.

Head west and you'll discover the Tanada House of Water Portal. You must put on the medallion at this point, or you will be informed that you have to complete a quest to enter the portal.

The dungeon resembles those of the Tanada of the house of Breath and Earth...however it pulses with portal energy, apparently from the Virindi these Tanada have allied with. It's very linear - as long as you don't turn around you'll reach the end. Along the way you'll encounter Acolytes and Masters of Decay (level 115), as well as Virindi Profane. The Masters are wearing Virindi Masks! The final room contains a master and what appears to be a Virindi Consul, but goes by the name Consul of Tanada. He's only slightly harder than a Virindi Consul.

Consul to the Tanada is reduced to cinders!
My master will take his revenge, weakling flesh puppet!

Loot the corpse to receive an untranslated Virindi shard, the Consul's Message Shard - surely something Meshenq would be interested in. Luckily, the translator required for Virindi Shards is right up the hill from him.

Thanks to Sweet Destiny of LC and fellows Vici, Flandamier, Madaster Mage, Linx, Sweet Destiny, Natalia Brynmad, GG, and Dipali!

Graveyard: 65.0S, 44.1W
NPC: Apep-Tek the Tolerant
 

Details from Devsie of SC: "Hi maggie, Tao found the graveyard in the Dires and a group of us went to check it out. We probably had about 20 toons running there in the end, such was the interest.

To reach the graveyard, take Wai Jhou portal or something closer? Run to 65S 44W roughly. Hard to miss it is a big landscape area.

As we arrived and ran through gravestones we were met by a lot of level 200ish undead and skellies. We later worked out that this spawn is present from "Gloaming till Dawnsong", and during the daytime are a bunch of very annoying rats (lightning works well if you're a mage).

So quickly people started dying left and right and we gave the command to jump onto the rooftops of the structures there. From here we could hunt relatively safely. But we still had a number of deaths and unfortunately a lot of people dropped out. You mainly need to kill Wights (undead), Wight Captains and Wight Sorcerers. They drop shrouds which every now and again you need to drop down to collect. The captains take more killing but tend to drop 2-3 shrouds. The sorcerers have fairly nasty spells. Hit me for 215 whilst I was buffed with a major ward on.

Eventually about 5 of us finished it - to the best of our knowledge so far.
We collected up 10 Shrouds each and handed them in to Apep-tek the Tolerant found amongst the gravestones and the rest of the spawn.
