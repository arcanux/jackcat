Asheron's Call Referral Program - from the Turbine Site, December 6, 2007

A Refer A Friend & Play For Free!

We’re proud to announce the Turbine Referral Program, a great way to earn free game time by introducing your family and friends to Asheron’s Call! It's so easy a Drudge can do it!

The Turbine Referral Program lets paying subscribers of Asheron’s Call refer friends to play the game and receive free game time for successful referrals, subject to certain terms and conditions (see myaccount.turbine.com/referral/rules/ac ). Each of your referred friends will be sent a Guest Key to try the game for free for fourteen (14) consecutive days!

For every friend that allows their trial to convert into the retail version of the game, you'll get a referral credit that can be redeemed for 30 days of FREE game time to be added to your subscription for the game! So get started today by visiting myaccount.turbine.com!
