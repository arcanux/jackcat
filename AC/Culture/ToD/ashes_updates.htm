Re: Daily Update - 11/17/05 (by Lupus Argenteus)

Thank you, Frelorn,

After the November update went live, players discovered that the Glenden Wood Recall Orb had a bug where you could not exit casting mode once you had used the recall spell. After research and assistance from players, we identified that it is the act of recalling across a server boundary which results in the bug.

In our test environment, we attempt to simulate the live worlds as closely as possible. However, some factors are unable to be simulated by a test environment. In this instance, our recall tests (we did multiple) all worked successfully, because the character performing the recall happened to be on the same subserver as Glenden Wood due to our test world's load balancing. Some people have pointed out similarities to bugs involving Recall the Sanctuary and Aerlinthe Recall, and after a brief amount of research thus far, the root cause of the GW bug appears to be similar to, but not exactly like, the Sanctuary/Aerlinthe issue.

One thing we strive to do is not to make the same mistake twice. Although in some cases (such as cantrips) we have our issues, overall we have prevented repetition of many serious bugs. This is a test case which we have not in the past thought to perform, but it is very clearly a necessary one. We will be adding it to our repertoire for future use whenever we introduce a new portal or recall spell.

Weekly Update - 11/28/05 (by Frelorn)

Hi there everyone. I hope that everyone had a great weekend. I know I did as I gorged myself on as much Turkey as my body would allow. Since this was a long weekend I figured that on top of the update on what we are doing this week I would answer some of the questions that were brought up. So without further delay, let’s get right to it.

These questions are in no particular order, so don’t put any meaning into the order that I answer them.

When will ToD be an online download with a CD key?
I spoke to our marketing team today about this and the ball is now rolling on getting this done. While you can download the actual game client already, the ability to purchase CD keys online is coming. As soon as I have a solid date, I will let you know.

Why don't monsters level anymore?
This was changed a while back when the skill system was redone. A creature having the ability to level was a side-effect of the old system. When we redid the skill system a long time ago the side-effect was lost. When it was brought up it was decided that due to the fact that some boss creatures could level beyond the player abilities to kill them, it was best to just leave that ability out.

What happened to the Darktide Hardware? Any update on this?
At the time this was announced to the community the makeup and personnel of the team was entirely different, and I wasn’t in on these discussions. I have put the question out to the appropriate members of the staff and as soon as I have an update on this I will let you know.

Will there ever be a Dev chat again?
We would love to do this again. Right now the biggest obstacle for doing something like this is time. The team is very busy and free time is in short supply these days. It is something we would love to do in the not so distant future. With that being said, as soon as we can find the free time to do something like this, we will give you all plenty of notice.

Weekly Update
And finally let’s get to the update for this week. During any cycle we do things can change as new issues or goals come up, so this will be a rough idea of what we have planned for this week without getting too much into the specifics of things.

This week the team is working on resolving issues that popped up for December. This is a week that a lot of testing gets done. The team puts in lot of work to make sure that when these events go live, they are as good as they can be. As some of you may know, when you introduce new code into existing code a lot of interesting things can happen, sometimes in unexpected places. So a lot of time is spent checking out everything we can to make sure that there are no surprises when the event goes live. Unfortunately this process isn’t perfect, but we are always trying to find ways to improve on this so that things that have gotten through in the past don’t get through again. The process of working on the January event will also begin this week.

That’s about it for now. As always if anything important comes up we will make sure to update you on it. Have a great day!

Update: In September, we changed the next billing date for subscribers who potentially could be affected by Katrina till Jan 1st 2006.
For the month of November, however, these customers were charged. We have since refunded these subscribers for the month of November.

Update: Due to popular demand we have decided to extend the date on the migration of accounts until January 10, 2006. After this date, players will no longer be able to migrate old accounts.

Weekly Update - 12/05/05 (by Frelorn)

Hi there everyone. Yes, it’s that time again, time for the weekly update post!

Let’s get right to it then. This week we will be continuing to test and fix any bugs that we come across for the December event, as well as fixing some already existing bugs. The planning for January has also gotten underway. The plans are by no means concrete, but we know the direction we are going, and the design plans are well underway.

Now on to something that I want to try and do every week with these updates, which is to answer a few questions that have come up over the previous week.

Can you make the Ashbane sword from the Dark Lady quest Ivoryable?
I checked with the team on this, and they don’t see a problem with getting this done in a future update.

With all the male mages complaining about that Fiun robe that looks like a dress is there any chance of having two versions of it one for the males (hooded) and one for the females?
This is actually something that was discussed the other day in our meeting. While we didn’t initially have any plans to do this, it may be something that we can take another look at.

Live Events Feedback.
We do appreciate all of the feedback we have gotten from you thus far as we have gone forward with the current story arc. One of things that was brought up was posting a schedule online somewhere so that players know when to log in so they can participate. We are actually a bit divided on this topic. On one hand we understand that everyone who is interested in these events would like the option to know when these events are taking place, so they can try to be there. On the other hand, one of the wonderful things about Dereth is that you never know when something interesting might happen.

That being said, we will be re-evaluating how we handle things the next time we want to push the story forward with live events.

And finally I just wanted to give you all a heads up that some time tomorrow we will be posting an announcement about the AC1 team.

That’s about it for now. Please keep an eye out for that announcement tomorrow. Everyone have a great week!
