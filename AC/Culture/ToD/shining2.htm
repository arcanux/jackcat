New Alduressa Shadow Armor!
Thanks to Dark-Maji of SC, Dakmor Kavu of HG, Blunt ibn mein of FF, Lyc, High King Moash of WE, Gandalf Zu'l Zoran of SC, Agusto from HG, N A S of MT, Stray Dog of MT, Pawel of HG!

Hey Maggie love the site! Quest is simple enough. You can find Iian di Alduressa at 89.0N, 67.6W next to the fortress of the Knights of Karlun nearby Silyun

Iian di Alduressa tells you, "With the aid of the other Master Armorers, I have managed to master the basics of crafting Alduressa versions of the Shadow Armors. I've crafted both the versions that may be set with the "Atlan Stones", and the more resilient, non-elemental versions. If you wish to trade one version of your Alduressa armor set for another, simply hand it to me. Please take out all Atlan stones from your armor before handing it to me."

Iian di Alduressa tells you, "Also, I have heard that the other Master Armorers have apprentices who sell paints for recoloring the Greater Shadow Armors. Those paints will also work upon the Greater Shadow Alduressa that I create. If you do not like the color, please give me the armor piece and I will restore the piece to its original colors, which you may then choose to recolor as you like."

Simply hand him the old shadow armor for the new kind. Both appear to be "greater" in name, but if you hand back the first suit (colored) you will receive the high AL and lvl 30+ wield restricted white suit.

~Dakmor Kavu, HG

Thanks to Tectonic Rifts, Absolution and Daawn of FF, Magicdef of HG, Fury's apprentice of TD, Rak'Than Lograthsun of VT, Minnie the Minx of VT, Reapist of TD, Sword Win of HG, Pharo of HG, Spaghetti Bender of TD!

Quest Details: Son of Pooky

Absolution of FF reports: "The quest is simple. You basically kill the White Rabbit (thanks Tectonic for your sacrifice). Loot the gem and port to dungeon. There are several new tyoes of rabbit from Midnight, Twilight, and Dusk with bunnies. There are quite alot and without melee you would prolly get nipped to death. At the end is "Son of Pooky" . He is a little harder to kill than his pappy, but not too difficult. He drops several keys to the rabbit hutch which seems to have a random reward. I got the "Ring around the Rabbit" ring spell which has the same stats as a slashing ring spell with a cooler effect. Sends 8 rabbits shooting out in a ring. Tectonic got the rabbit girth. Not sure what else there might be...possibly a full rabbit suit?

Thanks to Daawn and Tectonic Rifts for the hippity hoppity fun quest."

You give Ulgrim the Unpleasant Green Marshmallow Eep.

There is no indication, other than what Ulgrim says, that you received the title. Purple Eeps are the only item that grants you the title.

Latest News - In-Game Help Files Updated!
As the conflict between Queen Elysa and King Varicci continues, some of the people of Dereth have reported seeing the return of Commander Kurth. It was thought that after defeating Antius Blackmoor that Kurth had been killed. This was not the case. Why has Kurth returned, and what does this mean to continuing war between two lands?

Ciandra has reported that there has been more movement by the Essences of Magic. She is requesting that any brave souls wishing to gather information for her about this, should do so with caution. The Essences appear to be gaining in strength.

Spring is upon Dereth once again. Adveturers will notice the snow has melted and some of the younger creatures of Dereth have returned to the landscape again. The trees and flowers have also bloomed again, making Dereth once again abundant with life.
