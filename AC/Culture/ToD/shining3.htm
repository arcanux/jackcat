Hi Maggie, love the site and all.

I was playing my new level 13 character on Solclaim (April 17th) when this message came up:

The townsfolk of Stonehold cry for assistance! The town is under attack by Commander Jared Kurth and a detachment of Viamontian Knights!

Some people on the server thought the event would be in Holtburg or Shoushi or Glenden Wood because of the past events, but was this a surprise!

Thank you for your time in reading this.
