Daily Update - 9/29/05 (by Calandryll)

October Update
Documentation is nearly complete and we’ve already started working on test plans. Should have some information about the content next week.

Upcoming Downtime
This is probably the last thing any of you want to hear about, but the situation with our database hardware isn’t resolved yet. We’re scheduling another downtime for Wednesday, October 5th. This downtime will start at 4am EDT and last until 8pm EDT. I know this is another long downtime and I wanted to give you all an explanation for this.

During the AC1 game server reboots and security updates on Monday September 12th, there was an error on the database servers during the reboot. We therefore scheduled on-site maintenance to work on the inactive database server on Wednesday September 14th.

During that downtime, maintenance was performed on the inactive database server. However, when the server was brought back online, we encountered another error. This error caused the database corruption that resulted in the rollback. At the end of this, we were only able to bring up one of the two database servers, resulting in the additional downtime on September 27th.

The maintenance window on September 27th did not go badly. It simply was not enough time to adequately troubleshoot and solve the issue. We executed our original plan, but it uncovered a hardware issue which we were unable to resolve in the time allotted for the downtime. This is why the downtime lasted longer than expected and it’s why we have to schedule another downtime.

To ensure that we do not have any reoccurrences of the September 14th downtime, it is best to have the worlds down while these issues are investigated and corrected.

We’ll keep you updated on the progress of this fix. Again, my apologies for the downtime, but it is necessary in order to ensure a stable experience for all of you.

Daily Update - 10/5/05 (by Calandryll)

For today's update, I’d like to talk about some of the topics that many of you have been asking about on the forums. Not everything is nailed down yet, but I did want to share the information that I do have.

Dawnsong
Turbine will be centralizing support for all Asheron’s Call subscribers at our MA offices in a few months. Therefore, the Dawnsong server will not be opened as originally planned. Adding a new server would further spread players out and splinter existing communities. We understand that some of you were looking forward to this server; however at this time we feel that opening another server would not be good for Asheron’s Call.

Face Artwork
At this time, the artwork for the faces isn’t going to change. While we understand that some players do not like the new looks, we cannot rework all of the faces. However, we are discussing adding the ability for players to change their look in game. This is something we are currently investigating but it won’t be part of the October publish. Right now this is not scheduled, but I will update you on this as we move forward.

High Resolution Textures
The high resolution textures are not ready yet, however we still intend to get these in the game. After further investigating the status of this, we need to do some more engineering work and they need a full QA pass. The QA pass is very important (and a bit time-consuming) to ensure the new textures do not cause crashes with certain graphics cards. I don’t have an ETA for this yet, but it is still on our “to do” list.

Today’s Maintenance
So far everything is moving along smoothly. The Ops team tells me we are on schedule. I’ll update you more as I get more information.

Hiring
We’re currently in the process of hiring an engineer (programmer), a designer, and a QA person for Asheron’s Call in order to fill out the team. You should see the job descriptions on the Turbine site in the coming days. We’re also interviewing some internal candidates for these positions as well. More on that as the hiring process progresses.

October Update
The October update is still looking good. We’re about code complete and then we’ll swing into full QA mode. We have an article planned for later this week to give you a quick look at some of the update.

Live Events
Some more good news. After getting a lot of positive feedback about the events, we have decided to continue these for the foreseeable future. So we’ll be doing both game content updates AND live events from now on!

I'm Back on the AC Design Team - 10/13/05 (by Speed)

Hey everyone, I wanted to drop a line and let you know that I’ve returned to the AC1 Live Team. I’ve never been a big poster so most of you may not remember me. I’ve worked on the Live Team on and off in one capacity or another since AC’s Launch.

Background: I started out on the QA Team and eventually moved into the roll of AC1 Data Integrator where I made sure that the designer’s quests and quest data made it into the game. I tried my hand at content whenever I could, starting out mostly with housing items and Ulgrim the Unpleasant. Interestingly enough Crowley who I’m working with now was the original creator of Ulgrim. Expect more Ulgrim strings! After awhile I started making small quests and dungeons and became a sortof Designer/Integrator. Really everyone on the team had multiple hats anyway. Scenario created lots of content as did Srand and Ibin. Man those were fun times. Before the game moved out to LA I had become a full time designer. I unfortunately was unable to go to LA since I didn’t want to leave my family here in New England.


You may know me from such content as:

Ulgrim’s Monthly Sayings

Flag Quest: Grievver Hive with the Spinning Grievver, good old Grievver Scholar

Ulgrim’s Island

Blind Snowman

I did the rolling balls of death mechanics for the Gaerlan Arc

Snowman Village

I worked with Todd on the rebuild of Arwic

Candeth Keep and the Stronghold collectors

Fishing

Implemented Chris Foster’s Tekapuapuh and his Putiputipuh grub Quest (aka A boy and his Grub)

Past Masks – I’m working on Masks for this October too

Gareth Dain’s Dissapearance

Renegade Garrison – The Lugian Garrison attacked by the Olthoi

Skeleton Castle – Baron Nuvillus and his crown

The Quest Gems – Drudge Fight, The Homunculus, Resting Place, Dark Towers, The Gibbering, Coin of Attachment

I had been working on AC2 for a bit after the AC1 team went to LA and then I went back to AC1 QA for a bit, which is where I was when we found out that the AC1 team was coming back east. I’m really happy to have the Live Team back here in Westwood. If you can’t tell by my history with Asheron’s Call, I love the game and it’s players. It’s very unique in it’s design and feel in the MMO space and I hope to keep it that way and create some fun content. I’m sure you’ll let me know if I jump the reedshark.

As Ulgrim would say, “What are you doing just standing there for? Go fetch me some stout! I’ve got important stuff I’m working on, with complicated circuits and things that are beyond your primitive intellects.” Well, that’s Ulgrim, not me. Have fun, I’m going back to work!
