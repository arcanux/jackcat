New Allegiance Updates!
 Thanks to Larceny of TD, Ned Cleversun of TD!

@allegiance - Commands to help manage your allegiance.

@allegiance boot [-account] <name> - Removes a character from your allegiance.
@allegiance ban <add/remove> <name> - Bans all characters on the given character's account from your allegiance (and boots them too!)
@allegiance ban list - List the characters whose accounts are banned from your allegiance.
WARNING! Officers banning or booting a character by account could wind up in a situation where they are no longer in the allegiance if they boot a character that is above them in the hierarchy.
@allegiance info <name> - Requests information on a member of your allegiance.
@allegiance chat <on/off> - Turn allegiance chat on and off.
@allegiance chat kick <name>[, <reason>] - Kick a player temporarily from the allegiance chat room.
@allegiance chat gag <name> - Gags a player so that they cannot see or speak in the allegiance chat room for 5 minutes.
@allegiance chat ungag <name> - Ungags a gagged allegiance member so that they may once again see and speak in the allegiance chat room.
@allegiance broadcast <message> - Broadcast a message to the entire allegiance. Limited to 10/day. Also: @ab
@allegiance officer <add/set> <level #> <name> - Assigns the position of officer, with the given level of permissions, to the named character.
@allegiance officer <remove> <name> - Removed the named character as an allegiance officer.
@allegiance officer clear - Clears all officer positions.
@allegiance officer [list] - list your allegiance officer. Can be used by anyone in an allegiance.
@allegiance title set <level #> <title> - Sets the title of the given officer level.
@allegiance title clear - Clears all officer titles.
@allegiance title [list] - Lists all the officer titles for your allegiance.
@allegiance name <set/clear> - Displays, sets, or clears the name of your allegiance.
@allegiance lock <on/off/toggle/check> - Locks, unlocks, or displays the locked state of your allegiance.
@allegiance lock bypass <clear/name> - Sets, clears, or displays a single character as an approved vassal. That character may then swear into a locked allegiance.
@allegiance hometown - Recalls you to your allegiance bindstone, if your allegiance has tied to one.

Several Viamontian attacks have take place recently, led by Count Dardante and his evil experiment Commander Kurth. These attacks have made many people of Dereth nervous, as the count tries to recreate the effect of the Heart Breaker, the sword that cause [sic] the permanent death of a true hero, Antius Blackmoor.
