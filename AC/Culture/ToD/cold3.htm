Live From Leafcull!

Hi Maggie!

Leafcull reporting in from its live event. An abominable snowman attacked Frost Haven and on it had lots of several presents that were frozen. Just then Scold popped down from the mountaintop and we killed him along with all his minions. On the minions were a bunch of coals. Use the coals on the presents to melt them and then open them up to reveal a prize.

As of now we know of 6 different presents that are available and all are random. The Holiday Hearth, Holiday Feast, Dollhouse, Pack Scold, Snowmaker, and Holiday Lights. The Holiday Lights are ENORMOUS and will cover an entire side of a wall. I am including several pics with more to come soon.

On a side note, if you are unlucky enough to NOT get a present from the Abominable snowman, make sure you get a coal from the little scolds. You can turn it into the mayor and still get a present. :)

Orb of Eternal Frost!

Hi Maggie! I love your site!
Ran across this item and couldn't find it on your site so I figured I'd send you an update.

A "special" Abominable Snowman is spawning in Frost Haven every four hours this month (December 2006, Cold Tracks). He has 400k hit points and is a bit tougher than his standard Abominable Snowmen brethren that wander the landscape. I ran up to him fully buffed and baned and he killed me in two hits before I could even attempt to heal. He drops a LOT of frozen gift boxes and his corpse stays around a bit longer than a usual critter corpse. About 20 seconds after he dies a Scold (I think that's its name) magma golem spawns and drops a lot of hot coals that you use to thaw the frozen gift boxes and claim your prize. There is a 24 hour timer on looting the frozen gift box.

Attached are some screenshots of the prize I got. The first one is of me holding the orb and the second is the orb's stats. This little orb can fire snowballs very quickly.
