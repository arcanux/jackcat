New Quest - Gardens of Menilesh!

Kalazar of SC with the new Graveyard quest concerning Lord Rytheran.

First I went to Wai Jhou and talked to the new Radiant Blood NPC to start a new quest for the Lord Rytheran search, I ran to Graveyard and there was the Gardens of Menelish portal (64.7S 43.8W), along with the summoning stone for the Jester (not involved with the quest). Inside there he was Lord Rytheran who had a task for me.

Dungeon: Gardens of Menilesh - 64.7S, 43.8W, Levels 150+

As you touch the stone's surface your fingers leave glowing trails that slowly seep into the stone. you hear distant laughter and then...

Lord Rytheran tells you, "Ah, you seek my mnemosyne, do you not? My memories of that long-ago day when I read from the bok... Your masters desire it so that they may meddle in this affair and save their fragile settlements from the depradation of the Blight. Well enough. I will give you the mnemosyne you seek if you do something for me..."
Lord Rytheran tells you, "In the ancient garden below, my followers have gone mad, forgotten themselves and their allegiance to me. Regrettably, they had something that is dear to me in their possession when they lost their minds. A ring of some... sentimental value. Find that ring, and bring it to me. I will give you the mnemosyne you seek when you bring me the ring. You will have to kill me to get the mnemosyne otherwise, and I can already see you lack the power and the will to do that."

After running down, the new Lost Menelish Denizens liked to vuln you and shoot level 7 wars. There was also a strange pillar which I used and it spawned several Moarsman. They dropped nothing that I found.

New Quest - Harlune's Diplomacy!
Magicpipe II of TD reports: "Lock pick is required to get to the NPC in Mite Maze to start the quest. I'm guessing a level requirement of 160 +, but I can't be sure on that. Able to use the portal to Bur, or Bur recall.... if you have not been flagged yet to get to Bur, there is an NPC in Cragstone that starts the silly quest (Shan Zhen).

Start by heading to the Mite Maze. I always go to the surface of hub, then due east to the mountain shortcut portal, and then a very short jog to Mite Maze east of the drop.

Directions to the locked door as follows: left, left to long tunnel, at the end (lamp on table) go right to square room and pass to fork, left on fork, the next fork go straight (or right), and to the last fork go right which should lead you up a path to the locked door... NPC room will be the first right after the door.

Roderick tells you, "Hmm...Ya think ya might be interested in doin' a favor for Harlune? Ya look tough enough to survive what he has in mind."
Roderick tells you, "The Falatacot Matriarchs sent him a message, ya see, askin' if he'd like to ally. Well, of course he don't - the Falatacot can't be trusted one bit. So he wants to send a message back...but ya know how Harlune can be."
Roderick tells you, "Anyways, he asked me to find some volunteers to deliver the mission. He didn't tell me to mention it, but I'll be nice and mention it anyways - the Falatacot ain't gonna be happy when they read the message."
Roderick tells you, "Here, take this and bring it to Bur. One of the statues up there should lead to a new place where ya can go and deliver the message, and then ya better be ready for a fight. If ya win, bring something back to prove to me that ya won."
Roderick gives you Diplomatic Message from Harlune.

Diplomatic Message from Harlune
This official diplomatic message from Harlune is written in three languages. Two versions are in what you'd guess to be Empyrean and Ancient Falatacot, but the third is in Roulean... unfortunately. You've never seen a cruder, irreverent, more spiteful letter in your life. This letter was designed by a master to anger whoever it is addressed to.
Give this note to the Falatacot Herald in the Falatacot Visitor's Alcove.

The statue's eyes light up suddenly - it can be used again, you would surmise.
The statue whispers in your head, "You bear a message for the matriarchs. You may pass."

Speak to Roderick to start the quest then head to Bur's South Catacombs... to get to bur you will need the recall or run there from New Colier settlement portal in Holt.. (Bur portal is at 67n-ish 30e-ish I think... top of hill, lots of snow, big portal, purple orbs, and an unfriendly golem npc.)

Buff up...helpful weapons for this quest would include, but are not limited to, undead slayer, ghost slayer, and frost rends for lizards.

Once in the southern catacombs, use the rock on the wall (that be a right from drop past tunnel then right again till you see a portal... use rock next to portal rather then going inside). Once inside you will enter a giant room with doors all around... this is actually a giant square room. Once on the "track" head right, down to the end, then head left down to the end again. Now this time, turn left again, and go in the small room 4th door down on the right side... have your party use the statue on the wall and it should port you to a safe drop.

Once you are ready to move again, head out of the door then turn right, follow the path until the second door on the right... it's easy to miss the second one if you are running fast because it's right after a right turn and then there's another right turn after the door. Go inside the door, at this point there is really only one way. Follow the path past a few doors, eventually you will open a door to a large room with corpses of undead... and players who got owned.

Running into the room will trigger a spawn of high level undeads and an unusable statue in the back... you can either stay at the door and fight or you can go inside to the back near the statue and kill too (be wary of war spells that the robed ones will constantly chain cast). After you see a green spam have the party use the statue to be ported to the next dungeon...safe drop rebuff as needed.

Quest Update - Green Mire Grave!
Again from Sanndh of WE: "So I made my way to the Greenmire. Since I have not such fond memories of trying to get the Greenmire Armor there as a starting player....

Ok, update from Green Mire: There is a new NPC in Shoushi where the Shopkeeper is, at 33.7 72.8: Hiro Ishigame, who is wearing a VERY bright green outfit.

Hiro Ishigame tells you, "Greetings, I am Hiro Ishigami, a scholar studying the history and artifacts of the Green Mire Warrior."
Hiro Ishigame tells you, "Perhaps you could assist me. When the Green Mire Warrior died, he was set to rest in a large complex used to serve as his grave. Since that time, however, it has been completely overrun with drudges, mosswarts, and all sorts of other horrible things."
