Pet Penguin!
Thanks to Flandamier of LC, Dakmor Kavu of HG, Joxer T Mighty of SC, Shen-Mara

Flandamier reports: "Hello Maggie! The Mr. P quest is a nice little quest that will net you your very own pet penguin to follow you around!

To start the quest you will need to head to Freehold. You can get there by taking the south passageway in umbrall halls. The portal is the last one on the right. QUEST TIP: Tie to the Freehold portal as you will be making a number of trips back here.

From the drop head directly east about .4 clicks and you will see Mr.P there (72.8S x 93.0E) basking in the sun.

Click on him and he will give you an empty candy wrapper.
 

Marked Candy Wrapper
A crumpled and re-flattened candy wrapper. Marked on tthe inside, in black ink, is a penguin footprint. Mr. P gave this to you, to give to Larinne Kerendova, the Pet Store Manager, in exchange for some 'proper' penguin food.
This item cannot be sold.

Value

 0

Burden

 5

Special Props

 Attuned, Bonded

Mr. P reaches into his "P-pocket" and pulls out a small piece of candy. His eyes light up at the sight of it and he quickly tosses it into his mouth.
Mr. P leans back and continues to enjoy his sweet treat when he realizes he is being watched.
Feeling a little embarrassed Mr. P puffs up his belly and squints at you. You feel as if this strange little penguin is judging you. After a few awkward moments he picks up an old candy wrapper from the ground, smoothes it out a bit and hands it to you.
Mr. P gives you Marked Candy Wrapper.
Mr. P sits back again and hums to himself while rubbing his tummy.

Take this to Larinne Kerendova (the petshop owner) out of Holtburg (35.2N x 39.3E) and hand it to her. She will give you a bag of candy.

You give Larinne Kerendova Marked Candy Wrapper.
Larinne Kerendova tells you, "What's this? Ahh, Mr. P is looking for more penguin treats. Very well, here you go."
Larinne Kerendova gives you Small Sack of Penguin Treats.
Larinne Kerendova tells you, "What? How do I know that? It says so right here, see?"
Larinne Kerendova points at the paw-print, and then at the placement of the toes.

Small Sack of Penguin Treats
A small sack, filled with sweet, yet nutritious, penguin treats.
This item cannot be sold.

Go back to Freehold and give the candy to Mr.P.

You give Mr. P Small Sack of Penguin Treats.
Mr. P is sitting outside nibbling on cookies when you hand him the Small Sack of Penguin Treats.
Mr. P sniffs at the sack and can smell the pengy food. Mr. P sticks his tongue out at it but knows that a proper diet is best for young pengys. Mr. P nods at you for a job well done.

Mr.P will now hand you an igloo shaped cookie.

Mr. P continues to nibble on his cookie a bit then hands it to you.
Mr. P gives you Partially-eaten Cookie.
Odd, it's shaped like an igloo... Maybe you should look for a mechanic who knows a thing or two about igloos.

New Quest - Tachi of Grace!

Hi Maggie, Fzzt from FF here, Did the follow up Tanada Clan quest that links to last months series for the schools. The end result was a pretty decent looking Tachi of Grace; however, being a mage, I am not able to wield it, but it's ivoryable, so at least good for a trophy.

Here's my write up and I've included some pictures:

To make the quest go easier I suggest finding a crater portal, a couple of yanshi & shoushi portal gems.

Talk to Sayuji Jina (33.3S 72.8E) in Shoushi

Sayuji Jina tells you, "You've already aided me once, for which I thank you. I would ask you to aid me a second time."
Sayuji Jina writes out a note, and then places the Enlightened Master's Medallion into the letter and seals it.
Sayuji Jina tells you, "Take this missive to Ookami Kiri, in Yanshi. She may have additional work for you, once she reads this."
Sayuji Jina gives you Folded Missive
 

Folded Missive
A sealed letter, containing both a note and a jade medallion, addressed to Ookami Kiri, in Yanshi.
This item cannot be sold.

Value

 0

Burden

 210

Special Props

 Attuned, Bonded

Head to Yanshi, upstairs of the bar you will find Ookami Kiri

Ookami Kiri tells you, "You are one of the ones aiding my friend Sayuji Jina-san. Does your task progress well?
Hand her the missive
Ookami Kiri takes the note with a polite bow, and then reads over it quickly.
Ookami Kiri tells you, "It seems, looking at this medallion, that Sayuji Jina-san's fears have born fruit. My Mistress feared that this may have been the case, and has left me with a course of action, which I will now pass on to you."
Ookami Kiri tells you, "There is an Enlightened Master of the Tanada Clan, one who came to Dereth in the beginnings of the Portals appearing in Sho lands. My Mistress has left me with a message to send to him, to seek his aid should Jina-san's fears prove true. If this corruption upon the Tanada Clan of the Nanjou Shou-Jen be a recent one, which it does seem to be, then he may be free of it still."
Ookami Kiri tells you, "Take this letter to the Tanada training school, hidden at 66.3N, 7.6E, and deliver it to Master Seijuro. Be warned, however, that the students of this school will not take kindly to your arrival, and you will need to prove your worth to meet the Master Seijuro, much as you did in gaining this medallion for Jina-san."
"Ookami Kiri gives you A Formal Request"

Shortest path I found was to grab a Crater portal and take the Shaky Ledge Cottages Portal (66.4N,14.3E) which exits at 62.5N, 8.3E. Then it's just a short run to the NW. There is an underground portal, level 80 requirement at 66.3N, 7.6E.

Once inside the dungeon, it is exactly the same as the previous Tanada quests. Run ahead to the 'T', to the left is the altar & portal, to the right is a linear path to each Master. One each; Master of Earth, Master of Stone, Master of Metal, Grab the Medallion off their corpse (1 per corpse) and retrace your path back to altar. Give the altar the medallion for access to the next dungeons portal which is at the altar.

You give Tanada Shrine of Earth Medallion of Earth.
Though the Shrine of Earth does not move or change in any appreciable way, you can almost feel its approval as you present it with the medallion that once belonged to the Master of Earth.

You give Master Seijuro A Formal Bequest.
As Master Seijuro reads the letter, a look of mild surprise passes over his face.
Master Seijuro tells you, "Well, I honestly never thought I'd see a letter in this handwriting ever again..."
Master Seijuro tells you, "It's been years since the battle at Yanshi, and she's been gone ever since. Now, she resurfaces, with such dire words, requesting my help..."
Master Seijuro tells you, "Were these words to have come from any but the one who sent you, I'd have killed you on the spot for such an accusation. But, I owe her my life, and I trust her judgment."
Master Seijuro tells you, "Here, Take this token to the one who sent you. She will know what it means, and will know how to respond to it. Consider it my final test of you, to ensure that this letter is real. If this letter is a fake, and your Mistress is not whom I think it is, then you will know the wrath of the Tanada Clan. Now, go."
Master Seijuro gives you Medallion of the Sword.

New Quest - In Search of Asheron!
Thanks to Trinstar, Frieze, My Very Own Flag Jr of LC!

Kalazar of SC reports:
"Hi Maggie! Kalazar of SC reporting on the ISO Asheron quest:

Talk to Shan Zhan of Cragstone, near the blacksmith. Then go to Holtburg settlements and to the New Coiler Mine (40.3N 35.1E). Once in it's a short run to 67.4N 30.5E. After talking to him, go back to Shan Zhan in Cragstone and get Vault notes. Then back to holt and to the settlement portals again and this time in the West Holtburg Villas. Then run to 52.5 N, 38.9 E and in the Realaidain Eastern Vault. I ran straight and found a House Golem. I then went to the back of the dungeon, killed Karkuul the Defiler, and looted the imbued pyreal mote. Then took it back to the House Golem to gain his trust. He then gave me a note on the Ancient Necklaces. Choose the necklace West South West and says Crown studded with emeralds.

You should then get House Golem tells you, "That is not for me, human, but I sense that you have chosen the correct necklace." I went back to the Servitor(The holtburg new Coiler then to 67.4N 30.5E) and gave him the necklace. He then told me to get him a ring. Go talk to Shan Zhan again to get another note. He then sent me to 75.7N 3.7E. I went to Holtburg, then used the Neydisia castle portal and ran there. Talk to the House Golem again, then run through, kill Vizidh the Despoiler, loot the imbued pyreal mote (blue) and take it back to the house Golem. This time pick up the Ring Crest Emblem: Sword ringed in purple fire with amethyst shards. Give it to the house golem, he will tell you again it's not for him, but is correct. Run back to the Servitor once again and give him this ring.

Run back to Shan Zhan and get the last note. He then sends you to go fetch a goblet at the Realaidain Western Vault (58.0N 20.8W) from Stonehold. Talk to the house golem run to the end and kill the final boss, loot the imbued mote, give it back to the house golem. Grab the Goblet Brim Decor, Alternating diamonds and engraved leaves. Give it to the house golem, then for the last time take it to the servitor. The servitor then gives you a 6 page book of lore and you can then give it to Shan Zhan for some xp. Hope you enjoyed it!"

Quest Update - Dagger of Tikola!

Hile Troy of HG reports: "Hey Maggie, I couldn't beleive it myself, what I've found. I'm not some big quester like Paraduck, I just had a feeling. I went over to Lady Folthid's estate, and ran the dagger of Tikola quest. At the bottom in the chest, I found noo only the Dull Dagger, but an "Ancient Shard of Metal".

I researched a little, and apparently you could turn it in to the groundskeeper for a small exp bonus. I turned it in, and unfortunately, I don't have the exact words she used, but I do know she talked of her own research on the Fatalacot and her research on the whereabouts of Asheron.

She gave me a note:
"To create this portal, it will be of the utmost importance for the sit to be aerated properly. A simple square will not do, as the equidistant sides will diffuse the power. There needs to be a clear "direction of power". so to speak.

Perhaps the golden ratio would suit. The spiral which ma be derived from it occurs in certain magical creatures, most notably the niffis. A rectangle formed using this ratio may be the best base to work from, especially if the ley lines underneath were to match the internal divisions of it.

The golden ration is, of course, approximately 1.62804498874878482028683. That should be sufficient accurate for my purposes. And it would be a good number to use to encode the other message I must concern myself as well.

Note: The Mountain Sewer may be a good place to conduct further research and attempt to create he gems for these purposes."

I was stoked when I got this, of course, I checked you site to where the mountain sewer was: Outside of TouTou. I head in there and after wandering around (take a lot of rights), I came across a set of three interconnected rooms, and in one of the corners was a pile of portal gems. I picked one up. It has a 13 day timer for pickup.

The gem has an empyrean inscription on it, so my next step is to take it to Harlune. Thanks for reading, I hope this helps."

Update: "I hit the mite maze, and Harlune wouldn't take the gem. I went next to Bretself the Translator, and he surprised me with this:

You give Bretself the Translator Inscribed Purple Gem.
You are now level 28!
You have 258,475 experience points and 0 skill credits available to raise skills and attributes.
You will earn another skill credit at level 29.
You've earned 248,042 experience.
Bretself the Translator tells you, "I am surprised I have been seeing these in circulation. Can you keep a secret?"
Bretself the Translator tells you, "At one point a few years ago, I was honored to receive a letter from Asheron himself, through Scholar Antius Blackmoor. I was flattered to have received it, but what he asked me was unusual."
Bretself the Translator tells you, "He wished that I would watch out for anyone bearing these gems. If the person seemed powerful enough that they might know how to use them, I am to plead ignorance. I do not think you are, however."
Bretself the Translator gives you Letter to Bretself the Translator.
Bretself the Translator gives you Trade Note (10,000).
Bretself the Translator tells you, "Therefore, I shall tell you - they are from a hidden cache that Asheron stored in the mountains, for use in portal creation. I am not certain precisely where he intended to open a portal to, but the gems were to be a power source. The inscription reads, simply, "Property of Asheron Realaidain," nothing more."
Bretself the Translator tells you, "I shall share with you the letter I received. Perhaps it will tell you more about the gem and its uses. I'll also give you this trade note as payment for the gem - I am supposed to retain them, but I can at least offer you fair market value for them."

He took it, as you can see. The note he gave me was three pages long:

To the esteemed scholar Bretself,

It may come as a shock to receive a letter from the very subject you study, but you have become renowned for your studies upon my people. In a less tempestuous time, I would be delighted to sit down with you and speak of my past and what I know of history, as I still consider myself a teacher even after all that has happened. However, I instead find myself needing to request something of you.

Those who have come here from your home have proven to be adept in exploring the ruins of my people. I do not hold this against them, for my people no longer occupy these places and they can learn much from the artifacts of the past. There is one item in particular I would ask you to watch for, however. I have stored a cache of gems that I use for my portal research in the place known as the Mountain Sewer by your people. It is an old complex which predates my time.

I would appreciate if you, as a known researcher of my people, would watch for these gems. They are violet, and bear my name. They may be mistaken as artifacts of the past time, but in fact I am using them for present research. If people should bring you these gems, please appraise them closely. If you find that they may be powerful enough to attempt to use these gems, please ensure that they do not become aware as to their potential - simply claim the gems. You may disclose the truth about the gems to others as you see fit. Periodically a golem shall come to collect your gems and return them to the cache. I shall have to activate some new golems to facilitate this and other tasks I must do, and so will find myself traveling to the halls of a friend of my father to enable that.

The reason I ask you to warn people against using the gems is that most of these gems will not work properly. Only a gem whose energy is expended in a certain frequency - what scholars acknowledge as the base of the natural logarithm, approximately 2.71828182845904523536028747 - is useful for my purposes. This can be distinguished by its steady pulse, whereas most will pulse with increasing pauses between each pulse. Gems meeting this specification are hard to construct, and so I have ended up with many flawed works that should never be used for any purpose.

I offer my sincere thanks.

Written be Asheron himself, of course. I have a feeling this isn't the end to the quest, there has to be something more. I hope I've helped out, will look for more.
