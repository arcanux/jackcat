Amelia's Pet Gargoyle!
Hey Maggie. :) Kalazar of SC reporting on the new Graveyard NPC, Amelia. She only spawns at night, and once you talk to her, she runs, or should I say, walks around the graveyard and drops a dirt pile you must double click to get an item she's looking for. She drops 6 of these, and to help find them they are a yellow dot on your radar. Once you get all 6 you can give them to her for an Ancient Coin and a pet gargoyle. The items only last 1 hour after pick-up, so if you die - be quick.

Olthoi Scent Gland!
Rocklord reports finding this on an Olthoi Paradox Eviscerator!

Coruscating Olthoi Scent Gland
A small spherical gland retrieved from a Paradox-touched Olthoi. It smells absolutely horrible.
Use this gland to bypass the Sealed Olthoi Passage in the bottom of the Deeper Catacombs of the Paradox-touched Olthoi. Once used, this gland should mask your scent for 2 hours. (**Note: This gland will not work for characters below 120th level.)
This item cannot be sold.

Value

 0

Burden

 50

Special Props

 Attuned, Bonded

Zouf of LC reports:

NPC Starter: Ethan Wintermaine (43.8N, 54.9E)
Shortcut: Arwic's settlement Far Claw

It all start with a little hunt of Coruscating Olthoi Scent Gland which you get off the Paradox-touched Olthoi Swarm Eviscerator, Lacerator, Slasher or Ripper. They are easy to track, they are level 175. Once all your fellow got one, go kill the warded guardian. Becarefull, she's a lot more dangerous than she was from the last patch. When you killed her, go in BDC and enter the paradox's portal. Go to the egg room and give the scent gland to the sealed door. After a good run through many higher breed paradox-touched olthoi, you jump into a black mist to be teleported to the queen's room. Dangerous? Very!!! I hope you got a good group (or groups... there's about 19 carapace in her corpse) to kill her. Collect the carapace shard off her corpse than run back to Ethan Wintermaine. He will give you a weapon token which can be traded by the weapon crafter hidden in one of the tunnel below (the one @ NW)

Reward: up to 247M, choice of many type of weapon (sword, UA, spear, axe, altatl) or a shield. Title for the queenslayer.

(stats of weapons still to come)
 

New Kill Tasks!
Demogorgon of LC reports: "So I was running around in the olthoi area when I saw Npc's in a tent located at 43.8N 54.9E. Here's the text from all 4:

Ethan Wintermaine tells you, "If you're of a mind to help quell this invasion, I have a mission for you. We've found that, with the increase in the infestation of this area, that the stronger Paradox-touched Olthoi have some capacity to pass beyond the Sealed Olthoi Passage that those aiding the Guard Swiftblade have found."
Ethan Wintermaine tells you, "Those who have hunted them have been able to use these stronger Olthoi to gain passage beyond the door themselves."
Ethan Wintermaine tells you, "Beyond that passage lies my mission for you. It's a simple one, in concept anyways."
Ethan Wintermaine smiles.
Ethan Wintermaine tells you, "Before we get to that, you'll need to bypass the Wards on the Deeper Catacombs Portal. Guard Swiftblade, in the tent behind me, has more information in bypassing the Wards. Speak to him if you need more information on that process."
Ethan Wintermaine tells you, "Go beyond the passage, find the Paradox-touched Olthoi Queen, and kill her. Bring me back proof of her death, and I'll reward you for your part in it. Be warned, however. She's not a creature to be taken lightly, or hunted alone."

Adrien Swiftblade tells you, "Along with the rise of these strange new Olthoi, there is a Portal, warded by powerful magics, which formed in the Black Death Catacombs. You may've seen it already, or you may not have."
Adrien Swiftblade tells you, "Anyways, I've found a way to get the wards to drop for a time. There is a Ward Guardian in a cave located in a heavily infested area nearby, around 43.6N, 55.5E. When that thing dies, the wards falter for a time."
Adrien Swiftblade tells you, "Be warned, however, the Ward Guardian has grown dramatically in strength since it first appeared. It is now as strong or stronger than those twisted bugs that wander the inner areas of the infestation, and should not be taken lightly."
Adrien Swiftblade tells you, "Once the Ward Guardian is dead, so you should be able to enter the Deeper Catacombs of the Paradox-touched Olthoi. Once in there, look for anything that may be of use to use in our research and bring it back to me."
Adrien Swiftblade tells you, "If, for some reason, the Portal is closed again when you get there, you should find the Ward Guardian in his cave and kill him again."
Adrien Swiftblade tells you, "I would hurry though, every time the Ward Guardian dies, it's either reborn, recreated, or whatever roughly six hours after its death, and the Wards return with it."

Alicia Swiftblade tells you, "Greetings to you. I'm glad you've come up this way. If you're willing to aid us against these strange new Olthoi, I could set you to a task that you may find enlightening."
Alicia Swiftblade tells you, "Go out to the places where these new grubs have been appearing, and kill them before they grow into something far worse. Kill, let's say, 50 of these Paradox-touched Grubs, and I'll reward you for your aid in this. They seem to be most concentrated in the nearby valley, but I've seen signs of them all over 'Olthoi North'."

Alicia Swiftblade tells you, "You've done it! That should help thin their numbers for a while. We haven't been able to stop them completely, but your efforts should help keep the lands safe for a time. Come back in about a week, and I should have more for you to hunt."
You've earned 5,000,000 experience.

Tomihino tells you, "Greetings to you. I am glad you have come up this way. If you are willing to aid us against these strange new Olthoi, I could set you to a task that you may find enlightening."
Tomihino tells you, "In large numbers nearby, but seen throughout this 'Olthoi North', there are young Olthoi, those Nymphs of their species, touched by a strange Paradox of energies. Slay them, 50 should do for now, and return to me. Do this task and you shall be rewarded for it."

Tomihino tells you, "You have done well. With the losses you have inflicted upon their young, it should slow their expansion considerably. I offer my thanks to you, and humbly ask that you return in a week, to help keep their numbers down."
You've earned 5,000,000 experience.


Composite Atlatl!
Zouf of LC reports: "It's the same thing as the composite bow except you need to give the matty horn to Havala bint Mylos and, instead of a string, you need grievver spikes (Spiked Grievver drop them). A good place to hunt the spike is @ ancient caves. Add an handle et voila."

NPC: Havala bint Mylos - 60.1S, 87.9W (Ayan Baqur)
Text: Havala's Composite Atlatl Manual
 
Items: -2 matty horn (give it to Havala)
          -2 lugian sinew (use cooking pot to cure them)
          -3 grievver spike (use metal press to make a full oil vial)

Havala bint Mylos tells you, "If you wish to craft a Composite Atlatl for yourself I'll sell you a copy of my book on the subject for a Trade Note worth 1000 pyreals."
Havala bint Mylos tells you, "If you are looking to upgrade your atlatl or simply wish to help me collect some materials for my craft I have a small task for you."
Havala bint Mylos tells you, "I've heard of a chain of Ancient Caves where an unknown creature's bones may be found. These bones make exceptional handles for the Composite Atlatl. Unfortunately the caves are infested with Grievvers and can be quite dangerous for an unprepared explorer. Go and find a Hardened Bone in the middle level of the caves. I wouldn't suggest going deeper into the caves alone, but there are other bones that I can use buried in the deeper levels, should you risk the depths."
You give Havala bint Mylos Trade Note (1,000).
Havala bint Mylos tells you, "Here you are, the secrets to crafting your own Composite Atlatl."
Havala bint Mylos gives you Havala's Composite Atlatl Manual.

Bobo Updated!
Caius-Britannicus of MT reports: "Maggie, First off, love your site. Second I don't know if you have this or not but here is the new Bobo quest.

Grunter tells you, "Isn't Bobo great? You should talk to Jilna in Oo.. OLoo... Bobo's Refuge at the base of the volcano. She's a nice human who makes potions for Bobo to keep him from getting sleepy and sick."
Grunter tells you, "Bobo showed that ugly old Mudmouth! He drove Mudmouth away from Tusker Island with his mighty stone fist! Stupid, bad Mudmouth."

Start out going to Aphus Lassel and finding NPC Jilna Fullgood. She is on the upper level. Take the stairs up like you're turning in a tusk, then run around to the side.

Jilna Fullgood tells you, "Now that King Oolutanga is free of Mudmouth's hold I can treat him for the Jungle Orchid poison Mudmouth gave him. It's amazing he survived really. That much poison would have killed any normal Tusker. To create more of the potion he needs I'll need more King's Crown."
Jilna Fullgood tells you, "You can find the King's Crown water lily at the base of a giant tree overlooking the Chapel of Xi Ru on the last of the Vesayen Islands. Be careful, I hear it is protected by Moarsmen."

Jilna Fullgood tells you, "Oh, if you are able to find and destroy Mudmouth, I have been honored by Oolutanga to reward you in exchange for any befouled treasures he may have wrought."

Next, recall the Sanctuary (if your don't have that recall, buy an Ithanc Wedding center invitation from a wedding planner) then run to 83.4S / 89.8E and use the Portal Device there on the beach. There are a few mid level moarsemen around, but just run past. At that drop, do a U-turn and run along the beach to 88.6S / 85.3E. This is the point at which u can climb the mountain. Make your way up up up and oh did I mention... UP. Don't cross the bridge when ya come to it, is a dead end. You need to climb until ya come to a lake with large tree in the middle. 89.9S / 878.3E There you will find the King's Crown water lily that Jilna Fullgood sent ya for. Once everyone has one (check your pack to be sure...real pain if ya gotta run it again), recall Aphus Lassel or use monkey isle gem and give lily to Jilna Fullgood.

You give Jilna Fullgood King's Crown.
Jilna Fullgood tells you, "Yes, this looks perfect."
Jilna places the white petals of the flower into a mortar and sprinkles a number of dried herbs over it. She then uses her pestle to grind the ingredients together and adds the resulting powder to a potion of blue liquid. The potion fizzes and its blue color swirls to a pearlescent white.
Jilna Fullgood tells you, "Excellent, take this potion to Oolutanga and see that he drinks the whole thing."
Jilna Fullgood gives you King's Crown Infusion.

Now run normal route for Bobo Run.

You give Oolutanga, the Tusker King King's Crown Infusion.
Oolutanga, the Tusker King tells you, "I Oolutanga thank you. It isn't easy to make decisions for the betterment of all tuskers when Oolutanga, who is me, is sleepy."
Oolutanga gurgles the potion down and a wide smile spreads across his face as he smacks his lips noisily. His eyes shine with a renewed brightness.
Oolutanga, the Tusker King tells you, "You who are not Oolutanga have done well for a non-tusker. I will show you how I make other Tuskers smart like me, Oolutanga."
Oolutanga leans forward and hits you on the head with his giant stone hand. You feel a flood of awareness spread from the lump he made.
You've earned 148,617,928 experience.

I did this at Level 238 and had just level'ed. Heard it rumored to be 20% of lvl so best to do it right after a lvl. Again, thx for your site (12 day timer)."

Another note from Caius-Britannicus: "Maggie, I'm sorry for the multiple emails regarding this quest; however, I tried to run a couple of clansmen through it and one of em couldn't use the portal device. Upon some research, he had to be flagged for the Soul Stone quest of the Burun Slayer Weapon. So, If you haven't done Burun Slayer yet, then to be flagged, go to Uziz and run west passed an oasis to 20.8S, 22.5E. Inside the building you will find an "Open Journal". Make sure everyone uses the journal to get flagged. To find the journal is kinda tricky, the entrance to hallway is to the left as you enter the building, under the stairs."
