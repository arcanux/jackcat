Bur Recall!
 Flandamier of LC reports: "Hi Maggie! Baebarri and I went and talked to an NPC in Cragstone named Oswent the Pale (25.7N,49.2E)

He informs us about trouble in Linvak Tukal. Upon arriving we ran up to where Lord Kresouvas was and noticed a lockbox behind where he stands. We used it and it portaled us into this dungeon with a bunch of lugians. We went down the ramp and came to the door which was locked. Baebarri kept the lugians down with her sword while I took down the door with my compound bow. After it went down, we proceeded onward to the next room with more Lugians and a Virindi type creature called a Watcher in the Warren! After killing him, he had a message shard on him which we looted. The re-spawn time seem to be about 5 minutes or so. There was nothing further to investigate as the dungeon ends here. We proceeded back to Oswent in Cragstone and handed him the message shard.

Oswent the Pale tells you, "You found that a Virindi was somehow
connected to Lord Kresovus? This goes a long way to confirming our
suspicions. If you found a message shard on that Virindi, please give
it to me. I do not think I can share any information with you that we
get from the shard, because of the highly sensitive nature of what may
be contained there... But I will reward you for your service all the same."
You give Oswent the Pale Watcher's Message Shard.
Oswent the Pale tells you, "This is exactly the sort of thing I was looking for. I think the presence of the Virindi confirms our suspicions that the Lord Kresovus who resides now in Linvak Tukal is an impostor! But please, keep this discreet. I cannot even share with you what we translate from this shard. Some secrets are too sensitive. But I thank you for your loyal and courageous service."
You've earned 148,617,928 experience.
Oswent the Pale gives you Scroll of Bur Recall.
Oswent the Pale tells you, "You might also find this useful, since you've been to Bur... A scroll to help you recall to that mysterious and intriguing world.

Shard of Harraag's Dagger!

Thanks to Eric for the following details:
Special thanks to: Phaith, Deadlyer, and The Ninja Turtle, killerfenix, shouto man, redbud,darsh, and a little bird for coming with me on the quest.

Element: Ar Fire, slash

To begin talk to Guard Winterborn in Ayan Baqur he tells you the shopkeepers are concerned about the recent banderling sightings and that he wishes for you to help him in his investigation. He will send you to 60.2S, 81.9W to seek out some friendly banderlings he is acquainted with.

Chief Taraash says, "Hmmm... Winterborn sent you. I smell him on you and your friends."
Chief Taraash says, "Perhaps... Perhaps you can help. I trust you. I trust your friends."
Chief Taraash says, "There is Banderling, named Haraag. [sic] He changed recently. He changed a lot. Bigger, stronger, different. He come and try to get Tribes to follow him. Some choose to follow, we chose to fight. We drove him off. Chased him to cave. Then, he ripped a hole in the world and fled through it. Those who chased him through hole, did not return."
Chief Taraash says, "Now, those who choose Haraag come and steal artifacts of the Banderlings. If he get enough, he will use to get others to follow him. I want you all to retrieve those artifacts."
Chief Taraash says, "Near here, at cave to the east, you will find the hole in the world. Like those things you call portals, but worse. I have learned trick to protect you from hole while you travel to its end-place. Take your friends, who are group with you now, and go there. Inside, find Haraag. Retrieve what was stolen from us, and kill Haraag, if you can. Each bring back Artifacts, and I will reward each of you for them."
Chief Taraash says, "Now, go. And remember, if you leave group, you'll need new group and my blessing to enter hole again."

He will instruct you to go east and find a banderling named Harraag. Go east to 60.2S, 80.3W and go in the cavern to find him. The portal will have to be USED, not the walk in type. Once in you will see a portal marked Haggaar's Lair (Levels 120+). Go down the ramp and go all the way around till you reach Carenzis and the Virindis.

After you kill them, you will see 3 ways to go to the NE go down the ramps and out the opposite side to the south after you kill the 3 schisms. Go further up the ramp and to the SE.

Go down the ramp at the intersection which should be west and then go SW and follow the hall east to an intersection. Go east and go fight 3 more schisms. Go north and follow the hallway. Fight the 2-3 then go down the ramp to the south. Fight the enemies to the north through the red mana. Now go NE then go west and hurry through the tunnels, go north through the schisms and go east then.

You should now be at the portal called Harraag's Lair. Be careful. Use AR fire weapons and don't bother with minions. If you die immediately, run back and use the portal. He has 30k hp and is prone to using eye of the storm, the lightning ring spell, and casting astyrrians bane on you. After you kill him loot the sack and crystal [timer = 13 days] and then recall.
