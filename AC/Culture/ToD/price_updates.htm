Bug Reporting

If you feel that Asheron's Call is not behaving the way that it was intended, we certainly want to know about it. Please make sure that you follow all of these steps so that we can quickly identify the problem and report it to our quality assurance team:

Please submit a bug report via our knowledge base by clicking here . When submitting a bug report,

1. Please give a detailed account of the problem. It is important that you explain exactly what happened so that we can try to reproduce the results.
2. If the problem happened ingame, please describe where ingame it occurred (please include coordinates if available).
3. If the problem happened with a quest, please give as many details regarding the quest as possible.
4. Please state the character name, the character's level at the time of the problem, and the world that the character is hosted on.
5. Please indicate what time zone you are in and what time it was when the bug occurred.

Please be advised that although we would like to solve every problem immediately, some issues may take time before being resolved. We do appreciate your patience.

How to get our help on the forums

Considering the volume of posts with little to no information attached, I decided to give you some guidelines for getting help.
  1. Search the Knowledge Base. Please. Pretty please. With everything on top.
  2. Describe the problem completely and exactly. Include all error messages. Completely.
  3. Describe all troubleshooting that you have already done. Including any wacky rituals or smacking the side of your computer.
  4. Post a dxdiag of your computer. This can be obtained by clicking Start->Run, then typing "DXDIAG" (without the quotes). You then can save the information to a text file, and then post it to the forums.
    Also kindly enclose the DXDIAG with a pair of code tags (click the "#" sign in the editor). This makes it much easier to read.

Weekly Update  - 5/30/06 (by Frelorn)

Hello there everyone. I hope you all had a nice weekend, and for those of us in the US, a nice long weekend. Let’s get right into what’s going on this week.

As most of you know already Digital Distribution went live last week. While I won’t give out the numbers, let’s just say that from what I have seen so far, the launch was a big success. So now you may ask… What is next? What is in store for Asheron’s Call now? Well that’s what I am here to tell you all.

In the coming weeks we will be launching an advertising campaign for Asheron’s Call. You will be seeing ads for the game popping up in many places it has never been. When the time comes that these ads are out there and live, I will do my best to point them all out so you can see what we are doing.

Is that it? Not hardly!

We want to prove to you, our loyal players that we are committed to this franchise and its longevity. One of the ways we can do this is improving server performance. So, on June 8th, 2006 we will be doing major hardware maintenance on the Database server. This means that the worlds will be down for an extended period of time. As of right now the downtime is going to be from 4am EST - 6pm EST. We know this is a long time for the worlds to be down, but this maintenance is the first step towards improving performance across all worlds.

What do you mean the first step?

You also may recall that not so long ago we did a hardware upgrade to Darktide. Well now all of the other worlds will be getting this hardware upgrade. We will be doing this upgrade on June 15th, which is also the tentative date we have scheduled for the June event, From the Darkest Depths. We do not expect any complications from this upgrade, since we have done it already, but to be prepared we are going extend the downtime window on that day to 8am EST – 6pm EST. When players log back in on the 15th, the upgrade will be completed.

There you have it. We have alot of things in store for Asheron’s Call in the coming weeks and months, and I will do my best to keep you all informed as to what is happening and when.

Well that’s about it for now. I hope you all have a great week, and see you all in Dereth!
