Majestic Pumpkin Kill Task!

Sergeant Stab of SC reports: "Hey Maggie, I have an update for you for october. There is a new "Majestic Pumpkin" located outside of Glenden Wood (30.7N, 27.8E). If you talk to him, he will give you a kill task to kill 10 Harvest Reapers. If you talk to him again he will task you with killing 3 Pumpkin Lords.

When you kill the Harvest Reapers, you can get one of some random "Hard Rock Candy" that give you a boost in your skills for a short time (kind of like a mini rare). More when I get it ;)"

Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "Do not fear me friend. I am a spirit of the earth and I would have you help me."
A look of deep concern falls over the normal jovial features of the Majestic Pumpkin.
Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "A new threat to my kind has risen from some fouled piece of land. We know them as the Harvest Reapers, tall headless skeletons robed in black and wielding my kin in place of their lost heads. They seek out the growing places and slay my kin wholesale, wasting their spirit by leaving their smashed bodies to rot in the field. The destruction must be stopped if my kin are to fulfill their purpose."
Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "The Harvest Reapers hunt the forests and plains for the growing places. The more dangerous the land the more likely you will see one of these horrors."
Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "Don't fear the Harvest Reapers, they are strong, but I believe you to be stronger."
Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "Return to me when you have killed ten of the unnatural horrors."
Kill 10 Harvest Reapers and return to the Majestic Pumpkin.

Majestic Pumpkin tells you, "You must find more Harvest Reapers. Return to me when you have ensured the safety of Dereth's Pumpkin Patches."

David the Mage Bear of SC adds the following: "Found a Splinter of Wood from a scarecrow, no clue what it does (yet). Got the Scroll of Spectral Flame as a reward for my Harvest Reapers kill task. I also found out that my kill task quota from last October's Pumpkin Lord kill task carried over to this year. I had one killed when the November 2006 event went live. Killed one this year BEFORE I talked to the Majestic Pumpkin, and found out that I only needed to kill one more to complete the task."

Claude Returns!
Dakmor Kavu of HG reports: "Claude the Archmage's New Makeover: Yes, our beloved cloaked friend is back and with a Spook-tacular makeover to boot! He seems to have gone a bit crazy though, welcoming you as a "fellow human", and this change is not missed by his good friend Leopold.

Leopold tells you, "Greetings. I am a member of Claude's pod. It is good to meet you. The environment is pleasing today, agreed..."
Leopold tells you, "Do I seem less exultant than normal? I confront two issues, comrade. Claude has returned, yes, but is now more independent than before. I worry that if the Singularity were to find out, Claude would be reabsorbed or worse."
Leopold tells you, "I also find myself increasingly thinking of myself as separate from the Singularity. How can this be? Do you understand, comrade? How can I have a desire above that of the Singularity? I suppose you might. It is the nature of this place to distance us from the Singularity - to slowly cause us to lose our connection and sanity."
Leopold tells you, "You cannot help me, but perhaps you may function to help Claude."
Leopold tells you, "His consciousness has been closed to me. Please, if you can unlock it and discover the error in his mentality, do so."

In addition to selling all the same goods and services he used to, you may now also purchase a Disturbing Rumor."

Scepter of the Portal Currents!
Thanks to Mustafah ibn-Jubair of FF and Tlosk of HG (posing with scepter)!

There are four new NPC's that give out some Virindi kill tasks. All the Virindi are located in VOD, I would suggest flagging for all of them before you start hunting.

Guard Taziq [Qalaba'r - 74.3S, 19.1E] tells you, "Greetings, brave one. Fate has brought you this way, for Queen Elysa has need of those who would aid her and their homeland. The one known as Aerbax has many servants who trouble the land, and I would seek you aid against those known as Virindi Paradoxes."
Guard Taziq tells you, "If you slay 75 of these strange beings, come to me. You shall be rewarded."

Guard Taziq tells you, "As the hero Yaziq al-Tazar returned triumphant, so do you return to me now. I reward you with this brass banding, which may help in the creation of a new scepter, should you have other appropriate components. May your path continue to be so triumphant."
You've earned 32,000,000 experience.
Guard Taziq gives you Ornate Brass Banding.

Ornate Brass Banding
The brass in this banding is not expensive, yet fine lines form magical runes on its surface.
Fasten this banding onto the Carved Wooden Shaft.

Value

 70
Burden 10
Special Props Attuned, Bonded

Guard Q'alia [Ayan Baqur - 60.0S, 88.0W] tells you, "Queen Elysa set me here to watch for incursions by Aerbax's Virindi...yet she allows the other Virindi, like that thrice-accursed Claude and Leopold, to remain here."
Guard Q'alia tells you, "Allow me to confide a secret. My name is Q'alia bint Hakim. My father was the Archmage Hakim, one of the bravest and strongest of the land."
Guard Q'alia tells you, "That cowled beast killed him, and left the body out to rot all these years...and no one dared raise a finger! Even still they are allowed to hover smugly in their tents, and the only one who will even hear me out is Ulgrim!"
Guard Q'alia tells you, "What I would have of you? Claude and Leopold are too closely watched. However, their kin in the Valley of Death are not. Go there now, and slay 75 Virindi Quidioxes. That will grant a small amount of vengeance for my father."

Guard Q'alia tells you, "It is done! You recognize the danger of the Singularity, and that is wise. Take this scroll - if read over an inert scepter, it may give it power."
You've earned 32,000,000 experience.
Guard Q'alia gives you Barely Comprehensible Magic Scroll.

Barely Comprehensible Magic Scroll
This scroll might do something...you think. It's very difficult to understand, but it implies that it might be usable to activate an item which stores and casts magic.

Guard Alfric tells you, "Poor blighters got themselves tainted by Aerbax, so it's up ta us ta put 'em down. Kill 75 and come back ta me."

Guard Alfric tells you, "Why, ye've done exactly the task I asked o' ye! Well done! Clasp this to ye - this gem may be put on the right kind o' scepter and, in doin' so, attune it ta Candeth Keep itself."
