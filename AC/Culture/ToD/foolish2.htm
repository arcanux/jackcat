New Quest - Jester Quest!
Sergeant Stab of SC reports: Maggie, Just wanted to give you a heads up on the new patch. A new Jester Quest is available in Graveyard.

- Head to Graveyard and go to the bunker with Lady Tarlia Mhoire's chest in it (64.9S, 44.1W).
- Take a right inside the bunker and up the lower set of stairs to find a new dungeon portal (behind a hidden wall)
- Inside the portal is a "Wall" that you must talk to (look for it on your radar).
- Head back outside into graveyard and look for a new NPC named Laylana (65.2S, 44.3W) (see screenshot).
- Talk to her and she will give you two notes, a love letter, and a pleading letter.
- Now you must complete Lord Mohire's arena (graveyard colloseum), which starts by finding an Ornate Bone Key at night in the graveyard,
- Once you defeat Lord Mohire, give the pleading letter to Lord Crynfet Mohire, who spawns in the arena. He will give you a Jester Prison Key.
- Now head back to the original portal you went in (near Lady Tarlia Mohire's bunker), and hand your Jester Prison Key to the door to gain entrance. The key has a 24 hour timer, so you can use it as many times as you need to if you die, but it will expire in 24 hours.
- Inside there are many "statues" that come to live to attack you (blugeon and acid work best)

Maggie, An update to the jester quest... The 2nd time you do this quest on the same character, you will get portaled into a DIFFERENT end boss arena than those who are doing it the first time. The 2nd time through, you will be forced to fight the jester in order to get your pyramid/scarab. This time, you will receive fireballs that you must throw at the jester. The best way to do this is to perch on the platforms in the arena. Buff your TW skill and then, for every fire ball, buff it like a weapon and throw it at him! This is pretty easily soloable if you can perch.

--Sergeant Stab of SC

New Quest - Rytheran's Journal
Sergeant Stab of SC reports: Hey Maggie, I got one more quest write-up that we completed tonight...

- Head to Graveyard and get a "Rytheran's Master Seal" from the "Ancient Chest" in a bunker that is right near the drop (65.6S, 43.9W).
- Head to Nadyssia Castle and run to 74.7N, 18.5E, to find a "Master's Sentinel" pillar.
- Hand your seal to the pillar for entrance.
- Work through the dungeon (linear dungeon) until you get to a big room with a North, East and South exit.`
- Take the South exit until a locked door. Here you will find a "Majrodomo fo the South Cloister". Kill him and loot a key.
- Use key on the locked door and move through the dungeon until you get to a big room. Look for a "South Cloister Key Half" and loot it.

South Cloister Key Half
This is half of the Key to the Lord's Sanctum.
Combine this with the North Cloister Key Half to complete the Key to the Lord's Sanctum.
This item cannot be sold.

Value

 0

Burden

 50

- Run back to the big room with the North and South exits and repeat the same thing on the North side.

North Cloister Key Half
This is half of the Key to the Lord's Sanctum.
Combine this with the South Cloister Key Half to complete the Key to the Lord's Sanctum.
This item cannot be sold.

Value

 0

Burden

 50

- Now you should have two key halves. Combine them to create one key.

 100

- Head to Zaikhal and hand the Journal to "Danira the Dusty" (yellow robe inside the building at the top of the hill to get it translated.
