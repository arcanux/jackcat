New Quest - Well of Blood!

Hey Maggie :) Kalazar of SC here.

I got home and logged in at Ash's place and found a new NPC in blue mage armor, Hoshino Kei. I gave him my 3 quid ingots from last months Hope of the Past quest and he sent me on 3 quests, only one of which I was in the right level req. for.

It was located on dark isle, a portal right behind the deep. Once inside there were Sclavus, Spirits and Remoran. You had to jump on floating and disappearing steps. Above a lava pit. After fighting my way through what seemed thousands of Slithis (the tentacles that harm and shoot martyr hecatombs), I found another difficult jump. You have to jump into a small hole in a wall of fire. Once in you come to a locked door, I don't have lockpick, so I jumped over it, you can also jump around it.

Once in DO NOT USE THE WELL OF BLOOD. It will kill you. :) Instead give it your ingot. I got 197 mil and a purified ingot back. I returned back to Kei and gave him my now purified ingot. He said there's nothing more I can do with it now. I'm guessing next patch :)

You give Well of Blood Quiddity Ingot.
You immerse the ingot within the well of blood. The ingot's glow changes from red to purple.
Well of Blood gives you Purified Quiddity Ingot.
You've earned 197,347,085 experience.

Roots of the Deru Tree!
Thanks to Qorta of VT!

Town Crier tells you, "A new portal has appeared somewhere on the Vesayen Isles. It may have to do with the Deru tree."

At the base of the waterfall in front of the Deru Tree, behind the Sclavus Cathedral (the one with Xi Minru's Chapel portal in it) is the portal Roots of the Deru Tree. No level limit, can be tied, cannot be summoned.

Inside are Giant Phyntos Swarms (Level 110), Miry Moarsmen (Level 110), Muck Glutton (Level 118) and Copper Gromnies (Level 115). Easy fight on the way down, every now and then one or two of the above monsters appears. At the bottom there's a giant swarm of Giant Phyntos Swarms - might cause a bit of trouble for someone who wandered in but not someone 130+ (the kind of people who should go in the dungeon I'll go into that in a second).

There's a Pool of Water at the bottom that restores your health, also you can place the White Ingot in it from the Asheron's Armour quests to make a Purified Ingot. Not exactly sure what to do with these ingots as I didn't actually put one in, but I think you need to give them to Hoshino Kei.

TIP: For finding the Deru Tree and it's portal, take Watcher of the Dead drop in Ayan Baqur then press Enter to get a bird's eye view and head NW looking for a giant tree and an odd structure below it.


New Quest - Resonating Crystals!

Hello Maggie, Today I went out to Southern Osteth when I stumbled across a new monster, a Shroud Cabal Forager Captain. He dropped a new item at well that lead me and my awesome monarch Angeni to a new quest. Here is the write-up I made, hope you can use it.

Southern Resonating Crystal
---------------------------

Find and kill a Shroud Cabal Forager Captain in southern Osteth. I found mine at 60.9S, 29.6E, near the Gredaline Consulate. The captain had 3 or 4 Shroud Cabal Forager next to him. Loot the Forager Captain's Resonator off him.

Go to the Southern Resonating Crystal at 52.7S, 46.7E. Hand the Resonator to the Crystal to get portaled inside a dungeon. Drop seems safe to rebuff. This dungeon contains Shroud Cabal Forager as well as Drudge Ravener. Dungeon is linear, so keep going until you come to the end room. Defeat the Shroud Cabal Ley-Leech (???, forgot to write the name down) and loot the Ley Leech's Medallion. You can loot the text named Ley-Leech's Orders as well, but I found no use for it besides lore. Head to Sawato and hand the Medallion to the Geomancer Nomendar al-Rakh at 28.6S, 59.2E.

You give Nomendar al-Rakh Ley Leech's Medallion.
Nomendar al-Rakh tells you, "Very interesting! This medallion can actually be used to directly siphon mana out of a node between ley lines, if one possesses the geomantic skill necessary... Not useful to one such as yourself, you understand, but I have colleagues who would find this medallion to be valuable and informative. All I can do is thank you with some practical experience..."
You've earned 1,022,688 experience.

XP reward for me was 1,022,688 - that is 5% of the XP from 60 to 61.

Northern Resonating Crystal
---------------------------

Find and kill a Shroud Cabal Raider Captain in northern Osteth. I found mine at 49.15N, 16.17W. The captain had 3 or 4 Shroud Cabal Raider next to him. Loot the Raider Captain's Resonator off him. Go to the Northern Resonating Crystal at 51.5N, 32.6E. Hand the Resonator to the Crystal to get portaled inside a dungeon. Drop seems safe to rebuff. This dungeon contains Shroud Cabal Raider (lvl 115) as well as Telumiat Hollow Minion (lvl 120). Dungeon is linear, so keep going until you come to the end room. Defeat the Shroud Cabal Node-Leech and loot the Node Leech's Medallion. You can loot the text named Node-Leech's Orders as well, but I found no use for it besides lore. Head to Sawato and hand the Medallion to the Geomancer Nomendar al-Rakh at 28.6S, 59.2E.

New Lore - Leech Orders!

Ley Leech Order

Our backers have made available enough resources for you to establish a base in the highlands of southern Osteth. I know you have not been as fully trained as your brother, but you are skilled enough at tracing ley lines and at tapping their energies that you should do fine with this limited task - especially in the wake of the recent rise in power throughout the lines.
Be on the lookout for raiders from the surface. Make sure that only trusted captains carry the resonating crystal that allows entrance through our gateways. Above all, be discreet. We must not let the other factions know that they have a new competitor for the potent nodes of this island. Our backers would be displeased to draw such attention. We already know that Nomendar is in Sawato, seeking adventurers who can bring him our medallions. Nomendar must not have our secrets!

Node Leech Order

To you I am entrusting the task of establishing a base in the comparatively wilder mountains of northern Osteth. You are the most skilled geomancer I have currently available to me, and our patrons are very eager, in their own inscrutable and unemotional way, to have us establish facilities throughout the island and begin drawing and storing the power that runs across Dereth.

I must only caution you to be discreet. I, too, feel the heady and intoxicating surge of ancient, chaotic power that threatens to overflow the ley lines and nodes. I know that the blood sorcerers and the various pawns of the Empyreans all are drawn to the re-awakened nodes and the power that spills forth from them. Temper your own ambitions, lest you overstep yourself and expose yourself to risk. The fool Nomendar al-Rakh has arrived in Sawato, asking after us and the medallions provided to us by our bacters. Do not let your medallion fall into his hands!
