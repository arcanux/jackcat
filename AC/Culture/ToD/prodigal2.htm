New Drudge Fort!
Thanks to Tiny Elephants of LC, Kalazar of SC!

New Drudge Fort - 25.8 N, 46.0E

Tiny Elephant of LC reports: "Inside of the drudge fort are three inner walls, each with a weak spot where you can kill the wall and go through to the next set (watch out for the drudge balloons they're very nasty). At the top of the fort is a cottage with 2 banderlings in it and a drudge in front of it. The drudge is hungry and wants ursian shanks. When you have fed [food at 25.3N, 46.0E], you are then able to loot the fort blue print from a corpse of the one the level 110 drudge biters.  Once inside of the cave, I have no idea. I have been told there is a big boss that is a banderling. Thanks for your awesome site. Have a great patch day."

Jorrdane of MT adds: "To do the drudge fort west of cragstone, you have to bring food to feed the drudge inside the walls. To get inside the walls you run around until you see torches and you attack the walls until they fall. Then you make your way to the center where you give food to the drudge. That flags you to pick up the blueprints. Kill little biter drudges until the blue prints drop, pick them up and run around the back of the cottage and give them to the bandie in the window. He then asks for a shovel. To get the shovel you do the Farmer Kao quest and get the shovel, then you take the shovel to the bandie again in the window. He gives you 27 mill and tells you to wait a week before anything else can happen."

Shamrock of LC sends this addition: "Hi Maggie, I decided to go try my hand again at taking out a drudge balloon. The last one I tried to kill took off on me at half -health. New Quest - The Archivist!
Thanks to sworsman the player of HG for details below:

*you must have completed haraags dagger for this quest*
Restriction - none
Element - fire and slash virindi slayers, acid, frost and shadow slayers

Speak to the Guard Enmio di Cinghalle at 59.6S, 87.7W and he will send you to the Chaos Virindi Cave at 58.6S, 81.5W to investigate the cave there. So head down into the cave and prepare to fight dolls and virindis. Go NW and arrive at a wall. Head south to a wall then west down a ramp. Down the ramp you will start to see devs. Fight through them and then continue SW. Go straight at the maze you will begin to fight virindi and rifts at this point. At the crossroad go north. Fight the devs and marrionettes that appear and continue south into the big room. Kill the three desecrators and the shadows that will come. After that head to the ramp and go down kill the hollow minions and go around the corner and then NE and then into a series of caverns, and then down either ramp. Kill the two marrionettes you find.

Go down the two ramps and then go north down another and kill the devs you find. Go out the opposite tunnel and kill the marrionette and then go south down the ramp. This is a good spot to rebuff. After you come down, prepare for an onslaught of rifts and hollow minions. Once you clear them away, then go east and kill all the hollow minions there. Go NE now and then west then north and west again, and then west into the big room, then NW into a hallway. Go through the hall and kill the devs there. Up the ramp and to the NW, down a ramp kill more devs, then go SE and up ANOTHER RAMP!!! And then down to a room full of virindi quidoxes. Up the the ramp and then down the SE, and then through the big room and then to the NE and into the archive portal.

The Archive - no level restrictions, Virindis

Start by jumping and killing the V's with hit and run, then go west and follow the halls around. Continue straight SE then north, then SE down the ramp. Again hit and run tactics. Kill around so none follow you down. Then if you have a small group imp and vuln from above so you can kill the rest when you go down. BE CAREFUL SMALL GROUPS!! The Archivist is a level 175 virindi. He appears incapable of using war magic and will vuln and cast debuffs and drain, but seems to need his swarms to finish you off. After you defeat him, loot one of the ten shards he drops and get them translated in Zaikhal. After that, return to the guy in Ayan and you will receive xp and the title Royal Investigator from him.

Text: Observations: Harraag
