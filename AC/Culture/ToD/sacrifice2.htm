Blackmoor Helm!
Thanks to Flandamier of LC!

NPC: Bromord Morresten - 12.4S, 42.3E (Yanshi)

Bromord Morresten located at the statue in the newly rebuilt Yanshi (12.4S, 42.3E) will give you a helm for your Kurth's Shard. This shard was obtained from a live event that resulted in Antius Blackmoor's death.

Bromord Morresten tells you, "I am here to stand vigil at the memorial of brave Antius Blackmoor. The Queen has also charged me to reward those who helped slay his murderer, the traitor Kurth. If you have a shard of Kurth's armor or a shard from that loathsome sword he wielded, give it to me and I will reward you."
You give Bromord Morresten Shard of Commander Kurth's Armor.
Bromord Morresten tells you, "I see that you took part in the fight against the traitor Kurth. The Queen thanks you for your service. Wear this helm, that all may know of your deeds."
Bromord Morresten gives you Blackmoor Helm.

New Quest - Viamont Staging Area!
Thanks to Sif al Taak of MT (posing below), Qora's Allzweckmule of SC, Wiccan Witch of DT, Kronikjames of FF!

From Kronikjames of FF: "Thanks for all the hard work on the site. Here is what I know so far on this quest. It is similar to the one last month, 3 different dungeons, 80+ 100+ 130+. We did the 130+. It is a tuff dungeon. Lots of Eaters and Viamonts in it. Viamont Hands and Abhorent Eaters. The top part of the dungeon is a ton of levers to pull to get through it. The reward seems to be XP and also either a Bow or a set of Guants. Ok here is the dungeon layout as I can remember it. Once you get in you can only go straight down. After that it was a blur for me. I know we ended up in a room from the way we came down. Then there were 3 ways in front of us and 1 on each side then 1 on each side above us. The goal is to get either of the doors in front of you open the middle door is not the way down. Once through there is only one way to go: down. Be warned! You will face lots and lots of hands then you will make it to a room with a door lock resistance 9999. There is an Unchained Knight in this room that drops a key with 2 uses. The door in the room and also the last room. As you go down you will see a room with a grate in the floor. In this room you will see a Viamont trapped behind a Mana barrier which is attackable. Once you kill the mana barrier you kill the Viamont behind it as well. It is a level 185 Royal Vivimancer. We seemed to kill both the mana barrier and the Vivimancer fast. Once the Vivamancer is dead the other barrier in the room goes down. Then you just head down to the bottom where there is a room with a bunch of captives in it. This is where u use the key again. Talk to the captives."

--Granite Quarry - 11.8S, 39.0E (Underminer, Underminer Leader)
--Text: Underminer Notes
--Yanshi Lower Undermine - Levels 100+ (Repugnant Eater, Abhorrent Eater)
--Yanshi Upper Undermine - Levels 100+ (Olthoi Swarm Warrior, Olthoi Swarm Mutilator, Yanshi Swarm Matron)

 Royal Guard Adrana tells you, "Ah, you might be just the person to help me. As you can see, Yanshi has been rebuilt, stronger than ever. But I suspect that there are some who are making ready to tear down all that the townsfolk have worked hard to achieve. Please travel to the nearby granite quarry at 11.8S, 39.0E and see if there are any suspicious characters about. Bring me any evidence you can find of plots against the new town."

 Devil reports: "Me and Tao just completed a quest for town of Yanshi. ,talk to npc and head 11.8, 39.0. You head into a mound. Inside are various underminers level 135. Stay left and you'll find bedding and a Underminer Leader. Kill him and you get a note. Return to NPC in Yanshi. Head back to the mound, stay right, and go through a portal. In this dungeon are a bunch of eaters. We stayed left all the way through till next portal. Inside are Warriors and mutliators. Stay left and you bump into a Matron. Kill it and get the claw. Return to NC for a mod coord shield!"

Thanks to Darius of the Nexus - HG!

--Quest Details: Temple of the Stirring Shadow
--NPC: Shade of Farelaith - 74.3N, 58.7E
--Temple of the Stirring Shadow - Levels 80+ (74.3N, 58.7E)
--Text: Our Bitter Winter

Shade of Farelaith tells you, "You... Yes, you could help me, mortal. I need you to slay my younger brothers, who were corrupted into beings of Shadow by our former master Garel. Please, you must enter this Temple of the Stirring Shadow. The temple has three wings, and one of my brothers serves in each wing. They have lost their names and faces to the Shadow corruption, but they still bear their own weapons... I was a mage in my past life, mortal. Help me, and I shall reward you."
Shade of Farelaith tells you, "Enter the South Wing first, and seek out my poor youngest brother, Baranaith. He wielded a spear in life, as he does now in the shadow. Bring me his spear so that I may know his torment is no more."
