New Quest - Prodigal Shadow Child!
Thanks to Tlosk of HG for details below:

Reward: 10% level; Aegis of the Golden Flame Shield; "Prodigal Shadow Slayer" title
Requirements: 140+, Singularity Caul access
Map: Artifex Collegium (temp link)
Map: Orphanage
Map: Orphanage Wing (temp link)
Text: Isin Dule's Orders
Text: Translated Elemental Artifex Shard
Text: Translated Shadow Artifex Shard

1. Everyone needs a Pulsating Bony Lump from the bottom of the Artifex Collegium at 91.9S 93.7W. If you have time, get two, if you die before getting to the final portal you will need another lump.

2. Find a randomly spawning Orphanage portal on Singularity Caul. To get to the bottom requires getting a Pandemic key, start by going east from the drop and pulling the middle of the three levers on the east side. (If you are leading the quest it can save time by getting one or more Pandemic keys ahead of time.) Go SE from the main room and up to the door opened by the lever and pick up a Wretched Key. Go NW from the main room and open the door to get the Depraved Key, back to the SW to get the Abyssal Key, and finally NW again for the Pandemic Key. Go down the SW tunnel and there will four locked doors surrounding a pit, open any one of them using the Pandemic key and jump down three times. At the bottom run north and hook around into a large room, take either the west or the east passages out of the room to go up and south to the room with the exit portal.

3. Be careful to warn your group not to go to the red statue in the north room. The statue you want is black and is in the same room as the exit portal, a Statue of Bael'Zharon, the Hopeslayer. Everyone hands their lump to the statue to be portaled to the Orphanage Wing. Do not try clicking on or using the statue, it will just give 5% vita.

4. From the drop, head north and stick to the left until you have passed through two rooms, then at the next intersection go right. Go straight through two branching 3 way intersections and go left at the Y intersection. Take the next right and you will eventually come to a portal, go right inside. Going through it flags you for two hours of access to the Prodigal Shadow Child's Lair. If you die you can return rapidly by using the statue just north of the drop on Singularity Caul (using the statue after the two hours has expired will summon several high level shadows).

5. On the north side of the room is the prodigal shadow child, Kirienne Palacost, in a protective bubble. Kill each spawn in order to cause two protectors to eventually spawn (A Shadow of Ler Rhan and A Shadow of Black Ferah, along with Black Flames). Once they have been killed the bubble will go away and you can kill Kirienne Palacost. This room is very dangerous with lots of war, dispelling of buffs, hecatombs, etc. Be prepared with lots of items you can turn in to drop vita so you can quickly return to the fight. Once dead, everyone loots a Scrap of Shadow Essence (13 day timer).

6. Clear bodies before respawn and take the Scrap to Riordin Nightbane in Wai Jhou (61.9S 51.4W) for the xp, title, and shield reward.

Note: There are 12 lore items available. The Pulsating Bony Lump and Message to the Shadow Artifex are found in the Artifex Collegium. The Fleshy, Amorphous, Pulsing, Oozing, Throbbing, Sticky, and Shriveled Lumps and the Aerbax's Message, Obsidian and Misty Message Shards are found in a side room of the Orphanage Wing. The lumps can be translated by Bretself in Cragstone (26.0N 47.0E). The shards can be translated by Diyas al-Yat in Zaikhal (13.8N 0.1E).
