Glenden Wood Betrayed!
The Knight of SC reports: "In Glenden Wood where the Scrivener's house used to be there is a corpse there you can select. And on the Scrivener's corpse has a stack of papers on it, says it's gibberish. Give them to Londigul to find out who betrayed the town of Glenden Wood." Thanks also to Dragon Rider of VT!
 

Scrivener's Corpse
The charred remains of one of the scriveners.
Value 0
Burden 3100

You give Londigul Ellic the Armorer Stack of Papers.
Londigul Ellic the Armorer tells you, "These appear to be secret messages to the Scrivener of War Magic. I can't deciper wha they say, but they are definitely Viamontian. I'm afraid she may have helped the Viamonts plan the attack on the town."
Londigul Ellic the Armorer tells you, "Thank you for bringing these to me. I'll have Tomo take a look at them, perhaps she can decipher some of the text. Come talk to me ifyou are interested in helping protect the town."
Londigul Ellic the Armorer tells you, "You've helped me greatly. Return tomorrow and I may have more militia work for you."
