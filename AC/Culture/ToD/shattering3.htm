White Totem Report From VT
Did a little write up after we completed this on VT. Thought I would send it your way in case you wanted to use any of it.

Cheers,
Tanaga

This quest was restricted to level 99 or lower. Not sure if this was the first, but Popa Smurf did get a global. :)

Nicolae (Ashton) really, really had a need to do this. :D Together we plotted the demise of the White Totem. Flagging in AB with Lamorda Loschi, just down the hill from Leopold. Then out through a PotB exit portal to the White Totem location and Ruschk boss spawn, identified by Lamorda. Looting the Chieftan for a flagging item, and then back to AB to give it to Lamorda. (*13* day timer) She offered each a "White Gateway Token". It was then back again, to the White Totem location.

The initial foray was Ashton, Tr and T's Squire, the latter handing the white totem the gateway token, which opened the portal for all. Cannot really speak to the map of the dungeon, 110+ Ruschks throughout, debuffing item/life/maybe even critter. We were doing the ol' right, right, right, when in doubt right approach. It seemed within 30 minutes we were at the final room. The spawn there was insane (for the 3 of us at least). You approach the room from the north, once inside the room there is an incline, at the top is what looks like an altar with a skeleton staked upon it. This is the the target that must be taken out. On the south wall behind the White Totem there is a short dead end hall. Ashton, Tr, and T's Squire found their way to this short hall and started killing. Alas, the re-spawn would start within a second of killing the primary spawn. After a few rounds of killing spawns, Ashton got the fatal end of a Ruschk weapon. The last two held out, clearing two more spawn and then logged out between spawns.

Nic was busy trying to muster a few more willing souls, I had some rl things to do. An hour passes, and Nic and I start a broadcast on /cg. That was when it was clear Moma and Popa Smurf were eager. I was really hoping for a lvl 80-99 bm, but we got a couple of lvl 70 spears. :D I recalled Tr out of the end of the dungeon, so that he could lead the second wave in. (He logged back in inside the dungeon by the way).

Walked Moma and Popa through the flagging routine, with Nic and Tanaga handling the Chieftan - Pop's did some damage of his own. ;) So round two started with Ashton, the lvl 42 wonder-healer, Tr, battle mage for the heavy lifting, and Moma and Popa Smurf, Spear folk extraordinaire.

On the second run, Moma got half way and was the second to succumb to the hate from the inhabitants of that frozen den. Pop recovered the needed items and on we drove.

As we pulled up, just past the lower portal exit, a second was taken to give a briefing of the final room and the plan. This is where Louisville Slugger made his appearance, perfect timing indeed.

We killed the first spawn from the entrance hall, ran to the South hall behind the alter and killed the next spawn. As the third spawn came, with Ashton healing everyone in the party, Pops and Louisville moved directly to the alter to start wearing it down. To get the biggest bang for the buck, we stacked, ala the old caul-cano approach, with Tr burning 5-6 Ruschk at time with ring spells, Ashton healing away, and LSlugger and Pops hacking the White Totem.

Shortly after 10 PM PST tonight, the following rang out:
Popa Smurf has struck the final blow, shattering the White Totem of Grael! The Heart-Render shrieks in a rage as part of his soul has been split!

All grabbed a shard, while fighting the denizens, still unwilling to accept defeat. After all had in their possession a shard, we backtracked to the exit portal - and then to AB.

Turning the Shard into Lamorda Loschi, she exclaimed,
"Excellent! This means that Grael's power has been dealt a serious blow! The shattering of the White Totem temporarily split his avatar. I will get this to our mages, who might be able to work the proper rituals of sympathetic magic to keep Grael's Spirit sundered. You have done the realm of Dereth a great service, friend."

With that, the survivors received the Helm of the White Totem. Again, congratulations to all and a special congratulations to Popa Smurf!

Abyssal Totem Report from DT
Hello Maggie -

I just wanted to update the quest my fellow Blood, Wind of terror II started. With the new arrivals of the tents in Tents in Ayan Baqur, at 59.6S, 88.0W, I noticed that one of the npc's was in fact Hoshar ibn Jalaq, the person that we were looking for earlier for the mysterious totem that appeared on the dark isle after this patch. Well after talking to him, I was able to pick up the ichor and returned it to him witch then he made into a abyssal gate way token. I was then able to hand it to the totem and enter into the dungeon. The dungeon was pretty straightforward -- Turbine's all rights dung basically. And at the end there is a abyssal totem who you have to kill and it drops a shard which you can turn into the helm back in Ayan and a 140 mill xp bonus.

The boss is very easy to kill and is surrounded by hard monsters, but you can snipe him easy with a rend and be done with this in like 5 mins.

I basically solo'ed em down to 10% hp and called in a few guildmates to reap the award.

After, I got a global.

Thank you always and I hope this helps you.
