New Quest - Shadowfire Stone
Thanks to Tlosk of HG, Dakmor Kavu of HG!

Quest Details: Shadowfire Stone
Dungeon: Empyrean Workshop - 66.9N, 15.9E (Levels 125+)

You allow Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist to examine your Repaired Shadow Stone.
Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist tells you, "I've found a way to alter these stones to work with an Isparian Weapon, but I need you to get something for me first. I've managed, in my research, to locate a possible location for one of Atlan's Workshops, where he made the first weapons we based the Isparian Weapons on."
Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist tells you, "The portal should be at 66.9N 15.9E, along the outside of the Mt. Esper Caldera. Go there, and gather for me whatever information you can."

Shadowfire Stone
A fragile black crystal enshrouded in blue flame. It looks like it can be fitted into any Perfect Isparian Weapon.
Use this stone on a Perfect Isparian Weapon to create a Shadowfire Isparian Weapon. *Note: This change is permanent and cannot be undone.
Value 5000
Burden 5
Special Props Attuned, Bonded

New Quest - Plateau Village Transit!
Thanks to Inferno-Mage of TD, Ahlindra of HG, Tulia of MT, Aralcarin of HG, Little Thor of HG, Zegeger of HG, Chatlin and Ninavie of HG, Q Manguy of MT, Kala Bon of TD, Epona Macha of TD!

NPC: Lord Tyrell Marsan - 42.9N, 42.4W
Timer: 13 days

Lord Tyrell Marsan tells you, "You're here to volunteer? Good, good! I could use some volunteers. Now, listen well."
Lord Marsan gestures to the southwest.
Lord Tyrell Marsan tells you, "I've been given orders - by Her Majesty herself, no less - to arrange for safe transit from Plateau Village to Fort Tethana. Her Majesty's going to finally do something about Teth - don't know what, myself. Maybe she's going to attack the renegades, maybe she's going to remind those Fort Tethana vagabonds where their loyalties belong!"
Lord Tyrell Marsan tells you, "It's my job to make sure that the army reaches the Northern Direlands, and to make sure that the troops get their supplies after they've wandered through here."
Lord Tyrell Marsan tells you, "Problem is, some Viamontians have set up a castle seemingly overnight in our path. Spies report that they may have originated from Bluespire. It may be worth your time to check out that report. There's also a long-standing Renegade Tumerok fortress along the way, though their fortifications aren't nearly so frightening."
Lord Tyrell Marsan tells you, "So your job, should you choose to accept it, is to clear a patch for the army by defeating the inhabitants of either fortification - both, if possible. It's often customary, when you win a battle, to take the colors of the other side, so that's how I'd like you to prove that the job's done - bring back the pennants of the opposing force. And, if you should happen to find any intelligence about what the blue bastards are up to, you let me know."

Landmark: Castle Pietrus  - 19.4N 49.5W

Inerno-Mage reports: "Once there you will encounter about 100 level 70-85 mercany/viamont footmen. Once you penetrate their defenses, you approach a villa, like most normal single courtyard villas. There is a book on the ground entitled Captian Vietre Journal or something. Well after I killed him (he looks like the other viamont footmen but he is shorter than them), grab the Pennantt on his body (it has the Viamontian Symbol of the Oxen on it).

Viamontian Pennant
A pennant found upon the corpse of the captain of Pietrus Castle.
Value 0
Burden 50
Special Props Attuned, Bonded

You give Lord Tyrell Marsan Viamontian Pennant.
Lord Tyrell Marsan tells you, "You've beaten the viamontian swine? Good job. We'll begin to mobilize the scout detachment to move through the Direlands, and then once we've got a solid base established, we'll do...whatever needs to be done. Here, take this pennant, bearing the arms of Her Majesty. Display it proudly."
You've earned 288,496 experience. (Level 217)
Lord Tyrell Marsan gives you Strathelar Pennant.

You can use this pennant on roof and yard hooks.

Loot Captain Iacollia Dimari for 2nd pennant. Lore found in one of the towers (must jump to reach)

Quest Details: Prismatic Oil
Text: Instructions for Essence Recovery

Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist tells you, "The four Essences have returned to world. They have been seen near the broken menhir rings that once focused and held them. From what I've been able to surmise from our Arcanum Agents, the Essences are much more aggressive than they once were. Perhaps Gaerlan's tinkering with the elemental forces has changed them."

Find yourself a group of adventurers at least as powerful as you before you go anywhere near these new Essences."

New Dungeon
 Thanks to Larceny of TD, Aircraft Carrier of WE!

Dungeon: Viamontian Laboratory - 39.4N, 75.3W, Levelsl 30+ (Bluespire)
Note: A quest restricted portal to Castle Pietrus (Levelsl 30+) is found inside this dungeon!

Bracelet of Passage
A bracelet looted from the corpse of a Viamontian Mage.
Value 500
Burden 25

Qorta of VT reports: "I go inside the Viamontian Laboratory (30+), expecting to find a load of NPCs and big machines. But instead I find a door with a Mercenary behind it (wasn't Viamontian, Lvl 81). I came to the assumption that it had been taken over. Either way, a level 81 wouldn't do me much harm so I armed myself with the lowest spells I still had, 5s and headed down there.

Bang! Acid stream! Mercenary dead! Now I was faced with three passages: front, left and right. Behind all three was a Viamontian Footmen(sp)(Level 70).
I pulled open the right door, bang, acid stream, corrosive flash, 1 dead.
Center dudes flooded down and tried helplessly to get through a closed door while Mercenary Mages (Level 85, non-Viamontian) showered me with resisted debuffs.

I took out the left one as the extras from the center trailed back to wherever they came from. I pulled open the center and took out the center, two more came, took them out too. I took the left. Then left. Then center. Searching for a Viamontian Portal Mage I saw on the radar (Level 100). Dead. Grabbed a Bracelet of Passage.

Went back to the center, and traveled straight ahead, taking the route round then center when I couldn't go straight ahead. I met with a portal. Castle Pietrus. As I thought, I could not get through the portal without the Bracelet of Passsage. Get ready is my advice."

Thanks to Ashla of SC, Dark Sigma of LC!

Quest Details: Rat Catcher
NPC: Lo Raen - 33.5S, 72.4E
Text: Lo Raen's List of Tasks
Landmark: Rat Cave - 32.9S, 70.5E

Lo Raen tells you, "I require an experienced hunter for the task I have. Let's see what you've hunted..."
Lo Raen tells you, "Excellent, I believe you are ready to help me with my little problem."
Lo Raen tells you, "You see, the rats in town have been getting a bit bolder than we would prefer. It seems they have some sort of leader organizing them and helping them to steal the town's food supplies. It's my job to keep the rats out of town, so I'm too busy to go frolicking about the countryside looking for some leader rat."
Lo Raen tells you, "You must go and find this clever rat and kill it before I loose my job. I've heard rumors that it lives in a cave to the west of town. Go and bring me proof of the clever rat's demise and I will reward you as only a Rat Catcher can."

You give Lo Raen Rat King's Tail.
Lo Raen tells you, "Ah yes, that was a big rat wasn't it? You appear unscathed as well. Excellent work my friend. Now you will see the ancient rat catcher art of rat tail weaving."
Lo Raen deftly weaves the Rat King's tail into a bracelet.
Lo Raen tells you, "Here take this and these as payment for your service to me."
Lo Raen gives you Rat Tail Bracelet.
Lo Raen gives you Rat Reaper Token.
You've earned 500,000 experience.
