Re: Dev Team - 3/6/07 (by Django)

Exploding Arrows in my post did not mean AoE, that's an entire other can of worms. As of right now you might notice that all of our AoE spells orriginate from the caster, at this time we don't have a way to make a spell/skill effect originate from a target creature or location.

About exploding arrows in my first post, here's a praticle example. When you hit a creature a particle effect is emitted, one may think that it's the weapon that causes this to happen, however the particle effect of being hit is actually part of the creature that it knows to set off when it's hit. So for a creature to look like it's been hit by an exploding arrow it would need to have a particle effect that it knows to set off only when hit by a certain type of arrow or bow. It's not all that trivial.
