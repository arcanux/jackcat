Weekly Update 8/21/06 (by Frelorn)

Hello there everyone. I hope you all had a great weekend.

This week we are busy getting everything ready for the Penny Arcade Expo, which begins this upcoming Friday. For those of you who will be attending the event, I wanted to let you know what will be going on. Aside from being able to play a little AC at the event, people will also be able to win all sorts of prize goodness from the many different contests we will have going. We are also going to be having a bit of a party at the Red Lion Hotel on Saturday night. At the party, which is being sponsored by Nvidia, we will be giving away 3 Nvidia 7800 GTX video cards, along with other prizes. That is on top of the drinks, giveaways, trivia, and more. It should be a great night for everyone in attendance. On a personal note I am really looking forward to meeting those of you who are able to make it.

As a result of my being at PAX this year, next weeks update will be late. I will be taking a redeye home on Monday night, so Tuesday is slated for me to sleep a bit. I will of course have a full report and pictures from all the events when I do finally get out of bed.

The team is continuing to work on things for the September event, and things are shaping up nicely so far. This is also the time of year when we begin thinking of the festival season. This is personally one of my favorite things in Asheron’s Call, when the leaves change colors and the costumes come out.

That’s about it for now. We will have updates from PAX as often as time allows for posting so make sure to keep checking in for those updates.

Have a great week and see you all in Dereth!

Re: Penny Arcade Expo 2006 - 8/29/06 (by Frelorn)

I would like to thank everyone who was able to make it to PAX and to the dinner that was held on Sunday night. I had a great time and got to meet alot of great current and hopefully future players.

Alot of the stuff I mentioned there was in concept. The Texas Hold em type game is my little pet project I would like to see in the game, but it is far from done. The pets I was showing off were also still very much concept pets. There is a very fine line that we do not want to cross with interactive pets. We would much rather take our time with this then rush into things.

I will have more on other things that were mentioned over the weekend tomorrow when I get the weekly update out.

Weekly Update 8/31/06 Late Edition (by Frelorn)

I know it is already Thursday, but better late than never.

First off PAX was a great time for everyone. It was great to get a chance to meet alot of present and former Asheron’s Call players, as well as hopefully some new ones. This was my first game related show, and it was definitely not my last. Getting the chance to talk to that many people about the game we all love was something I had been looking forward to for a long time.

So let’s get on with how things went. We had two stations set up so people could either log into some pre- created characters, or they could log into their own accounts if they wanted to, since these stations were connected to the live worlds. During the show there was almost never a time when these stations were not being used by someone, which of course meant that I never got to eat lunch during the two days the show floor was open.

On Sunday after the show was over, I had the chance to sit down with about 30 or so people at the Seattle area AC Dinner. This was truly fun for me, though I was a bit nervous. There is no tougher crowd than a room full of AC players. This is where I started to loose my voice finally after 3 days of talking to everyone who would listen to me about how great Asheron’s Call is. While at the dinner I pretty much gave away everything that wasn’t nailed down. Including shirts and some signed game boxes as well as a nice little silver Lugian I found hanging out in the office. Everyone there made sure he went to a good home.

Alot of people have asked me to confirm what was said at the show and over the weekend so I will no try to remember everything that was said.

Of course the big feature of the weekend was the Pets demo that I was showing off. The two particular pets being shown were the healing Penguin and the dispelling Olthoi. Both of these pets were in there just to show some of the things we might be able to do going forward with this new ability. Right now the pets just cast randomly, but we are working on trying to get things more controlled. We also announced the four pets that will be available upon release: Drudge, Armoredillo, Thrungus, and of course the Ursuin. Players will be able to own all 4 pets but they will only be able to have one out at a time. These little pets will be non- combat, and they will in no way be able to block doorways or other entrances. For now they will be cute and cuddly companions for everyone to have if they so choose. But who knows what else we might be able to do with them going forward.

We also discussed something that can sometimes be a sensitive subject around here, advertising. We announced that the premier issue of a new MMO magazine, Massive Magazine, will feature Asheron’s Call on the back cover with a special offer for new or returning players. We also announced that the online ads will also be appearing soon to help continue to drive up the population of Asheron’s Call, which has been steadily rising for the last several months now. All of us here are very excited about what the future has in store for Asheron’s Call.

There were a couple of other smaller things I let out there, but I figured I would save those for next week’s update since this one is coming out so late.

We will hopefully have some pictures of all the fun posted as soon as I sort though all the photos that we took. All in all it was a great weekend for everyone involved.

That’s about it for now, everyone have a great weekend and for those of you in the US enjoy the long holiday weekend. I know I will.

See you all in Dereth!

Weekly Update 9/5/06 (by Frelorn)

Hello there everyone I hope you all had a great weekend. It was a nice long weekend for most of us here, so we are all well rested.

This week we are in the final stages of testing for the September event, Come What Follows, which is scheduled to go live on Tuesday September 12th. We are looking forward to getting everyone’s reactions to some of the new features going into the game this month.

One of the key things going in for the September event is Uninscription Stones. For a fee players will be able to purchase these stones, which when used on an item with an inscription, will clear that inscription off of that item. These stones will only work with loot items. If you have a quest item that was inscribed by another player, the stone will not be able to remove the inscription. The stone also does not work on unique game inscribed items such as Pack Cows and Ulgrim’s Gromnies. This is another item we thought was previously not possible. Once again the team has gone above and beyond in order to make things work that were previously thought impossible.

We will also be revisiting a couple of older quest items that could use some attention. I won’t say which ones, but we think alot of people will be happy to see these particular items getting some love.

I also wanted to take this opportunity to explain the mix up with some live events this weekend. Basically for some strange reason the person who was going to set off the events found himself unable to create the things needed to start the events. We are still trying to figure out why the permissions he needed were not there for him. As far as we can tell it was just a mix up on what game accounts should be allowed to do what. We apologize to those who saw the global announcements only to find no event.

Before I forget to ask… In the next couple of months we are going to have the opportunity to go back and do some “clean up” on some older quests, but we need your help. If everyone could give us their top 10 quests they would like to see us go back over, we will take all of the feedback and go check those quests out again. So feel free to reply here with your top 10 quests you would like to see us revisit. We can’t promise that every quest that players list here will be redone, but we want to make sure that we are hitting places that you the players want us to.

One last note and this is to make sure everyone is paying attention. :P On this upcoming Thursday September 7th, you may want to make sure you are in game between the hours of 7pm EDT- 10pm EDT. I suspect it will be an interesting night for everyone involved.

That’s about it for now. Everyone have a great week and see you all in Dereth!
