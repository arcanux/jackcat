From the Throne of Destiny Expansion.

Rare Items: Frankenstein Lives

Following is the list of rares that have been discovered in the Throne of Destiny expansion and the expansion preview! When you kill a monster that is carrying a rare, you'll receive a local broadcast. To find a rare, the creature you kill must be of a higher level than you. However, if you are over level 100, any creature level 100+ may spawn a rare for you. Rares also ignore loot sharing in fellowship. Rares always drop on death and require the ToD expansion.
