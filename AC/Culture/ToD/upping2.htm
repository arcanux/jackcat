New Quest - Blightfinger and the Club of Surprising Cunning!

Kelsten of HG reports: "Heya Maggie ... When my husband Richard Grieco and I logged in to start seeing what was new this patch ... we found this Mosswart Merwart Mundagurg in Ayan at 59.7S 88.2W and when we spoke to him, he sent us North to the beach to find a hole in the ground ... and find Blightfinger. The dungeon was pretty easy to navigate and we finally reached the bottom and Blightfinger. He was really easy for us to kill. We looted his hand and went back to Ayan to turn in. We received a Mace called the Club of Surprising Cunning and at level 232 I received 13,765,377xp. The club was of no use to me, so I handed back to him and received another 3,441,334xp. We love your site!"

Thanks also to Falcoz of Yalain - FF, David the Mage Bear of SC!

Kill their leader, the biggest and stinkiest one of them all! Bring me hand of Blightfinger, and I give you reward!

New Quest - Ghostly Messenger!

Olthoi Master of LC reports: "Hi Maggie, I'm Olthoi Master from Leafcull. Upon entering Bur with my fellow, we went on the new quest activated by a Burun NPC called Kaurik. We went through to the statue's corridor used to enter the burun weapons quest and found the necessary statue. The whole point of the quest is to hunt down the Ghost Messenger and make him go into a seyance circle on the top floor of the dungeon. Once he is in there, he will be killable. When he is destroyed, he drops one Messenger Missive that can be handed into Kaurik for XP and the title of Honorary Burun Scout. I've included a screenshot of the reward and one of the title and how much xp I got. I'd like to also include fellow people in this find too: Marombi Feuler, Ultimis', Og Angel, Blue Laser and Elindil The Wicked. We all worked as a great team to get this quest done and it is only fair they also get most of the credit for this find too :)."

Marombi Feuler of LC reports: "Hey Maggie, I just did this new Bur quest. One of my fellows, Olthoi Master, sent you some info. Here's some more. Have to talk to Ghost Messenger NPC on all the levels quickly to get him to spawn on the top in a circle. He is level 191 and drops ONE note when he dies: Ghostly Messenger's Missive, which is on a 6 day timer."

Text: Ghostly Messenger's Missive

Vision of Truth on FF sends the following: "Hi Maggie thanks for providing this site…. I wanted to add a few things for this quest and correct something that Olthoi Master and his group submitted not that their information wasn’t helpful.
You first start off the quest of course in Bur. In case people don’t know you can flag for Bur from the Cold Tracks page (+80 search for asheron or <80 Dagger of Tikola!) on your site and they can get recall to Bur from the saving Asheron quest I believe. Once in Bur go to the portal and then find Kaurik to start the quest.

Kaurik tells you, "Greetings, human. You are just in time, if you can help."
Kaurik tells you, "I am Kaurik - I follow Tmauruk's commands in all things. He is clever and quick, much more so than most of us."
Kaurik tells you, "He has asked me to ask help of humans who come by in an important task."
Kaurik tells you, "Recently, a ghost appeared at the spot that your portal sends people when they come here from your Dereth. As we scouts drew closer, it fled quickly away. It moved too quickly for us to catch it, but it sealed itself into one of the many ways hidden in the Upper Catacomb."
Kaurik tells you, "Tmauruk thinks that it is a messenger, going to see the Falatacot which live in the catacombs. Anything which helps them is bad for us, so we want to catch the messenger, see what it plans."
Kaurik tells you, "I will show you how to use the statue so that you may gain access to the ghost's hiding place. Help us - bring us the message - and we will hail you accordingly. You also help your own people - the Falatacot do not love the humans who have defeated them and freed their prisoner."

Now when Olthoi Masters group says “We went through to the statue's corridor used to enter the burun weapons quest and found the necessary statue.” That part threw me for a loop as to where to go. To be more specific go to the Southern Catacombs, find the rock from the previous quest from Tmauruk to find the upper catacombs that Tmauruk tells you to find. Then Go to the North wall turn to the right and go down four or five doors on the left to find the right statue.

This is the statue that Kaurik told you about. You activate it and feel yourself teleporting to the cavern where the ghost messengers tend to run away to.


Once in you will find lots of bad critters and you will want a group of at least 3 for this, meats and chugs ect… A lot of stuff on you and tons of vulns/fire traps. You will find a NPC critter in there named Tikreek.

Tikreek tells you, "Juruuka. The messenger is in here somewhere, but it teleports away too quickly for us. You will need to hunt it down and catch it carefully. Be aware that this place is heavily trapped - I am not sure what is further in that they feel the need to guard so closely. Of course, what they don't know is that I know a bit of the art of the shaman, and I've trapped their summoning chamber. If you encounter the messenger there, it will be trapped and have to fight you. Good hunting."

This basically just a clue… In the dungeon you have a lower level, middle level, upper level and the top circle where the messenger finally spawns after you click on his npc in the dungeon like 7 or 8 times. In our group he spawned mostly random on the lower level and middle level for the first 6 times he spawned as a NPC. Then the last two seemed to be on the top level then he was spawn at the top circle. While you are clicking on him you get the message:

The ghost spies Cyphers Archer and shrieks, vanishing as it does. You surmise that its powers are not great enough to teleport it outside of this complex - it's still here somewhere...

Until you get the last one and he spawns in the top circle. Navigating the dungeon is a pain until you get it down from level to level just know that going up or down is never on the perimeter but rather amongst all the pillars. When you finally kill the messenger he drops a note on the ground and the chasing down the messenger will have to be repeated for everyone in the group to kill him over and over for everyone to get a note. Once you have the note recall back to hand in the Ghostly Messenger's Missive.

You give Kaurik Ghostly Messenger's Missive.
You've earned 164,455,904 experience.
You have received the title Honorary Burun Scout!
Kaurik tells you, "Hail, speedy one! You have deprived the Falatacot on Dereth of the advice they need. This will hopefully cause confusion in their ranks, and who knows what the result will be. I shall watch for more messengers - return in a short time to help us again."

I got help from Hoggy and Cyphers Archer so they definitely helped get things figured out. This quest would be a pain to do solo but possible.
