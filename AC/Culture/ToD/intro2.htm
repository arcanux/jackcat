New Factions!
Count Monte Cristo of LC reports: "Hi Maggie, Count Monte Cristo here. I've just recently done one of the new quests which are located on the outskirts of the graveyard and I've included 3 of the tomes that you get from the quests. It looks to me as if there will be three new factions to join during this current story Arc: The Eldrytch Web, The Celestial Hand and The Radiant Blood. Each each of the factions has their own tomes telling you that each faction has their own agenda. These tomes are Canon of the Eldrytch Web, Principles of the Celestial Hand and Tenets of The Radiant Blood. Each faction also has their own leader. Two of these leaders are very well known in the AC lore; Nuhmudira is leader of the Radiant Blood and Ben Ten leads The Celestial Hand. The leader of the Eldrytch Web is currently unknown since their leader wasn't named in their tome. I have included the texts of the three tomes from each of the three new factions. I hope some people will get some joy while reading them, as I have when I read them :).

Hey Maggie, Kalazar of SC here.

First I talked with Jondor Torgren of Zaikhal; he's in a blue robe to the left of the scroll shop. He sent me to the Graveyard to complete 3 tasks for 3 people, to the N, E, and W, so off I went. I went down into the north pit, and killed the large rat who dropped a key, and then opened the chest. Inside was a sceptre that I turned in for a letter to one of the new 3 society fellows in Ayan. Return to Jondor Torgren of Zaikhal and give him the 3 books for some more experience.

New Quest - Moarsman Temple Tou Tou!
Yan Cancook of LC reports: "Hiya Maggie, I visited Tou Tou today and on my way along the coast (I enjoy the scenic route) I came across one of the new Moarsman Temples that Release Notes hinted were out there [28.9S, 92.6E]. It was neat, and it was full of an incredible amount of new Tainted Moarsmen. I ran right to Tou-Tou to get lifestoned because I anticipated some difficult fights. They weren't high level compared to most people, but to a level 29 Adventurer they could have been trouble.

Sure enough, I found a soldier named Guard Taizo who sent me on my way to clear everyone out.

Guard Taizo tells you, "Down on the beach, hostile moarsmen have created their own little fort."
Guard Taizo tells you, "If you would go and destroy the inhabitants, it would help the town greatly."

It was a long fight going it alone. I had to take many breaks because, while they didn't pose much of a threat, dodging dozens of Moarsmen really wears you out fast. There were many of them, beyond count. Rough math puts it at... 14 to 20 outside, and then numerous groups of three inside. Four groups on the upper level and then nine or ten on the bottom making it about 24. It took me a very long time, but I was never surprised by any unfortunate respawns to set me back.

Moarsmen Temple Ayan!
Hi Maggie. A couple of days after the patch, but here's the fort at Ayan pretty much as shown in the teaser pic.

I found a guard (59.8S, 88.2W) staring out towards the coast and thought he was new so had a chat and he asked me to ... well, bring back anything of note, as in the Academy quest. He's not quite facing the fort, but I spotted it through the trees. Boonwillow was there before me, hunting them for Moarsmuck for brewing. In Dereth, being besieged by the creature from the black lagoon and its thousand cousins just means beer!

Guard Bey tells you, "Would you like to do us a favor, sir? There is a fortress of moarsmen down near the festival stone. I think they may potentially pose a threat to the town, unless we strike first. If you find anything of interest there, come back and give it to me."

The fort's incredibly simple (60.9S, 89.8W), a shape that'll look familiar to Blackmire and Burun King run fans inside an incomplete ring of crystals. Inside there's a set of steps down from the far side of the room. The spawn numbers are huge (see radar in screenshots), and an occasional debuff comes in from randoms outside. The things themselves aren't too bad, and they're worth 44,500 or so each. Once they've all been burned up, a cluster of chests spawns in the alcove at the top of the steps. Each once contains a tainted egg. I forget the timer but you can see the message from when I tried to grab another. The egg goes back to the guard for an xp reward, and that's that.
