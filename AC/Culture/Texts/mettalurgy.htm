Tome of Mettalurgy and Crafting of Shadow Armors
This massive tome, written by Master Armorer Jibril ibn Rashid, is a complex set of directions for the crafting of Shadow Armors. Written in the front cover is a note to the Viamontian Master Armorer, Iian di Alduressa
This item cannot be sold.

Value

 1000
Burden 200
Pages 2 of 2 pages full.
Special Props Attuned, Bonded

Greetings to you,

This tome is a copy of the combined notes on the creation of the Shadow Armors, as researched by myself, Alean the Steel Forger, and Koga Hideki. Hopefully, the knowledge here will be of assistance to you in the creation of Shadow Armor in the manner of your people. To use this knowledge, you will need samples of the Crystal Shards and the Shadow Shards used in this process. Seek those from Alean, North of Eastham, and Koga, by the lake of Kara.

For this knowledge, all I ask of you are these two things: First, as you experiment with the information in this tome and learn, that you share your findings with myself and my compatriots. Second, that you do not deny those seeking your craft the creations you make with this knowledge.

By sending you this tome without speaking to you personally, I am taking on trust that that you will honor these humble requests. Hopefully, we can find the time to meet in person in the near future.

-Jibril ibn Rashid
