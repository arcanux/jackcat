A Letter to Jibril ibn Rashid
A note, written by Iian di Alduressa, seeking the aid of Master Armorer Jibril ibn Rashid in the creation process of Alduressa Shadow Armor.
This item cannot be sold.

Value

 0
Burden 10
Pages 1 of 1 pages full.
Special Props Attuned, Bonded

Greeting to you, Master Jibril,

My name is Iian di Alduressa, son of Count Alain di Alduressa. Once, before we all came to Dereth, my father shared his knowledge of the smithing of armors with the Masters of your people. Now, I seek to do the same with you.

On this world, you and your peers create a special armor, reportedly crafted using an alloy made from the shards of animate crystals and living shadows. I humbly ask for your assistance in learning this process. Long have the Gharu'ndim prized the pursuit of knowledge and the value of teaching. I ask now only for the chance to learn.

As to any concerns you would rightfully have about my involvement and loyalties in the war between our peoples, rest assured that I will have no part in it. I seek only to advance our mutual craft.

-Iian di Alduressa
