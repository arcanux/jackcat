Rumors

 Posted on the Turbine site during the Waking From the Abyss event (Dec. 20, 2003).
 
Vines of iridescent azure energy arc through roiling violet clouds; within the tumult, time ceases. There is energy, and nothing more. This is the succor of the Quiddity. Distance is irrelevant; all are connected. Thought is a misnomer. There is no thought. There is all.

“Why now? Why comes a being unique unto the Singularity, born of the Quiddity, categorizing the energy of the collective and restructuring the order?”

Quintessence contaminated; extrication and sanitization imminent.

There is no thought; it is a misnomer.

Energy arcs along a fissure of erupting amethyst clouds. The undulation signifies failure. A pattern not indicative or decipherable passes in its wake.

Anomalous discharge of charged particles sequestered for analysis.

A cobalt bolt of kinetic possibility passes through a billowing burst of lavender, a portent of unscrupulous behavioral patterns of representatives currently abroad. Intelligence is acquired and redistributed as collective understanding. Only information that influences possible agents, in a positive comportment, is allowed to pass into the Quiddity.

Re-allocation of deviants continues with success.

Exact information is shielded from the collective. No emotion.

Meatling identified as Nuhmudira has forsaken accord.

Entity Aerbax’s presence ceases its transmutation; whereabouts unknown.

Heightened presence of anarchic anomalous beings intensifies at previous incursion point.

Secondary “being” asynchronous to Aerbax’s signature assumes direction of forces.

Intentions of secondary “being” impossible to ascertain; overseers accountable for study of “being,” virtually consumed by madness, advise.

Sapphire vines lick the violet clouds and the collective responds. The Singularity continues to pulse, ebbs of energy streaking through a latticework of incessantly undulating clouds.

Abandon incursion point. Maintain aggressive posture during withdrawal.

Redirect efforts to re-collect errant servitors to eradication of fleshlings.

There is no thought. There is no emotion. There is no individuality. There is control.
