 The Culture section has information on the three Heritage groups of Dereth: the Aluvians, the Gharu'ndim and the Sho. Each heritage section contains information on the groups' history and factions, and includes several stories which illustrate some of their unique culture.

The Ispar History contains links to Nayt al-Yad's exemplary work on the people of Ispar.

The Auberean History section contains information on the unfolding story in Asheron's Call, in Dereth, the Vesayen Isles and on Marae Lassel. You'll find a full listing of the monthly events, with teaser information, screenshots and more!

The Text and Parchments sections contain a compilation of the rich lore and many texts and notes in the game.
