Note scrawled in blood
Use this item to read it or write in it.

Value

 90
Burden 160
Pages 2 of 2 pages full.

I had never expected there to be so much blood. It seeps everywhere now that I have finally killed the man. It stains everything that I touch and has even begun to seep into my armor. Everything is washed red. There is no escape from the screams and the look on his face. I feared, very briefly, that I would lose my mind. Thankfully my greater sense prevailed and I was able to gain control before I slipped into a catatonic madness. Read well my words you who have trespassed here: This blood will seek all who steal from it, though I am protected.

Think before you take what is in this place it will mark you and curse you.
