Rumor of an Artifact
Rumor of an Empyrean Artifact.
Value 10
Burden 25
Pages 1 of 1 pages full.


A friend of mine came across a man in a small cottage northwest of the West Lytelthorpe Outpost. He said his name is Slithe Tradittor and claims to be an Explorer for the Explorer Society. He expounded upon a tale of discovering and unusual locale which harbors a strange device. This device is apparently an artifact of Empyrean technology. This Explorer hungers to obtain this device and study it.
