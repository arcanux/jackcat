Instructions for Essence Recovery

Instructions on procuring samples from the four Essences.

Value

 10
Burden 5
Pages 1 of 1 pages full.


Instructions:
Travel to the Arcanum Research Facility outside of Al-Jalima and collect four Enchanted Decanters.

Then travel to Old Yanshi and gain access to the Tunnels of the Harbinger by talking to the two creatures that control the portals. Our Agents report a series of Essence Chambers that the Harbinger has trapped the Essences in. Find these chambers and destroy the Essences.

Use the decanter on their Crystallized Essence remains to obtain the smaples I require. Return to me with all four of the samples and I will reward you for your assitance.

Ciandra, Arcanum Alchemist
