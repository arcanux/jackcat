Beings who graced this land prior created a machine which may make our maddened kin maddened no more. Return this machine to me and you shall have Fiun gratitude never-ending.

Pieces of the machine may be found at these locations.

Small Empyrean Vault is located at 90.9N, 43.5W.

Hidden City is located at 94.4N, 70.0W.

Should you happen across artifacts which puzzle your mind, bring them to me. You must help, friend. The Slayer has hurt us much.

-Fiun Scorus
