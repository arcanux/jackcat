DWhen I award you with XP, I'm not just setting your level to a certain value. I'm actually just giving you a bundle of XP. Each token tells you how much XP I will give you in exchange for that token. I assume that you are a fresh-faced level 5 straight out of the Academy. The amount of XP I give you is based around that assumption. If you are higher than level 5 when you give me your token, then I am still going to give you the same amount of XP as if you were level 5.

-Turbine Emissary
