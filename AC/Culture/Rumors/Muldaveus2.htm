Last Known Location of Muldaveus
Value 10
Burden 100
Pages 1 of 1 pages full.
Special Props Attuned, Bonded


The reconstructed journal of Muldaveus has given us a clue to his last known location. It appears that he was recently exploring an icy dungeon on the Haleatan Islands.

The dungeon can be found at 89.5N, 56.3W on the Isle of Ruin.
 

See the Hunt for Muldaveus quest write-up for details!
