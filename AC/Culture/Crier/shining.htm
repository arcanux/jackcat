Town Crier tells you, "Another beautiful spring! And all of the animals are having cute little babies."

Town Crier tells you, "So have you heard about these Runed Weapons? Very fancy! I hear tell they were commissioned by Varicci's forces long ago, but they were looted from the royal vaults!"

Town Crier tells you, "I've heard that the White Rabbit has a son. Imagine if you will all of the rabbits of this world being as dangerous as the White Rabbit. You know what you have to do."

Town Crier tells you, "Adventurers have reported that they've found Gems that will open a gateway to a nest of Mukkir, whatever they are."

Town Crier tells you, "If you have loyally served Queen Elysa and the rebels of Silyun, and have already helped the Whispering Blade, you may want to consider going to Ayan Baqur to speak with a man named Harkwull."

Viamontian Rumors

Town Crier tells you, "Those Runed Weapons? I have nothing to say, except that I strongly urge you to recover some of them. Seek them out in chests of treasure..."

Town Crier tells you, "There have been reports of adventurers finding their way into a nest of Mukkir. This should be investigated."

Town Crier tells you, "Even you, thin-blooded outlander worm, can aspire to serve the Rossu Morta if you prove your loyalty. If you are one of those Bloodless enlightened enough to have previously earned their trust, seek out the local Experimental Thaumaturgist in Sanamar."

Town Crier tells you, "I have heard reports of variants of Alduressa Armor being made out of alloyed crystal. Is that even possible?"

Town Crier tells you, "King Varicci has asked all loyal Viamontians to aid the Bloodless alchemist Ciandra in studying the return of the Essences. The Essences are dangerous. On this subject we and the Bloodles are in agreement."

Town Crier tells you, "Yes, the air is fresh and the trees are green. If you attempt to give me a flower I will cut you down where you stand."

Town Crier tells you, "Rumor has it that the Chaotic Virindi are looking for some ancient power source. Kill all Virindi you see in order to insure they do not obtain it."

Town Crier tells you, "I heard from another Crier that the drunkard of Ayan Baqur is looking for the same power source the Chaotic Virindi seek. I would be amused to see him blow himself up."

Town Crier tells you, "If the White Rabbit's children were to survive I fear that a new dominant species would sweep across the land."

Town Crier tells you, "I have heard reports of an Alduressa armorer creating armor using gems crafted of crystal and shadow. The reports place this individual near the Knights of Karlun fort, near Silyun. This should be investigated as quickly as possible."

Town Crier tells you, "Runed Weapons! Masterfully constructed weapons, commissioned by our own King from Silveran smiths long ago and brought at great expense to this land... Stolen by what I can only assume are Bloodless thieves and scattered to the four winds... What perversity!"

Town Crier tells you, "There have been reports of adventurers collecting gems that transport them and their fellows to a nest of Mukkir. If these reports are true, it could be of great use to those who have been researcing these strange creatures."

Town Crier tells you, "I can tell just by the smell of you that you are a Bloodless weakling, but if you have managed to choose the correct side in this war, and have earned the trust of the Grand Knight and the Ordina Rossu Morta, you should report to Melaverre of Sanamar to make yourself useful."

Town Crier tells you, "After the loss of life in the North Dires, our King is taking more active involvement in any strange events in the world. For instance, he has his own alchemists attempting to uncover the secrets of the Essences. For now King Varicci urges those with strength enough to go forth and impede the Essences' progress."
