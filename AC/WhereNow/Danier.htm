Where Are They Now?

Danier - Level 73

Well, he's as handsome as ever! I caught up with Danier in Glendon Wood, where he and his monarchy gather to mule and socialize and coordinate quests and so forth. Danier posed for me in his Greater Celdon Shadow Hunter Armor for the picture above. He's now an impressive Level 73 with hundreds of followers. His stats have even landed him on the Top 10 Zone list for Focus on the Thistledown server.

I've had plenty of opportunity to see his amazing Magic Skills in action. Danier's group always seems to have some event going on, and they've been kind enough to extend an open invite to me for their questing adventures! I may not have ever made it to the Black Death Catacombs with him, but I've been to Artifex and Amperehelion Vaults! (Visit the Major Sparking Stone quest to see more pics of Danier in action!)

Danier's usual wardrobe when mage-ing is a buffed robe. He wore the white one above when we went for the Major Sparking Stone. He wore the pink one (pictured below) on our trip to the Fire Stone Vault. And when I run into him in Glendon Wood, I usually find him relaxing in the casual tangerine outfit pictured above. I must say that Danier carries these simple outfits off with style. The White Viridi Wand is a perfect accent! 

Occasionally, Danier will send me a screenshot that has a picture of his inventory open. I've collected a few views of various packs. The dye pack belongs to Sugardaddy, his "sad mule of doom."

I asked him why he called him that. Danier told me that Sugardaddy was on the Top 10 lists for both Cooking and Alchemy, but he only has 6 health! One day, his followers somehow let a rabbit into the mule house! Havoc ensued! 

Sugardaddy has helped me in the past to keep the Cooking Section up to date. I love it when new recipes become available because it gives me an excuse to hang out with him talking about food. If I'm really crafty, I can manage to wheedle some Green Tea Ice Cream out of him!

As much fun as I've had with Danier and his gang, I am most grateful to him for his Loyalty skill. His goading of my lovely vassal has given me more experience than I can even absorb! Ned Cleversun is just points away from Danier's stat. Danier has even offered to dump a few more points into loyalty, just to keep Ned on his toes! ;-) (Of course I declined.)

I'd say that they most apparent characteristic of Danier's Monarchy is that they really enjoy the game. They seem to spend the bulk of their time having fun actually playing! Whenever I stop by GW, there's always someone -- Peanut or Doogen, Bayud or Dacia gearing up for more fun! Take a look at some of the pics of their adventures! There's even one of Danier killing the White Rabbit!! (ok, ok, it was before the great buffing of the bunny. :) 

Thanks for sharing your adventures with my vassal and me! And thanks for letting us catch up on Where You Are Now! We'll see you when you hit 100! ;)
