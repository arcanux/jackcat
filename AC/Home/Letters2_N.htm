New Stuff

 Thanks to everyone who helped me celebrate my wonderful vassal's achievement of reaching level 80! A lot of Tuskers sacrificed their lives to make this event a success. As I state on my home page, the site wouldn't even exist if it weren't for my vassal, Ned Cleversun. From some of the strong feelings expressed to me in your letters, I am more aware than ever of how much you enjoy Maggie the Jackcat's Kick-Ass Fabulous AC Web Site. I'm so pleased that I was able to give you all the opportunity to get to know the man who helped make it all happen just a little bit better.

Below are some of the letters I received from my regular visitors: the amused, the confused, the angry, the indignant. As more came in each day, patterns began to emerge. I've separated a sampling of them out into different types. I left out a couple of categories: the begging, the crying, the pleading. And actually, I was just a little bit disappointed that there weren't more attempts at outright bribery! ;-) I couldn't possibly publish all the letters I received, but truly, I enjoyed them all. Even the ones that were so surly they could only have been written by (yes, you guessed it) that dastardly "Anonymous." And if I seem to be poking a little fun at some of you by publishing a few of these, please remember: it doesn't mean that I don't love you!
