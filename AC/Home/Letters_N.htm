April 30, 2002 - A Report From the Field

Hi Maggie! Just a quick hello and an interim report on how things are going.

The responses to the web site that I've received in-game, from friends and strangers alike, has been overwhelmingly positive! Everyone is excited about me and wants to know more.

Several people have realized that it won't be easy to play the game without the fine information you provided, but they are determined to do the best they can!

As one warrior said, "When Maggie's site was up we all benefited and now -- we all face the same challenge, ignorant and confused together." It's kind of sweet when you think of it that way.

Amen. So, thanks again for the very nice tribute you created for me! I will send new pics when I get a chance. I understand that the area above OP BSD is pretty exciting. There are tuskers.

Ned Cleversun
Thistledown
Level 80 archer/mage


Hello, my wonderful vassal -
I'm excited to hear all the positive feedback. I've been inundated with mail as well. Hundreds and hundreds of letters, all about YOU! Ned this, Ned that, Ned, Ned, Ned. You're all they can talk about. It's certainly been an exciting few days!
