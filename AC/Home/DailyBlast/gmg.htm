Green Mire Grave - Nov. 15, 1999
Hi Maggie,  Here's a screenshot taken on Nov 15, 1999, almost exactly 5 years ago (my birthday too!). The Green Mire Yoroi Cuirass was the hot item to have, with its incredibly uber AL of 108! Five of us camped the dungeon for the better part of an evening to get ours. In the center of the group is my first character, Olias of Sunhillow, who became a mule shortly after this (he was an Aluvian with UA and no healing!) and remained so for more than 4 years, until I dusted him off, redid his skills and stats, and made him into an uber swordsman. But on this day, he truly was king of the world!

Confucius of FF
