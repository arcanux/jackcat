Dear Turbine and Microsoft -- 
About a year ago I sat on a high cliff-edge by the sea, with my companion and Monarch Maggie the Jackcat, and watched a comet descending, as we waited for the world (of beta) to end. It was sad, but Turbine promised that the best was yet to come.

And it's been a good year in Dereth. A lot of tough fighting, hard traveling and trial-and-error. Good pain, good loot, good times!

Turbine and Microsoft have created a game that's like any good world anywhere -- it never ends, never stops, never grows stale.

And, soon, soon, my strongest wish will be granted. Just today I pre-paid the armorer in Ayan Baqur, Sang Nen-Kai, for an "extra commodious" Specialty Codpiece to attach to my Celdon leggings. The device possesses certain magiks that will enhance my capabilities.

Joy is love and achievement! Thank you -- Turbine and Microsoft -- for what is and will be.

Ned Cleversun, Level 51 Archer/Mage (life, item)
Fort Tethana, Thistledown, Dereth
