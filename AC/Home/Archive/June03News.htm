 June 22, 2003
The Minor Atlan Stones quest has been updated! New rumors, new creatures, new spells, and a few new locations! Don't make the same mistake I did and check out the old Zefir Cave just for fun!! Ouchie! If you thought the iglooo update was fierce... ;-)

June 21 2003
 A hundred down and a hundred+ to go! Whew! More updates to the Heart of Woe patch page. More creatures added, including an undead pirate crew (Yarr!) and more updated versions. I've also started updating the Isparian Weapons and Atlan Weapons pages with the new stats. I still need many many more, so if you have an Atlan or Isparian, please take a minute to snap a screen of the stats (full stats with icon showing) and send me an email! More coming soon!!

June 20, 2003
 The Heart of Woe patch page received tons of updates including gobs of creatures, new quests and more! Thanks for the all the awesome contributions today! I got so many MBs of screenshots that you brought my email to its knees! There's still more to come! I have at least 200 more submissions to go through, and I haven't even touched the Isparian or Altan updates yet! I know I'm still missing a ton of them, so if you've got updated stats of an Isparian Weapon or an Atlan Weapon, please send me an email (full stats).

June 19, 2003
 Yay, it's patch day! And I could use the break from real life!! ;-) As always, I'll be updating the Heart of Woe patch page through out the day as I'm able. Teaser, Dev Notes (now available!) and Letter to the Players can be found here! Town Crier Rumor are available here! Again, just a reminder to use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address for any screenshots!

If you don't know how to take screenshots, you can visit my Mail and Screenshots tips page for pointers. A quick summary: If it's dark outside, your pic will not turn out; go inside a building to pose if necessary. Click "e" 2x if the icon isn't showing on the examine panel. Full stats please!

June 16, 2003
Once again, I must apologize for the lack of updates. I had the kind of week at work that you have after you get back from vacation! I should be back to a normal schedule this week!

June 6, 2003
Didja miss me?? I snuck out of town on a short Internet-less vacation, but I'm back from the beautiful mountains of Colorado. More coming soon! :-)
