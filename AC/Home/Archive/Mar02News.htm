 March 31, 2002
More quest updates! Staff of the Nomads and Asmolum's Throwing Daggers have been moved into the completed pile!

March 30, 2002
Many of you have asked me, don't I think it's time I got around to moving some of the old partially completed quest guides into the completed list? I do! Bandit Hilt quest is now complete! More coming soon!

I'd also like to take a moment to congratulate my wonderful vassal on his new level (78!). *sigh* They grow up so fast. ;-)

One last item -- if you happened to make a Yahoo email address for your .NET passport account, you should check your privacy settings. You can read this article for more information.

March 26, 2002
 Letters updated! In those famous words of Julia Roberts, I was feeling a bit loquicious! Find out about Paper Dolls, Hollow Armor and Teen Digital Diva! I also answer a few more of the questions posed to me about AC2.

February Letters archived.

March 22, 2002
 The Fletching Steps were getting too big for one page. I've modified the Fletching Section so that the Fletching Steps are now separated out into Arrowheads, Darts, Arrows & Quarrels, and Whittling. The full list is still available on one page if you prefer. Difficulty for Deadly Arrows and Quarrels also added!

March 22, 2002
 Thrown Weapons updated with the new Fletched Atlatl Darts! The steps to make the darts also added to the Fletching Steps page. Thanks to Ching Po of WE  and Majoe of LC for pics and stats! Martinate Trove Key added to the Quiddity Ingot quest page thanks to Matthew the Hawk. Lugian Cookie Cutter added to the Pantry, thanks to Gertarh's Disciple of TD!

March 19, 2002
 Thanks to the wonderful Fletching skills of my vassal's mule, Reefer Madness, I've added the Deadly Quarrel stats to the Arrows & Quarrels page. Alchemy Formulas, and Fletching Steps also updated. More to come!

March 17, 2002
 I've started adding the Persuasion patch information to the various sections. The Sweets page in the Cooking section has been updated with the Ginger Bread Cookes, made with the new Cookie Cutters. Thanks to Matthew the Hawk for pics and info!

Deadly Arrows & Quarrels have been added to the Arrows & Quarrels page in the Sports section. In addition, I've also started updating the Max Base Damage chart with the new changes. Thanks to Evangeline of LC for some great data!

The Formulas page in the Alchemy section updated with Bloodhunter Oil, and the Fletching Items and Fletching Steps have also begun to be updated with Deadly Arrow & Quarrel info. Thanks to Narisah/Juanita Hot Tamale of HG for pics and details for many of the Arrows!

Thanks once again for all the great help with the new patch info. If you see anything I'm missing or that needs correction, please send me an email!

March 15, 2002
 More updates to the Persuasion patch page! A few pics added to the Quiddity Ingots quest. Atlatl info added! Cookie Cutters added to the Pantry, and Cooking Bargains updated as well! Spring Plantings from the Woodsmen added to the Interior Decorating page. Housing Purchase Information page updated with the new items needed for the latest housing release: Mattekar Sleeves or a Bunny Slipper for a Cottage and a Peppermint Monouga Chew for a Villa. More updates to the Settlement Portals page, plus many other miscellaneous details!

March 14, 2002
 It's official. My vassal has declared this to be the best patch ever! The new arrows alone do it for him! Me? Well, I'm pleased as punch about my two extra skill credits. Between the Missile Defense refund, and my triumphant slaughter of Ralirea (thanks to the excellent assistance of Julian's Touch of TD), Maggie the Jackcat's future is looking quite bright!! For more information about this fabulous patch, visit the Persuasion event page! Weapons love, the return of Quiddity Ingots, the end of house camping, pretty new colors for Greater Shadow Armor, free buffs, and a creepy sacrifice! I think this just might be the best patch ever! Oh wait... I already said that. ;-)

Other additions: Town Crier Rumors added, and Settlement Portals partially updated.

March 13, 2002
 The information has been coming in fast and furious. I snuck in a little updating at the office to start the Persuasion patch page! Build Notes, etc. can be found here! Thanks to everyone who has sent pics and info so far! Keep it coming! :)

March 13, 2002
It's patch day! Yipee! Time flies when you're overworked! ;-) I made an emergency update to the Fever Dreams patch page, belatedly adding the Kalidan of Palenqual. Thanks for all the great submissions last month and I apologize for being so tardy in getting those last bits and pieces up (Ok, so they're not all up. More coming soon!! ;-) I will try to do better!!

Just a reminder to use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address when sending screenshot for the Persuasion patch page!

March 12, 2002
More Hunting Recommendations! I've begun the Recommended Hunting Areas for Levels 50+. In addition, I also added several more suggestions for both the Levels 30-40 page and for the Levels 40-50 page. I still have quite a few more to add, just in case you're not seeing your name up there yet. Thanks again for all the great suggestions!

March 11, 2002
It might be the most overused saying regarding AC, but sometimes... nothing else will do: Dereth is full of wonders! The Easter Eggs page in the Odds 'n Ends section has been updated with the Camping Mastery chest. Be amazed!

March 10, 2002
Whoo-hoo! An update! A timely snow storm finally gave me a day off. Looking for phat lewt or mad xp? The Sports section has been updated with Recommended Hunting Areas for levels 40-50. Thanks to everyone who shared info on their favorite hunting areas. I also owe big thank you's for recent contributions to the tip jar! :)

March 7, 2002
Once again, that pesky job has been keeping me busy. I did, however, want to post a warning, responding to several emails I've gotten lately, asking me about stuff/houses, etc. they've seen me selling on eBay. Here is the warning: that is NOT ME!! I have never sold anything on eBay. Use caution. And if someone is pretending to be me... stop it right now! :)

March 4, 2002
The Restaurant Guide & Cooking Sections which I began to update last month are now complete. The Foods page, like the other newly updated sections, is now broken down into Store Bought Food, Spawned/Loot Foods, Quest Foods, and Prepared Foods (page slow to load). By popular request, I've now added separate pages to the Recipes which give step by step instructions for prepared foods. The Pantry was also updated to show all cooking items, including the intermediary cooking steps.

The Fever Dreams patch page updated with Gingerbread and Spiced Milk for Eggnog. Thanks to Narlan of MT, Matthew the Hawk, and Nickolaus.
