June 30, 2006
Count Dardante rewarded a mercenary on TD with a few points of vitae! Read about Nastazio's adventure on the Live Events page.

June 29, 2006
I added a few two clarifications to the Live Events page. I over-edited the Killcrane's Ally submission, mistaking the Makkar as a misspelling of Mukkir (thanks to Jurist of TD for sending a pic -- I'd still love to get a clear shot of the creature or the claw if anyone grabbed one). Lan'fear of HG also wanted to make sure that everyone knew the RM didn't suck; they were just outnumbered! ;-)

I'd love to hear reports from other servers if you participated in a live event! Thanks to everyone sending their info in!

June 27, 2006
The Live Events page has been updated again, this time with a DT event -- Killcrane's Ally, plus the results of the battle between the Rossu Morta and the Whispering Blade on Harvestgain!

June 24, 2006
Visit the Live Events page for reports on the Whispering Blade vs the Rossu Morta conflict, plus reports of the latest Shadow invasion which rewards you with the title, Shadow Hunter! Thanks to everyone sending in info and pics! They're great fun to read! If you participated in a live event, please send in your report also!

June 23, 2006
June's live event has now occurred on 4 servers, with the Rossu Mortu victorious on MT and FF and the Whispering Blade taking the advantage on WE and LC! I'd love to get some first hand reports and screenshots of the battles! If you participated in this month's live event, please send me your info!! Thanks! The schedule for the upcoming servers can be found here! Note: The events have been rescheduled to 8PM eastern time.

June 17, 2006
The adventure continues! Visit the From the Darkest Depths live events page for report of the battle against the Soul Crystal of the Dark Isle! If you've participated in a live event, please send your pics and stories in!!

June 16, 2006
Grael's forces attack! Visit the From the Darkest Depths live events page for details and information on how to obtain the Apprentice Shadow Hunter title!

June 15, 2006
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating the From the Darkest Depths patch page as I'm able throughout the day. For submissions, please include full stats, fabulous up close poses and your in-game name and server! Screenshots are taken with your * key and can be found on your hard drive in the My Documents/Asheron's Call folder. Thanks!

Turbine has announced a Live Op Schedule for the June event! You can find it on the live events page here!

June 12, 2006
The latest Dev Update is available here! Also available are the Letter to the Players, June's From the Darkest Depths event teaser, plus a teaser video (small (6.60MB) or large (50.5 MB)!

June 6, 2006
Frelorn's Weekly Update is available on the Dev Updates page here!

You can also find the exclusive teaser pic for June here!
