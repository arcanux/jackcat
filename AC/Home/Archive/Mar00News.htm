March 27, 2000
You may have noticed a slight alteration to the front page. I sent the URL of my site to the Zone to be added to their AC Links, but was told that although humorous, some of the content was not suitable for a general audience! Since I hadn't really expected the site to be as popular as it is, I wasn't too concerned about the End of the World "Carpe Diem" screen shot before now (I claimed that it portrayed me and my vassal getting a little frisky as we waited for the end of the world). However, the site has begun to get quite heavy traffic. Therefore, in my quest for a G Rating (or at least PG) and higher box office returns, I have removed that page. Besides, I was getting a little miffed that it continued to be one of the most frequently viewed pages day after day, while something like the charming Mage Robe Adventure only got a few dozen hits!

Additions:
Added a button on the home page so my fans can more easily send me mail! :-) Thanks to Toejoe of Leafcull for the suggestion.

That's it. Had to watch the Oscars, you know! Anyway, I gave you that great Atlan Weapon Guide the other day; that should hold you for a bit. Don't want you to get spoiled!
