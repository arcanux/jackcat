January 31, 2005
Ibn has posted this item regarding the February Letter to the Players.

January 29, 2005
I've added many more reports of live events from the past week to the Assault patch page! Some new titles have appeared on the scene besides Curmudgeon's Friend: Queens Crafter, Wardens Assistant and Wardens Thug!

Even though it may look like I haven't updated in days, I've been busy correcting the portal drops, lifestone changes, meeting halls, etc. in the Tourism section! There are a few that I haven't gotten to yet, but your routes should be a little more accurate now!

January 25, 2005
 The Dereth Museum has announced the winners of the raffle tickets! You can visit this page for the results! Thanks to everyone who participated to make the opening such a success! If you haven't stopped by to visit yet, you really should! It's very cool!

January 24, 2005
I've updated another round of starter quests, this time from Shoushi. You can find out more in the Braid Mansion Ruin, the Stone Cathedral and the Four Stones of Jojii quests!

Thanks to everyone sending in their live events reports! I'll try to get them up on the site soon!

January 21, 2005
Yet more live events have been going on in Dereth. This time FF and HG participated in a little scavenger hunt as an Emissary of the Royal Guard sought help gathering items that would aid in protecting Dereth. Items such as Dinner Plate imbued with Crippling Blow. I'd love to have a seat at *that* dinner party! ;-) You can find out more on the patch page here!

Update: MT also had an event yesterday! Case III tracked down the escaped convict Ellis the Grey who committed suicide to avoid being captured and returned to Glenden Wood! You can read Case III's account here!

January 20, 2005
Curmudgeon is making the rounds! MT received a visit from this little Drudge and helped him retrieve the Baron's Amulet for his friend, Nen Ai's Drudge! See the Curmudgeon's Friend page for detail.

MT isn't the only world that received a visitor. Word of the Dereth Museum's grand opening is obviously making the rounds on Dereth. Some curious Burun stopped by to take a look! You can find out more here!

January 19, 2005
A live event took place last night on the Verdantine server! Read Hamfast's report of their "kwest" with Curmudgeon the Drudge on the patch page here! Is that cute or what? Mosswarts, Mice, Drudges. I swear, if Turbine keeps this up, I won't have any creatures left to kill. ;-)

January 18, 2005
The banderlings attacked again last night, this time on Thistledown. I was lucky enough to participate!! You can read my first hand account on the patch page here. Of course, my account involves a lot of trauma and staring at the lifestone, but I suppose that's all a part of life on Dereth!! ;-)

Turbine has also announced the distributor for the upcoming expansion pack! You can read the Press Release in the Throne of Destiny section here!

January 17, 2005
The fireworks were certainly timely! Damien Sarin has announced that the Dereth Museum is having its Grand Opening! This community project is a collection of 16 exhibits at Frostfell's Mosswart Place housing settlement (easily reached by any newbie via the portal from Shoushi at 31.3S, 72.6E)! Both my wonderful vassal, Ned Cleversun, and I are curators of several of the exhibits and we're excited to be a part of this amazing collection! Stop by and see for yourself! Damien is also holding a raffle to help with the maintenance on the cottages.

Two more Banderling Assaults occurred last night on Morningthaw and Frostfell! Visit the Assault patch page for details!

January 16, 2005
Another Banderling assault occurred on Leafcull! See the patch page for details! The Tursh Totem quest was also updated with info on the 40-59 section and the 80-99 section! Thanks to everyone continuing to send info in!

The Holtburg Starter Quests have been updated with the changes from last month's event. See the Holtburg Redoubt, Brogord's Axe and Drudge Hideout write-ups for details!

January 15, 2005
It's an assault! Zerono of HG reports a new global (12:39AM CST)!

A Banderling war party has been discovered in the ruins of Edelbar!

January 14, 2005
More updates to the Assault patch page, including a full write-up of the Banderling Shrine quest! Thanks to everyone who contributed to the patch page! Balloons, champagne, fireworks, resolutions, new hunting grounds, I-see-blue-people, fancy new Royal Oil sleeves, dancing Banderlings, flying Drudges, higher wield weapons, and... a tiara!! I think this just might be the best patch ever!

January 12, 2005
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating the Assault patch page throughout the day, as I'm able. If you need help taking screenshots, please visit the screenshots and mail tips page! Don't forget full stats with icon and your in-game name!

Update: Dev Notes & Roll Out Article are available here and here!

January 11, 2005
Thanks to Flagon, Keep and Travell, Og Doh-Sho (welcome back!), I was finally able to buff and fluff the hot pants quest. You can find the full write-up for Suzuhara's Care Package here!

Update: A new Throne of Destiny article has been posted: Rare Items: Frankenstein Lives! You can find it in the ToD section here!

January 9, 2005
Today was a very exciting day, and one I've been eagerly waiting for for a long time! I finally got my migration invite and was able to shake Zone Billing from my Maggie the Jackcat account forever! Yay!

January 7, 2005
The January teaser is up in the Assault Patch section here, along with an exclusive teaser pic here!

January 6, 2005
Ibn posted a new article on the upcoming Higher Wield Requirement Weapons! You can find it here or here!

Update: AC is experiencing some unscheduled maintenance for a major bug fix.

To tide you over, you can read the Letter to the Players here!

January 3, 2005
Happy New Year! I guess there's no way to get around it. I'm officially back at work. Obviously, the updates were light over the holidays; too much going on. I'll be getting back into the swing of things as soon as I shake off vacation mode! In the meantime, the State of the Network was updated with some upcoming downtimes: the 4th and 11th for scheduled maintenance, and the 12th as the tentative patch day!
