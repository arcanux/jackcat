December 30, 2005
 I'm back in town. I always think I'll have more time to update the site when I'm gone, but it seldom works that way. I'll try and get more of your submissions up over the next few days.

In the meantime, I've added the new rare spear found last night by Fitty-Cent of HG: the Pillar of Fearlessness (#259)! You can find it on the Rares page here!

December 25, 2005
 Merry Christmas and Happy Holidays to everyone! In case you missed it, Frelorn posted a announcement the other day about Darktide Hardware and the High Resolution Graphics textures! You can find it on the Through Sacrifice, Strength dev updates page here!

December 21, 2005
 I've made a start on the Through Sacrifice, Strength patch page. Thanks to everyone who sent submissions! I could still use help filling in the missing pieces! Once again, there may be a delay in getting them up on the site as I'm very busy with family events and traveling this week. Thanks for your patience!

December 19, 2005
The exclusive teaser image for tomorrow's December event is available here!

I'm leaving town tonight for the holidays and will be traveling with very limited internet access for the next few days. I'd like to encourage everyone to please still send in your patch submissions! It's unlikely I'll get them posted right away, but I will update the page as I'm able to over the next few days! Thanks for your understanding!

December 16, 2005
The December Letter to the Players has been posted! The latest weekly update has also been added to the Dev Updates page!

A few announcements have also been submitted. There's a new Decal Update and Bivitar Hawkins of Meginjarder Allegiance sent news of a MT server-wide holiday event!

December 7, 2005
Thanks to The Knight of SC, another account of the Antius & Kurth Live Event has been posted on the Ashes and Dust Live Events page.

The latest Dev posts from the Turbine Boards can also be found on the Dev Updates page! Congrats to Crowley for moving in the Producer slot and to Kintani, a new addition to the Live team!

December 2, 2005
The Ashes and Dust Live Events page has been updated with an account of a player's new titles, plus a great report of the Live Event in Glenden Wood yesterday. Read about the death of Antius and Captain Kurth here!!

If anyone got the full text of Antius's speech or has more pics of the event, please send them in! Thanks!!

Frelorn's weekly update from the 28th was also added to the Dev Updates page!
