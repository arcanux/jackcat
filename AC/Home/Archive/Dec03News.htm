 December 31, 2003
Just wanted to wish everyone a safe and happy New Year!

December 30, 2003
I sorted through some of the mail I received while I was on vacation and did some more updating to the Waking from the Abyss patch page! A few more creatures, letter quest rewards and leather armor updates added!

I also updated numerous pages in the Auberean History section. If any of the old patches were missing rollout articles or build notes, etc., they have now been added!

December 25, 2003
Merry Christmas, Happy Holidays to everyone!!

December 24, 2003
Turbine has released a sneak peek of a new creature for the upcoming January event: the Margul. You can find it here! Part 9 of Reawakening is also available!

December 23, 2003
News on the morning's patch can be found on the Turbine site here. In addition, parts 1-8 of Reawakening are up! Last, Ibn has posted a correction to the Roll-Out article. You can find it here!

December 20, 2003
More buffing and fluffing to the Waking from the Abyss patch page! Thanks to all the fabulous submissions! The festivus season continues into a winter wonderland! Snowmen, snowflakes, snow pies (better than cow pies, don't you agree?), snowballs! Sleek and Dapper clothing, new detective hats, new leather armor, updated Exploration items, new quests, new creatures, new house furnishings, and best of all... air conditioning (now hookable)!! I think this just might be the best patch ever!

You think, "It's always the best patch ever. I was struck by an odd feeling of deja vu...."

I'm heading out of town for the holidays, but I'll be bringing the laptop with me and plan to do some updating if I get a chance. Byeeee!

December 18, 2003
The transcript for the chat with Jessica about the AC Return to Turbine can be found here. Special thanks to Magnus Kaliptic of DT for recording it!

December 18, 2003
I'm sure you've heard it before: "You may be old school if...." Well, if you have Run specialized (like me!), you just might remember The Spin From Turbine, a precursor to the Letter to the Players. I've ferreted out as many as I could and posted them on the various patch pages. You can find them in the various event pages of the Auberean History section -- from the Fire in the Sky through The Child of Daralet (minus a few). A little something for you to read while the servers are down for patch day!

As always, I'll be updating the Waking from the Abyss page as I'm able. Screenshots should be sent to jackcat@spacestar.net - use your @day command (I get a lot of pitch black pictures every month that I can't use), complete stats with icons please, and don't forget your in-game name!! For tips on taking screenshots, visit the Mail and Screenshots Tips page!

Update: Roll-Out article and Dev Notes are up! You can find them here!

A two part story, Reawakening, is also available on Turbine's AC site!

December 17, 2003
 It may be the most exciting Asheron's Call news ever!!! Turbine has announced it's buying back the franchise from Microsoft! You can read about it here! You'll also find letters from Jessica and the AC Live Team about the news! May I just say: Whoo hoo!!

Update: Some of your questions may be answered on the Microsoft Insider site. They've posted a FAQ and a Letter to Subscribers!

Additionally, you can join a live chat with Jessica Mulligan, Producer of Asheron's Call 1 & 2, tomorrow night at 6PM - PST (9PM - EST) on the IRC sorcery.net channel, room #chatwarcry. Questions are being gathered here.

If that isn't enough, Scenario has posted a festive holiday lugian wallpaper on the Turbine Forums! *faints from all the excitement*

December 14, 2003
Another teaser pic, posted on the Turbine Forums is now available on the Waking from the Abyss page. You can also find information on last night's hotfix on the Turbine forums.

December 12, 2003
I'm on a roll! A dozen or so more missing pics added to the Pets section. Continuing thanks to everyone helping to fill in the blanks!

The teaser, plus an exclusive screenshot for next Thursday's December event is up and available on the Waking from the Abyss page!

December 8, 2003
Yes, even more pics have been added to the Pets section! I should have updated the Stuff I'd Like page ages ago! ;-) Thanks once again to Luthien Bedwyr of WE and Ash the Man of SC, and also FrozenFish of FF and Tobias Truesoul of TD!

December 6, 2003
Another batch of pics added to the Pets section, this time courtesy of Luthien Bedwyr of WE! The December Letter to the Players is also up and can be found here!

December 5, 2003
Ash the Man of SC was at it again. 25+ more pics added to the Pets section. Thanks also to many other players helping to fill out the missing pics and info from the Stuff I'd Like page!

Brute also sent in the stats for the 4th type of Olthoi Shield (Lesser  for levels 40-60) which has been added to the Visions in the Darkenss patch page!

Two other short items:
1) Ibn has requested player feedback on Live Lore Events and PK(L) radar blips. Visit the Turbine Forums to pass on your comments!

2) My hosting company's data center is moving this weekend, so the site may experience periodic outages.

December 1, 2003
Thanks to Ash the Man of SC for sending a huge pile of missing pics and stats from the Stuff I'd Like page for the Pets section! About 30 new pics have been added to various pages!
