October 30, 2000
Masks page has been updated with the King's Helm, courtesy of Tuesday of Solclaim! Thanks Tuesday!

I've also updated the Letters page with a selection of letters praising the site! What can I say? I am eternally self-indulgent. Also included is a letter mentioning that the ore for the Impious Staff is no longer available in the Arwic Mines. 

I absolutely love getting your letters, and love all the contributions you make, but I must admit that I may have to change my statement that all letters will receive a personal response. I've been from 200-300 letters behind for several months now and don't seem to be any closer to catching up. I'll see how it goes in the next few weeks and hope that I haven't hurt anyone's feelings! It doesn't mean that I don't love you! :)

Ned Cleversun's death total is waaaay out of date. Up 93 (eeek!) to 684. But on a more exciting note, his loyalty is also up 2 more points to 142! I must say that Danier's bragging about his 147 Loyalty stat turned out to be super-good for me! Congratulations to both Ned and Danier: Ned for breaking the Level 50 barrier (he's actually Level 51 now!), and to Danier for making the Top 10 list for Focus!

October 21, 2000
Yes, it's more on boobies! All I can say is I'm soooo glad that Maggie the Jackcat's Kick-Ass Fabulous AC Web Site doesn't get the kind of visitors that seem to populate Lum's site and CoD's boards!! ;-) I received so many thoughtful, interesting letters from people commenting on my Boobie Dress page, I was impressed once again with the intelligence and maturity of AC players! I have the *best* fans! 

Some still like them and some still hate them, but I think this discussion over the new boobie dresses has generated more interesting discourse than any other issue in AC so far. Read more letters here! I've also added a pic of one of the dresses dyed pink, courtesy of Maddy.

October 11, 2000
In any other circumstance, the lack of a new fez during a monthly update would have been a crushing blow. But how can anything be upsetting when Turbine has bestowed such a charming plethora of riches on the inhabitants of Dereth. Including cleavage!

I admit I wasn't too keen on the idea of girlie clothes, but the new duds are wonderful and are not restricted to female characters! 

And that's not all. Cool new masks to wear and carved pumpkins to throw! New creatures, new city, new recipes, new services. Mages rejoice over the newest version of Split Pea. Halloween has always been one of my most favorite times of year. I was only in the game for a short while tonight, but the land was alive and buzzing with... FUN!

Read more about this great new patch on my Hollow Victory pages. See Maggie's new cleavage! As always, the new Town Crier Rumors have been added, as well as Ulgrim's new babblings.

In addition to the patch info, I also added the pronunciation of Caulnalain, Fenmalain, & Shendolain to the Abbreviations, etc. page, courtesy of Stormwaltz.

Letters Section was updated and September Letters Archived.
