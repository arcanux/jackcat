 November 30, 2001
Creature Magic Mastery spells and Creature Ineptitude spells updated with all icons. All broken/mis-directed links should now be corrected.

Marae Lassel Crafters & Collectors page finally updated with the new reward for an Olthoi Claw. Thanks to the many, many people who wrote me with the information!

November 29, 2001
I'm sure many of you know how it is: you take a few days off work, and you have a few weeks worth waiting for you when you get back! (How does that happen, anyway? ;-) More updates soon! (I did manage to fix a few wrong links on the Creature Magic Mastery page and started adding the icons, though!)

November 26, 2001
I'm back! Continuing with the updating and filling out of some of the older pages, I've redone the Gems page in the Shopping section. In addition to the quest/crafted and store bought gems, I have, as usual, included a sampling of the randomly generated loot gems. Locksmith page updated with several more keys as well. Last, Creature Magic Spells added to the Magic Section!

Thanks for all the great mail while I was gone. And thank you also for the contributions to the Tip Jar! Much appreciated! 

November 20, 2001
I'll be out of town from today through the holiday weekend and may not have a chance to update the site until I return. Just in case, Happy Thanksgiving!

November 19, 2001
 I've started adding the names of the settlements to the Real Estate Guide and cross-linking the Settlement Portals to the various settlements. If anyone's spotted portals near Lin, Nanto, Baishi, or Uziz, let me know. I think there have got to be a few more out there! (or maybe not!) I've also gotten back to work confirming more locations on the Coordinates page. Lytelthorpe, Dryreach and Kara area pages added. 

Vendor Rates page updated with the new Healer in Dryreach! It's now a full service town! Yay!

Siraluun Matihao information updated to indicate the weapon may now be buffed.

November 17, 2001
 More info added to The Gathering Storm event page! See Said Nuff of SC posing with an Atlan Staff of Black Fire (cool new effects). Many more portals added to the Settlement Portals page! Mansions now for sale! And last, but certainly not least, an updated cow! This just might be the best patch ever!

November 16, 2001
 The Gathering Storm event page is up and running! Garden Drudges! Mosswarts! Hooks and portals galore! As usual, Town Crier Rumors and Ulgrim the Unpleasant sayings updated with the latest news! It you see information I'm missing, please send an email to jackcat@spacestar.net.

November 15, 2001
 Just popping on for a minute to slap a few new patch pics up on The Gathering Storm event page! Rockys mage poses in the updated Canescent Robe and Astral Dominae of TD sends information on Lawn Drudges! Yay! More later tonight!

November 15, 2001
 For those looking for a little something to read while the servers are down for the new patch (!!!) today, I've updated the Letters section!

I've got a busy day at work and an office holiday party to attend in the early evening (I know. Can you believe it? It's not even Thanksgiving yet!), but I'll do my best to get a patch page up as usual. Just a reminder for everyone sending screenshots to please use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address! Thanks!

November 14, 2001
My hosting company was doing some upgrading last night, so the site might have experienced some down time.

Another spelling correction, thanks to Terror and Pain. The two spelling variations of the Hea Tumerok who drops the Elari Bow Parts have been consolidated into one correct spelling of Hea Temenua! 

November 13, 2001
Final weapons page in the Sports section completed: Unarmed Weapons! Added the missing Greater Mana Charge to the Olthoi Claw reward on the Marae Lassel Cafters and Collectors page thanks to Scott S and Y A N G of LC. Information on what creature drops the Aun Pendant also added, courtesy of Zangria of MT. I also replaced an ugly old pic on the Cooking page with a prettier one of a nice food spread! Other miscellaneous typos and spelling errors corrected. 

As usual, I'm extra behind on mail. I'm working bit by bit on getting everything up on the site! Thanks to everyone who keeps sending information in!

November 12, 2001
Almost through with the updating of the Sports section: Sword and Thrown Weapons added. Pages may take a bit to load. The loot generated samplings are a bit larger since there are so many different kinds of each type!

November 11, 2001
Spear and Staff pages updated in the Sports section.

November 10, 2001
Back to some older projects! Mace page in the Sports section updated in the new format, with Crafted and Quest Maces separated out, then Store Bought Maces, and, as usual, a sampling of the randomly generated loot maces! Thanks to Protura of Darktide for pointing out that I forgot to mention the Singularity Caul as one of the other islands on Auberean!

November 9, 2001
I've made a change in the Culture section that has been bugging me for a looooong time. Just another little house-keeping item. The Dereth History section is now called Auberean History. With the addition of Aerlinthe, the Vesayen Isles and now Marae Lassel, Dereth History just didn't cut it. (Dereth is only one of the islands on the world of Auberean.) Parchments is also now called Rumors. I've kept all links and URLs the same for anyone who had links to the pages.

I also fixed the typos on two misspellings of Ralirea's name for all you sixth sense users who were freaking out. ;-)

November 8, 2001
Major breakthrough on the Hea Totem Quest last night. Zender the Crusher of LC turned in his Rubble and received one of the Totems that can be attached to one of the three weapons' bases. Thanks for the great pics and info Zender!! Thanks also to T'aeg of SC for the last of the three bases: the Hoeroa of Palenqual! 

November 6, 2001
Last few Marae Lassel towns added to the Vendor Rates page. I also filled in a few missing crafter coordinates on the Marae Lassel Crafters and Collectors page. Hea Town Crier Rumors added and a few more of Ralirea's saying added to Ralirea's Songs. 

November 5, 2001
Even more additions to various Dark Majesty pages. Kalindan of Palenqual added to the Hea Totem Quest page. Enigo Montoya tells about his all expense paid trip to Dryreach during the Skill Credit quest. Tewhate of the Forest added to the Palenqual Weapons. Bracelet of Acid Protection added to the Olthoi Titles quest, Singularity Mace and Axe added to the Singularity Upgrade page.

You can also find a great Map of Marae Lassel by Lorenth of FF!

November 4, 2001
I've started adding the Marae Lassel towns to the Vendor Rates page.

A few more items added to the Marae Lassel Crafters and Collectors page. 

I've separated the Palenqual's Living Weapons quest from the Hea Totem quest to avoid confusion. If you have additional information or screenshorts for either of these quests, please send me an email. Many miscellaneous items, comments or corrections to the various other Dark Majesty pages. Now that the frantic pace of the initial discovery phase is settling down, I'll be working on revising the various Marae Lassel quest pages into real write-ups!

I've also been very remiss in not thanking the people who have contributed to the Tip Jar. Thank you very much! I deeply appreciate it.

November 3, 2001
More miscellaneous updates. A Journal by Martine and the new Zone story Blood Brothers added to the Texts section (thanks to Shadymilkman for all the typing!). Pic of The Great Machine added to the Dark Majesty Expansion page. More updates to various Dark Majesty quest pages, including some additional Totems and weapons on the Palenqual's Weapons (more to come), the Crop parts and Scalpel parts. Marae Lassel Crafters and Collectors information reformatted on a separate page for easier use. Housing Purchasing Information updated. Plus other doo-dads here and there.

After days of rampant speculation, someone finally had the nerve to try out their cooking skill on the Olthoi Eggs! See what fabulous concoctions Ravlen has created on CoD's spoiler board. Stormwaltz also stopped by CoD to comment on the Olthoi Queen quest.

November 2, 2001
After a pack of prairie dogs chewed me to death by my ankles on Marae Lassel (how embarrassing), Danier helped me recover my body and escorted me up to the Tall Trees through an Olthoi swarm. Tall Trees sayings added to the Culture section. 

(Thanks once again for the continuing information. I'll try to get everything up this weekend.)

November 1, 2001
Sharky snacks! That's just one of the new items added to the various Dark Majesty quest pages. You can find them here! More information added to Reata's Necklace, Palenqual's Weapons, Awakener, Scapel, Skill Credit quest, Olthoi Titles, Canescent Mattekar Robe and more. Dark Majesty Expansion page updated with a few new locations, more crafter rewards and other miscellaneous details. More coming soon!
