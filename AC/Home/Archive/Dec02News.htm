 December 27, 2002
 Runed Chests have been added to the site. Currently, the static unlocked and 100 resistant chests from the recent Holtburg updates have been added. If you have info on any runed chests I'm missing, please send me an email!

December 25, 2002
Merry Christmas! I hope everyone is having a safe and happy holiday! I know I am. burp!! ;-)

We finally got the FTP kinks worked out, so I could publish to the site. There are many miscellaneous updates, including Ulgrim's Scroll info on The Hall of the Tusker King page (thanks to Magehart of TD), patch items added to the Interior Decorating section, and an updated Lilitha's Bow quest! A few more pets pics and xp also added.

As I mentioned before the patch, I'm beginning to compile info on the updated loot profiles. If you'd like to help, please keep detailed notes on what creatures drop what items and I'll be adding them here on the loot profile page (location information also to be added). Currently, I have the quest and crafted items. I'd like to get info like AL, burden, difficulty ranges, min & max damage, plus which creatures drop potions, elixir, heal kits, etc. Anyone willing to help in depth with this section, please send me an email!

Last, I've belatedly added more November letters. December letters coming soon!

December 20, 2002
More miscellaneous updates to the The Hall of the Tusker King patch page, including additional hooked items. Thanks to all the people who sent the info on the lowest Gift Box dungeon too!

Pets section updated again with over 25 pics. Special thanks to Chupon of LC for such great shots!!

December 19, 2002
It's a small update to the The Hall of the Tusker King patch page, but a shocking one. Sek the Wise of MT, wanting marshmallows for his hot chocolate, HACKED HIS EEPS TO DEATH!!! ;-)

A few new additions: Visit the Snowglobe page to find out the reward for giving the Blind Snowman a Peppermint Stick! Thanks to Tofino of WE. A few more rewards added to the King Bobo quest. Thanks to Machine of MT for the Magic Defense Tatoo stats!

December 18, 2002
Numerous updates to the The Hall of the Tusker King patch page! Bobo appears and puts the Ulgrim rumor to rest, Gift Boxes appear, complete with the chance to plummet to your death; quest updates, cool hats (get it??), lumps of coal for the naughty, festive holiday lights the nice, some marshmallow mystery still to be discovered, and a Buffy shout-out to top it all off! With the new furnishings, hookable items, dangerous dirks and an updated loot table, I think this just might be the best patch ever! Continued thanks to everyone who sent information in for the patch page!!

December 17, 2002
What do you know, it's patch day! As always, I'll be updating The Hall of the Tusker King event page throughout the day. Dev notes, etc. will be found here. Please use jackcat@spacestar.net to send screenshots! Thanks!

If you'd like to help compile information on the updated loot profiles, please keep detailed notes on the type of creature and *all* loot they drop, down to the tiniest stat. I'm hoping to compile this information -- max AL for different armor types, new max weapon damage and wield requirements, what drops what etc. -- by popular request. I'd like to get a group of volunteers willing to work on this on an ongoing basis. If you're interested, just start taking notes and we'll sort the details out later! ;-)

Another dozen Pets added, thanks to Chupon of LC who sent in a bundle of pics, including a nice selection of Marae Lassel Tumeroks, a few Gromnies and the elusive Entropy Wisp! A few more miscellaneous xp values also added.

December 15, 2002
I've been distracted by holiday festivities, but I found a little time to add, yes, more Pets! 35+ more pics added here and there. I think I have most of the submissions added at this point. If you still see a "pic needed" marker, please feel free to send a screen to jackcat@spacestar.net (high quality .jpg preferred).

December 11, 2002
It's more Pets! 30+ additions, including many more Elementals and Crystals. I nearing the end of the piles of submissions (but I still have one more batch). If your picture doesn't get posted, I may have decided not to use it, most commonly because the picture is too dark and the creature is hard to see. It's also possible that with all the mail I get, I've overlooked some submissions. If you see any pics I'm missing, please send a screen to jackcat@spacestar.net. Thanks to everyone who helped me fill the section out once I made the late decision to add pics of all the creatures!

December 10, 2002
I'm back! Did you miss me?! ;-) Pets updated with about 35 more various pics. Creature experience table also got a few minor updates. Still more to come!

December 6, 2002
Continuing with the house-keeping in the quests section, the Canescent Mattekar Robe quest has been updated. Fabulous pics of both the old and new version included. Costumes also updated in the Toys & Games section.

I'll be out of town for the next few days, but if I have a chance, I'll try to go through some mail and do a much needed update to the Letters section.

December 5, 2002
Another update to the Quests section: the Hollow & Phantom Missile Weapons from the Festivus patch has been added. Also included is information on the Chorizite Fletching Tool used to make Chorizite Arrows, Quarrels and Atlatl Darts!

December 4, 2002
 About 35 creature pics added to the Pets section. Thanks to everyone helping to fill in the missing info! I still have a big pile to get up, so if you don't see your contribution, it's coming soon!

The Pumpkin Carving page in the Arts and Crafts section has been updated with info on Great Pumpkins from the Festivus event.

December 2, 2002
 Scads of minor updates to over 30 pages throughout the site. Don't get too excited; the to-do list is still extremely long. ;-) Some misspelled names corrected, minor updates or corrections to numerous quest write-ups, Baishi shortcut finally updated, many new creature experience values updated, additions to the Scrolls page, plus o.O added to the Abbreviations page. Thanks to Jebus christ of FF for the suggestion. ;-)

December 1, 2002
Yet another quest moved into the completed pile. Find out everything you need to get your Major Smoldering Stone from the Jehanna Vault!

I know that some of the older write-ups really need some attention, especially the ones written back in the day when people freaked if you even gave out coordinates! (The spoilers are Eeeeviilll days ;-) The Dagger of Tikola quest is the first of these to be buffed with detailed notes and fab screenshots.
