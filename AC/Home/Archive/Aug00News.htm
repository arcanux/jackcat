Letters

August 31, 2000
 Several people have written to me asking me to put a search engine on the site. I've added a link to one on the Home page. However, I haven't tested it at all yet, so if it doesn't work, give me a few days to tweak it, before sending me mail! 

Speaking of mail, I realized I hadn't updated the Letters Section in forever. That's because, of course, I am still so behind answering mail. But I am catching up, slowly but surely. I am only two weeks back instead of three! 

I also added the base stats for the Medium Shadow Amuli on the Shadow Armor page.

August 30, 2000
 I sent out a newsletter a few days ago, but didn't put the last edition up on the web as I usually do. It's up now! You can find the link to the July Newsletter as well as links to other past newsletters on the lower right of this page.

August 29, 2000
 I know you like the cups and bowls as much as I do! The For the Table page was updated with a few more colorful selections of Dinnerware.

A selection of Rapiers was finally added to the Swords page.

August 28, 2000
It's been several days since I updated the site, but I have a new Runway feature to offer! Check out Razhell in his fancy red suit!

Other site additions: I've update the Culture Section with many of the new texts from the latest patch. I also added Dave Javier's Zone story on Mosswarts. You can find them in the Texts Section.

And you can really tell how long it's been since I updated by the rise in my vassal's Death Stats. Up FIFTY to 530. Working off his vitae has sent so much experience my way, that it turns out I haven't earned ANY AT ALL myself!! 

We stumbled across this bug the other day, realizing that my total experience was 50K LESS than the experience my vassal had supposedly earned for me. That's just not right! ;-)

August 23, 2000
Thanks go to Danier once again for stats and screenshots of the Greater Shadow Amuli with the new elemental protections! Check out the Updated Shadow Armor page for more, including the new text, Aliester's Corollary. I'll continue to update the Shadow Armor page with more stats as I collect them.

August 21, 2000
New Runway feature! Check out Detriment in a sharp outfit of black and gold.

I've also revised the Locksmith page again. I've added many of the keys found in the game with descriptions and a note on what they're used for. If you have a key that you're not sure what to do with, you might check this page.

Thanks to lots of generous contributors, I've tweaked the Hollow and Ravenous Weapons quests with a few more stats. 

The Taste of Twilight page also got a small update. The fabulous Danier sent me some stats of the new Wasteland Rat and the Banderling Mangler.

Several people have sent me great info and pics on Composite Bows made with Fire Auroch Horns. I'll try to get the page updated soon.

Thanks once again to everyone who's been sending me information. I'm slowly catching up on answering mail, but am still about 3 weeks behind. I'm also sorry to say that I'm going to have to stop taking volunteers for the Runway. I have such a line backed up that I probably won't get everyone up until 2003. :)

August 15, 2000
The Alchemy Section got a little love! Thanks to Malleus Eminus and Merlyn of Harvestgain, I've added a page on Potions, including the new Dispel Potions! I also slightly modified the Alchemical Formulas page, moving the Draughts over to the Potions page.

I've had a request to display the icons with the Alchemical Formulas and will try to revise the page soon. I did format the Potions page in that manner. The final addition to the Alchemy section is a page describing its Special Uses in quests.

Have I mentioned that the wonderful Danier arranged to have all three of my dear vassal's strings Excellently Oiled as Ned pursued his Composite Bow? That he did.

August 14, 2000
Thanks to Malleus Eminus, Feyd al-Zahah and Charsui Bouw, the Taste of Twilight pages now boast lots of pics of the new foods and chocolate treats! I just love this patch!

I've also started updating the stats on my Food page. Many of them still reflect the old value and burden. I'll be trying to fill them in bit by bit. The yellow reflects updated data.

Many of the Arrow and Quarrels also still show old values. I'm working on updating these as well. A big thank you to Aeros Archimedea for sending me the missing pics of the Greater Elemental Quarrels!

New Letters! Find out where I got my boots! 

Did I mention how much Ned likes his new bow handle?! That Danier has got to be the nicest, bravest, strongest, most clever Monarch on Thistledown!!! (How was that Ned? I'm not laying it on too thick, am I?)

August 11, 2000
I promised Ned I would say nice things about Danier every day for a week! Thanks to this high powered player, Ned now has a handle for his beautiful composite bow! The Dark Revenant Handle intricately carved is one of the most sought after and valuable items in the game. I can't thank you enough, Danier, for supplying my special vassal with such a prize! You are SO COOL!! :)

(No, Ned didn't get it for free. He paid in Sturdy Keys!)

While Danier was busy killing Dark Revs, he also discovered a few new items. Check the updated Taste of Twilight for a pic of the Decayed Scrap of Paper!

Thank you, thank you to all the people who have been sending in pictures and info to me. I've been trying to credit you as I go, but am extremely busy at work right now and very behind on answering mail again. If you haven't received a personal response yet, just want you to know the info is much appreciated!

Just a few quick additions to the Taste of Twilight page, including the Umbris Note Translation, thanks to Danier!

Patch Day! Yay!! I love it already. New Shoes and Chocolate! What more could a girl ask for?! Check out my new footwear on the Taste of Twilight pages!

New icons for Potions and Lockpicks. And a new Death Total For Ned Cleversun!
