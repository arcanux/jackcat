 May 29, 2003
If you missed your chance to play on Shadowclaim, the test server, before, here's your chance again. Shadowclaim will be open again this weekend, with some special events scheduled at Friday 4:00 PM (EST) and Saturday at 7:00 PM (EST). To reach the test world, go here!

May 28, 2003
The Sword of Frozen Fury quest has been organized into a full write-up. Sword in the Stone! Virindi! Portal Puzzles! Big green giant stairs! Don't miss it!! A few more maps have also been added to the Halls or Knorr quest, courtesy of Immolate of FF!

May 26, 2003
The Halls of Knorr quest now has a full write-up complete with fabulous pics! Find out how to upgrade your Hieromancer's armor, or to get a cool new helm, mace or UA weapon here!

Thanks to everyone who sent info on the patch! Thanks also to Archangel of WE and Dorak of WE who filled out the missing fishies! As always, more coming soon!

May 25, 2003
It was the update everyone was waiting for. That's right... mites! Visit the Branith's Shirt and Blazing Staff quest for the changes from the latest patch! Kill the wise and beautiful Mite Warrior Queen for additional rewards: the Mite Queen's Staff and Discus.  Plus A Crumpled Letter from Elysa! (That is, if you don't mind the teeth marks.)

May 24, 2003
Looking for something to do this holiday weekend? How about Fishing! The Fishing quest has been organized and fluffed into a full write-up. Thank you to everyone who helped compile info! Special thanks to Wrath of Dool - SC for completing the fishies reward tables! If you want to help fill out the colored fish icons I'm missing, please send me an email!

May 20, 2003
Many more updates to the Discoveries patch page! And still more to come! Midnight patch days on dial-up = very tired Maggie! Sleep won out over finishing up the patch page last night! ;-) Sword in the Stone, new mage armor upgrade quest, updates to casting items, new and improved gambling rewards, FISHING! Not to mention mighty mites and squishy green balls of gunk. This just might be the best patch ever!!

May 18, 2003
It's the stealthiest stealth patch of them all! The Discoveries patch is live! Visit the patch page for updates throughout the day, and just a reminder to send screenshots to jackcat@spacestar.net! Letter to the Players, Teaser, etc. can be found here. Town Crier rumors can be found here.

May 13, 2003
No, really! More coming soon!! ;-) Things should be back to normal for me in a few days. Thanks for the patience. In the meantime, take a look at this new update: Registration for the ACPL! Yay! Thanks to Akiva of TD for the head's up!

May 6, 2003
"What happened?" the email asks. "Where are you?" I apologize for the lack of updates! You are witnessing what happens when three out of four people in the office leave for vacation at the same time - me, of course, being the 4th. *pulls hair out*

Darn this pesky real life! *quits job to play AC full time* ;-) More coming soon!
