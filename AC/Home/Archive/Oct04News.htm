October 29, 2004
Thanks to Arboc of SC, the Tourism section has been updated with another category of locations, this time of all the NPCs in the game! Click on the name of an NPC for further details, including closest town, related quests and shortest route! I'm still going through my screenshots to add pics and descriptions (coming soon!), but the list itself should be pretty thorough. If you notice an NPC I'm missing, please send me an email. Thanks again, Arboc!!

October 28, 2004
The November Letter to the Players is up on the Turbine site here, or you can find it in the patch section here! There's also a new Dev Bio on Jessica Mulligan, Executive Producer for AC and the Creative Director for the expansion pack, on the Throne of Destiny site here!

October 26, 2004
Turbine has posted the first look at one of Throne of Destiny's creatures -- the Viamontian Knight. Check it out here.

October 24, 2004
Guess what the update is today! That's right; more raids!! Visit the All That They Survey patch page for more fun and mayhem!

October 22, 2004
Town attacks are still occurring! I've added another handful of reports to the All That They Survey patch page, as well as a few more pics on the door assult from Zedjin of TD, including one of Mgrauleshk's stats. Thanks, as always, to everyone continuing to send in patch info! 

October 20, 2004
Whereas most of the servers have destroyed the door, releasing Reeshan into Dereth, Morningthaw and Verdantine have mounted a defense! Exciting times! Server divergence, perhaps? See this post by Ibn for an intriguing comment.

Update: The doors on MT and VT have fallen. We're doomed!!!! :)

There's also a new Dev Bio on the Turbine Site here!

October 19, 2004
Lead [sic] by Mgrauleshk, a Guruk Gorefiend, the Burun storm the Temple of Izji Qo. The portal gateway in the matron's crypt has become active and the Burun have stormed a receiving chamber containing a sealed portal. Mgrauleshk and his force will break through the wall and activate the portal beacon, bringing the Burun masters to Dereth unless they are stopped within ninety minutes.

Thanks to Zerono & An'iya of HG, Mykell of TT and Chazzy Chaz of TD! Visit the patch page for more information!


October 18, 2004
I've gotten a pile of new screens and raid reports form the weekend up on the patch page here! Thanks to everyone continuing to send info in!!

FYI: The Turbine AC Forums will be down for maintenance today starting at approx. 10AM PDT and lasting several hours.

Update: several more reports of raids from this past weekend and today added (including new pics!) to the All That They Survey patch page.

Two new articles have also been posted on the Turbine Site. Three examples of new Augmentation Gems that players will be able to acquire and use to gain new abilities and bonuses, and the first in a series of fiction leading up the launch of Throne of Destiny: The Invasion of Ispar.

October 15, 2004
 A dark cloud has settled over Ayan Baqur. Monsters have been seen dangerously close to town fleeing from the Burun in the Dires.

Pookie and the TM go on a rampage in Ayan Baqur. Visit the patch page for reports and pics!

October 14, 2004
The All That They Survey patch page continues to be updated as another new report of a town attack comes in! Turbine has also posted a new Billing Update! A new Salvage Skill, Blackmire 5, Ralirea out of hiding, a new island, crafter love and anchovies! I think this might just be the best patch ever!! What? Cabalists in Ayan... Anchovies! ...Free content! And don't forget the billing update (my favorite)!

Update: Turbine Billing has launched for new accounts!! You can find more information here!

A second dev diary, this time on Dave "Crowley" Javier is up on the Throne of Destiny site here!

October 13, 2004
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating the All That They Survey patch page as I'm able throughout the day! If you come across any new patch info, send it in! Full stats with icon showing, please! And don't forget your in-game name and server!! If you need help taking screenshot, you can visit the Mail and Screenshots Tips page.

The Turbine site is having technical difficulties, so Ibn forwarded the Roll Out Article and Dev Notes. You can find them here and here!

October 12, 2004
Now here's a fun announcement! The Throne of Destiny website has gone live! They've started things off with a Letter from the Team, a bio and Dev Diary from srand, an article on the level cap increase, and two lists of FAQs!

You can also find out who's on the team here!

October 11, 2004
Ibn has announced an addition to the AC Community Relations team: Aldryth, formerly known as Eldreth! Congrats Eldreth!! Er... Aldryth!! You can read the announcement here!

October 10, 2004
Thanks to Ravek of SC who sent in a handful of Spears for the Palenqual's weapons poses page!

Another quest cleaned up with pics added! See a virindi experiment gone very wrong in the Gibbering Claw quest!

October 9, 2004
Just a reminder to everyone to redeem your free experience from Jalina al-Hajj in Al-Arqas before the patch next Wednesday! Ibn suggests not waiting until the last minute!!

Also, a request for DT or VT players before the next patch! I think you're the only two worlds to have not yet received the 5 available globals from the Noble Weapons/Blackmire 4 quest! If anyone is going through this weekend, I'm hoping you can snag pics and stats of the Guruk Titan Watcher, the Ruuk Fiend Watcher and the Burun Egg! If anyone is so inspired, please forward to jackcat@spacestar.net!

October 8, 2004
October's teaser is part three of The Shattered Coil! You can find it on the Turbine Site or in the patch section here, along with an Exclusive Teaser pic here! )Parts 1 & 2 of The Shattered Coil can be found here.)

Turbine also posted a weird little community survey opened only to the first 200 people. You can find its former location here!

October 6, 2004
A half dozen more Palenqual's Living Weapons added to the weapons poses page! Thanks to Raybow of WE for the Panaq pics and to Killz al-Evil fo TD for totem hunting for my new Ukiras! Numerous creature stats and xp values updates as well.

The following is an off-topic remark, but I just gotta say: Bruce, Michael, Neil, Conor and John... you ROCK!

October 3, 2004
The Palenqual's Living Weapons quest has finally been updated with the changes from the Mired Hearts event! I've also created a new page to show off all the completed weapons! I'm missing quite a few of them, so if you've got pics and stats (with icon) of weapons I'm missing, send them in!

The October Letter to the Players also received an addendum from Ibn regarding keeping some pack space free before logging off for the patch. You can find it here!
