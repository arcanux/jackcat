March 31, 2005
Turbine has announced that Jolt will be opening a new European server when the Throne of Destiny expansion launches! You can read the announcement here!

March 28, 2005
Pookie has been making the rounds! Monkey Fist of HG reports that the Yaraq orchard was hopping again, with a return of the Tremendous Monouga and a visit from our favorite little wabbit! But that's nothing compared to the scene on Morningthaw! Legacy of Order sends a report and screens of the rascally rabbit raid in Al-Jalima!

March 27, 2005
Reports from a few recent live events have been added to the patch page. Read about Renswic of HG's harboring of a criminal here, and see Angelina Rayne's pics of the Tremendous Monouga attack on the Yaraq Orchards here!

These events make for some fun reading! My favorite line? When Harmlet the Green prods Renswic to kill the +LeO who's after him, Renswic replies: "I can't. I'm a thrown weapons toon." ;-)

March 23, 2005
Turbine announced today that Codemasters will be distributing the Throne of Destiny expansion in Europe! You can read the Press Release on the Turbine site here or in the ToD section here!

More uninscribe events have also been announced. You can read the schedule here!

March 22, 2005
Update: A new story has been posted about the appearance of the Eaters! You can read it on the Turbine site here or in the Viamontian section here!

If you missed it the first time, you can read the account of Brayden Von Grohl, the man who originally killed the Eater, in the patch section here!

March 22, 2005
I know the news has been slow, but that doesn't mean I'm ignoring the site! I've been taking what would normally be my patch updating time to go back through the enormous backlog of old email and fill in missing bits and pieces on the older patch pages. I'm about 3/4 of the way through the year 2000. (I promise I'll get to the Gromnie Hides updates soon! ;-)

Read the Zone's Field Report on the Shadow Invasion; see some of the Nexus Key pieces; re-live the White Rabbit's first attacks after Wi's successful Buff the Bunny campaign!

Were you around during the assault on Frore or the Shadow Invasion? Did you track the spire movements and see Tufa destroyed? Were you responsible for releasing Bael'Zharon from his crystal prison? If you still have your old screenshots from the early days, send them in with your tales of adventure and I'll add them to the early patch pages!

March 18, 2005
A big welcome to Turbine's new Director of Community Relations, Jonathan "Calandryll" Hanna! You can read his introduction here!

Ibn has also announced that pre-order boxes will be arriving in some stores as early as next week! You can find out more here!

March 15, 2005
A few more pics of the Viamontian attack on FF added to the Infiltration events page here thanks to Hoggy!

Ibn posted an updat to the retail FAQ removing Amazon.com as one of the pre-order vendors. You can find out details here. In addition, Euro MasterCard is now accepted by Turbine Billing. Details can be found here!

March 14, 2005
The Viamontians have appeared again, this time on FF! Thanks to Mykell of FF for sending a pic and stats of the Viamontian Tribune from yesterday's attack! You can see it on the Infiltration events page here!

March 10, 2005
If you haven't seen it already, I've updated the Throne of Destiny expansion section with a scan of the advertisement in Computer Gamer magazine. You can see it here!

The Live Events page was also updated with the rescheduled Gladiator event and another account of a Holtburg Suvivor Champion!

March 9, 2005
The Viamontians attack! Click here to read VanDyne of MT's account and a copy of General Corcima's orders!

Update: Shadow Tempest of TD's report added!

Turbine has also published a Retail and Pre-order FAQ! You can find it here, or in the ToD section here!

March 7, 2005
The report from Day 2 of the weekend Fashion Show is now up here! Tahjn of HG, if you're out there, send me a pic of your outfit! Again, if your pic's not up there, it doesn't mean I don't love you, or that you're not notable! I tried to post as many of the vote getters up as I could, but couldn't fit everyone. Also, if I mangled your name, feel free to send me an email and I'll fix it!

A few more bits of news. Turbine is looking for a few people in the Los Angeles area for a focus group. You can find details here! Forced migration is also be temporarily disabled due to some credit card issues. Turbine is paying rent on in-game housing for the next month to avoid inconvenience for any players. Details are here!

Finally, reports of Eaters, new creatures from the Halaetan Isles, have been spotted on HG! You can read Brayden Von Grohl's account here!

March 6, 2005
The report from Day 1 of the weekend Fashion Show is now up here! Whew! Fashion overload! But it was great fun. Thanks to everyone who came out for it, and thanks to Jolt for hosting it! You guys looked great!

March 4, 2005
The final Blackmire quest has been revised into a full write-up with fabulous pics! You can see the Blackmire 4: Kivik Lir's Temple quest here! Once again, thanks to Miss Courtney, Bled Jr, U-Hauler, Lizzie al Borden, Arcturus, Cross eyed, Lurid Valor and Chungs mule for assistance!

A Fashion Show has been announced for this weekend! Haul out your favorite duds and take a walk down the Runway! Hope to see you there!

March 2, 2005
Another Blackmire quest has been revised into a full write-up with fabulous pics! You can see the Blackmire 5: Izji Qo's Temple quest here! Part 4 coming soon; five was just easier! ;-) Special thanks to U-Hauler, Lizzie al Borden, Chungs mule, Bled Jr, Cross eyed, Lurid Valor, and Miss Courtney for assistance!
