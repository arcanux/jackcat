Letters

Dec 31, 2000
 It's the last update for the Millennium!! I've added walkthrough for the Minor Atlan Stones to the Quest Section. Major Stones and Tools coming soon! (famous last words ;) 

As usual, I am so behind on mail that I may never catch up. (Almost 600 unanswered emails, so please forgive me if one is yours!) Thanks to everyone who sent such great stuff in to me. I'm working hard to plow through it and put everything up on the site. I'll do my best to give personal responses, but I'm afraid I may not be able to do so for everyone. If you don't get a response personally, well, it doesn't mean that I don't love you! ;-)

Have a safe and Happy New Year, everyone. See you next year!

Dec 30, 2000
 I'm back! No new update, but somehow, my code got screwy when I tried to change the Top 100 button at the bottom of the page. Things weren't showing up in Netscape and all kinds of havoc was going on. After endless fooling around with it, I've reverted the site back to an earlier version, so if you're still having trouble viewing it, let me know. 

I did add a few more locations to the Bargains section and will try to get back on track updating as soon as I get back in the groove. I hope everyone had a great holiday!

Dec 22, 2000
I'm heading out of town again for the holidays, and it's unlikely I'll have a chance to update while I'm gone. Have a safe and happy holiday and I'll see you when I get back! 

I wanted to try to get out another newsletter before leaving town, but you know how it goes! Instead, I've gone ahead and posted November 2nd's Newsletter on the site.

Dec 19, 2000
 The Child of Daralet event page has also been updated with some pics of the new crafted items. Special thanks to Aeros/Doh Jo-Sho for the great pics of the hides and items! Thanks also to Veritus Venatus for posing in a new Furry Matty Coat and to Yuuka for posing with her Dark Heart, one of the new Wisp Items. It's really cool looking!

The Rewards Table of the Exploration Society Letter Quests is filling out nicely thanks to all the contributions!. Thanks one again to Arisana and Sar Deliac of Solclaim for stats of all the Explorer Wands, and many of the Explorer Armor pieces. Russano the Wise and friends of Harvestgain also sent another batch of stats and pics. And thanks to Lipskid of FF for pics of the Explorer Yumi. I'm not sure how it happened, but there were almost no duplicates in the contributions people sent to me! It's really helped to fill things out.  

Dec 18, 2000
 Another update to the Rewards Table of the Exploration Society Letter Quests. Thanks one again to Arisana and Sar Deliac of Solclaim for filling out the Society Wand info, plus a few Society Armor pieces. 

Thanks also to Hereduth Margumsun of MT, Burimpu of TD and Kaitlynne Swanheart of MT for some more stats of both Society and Explorer Weapons.

Letters Section updated. Looks like everyone is looking for Minalim! November Letters archived. If you're interested in reading any of the old Letters, you can find them here.

Dec 16, 2000
 Exploration Society Letter Quests guide updated with the a few more stats. Thanks to Quixotic and Iron Chef Gharu for filling in the last few missing slots on the Rewards Table.

Special thanks also to Arisana and Sar Deliac of Solclaim, Veritus Venitas, Russano the Wise of Harvestgain, and Aeloryk the Explorer of Thistledown for pics and stats of some of the rewards!

Beasties updated with stats of the Plasma Golem and Ember courtesy of Akilah bint Ani & Tethys!

The Child of Daralet event page has also been updated with a pic of the Minalim plant thanks to Usagi Ryu-zan and a Sickly Wisp Heart from Veritus Venatus.

If you have info or pics of things I'm missing, please send me an email. You can check the Stuff I'd Like page for my Christmas Wish List! ;-)

Dec 15, 2000
 It's Newbie Love! I know it's a little soon after the patch, but I've gone ahead and put up a quest guide for the Exploration Society Letter Quests. There've been a lot of people who have ended up with a simple Mana Potion and were disappointed that they didn't get a more exciting reward for their hard won Letter.

Very special thanks to Kageyama and Iron Chef Gharu for initially compiling and coordinating the efforts on the boards! I have very few screenshots of the rewards at this point. If you've completed one of the Letter Quests, I'd love it if you could take a minute and send me an email with a screenshot of the stats and of you wearing/wielding your prize! Thanks!

Dec 13, 2000
 New Patch! Yay!! News! Bael'Zharon is free! And he's causing all kinds of havoc in the land of Dereth. Rivers of blood, death and destruction, dogs and cats living together, etc. etc. And those cheeky Town Criers are not only fezless, but they've learned to SPIT! My god! What is the world coming to?!

Visit The Child of Daralet pages in the Culture section to learn more about this fabulous patch!

Town Crier and Ulgrim Sayings updated.

A few new texts added to the Texts section, including the Book of Seasons and A Book of Quests for New Explorers.

Lockpick page also updated in the Bargains section with information from several towns.

Most exciting of all, Maggie the Jackcat is now level 35!

If you have cool pics or info from the latest patch, send me an email. 

Dec 8, 2000
Just wanted to let you know I'll be out of town for several days! Everyone play nice! :-)

Dec 7, 2000
 I've been kind of a slacker with this last patch, but I've finally upadated the Beasties page with stats of the new creatures from the Vesayen Isles! 

Cooking page updated with information from several towns in the Bargains section.

Dec 6, 2000
It's been almost 2 months since Danier sent me data about the quest for Baron's Amulet of Life Giving, but I finally have it up in the Quest Section. Read all about this quest here!

I also updated the Locksmith page with the numerous keys involved in the quest.

Dec 4, 2000
Ned Cleversun's Loyalty increases to 144! Congratulations to my vassal on Level 54! The gap widens to 20 levels between us! As usual, deaths also increase to 777.

Underground City and Mite Maze vendors added to Vendor Rates page. I also added the delicious Herbal Tea and Tempting Apple, which can be purchased in the Mite Maze from Alia Dunolmad, to the Food page.

Added various town information to the Alchemy Supplies, Arrows & Fletching Supplies, Components and Clothing pages. I know it's not the most exciting update in the world, but it should be a nice resource once it's finished! 

Dec 1, 2000
Just a small update for today: added Crater Village, Neydisa Castle and Holtburg to the Vendor Rates page. I hope to be finishing up the section soon.
