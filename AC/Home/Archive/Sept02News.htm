 September 30 2002
It's back to an old project: Pets section updated with pics for the Banderlings. And for Mites!! I know I recently had someone send me a great pic of Gertahr, and it will be added, just as soon as I dig it up.

September 29 2002
I've been reformed! The Letters section has been updated before the end of the month!! August Letters archived.

The Verdict patch page has also been updated with a few more pics and stats of the Harbinger battles. It also appears as though there are a few versions of the Harbinger. More details as they come in!

September 28 2002
Elysa appears in Yanshi and opens portals to some level restricted dungeons. And finally, we meet the Harbinger!! I don't know about you, but I kept expecting it to burst into a song and dance routine. ;-) Find out more on the Verdict patch page!

September 25 2002
As promised, the Gaerlan's Citadel quest page from the Verdict patch has been updated with more pics and stats of the new weapons. Right now you can see the Shield, Bow, Staff and Cestus (as well as the Robes and Wands from before). The write-up has also been cleaned up a bit. You'll also find links to two diagrams -- one of the Rolling Balls of Death by Sangre Fria of SC and one of the Floor Puzzle by Archmage Hunter of FF. Once again, thanks to everyone who keeps sending info in. More to come! :)

The Dereth Maps page was also updated with links to two new portal maps, one to Greeneye's new portal map over at AC Maps and one by Link from Hyrule of LC.

Finally, congratulations to my wonderful vassal, Ned Cleversun, who is now level 90!! His recent need for 8 more points towards his base bow skill has endangered tuskers everywhere. And has helped propel his mighty monarch (that's me ;-) to level 63!!

September 24 2002
Expect more buffing and fluffing of the Verdict patch information in the next few days. In the meantime, I've added the new bow stats to the Gaerlan's Citadel quest page. Thanks to Pervis of FF who caught a shot of Relix's prize!

September 23, 2002
The Transcript for tonight's Dev Chat is now available here.

September 23, 2002
Even more updates to the Gaerlan's Citadel quest page -- pics, stats and more! You can also visit the Verdict Patch Page for more information and fabulous screenshots! New Robes, new wands, a cool Empyrean sword, riddles and puzzles, rolling balls of death, lore galore... I think this just might be the best patch ever!!

September 22, 2002
Gaerlan has fallen on several servers. Some preliminary info is available on the the Gaerlan's Citadel quest page!

September 22, 2002
Details on the Gaerlan's Citadel quest with fabulous pics are now available. Thanks to everyone who sent in pics and info, especially Flubber's Ego of TD, Valinar of WE and Tyrian of WE!

Verdict Patch Page also updated with a pic of the Fire Hearth Crystal and more info on the town attacks!

September 21, 2002
For those of you following such things, last night the final Crystal Core fell on Harvestgain.

And it begins! Portals open in Eastham, Qalaba'r is under attack and Asheron appears at the Fallen Citadel. Visit the Verdict Patch Page for information!

September 20, 2002
 A few miscellaneous updates and more Martine Mask sayings added to the Verdict Patch Page.

September 19, 2002
Roll Out Article (with movie!), Developer's Notes, and Letter to the Players are now available here.

And it begins! Visit the Verdict Patch Page for updated information throughout the day. Town Crier rumors can be found here and Ulgrim sayings can be found here.

Update: Gaerlan Attacks! Visit the patch page for more!

September 19, 2002
It looks like those crazy kids were at it all night. Will Martine take the portal to Ispar? Will Gaerlan destroy the lifestones? Will Asheron kick Gaerlan's butt? I'm on the edge of my seat!! Visit the Cisterns page for the text of their continuing battle.

It's patch day!! Just a reminder to use the jackcat@spacestar.net for any screenshots. If you need instructions on how to take screenshots, visit the Screenshots & Mail Tips page! Thank you!

September 18, 2002
If you recall, when we left our story last night, Martine went off in a huff saying, "Nevermind... I'll do it myself!" Tonight, he faces Gaerlan in a sky spiltting showdown! Visit the Cisterns page for the text of their battle.

September 17, 2002
 Elysa's might proves to be no match for the PK hordes on TD. Visit the Cisterns page for updated details and pics on the battle at the Extreme Lightning Cistern Core!

September 16, 2002
Yesterday, Linkmage and Seltor the Conquerer of HG reported that a group of PKs were defending the Core on HG and TD. Today, Queen Elysa steps in and gives the traitorous defenders 2 hours to stop their folly... or else face her might!

Visit the Cisterns page for details!

September 14, 2002
The Lightning Core portal has opened in Dryreach (Levels 20-40)! Thanks to Icehydro of TD and XYZ for the head's up.

The Cisterns page has been updated with a few first hand reports, pics and the text from Martine!

September 12, 2002
I know there's been a lack of updates lately. I've been home sick for the last few days, and have also been working on putting my AC2 site together. But I do have an update that's looonnngg overdue.  Ned Cleversun's death total increases to 1492 and his loyalty increases three points to 157!! ;-) Congratulations to my vassal on reaching level 89 as well!

September 8, 2002
Quest section has been updated with the Orb of the Bunny Booty. In the mood for a good bunny smite? Then this page is for you! The White Rabbit also drops the carcass for your hippity-hoppity white bunny slippers!

September 4, 2002
It gets worse and worse every month; I fall further and further behind. (It *is* summer, after all! ;-) August Letters published in the Letters section!! You'll find A Song For Maggie, praise for google, quest updates, and the answer to why Ned Cleversun is so old.

September 3, 2002
If it's not one thing, it's another. Thanks to a thunderstorm my modem got fried (even with a nice UPS thingie), just as I was coming upstairs to shut down the computer. But I'm back!

Thanks to Cyrano de Bergerac, I have a beyoooteeful set of purple Covenant armor! I can't quite wear it all yet, but after shaking off my perpetual elemental vitae and dumping some xp into my defenses, I'll be sporting it in no time! Thanks Cyrano!

And now... ta da! To stay the steady stream of email asking me when the heck I'm going to start my AC2 site, I've decided to go ahead and make the URL public. Introducing Maggie the Jackcat's Dereth Living - a guide to Asheron's Call 2. That's right! There's nothing there!! But there will be in the future, of course. More coming soon!! ;)

No, I will not be quitting AC when AC2 comes out and I will still update this site. *anticipates the next barrage of mail!* ;-)
