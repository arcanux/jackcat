March 28, 2001
Happy Birthday to me! Happy Birthday tooooo meeeeeee! :) For those of you who don't get my newsletter, I just wanted to take a minute to thank you for making the first full year of Maggie the Jackcat's Kick-Ass Fabulous AC Web Site so fun! You're the best!

Letters updated! I've posted a boat-load of them, and still feel like I haven't even made a dent. Read more on Death Items, Dead Shopkeepers, Gragghk the Mosswart, Belly Buttons, and recent charges against Maggie the Jackcat! ;-)

Thanks to Angry Rooster of Thistledown and Tyler C who both wrote to me about the new Sclavus helmet. It also changes its trim color based on the color of your gloves! Totally groovy.

March 26, 2001
Just minor, not worth mentioning corrections to the site. The Oscars were on, after all! I did update the Stuff I'd Like page with a few missing items. If you've got pics of the new hides in particular, I'd love it if you could send me a screenshot. Thanks!

March 24, 2001
Some small tweaks to the Spring's Sorrows patch info, including types of Sclavus for Hides, and where to take your Fang for a new Mace. I also noted on the Mnemosyne quest page that it's been confirmed by Troop that the Arrowheads were left out of this month's patch due to tech reasons and that we can probably expect the rewards to be tweaked in the next patch. Thanks also to Virvius for pointing out a typo and for the stats of the Wily Monouga.

March 23, 2001
More patch info! See the new Booties which change their accent colors according to your gloves! Thanks to Eric the Unready for posing with the Boots and Catri for clarifying their cool properties! And thanks to Gheres Reficul for filling out the Mnemosyne rewards. Thanks also to Lycius, Magic Mike, Gaidheil of Harvestgain, Clark W., guillaume & Shimmerglow of Leafcull for creature stats, small corrections and clarifications (such as Virinidi Executor, not Executioner! ;-) and pics!

March 22, 2001
New Patch! Yay! Lizard Heads! Evil Clowns! Little Green Triangle thingies!! To find out more about the Spring's Sorrows event, visit my event page here. I also tossed up a quick quest guide for the Mnemosynes. updated!

I'm still gathering data for the other uses. If you have info I'm missing, please send me an email! And I'd love to get some pics of those scaly boots. Those Town Criers can't stop talking about them!

March 21, 2001
I've posted the February newsletter for your enjoyment while the servers are down for the patch. You can find it here!

March 19, 2001
All that work on the Shopping section has turned my mind once again to fashion! I thought I'd catch up with another Runway participant to see Where Are They Now? Read all about Roberto-Eduardo and the tragedy that occurred on the route to obtaining his Greater Shadow Armor! All I can say is... ouch!!

I also decided it's time to clean up some of the older pages and update them a bit. I started with the Head  page of the Armor section. These pages are only meant to be a sampling of the armor available in the game. As an additional note, I don't actually physically have these pieces on my mules. So please, don't ask me to twink or trade! ;-)

I say this quite often, but think it bears repeating in case you've missed my earlier explanations about my bulging Inbox. Thank you to everyone who has sent me mail. It's become almost impossible for me to keep up with it and to respond to everyone individually.  If you don't hear back from me, it doesn't mean that I don't love you! I appreciate all the helpful tips, letters and suggestions, so please keep them coming. I try hard to update the site with your suggestions and corrections and will work to get more of the content you request up soon.

March 17, 2001
The Vendor Rates page is now complete! (With the exception of the Lich Archmage, because I can't seem to find where I saved my data on my hard drive! ;-) The Bargains Supply pages have had the data from a few more towns added thanks to Dragon Warrior, Rhys Edn of Solclaim and Abbott. 

March 15, 2001
Big news! Ned Cleversun's death total tops 1000! After reading my letter on death items he commented, "I'll tell you something... few players understand death as well as Ned Cleversun." ;-) And Congratulations on Level 62! Although I've been on a killing rampage, Ned Cleversun has still managed to increase the level gap to 22. But as they say, a rising tide floats all boats. For the first time ever, Ned Cleversun has surpassed the Loyalty bar first set by Danier: up a point to 148! Thanks Ned. You're the best vassal EVER!!

I've finished putting up the remainder of the Starter quests, with the addition of Yanshi & Yaraq. Since I researched the quests before some of the changes made in the last patch or two, some of the values for items may be incorrect (most of the "free" ones were changed to 0). In addition, you are now given an announcement for experience gained. If you notice any of these minor errors, please drop me an email.

A few other minor errors corrected. I revised my Pea explanation to indicate that you only get 20 each (not 50) for scarabs and talismans. Thanks to Fu' Leng of the Elder clan for the correction. Thanks also to Gheres Reficul for pointing out that my Crystal-tip arrows & quarrels were pointing to my hard drive. The icons should now be appearing. I also corrected the titles of a few pages thanks to Gadaric. I know there are broken links in the Maps section (mainly because of the CoD revamp!) and plan to correct those in the near future. I appreciate the help keeping the site accurate as possible! I couldn't do it without you! 

March 12, 2001
New Quest! I went on the Shendolain Vestibule quest weeks ago with Gheres Reficul and his allegiance, and finally wrote up our adventures! Sorry for the delay guys. But it *was* 3AM. ;-) Find out how to make your pretty purple see-through shield here! Screenshots included!

Gheres also made the ultimate sacrifice so that I could finish out the stats on my Caulnalain page by turning his Bow into a Crossbow. He even made the quarrels! Thanks Gheres!

Letters updated. Lots of newbie-ish questions have been coming in lately. Read information on Death Items, Arcane Lore and Peas.

As many of you know, I've been working on filling out my Arts & Crafts Section. A conversation with Rim the Dragonfly in Lytelthorpe the other day prompted me to finally add the long planned Gallery section. So far, it houses some of the Aluvian art work scattered throughout Dereth. View these beautiful paintings here!

March 11, 2001
More towns added to the Vendor Rates page: Xarabaydun, Lytelthorpe, Nanto & Sawato. The Bargains section, with information on vendor inventories and best places to buy, is almost complete! 

March 8, 2001
I've pretty much completed the surveys of the Vendor Rates & Inventories, thanks to the assistance of many, many helpful people. Like Baron Dorian Gray of Leafcull who recently sent me complete information on Fort Tethana, right down to suggestions on the best place to sell random weapons loot (at the bowyer). I'll be adding the rest of the missing towns in the near future. 

But since I now have the updated list of all the vendors and NPCs compiled, I finally added the Crafters & Collectors information under the Arts & Crafts section!! I know many of you have been asking for this and have sent piles of screens & data to me. There are too many of you for me to try and give appropriate credit with any sort of accuracy, so I'll just say a quick thank you here. You know who you are! :-)  

March 7, 2001
A few more starter quests are now up in the quest section: Samsur & Shoushi. Learn all about the Four Stones of Jojii!

March 6, 2001
In an effort to try to finish the Bargains section, I've added the Bandit Castle & Uziz Vendor Rates and inventories for the various Bargains sections. Also, thanks to Min for pointing out a typo in the Peas section for the splitting tool at Kryst and to Lai Tan of WE (among others) for letting me know that Skeleton Masks, unlike the other creature masks from the Hollow Victory patch, can still be made! 

And I've been promising Danier for a while that I would update Sugardaddy's stats on his Where Are They Now page. A level 53 mule with only 6 health! He's probably got one of the highest Cooking skills on Thistledown. Just don't let him near a rabbit! ;-)

March 5, 2001
Letters updated! Thanks for all the letters on the PK story. I'll make sure to pass on your appreciation to my wonderful vassal, Ned Cleversun. (who took my "screams like a girl" quite graciously :-)

March 4, 2001
I hate it when my vassal makes me admit he was right. So I killed him!

Read all about Maggie the Jackcat and Ned Cleversun's PK adventure here!

I also added a few more of the Starter Town quests to the Quest Section: Lytelthorpe, Nanto & Rithwic.
