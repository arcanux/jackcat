I know I've once again been behind on updates, but... exciting news!! (For me, anyway.) I *finally* got high speed Internet via satellite out in the boonies where I live. Of course then I had to do a little upgrading on the home machine, set up a wireless network, reinstall software, etc. That ate up most of my limited computer free time for the past week or two. And once I figured out how to get AC installed with IE7 on my machine, I ran out of time to test it out, so I still haven't played on my new connection! Keep your fingers crossed. :)

I hope with all this computer goodness that I'll be able to get back into a more regular routine of updating the site. As I like to say, more coming soon! Including several rares that have been sitting in my inbox for a week. Thanks, as always, to everyone who continues to send in information!
