March 12, 2001 - Death Item

Maggie, 
I'm trying to find information about Death Items.  Some robes are always lost and I need to know more about it.  Let me know...

Zorball
Solclaim
Level 33 Warrior and Mage

Hi Zorball -
When people refer to Death Items, they usually mean items of high value that they carry hoping those items will be selected to be lost, instead of their regular, crucial every-day armor & weapons. I don't remember exactly how death items are calculated, but the higher your level, the more items you will lose. Items are also categorized (weapons, items, armors, etc.) and you'll tend to lose one of the highest valued items from the different categories. 

Mage Master Robes are popular death items because they are lightweight and they are a very high value. Plus they are easily replaceable. The new Recall Gems to the Master Robe Archmages are also good death items for the same reasons. Some people carry useless (to them) pieces of armor with absurdly high values (nearly flawless pyreal greaves with Strength VI, Coor VI, Imp VI, Rank 8+ -- value: 46,974p) or pieces of jewelry, or some outrageously expensive footwear, like Sara Truflight's Armoredillo Hide shoes.

After my recent death in Chakron when I lost my purple Hauberk, my purple Leggings, my purple Boots and my good Sword (OUCH!), I was given a bag of C notes with explicit instructions that I wasn't allowed to go on any more quests which involved jumping and nearly-impossible recovery areas without a good selection of death items. (Thanks Danier! ;-) I'd just like to take this opportunity to thank Laurianna and her fine allegiance for escorting me back through Chakron for a 2nd time to recover my corpse. And Ned Cleversun thanks you too. He loves his Amulet! 

I hope that's what you were looking for! If anyone sends in more explicit details on the the selection process, I'll be sure to pass it on!

March 12, 2001 - Arcane Lore & Explorer Items


Do i need arcane lore to use the Ring of Endurance. I am specialized in Meelee Defense, Healing, Run, Sword. Can you help?
Latimus

Latimus
Darktide
Level  6


Hi Latimus -
You don't have to be specialized or trained in Arcane Lore to use the items, but you do need an Arcane Lore of 15. The Difficulty of the item is the required Arcane Lore to activate the item's spells. If you don't have Arcane Lore trained or specialized, you cannot add points directly to the skill. However, you can raise it by putting points into Focus. If you put your mouse over any skill, you'll see the formula appear in the top left of your screen. The Arcane Lore skill is calculated by Focus divided by 3. So for every three points of Focus you raise, your Arcane Lore skill will raise by 1. I hope that helps!

- Maggie the Jackcat
