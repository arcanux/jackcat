May 29, 2001
New Quest! After my third time (well, actually 4th, if you count the time I died ;-) through Chakron Flux, I've finally written up the quest guide to the Virindi Servant's Amulet & Fragment of the Singularity (used for the Sword of Lost Hope quest). Screenshots of the quest included! Special thanks to the allegiances of Danier, Laurianna & Garibaldi di Alia!

I also sent out another newsletter the other day. If you signed up for the mailing list and didn't receive it, please send me an email.

May 23, 2001
It's another Tip from my vassal, Ned Cleversun! He wrote me email, saying he liked this month's Letters maybe better than all the other months! I think it's because I published a really nice letter about *him*! ;-) 

May 21, 2001
Lugians, Lugians, Lugians! I've finally transferred the info from last month's patch page and written up the Arm, Mind, Heart (Lugian Armor) quest! 

As a little extra bonus, I added a page of screen shots of the amazing Linvak Tukal, complete with Hoary Matty and real stained glass! It's amazingly pretty.

The Gallery was also updated with the Lugian painting found in Linvak Tukal.

There are even Lugians in the Letters! I've published a few more, including a couple about the Lugian quest from last patch, some info on the missing NPC for the Explorer Quest Add-On, and a correction to my response to Iron Savant. There *is* a Taxi Service on Morningthaw! I think I'm having a little server jealousy! ;-) 

Last, I've updated the Yellow Pages with the new address for ACExplorer and ACTracker. I also added a couple of new links. For those who made the mistake of using Ned Cleversun's ACTrack #, here is the correct one:
0 x 0 0 6 6 5 f 6 c

May 21, 2001
Letters updated!

Thanks to Josef for pointing out a mistake on my sloppy updating of the Fletching Steps page! Wrapped Bundles of Greater Arrowheads are, of course, made with the Concentrated Oils as mentioned on the Patch Page, not regular Oils. I also added a link to the Arrows & Quarrels page in the Sports section for those folks looking for the stats and damage of the Arrows/Quarrels. 

Thanks to Dyteth for sending the missing Collector reward for a Jungle Wasp Wing and to Areiela of Morningthaw who found out the hard way that Lou Ka, although assumed to be a Wing Collector, gives rewards based on Collector values. He's been moved on the Crafters & Collectors page accordingly. 

The Changing of the Ways patch page was updated a bit more with information on the 24 hour timer for the Titles Quest, a list of portals found in the bottom of the Desert March dungeon (outside of  Danby's Outpost), a bit on Pack Space Love, and a mention of the new Spear Quest (believed to be broken) with a pic of the Token, courtesy of Markus Ragnoroc. 

I also added the following names with my thanks for sending information about this month's update: Dru Down, Frito D'Mojo, Morris, Rhydel, Baxlax, Terenya, Eleeos, Disturbed, MeiHarada, and ronin arcane
May 19, 2001
Thanks to the amazing skills of Sugardaddy, the Sad Mule of Doom, I've updated the Alchemy and Fletching sections with the new patch info. Visit the Alchemy Formulas page to learn how to make condense Infusions and Oils, and the Fletching Items and Fletching Steps pages for info on the new Wrapped Arrowheads and Shafts.

I've had a report that Golden Gromnies *will* disappear after about 12 minutes, unlike the other pack dolls. Please use caution! This is not something I plan to verify myself. ;-)

Gheres Reficul also reports that unlocked Virindi keys may now be given away! Yay!

Ned Cleversun has calculated ACTracker's updated memory address: [this was wrong!]. He sent me an extended explanation of why the program is still useful, even though death coordinates are given in-game; however, I will spare you all. If it's wrong, blame him! ;-)

May 17, 2001
Patch day! Yay!!  As Ned Cleversun overheard in Ayan Baqur, the "ground grubbers" will be going broke! No longer will people be dumping stacks of pyreal on the ground to lighten the load before heading out to hunt! Pyreals now have a burden of 0! 

Stop by The Changing of the Ways patch page to find out more about this latest update! A new Town, new titles, a new text, death coordinates, buff and debuff tweaks, Fletching love and evil pink bunnies! Best news of all: "Pack toys no longer decay when left on the ground." Yay again! This just might be the best patch ever!!

As always, for Town Crier Rumors click here.

Vendor Rates updated with the rates from the Danby's Outpost vendors. Crafters page updated with the new Leather Crafter as well. Alchemy Formulas updated with the new Aqua icon and info on Condensed Infusions.

May 11, 2001
Just a few minor updates before I head out of town for a few days. The A Reign of Stone patch page was updated yet again with a few more pics. Darthmaull poses with the Staff of Clarity and The Lion Heart shows off the color changing properties of the Sleeves of Inexhaustability. Special thanks to Gui Szuhu of Harvestgain for info on the sleeves.

Thanks also to Pippy II for pics of the new Shadow Armor gem icons.

Last, but certainly not least, a new tip from my wonderful vassal, Ned Cleversun.

Letters updated with information on Yuan Hanzu's new location, a tidbit from the Hieromancer's quest and more Dye information courtesy of Danier and his Sad Mule of Doom, Sugardaddy (who arguably has the highest Cooking skill on the server... and the lowest health! :-).

I added a fewpics of the Mattekar Arm Guard to the A Reign of Stone patch page. Find out about its neato-cool color changing properties!

Thanks to Eldridge for sending the stats of the Soulrender Quarrels. Also, thanks to lauriel, Felix, Josh M., Shard, Rathrot for some of the updated stats on the Atlans.

It's been a long time coming, but I've finally updated the stats for the Atlan Weapons. I'm still missing some of the Fine stats and I'm pretty sure the Mace stats are still outdated. If you have stats I'm missing, please send me an email!
