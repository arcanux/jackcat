 April 30, 2004
I'm still hard at work filling in submissions for my housing section and filling out Monarchy information. Strangely enough, I haven't had a single Darktide resident volunteer their residential information. Huh. Must be chicken.

(Just kidding!!! Please don't kill me!!! ;-)

April 29, 2004
The new housing section is a hit! I'm still working on adding resident submissions! Keep them coming! :)

You can see how the section works by looking at the Hero's Vale settlement page! There are residents from three different servers listed and a Harvestgain allegiance, the Legends of Cragstone, occupies 5 of the 6 villas in the settlement!

If you'd like to add your name as a resident to any housing settlement, simply go to that settlement page and use the email link in the right column to send your GAME NAME & SERVER to me. If you live in a non-unique named "Villa" or "Cottage" you'll also have to include the coordinates. (Monarchy info is still in progress.)

Ibn has sent word that the zone is experiencing technical difficulties. If you're having trouble getting in game, try using the Quick Launch link. The tentative date for the May event has also been announced: Wednesday May 12, 6:00 AM - 11:00 AM PDT!

April 27, 2004
Update: Letter to the Players for May is up here or in the Mired Hearts patch section here! Turbine has also increased its customer service with the Fuze Q&A Suite as its knowledge base provider. You can read the Press Release & announcement here!

Several months ago, you may remember me asking for help with some boring, tedious research! Real life circumstances put things on hold for a bit, but I'm happy to announce yet another new addition to the site: housing information!! You can find information on all the housing settlements in Dereth under the Tourism section! Click on a settlement name for # of units, trophy item needed to purchase, shortest route, closest town and lifestone, and here's the fun part, a list of residents (currently awaiting your submissions)!

The residential information is strictly voluntary (unless you own a mansion)! I thought it would be fun for players to have somewhere to publicly post where they live. (For example, I live here!) If you'd like to add your name as a resident to any housing settlement, simply go to that settlement page and use the email link in the right column to send your GAME NAME & SERVER to me. If you live in a non-unique named "Villa" or "Cottage" you'll also have to include the coordinates.

My volunteers also collected the names of all mansion owners and their allegiance information for me. As I loooong ago mentioned, I was putting my Monarchy section on hold until I re-envisioned what I wanted to do with it. Now, when you pull up Mansion records, the owner of the mansion will be listed, along with a link to allegiance information in my Monarchy section. (If your allegiance is based at a Villa, it may also be listed.) Only a small fraction of this information has been filled in so far, so if you don't see your Monarchy listed, it may still be slated to be added. Once I get all my information uploaded into the database and the Monarchy section filled out, I'll be posting requests for verification a server at a time. Until then, please hold off sending allegiance information!

Special thanks to Arboc of SC for coding this section! I tell him what I want to do and he makes it happen! He figured out the tricky problem of how to deal with those sneaky settlements with both Cottages and Villas! Thanks to my many volunteers who collected info for this section, and especially Furay the Less of HG and Svetlana the Homeless of HG!
