August 31, 2001
I've started work on the next level of Recommended Hunting Areas. You can find a partial list of suggestions for levels 10-20 here. I'll try to get the rest of them up in the next day or so. There were a *lot* of great suggestions for these levels! 

August 29, 2001
Ta-da!!! The Runway reopens with a fabulous display of fashion by Dykstra of Thistledown! Yay! 

I also finished adding the Renalim pics to the Dyes page. Thanks to the amazing Mule of Doom, Sugardaddy, for crushing up some plants for me to photograph. Thanks also to Prox for yet more Colban plant locations! You can also see a sampling of dyed armors on the Dye Poses page. More coming soon! (Just as soon as I can figure out where I put them! ;-)

I also wanted to thank everyone who has sent mail. I am, as usual, *very* behind on the mail, but I am getting your letters and hope to get everything up on the site asap. I apologize for the lack of personal responses to some of you.

August 26, 2001
Letters updated! Ned Cleversun's latest Tip, as usual, generated quite a few letters. You can read suggestions from Wryan of Solclaim and Immacolata of Wintersebb. Ned Cleversun even updated his tip as a result! You'll also find a few nice letters on the Hieromancer's Armor.

Misspelling on the Yellow Pages corrected after 2 attempts! 

Thanks to Eckie of Wintersebb for suggesting a few missing Abbreviations (e.g. SIK) in the Odds 'n Ends section. I also added a few more pronunciations while I was working on the page.

Satyricon of Wintersebb sent me several corrections, including some  level restrictions on the Coordinates page and a broken link. Both Saytricon and anidasle pointed out some outdated information on the Magic Research page.

Fossil U of TD pointed out a typo in the Tibri's Fire Spear write-up, and both Butiku and Iseri Shasar reminded me to update the Grievver acid drop info on the Hieromancer's Quest.

Thanks to Sion of Leafcull for adding another work of art to the Gallery: a wall painting found at the Ithnaec Cathedral!

Last, Mindless dconfused pointed out yet another correction needed for the Shadow Armor page! 

Phew! Only 3,108 emails to go! More coming soon!!! ;-)

August 24, 2001
Yellow Pages updated. I've added many of the sites I've featured in previous newsletters for easier reference.

August 22, 2001
I've started work on the Recommended Hunted Areas in the Sports Section. For now, you can find suggestions for Levels 1-10. More coming soon! ;-) If you'd like to add comments, make corrections, or suggest a new hunting area, please use this form.  

August 21, 2001
What could be better than patch information and quest write-ups? That's right! It's another Tip from my wonderful vassal, Ned Cleversun: Tip for using keyboard commands in spellcasting.

August 20, 2001
More rewards added to the Tumerok Banner Quest. You can find the full table of Banners & Rewards here. Full quest write-ups now available for both Banners & Assault Weapons.

August 18, 2001
Yes, even more updates to the Crests of a Turbulent Sea Patch Page! You can now see screenshots of at least one of each of the 6 Clan Banners being wielded. I also starting moving the data over to the Quests section. Full write-ups to come for Assault Weapons and Tumerok Banners. In addition, you can find a table of the possible Banners and their rewards. It's still got a lot of holes, so I'd love any help to fill in the gaps. 

August 17, 2001
More updates to the Crests of a Turbulent Sea Patch Page! I haven't had time to go through all the email with Tumerok Locations, stats and drop information, but I'll try to get that done soon. Starter Yaoji added to the Starter Weapons, and the Professions page has been updated with the new templates! Thanks again to everyone sending information in!!

August 16, 2001
Patch Page updated! More pics and stats added, more info on locations. Fixed (I hope) most of the typos and errors -- Axe & Spear corrected, Reinforced Haft pic corrected, Tokens, Symbols, etc. added. Page may take a while to load. I still may be missing a few credits, but I'm working through the email bit by bit. Big thanks to everyone who sent info on the patch!

Big update to the Crests of a Turbulent Sea patch page! Learn more about the Banners and locations of the 6 Tumerok Tribes and the new Assault Weapons! You can also see several more examples of finished banners, thanks to Edain of Leafcull and Loup Noir of Frostfell among others. 

I'm still missing pics of a few of the banners, Spear Heads, Crests, Symbols and Coins. If you have any info, stats or pics of these items or other finished banners, please email them to jackcat@spacestar.net. Thanks! I also wanted to say thank you to Ronin arcane for escorting my level 1 self on my journey to meet Enclave.

The locations of the 9 Towers have now been personally confirmed. Several of the coordinates were incorrect. Thanks to everyone who pointed out the errors. Starter Shou-ono added to the updated Starter Weapons!! :-)

I also wanted to thank everyone who has filled out the Recommended Hunting Area form. The response was really incredible. It will take me a while to go through them, but feel free to keep them coming!

August 15, 2001
Patch day! Yay! Virindi Towers! New Quiddity sporting Tumeroks! Newbie Love! Banners! Find out more in the Crests of a Turbulent Sea patch page (contains spoilers). And as always, Ulgrim sayings and Town Crier Rumors updated. 

August 14, 2001
I usually try to have something up for people to read while the servers are down for the new patch (yay!), but sadly, I got home from work very late this evening. If you have a little extra time on your hands, however, please consider filling out this form for a future section on the site: Recommended Hunting Areas! 

I get so many letters a week from people asking for this sort of information that I've long wanted to create an area of the site to refer them to. I know there are a lot of experienced players out there who have a wealth of knowledge to share. 

*curses last week's stomach flu, knowing there's no way calling in sick will be believed tomorrow!!* ;-)

As always, all patch information appreciated. For screeshots, please use the following email address: jackcat@spacestar.net (it's able to handle larger files). Thanks!

August 12, 2001
Letters updated! Enter the Dragon of
Harvestgain offers a nifty shortcut to the Warden for the Shackles of Obedience. I also answer a question I get asked many times a week: can I link to your site? And Ned Cleversun steps in to answer just how he managed to hit an Umbris for 606 points of damage! July Letters archived.

Thanks also to Athelred of WE/HG for pics and stats of the last Quiddity Weapon I was missing: Mace of the Quiddity. Beasties page (page takes a while to load) updated with all the creatures from the Lost in the New Horizon patch page, plus stats on the Repair Golem, the Smithing Golem and Nexus Fragment stats from Win Lee and Tomoki of Thistledown! More coming soon! ;-)

August 10, 2001
BIG news!! My wonderful vassal, Ned Cleversun, turned Level 70 last night! All those Umbris really add up! We had a small ceremony while he celebrated by dumping over 4,000,000 xp into his new Creature Magic! Yay! He tells me he is going to retire from power-leveling and become a grief player full time. ;-)

All that skill didn't come without a cost. Death total increases by 56 to 1251. 

August 7, 2001
Thanks to all the Runway volunteers so far. My goal is to have the section back in force by the end of the month. Just a few miscellaneous updates. A few towns added to the Vendor Rates. Thanks to Cylyria the Mage of FF and Bane Grievver of MT for filling out the icon and stats of the Quiddity Axe. Many spelling and typo corrections and a few broken links repaired. 

August 3, 2001
I've been working through some email and have done a few miscellaneous updates. Thanks to Danier for all the screen shots from the last patch. I haven't really had a chance to throw more of them up, but the pic to the right is from his contributions. 

Thanks to Josiah I of Morningthaw for a copy of the Forgotten Text found in Xarabaydun and the Standing Orders Translation. 

Wohc of Thistledown also contributed a few texts: Brief Letter translated froma Damp Scroll and Translated Letter from a Tear Stained Parchment. 

Updated Summer version of the Book of Seasons also added.

Prox sent a few more coordinates for Colban plants.

Thanks to Forceful Chris of Solclaim for pointing out an error on the Celdon Shadow Armor stats!! This has always been one of the TOP viewed pages of the site I was amazed it hadn't been caught before now! Thanks Forceful Chris.

Thanks also to Steve for mentioning that making potions only kills you if you have exactly the number of health points required to make the potion. Tip on the Alchemy page updated accordingly.

Baz-al-Araj  Thistledown suggested I mention that the Rubies for the Silifi of Crimson Stars can only be activated with an Axe skill of 280+.

I corrected the description for the Bandit Shield as well. Thanks to everyone who pointed out the error. The dangers of copying and pasting!

July New Stuff archived.

Last, as I've mentioned, I plan to start doing Runway updates again. Because so many of the Runway volunteer requests are soooo outdated and many of the people I've tried to contact either no longer play, or have re-rolled and started someone new, I've decided to start from scratch taking Runway Volunteers. It was just the easiest solution I could think of to handle it. That means if you've previously sent a request in, you'll need to re-send one, even if you asked just last week. I will do my best to try and start with people who have already requested being featured, and apologize in advance to anyone who gets overlooked. Remember that good pages highlight not just fashion, but interesting stories and personalities. Any information about your character or your game-play that you'd like to add in your letter is much appreciated.
