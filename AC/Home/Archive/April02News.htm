 April 29, 2002
The site experiences a temporary transformation in honor of Ned Cleversun's achieving Level 80! It's All Ned Cleversun, All the Time!

April 27, 2002
Small update to the Letters section. Maggie hints at exciting impending changes to the site!

April 22, 2002
More updates to some of the newbie quests. Holtburg Starter Quests and Brogord's Axe complete.

I'd also like to just briefly remind people to keep their virus software up to date. There seems to be a *lot* of viruses making the rounds. One of the viruses going around chooses a random name out of the infected person's address book and uses that as the mask for the from field, then sends it to everyone in their book. Since I'm in a lot of people's address books, people have been reporting they've received infected mail with my name in the from field. This mail is not actually coming from me; I'm just the random name that got pulled from the person's address book. I will never send you mail with an attachment unless it's something we've discussed ahead of time. If you get mail like this in your inbox, delete it immediately.

April 19, 2002
 Letters updated! So I'm not back to once a week, but more than once a month has got to be an improvement! Danier explains the changes in Item Magic, updated newbie quests, a wedding story *and* an attempt to solo marriage! Dereth is full of wonders! Plus a letter from a very special person: my vassal, who explains that yes, Maggie does play AC! ;-)

Thanks to everyone who responded to the Stuff I'd Like requests. I got some really great stuff! I haven't updated it fully yet, but I do know that I'm missing many of the stats from the active Bronze Statues if you'd still like to help out. Thanks again!

April 17, 2002
 Yaraq Starter Quests updated. Bronze Cragstone's Axe added to the Bronze Weapons page, courtesy of Ryo-ohki Kiyone of MT. Since so many people have written to me asking how to make a Wedding Cake, I added the Wedding Planner items to the Wedding Quest page (it should be self-explanatory from there). Various minor items added here and there, and Stuff I'd Like partially updated.

April 16, 2002
 Interior Decorating page updated with this month's housing items, including the sunflowers. The various Sports pages have been updated with Bronze Weapons and Bouquets! I'm still missing a few of the Bronze Weapon stats and stats of the attacking statues. If you have them, please send me an email! Map of Gelidite Library added to quest page, thanks to Gunner D of HG!

April 15, 2002
Another starter quest update: Yanshi Starter Quests. I also corrected the updated stats on Miyako's Moonstone (which was upgraded to Armor Self IV from Regen I).

April 14, 2002
Information on the Betrayal event is still coming in! Most elixirs have been added to the Gelidite Library/Nuhmudira quest page, and I've written up the details on the Bronze Weapons dropped from the attacking statues. Thanks once again to everyone who sent information in!

April 13, 2002
Starter quests were buffed with enhanced rewards this event. Nanto Starter Quests updated. More to come!

April 12, 2002
The Wedding Trials quest write-up is now complete -- fabulous screenshots included! Additions made to the Gelidite Library/Nuhmudira quest and the Thorsten Cragstone's Armor & Axe quest. Real Estate guide updated with Residential Quarters info, plus more Settlement Portals added.

April 11, 2002
More updates from the Betrayal event. Gelidite Library quest page updated with information on amulets and elixirs. Updates to the Thorsten Cragstone's Armor & Axe and the Wedding Trials still to come. I've started updated the newest Settlement Portals as well.

Thanks once again to the many contributors this month! If you don't yet see your name up in lights, it's because I still have a huge batch of emails and screenshots to weed through. As always, more coming soon! :-)

April 10, 2002
Quick update to the Betrayal patch page. Information on Deadly Hollow Weapons now added thanks to Dark Topaz of WE and Padre-Adamo of SC! More coming soon!

April 10, 2002
What a patch! I've added lots of information to the the Betrayal patch page and there's still more to come! New quests, the return of weddings, new lore, residential quarters, a new and improved Ulgrim with fancy emoting action, *plus* endurance love. I think this just might be the best patch ever!

Now, must sleep. :) Thanks to everyone who sent information in. There was so much of it, I didn't get everything up yet! I'll have to finish things up over the next day or two.
