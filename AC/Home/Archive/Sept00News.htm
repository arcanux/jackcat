September 13, 2000
I logged this evening and immediately received this tell: "MAGGIE! AHHHHHHHHH! Is this not the greatest patch ever or what?" I agree with The Green Flash wholeheartedly! Turbine has done it again! Three new types of creatures, the long awaited Secure Trade, a Fashion update with new tasks for the cooks and the alchemists. More quests, dungeons, effects and even a giant Monouga named Paul!

Click here to read the latest rumors from those cheeky Town Criers. And is it my imagination, or have they put on a little weight? ;-) Ulgrim has plenty of new things to say as well. My favorite items, the delightful crockery, got a little love. Take your next enemy down with a lovely ornamental bowl!

To top it all off, mayhem and destruction!!! Tufa and Arwic have been DESTROYED! Wheeeeeee!!!

To read all about the latest patch, visit my Twilight's Gleaming page in the Culture Section.

Yes, there's a new fez too. :)
