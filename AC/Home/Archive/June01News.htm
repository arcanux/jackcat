It's a new Ned Cleversun Tip! This time for Life Mages.

June 24, 2001
I know it's been days since I've done an update. It's just because I've been occupied with other things lately. (!!!!!!!)

*jumps up and down with excitement!*

I did add a few of the items I've received lately: Shendolain Crossbow Stats, the Sunstone Geode to the Heiromancer's Armor (no-drop, no-give  & on one week timer), and the Carved Singularity Key to the Locksmith page. Thanks to everyone for sending the shots in. I've updated the Stuff I'd Like page accordingly! :) 

Thanks also to Baxlax for the stats of the Southern Black Claw Raider for the Quiddity Orb quest. I had also incorrectly identified the fellow holding the orb. That was Gunko. Sorry Gunko!

Last, if anyone happened to get screenshots of the Virindi Townspeople before they were killed inside the dungeon and returned to Ayan Baqur, I'd love for you to send them to me. It all happened so fast, noone seems to have documented the event. Thanks!
