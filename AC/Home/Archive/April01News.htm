April 29, 2001
Continuing with my desire to flesh out the Arts & Crafts section, I've added a guide to Dyeing. Special thanks to Rim the Dragonfly and to Danier for their help with this section, and to Auralyn for sharing her remarkable collection of dyed dresses. In addition to the Dyes page, you'll also find information on Plant Locations.

April 27, 2001
I've updated the A Reign Of Stone page with a few more little items: the Obsidian Shard, Orb of Clarity and the Lugian Crest. Thanks to Malacon Wolfsun for sending the pic along to me. I also corrected a few typos in some of the stats.

@commands was updated with the @consent commands which allow others to loot your corpse!

Vendor Rates updated with the new Lugian Vendors in Linvak Tukal. I ran out of time to post some of my screenies from the city, but I hope to get them up in the next day or so. That is one cool place!! The stained glass walls are see through!

Letters section updated with info on Level VII spells, the new arrows and more. I really get some great letters! I've come to accept that I will never ever catch up on my mail. I hope you all forgive me. :) 

Thanks to everyone for sending me pics of stuff I've been missing: the Small Ursuin Hide, the rewards for the large Mnemosynes, etc. etc.. -- Gregor of the Axe, Frostfell Jeff C., Coulson of Solclaim, Candi Seraphim of Harvestgain. I know I'm missing a bunch of you. If I get a little more organized, I'll make sure to toss your names up here too. Sorry!

And last, Erde of Harvestgain sent me pics and stats of the Lesser Shadow Amuli! That, along with Ravlen's pics of the Lesser Koujia, finally complete the full array of Shadow Armor. Thanks very much Erde and Ravlen! You look great!

April 25, 2001
More coming soon! :) Been very busy again. And, of course, enjoying the game when I can squeeze it in. I think this has got to be the Best Patch Ever!!

April 19, 2001
Big update on the A Reign of Stone patch page. Lots of pics and stats for the new Lugian items, plus new lore, the Dread Mattekar Claw and more. Page contains spoilers, so consider this the warning! I'll be transferring the info on the Lugian items into the quest section as soon as I verify a few more details.

Also included is the new URL for ACTrack and the udpated calibration number from the program author himself.

April 18, 2001
Patch Day! Yay!! I got home from work way too late to get everything from the patch up on my A Reign of Stone patch page, but it's a start. 

Lugians galore; cool new armor, weapons and crafted items; casinos; new dungeons (view a nice map of the Linvak Tukal by Doogen here; a new pack monster; and my favorite -- thrown weapon love!  

Mnemosyne walk-through has also been updated with the new rewards (most of them) and data on the new Arrowheads.

More coming soon! ;-) 

April 17, 2001
I've published the March 27, 2001 Newsletter, featuring some of the new 3rd party programs, to give people something to read while the game is being updated. 

April 15, 2001
Yay! Finally, a day off, taxes are done before midnight for once, and I got a chance to dig through the Inbox a little. 

Letters updated with a few short questions and answers. Also, if you still don't have any clue what "All your base are belong to us" means, there's a link inside to a very enlightening FAQ.

I also just wanted to say a little howdy to the folks I met in person yesterday at Allen Grey's impromptu farewell party. For those of you who don't know Allen, he's been the Site Manager for Crossroads of Dereth for the last 2 years and is responsible for making it one of (if not *the* ) premier AC sites on the web. Allen recently decided to step down from the post for a variety of reasons and folks in the area were given an open invite to come out to say "thank you" in person. It was a blast! Food and fun and hours and hours of hilarious talk about AC. It was a real pleasure meeting a few more of the folks behind the pixels. Thanks to Wee Mad Arthur for setting it up. And no guys, I do not need a Patron. ;-) 

Thanks also to Tom K. for updated stats on Singularity Spear, to Celestian for correcting a small error on the Crafters page and to Golshan Patel of Harvestgain for pointing out a missing emote. 

April 13, 2001
 I hope to be able to do an update this weekend. I've still been extra busy at work, with very little free time. 

April 9, 2001
 Ned Cleversun raises his loyalty another 3 points and breaks the 150 mark! Up to 151! Deaths increase by 50 to 1051! Thank you!!

Sorry for the lack of updates the last few days. Work has been killing me. Then over the weekend I had to deal with a few computer issues resulting from a lightning storm. Yuck, yuck, yuck. More coming soon! :-)

April 6, 2001
 This tale of romance started with just a few simple words: "You need a Patron?" ;-) The Where Are They Now section has been updated with news from Archer From Hell and his lovely bride, Savona. Read all about how they met, his hacking misfortune, and their heart-warming (and hilarious) wedding. 

April 3, 2001
 Added updated stats for Singularity Sword & Axe. Still missing Mace & Spear if anyone has the new damage. That's it! Busy at work again. :)

April 1, 2001
 What kind of April Fools Joke is this? Overnight, the Virindi have overrun Ayan Baqur! I've updated the Spring's Sorrows event page with more information! And read the new Ulgrim rumors (he's now a Virindi!) here.

All this new lockpick love, but let's not forget the Bandit Shield, prize of lock-pickers from way back! I've added a few more classic quests to the Quest Section: Oswald's Throwing Daggers & the Neydisa Hauberk, plus a short write-up for the Bandit Shield.

I've also updated the Crafters & Collectors page with the new patch stuff. Special thanks to Asphodel Kai of Frostfell, Archer from Hell, Gheres Reficul, Sibella, Tenrec, Dilvish and Ivan II of Leafcull for sending me items I had listed on the Stuff I'd Like page!
