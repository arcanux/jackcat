Today's weekly updated has been added to the Dev Updates page! In addition, the new dev Severlin has opened discussion on PvP housing barriers. You can find the thread here.

October 28, 2006
Another rare has been added to the Rares page: #234, the Dreamseer Bangle, courtesy of Allgeron!

October 27, 2006
Turbine has announced an upcoming Free Trial for AC! Read the details here!

October 25, 2006
The Rares page has been updated with two new entries, the Infinite Deadly Armor Piercing Arrowhead from Ima Enchantress of HG and rare #210, Valkeer's Helm courtesy of Dog Hood of TD!

October 23, 2006
Wowie, I've been such a slacker these past few weeks (er, months). I blame the baby. ;-) I do still have additional patch submissions to add, so for those of you writing to ask if I've fallen off the face of the earth, I'm still here. I'm just trying to figure out how to juggle running a  business, raising a baby and squeezing in AC time. As I like to say... more coming soon!! :)

The Dev Updates page has been updated with the (now late) news of a new AC staff member, plus new advertising. Welcome, Severlin!

Today's update includes a new screensaver (with or without sound), plus a sneak peek at a new heritage mask slated for November!

Magicpipe II of TD has also created another fabulous quest video (right click and choose "save as")! Thanks Magic!!
