 October 29, 2002
The new online gaming magazine published by Versus books, Massive Online Gaming, has hit the shelves!  There are several articles on AC, including a short feature about yours truly!  I've added a link to where you can purchase it from online from my Amazon referral page!! For those of you who gave permission to use some of your screenshots and email for the AC news section, here's what it was all about!

October 25, 2002
Tabitha of MT has received another reward from the Gaerlan's Citadel quest: a Magic Resistance necklace called Daulan Faur! A few more new creature sayings to the Festivus patch page!

October 21, 2002
There's a new section under Hobbies called Toys and Games. I was never quite satisfied with calling something like Casino Rewards a quest. Now there's a good home for it and for other miscellaneous game treats. The newest addition is the Chess page! Pets have also had numerous updates, including the Chess Drudges and Mosswarts!

October 19, 2002
Want another way to contribute to the site? As an Amazon.com associate, I can receive a small percentage of sales that originate through my site. If you're planning to pre-order AC2, consider doing it through this referral page!! Between Oct. 8 and Oct. 31, Amazon.com is running a special promotion where you can reserve your character name (as long as it's not too long, like, say, Maggie the Jackcat. Grrrr). Be warned: it's for one name on one world only, and you can only receive one per pre-order (don't order multiple copies on one order). Thanks very much to all the people who have contributed, and continue to contribute to this site. I appreciate it very much!

October 18, 2002
Once again, it's more updates to Festivus patch page! Marcus the Mole of FF sends info on Ulgrim's Scroll; 4 colors for Mu-Miyah Guises, dyed Covenant Armor, plus many more additional pics and stats. Map for Frest Greelving's Dungeon by Greeneye at ACMaps added to the Greelving's Mansion quest!

October 17, 2002
Many more updates to Festivus patch page! Saucy Golems with phat lewt, more craft rewards, Great Pumpkins, Banderlings, body parts galore, and more on fletching chorizite ore! Hey, I rhymed! I don't know what it is about this patch that makes me want to break out in song. ;-) More info added to the Frest Greelving's Mansion quest. Full write-up coming soon!

October 16, 2002
Numerous updates to Festivus patch page! Some info and fabulous screenshots for the Frest Greelving's Mansion quest can be found here.

The screenshots and information are still pouring in, but I'm afraid I'm going to have to finish things up tomorrow. It's getting late! :) New Masks and Costumes, a Haunted House, Chess, Dye-able Matties, the return of the Wi Flag, Puffy Shirts, an Evil Couch, and... Thumbs! I think this just may be the best patch ever!!

Thanks very much to everyone who sent in information for the patch page. More coming soon!! I'd also like to get more of the individual costume pieces. If you've like to lend a hand -- or a leg or a torso! ;) -- please email the examine screen with icon to jackcat@spacestar.net. Thanks again!

October 15, 2002
It's patch day. Yay!! I'll be updating the Festivus patch page throughout the day. As always, I'd like to remind people to please use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address for screenshots. If you need instructions or tips on how to take screenshots or how to convert to .jpg, visit the Screenshots  & Mail Tips page! If your icon isn't showing on new items, try hitting your "e" twice. Thank you!

Update: Roll-out article, Build Notes and Letter to the Players are now up here!

If you'd like to peruse the Pets section (aren't you glad there's a patch, so I am distracted from my creature updates?! ;-), the Rifts, Simulacra, Statues, Targets, Tumeroks, Humans and Wasps now have a few pics!

October 14, 2002
 Yet another item has been found on the Gaerlan Citadel quest: the Bracelet of Knowledge (Arcane Lore). Thanks to Tabitha of MT. More updates to the Pets section. Don't worry; I'm reaching the home stretch!! ;-) Niffis, Olthoi, Rabbits, Rats, Reedsharks and Shreth.

October 12, 2002
 Gaerlan Citadel quest updated with a few more items: a pic of Raudaloi, the Sword, from Gheres Reficul of TD (thanks also to Ragoon of FF); Habraeloi, the atlatl, courtesy of Vlaid; and the stats for the Ring of Intellect, thanks to Demolay of TD!

October 8, 2002
Today it's Monouga, Mosswarts, Moarsmen and Mu-Miyah! Yes, I know you're probably getting tired of nothing but creature pics update. Hey, try going through 3 years of screenshots and email!! ;-)

October 8, 2002
More Pets updated! Gromnies, Hollows, Idol, Knaths, Marionette, and Mattekars. More pics filled in on some of the other recently updated sections as well. If you've got a nice screen of a creature pic I'm missing, you can send it to jackcat@spacestar.net. I hope to finish updating the final creature types in the next day or so and get back to other fun things!

October 7, 2002
Another Pets update. This time for Grievvers. The Verdict page has also been updated with the latest information on the upcoming Skill System Changes that were recently announced on the Zone!

October 5, 2002
Yet another weapon has been found for the Gaerlan's Citadel quest: Dauloi, a morningstar for Mace users. Thanks to Trimfect of MT!

October 4, 2002
 Two additional rewards have been found in the Gaerlan's Citadel quest: Sacraloi, a dagger, and Shauraloi, a Warhammer. Thanks to Ioana of MT and Washburn of SC! Pets section updated with Golem pics!

And in other news, looks like the days of combat macros may be numbered.

October 3, 2002
Another beastie in the Pets section now has pics: Fragments. A few of more pics added to various other pages as well.

October 2, 2002
More updates to the Pets section: Carenzi and Cows/Aurochs have had pics added. Special thanks to Masamune Zaro of WE for numerous shots!

Gaerlan's Citadel quest from the Verdict patch updated with a few more rewards. Thanks to Landsknect of SC and Dave the Bowman. Dave will be especially relieved to hear that the high missile wield requirement on the new breastplate he dubbed "Gruuk got hosed" is a mistake. ;-)
