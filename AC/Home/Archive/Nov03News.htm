 November 30, 2003
The Preparation for the Ritus quest page updated with details on how to obtain the Energy Crystal, the Bracelet of Dark Essence and the Living Tome! Thanks to Fist of MT, Tears of Blood of WE and Magicpipe II of TD for help on the walk-throughs!

Elemental Weeping Weapons page also cleaned up into a full write-up. I've received tons of poses for the weapons, but many of them are too far away or too dark to use. Please email jackcat@spacestar.net with pics of you posing with your new elemental weapon (please include icon also)! If it's dark outside, go inside a building to snap your shot! See the Stuff I'd Like page for missing poses.

November 29, 2003
I've gone through a bunch of mail and made numerous updates to the site. Many additions to Pets -- pics, xp, etc., barbed ammunition added to Fletching, lots of miscellaneous corrections throughout. If you sent in info in the past year, there's a good chance it's made it onto the site! If you don't see it yet, well, more coming soon!! ;-)

November 27, 2003
I've finally got a chance to add some more updates to the Visions in the Darkness patch info. Thanks to everyone continuing to send in missing info!! Stats to the Good Olthoi Shield added, as well as other minor additions and corrections. Hidden Caverns page updated with more pics and Shadowcouncil of MT's walk-through of the Drudge side! Undead Mechanic quest updated with a suggested dungeon order courtesy of Bivitar Hawkins of MT! Minor updates to Elemental Weeping Weapons (see page for missing weapons) and the Gareth Dain quest. Full write-ups coming soon!

And finally, Happy Thanksgiving!!! Safe travels.

November 23, 2003
Directions to the alloy pieces from the final 10 dungeons added to the Undead Mechanic quest! Thanks to Danier, Leslieo, Shien Hao, Magicpipe II and everyone who helped me get my speedy new boots! More minor details added to the various Visions in the Darkness pages!

November 22, 2003
It's Dereth's version of the lampshade! That fun party hat worn to amuse your friends! Visit the Visions in the Darkness patch page for yet another festive mask - the Knath Head! Many more miscellaneous bits and pieces added as well. Thanks to everyone continuing to send info and pics!

November 21, 2003
You know with pink and purple chicken hats available in-game what I'm going to say. But I'll let Buttons the Clown of WE say it for me. He reports: "Best patch ever!!"

Numerous updates to the Visions in the Darkness patch page! Dyed Reedshark Robes, new trophy rewards, banderling backpacks, leather updates, a new pack doll, new masks, eye patches, and a talking drudge! Thanks to everyone who sent in patch info!! If you see anything I'm missing, please send me an email!

November 20, 2003
It's Patch Day! Yay! I'll be updating the Visions in the Darkness patch page throughout the day, so  send all your screenshots to jackcat@spacestar.net. For tips on taking screenshots, visit the Mail & Screenshots Tips page -- full examine panels with the icon showing, and don't forget your @day command!

Rollout Article and Dev Notes are already up! You can find there here. Thanks to Dark-Maji of SC for the head's up!

Town Crier Rumors are now available here!

November 18, 2003
The State of the Network has been updated with the time for the Visions of Darkness patch on Thursday!

The Undead Mechanic quest has also been updated with directions to the first 10 alloys. Updated maps also added, courtesy of Greeneye at ACMaps! Directions to the remaining 10 coming soon!

November 14, 2003
The Reign of Terror seems like yesterday, and already the new teaser for Visions of Darkness is out! You can find it here, along with an exclusive screenshot!!

November 10, 2003
The November Letter to the Players is up! You can find a copy here (now with the correct link!! ;-). In addition, the revamped Turbine AC site is back up with a new Q&A archive!

November 9, 2003
The Platemail Hauberk quest updated with the changes from the latest patch! If you're a lowbie looking for some attractive purple armor, this is the quest for you! The location of the Alloy Tool also added.

November 8, 2003
The assault begins! Visit the Ancient Olthoi Queen quest page for the latest on Nuhmudira and the attack against the Queen!

Be relentless. Be ruthless. Crush her beneath your heels and grind her carapace to dust.

Undead Mechanic quest also updated with new rewards!

November 6, 2003
Nuhmudira has returned and requested assistance to prepare for a ritus to rid the world of the menace of the Ancient Olthoi Queen. Visit the Preparation for the Ritus quest page for preliminary details on the items she is collecting!

November 5, 2003
Somehow Grim Goby of LC was able to snag the stats on the Ancient Olthoi Queen. The Reign of Terror patch page updated accordingly! The quest write-up  was also updated with a map of the Crystal Mine and an updated map of Nuhmudira's Mansion & Dungeon, thanks to Saagn of HG from AC Maps!

November 4, 2003
The Ancient Olthoi Queen quest got a minor update with info about the secret wall leading to Nuhmudira's Dungeon (see the Nuhmudira's Journal quest). Guess I didn't do enough sleuthing the first time, even with my Hardy Boys, Nancy Drew clues! The last of the alloy pieces for the Undead Mechanic quest also filled in. Thanks to everyone continuing to send info!

November 2, 2003
Happy Anniversary AC!

Four years ago today, new portals opened from Ispar to Dereth and adventurers began to explore the dangers of a new world.
In celebration of that day and in honor of the heroes who have swept back the forces of darkness, the Council has decreed that fireworks shall be set off at the bronze statues in the cities of Dereth.
Happy Anniversary, Dereth!

Thanks to Arrden of TD, Angelica Barroun of MT, Irish Faerie of SC, Andon of MT, Bosch of FF, Stormborn of TD, Harbringer's Slave of SC, Akarn Winfield of MT, Siner of TD, Parka of TD, Lightning Dragon of TD for reporting this morning's global!

November 1, 2003
An Ancient Olthoi Queen has been discovered! Visit the quest page for details. Reign of Terror patch page also updated with info from the first encounters! Well, we've certainly found her. But can we defeat her?
