Letters

November 29, 2000
 Did I mention that I am *obsessed* with the election?!!! ;-) 

But I have managed another update! The Vendor Rates page is finally under construction in the Bargains section. I've added the rates of all the towns that have been verified so far, including the new island settlements of Kryst and MacNiall's Freehold. Special thanks to Maddy and Aunyay for assistance. I'll be adding the info on several more towns to the Bargains pages soon.

I also updated the Towns and Portals page with Kryst, MacNiall's Freehold, Neydisa Castle and several other misc. locations. I corrected a few wrong portal locations as well! Although Alissa Moon-Ray of Harvestgain insists it's not my fault that the coordinates I had listed were wrong, I still feel guilty that they didn't lead to actual portals, but deathtraps instead!  Drat my sloppy tpying skill! Thanks for pointing out the errors! :)

Letters Section updated with info on the new Spine Axes and a trick emote. Plus a few more that just say nice things about me and the site. I am so self-indulgent! ;-)

November 26, 2000
 I've been busy having fun, dying on the Vesayen Isles. For a while there, with all those cool sound effects and eerie noises, I thought I had somehow wandered onto the set of Survivor! Special thanks to Adamant and Ellencil for helping me recover my corpse and my pretty purple armor!

New Quest!
Thanks also to my wonderful vassal Ned Cleversun for helping me with the research for the Rumuba's Jade Spear quest! I'd also like to say thanks to famous writer Nandesu Ka, and her companions Dalliance and Obunga for sending pics and info to get me started.   

November 25, 2000
 Yes, it's true. I've been pretty lax updating the site this month! But things are still happening in Dereth; Ned Cleversun's death stat has increased by 74 to 758!

I've added several more towns merchant info to the Bargains section: Al-Jalima, Arwic, Ktayyaban, Hebian-To, Mayoi, Lin, Qalaba'r & Tou-Tou.

More coming soon! ;-)

November 20, 2000
 My vassal has informed me that I need to stop arguing politics and get to work updating my site. And he wants to see my ass on the islands pronto! Hahaha. To sweeten the deal, he's raised his loyalty yet another point to 143! I dread to ask what the new death total is. ;)

Yes, while I've been distracted, all hell's broken loose! No, I don't mean in FL, I mean Bael'Zharon! I'll try and have more patch info up soon. In the meantime, head on over to CoD and check out the amazing events happening in Dereth. Thanks to Veritus Venatus for the fab updating!!

Site Updates:
New quest! No, it's not one of the spiffy new patch quests, but one from a few months ago: Mi Krau-Li's Improved Jitte. Thanks to Vilegoroth for all the help in the Labyrinth.

Broken Virindi Mask added to the Masks page, courtesy of Anaboo.

Lesser Celdon stats added to the Shadow Armor page. Thanks to Listos!

And finally, Letters updated. Find out why I think ACSage is one of the best sites in the AC Community!  And if you're pissed at me for my politics, come take pleasure in the nasty things being said about me on the 'net! Let me warn you, though, it's not for the delicate! ;-) 

(I also noticed that I must have somehow deleted the link to Catri's page on the Nov. 14th update. I've added it back, in case anyone missed her the first time!)

November 15, 2000
 New Patch! Yay!! While I'm personally disappointed that there's no new fez, the rest of the elaborate goodies more than makes up for it! Archers get some love, new quest, scads of new creatures, plus an amazing series of 13 islands off the SE coast of Mayoi. From what I've seen so far, I think this just might be the BEST PATCH EVER!!!! Go Turbine! :)

As always, I've started a patch page with some initial reports of some of the goodies in the Should the Stars Fall event. Ulgrim and Town Criers Rumors have also been updated.

November 14, 2000
 I did mention I'm a political junkie, didn't I? ;-) I have to admit, I've been too absorbed in politics to spend a lot of time updating the site! But there's new excitement hitting the scene -- a new patch! Yay! Because I know the site always gets a ton of traffic while the servers are down, I wanted to get some new content up.

Come visit Catri and her well dressed mules up on the Runway. Leather has never looked so good!

November 8, 2000 1AM Central
 Arggghhhh. I can't take it anymore!!! :)

- Maggie the Jackcat, political junkie

November 6, 2000
Thanks to Legionel of Harvestgain, I have the Medium Shadow Amuli updated on the Shadow Armor page! I'll update the .gif as soon as he gets his last two stones! ;-)

Other additions include the last Gharu'ndim story contest winner from the Twilight's Gleaming patch. Read Anjwar al-Jaenth of Morningthaw's Recalling the Calling.

Beasties updated with the stats of the Silver Tusker, thanks to Hired sellout. I also added the Baby Desert Rabbits that are found in Xarabydun.

Xarabydun also added to the Towns and Portals page. I made a few other minor corrections on that page as well.

I've had so many requests for info on where to get what, that I finally got around to working on a section I had planned to finish long ago: the Bargains section. There's not much there yet, but I'll be adding to it regularly. If anyone wants to volunteer to take on a town or two, I wouldn't turn down the help! ;-)

November 3, 2000
Beaties updated with the stats for the Unstable Magma Golem and Mite Warrior Princess. In addition, I added the stats of the creatures from the Twilight's Gleaming patch (Ursiuns, Niffils, and Grievvers) and the new Scarecrows and Hollow Minions. I'm still missing various stats of some creatures. Check out the Stuff I'd Like page if you ever run across them!

The Cooking Section was also updated with the new Pumpkin Recipes. I added the Pumpkin to the Pantry page, and the various pumpkin mixtures to the Food page. Green Tea icon  and Specialty Cookbook also updated. 

Broken links in the Cooking section should finally all be fixed. Thanks to everyone for the head's up. If you run across any more broken links in that section, please let me know!

Added a few Birthday Wishes to Turbine on the Happy Birthday page. If you'd like to add your well wishes, send me an email at Maggie@thejackcat.com. 

I had some major trouble with my email in the last few days. I apologize to anyone who may have received the newsletter twice and to those who may not have received responses to mail.

Happy 1 Year Anniversary, Turbine and AC! I've sent out a new newsletter commemorating the occasion, plus I've made a little Birthday Card on the site here. 

If you'd like me to add your birthday wishes to the page, you can send me an email at Maggie@thejackcat.com.

The last Heritage mask has been added to the Masks page, courtesy of Djell al-Naraq! Thanks also to Loki the Lucky and to Soong of Morningthaw for pics to round out the head section.
