August 26, 2005
Don't forget that tonight starts another bonus weekend! Double XP on creature kills for ToD subscribers and upping the odds for cantrips for all! You can read the annoucement on the Turbine Forums here (along with all sorts of other news, such as moving the AC1 team back to MA, a delay in patches, the death of AC2, the departure of Srand, and more!).

August 24, 2005
I know it goes without saying, but... best patch ever!! Holy questages!! I'm still not done adding all the submissions to the Friend and Foe patch page, believe it or not, but I continue to make progress! I also moved the Cow Attack onto a separate Live Events page and added several more accounts from various servers! Thanks to everyone who sent and continues to send missing information! You're the best!

Just a quick note on creature/NPC submissions. Although the new stats panels are great, I still need live game shots for the databases!

New trophies, ravenous house plants, new hunting rewards, a chew toy, new weapons, new lore, new armor, tinkering love, withered updates and a boiling bunny! This patch has it all! I said it once and I'll say it again... best patch ever!

August 18, 2005
MT Has already been assaulted. Now HG is receiving a visit from a Very Mad Cow!

 Warriors of Dereth, long have you had your way with the defenseless cows of this world - slaughtering, mocking, tipping. You wound these noble beasts for your demented amusement, and still they maintain peace.
But the cows have suffered long enough. There is one who has risen, one who would scour the world of your two-legged kind. She is mad, oh so very mad. And she stalks the hills east of Ayan Baqur.

Numerous additions have been made to the patch page, including a pic of the cow attack on MT! Still more to come!

August 17, 2005
It's patch day!! Yay! I'll be updating the Friend and Foe patch page as I'm able. If you can't find your screenshots, they're now in the My Documents/Asheron's Call folder.

Roll-Out Article and Dev Notes are now up!

August 12, 2005
The teaser for the Friend and Foe event has been posted! You can find it on the Turbine site here, or in the patch section, along with two exclusive teaser pics here!

August 8, 2005
While I was out of the office today, the August Letter to the Players went up! You can find it on the Turbine site here, or in the Friend and Foe patch section here! Woo hoo! Patch day is back!

I've been extra busy in real life, but I promise to get your rare submissions and other updates on the site soon! Thanks to everyone continuing to send in updates!!

August 3, 2005
Turbine has announced a Bonus XP Weekend: "Starting at 6PM PDT / 9PM EDT on this coming Friday evening, August 5, all experience from creatures killed by Throne of Destiny subscribers will be doubled!

This is for all creature kills -- not quest rewards or experience earned in any other way. The doubled experience will be shared normally to any members of the character's fellowship and passed up to the character's patron. Only Throne of Destiny subscribers are eligible for this bonus.

The bonus experience weekend will end at 9AM PDT / 12PM EDT on Monday, August 8th."
