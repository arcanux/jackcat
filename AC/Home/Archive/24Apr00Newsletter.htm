THE NEXUS!
-----------------------------------------------
If you've been paying attention, you will have noticed that the Shadows are on the attack!   Turbine's Thorns of the Hopeslayer event is in full swing. First Ferah stormed the land, wreaking havoc and destruction. Then Ler Rhan joined the fray. And finally, the Shadow Keys, which many had falsely claimed to possess, became a reality in-game. Brave groups of heroes pooled their resources to gather the pieces, scattered in the various spires throughout Dereth.

Each key is composed of three pieces, and the gateways to the spires are level restricted. Players as low as level 6 could possibly find themselves completing a critical part in the ongoing battle against the dark ones. Once the keys were assembled, these brave warriors traveled to the Nexus in the dead of night to battle the mighty Nexus Crystal.

As the Nexus Crystal was destroyed, it dropped 2 Scintillating Gems and a Nexus Core Crystal. Tron is the possessor of the totally kick-ass Nexus Shadow Armor made from this Nexus Core Crystal on the Thistledown server. Although Tron did not  make the killing blow, his comrades recognized his fearless leadership and ensured that this most special prize was rightfully bestowed. Alas, on other worlds, the outcome was less desirable as the evil of the Shadows permeated the land and hardened the hearts of its inhabitants.

The destruction of the Nexus Crystal also succeeded in ridding the land of the threat of the Umbris Shadows. (Drat! No more ph4t L3wt!)

AC SITES IN THE NEWS

Speaking of cows... Dr. Twister is in the news for using my Dereth cow picture on its site as the Shot of the Week. As glad as I am that they admired my handiwork, I do ask that people get permission before lifting graphics off my site. And please attribute the source. Asheron's Call Environmental Defense League
http://gregdan.com/acedl/acedl.html
The ACDEL is also in the news. Not for their cow tipping aversion, but for making their swimsuit calendar for 14 P.Y. available to the public free of charge! The ACDEL is dedicated to preserving the natural resources, defending the native wildlife, and protecting the environment of the Worlds of Asheron's Call. Check it out!

Asheron's Call Sage Central
http://www.acsage.com/
AC Sage is up and running. This news and information site offers guild hosting, guides, forums and more! A lot of the content is still waiting to be filled in, but take a look at the designs for the different worlds! They look great! What sets this apart from other news sites is their server specific news, including reigning monarchs.

MAGGIE THE JACKCAT'S KICK-ASS FABULOUS AC SITE NEWS!
-----------------------------------------------
Again, I'd like to thank everyone for their enthusiasm for my site. It helps keep it fun. I love the letters! I especially like the ones that say how wonderful I am. Can't get enough of them. Send more!! :-) Special thanks to Bob at the Zone for the link on their site, and thanks also to AC Trade Post & Dr. Twister for links.

I also want to take this opportunity to thank the volunteers for my new section: The Runway.
