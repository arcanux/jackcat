November 15, 2001 - Green Mire Yoroi

Hey Maggie the Jackcat, 
I was wondering if the Green Mire Yoroi was any good. I read the little bio of it (great job). I also wanted to know the coordinantes of the yoroi, and any details of the armor or dungeon would be helpful. Keep up the good work!

Beo-Wulf
Frostfell
Level 5 Custom

P.S. How do I make an item a no drop/no give??

Hi Beo-Wulf -
Yes, the Green Mire Yoroi Cuirass is great for low level characters, like yourself. It's almost like a rite of passage in AC! People used to STAND IN LINE to get their chartreuse bundle of fashion joy. You must have read Minerva of Moom's tale on the Runway. For information on the quest itself, you'll have to look in the Hobbies Section. You can find details here. (It's slightly inaccurate, but I've never gotten around to updating that page. It should have enough information to get you what you need though.) 

As to your second question, you can't make items no drop/no give. They either are that way, or they're not. For example, Mi Krau-Li's Improved Jitte and the Mace of the Explorer are no give. Which means I've been lugging them around in my backpack along with my Minor Atlan Stones, Tumerok Banners, Mosswart Shroud, Gwillim's Alchemy Bag, Grievver Acid, etc. etc. and so on and so forth. It's a good think I'm a Melee Fighter and very strong!! Other items, like the Green Mire Cuirass, can be given away, so when you outgrow your armor, you don't have to keep carrying it around with you forever, but can stow it safely away in your storage chest, or on a mule. Good luck!

- Maggie the Jackcat

Hi Maggie, 
I LOVE your site. It has more information on the quests than anywhere else. I come here whenever I need to know something my Patron does not know the answer to (needless to say, I am here quite often :) lol, just kidding).
