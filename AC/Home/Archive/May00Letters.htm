May 7, 2000 - Nexus Shadow Armor

Hello...

I am interested in any information you may have on the Nexus Gem. I would like to obtain the Nexus Armor but do not know how to go about it. Any help would be greatly appreciated.

Argon of Frostfell
Honor Above All Else

Great page lass, I'm enjoying it very much. Question: where do you find the nexxus gem and how does it all fit into the shadow armor quests?
Any and all info you have on this would be much appreciated >:)

Haggrim Blackblade
Harvestgain
Level 34 Swordsman

This was a popular question in the recent email I've received, so I thought this would be good mail to get up in the Letters section. The nexus gem was a one time only quest during the Thorns of the Hopeslayer event. The random portals which kept appearing throughtout Dereth opened into the spires. Once inside, people faced the Shadow Captains and were rewarded with pieces of keys.

There were two keys needed, three parts each, that had to be put together to get into the locked doors of the Nexus. Once there, the heroes vanquished the Nexus Crystal and were rewarded with 4 Scintillating Gems and a Nexus Core Crystal. This Core Crystal could then be made into the Ultimate Shadow Armor. Sorry, only one set per server. I'm still not sure if the keys to get into the Nexus are still available, sort of like Frore after the Great Work was destroyed, but I'll be hoping to clarify this in the near future. I hope that answers your questions. The Stats for the Nexus Armor are listed on my Shadow Armor page.

- Maggie the Jackcat
