 March 31, 2003
 The updates keep coming in! Thanks to everyone continuing to send info for the A Perfect Paradox patch page!

With the removal of the level restrictions for the first 6 keys of the Sword of Lost Light quest, I took a run through this weekend for the first time since early retail! What a blast from the past! Sword of Lost Light Quest write-up has been buffed and fluffed and fabulous pics added. Thanks to Blodshand of TD for the escort through!

March 30, 2003
 A Perfect Paradox patch page received even more weapons updates, including a Shendolain Atlatl, the Fang Mace and the Spine Axe. Also added: many more creature levels updates, and several miscellaneous updates and minor corrections.

Staff of the Nomads updated in the quests section with the new steps, thanks to Julian's Touch of TD and Rayan of WE, and Oro of TD!

March 29, 2003
 Continuing updates to A Perfect Paradox patch page, including several new weapons stats, some great pics of the Adolescent Gromnies thanks to Feryn Wolfchyld of TD, numerous creature levels and xp data additions and more! I think I have all name misspellings corrected as well! ;-) Thanks to everyone for all the great patch information!!

With the new patch the xp, attack skills and magic defense of creatures have been updated as well. If you'd like to help research updated figures, click here.

I've also started work on making the revisions to the quests section. Tibri's Fire Spear updated. More to come!

March 28, 2003
 Numerous updates to A Perfect Paradox patch page, including Town Crier Rumors and Ulgrim's Sayings! Boss monster rare drops updated, numerous quests updated, atlatl love, new Marae Lasel lifestones, cool new spell icons, hookable items, and massive updating to the Beasties of the land, including some funny killer K'naths! You know... I think this just might be the best patch ever! ;-)

Updated again with a few more items and multiple creature level and xp data.

March 27, 2003
Yay, it's patch day! As you can probably tell from the lack of updates, I've been swamped at work lately. But I'll certainly, as always, do my best to keep the updates coming throughout the day for the A Perfect Paradox patch page!

Just a reminder to use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address for any screenshot submissions! Thanks!

March 22, 2003
Yes, it's another tattoo! Amaruk of MT sent in pics and stats of the Spear Tattoo for the Bobo quest!

The March Teaser and Letter to the Players added to the A Perfect Paradox patch section.

Several more of Greeneye's ACMaps added to various quest pages, including the challenging Portal Space (full write-up coming this weekend, I hope! ;-)

March 20, 2003
You know how it is when you get back from being out of town; it takes another week to catch up!! I have added another quick update to the Bobo quest. Big Bert of FF sent in info on the Tusker Skin spell, received from the Artificer's Wish statue!

March 17, 2003
Obviously, I didn't have time to do much updating this week, but I'm traveling home today. Bobo quest, however, has been updated with Bada Bing of FF's Dagger Tattoo. Thanks to his friend Morlock of FF for sending a screenshot! More coming soon! ;-)

March 11, 2003
Greetings from sunny (well actually rainy) Florida! I'm out of town this week on a business trip, but I have my trusty laptop with me. Time permitting, I'll try to get a few updates up on the site. For now, the Mace Tattoo added to the Bobo quest, coutesy of Azhrarn the Black of TD!

March 9, 2003
More buffing and fluffing to the quests section. Martine's Retreat and the Prismatic Isparian Stone quest have had pics added.

March 8, 2003
The Neydisa Hauberk quest has been updated with the recent changes. Many other pages on the site also received minor updates.

I've received a ton of letters asking when I'm going to update the Letters section. Um... soon!!

March 6, 2003
Looks like the Tusker Paws weren't the only non-xp reward from the Brawler's Statue. Ronbo of SC sent in pics and stats of the Unarmed Tattoo from the Bobo quest! Pauldrons updated.

March 5, 2003
Thanks to ACMaps, you can now find maps to all sections of the Bobo quest! Impressive work!!

March 3, 2003
Well, I spent too much time playing this weekend to work on the site (except for some boring data confirmation stuff). I did add the Monster Redistribution FAQ Orion posted on the AC Community Board to the That Which Is Ours patch section!
