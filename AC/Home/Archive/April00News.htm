April 5, 2000
An update! Yay! The Turbine sense of humor has struck the land of Dereth again! This charming message greets those players who were lucky enough to experience the permanent enchantment bug (now fixed): "You hear faint gleeful laughter as your enchantments fade away....." Town criers exclaim enthusiastically about taking your money while giving no news! Not to mention the euphemistic manner they have to describe the mating habits of rabbits. Everybody and their brother has a different answer for where the new portals atop the spires go. And the quest campers have gotten Gertarh assassinated!
