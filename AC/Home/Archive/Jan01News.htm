January 30, 2001
Wow! Has it really been that long since I've updated? My only excuse is that I've actually been *playing* the game! This is a great game, isn't it? :)

Letters updated. Read why I don't do Tech Support and find out what my needy vassal Ned Cleversun needs today!

January 23, 2001
I was going to post this tomorrow, but thought people might like something new to look at while the servers are down. Read all about the Caulnalain Vestibule in the quest section! Fabulous screenshots included!

Special thanks to Gheres Reficul, Fizz, Halen, Azzie the Demonic, Skjuda, Conan the Slayer, and Bezurgathan for the fun!

January 21, 2001
New quest! Read about my adventures getting the Major Shivering Stone from Serac Vault, including fabulous screenshots of the quest!

I've also added a few more items to the Rewards Table of the Exploration Society Letter Quests. I'm only missing stats of a few more pieces. Thanks to Jagged Fell for and Pain Machine for pics and stats!

And once again I will mention how behind I am on mail. Only 722 more letters to answer! If one of them is yours and you haven't heard from me yet, it doesn't mean that I don't love you! And that doesn't mean I want people to stop sending mail. Please keep sending it, and I'll do my best to get everything up on the site soon.

To help consolidate some of my responses, perhaps I can tackle a pile of them here!

1) No, you may not copy my quests (or other content) onto your allegiance site. Yes, you may link to my pages.

2) No, I can't hook you up with armor/equipment/uber loot.  

3) I play on Thistledown (mostly).

4) Thank you very much. I love you too!

January 19, 2001
Platinum everywhere! I'm sure most of you are aware by now of the flood of Platinum scarabs into the Dereth economy. Possible rollback! Ned Cleversun reported from remarkable scene at Teth, where people were dropping stuff all over the ground to make room for D notes. Trident sent some pics from AB. Claude has been busy! And you can just imagine the scene at Darktide. Complete carnage.

January 17, 2001
I've added another entry to the Where Are They Now? section. Find out what Zenk "The Greaves Queen" has been up to! You'll just love her Halloween costume! She was TWO contests with it! Visit with Zenk here.

I also finally updated the coords for the Eastham smith on the Shadow Armor page.

January 14, 2001
The Shadow Armor page has finally been updated with stats for Medium Celdon, and stats for both the Lesser Celdon and Medium Koujia! Special thanks to Schunard  for Medium Celdon, Pyros of KoC, Darktide for Medium Koujia; and Alexandre, Ophelia, Surfal &  Sundancer of Leafcull for Lesser Celdon. Ophelia tells me that her 4 year old son, an avid AC player, got the set of armor through the generosity of other players on the server, to help him with those pesky deaths!

I'm still missing stats, etc. for the Lesser Amuli and Lesser Koujia. If you have this armor, please email me!

January 10, 2001
Patch day was yesterday! The Snowmen are back! Yay! I've posted my Now In This Hush pages with info from the new patch. Sword Love, Dagger Love, Mage Love, Non Item Magic Love! You can find the info here!

I also updated the Town Crier and Ulgrim the Unpleasant pages with rumors from the new patch. 

To top it all off, Ned Cleversun was so impressed with my Danier page in the new Where Are They Now section, that he upped his loyalty another point to 146, just like that! And of course when loyalty goes up by one, the death total goes up as well. New total is 851.

January 9, 2001
To start off the launch of the Where Are They Now? section, who could I possibly have chosen but Danier?! This high level monarch and his fun crew have been extremely helpful, not only assisting in keeping my needy vassal supplied, but also by their generous contributions to this website. I originally intented to launch this section for AC's 1 year anniversary, but better late than never! 

For this first entry, I've gone the extra mile and posted snazzy screenshots from Danier and his monarchy's recent adventures. Come catch up with Danier in the Where Are They Now? Section!

I've also added the first guide to the sad, previously empty Guides page under the Careers section. Read Danier's essay, "How to Make (and Play) a Mage: here!

January 5, 2001
Just a quick update, since I've been a little under the weather. Eastham vendor information added to the Bargains sections, and the Gharu'ndim Master Robe info added to the Clothing page in the Bargains section. More coming soon! ;-) 

January 4, 2001
Letters finally updated! Kaddish of Leafcull wrote to me with some more information on the locations of dye plants, and Kelly Nicrath of Harvestgain tells you how to update the look of your Matty Coat to a nice furry one. You can also find out what's new on my vassal's Wish List. December Letters Archived.

I've been trying to continue to plow through my massive backlog of email and have done a hodge podge of miscellaneous updating. Thanks for bluss for some missing info on some Society Armor pieces, to Maex of Solclaim for the stats of a Society Axe, and to Suntur of Harvestgain who corrected my info for Lytelthorpe to Nanto on my Rewards Table of the Exploration Society Letter Quests.

Several people have written to me with the info that the ore is no longer available in the Arwic Mines for the Impious Staff quest. I've finally updated the page.

Thanks to PFalcon, I've added pics of the Medium Celdon Shadow Armor to the Shadow Armor page!

I also added a link to the Masks page in the Crafters section and the Potions page from Alchemy to the Quest list. People were having a little trouble finding them. 

Yellow Pages were updated with a few links to some popular utilities, such as AC Explorer and Split Pea.

And thanks to he special offer from the Seeker's Branch of The Chosen of Harvestgain I should have more info filled in for the Bargains Section and Vendor Rates soon. 

Zalii of Frostfell & Archit' ibn Ect of Harvestgain also sent me a fabulous list of vendor information. Special thanks also to Akya of Harvestgain and to Maddy for their assistance!

January 2, 2001
Happy New Year! My vassal Ned Cleversun was certainly busy winding down the last millennium. His new death total increased 52 to 829. Loyalty also increased another point to 145. I have the best vassal! 

Wait there's more! The Quest Section has been updated with the Major Sparking Stone! Thanks to Danier and Doogen's Mage, I survived unscathed. The quest write-up also has some fabulous screenshots of our adventure, including a cool pic of an Acid Wall spell! 

The Vendor Rates page has also been updated with several more towns, including Wai Jhou (thanks to Maddy!!), Dryreach, Eastham, and Rithwic. The individual bargain pages are still being updated for these towns. 

December 2000 New Stuff archived. 

And in case you haven't heard, Bael'Zharon has been defeated on Solclaim, Harvestgain and now Leafcull!
