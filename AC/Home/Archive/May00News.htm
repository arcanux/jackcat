May 31, 2000
Maggie the Jackcat shows off her sexy new leggings on the Runway!

I also changed the Fire Auroch Meat to show that it restored Health, not Stamina on the Food page, plus removed the incorrect portal information from Khayyaban to Yanshi on the Towns & Portals page, which actually didn't exist, but led right into a dungeon! Sorry guys!

Thanks Pharfig Neugan and Sunny D for pointing out the errors!

May 28, 2000
As I promised, I'll be adding several more additions to the Runway, including myself!! I want to look my best, you know. But for now, here's another of Dereth's most fashionable ladies: Nocta of Leafcull. The outfit is absolutely smashing! Take a look!

May 26, 2000
Subutai is on the Runway! I finally got the page finished of Subutai wearing his Greater Koujia Shadow Armor. Thanks for posing! I also added the new Greater Amuli Shadown Armor stats on the Shadow Armor page thanks to Achmed II of Leafcull.

Congratulations to Mary the Mad II of Morningthaw who got her Peerless Atlan Mace just the other night! And thanks for sending in the missing pic for my Atlan Weapons pages.

Thanks also to Chronus of Harvestgain for letting me know the stats of the updated stats of the Peerless Atlan Sword since the patch.

May 25, 2000
New stuff! Ok, well, I didn't finish the Runway piece of him wearing it yet, but I *did* get the updated stats of the Greater Koujia Shadow Armor contributed by Subutai of Thistledown up on the Shadow Armor page. Plus I added a note under the Nexus Armor, making people aware that it was one time loot only -- one set per server. You simply would not believe the amount of mail I get asking about it!

Thanks to Archer from Hell for the pic of the Tiny Shard!

Additions:
Updated the Letters Section. See Maggie cave in to vanity!

Put the April 24 Newsletter up.

Updated Ned Cleversun's Stats: Loyalty up by 1; Deaths up by 9. Does that seem unbalanced to you?!

May 24, 2000
Well, I've actually been playing the game lately, so no time for updates! ;-) But I'll do more soon. Going live with the Crossroads of Dereth quest section helped to introduce my site to many new people, plus it filled my inbox with lots of mail that still needs to be answered. I don't know how Allen Grey does it!

I'll have several new additions to the Runway in the near future, including ...Maggie the Jackcat! I just got some kick-ass stuff and want to show it off -- something even better than a purple chain girth!

May 21, 2000
Still catching up on the Heroes' Respite changes!

Additions:
Updated the Shadow Armor page with the new burden stats for the Greater Celdon, thanks to The Cooking Guy on Thistledown!

Updated more of the Foods with the new burden stats. Thanks once again to the marvelous Crick the Cook for the research! I also added Royal Cabbage as well as a few other missing foods to the page.

Updated the Alchemical Formulas Page with the new Potions, plus added a few other missing formulas.

May 19, 2000
Thanks to Nandesu Ka and her enterprising brother, Obunaga, I've added the stats to the Lesser Celdon Shadow Armor on the Shadow Armor page. It's a wonderful shade of deep red. Thanks to both of you!

Ned Cleversun' loyalty creeps up another point to 104!

May 17, 2000
Matty Coats for everyone! Yes, you guessed it. Ned Cleversun finally has his Mattekar Coat thanks to the generosity of Pigroast II. See both Ned Cleversun and Pigroast II modeling their furs on the Runway. And that's not all! Nandesu Ka has a Mattie Coat as well, thanks to her enterprising brother! I simply cannot forget to mention Vilegoroth of Thistledown, who also offered a Matty Coat up to the cause. Yes, there was joy in the streets of Zaikhal, most certainly!

Additions:
Updated the Shadow Armor page with information on the new elemental protections. I don't have *any* of the new burden stats. For anybody out there who has Shadowhunter Armor, if you could please take a minute and email me with the new burden stats, I'd certainly appreciate it! I'm also still missing most of the stats of the lowest level Shadow Armor. If anybody has this, I'd love your assistance in filling out the section.

Updated the Locksmith page with the Sturdy Iron Key and put a link to Truebolts page on uber chests.

Changed the Magic Items page to reflect the change in burden for scrolls, down to 30 from 270! Thanks to David T for the info!

Corrected some coordinates on the Dagger of Tikola quest page. Thanks to Angyr for the head's up.

Updated the Letters Section.

May 16, 2000
Sadly, I've just been too busy to do much updating. Today's news: Ned Cleversun's Death total is up, and his loyalty stat is up three points to 103! What could have prompted that?! Well... More news coming soon!

May 13, 2000
During the Heroes' Respite update, foods suddently became lighter! Thanks to Crick (of the great dungeon maps) for helping to compile some of the new burden stats for the Food page. Updated stats are in yellow. My favorite: while everything else is reduced in burden, cookies are heavier!!

Just a little minor updating fixing misspellings and so forth. I did update the Letters Section, though! Find out what a Terrible Patron Maggie the Jackcat is!

Archmage of Doom is the latest participant on the Runway! I secretly tried on his hat when he was busy with his mule. It matches my eyes!

Added the new town and Portal coordinates for Ayan Baqur on the Towns and Portals page. I also made several miscellaneous corrections on this page.

Added a few new map links -- one to the Town Maps page for the House of the Curious Dead's Ayan Baqur map, and links to two Allegiance portal maps from House of Thunder and EpiK under the Dereth Maps page.

Added a page with the sayings of Ulgrim the Unpleasant, the new town drunk.

May 4, 2000
The Town Criers are busy talking about fashion. They must have seen Nandesu Ka take a walk down the Runway. Check out the new addition -- simple yet elegant! Who says you can't go killing in an evening gown?

Additions:
Added several miscellaneous armor pieces courtesy of Spot and David T. Thanks for sending them in!

Added a new picture and background color under the main shopping page.

May 3, 2000
New Patch!! Yay! Maggie the Jackcat, Sword Fighter, is tickled with the latest update. Look at all the goodies! (Already she hears the faint rumblings of the Archers preparing their complaints ...unless that rumor about Lilitha's new bow is true!)

Pigroast II was kind enough to send along a picture of the new Rapier. If you remember, Pigroast II was the fellow who accidentally deleted Pigroast I on the way to work one day. His pain has saved numerous others from the same fate as Turbine responds with an option to restore deleted characters for a limited time! Read all about it along with the rest of the build notes here!

A new spell bar, a new town, alchemy advances, a facelift for the Olthoi Sword, and more cheeky Town Crier Rumors. (I've been anticipating the new Fez for weeks!!!) Looks like the Heroes' Respite is off to a fine start!
