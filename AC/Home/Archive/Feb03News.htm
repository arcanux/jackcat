 February 28, 2003
William the Bat of MT also in another new spell from the Bobo quest! Tusker Leap from the Leaper's statue adds 100 point to jump for a short period of time!

February 27, 2003
 The Cooking Bargains page has been updated with the locations for Elaborate and Simple Dried Rations!

February 26, 2003
 A new tattoo has been added to the Bobo quest! Mrs Pink of SC received a Mana Conversion Tattoo from the Statue of Archmage's Wishes!

February 25, 2003
 The Acid Axe and Ice Tachi quests updated with the xp reward you can receive from Ivory Crafters, thanks to Trevor of LC!

February 24, 2003
 The Sanguinary Aegis quest updated into a full write-up with additional pics! Mine's purple! ;-) Thanks to Gaetano, Rochefort Trappistes, Leijo the Og, Marie the Loyal and Darrien for the adventure!

A few minor additions have also been added to the That Which Is Ours patch page.

Kashell of WE reports on another spell from the Bobo quest!: Tusker Might from the Enchanter's Wish Statue. It adds 35 to strength, surpasses Lugian Might and lasts for 89 minutes (screenshot now added).

February 23, 2003
 Two new rewards have been added to the Bobo quest! Frederick the mighty of FF received Tusker Paws from the Statue of Brawler's wish, and Hirihoto Sakai of LC sent info on a new spell, Tuskers Hide, a Life Magic reward! Thanks also to Kerico of FF! Gauntlets page updated with the new paws.

The last of the missing stats added to the Silifi of Crimson Stars quest. Thanks to Cragmet Bruten of MT for info on the Bronze Spine!

Spear of the Given Heart quest updated with additional info and fabulous pics! Last, thanks to Cyrano de Bergerac, Arcayne, Dacia and Galfrida for various body recoveries.

February 22, 2003
 More miscellaneous updates to the That Which Is Ours patch page! A new tattoo has also been added to the Bobo quest! Vormav of MT poses with an axe tattoo! Pauldrons also updated.

February 21, 2003
 The That Which Is Ours event page has been updated with tons of patch info! Town Crier Rumors and Ulgrim Sayings  updated. New quests, including the Sanguinary Aegis (with a map by Birte of TD!) and the Spear of the Given Heart, new creatures, spawn changes galore, new icons, a new robe, rations, quest updates and more. Not to mention that Larry the Rabbit Master is back in business!! Best patch ever? I think so!

Thanks to everyone who sent pics and info in!! If you run across anything I'm missing, please send me an email!

February 20, 2003
 It's patch day!! Yay! I'll be updating the That Which Is Ours patch page throughout the day as I'm able. Teaser, Dev Notes, Letter to the Player can be found here. As always, please use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address for any screenshots.

For tips on taking screenshots, you can visit my Mail & Screenshots Tips page. I get so many submissions I can't use each month, that I'd love for you to take a moment to check out the page if you haven't before. Quick tips: if it's dark out, take your pic inside; use your camera views so I don't just get the back of your head; send the entire examine panel with the icon showing for any stats! Thanks! I couldn't do it without you!!

February 18, 2003
 Yes, it's another tattoo for the Bobo quest, this time a Sword Tattoo from Lorcan of HG! Thanks also to lash and Sjoring of SC who also sent pics and stats of the Sword Tattoo! NBK's Mage sent a little bit more info on the Tusker Fists spell as well, although it appears to be bugged since the Jan. patch.

The Bandit Shield quest was updated with the changes from the Halls of the Tusker King event, including the new stats!

February 17, 2003
 The Bobo quest rewards keep coming in! Peter Wiggen of TD sends the Crossbow Tattoo, and NBK's Mage of TD sends info on Tusker Fists, a new War Spell received from the Sorcerer's Wish Statue! Pauldrons updated with stats of the new tattoo.

The Banderling Camp quest was also updated with the changes from the Halls of the Tusker King event. The camp near the Aluvian Casino now drops the Quarterstaff of Fire, and the 2 other camps now have a Banderling Rogue which drops the Fire Spear. The Staff and Spear pages in the Sports section were also updated with the new stats!

Silifi of Crimson Stars quest updated with the stats of the Freshwater spine, courtesy of Wolf-Lord of SC!

February 16, 2003
 Bobo quest updated with more rewards! Paraduck discovered that you can create Plentiful Healing Kits with ingredients received from the Healer's Wish statue. Pharmacy updated with the new find. Machine of MT sent in yet another Tattoo, this time a Missile Defense Tattoo. Bracers updated accordingly. I also updated Machine's Magic Defense Tattoo with a larger and clearer pic, courtesy of Trinity Isaak of FF. Shadow Executor of FF sent in the response from Bobo when he's handed a Tusker Paw!

Quests section updated with another long overdue write-up. Find out how to get your Focusing Stone here!

February 14, 2003
 Happy Valentine's Day! I've been a little extra busy at work this week, but I'll try to get an update up this weekend!

February 10, 2003
 A few miscellaneous updates today. The Crystal Minds, Shattered Souls quest updated with a map of the Misty Mine, thanks to Birte of TD! Because I'm a "completionist" Voidhawk of WE sent a pic of the breastplate Saelgeloi being worn to fill out the rewards of the Gaerlan's Citadel quest. I dunno, do you think that's a nice way of saying anal retentive? ;-) Last, the Bobo quest updated with the hilarious response you get when you give Bobo a Plush Tusker Doll!! Thanks to Ninetoes of MT for this addition!

February 8, 2003
 Caul Recall Scroll quest updated with a few more maps, courtesy of Eskarina of MT!

February 7, 2003
 Thanks to The Cloud of WE, we have confirmation on another Tattoo, this time a Bow Tattoo, which covers the Upper Arms! Pauldrons updated in the armor section accordingly, and the pic and stats added to the Bobo quest. If you have rewards I'm missing, please send me an email!

February 6, 2003
 Long ago, you may have remembered me linking to a table I was working on with quest restrictions, timers, no-drop status, etc. Well, I finally just scrapped it because it was too unwieldy. Instead, I simplified it to a new list with just the level restriction, timer and the initial rumor/NPC. You can find a permanent link to the new table from the Quests section.

February 5, 2003
 Bracers updated in the armor section with Tattoos from the Bobo quest. A great pic of Shadow Death in his Melee Defense Tattoo also added to the quest page. If you have rewards I'm missing, please send me an email!

To clarify about the tattoos, the description says the ink is applied on the upper arms, but the stat panel says they are worn on the lower arms. Unless I get confirmation otherwise, I am going with the assumption these are bracers and the description is an error.

February 4, 2003
 It's a long overdue update to the Dyes section. The Dyes page revised with information on dyed Covenant armor, crafted items, the difficulty for dyeing Wedding Raiment, and discoloration. Dye Poses also got a facelift. Information on Little Green Seeds was moved from the patch section and Dyes page to the revised Plants Location page, which is now the Plant Locations & Little Green Seeds page. Auralyn of TD once again posed in her gorgeous dress collection and the Dyed Dresses page was updated with Colban and Renalim pics. If you've got a fabulous collection to show off, such as a collection of Bunny Slippers, Robes or Olthoi Helms, etc. (I know some of you have written in the past; please try again), send me an email!

February 3, 2003
 Yet another one of the recently revised quests updated. The Impious Staff now reflects the changes from the Iron Coast event. The Crafters and Collectors page also received a minor update. I just happened to find a Brown Rat Tail on a mule! ;-) And congrats to Cyrano de Bergerac on reaching level 126 today!

February 1, 2003
 The Silifi of the Crimson Stars quest has been updated with the changes from the recent patch. The Slumbering Giant patch page also received more miscellaneous updates. More rewards added to the Crafters and Collectors page. Thanks to everyone continuing to send in data!
