November 23, 2005
Magicpipe II of TD has created yet another great quest video, this time for Aerlinthe! Visit the Aerlinthe quest page for details!

November 22, 2005
A rampaging chicken named Gobbles has been making the rounds! You can find out more on the Live Events page!

Magicpipe II of TD has completed another of his great quest videos. Visit the Gateway to the Deep quest page for details!

I also wanted to say a thank you to Jat'two'k of SC who's been filling in a bunch of missing creature pics!

November 21, 2005
 A few live event reports were added to the Ashes and Dust Live Events page! You can see a few rares, plus a movie by Magicpipe II of TD of the Remoran event inside one of the new events dungeons!

Two more quests were cleaned up into full write-ups: the Rebel Hideout and Enrico's Betrayal!

November 19, 2005
 The Glenden Wood Invaders quest has been revised into a full write-up! Find out how to get your own Viamontian Helm here!

November 16, 2005
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating the Ashes and Dust patch page throughout the day as I'm able! For your submissions, don't forget to include full stats of items and your in-game name and server. If you can't find your screenshots, they are now under the My Documents/Asheron's Call folder.

November 11, 2005
Lots of updates today! Visit the Dev Updates page for the latest posts from the Devs. You'll find info on proposed PvP changes, an article on the Respec Timer Redesign, plus links to the Letter to the Players and the Ashes and Dust teaser! Thejackcat.com's exclusive teaser pic is also up!!

The Tricks and Treats patch page has received a few minor updates and the Live Events page has a few new entries!

Last, Tlosk of HG has updated his great Salvaging Chart with data on augmentation effects!

November 7, 2005
I've updated the Dev Updates with the last few posts on the Turbine boards. You can find an early teaser pic and the latest news on Decal!

The server will be down again later tonight for an extended period of maintenance. This should resolve any remaining issues and will hopefully be the last of the site outages for the foreseeable future. Thanks for your patience!

November 3, 2005
Today Maggie the Jackcat is 6 years old! And AC, therefore, is 6 years and 1 day old! Thanks to Yog-Sothoth of HG for reporting yesterday's global!

Six years ago, new portals opened from Ispar into Dereth and a wave of adventurers arrived.
Since that time we have overcome many challenges and seen our society grow and prosper. In celebration of that day and in honor of the heroes who have found glory and riches, the Council has decreed that fireworks shall be set off at the bronze statues in the cities of Dereth.
Happy Birthday, Asheron's Call!

November 2, 2005
The latest Live Ops reports from Turbine is up on the Turbine Live Events Write-Ups page!

I also just wanted to give a heads up that the server will be down for several hours tonight starting at 1AM central time for some hardware diagnostics. Thanks for your patience!
