 October 31, 2001
 I still haven't had a chance to get the latest batches of info up on the site. I've been too busy meeting my neighbors (my vassal, Ned Cleverson, lives right next door!), and showing off the beautiful view from my living room window to friends. More coming soon! Writ of Refuge added to the Texts section, Marae Lassel Town Crier Rumors added.

Just a reminder that housing commands can be found on the @commands page. Find out how to boot someone out of your home!

October 30, 2001
 Maggie the Jackcat is recalling home! Yay! I bought a cottage! Read about a few happy new home owners in the updated Letters section. Real Estate Guide has also been updated with Purchase Information for housing! 

I still have piles of info to add to the Dark Majesty pages, but, well, I was too busy playing the game! ;-) More coming soon!

October 29, 2001
 "Cheese makes a gathering!" At least that's what the description on the new Pepper Jack Cheese says! ;-) Many more updates to the Dark Majesty Expansion page and its various Quests. Believe it or not, there's still more to come. (I ran out of time!) Thank you to everyone for the incomparable contributions. Wow, there's a lot of new stuff! 

October 28, 2001
 My inbox certainly was filling while I was sleeping last night! Many updates to the site! Lots of quest spoilers, including information on the Olthoi Queen! Special thanks to Optoman for all the great screenshots! Don't click on this link if you want to find things out for yourself!!  

More updates to the Dark Majesty Expansion page. Aun Town Crier Rumors, a Hallucinogenic bonfire, more creature stats, trophy rewards, new items, and more!! Thank you to everyone who's contributed so far! 

October 28, 2001
 At 1AM I finally made it through the subscription process. So that means the latest batch of stuff sent in is not yet up on the site because I was busy dying on Marae Lassel! Yay! Since it's way late, I'll have to wait until tomorrow to update. (/me is now glad she *didn't* throw her computer out the window in frustration! :)

October 27, 2001
 The Queen has been found! Dark Majesty Expansion page updated with a pic of the Queen and Her Royal Guard, thanks to Optoman. Sinister Link poses in his Olthoi Cuirass, plus pics of the Olthoi Eggs, trophies added, lore and more!

October 27, 2001
 I finally had to give it up for the evening. No Marae Lassel for Maggie yet. Many of you wrote to me, telling me that you share my pain. ;-)

More updates, including a snazzy new feathered dress. And is that henna I see on his upper arms? Very sporty!! Plus new Totem, Talking Trees, more creature stats, trophies and more! Visit the Dark Majesty Expansion page for continuing updates! 

(And as a reminder, please use the jackcat@spacestar.net address for any screenshots.) Thanks to everyone who's been sending the great info in!

October 26, 2001
 It's just like Christmas! My pretty FedEx box was waiting outside my door when I got home! I ripped open the packaging, slipped the CD in the machine, loaded the game, oohing and ahhing over the cool new AC logo and fancy map and game guide (very slick looking), but... I can't get in the game! The Subscription page is down! Arrggghhh!!! Haha. Hasn't torture been outlawed? :)

In the meantime, the information is coming in fast and furious. I don't have everything up yet, but I've added a few more things to the Dark Majesty Expansion page, including information on the new Olthoi Titles! More coming soon!

Letters also updated with some info on a Grand Virindi Inquisitor, suspected to be an unfound boss monster from last patch!

October 26, 2001
 Wheeee! Dark Majesty arrives! Well, for some people, that is! That means, of course, it's time for the Dark Majesty Expansion page! Special thanks to Mopdit of FF and Avallach of TD for getting things started by sharing their great discoveries! There are numerous reports all over the web of other great items being found by those lucky few, and I'm sure there's much much more just waiting to be discovered! If you'd like share your finds with Maggie the Jackcat's Kick-Ass Fabulous AC Web Site for inclusion on the DM expansion page, please use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address for any screenshots! 

I'll see you on Marea Lassel. I hope one day soon!! *please be in the mail* *please be in the mail* *please be in the mail* :)

[warning: the Dark Majesty page contains spoilers] 

Shortcuts updated with the list of portals to Marae Lassel.

October 25, 2001
 *jumps up and down impatiently!!!* I think a total of 3 people have made it onto Marae Lassel, driving the rest of us crazy with envy! ;-) Once the expansion release gets rolling, I'll be posting as much new information as I can, as usual. In the meantime, I've added a page of Dark Majesty news links to the Culture section. If you haven't already seen them, you'll find numerous previews to whet your appetite!

Continuing with the site housekeeping, Dagger page has now been revamped in the Sports section. More Shortcuts added as well!

October 24, 2001
 Letters updated! And to make up for the long delay, I've including a long and amazing read from Doogens Mage, all about his adventures in the Bore, at the Caul, and his fight with a Diamond Lord. You can also read about the uses of marriage, who wants to get me high, and how to know where you're going.

To help with that last issue, I've finally put up a page I've long had planned: Shortcuts! If you've got a great route to a hard-to-get-to place, please send me an email!

October 22, 2001
 I've been mired within the minutiae of the site the last few days, changing the value of items on some of the Starter Quests to 0, adding a few keys to the Locksmith page, updating the Gallery with the 2nd painting from Linvak Tukal, giving Ulgrim a better picture, adding some missing notes and texts, and so on and so forth. More coming soon! ;-)

October 21, 2001
 Sports section updated again, this time with Crossbow information. (Thanks to Kiltear of MT for the missing Explorer Crossbow pic.) 

Energy Crown write-up added to the Quests section!

Many, many other miscellaneous stat and spelling errors, updates, etc. corrected here and there throughout the site.

October 20, 2001
Another update to the Sports section. Following the revamp to the Axe Section, the Bows page now is separated into Quest/Crafted Bows, Starter and Store Bought Bows, and then a sampling of Loot Generated Bows. Someone previously wrote to me asking why I didn't list the most important stat of the Bow: the damage modifier! Well, when I first did the page, lo many moons ago, the damage modifier was not visible! Archers would have to complete complicated field tests to gauge whether they had a decent weapon or not. So until I gather more bow stats, the sampling of loot bows will still be somewhat outdated.

October 19, 2001
I'm back! And, as ever, completely behind on mail. I haven't been in-game since my return (I *hope* I remembered to put on my Leadership Crown!! ;-), but I have gotten back into the groove a bit with a few updates to the site. The update to the Masks page that I began before I left town is now complete. In addition, I revamped the Currency/Shopping Bags page in the Shopping section with the new pyreal icon, a few links to exchange rate sites, and information on the new Tusker Packs. Continuing with the updates to the Bargains section, I've added a page on Containers. Find out where to buy a Basket or a Purple Pack!

October 13, 2001
I'll be out of town for several days and may not be able to check email while I'm gone. Please continue to send any letters, screenshots, information, etc. while I'm gone and I'll be back to updating sometime mid-week.

October 12, 2001
Pumpkin Mask and Ursuin Head added to The Widening Gyre patch page! I've also started adding information from the patch to the various sections on the site. Pantry updated with the Monougat, Recipes updated. Potions updated with information on the new Trade Elixirs, Apparel Head section updated, and I've started revision on the Masks page in the Arts & Crafts section. Thanks again to everyone continuing to send information!!

Rumor has it that the Bone Mace from last month's teaser pics is dropped by the Banderling Leader! If you find one of these weapons, please send me an email!

October 11, 2001
More updates to The Widening Gyre patch page! Zombie Head, Candied Apple, Mosswart Wand and more Dark Majesty portal locations (currently inactive) added. Mud & Golem Hearts (uses still unknown) added. Translated Singular Notes added to the Texts section (thanks to Furby Hunter).

Newbie Templates update completed. Suggested skills added, Bow Hunter stats changed, and Soldier graphics replaced with Axe pics. See the Professions page for details.

Energy Crown stats updated (longer duration) thanks to Shylok.

October 10 2001
Sclavus Mask added to The Widening Gyre patch page! The Interior Decorating page now has the complete inventory of the Apprentices' wares. The locations and text of the three Furniture Crafters also added. Stuff I'd Like page updated with a few missing items from the event.

And last, but most certainly not least, my wonderful vassal's Death Total and Loyalty stat have been updated. Death Total up 60 to 1311! Loyalty up an entire point to 152!! I've got the bestest vassal in all of Dereth! And that's saying something, because Dereth just keeps on getting bigger! ;-)

October 9, 2001
It's here! The Widening Gyre event opens with a bang! Drudgesicles! New cool looking Masks! Trade Skill loving! Interior Decorating! Virindi Shrouds! Visit the patch page for details on this month's fabulous event! Ulgrim the Unpleasant sayings and Town Crier rumors updated as well! This just might be the best patch ever!

Many miscellaneous updates to the site with this patch. Apprentice Robes stats updated. Gem of Impulse duration updated. Interior Decorating page added to the Shopping Section. @commands updated with housing commands.

October 9, 2001
Just to amuse myself, I've updated the Mana Stones page in the Bargains section! In case you're wondering why I'm amused, it's because I'm anticipating some Mana Stone love tomorrow (and, therefore, changes! ;-).

Sports section begins its update in earnest with the Axe section. Also included is a table of Maximun Base Damage for all melee weapons. Please email me with any corrections.

And, of course, it's patch day! Please use the jackcat@spacestar.net address to send screenshots! Wheee!

October 8, 2001
Three more additions to the Quests from September's event: Robe of the Tundra, Basalt Blade & Obsidian Axe, and Serpent's Fang! If anyone has pics or stats of the Pure One or the Dark Sorcerer, I've added them to the Stuff I'd Like page. Thanks! You can email them to me at this address: jackcat@spacestar.net

I've also added a page on Casino Rewards (an additional way to get Mnemosynes) with tables of approximate reward rates.

October 7, 2001
More updates to the Quests section! I've started transferring the new items from the Keep Your Enemies Closer event page. Crystal Sword, Diamond Shield and Soul Staff added so far. Fabulous screenshots of items and creatures included! 

Continuing with the housekeeping of some of the older areas of the site, I've started updating the Weapons pages in the Sports section as I did with the Armor section. I've started with the Arrows & Quarrels section, since all that required was some slight rearrangement and tossing in a few links.

Real Estate Guide updated with some Qalaba'r dwellings. (Other pages of the guide may also have been added to.) Coordinates page updated again with more submissions. I don't know about you, but my legs are getting tired! ;-)

October 6, 2001
Another update to the Quests section. Read about the quest for Trothyr's Hammer and Shield! Special thanks to the House of Colier for detailed research on this quest! Thanks also to Diabloborracho of DT and Faryas Makin of HG! 

October 5, 2001
The last pages of the Armor section updated: Leggings, Pauldrons, Shirts and Sleeves. Next maybe I'll tackle all those broken links in the Maps sections! :-)

October 4, 2001
Yet two more Armor sections updated: Girths and Greaves. Thank goodness for Zenk's Runway page, or I would barely have had any Greaves examples for the Random Loot Generated Section! ;-) I also realized that although I had been updating the elemental protections from the Changing of the Ways metal armor love, I had forgotten the changed values and burdens. I *knew* the Horned Helm burden was 666! Stats for the store-bought armor on the previously updated pages have now been corrected.

I've also finally finished going through all the email with housing coordinates. Real Estate Coordinates page updated accordingly. If you don't see the housing locations you sent in, please resend them. Thanks!

October 3, 2001
Two more Armor sections updated: Feet and Gauntlets.

Letters updated! Maggie talks about one of her favorite subjects: herself! (j/k, I actually talk about the site) Other topics include: When Monarchs Cry, and a Big Fat Thank You from Maggie the Jackcat.

October 2, 2001
Two more Armor sections updated: Coats/Hauberks and Cuirass.

By request, I've also added a Tip Jar for the site. Use the button to the right to send me money via Amazon's Honor System or Paypal! An About the Tip Jar page added as well.

October 1, 2001
Finally, on my third attempt, I retrieved the Blood of the Hopeslayer from the Remnants of Ilservian in the Sepulcher of the Hopeslayer! Thanks to DaMonchary under Danier for the grand adventure! Read about it in the Sword of Lost Hope quest write-up! Includes fabulous screenshots!

I've also started the long overdue updating of the Armor section in response to countless requests for cross-links to the Quest section or the Crafters & Collectors page. The quest/crafted items have been separated out, as have the store-bought items. As before, I've still included a sampling of the randomly generated items to show the wide variety of items available in the game. Bracers, Breastplates, Head and Shield pages completed so far.
