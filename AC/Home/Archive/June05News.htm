June 30, 2005
Ibn has announced that the highest level tokens for the Open Preview are available today!

June 28, 2005
Level 180 tokens are out for the Open Preview!

June 27, 2005
Were you devastated by the announcement that the Rhode Island Turbine Nation had been cancelled for August and will be replaced by a CA AC event sometime this fall? The Lord of the Rings Community is hosting its own player run event anyway, and has invited any at-loose-ends AC players to attend. Paraduck is corralling the AC folks here! When we have the new dates for the CA event, I'll let you know. (Hint, Callandryll!!)

Update: Level 140 Tokens are now available from the Turbine Vendors in the Open Preview! (Typo fixed!)

June 24, 2005
Ibn has announced that level 100 tokens are now available in the Throne of Destiny Preview. A dev-led quest will also occur at 6PM Pacific time!

The latest Weekly Status Update is also available here!

Thanks to everyone sending info for the Patch page! It won't go live until the 18th, but I'm working on getting it organized! As always, please send full stats panels, and include your in-game name and server! Also, since the new screenshots are already in .JPG format, there's no need to compress them further. Just send them as they are! (Look in MyDocuments/Asheron's Call for their new home.)

June 23, 2005
Several reports of live attacks came in while I was out of town this past weekend. I've finally had a chance to put them up on the Infiltration June Events page here!

The reports are slow coming in from the Open Preview (if you're like me, you can't even get out of the town because of the lag! Stupid dial-up!!), but the first rare has already been discovered by Vitae Boy! Congrats! If there are any researchers out there who'd like to help get the ToD info for the site ready for launch on July 18th, please send me an email!

June 22, 2005
Update from Ibn: "In order to address an issue with our billing system, it is necessary to bring the authentications servers down at 5:45PM PDT / 8:45PM EDT / 12:45AM UTC for approximately 1 hour.

During this time players will not be able to log in to AC1,AC:TD Open Preview, or AC2. Players who are currently logged in should be able to remain logged in."

If you've made it in and have some good screenshots to share, send them in or post them here!

June 22, 2005
The Throne of Destiny Open Preview is now live!! Download the client and get your key from Fileplanet here!

File bugs here!

Screenshots work a little differently in AC:TD! A new screenshot folder will be placed into your My Documents named Asheron's Call. Screenshots are already in .jpg format! Simply send them to Maggie@thejackcat.com, or feel free to post your discoveries on the Destiny Forum here!

June 21, 2005
Yes, I've been out of town *again*, but before I left, I visited the Halaetan Isles during the Press Preview round of the upcoming expansion. Here's a few pics to whet your appetite until tomorrow, when everyone has a chance to STFU or go Blue! (See the AC: Throne of Destiny Open Preview and Preview FAQ for more information.) Make sure you also visit the Throne of Destiny articles page for a link to Paraduck of ACVault's Preview. It includes even more details and some fab movies!

I know everyone is eager to scour the land for new information and to submit their findings! To that end, I've created a Destiny Server Forum here! Please read this post for information on how you can help contribute!

More coming soon!

June 16, 2005
Jackcat Ranger Magicpipe II of TD has created a quest video of the Queen Quest! Visit the quest page to download! Very fun!

June 15, 2005
Anxious to see the new city of Sanamar yourself? Turbine has announced the Throne of Destiny Open Preview! You can find out more information on the Turbine site here and here, or in the Throne of Destiny section here and here!

Update: The latest Weekly Status Update is now available here!

June 14, 2005
New Throne of Destiny screenshots! Yay! Take a look at the new character generation screen; see a cool new building, plus more! You can find them here.

June 13, 2005
What can I say? I'm a slacker. Terrible, terrible. It's summer, after all. But! I've started poking through some of the submissions that came in recently and have posted a few more live events! Read about the ferocious Margul, Al'Cani, in the May events and read about some attacks on Eastham and Cragstone in the June events! If you participated in a live event, don't forget to send in a report!

June 8, 2005
The latest Throne of Destiny update is now up on the Turbine site here!

June 7, 2005
I get back from vacation, then get sick! I blame air travel. ;-) Sorry for the lack of updates! If you missed it, Ibn posted another status update on the 2nd. You can find it here. An announcement about lag and the Mausoleums on DT can be found here. Um... more coming soon!! Uh, really! I swear!
