 October 31, 2003
Minor updates to the Reign of Terror patch page, including a few new NPCs! Thanks to Mike HeXt II of MT and Swift-Jake!

October 30, 2003
Another busy day at work, but I finally was able to add more updates to the Reign of Terror patch page! Falatacot, Scarecrows and Bats! Oh my!! Lots of  info added to both the Undead Mechanic & the Renegade Mace quests. All the alloy pieces have been located, but I'm still missing a few screens. And go poison something!! ;-)

Royal Jelly, cool new masks, pretty portal icons, sizzling cows, fellowship updates and more. Best patch ever? Definitely best teaser ever! That's good enough to qualify for me!

As always, thanks to everyone who sent all the fabulous patch info! If you find anything I'm missing, please send me an email!

October 29, 2003
In the most remote areas of Dereth, tremors threaten to topple the mountains.
The ground heaves as thousands of Olthoi prepare to burst through to take back the lands of northern and western Dereth.

Thanks to Andon of MT & Oldin of HG for reporting this global which was broadcast just as the servers were going down for the patch! *does the patch day dance*!!

Visit the Reign of Terror patch page throughout the day for updates! Build Notes, etc. will be found here when they are available. As always, please send screenshots to jackcat@spacestar.net! Don't forget your @day command, and your in-game name!! :)

October 28, 2003
I missed getting it up yesterday, but you can now find Part 4 of the teaser here!

And there's even more exciting news! The date and location for the 5th annual ACPL has been announced! You can find a copy of the press release here! At least this year there won't be any complaints about the short notice! ;-) Mark your calendars!!

October 27, 2003
Another weekend without any updates! I should be ashamed! Good thing the teaser is being spread out over the month so I have at least *some* new content for you! ;-) You can now find the exclusive teaser pic on the patch page here!

October 23, 2003
I know, I know! I'm very behind on updates! But at least Part 3 of the October Teaser is now up here! State of the Network also updated with information on next Wednesday's patch! Yay!

October 16, 2003
Yes, I know there have been no updates! I've been really busy. More coming soon; I promise!! In the meantime, here's a fascinating article on the Top Ten Ways to Help the +Admins Help You!

October 10, 2003
The Letter to the Players for October is now available here! PK Lite Corpse Timers, Mask updates, and Fellowship changes! And good news! The patch is going up the last week of October! Why is that good news, you ask? Because my hosting company is doing some upgrading in the next week or two, so the site may be up and down periodically. Great timing, huh? :-)

October 6, 2003
 A level 200 nest has been defeated! Visit the Assault Against the Olthoi Invasion for details! Thanks to Oldin of HG, Mike HeXt II of MT, Jelly Belly of SC, Graelyn of MT for initial reports! If you have additional information of screenshots, please send me an email! Congratulations!! Note: if you went through a nest exit portal or turned in a gland, you can receive a token & title. Click here for a complete list, thanks to Peerless of LC!

Also, for those with tokens, please note this post from Ibn: "If you are expecting a title from Elysa, you should go get one sooner rather than later.

She is very busy and will not stay at the Rithwic festival grounds forever."

October 4, 2003
Nuhmudira has returned again to Candeth Keep for the 4th gland. Visit the Assault Against the Olthoi Invasion page for details! Thanks to Thase Skotoso and Dragon Viper of LC, Thalum of WE and Koma Kakali of SC for reports! A few more Nest Destroyers were also filled in, as well as the pic of the of the Adolescent Olthoi Matron, thanks to Systor of TD!

October 3, 2003
 Nuhmudira returns again for another Olthoi Gland. Thanks to Mike HeXt II of MT and Thase Skotoso of LC for the update! Thanks also to Chupon's Savior of LC for helping fill in some of the missing Nest destroyers on LC. Visit the Assault Against the Olthoi Invasion page for details!

October 1, 2003
 With all the added excitement of Elysa showing up in Candeth Keep, the Groundswell Patch Page was getting too bulky! I've organized all the Olthoi Invasion information and moved it onto its own page: Assault Against the Olthoi Invasion. You'll find the globals and locations for all the nests, pics and stats, plus the new sayings of Elysa, Nuhmudira and Antius Blackmoor, along with the new text and trophy drop! I've added as many of the global recipients that I could extract from all the email reports I got, but there are quite a few missing. If you know people who should be added, please send me an email!
