February 28, 2005
 Part 3 of The Tournament is now up on the Turbine site! Ibn recommends re-reading Assassination and The Malika and the Individual for additional insight on the Viamontian society!

I've also fluffed another blackmire quest into a full write-up with fabulous pics! You see the revised Blackmire 3: Halls of Hizk Ri here! Thanks once again to Capt Justice and Chunglee's band of re-rolls for assistance!

February 26, 2005
The Blackmire 2: Liazk Itzi's Temple quest has been updated with a full write-up and fabulous pics! Thanks once again to Talon of the Vale, Sultry Flint, Lurid Valor, Hey-U, Chungs mule, Mac Roe, Magnus the Merciless and Miss Courtney for escorting me through!

February 25, 2005
Another new piece of Viamontian fiction has been published on the Throne of Destiny site here! You can also find it in the Viamontian section here!

Check this link for the schedule of upcoming uninscribing events.

Update: Part 2 of The Tournament, a piece of Viamontian lore, is up is up on the Turbine site here! I don't know about you, but I can't wait for part 3!

February 24, 2005
A few new items have gone up on the Turbine site. First, Turbine has introduced a Member Loyalty, Beta Rewards program! You can find info on it here! Sounds good to me! I'm still bitter about the AC2 beta. ;-) Second, Ibn has posted his write-up of the expansion preview from the Press Event on Feb. 17th! You can also see a copy of my spiffy invitation here!

I've also recently gone back through some old email and found my pile of AC newsletters, the Annals of Dereth! I'm missing a handful of them, specifically some of the early ones, but I had a good many of them. Where available, I've added them to the different patch sections in the Auberean History section! If you have some of the ones I'm missing tucked away on your hard drive, please send me an email!

February 20, 2005
You won't believe it, but I've published some Letters! My vassal, Ned Cleversun, made me. He's been nagging me for years to resurrect the section. It was always one of the most popular ones on the site, so it's long overdue. It's just that as the game gets bigger, and my site adds more and more stuff, there's only so much a person can do! :) You'll find my vassal's opinion on the upcoming non-patch months, a hello from Iraq and a video tribute to the Devs (17.8MB)!

I rashly promised to fill in all the missing months in the Letters section about, er ...a year + ago. With some extra time these next few months, who knows! Maybe yet!

I've got more live event info to get up on the site still, so... coming soon!

I know everyone's anxious for Expansion news. I was invited to CA for an exclusive preview last week, but with that pesky job business, I couldn't make it. (WAH!!!!) Check out some exclusive screenshots on the Vault, to whet your appetite!

February 18, 2005
Another Live Event has been announced: Team Gladiator! You can find the schedule on the Turbine site here!

February 14, 2005
Turbine has released another before and after pic of the Throne of Destiny expansion. In addition, a screenshot of the Halaetan Isles can be found on the Turbine site here or in the ToD section here!

The State of the Network has also been updated with a few upcoming downtimes.

February 13, 2005
Reports from the live events have been pouring in! Because there's so many going on, I've moved them over onto a new page in the patch section. You can find it here! Thanks to everyone sending in their pics and stories! They're fun to read!! If I'm missing your own tale of glory, please send me an email!

February 12, 2005
The live events are in full swing, and they are fun, fun, fun! I've been too busy playing to get a lot of the event info up, but... coming soon! In the meantime, you can you see the winners of the Run For Your Life competition on the Turbine Site here!

More exciting news: Turbine has released the first Before and After pic of the Throne of Destiny expansion. And these beauties aren't even the final version! You can find the pics here!

February 10, 2005
Thanks to everyone who submitted info for the patch page and is helping fill in the missing pics! February is the month of love... weapons love, that is! New tiers of wield requirements, plus new fallen and banished weapons. New Viamontian info, Shadowed Lugians, new Reliquaries and portal gems, and a fancy new shield, new hat and new robe. And to balance out all that love, there's also Mausoleums of Hate, Shadowed Lugians and pet murder!

To top it all off, enjoy one of the upcoming live events. You can read the announcement here! I think this just might be the best patch ever!

February 9, 2005
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating the Infiltration patch page today as I'm able. If you need help sending screenshots, you can visit the Mail & Screenshots Tips page. Please include full stats with the icon showing, a fabulous pose, and your in-game name and server!

Roll-out Article and Dev Notes are now up!
