The following series of letters refer to another case of a site lifting my content and passing it off as their own. They even went so far as to put their own copyright notice on my pages! I have a form letter I send out about once or twice a week (a little less often since the site got more popular) asking sites to remove my content. In almost every instance, the response is, "I'm sorry, I didn't realize, I'll remove it right away." Occasionally, I'll run into a less pleasant attitude. My first public run-in was, of course, with PCPunks. In that case, the situation was a bit different; it was done unintentionally and we resolved it quickly and with little bloodshed! ;-)

Other battles, though, have proved to be less pleasant, as many of you know. I haven't really mentioned some of the more recent incidents on the site because I figured most people would like to read about fun things, not the boring tedium of the back-end crap work. But then, I thought, why should I discriminate against EpiK?! All offenders should get the benefit of my public scorn! Ha. Actually, the reason I'm mentioning this latest incident with the Ftuoils is because there were unintended consequences to our disagreement. 

When the Ftuoils refused, quite unpleasantly, to remove my content, their host, ACSage, stepped in immediately and shut them down. The Ftuoils then started a thread on Asheron's Lore to say nasty things about me. Several people, reading the thread, mistakenly took my disagreement with the Ftuoils to be a disagreement with ACSage! When ACSage was down for server maintenance recently, some even thought that I, or other sites, had taken legal action against them to have ACSage shut down! 

Nothing could be further from the truth. I'd just like to take this opportunity to publicly thank ACSage for their quick response to my request and to clear up any lingering misinformation. It's a sign of their professionalism and integrity that they recognize how vital it is to the AC community that original content be protected. Their complete unwillingness to host content taken from other sites is praiseworthy. So deepest thanks once again to Kadran and Fizzle and the other members of ACSage. I had a high opinion of your site before this incident, and have an even higher one now.
