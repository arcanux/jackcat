 July 31, 2003
 Asheron's Island section of the patch page updated with Asheron's Greater Raiment, Ash's undies from the 60-80 hive. Thanks to Oldin of HG and his archer Rolament!

July 30, 2003
It's even more tidying for today. The Explorer and Society Wands updates from several patches ago have been added to the Exploration Society Letter Quests write-up and Rewards Table. The Flag Stamps page was also cleaned up and pics added. The Ruined Amulets quest updated with a note on Sunstone Guantlets.

Update: Reports are coming in of new quest robe undies(!!) Asheron's Raiment. See the Asheron's Island section of the patch page for initial details! Thanks to Keyla of HG, Chupon's Savior of LC, and Xao Bow of LC!

July 29, 2003
The Casino Rewards have been updated with the changes from the Discoveries event! No more easy bunny slippers or green tea ice cream, but you can get quest and crafter trophies instead. Thanks to Indigo of FF for his expensive research!

July 28, 2003
Two more quests have been cleaned up into full write-ups. The Aun/Hea Romance newbie quest on Marae Lassel, and more currently, the Heart of Innocence/Weeping Weapons quest! Thanks to Danier and crew for the escort through the dungeon!

July 27, 2003
The Mace page has been updated with revised properties and random loot generated info! And continuing with the Title quest updates, the Olthoi Titles quest page has finally been put into coherent form! Find out how to get one of the most highly coveted titles in the game: Gardener Weeder! ;-)

July 26, 2003
This time it's Staves! In addition to the revised random loot section which lists max damage, material type, burden and speed ranges, I also added a list of typical spells found.

July 25, 2003
The Axe page has now been updated to reflect the new property information from the latest patch.

The Asheron's Island info on the patch page updated with info about the upcoming hotfix to correct the Brood Matrons' spawn timing.

July 23, 2003
 The ground trembles, casting stones from cliff faces and snow showering from the highest peaks.
The quake originates near the city of Eastham, but the shockwave can be felt as far away as Marae Lassel.
A piercing wail echoes across the ocean waters near Eastham, and a groundswell of Olthoi can be seen pouring over the Island to the east of the city.

Thanks to Seltor the Conquerer of HG and Archmage Webbandit of LC for the report of the new global!

Update: Whee! I'm on Asheron's Island! Well, ok, I'm really at work, so don't tell anyone! (portal from Nanto or Uziz).

Another update: My old work computer just can't handle it. I will have to rely on your reports! Updates will be added to the patch page!

July 23, 2003
I've gone through some more patch submissions and added numerous updates to A Swelling Tide patch page! I know some of you are patiently waiting for me to declare this the best patch ever! Heck, it goes without saying. But I'll say it anyway: best patch ever!! For @day alone I'd declare it best patch ever! Not to mention cool new tinkering updates, thrown weapons love, fancy new hats, the teeny tiny golem invasion and infestations of hippity hoppity golden gromnies! (Ya, I know that's a (bug, but it's funny!)

Harker's Head quest also updated with a few more gauntlet stats!

July 22, 2003
Finally, after many aborted attempts ('cause, well, I'm kind of gimpy), I was able to make my way down to the Catacombs of Opposition, the location of the new Olthoi Town from last month's Heart of Woe event. What's down there, you ask? Olthois on a stick! New NPCs! And fabulous new Olthoi Weapons! You can find the full write-up here. Thanks, as always, to Greeneye at ACMAPS for use of his fabulous maps!

And remember... they're coming to get you!!

July 21, 2003
The Harker's Head or Sunstone & Opal Gauntlets quest has been revised into a full write-up with fabulous pics! If you didn't get your share of Olthoi grubs in the Panaq quest, there's plenty for you here! I don't know about you, but after touring the Subterranean Farms, I'm not too keen on eating food coming out of Rithwic any time soon!!

July 20, 2003
The overhaul of the Tinkering section has been completed, except for a few missing updated icons.

The various titles quests in the Quests section received numerous updates. Salvage Titles and Hats were added, as was the Harbinger quest (which receives the Champion of Dereth title). Links to all the titles quests were placed on each of the quest pages.

Other quest updates include the addition of Martine's Mask & Robe, poses for the Rusted Weapons added to the Fishing quest, and many other miscellaneous details throughout the section.

July 19, 2003
I've begun the overhaul of the Tinkering section. The bulk of it has been updated with all the new effects from A Swelling Tide. Lots of the usual typos corrected as well. Thanks to everyone who pointed them out. And thanks to everyone who submitted info on the latest patch! I'll try to get the remainder added this weekend!

July 17, 2003
As promised, you can find my write-up and pics from the ACPL here! And a good time was had by all!

As many of you have heard, there's quite a bit of chatter about player interest in a new expansion pack for AC. To have your voice heard, head to the AC Expansion Survey and take a few minutes to fill it out! Note: If you've already filled out a survey (not the petition) on another fan site, don't fill one out again. Thanks!

And don't forget it's patch day! I'll be updating the A Swelling Tide patch page throughout the day as I'm able. As always, a reminder to use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address for any screenshots!

Update: Build notes are now available here!

For screenshot tips, visit the Mail and Screenshots Tips page!

July 16, 2003
Links to the teaser video which was show at the ACPL are now available here! My progress? Pics are scanned and ready to upload (captions to be added, time allowing), and I'll try to finish my write-up in time for the server down-time for Thursday's patch!

July 15, 2003
I know everyone is waiting to hear more about the ACPL. I would have worked on my write-up, but something TERRIBLE happened to me when I got home. That's right... I had to go back to work! ;-) Coming soon! In the meantime, you can check out another Blast From The Past.

July 14, 2003
I'm back from the ACPL. Tired, but back! Lots of good fun and conversation with tons of AC players, arguably some of the nicest people in the world! One such conversation inspired a new item for the site: The Daily Blast From The Past, featuring posts from the old Q&A board (to be updated daily). It's pretty funny to look back on some of the old answers and see just how far the game has come. But as we all know, the more some things change, the more others stay the same. Click here for the first daily Blast from the Past, then visit the Letters section for a small update. Find out the name of the new town, then bend Microsoft's ear about an AC issue out of Turbine's control - a new expansion pack!

July Teaser also added to the A Swelling Tide patch page. With luck, the teaser video shown at the ACPL will be available to everyone soon.

July 13, 2003
Greetings from after the ACPL! How was it, you ask? It wuz fun!! Unfortunately, I brought one of those old fashioned kinds of cameras with that stuff called "film," so it will probably be a few more days before I get pictures developed and scanned. I'll tell you all about it soon!

July 10, 2003
 Letters updated! I told you I'd try to get back in the groove!

Now it's off to Boston for me! I'll be bringing my laptop with me, of course, and depending on the festivities, I'll try to pop on a time or two for those unable to attend. Looking forward to seeing everyone!

July 9, 2003
Many many pages updated with the items from the last few patches. With the exception of the xp table and individual Beasties pages, most of the main sections should be up to date now. I've also started revamping the weapons pages in the Sports section in response to the latest Letters to the Players. The Dagger page is the first re-vamp.

As always, you'll find the quest and crafted weapons, plus the store bought/starter weapons, but I've decided to do away with the random selection of loot generated weapons (while keeping the cute little icons, of course!). Instead, I've combined the much requested max damage info with a description of the range of stats likely for the different types of weapons. Dirks also finally added. I know I'm missing many of the new max values. Re-researching for the umpteenth time gets a little old! If you'd like to help fill in the updated stats, please send me an email!

July 8, 2003
The ACPL is just a few days away!! *jumps up and down with excitement* If you want to browse through pics from previous years to get yourself in the mood, you can find them here:

Looking forward to seeing old friends and meeting new!

July 6, 2003
The Beasties page in the Pets section finally updated with all the level changes from A Perfect Paradox (page very slow to load). Creatures from the latest patch also added. Individual pic pages and XP table updates coming soon. The updated stats are in yellow and the white stats still need confirmation. If you have updated info for any creatures I'm missing, please send me an email!

July 5, 2003
I'm still plowing through the massive number of emails from the latest patch. I'm down to the last 75! Heart of Woe patch page updated with numerous items. And what a patch it was! New quests, new creatures, new titles, new spawns. I think it just might be... the best patch ever! (Better late than never, eh? ;-)

Many other pages on the site were also updated with miscellaneous information, including additional stats and poses on the Heart of Innocence page. Crafters an Collectors finally updated with the most recent golem heart rewards. Many quest pages and other pages updated throughout the site.

Thanks to everyone sending in updates! I've also started working on the backlog of submissions from previous months. The Stuff I'd Like page has been updated with new requests. Previous requests have not yet been updated, so if you don't see your submission yet, it's coming soon!

July 4, 2003
If you're looking to get flagged for the Heart of Innocence quest, you'll need to complete part of the Oswald's Dirk quest. A full write-up is now available here, complete with fabulous pics! If you like puzzles and heart-stopping adventure, you'll love this quest!

Numerous quests were also updated with new maps, courtesy of Greeneye at ACMaps! Congrats to the team on reaching an impressive total of 400 maps!!

Happy Independence Day!!

July 3, 2003
Drumroll please... Yes, the Letters have been updated!! I promised they were coming soon! What's a few months between friends anyway? ;-) I know I have a half a year to make up for. I think the sheer mass of my Inbox just made my head ache and my eyes cross and then I'd put aside the task for another day. I've decided to start small! A few letters for July to kick it back in gear. Then bit by bit I'll be filling in the missing months... um... soon! No, really! Cross my heart!
