EXCITING NEW SPELL RESEARCH IN DERETH!
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
It's the most exciting part of this month's event, in Maggie the Jackcat's opinion! Mages from all worlds have banded together to pool information on the new War Spells introduced in June's patch. There's been much gnashing of teeth, flaring of tempers, moments (or days) of frustration and despair, and the usual accusatory remarks tossed at Turbine for not doing things right! ;-) But who can't sense the thrill of an impending discovery, hovering on the edge of the horizon!

Incredible research is currently taking place at the Council of Collegium Arcani -- a group of players determined to unlock the secrets of the new spells. These travelling teachers will then use their new found knowledge to assist other players through demos and instruction. Building on each other's work, they are compiling a database of information as they methodically test new theories and formulae.

Two Darktide mages, Shattenkind, a well-known monarch famous for his role-playing skills, and the mysterious Brittle Mage, have apparently uncovered some of Dereth's magical secrets. Links to screenshots of "Ring of Fire" and "Searing Disc" have appeared on the Crossroads of Dererth's Collegium Arcani board! Also released have been some fascinating and mysterious missives by the two mages, which give hints to the elusive formulae. "Sentinels gone bad?" Jesse in disguise? These are just two of the theories bandied about by the non-believers! I, however, have complete faith in the citizens of Dereth and know that their hard work, intelligence, native craftiness and cooperation will soon pay off in knowledge hard won!

*Sigh* Ahhh, nostalgia. It all reminds me of the early days of Beta when the winds carried whispered rumors of "Portal Spells."

For more discussion on magic and the new spells, visit Crossroads of Dereth's Collegium Arcani board:
http://boards.xrgaming.net/cgi/cod/magicboard.cgi?view
Be warned, though! They sound like a buncha Archers, before the patch! ;)

MAGGIE THE JACKCAT'S KICK-ASS FABULOUS SITE NEWS!
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I just love checking my stats, to find out what people like and what they don't like. I thought others might like a quick rundown of the May stats of my Jackcat site. For the month, there were over 1,000,000 hits and over 133,000 page views. Unique user sessions have steadily risen over the past weeks and now average over 1,200 a day.

The most popular pages continue to be the quest pages, with the Shadow Armor and Atlan Weapons leading the pack. Frore and the Impious Staff are not far behind. The Runway continues to get amazing amounts of traffic, and generates quite a bit of mail as well. I was disappointed to note that I was *not* the most popular Runway model for May. (No, I won't tell you who was!) I am sure it could only be because I was on at the tail end of the month. Ha.

Volunteers for the Runway are stacking up, in part because of my limited play time lately. Thanks to everyone who has volunteered so far. I will try to get each and every one of you up on the site soon. Try not to lose your stuff in the meantime!

Letters, Maps, Portals, Coordinates, Fletching, Alchemy, Designer Wear and Beasties round out some of the top pages. Thanks to all for visiting! And thanks for all the fan mail. Sometimes it takes me a while to respond, but I appreciate each and every letter. I will continue to try and keep my ego in check in the face of your effusive praise and generous compliments! I am also happy to report that the mail requesting information on the Nexus Armor has slowed to a manageable trickle!
