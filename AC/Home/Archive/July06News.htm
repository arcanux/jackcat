July 28, 2006
A few more reports of Grael's initial attacks have been added to the Live Events page! The Toward Ancient Shores patch page has also been updated with additional submissions. More to come! New armor, a new island, new critters, and a new big bad! I think this just might be the best patch ever!

July 26, 2006
Grael appears! Visit the Live Events page for initial reports! If you have pics or a tale of your encounter with Grael, send it in!

July 25, 2006
It's patch day! Yay! I have a busy day, so I probably won't have much time to update the Toward Ancient Shores patch page until this evening. I've posted a few reports of the new island flagging to get you started! Please keep the submissions coming! Thanks!!

July 24, 2006
Today's weekly update has been added to the Dev Updates page!

July 23, 2006
The Rares page has received another update -- the Star of Gharu'n, a rare spear found by Ramgah of SC!

July 21, 2006
The Letter to the Players and Event Teaser are now available here and here!

July 19, 2006
Monday's update has been added to the Dev Updates page. Of note is the new date for July's event: Tuesday the 25th.

July 15, 2006
The Rares page has been updated with 2 more rares, the Guardian of Pwyll, a sword wielded by the Black Knight during recent events, and the Ebonwood Shortbow, a rare found by Nea Uarina of HG off an Othoi Ripper!

July 12, 2006
An exclusive teaser pic for July's event, Toward Ancient Shores (scheduled for July 20th), is now up! You can find it here. In addition, a new update from Frelorn regarding the authentication issues can be found here!

July 10, 2006
A Weekly Update is available on the Dev Updates page regarding the authentication issues. You can find it here!

July 7, 2006
The Live Events page once again received updates! Read the report of the WB vs RM fight on Darktide. Plus a new event opens in Arwic! Read about  the Shadow Vortex with the Grael's Claw reward and the Knight of the Golden Flame title!

July 3, 2006
Two more reports have been added to the Live Events page : the log from SC's event, thanks to TheKnight, and a second report from LC of the Squire of the Golden Flame!

Have a safe and happy 4th!

July 1, 2006
The Live Events page has been updated with even more reports: Chef Wubbie's Side of the Story, The Black Knight on SC, and the latest title from the Shadow Invasions: Squire of the Golden Flame! Thanks once again to everyone sending in reports and pics!!
