NEW QUEST SECTION ON CROSSROADS OF DERETH
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I'm excited to announce that I've been recruited by Allen Grey and Caeryn Dryad of Crossroads of Dereth to be the new Quest Editor! I've been working closely with them for weeks to get the section up and running and today it went live! Go check it out at http://cod.xrgaming.net. You can blame them if the updates to my Jackcat site have slacked a bit lately!

The section is designed in layers, so you can get as little or as much spoiler information as you wish. I've already received lots of emails congratulating me on my "promotion," with lots of offers to help! Forgive me if I can't get back to all of you right away. I truly appreciate all the mail, suggestions and enthusiasm for the project. Although I'm just slightly disappointed that no one mentioned what a nice job I did on the Yanshi starter quests. ;-) Special thanks to Avar Helgiv of Darktide for catching an error on the SoLL quest!

Most of all, I'd like to mention what a pleasure it is to be working with such a fabulously talented and fun group of people!

NEXUS ARMOR REVISITED
----------------------------------------------
Ok, once and for all: the Nexus Core Gem was one time loot, dropped when the brave Derethian heroes felled the Nexus Crystal during the Thorns of the Hopeslayer Event. Only one set per server. (Maggie the Jackcat gets mail almost daily asking how to get that cool Nexus Armor. The short answer: you can't!)

MAGGIE THE JACKCAT'S KICK-ASS FABULOUS AC SITE NEWS!
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Runway continues to be one of the most popular features of my web site. I first started the section because I knew that if I was curious about what things looked like, then others must be too. The chances, after all, of my 20-something sword fighter stumbling across a nice 112% Yumi are pretty slim! Once again I'd like to thank all the volunteers who have so generously shared their cool loot with the rest of us!

I've also been approached by someone who asked me to help put together a petition for game ideas to send to Turbine. I was hesitant, because I think Turbine and the game just ROCK (always wanted to use that phrase!) and I certainly don't want to appear critical. I tend to think they're very responsive to the players. However, if there's a lot of interest in this idea, send me an email and I'll forward it the petition organizer. Some of the items on the list are really spiffy. Here's a sample:

15. New char command. /V1, to my #1 vassal
18. New char command. /FA, to my friends list
23. Change lock pick to include a chance to disarm "traps".
25. Loot body option. If someone attempts to loot your body you receive a
message asking if you give permission to loot it. If you type yes then they
can get your stuff for you.

SCREENSHOTS OF SHADOW ARMOR WANTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A dynamic gaming environment is a lot of fun! But if you're trying to compile stats, it can be a big pain! With the Heroes' Respite update, Shadow Armor got lighter! I'd love pics of any and all Shadow Armor stats so I can keep the info up to date. If you have a few extra minutes to snap a screen shot or two, I'd appreciate it. Send pics to thejackcat@xrgaming.net
.jpg format preferred!

See you in the game!
