 September 30, 2001
 Real Estate Guide updated!! (Ok, ok! I'll udate something else soon! ;-). The road from Shoushi to Baishi has been added, as well as the Lin area. Hebian-to to Nanto updated with more cottages. And I think I've finally gotten all the locations sent to me added on the RE Coordinates page (except ones sent yesterday or today). Thank you!

September 29, 2001
 Once again, more updates to the Real Estate Guide. Several groups of cottages added to the Al-Arqas area; Hebian-To to Tou-Tou mansion added, and Glendon Woods Villas added. RE Coordinates page also updated. Thanks to everyone who continues to send in missing locations from the list!

September 28, 2001
More updates to the Real Estate Guide. See Mansions and Villas near the Eastham/Arwic coasts! RE Coordinates page also updated.

September 26, 2001
Real Estate Guide updated with housing from Cragstone to Rithwic. If you'd like a place on the river, you should check these pretty locations out! Uziz Area also updated with several more cottage locations. Rithwic to Eastham section started. More locations added to the RE Coordinates page. I also organized the section of coordinates that still need to be added to the guide, to make it easier to spot what might be missing.

Special thanks to Zakaro of TD for a pic of a Green Marshmallow Eep to even out the set on the Bunny Master Title Quest page! 

September 24, 2001
Quest Section updated! Now that the patch frenzy has died down a bit, I've started working on moving some of the partially written quests into the completed category. The latest additions are the Hammer of Lightning and the Bunny Master Title Quest. I just can't resist those cute little Eeps! (There are still reports of Larry being broken.)

In addition, I've started adding some of Greeneye of FF's maps (of AC MAPS) to the Travel Section, with links from the Coordinates page, and from the various quest pages! Thanks Greeneye! Nice work!

September 23, 2001
I've been working back through some of the piles of emails from the last month or so. Many miscellaneous updates. Thanks to Yoshi Kwang of SC for updating the reward for a Tan Rat Tail: 75p, not 50p. Crafters & Collectors page updated accordingly. Gyes the Intrepid sent Forest Lord Stats and updated Scepter of Might stats (no longer has the -20% mod) for the Arm, Mind, Heart quest. Many missing creature stats added on the Beasties page (I still haven't added the current month's creatures or last month's Tumeroks). Filled in some missing pics on the Tumerok Banners quest and the Reward Table. Stuff I'd Like page given a slight update with many of the credits for these changes. Thanks to all!

I also added the updated Lockpicks page in the Bargains section. 

Uziz Area housing locations added, as well as Coordinates page updated.

September 20, 2001
A few updates to the patch page. More creature stats added and magehart poses in his Energy Crown! Updated the For the Head page in the Armor section to reflect the lowered burden of Crowns. Tumerok Banners quest updated with 2 of the 3 new icons for the Gems. Thanks to Vitripius II of HG. (Others of you have sent in missing icons for this quest that have not yet been added. Coming soon!) More credits and some more housing locations added to the Real Estate Coordinates page.

September 19, 2001
Added a pic of Sunspiral holding the Dark Sorcerer's Phylactery on the Keep Your Enemies Closer patch page -- thanks to Julian's Touch for the pic. More pages updated in the Bargains section. I split the previous Arrows/Fletching into two separate pages: Arrows & Quarrels, and Fletching Supplies. Food page also updated.

More housing added to the Real Estate Guide. Zaikhal Mansions, Villas between Samsur & Tufa, the Al-Arqas & Xarabaydun Area and the Uziz Area (still not complete). 

I still have a batch of email to go through, so not everything sent in has been added yet. I'll try to get the patch page updated again tonight. As always, thank you!

September 18, 2001
Made some minor corrections on some of the stats on the patch page. Everyone knows how sloppy I am at copying and pasting! I hope noone *really* thought the new orb was AL:130 ;-)

Updated Peas page added to the Bargains section.

More reported Housing added to the Housing Coordinates page. Many of these reported locations have been confirmed and I hope to get the Real Estate Guide updated soon.

September 17, 2001
Cool looking orb thingy discovered on the Vesayen Isles! See the stats on the Keep Your Enemies Closer patch page. Cyllan of Chaos also sends info on the Guantlets of Marksmanship! More creatures added: Master of the Pack (Reedshark) and  Red Phyntos Drone. Thanks to Greymalk of WE.

I've also begun revamping the Bargains section after re-surveying the towns. So far, I've completed the Alchemy Supplies and Cooking Supplies pages.

Many more housing locations were sent in, but I haven't had a chance to update. I also still have quite a backlog of other information to get up as well. Thanks to everyone for your help and I hope to get everything up on the site soon.

I also wanted to thank Danier's Monarchy for taking me to get killed again on the SoLH quest. I had a blast!! Three times a charm, right? ;-)

September 16, 2001
Yet more stuff from the latest patch. Miracle Forest poses with the Obsidian Axe! Tharkun of MT sends pics of the difference between the Crafter and Infiltrator Amuli. More creatures added, pic of the Inquisitor Mask being worn and more! 

I also added the Yaraq North Beach properties to the Real Estate Guide. Since I'm still only about a 10th of the way through mapping all the housing, I've added a page of Coordinates for the locations reported to me so far, but that I have not yet placed in the guide.

September 15, 2001
It just keeps coming and coming. More patch stuff! Visit the Keep Your Enemies Closer patch page to see a Broken Virindi Mask, the Serpent's Fang found by Vermine of TD and more of the new creature pics and stats! Translated Asteliary Crater's Note also added, courtesy of Lucaze.

Glendon Wood housing added to the Real Estate Guide.

September 14, 2001
Eeeek!! Diyas al-Yat removes his head! See this hilarious pic on the patch page. I also added a close-up shot of the faces on the Soul Staff, stats of the Balsalt Golem (thanks to  Neveryll of HG and Brackish of SC). Stats to the Energy Crown added.

More coordinates added to the Real Estate Guide. You can now find information about the housing along the roads from Hebian-To to Tou-Tou and Hebian-To to Nanto. Pics still to be added.

Letters updated. August Letters Archived

September 13, 2001
Even more updates! Visit the Keep Your Enemies Closer patch page to see Shobloth posing in the Heavy Ursuin Coat. Vangar of FF poses in his Imbued Helm of the Simulacra, Brackish of Solclaim sends pics of the Basalt Blade with very interesting information on the creature drop! Frozen of LC, Modius Primus of MT, and to Ciang of MT all sent confirmation of the Dark Rain scroll found on a Dark Inferno. And Dark Infinity of HG send stats and pic of a Lugian Warlord. New spawn map on the Zone as well! This just might be the best patch ever!

September 13, 2001
More updates to the Keep Your Enemies Closer patch page! Shobloth and Radiant the warrior pose with Crystal Swords, Mohan Ram shows off the rainbowed Diamond Shield. New info on chests from my wonderful vassal Ned Cleversun. Ulgrim rumors also added, (and I forgot to mention the Town Crier sayings last udpate).

Stats to the Soul Staff added. Thanks to Merlins Minion of DT.

Pic of Danier fighting a Diamond Lord added! Thanks also to Psi of MT, Gyes the Intrepid of SC, Guillotine for stats on some of the new creatures.

September 12, 2001
The patch page is up for the Keep Your Enemies Closer event! New Shields! New Matty Robes! New Staff! New Creatures! And, of course, Houses! For more detailed information on the housing situation in Dereth, you can visit the new Real Estate Guide! Thanks to everyone who sent pics and stats in.

September 12, 2001
I usually try to post something fun for people to read while the servers are down for the patch, but the day was just too heartbreaking for such things; my mind was focused far from Dereth. My thoughts and prayers are with all of you who have been affected by these devastating events.

I still plan, as usual, to do a patch page for this month's update. For those of you sending screenshots, just a quick reminder to use the jackcat@spacestar.net address. 

September 11, 2001
I've finally finished updating the Vendor Rates page (page slow to load), including all those out of the way Peddlers and Wandering Bowyers! I'll be updating the various Bargains pages in the near future.

While rounding out the Vendors, I also added a few missing NPCs to the Crafters and Collectors page, such as Lilly of Glendon Wood. 

Thanks to everyone who has been sending mail, pics and info. I hope to get more of it up on the site soon. I'm behind in my responses (as always), so I apologize to anyone who has yet to hear from me with a personal thank you. I do appreciate all your help.

September 10, 2001
After an especially grueling week at work, I spent all my AC time playing the game -- finally hilting a sword after carrying around my Bandit Hilt for months, dying on the Sword of Lost Hope quest, etc. -- instead of updating the site. AC sure is fun!! More updates coming soon.

September 7, 2001
More places to hunt! Lots of great suggestions for some mid-level hunting! You can see the Levels 20-30 Recommended Hunting Areas here!

September 4, 2001
If you've been looking for Damien Saren's Aerlinthe Report since his hosting issues, it can now be found here. It hasn't yet been updated with many of the newer changes, but I at least wanted to make it available again. But good news! Tlosk of Harvestgain updated his maps of the Foundry and the Reservoir. I also updated the map links on both Damien's Aerlinthe Report and the various quest pages in the Quest Section here.

More Hunting Recommendations added to the Level 10-20 section. And I *still* haven't finished that page! More coming soon! ;-)
