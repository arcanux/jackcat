December 31, 2004
I should be back to updating the site soon. In the meantime, I wanted to wish everyone a safe and happy New Year's Eve! And please don't drink and drive!

December 25, 2004
Merry Christmas and Happy Holidays to everyone!

December 21, 2004
The Siraluun Headdress & Dress write-up has been updated with the new versions! If you have any poses or pics I'm missing, please send me an email! I also added the new Social Center portals a few days ago, so the route information in the Tourism section now reflects the updated shortcuts. Thanks again to Arboc of SC for this nifty feature!

The Billing Migration has finally begun! Click here for the latest announcement.

December 17, 2004
An update on the Billing Migration has been posted on the Turbine Site here. You can also find it in the patch section here! A client will also be propped today in preparation for the billing migration. Zone lobbies will be down during the prop, although you will not have to log out if you are currently in game. The end of Zone billing. It's a dream come true!!

December 16, 2004
I added a few more bits and pieces to the Calm patch page, including the forge locations and the double peg legs! (Arrrr!) Thanks again for the continuing submissions and sorry for the delay in getting them up! I'll be leaving town again tomorrow for the holidays, but I'll have my trusty laptop with me. I should be able to continue to update the site while I'm gone.

December 14, 2004
Four new Throne of Destiny articles added to the Viamontian Culture section: The Ruschk, Assassination, the Fall of the Fiun, and an Viamontian Armor! The Calm patch page also received more updates including another mask and a wooden peg leg to go with the hook! Arrrr!

December 10, 2004
I snuck out of town Thursday morning, so got a little behind on getting the patch page submissions up, but it's coming together! If you'd like to contribute, I'm still missing guise pieces and new feathers, claws and grooming products! Thanks, as always, to everyone who sent pics and info in!!

Gambling moves to the cities, portal love, revamped starter quests, new masks and guises, pirate hooks (Arrrr!), new quests, Siraluun reward updates and more. Not to mention a tale of tragic interspecies love! (The devs are twisted, I say. Definitely twisted.) I think this just might be the best patch ever! Or at least it would be if I could get some Siraluun lawn ornaments! ;-)

December 8, 2004
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating the Calm patch page throughout the day as I'm able. Visit the Mail & Screenshots Tips page if you need help taking screenshots. Please send full stats with icon and don't forget your in-game name!

Update: Roll Out Article & Dev Notes and now up!

December 4, 2004
The teaser for The Calm event is up in the patch section here! You can also see an exclusive teaser pic here!

December 2, 2004
The December Letter to the Players is up here! You can add your feedback on the Turbine site here!

There's also a new page on the Turbine Site compiling the In Concept and In Development Topics on a single page for easier reference. You can find it here!

I'm behind on getting your pics and stats up on the patch page, but I'll try to add more this afternoon. Thanks to everyone continuing to send in info!!
