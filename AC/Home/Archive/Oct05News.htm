October 29, 2005
I've added more updates to the Tricks and Treats patch page. Thanks to everyone continuing to send their finds in! Masks, pumpkins, costumes, candy, and Bael'Zharon wings! I think this just might be the best patch ever!!

A few Player sponsored festival events are being run over the weekend. Visit the Server News Forum for details!

Apologies for the extended downtime on the server. My server management company was recently sold and the new owners let things get into kind of a mess. We've been doing a lot of work on it over the last week or so to get it back in shape. Thanks for your patience!!

October 26, 2005
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating the Tricks and Treats patch page throughout the day. Don't forget to include full stats of items and your in-game name and server. If you can't find your screenshots, they are now under the My Documents/Asheron's Call folder.

October 25, 2005
Turbine has posted their latest Live Ops report! You can find it on the Turbine site here, or Turbine Live Events Write-Ups page here!

October 24, 2005
The Tricks and Treats teaser is now up! You can find it in the patch section here!

I've cleaned up another quest write-up and added pics plus the link to the latest map from Greeneye at ACMaps. You can read all about the Darling's Collar quest here!

The Xik Minru's Temple quest was also updated wtih a few pics of malfunctioning dye.

October 23, 2005
The Live Events page has been updated with even more Thunder Chicken sightings!

Just an FYI: there's some work being done on the server this weekend. You may see some periodic outages. Thanks for your patience!

October 22, 2005
Thunder Chicken strikes again! You can read about it on the Live Events page here!!

The Letter to the Players is also up, and I've posted my exclusive teaser pic for this month's event. You can find them here!

October 21, 2005
Once again, I've posted some live events reports on the Under Cover of Night live events page! Read about the imposing Thunder Chicken of Shoushi, being frozen in mid-air, and Rolling Balls of Death! Thanks to everyone who sent in their stories! They're great reading!

The Turbine Live Events Write-Ups page was also updated with their latest entry.

October 19, 2005
A few more reports have been added to the Under Cover of Night live events page! The Galvanic Knight made an appearance on TD, and Mr. P was protected from danger!

October 16, 2005
Several more live events reports are now up. Read about the Title to Own All Other Titles, more Friends of Mr. P, and a strange little tale about Stuff You Just Can't Make Up!

One more quest has been cleaned up with fabulous pics added. Read about the Books of Grael here.

I've also gone through the backlog of Allegiance Updates and Housing Residents. Thanks for your patience and thanks for all the missing pics!

October 14, 2005
It's that time of year! Vist the Under Cover of Night live events page for a few reports of a scary pumpkin that terrorized FF! The Turbine Live Events Write-Ups also has a new edition, this time of a Scarecrow Invasion!

Update: Another ghost report comes in, this time from MT! Today's Turbine updates include news on the Decal front, the tentative Oct. patch date (26th), a great story about Mr. P, and a few teaser pics!

October 12, 2005
Mr. P is one busy little birdy! Visit the Under Cover of Night live events page for a report of his revenge on LC! More pics from Mr. P's recent TD visit have also been added, as well as a few reports and pics from the Ghost Buster event on HG!

The Temple of Xik Minru quest has been updated with more quest details, pics of the dyed Blackmire Armor and information on the fabled Rose of Celdon! Thanks to everyone continuing to send in updates!!

October 10, 2005
A few more reports of Mr. P's latest visits have been added to the Under Cover of Night live events page!

I also finished going through all the Worn and Battered Weapons submissions and added them to the quest page here! I'm still missing pics, stats and poses of quite a few, so if you run across any I'm missing, please send me an email!

October 7, 2005
With today's Daily Update from Turbine, we get a sneak peek at October's patch! You can find a copy on the Turbine site or in the patch section here! Another event write-up has also been added. You can read about on the Turbine Site here, or on the Turbine Events Write-Ups page here!

I've also cleaned up another quest write-up, the Counterfeit Writ quest, here!

October 6, 2005
The Gateway to the Deep quest has been updated with a great write-up by Burberri of MT and links to maps by the ACMaps crew! If you'd like to get to the new island, you can find out how here! Give me 30+ more levels and I'll be right there with you!!! ;-)

October 4, 2005
Another great Live Event report has come in from MT. You can read all about the Ghost Attack by the Ghostbuster himself, Dread Lord Boba Fett, here!

Magicpipe II of TD has also completed 2 more great quest walk-through videos. You can download high, medium or low quality videos from the Augmentation Gems and King Bobo quest pages!

I've been doing a bit of cleaning up of some of the content from the last month or two. The Jacob's Axe quest and the Hea Bone and Hide Shirt quest have both been revised with pics and all the bits and pieces added to the proper databases.

The Townspeople Sayings page has been updated with the Marae Lassel spire towns' new sayings, and the Town Crier page from Under Cover of Night has been updated with the Marae Lassel Rumors!

I added a few more trophy rewards to the Friend and Foe patch page. I'm still missing the following if you have a chance to snap a pic of the examine panel for me: Green Mosswart Armband, Olthoi Larvae Steak, Kirit Zefir Wing, Phyntos Stinger, Lugian Commander's Insignia, Lugian Lieutenant's Insignia. (Thanks, Validus of MT!)

October 2, 2005
Mr. P has been making the rounds! I've added a few more reports of new titles, plus Saya al-Ahanra of SC's account of her wedding to Tempus Thauleez, complete with the cutest feathered ring bearer ever! You can read the reports here!
