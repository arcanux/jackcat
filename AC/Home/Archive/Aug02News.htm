 August 31, 2002
 Mage Academy quest completed! Yay! I must say, this dungeon drove me nuts! Special thanks to -Sol Chen for initial information, and to Artuim of MT and Julian's Touch of TD who are my friends forever! ;-) For whatever reason, I couldn't get it to work solo, but the guided tour was a blast. Locksmith page and Gems page also updated with Mage Academy quest items.

The Acid Cistern Core opened early this morning in Ayan Baqur. A few first hand reports, screenshots, and the Martine text are now available on the Cisterns page! Thanks to everyone who sent info!

August 30, 2002
I may be slacking on the updates again, but it's only because I am hard at work, researching a quest for a new write-up. Sadly, I am *stumped*!! If anyone knows how to get through the Mage Academy without 1) jumping on the head of a Copper Golem (or the head of a Banderling, or on a lantern); 2) attacking a Wood Golem through a wall; or 3) using a log out trick to get through a locked door, please send me an email and I'll be your friend forever! ;-)

The last of the colored Bunny Slippers added to the quest page.

August 27, 2002
My new computer is (for the most part) up and running! Yay! To get back into the swing of things, I've added another of the old lowbie quests to the Quests section: the Metal Round Shield from the Eastham Sewers! Thanks to Link from Hyrule of LC for bringing it out of obscurity!

A few more Tesserae and other miscellaneous updates added to the site.

August 24, 2002
The Fire Cistern Core (restricted to Levels 30-60) has opened in Stonehold! Thanks to Sigmore II for the early report!

Visit the Cisterns page for pics, first hand reports and a transcript of Martine's text and the sayings of the new NPCs in Yanshi!

August 23, 2002
Yes, I miss you too!! As I mentioned in my Letters section earlier this month, I've been having a few hardware issues. My first round of fixes didn't quite do it, so I ordered a new mother board and chip (and a new video card while I was at it! ;-). My box arrived today, so if all goes well this weekend with my mixing and matching, I should be back to updating in a few days. I do have the laptop to surf and do email, but I prefer working on the site on my desktop.

I have updated the Tesserae quest in the Quests section (still missing a few of the icons). -Sol Chen is helping to "audit" the section so that I have a 2nd pair of eyes checking over everything and compiling which ones are still in the incomplete "partial" stage and which ones may have outdated information. More coming soon!

August 19, 2002
As many of you have written to tell me, the search function just sucked! Haha. Well, it worked great for any content that was over 6-8 months old, since that's the last time the indexes were updated. The problem had to do with the Search function being a Front Page feature; the site has simply gotten too large to use the front page extensions. If I wanted to update the indexes, I either had to republish the entire site, or have the hosting company do it server side. Neither choice was practical on an ongoing basis.

So I fooled around with scripts and indexing spiders this weekend, working on an alternative. Then my vassal, Ned Cleversun, mentioned that google had a free "search this site only" feature. I gave it a try and it works great! The very newest content may not come up, but you can always find that here, on the New Stuff page. For the time being, I hope that alleviates some of issues with searching the site. I also plan to add a Site Map in the near future.

You can find the search page here.
