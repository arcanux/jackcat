 July 29, 2004
Turbine Nation and the ACPL are rapidly approaching! Ibn sent a reminder that if you want to attend the ACPL, you must buy tickets by the 2nd. Turbine Nation tickets will be available at the door, but those attending the lunch must purchase tickets in advance.

Also, the deadline for the Make-a-Monster and Make-a-Movie contests is midnight EDT this Friday.

July 28, 2004
The August Letter to the Players is up! You can find it on the Turbine site here or in the From the Darkness Born patch section here. The feedback thread is located here!

I also added a few more of the Singularity Weapons submissions. See the patch page for ones I'm still missing!

July 27, 2004
The download page has been updated on the Turbine website with a .zip archive containing the portal.dat file from the July 2004 event, and the cell.dat file from April 2004.

July 26, 2004
I've been busy with some behind the scenes maintenance of the site, so it may look as if I was ignoring you, but I really wasn't! More coming soon! :)

Update: SC, FF, LC and TD are down for emergency database maintenance. You can see this thread for details.

July 18, 2004
I've finally gotten a chance to add the pics and stats of the Singularity Weapons variations on the Treaties in Stone patch page and the corresponding quest pages (Singularity, Bound, Ultimate). If you have pics and stats of the ones I'm missing, or any of the mid or low Blackmire rewards, please send them in! Thanks to everyone who helped with the patch page! You're the best!

A new Blackmire quest, new moarsmen, Guruk Burun, Lin updates, fetishes and grave robbers! I think this just might be the best patch ever!

July 14, 2004
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating the Treaties in Stone patch page as I can today. If you'd like to help with submissions and don't know how, you can visit the Screenshots and Mail Tips page!

Update: Rollout Article and Dev Notes are now up here and here!

July 9, 2004
The July Teaser is up! You can find it on the Turbine site here or in the patch section here (along with an exclusive teaser pic!)! The feedback thread is located here.

Update: Turbine has posted yet another contest for Turbine Nation: the Make-a-Movie contest! You can find out more here!

July 8, 2004
Turbine announces a Make a Monster contest!! "Ever wanted to see one of your creations in the game? Now is your chance! Click here for more information."

Server maintenance dates (Tues, July 13th) and tentative patch day (Wed, July 14th) also announced here.

July 6, 2004
 Yet another quest from the Mired Hearts event has been added. Read about the Burun attacks and the little mosswart Bleeargh in the Diplomat Title quest (retired?)!

July 5, 2004
Another quest write-up buffed and fluffed, this time the Aun Golem Hunters! And if anyone knows if the Crystal Lord gives a title, please send me an email!

July 4, 2004
Happy 4th of July! I've cleaned up a few of the recent quests, including Olthoi Titles, Aun Tanua's War Taiaha, Renegade Stronghold & Bone Charm, and the Chorizite Veined Shield. Thanks to Magicpipe II of TD for running me through the Strongholds! The new Marae Lassel rumors also added to the Rumors section! Several creatures added/revised in the Pets section as well.

Uninscriptions will be available this weekend. See this post for details.

July 2, 2004
There are two new items of note on the Turbine Forums. The first is a piece of chess concept art by Scenario (also added to the Treaties in Stone patch section). The 2nd is a FAQ regarding UMC & the CoC.

Ibn has also posted a special offer for Monarchs, Figureheads and Ruling Councils at Turbine Nation. You can see the post here!

A pic of the dyed lugian helm also added to the Pillars Made of Sand patch page!

July 1, 2004
Damien Sarin of FF and Deven-Kenyon of SC report that Gabrielle's message has once again changed and a new Renegade Stronghold has opened up! Visit the Pillars Made of Sand patch page for details!
