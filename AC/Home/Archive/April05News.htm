April 28, 2005
Finally! The date for this year's Turbine Nation / ACPL has been announced! It's being held at the Rhode Island Convention Center on Saturday, August 13th, 2005! Yay!! Mark your calendars now! If you've never been to one, they're boatloads of fun. You can see last year's write-up here. Can't wait to see everyone again!

April 25, 2005
Thanks once again to Angelina Rayne of HG for reporting on the Viamontian attack in Ayan this weekend! The winner for the DT Last Man Competition has also been posted on the Live Events page.

Ker-Lin of FF has also sent in a helpful guide to finding War Scrolls in Steel Chests. You can find it here!

Thanks also to the players who responded to my off-hand comment offering to hand out mini-research quests for those looking for something to do while waiting for AC: TD! :)

Two new articles are up on the Turbine Site! Read the updated Level Cap FAQ here, and a new piece of lore, Sanamar Under Siege here!

April 22, 2005
Turbine just posted 2 big announcements! First, the release date for the expansion pack has been pushed back to July 18th. I for one, am relieved! This way, it's not coinciding with my week long trip to Disney with my nieces! ;-) Second, Jessica Mulligan will be leaving Turbine. Wah!! You can read the details in the April Letter to the Team.

This week's Post of the Week is up on the forums. Congrats to Kelvar Silvermace!

April 21, 2005
Another quest has been revised into a full write-up! Looking for Writs of Refuges? Visit the three Power Forges along the shores of the Inner Sea! Thanks to Capt Justice and crew, once again, for assistance!

April 18, 2005
I've added a few more live event reports to the site! Triple and his wife Demonwoman fought a Golem Attack on HG and got some nifty titles as a reward! Pookie's been out and about again on SC, trying to find his way home. You can read about it here!

April 15, 2005
Taxes are done (yay!), so I can get back to updating! ;-) Add another critter to the list of those who just can't stay out of the Yaraq orchards: Rats! Read Angelina Rayne HG's account here!

Today the forums are 1 week old! Thanks to everyone who stopped by and is making it the fun, laid-back kind of place we envisioned it to be. And congratulations to William the Bat for writing the first post chosen as the Post of the Week!

April 11, 2005
Last night, Morningthaw had a run-in with a Viamontian named Sir Giancarlo di Lotila! You can read a few first-hand reports about it here!

Update: I've had several reports that Pre-Orders have been found in stores! A Press-Release also went out today about the pre-orders. You can find a copy here! A few new pieces of Concept Art and 2 new screenshots are also up here and here!

April 10, 2005
Thanks to Capt Justice and his up-for-anything friends, I've finally gotten another quest write-up buffed and fluffed. Find out how to earn millions of XP, a fancy crown and a big pile of lore in The Moars!

The April Events page received several more entries. Those apples in Yaraq must be super-yummy-delicious. Even the Viamontians are after them now! You can also read about a Scavenger Hunt and a Mad Cow in the Marketplace! Click here to begin!

Thanks to everyone who has registered or stopped by on the new Forums! If you haven't popped in yet, you can find them here!

April 8, 2005
A few more events occurred in Dereth last night: a Golem Attack and a Viamontian Raid!

And now for a big announcement! Today is the grand opening of the Jackcat Forums! I've always said no in the past, but I decided it was time to give them a go! Please stop by and say hello. You can find the Forum Roolz here and a Welcome message here!

I'd also like to introduce The Jackcat Rangers! The Rangers are a group of volunteers who will be helping on the site in a more organized fashion. I'm pleased to welcome them and look forward to their help in making the site even better than ever!

April 7, 2005
More lore! A new Viamontian piece has been published. You can read The Execution on the Turbine site here, or in the Viamontian section here!

Ibn also posted an introduction to the Codemasters' Community Manager for those of you with questions on the European distribution.

April 5, 2005
Weren't able to attend Quiz Night, but curious to see how you would have done? Visit the Events Page for a list of the questions and find out who the latest Lore Masters are (:preens)! A few more April 1st events also added!

Update: A press packet for Throne of Destiny has been released! See a few new screenshots, the Viamontian Knight and the Fiun, here! You can also see some Concept Art and a Fact Sheet which includes System Requirements. Last, if you did yet see the ToD preview movie, you can download it from the Throne of Destiny section here!

A new story, Discovery, is also up on the Turbine site here, or in the Viamontian section here!

April 3, 2005
A new event -- Quiz Night -- is scheduled to take place today at the Shoushi Meeting Hall. You can find the schedule on the Turbine Site here. You'll need a team of 4, and don't forget to bring your own book!

You can also read about another April Fools prank perpetrated by those cheeky +LeOs here!

Another Easter Egg has been added to the Odds 'n Ends section. Find out the secrets of Dereth Cows here!

April 1, 2005
I've made a new page to accommodate live events for the month of April and added to the Infiltration event section. You can read my account of the Shadow Invasion on TD last night. Unfortunately, I didn't see much as I spent most of it at the lifestone. ;-) A second item can also be found on the page. It's April Fools and Sammy the Drudge has been up to some hijinks in Dereth! You can read the story here!

I'm still taking advantage of the patchless month to fill in some missing bits and pieces on the site. The Easter Eggs section has finally been updated with the Chest of Camping Ineptitude! Expect a few more entries to be filled in over the next few days!
