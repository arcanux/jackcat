 April 26, 2003
Numerous updates to A Gathering of Colors patch page! Hookable items, updated Soul Staff graphics, Special Tokens, and more! Fletching Steps for the new Spikes added to the Darts & Spikes (stats can be found here). The updated rewards table for the Tumerok Banner quest has been filled out. Special thanks to Archess-ney of SC! Shadow Armor also updated with the new Shard info! Thanks to everyone continuing to send in patch information!! I'd still love to get icons of all the sharded Shadow Armor. If you have one I'm missing, please send me an email!

In case you haven't heard, there's a new test server open this weekend called Shadowclaim. Already reports have come in about slaughters in the Marketplace, and sightings of Gaelan, Pookie, Bael'Zharon. King Bobo was even spotted in the Tou-Tou watchtower!! Find out more information here! Ken Karl also comments about the hardware upgrade in a new Letter to the Players.

April 24, 2003
A Gathering of Colors patch page received tons of updates! Updated Shadow Armor, new Tumerok Banner rewards, new and updated Assault Weapons, Spikes, Graphic Updates, and best of all, a Holy Handgrenade!! Not to mention Fish Boots, shiny Treasure and a Hangover Cure! I think this just might be the best patch ever!!

Thanks to all the great patch submissions! If you'd still like to help, I'd love to fill out all the updated shadow armor info! Check the patch page for missing types.

April 23, 2003
Surprise, surprise! Turbine unleashes another stealth patch. It's today! I'll be updating the A Gathering of Colors patch page throughout the day! As usual, please send any screenshot submissions to jackcat@spacestar.net Dev Notes can be found here and Town Crier Rumors located here!

For tips on taking great screenshots, visit my Mail and Screenshots Tips page!

The patch info is pouring in! I have an appointment for a hour or two, but more updates to come later this evening! In the meantime, everyone who sent a pic of their great new weapon taken outside in the dark, go back inside in the light and take one again!! ;-)

April 22, 2003
Fetid Dirk added to the quests section. Numerous quests received minor updates, including the Spear of the Given Heart which may now be given! Thanks to  Sr'Questor of FF for the info! Various Sports and Apparel pages updated with new stats.

April 20, 2003
The Overlord's Sword received a major update during A Perfect Paradox. Find out the details in the latest quest write-up, complete with fabulous pics! That's a lotta Tumeroks! Special thanks to Danier and Blodshand of TD for the escort through the Dires.  Swords page also updated.

April 19, 2003
The Tusker Sprint spell added to the Bobo quest, thanks to Amaruk of MT. The Skeletal Atlatl added to the quest section. The Teaser and Letter to the Players for the April patch, A Gathering of Colors, added.

April 18, 2003
I promise I haven't abandoned you! I really have been swamped at work. Plus, er, too much TiVo! (I am slave to reality TV!! :) While I'm finalizing the details to the Overlord's quest write-up, I have this small update for you: a link to the announcement by Turbine of this year's ACPL, to be held in Boston on July 12th! Will Maggie the Jackcat be attending? *shakes her magic 8 ball* Outlook looks good!

April 13, 2003
The Caulnalain Vestibule quest updated! Item Spells were also updated with the long overdue new durations, and with the changed icons! All the new Atlatls also added to the Thrown Weapons page in the Sports section!

April 12, 2003
Whew! Finally got a breather at work. I've started updating the various quest pages with some of the recent changes. The Gift Box quest, the Crystal Minds, Shattered Souls quest and the Fenmalain Vestibule quest updated!

April 8, 2003
Once again, I'm just swamped at work. I can, however, say: more coming soon!
;-)

April 2, 2003
Yes, again more updates to A Perfect Paradox patch page! Thanks to Sher Khan of HG for pics and stats of the Dev Team K'naths. Description on Blue Orb from the Hieromancer's Armor quest also updated, as well as other miscellaneous additions!
