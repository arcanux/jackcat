The Dev Updates page has been updated again with the latest weekly update: news on advertisement and hardware upgrades!

May 26, 2006
The Dev Updates page has been updated with some exciting news: the announcement of Digital Distribution for AC! You can find the original announcement on the Turbine forums here!

May 17, 2006
It's patch day! Yay! I'll be updating The Price of Loyalty patch page throughout out the day as I'm able (probably a little slower than usual for the next month or two still with the new baby, but hopefully better than last month!). Please remember to submit full stats and great poses for new items, and action shots as well as stats panels for new critters, and don't forget your in-game name and server! Screenshots can be found in the My Documents/Asheron's Call folder. Thanks!

Update: Rollout article and Dev Notes are now up here and here!

May 15, 2006
The latest weekly update can be found on the Dev Updates page here! Patch is still scheduled for Wednesday the 17th!

May 12, 2006
The Letter to the Players and the Teaser for the May event are now up in The Price of Loyalty patch section. You can find them here and here!

May 10, 2006
A few more reports have been added to The Live Events page, including a new log with Adso! You can find them here! If you don't know how to log in-game chat, simply type /log logname.txt (with logname replaced by the name you want to give your log) in the chat bar in-game and it will be saved in your ProgramFile//Turbine/Asheron's Call folder.
