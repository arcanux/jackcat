 September 30, 2003
Looks like things are heating up! Jyng of WE reports a new global on WE and DT:

High Queen Elysa Strathelar and her entourage arrive at Candeth Keep. They pour over plans to assault a vast network of hives that extends beneath the majority of Dereth. Antius Blackmoor stands at her side as forward scout, and Nuhmudira acts as her chief advisor. The three join the forward guard there and await assistance from any who would aid in removing the Olthoi from Dereth.

September 29, 2003
The Groundswell Patch Page has been updated with details from the continuing fight against the Olthoi! Over the past few days, they've been driven back from Kara, the Crater, the southern landbridge, Danby's Outpost, Dryreach, Stonehold, Beach Fort, Mountain Pass, Hidden Valley and the North Esper Mountains!! Special thanks to Tara Malkav of MT, Julian's Touch of TD, Zeral of LC, Rising Phoenix, and Raedwulf of TD, among others, for details and screenshots! Congratulations to all!!

If anyone has a pic or stats of the Adolescent Matron or Queen, please send me an email!!

September 26, 2003
Another Nest has been discovered near Kara! Thanks to Ahms of SC for pics and info!! Again, details available on the patch page!

September 24, 2003
Julian's Touch of TD again sends details on the 6th Matron Nest! Also added to the patch page are a few Olthoi Invasion extras: a map by Thase Skotoso of LC marking the nest so far, and a video of one of the Matron Nests made by Julian's Touch of TD!

September 22, 2003
Yet another nest has been found!!  Tara Malkav reports from MT and Julian's Touch sends word from TD! Visit the patch page for details!

September 21, 2003
A few more miscellaneous details added to the Groundswell Patch Page. The Olthoi Armor quest write-up was also revised to add information on the 20+ and 80+ armor! Special thanks to War Wizard of Chaos - HG for directions and diagrams through the 80+ hive!! Thanks also to Aargau of DT and Zen Tek Ken of FF for pics and stats of the Matron and 60 Nest!

September 20, 2003
The fight against the Olthoi continues! Bella Raven of TD destroys a level 60 nest in a 20-39 dungeon! Details added to the patch page! If anyone snapped a pics and stats of the Matron or stats on the nest, please send screenshots to jackcat@spacestar.net!

September 19, 2003
Zen Tek Ken of FF discovers a way to repel the Olthoi Invasion!! Details added to the patch page!

September 19, 2003
Lots of updates to the Groundswell Patch Page! Flying Olthoi, Icon Love, new and improved Olthoi Armor, PKLite, random slaughter and a carpet of corpses, not to mention... chickens! Chickens that lay eggs! Best. Patch. Ever!

The Survivor premier and a new patch all on the same day? Now that's entertainment. Arrrrr!

September 18, 2003
It's patch day! As always, I'll be updating the Groundswell Patch Page throughout the day! Teaser (now available in French, thanks to dEOS of SC!), Rollout Article and Build Notes will be found here.

Just a reminder to please use the jackcat@spacestar.net email address when sending screenshots. If you need instructions on taking screenshots, please visit the Screenshots and Mail Tips page for help! Thanks!!

Now STFU and go pink! ;-)

September 16, 2003
I know the updates are a little slow, but I promise... more coming soon! And just in case anyone missed it, patch day will be Thursday! Yay!

September 12, 2003
The teaser for the September patch has been posted! For a peek at an exclusive teaser pic, visit the Groundswell page here! Purple armor! Looks like I'm going to have to get me some of that!! ;-)

September 10, 2003
I finally got a chance to write up the Olthoi Armor quest after my jaunt through this past weekend! Thanks to Latte of TD for the tour through the 60+ hive and to Rokkette of FF for directions through the 40+ hive!

September Letter to the Players also added. Just a reminder that the servers will be down tomorrow for maintenance. See the State of the Network for details!

September 5, 2003
The Sword of Lost Light quest write-up has been updated with the changes from the latest patch, along with the improved Enchanted Fire Arrows! Timers were also updated with several corrections, thanks to Legion of Light from WE! And I'm thankful to Capt Justice of TD for sending in details for the jump section on the Harbinger quest. Very useful, as my own foray, sadly, ended in a magma pit. ;-) Full write-up coming soon!

September 4, 2003
The last two Harbinger trophies from Into the Darkness have been added, thanks to Amakua of LC and Nauscicaa!

September 3, 2003
 State of the Code updated with a Resolution for black 3D area under Windows XP.

Thanks also to Koma Kokali of SC for the Primeval Shin Bone and to Mike HeXt of MT for the Drudge Sage Charm!

September 1, 2003
As I've long known, if it's not in the quests section, it doesn't exit! I've pulled the info for obtaining a Writ of Refuge from various pages of the site and put it into a full write up. You can find it here!

Trophies for the Harbinger quest also added to Crafters & Collectors page. I think the only ones I'm still missing are the Drudge Sage Charm, Banderling Slayer Scalp, Insidious Monouga Idol,  and Primeval Skeleton Shin Bone. If you have a screen of the examine panel, please send me an email!
