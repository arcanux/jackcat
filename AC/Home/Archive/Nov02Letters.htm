You can get money the old fashioned way: by hunting and selling your loot! Of course it means that I'm constantly overburdened, but I support myself quite easily. Good luck! I'm glad you like the site!

November  2002 - What Does [sic] Mean?

Dear Maggie,

I have been a fan of your site for a very long time. I luv it!! I did have a question though.... I've seen many times on your site the letters [sic]... Maybe I'm just dumb, but I have no idea what this means. Couldcha help me out? Thanks!

Orcrist of TD
Level 55 Dagger


Hi Orcrist -
Sic is Latin for "thus" or "so." When it appears in brackets [sic] it indicates that quoted material is being precisely reproduced. Usually it is used when the quoted material has an error in the original. In other words, when you see [sic], it's my way of saying, "No, that's not a typo, Turbine spelled it wrong." or "Yes, I know it should be 'its' but Turbine, once again, is using 'it's' instead." You see them a lot on my site because Turbine didn't specialize their Proofreading skill. ;-)

- Maggie the Jackcat
