October  2002 - Gaerlan Quest Updates

Hello Maggie!

Ragoon from FF here again. I think that there is still a lot of confusion about what exactly triggers what in the Gaerlan quest, especially since the patch. I've done the quest several times, including three times in a row today and I think I have all the triggers down. First the guardian portals. These portals appear apparently either randomly or when one of the elementals that is at the essences dies. I was there when the portal spawned and there were no named monster or anything like that, so I believe the "unique creature" from the letter to the players is just one of the elementals that is specially tagged.

After the guardian golem is killed, the portal to the guardian lair disappears. It reappears when Gaerlan is killed. So if it's not around for a long time, it's probably that someone messed up the quest and it hasn't reset.

The next issue is when the portal for Gaerlan's dungeon (i.e. Icy Demise, etc.) will spawn, other parts are just for people getting flagged to enter. The portal for these dungeons appear when the portals to their related proving grounds open. So after Gaerlan is killed, the portal for the specific level restricted dungeons wont appear until the Asheron's Platinum Golem gets his pyreal nugget and the appropriate proving ground portal spawns.

Important: Mage flag for getting items is broken this month. My mage friend Gaipan Imperiled, Vulned, Drained, War Critted and festered for two Gaerlan spawns and got nothing. On the third run later that day, he used his racial melee skill and got his items.

*So moral of the story is if you start the quest, finish it. Or else everyone else is going to be waiting for a week or more on that shady Guardian Lair portal*

Ragoon from FF

October 2002 - Gaerlan's New Improved Sword

Since the update it seems Gaerlan has gotten a few new imbued special properties on his Sword in the 60-79 level quest. Instead of just 25-50 Human Slayer, it now has Crippling Blow and Armor Rendering on it too! This causes anyone without a shield to get critical for anywhere from 100-225 in one of the three swipes of his triple striking sword!

Most of the time if you're caught without a shield and get a crit on you, you rarely see the end of the quest. Melees with a diamond shield with level 7 banes get slaughtered anywhere from 8-30 seconds, depending how fast you can heal. Our group died 3 times before we finally made a plan to create a wall of melees and have the archers and mages fire away. The only problem is the melees can't attack or he will attack the wall and everyone is dead in 3 min. You also have to take into account the level 6 imperil usually cast on you by the elementals.

Since the update, the portal into the loot room stays closed until Gaerlan is dead.... So no more getting your critical hit and running through to the end. Also, there's a portal back into the library now, and be aware there is a timer on how long you can use this portal. A few of our group members were not able to get their quest items because of this. I only know about the new improvements for the 60-79 section; I'm not sure if they've changed the other swords in the other dungeons. I think they've added a new and better challenge to the quest now. Love your site!

October  2002 - Corrections on Gaerlan's Citadel Quest


I love your write-ups. Most of the time you've got it so right it's amazing.

However the (admittedly confusing) Citadel quest write-up needs some corrections:

- The mountain route is a bug. You won't be able to do that next month. Waiting for the Guardian portals in Eastham will be the only way.

- You can ID the door you're trying to kill. It doesn't regen because of that.

- Trail#4 -- Reverence of the Ceremony
(First, shouldn't that read "trial" not "trail"?)
You can run down the stairs. Do not speak or SPELLCAST; I'm not sure about battle mode. We had the most success when we walked in the actual ceremony chamber. If you speak/spellcast in THERE, nasty golems spawn and try to kill you. Sending tells seems to be okay.

- You must complete the Proving Ground trials within 3 hours of giving the nugget to the golem, or you'll no longer be flagged for the Proving Ground portals.

- You can log off in the landing zone in the last dungeon, where you fight Gaerlan.

- You can get the imbued seal and portal gem, and hold onto them for days before using them. If you're having trouble getting a large enough group for your Gaerlan assault together, it's a good idea to do that. If nothing else, you can get another seal and gem after 3 hours (3 hours after picking up the seal pieces, that is), so when you pick things up in a few days you're no WORSE off than trying to kill Gaerlan and failing.

- I recommend you emphasize in the last section (the Kill Gaerlan part) that you make sure you have at least one mage with you, and that this mage is capable of casting Frailty, Imperil, AND slash vuln. We went with someone who could only cast the latter two, and 8 of us couldn't beat on Gaerlan hard enough to reduce his health under 90% without Frailty reducing his regen rate.

Thanks, and keep up the great work! I hope things at your *paying* job let up so you can get back to Dereth!

Shukar Jhusen of TD
Level 66 Swordfighter


Thanks to everyone who sent in information on the updates to the Gaerlan's quest. I'll try to get the write-up revised soon!! In the meantime, I'm sure people will appreciate all the tips above!
