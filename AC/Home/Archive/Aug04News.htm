August 30, 2004
The updated stats for the Exploration Society Weapons updated with the changes from From the Darkness Born.

Turbine has also announced an agreement with Jolt Online Gaming Ltd regarding European servers! You can read the press release here.

August 28, 2004
The Hidden Entrance quest has been cleaned up into a full write-up with fabulous pics! Find out how to get a festive island shirt here!

August 24, 2004
The September Letter to the Players is up! You can also find a copy in the Sept. patch section, A Small Victory, here!

August 22, 2004
Find out how to get your own big giant eyeball! The Bleeargh's Gratitude quest has been revised into a full write-up with fabulous pics!

Turbine also published Div' of HG's movie (the winner of the Make-a-Movie contest) on their website. You can find it here!

August 15, 2004
The From the Darkness Born patch page continues to receive tweaks. Aerlinthe updates, Bleeargh is back, new war paint, big giant eyeballs, wasps updated, gromnie love and new winged armor, new fellowship spells, and TWO new subways!! I think this just might be the best patch ever!!

There is also a new Transition Diary up on the Turbine website as well as a copy of the expansion pack presentation from Turbine Nation available for download!

I also want to say hello back to all the people who have written to me after Turbine Nation and ACPL! I haven't had time to write anyone back yet, what with the patch coming on the heels of my return home, but I had a great time too! It was great to see old friends and meet new ones. Pics coming soon! (Soon is a relative term, I'm sure you know. ;-)

August 12, 2004
I've made a good start on the patch page, but I still have a huge pile of mail to go through. If you don't see your submission up on the page yet, it's coming soon! Thanks to everyone who sent in info!!

August 11, 2004
It's patch day! Yay! If you need something to read while the servers are down, I finally posted my ACPL write-up last night. Pics to follow soon!

As always, I'll be updating the From the Darkness Born patch page throughout the day as I'm able! If you'd like to help, but don't know how to take screenshots, you can visit my Mail & Screenshots Tips page for pointers!

Update: Roll Out Article and Dev Notes are up here and here!

August 9, 2004
Just stopping in to say hi! I'm still on vacation in Rhode Island, so if you're looking for my Turbine Nation/ACPL write-up, it's coming soon! :) For now, I've added some links to a few write-ups on the Turbine Nation 2004 page! If you've got a link I'm missing, send me an email! Or, since my camera batteries died half way through the day, if you've got some good pics, send them in and I'll add them to my eventual coverage!

August 7, 2004
I'm back from the evening's social festivities and found the exclusive teaser in my inbox when I returned!

The 3rd and 4th place finalists in Turbine's Make-a-Monster contest were also posted on the Make-a-Monster page!

August 6, 2004
I'm off to Turbine Nation and the ACPL! See you all there! :)

August 2, 2004
Ibn sent word that the ACPL part of Turbine Nation is now sold out! Tickets can still be purchased at the door for the rest of the event for $40.

Ibn has also published an article about Hardware Upgrades for FF, LC, SC, and TD. You can read it here. In addition, the Turbine AC website and forums will be migrating to a new datacenter on Wednesday the 4th. Expect some downtime as the DNS propagates.

I know updates have been a little slow lately, but... it's politics season, and, as those of you who know me can attest, I'm obsessed!! ;-)
