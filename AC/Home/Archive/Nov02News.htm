 Nov 30, 2002
The Empyrean Ice Propylaeum has been added to the Quests section! Learn how to get a Frigid Bracelet with Minor Coordination. This quest, like the other Propylaeum quests, also rewards you with one of the four Prism Fragments necessary to craft the Helm of the Elements.

Nov 27, 2002
A pic of the Circlet of Supremacy being worn added to the Gaerlan's Citadel quest. Thanks to Tabitha of MT for posing!

Nov 25, 2002
 The Harbinger returns! Yuric the Cruel of MT reports on the battle and shows off his prize: a Royal Coat of Arms Flag. He also received the title "Champion of Dereth" in exchange for a token. Read more on The Iron Coast patch page!

Nov 25, 2002
The Water of Mount Lethe quest has been updated to reflect the changes from the latest patch. Find out how to get your Scroll of Mount Lethe recall here!

Nov 24, 2002
Another old quest write-up finished. If by some miracle you've never been on the Stone Tool quest in the Artifex Vault, you can find out everything you need to know here! A brief description of the Water of Ithaenc also added.

Several creatures in the Pets section updated with the latest patch information, including Virindi, Tuskers, Reedsharks, Humans.

Nov 23, 2002
The last remaining section of the Apparel section has been updated to match the newer format seen in the Armor section. Clothing is now broken down into Quest/Crafted Clothing and store bought clothing. Formal Wear has been updated with Wedding Raiment and the former Designer Wear page was removed and replaced with a list of where to buy different colors of basic store bought clothing. If you want that perfect shade of tunic to complement your Covenant Armor, this is the place to look!

The Tusker Titles quest page was updated with links to three great new maps by Greeneye of ACMaps: Tusker Quarters, Tusker Barracks, and Tusker Pits.
 

Nov 22, 2002
The Scrolls page updated with a few more creature drops. Thanks to everyone sending them in! Also, as many of you pointed out, I was missing the Martine Retreat's master mage info in the Bargains section (in Rithwic), important info for those low level mages. Actually, the whole town was missing. I surveyed it when it first came out, but forgot to update the pages!! Oops! Thanks also to the many Tip Jar contributions I received this patch, and for all the orders made through my Amazon.com referal page. I love it when people give me money! Hmm... did that sound too crass? ;-) I appreciate your support for the site.

Nov 20, 2002
The Magic section has received an update (spell durations have not been updated yet). The Scrolls page now has information on Foci in addition to the previously available scroll location list, as well as a list of Quest Scrolls with links to the corresponding write-up. I also started a table to list which creatures drop which scrolls. If you'd like to help fill this out, please note the creature, the spell name and the level while you're out hunting and send me an email with the data.

Level VII spell information has also been added. Finally. If you're wondering what took me so long, well... I have no excuse! ;-) Thanks to the many of you who sent your spreadsheets and info for this section. I apologize for the delay in getting it added.

Last, I removed the Magic Items page in the Shopping section, and relocated the Mana Stone info to the Mana Stone Bargain page instead. Links to the various Bargains page added to the main Shopping page.

Nov 19, 2002
Many miscellaneous updates to the site. Vendor Rates updated with the newest NPCs. Chitticks added to the Pets section.  Numerous updates to the Creature Experience table. Newest Masks added to the Head wear page in the Armor section. Chess page updated with the new boards from the latest patch.

Nov 18, 2002
The Skill Specialization and Skill Sell-Back quests have been buffed and fluffed with full write-ups and pics. The Iron Coast Patch page also received miscellaneous updates including dungeon coordinate corrections and clarification on coconuts! The Tosser Coconuts are wielded by the Tuskies; the Coconuts are dropped by Tuskies (use unknown).

Nov 15, 2002
 Letters updated! And the question asked by many... has Ned Cleversun finally been wooed out of BSD? To read about Ned Cleversun's experiences as he explores Aphus Lassel for the first time, click here!

Once again, more updates to The Iron Coast patch page! New Nanner recipes, some additional quest updates, more tweaking of the dungeons list. The Tusker Titles quest also received several new additions.

October Letters archived.

Nov 14, 2002
More updates to The Iron Coast patch page! Items added throughout the day include Nanner Bread, additions to the dungeon list, a few new creatures, updated pics and info for the Impious Staff and Water of Mt. Lethe quests, plus the usual numerous typo corrections! Tusker Titles quest also received numerous updates, including a link to Greeneye's map (ACMaps)  of the Tusker Temple (static portals to the island)! Once again, thanks to everyone who contributed pics and info. More coming soon!

Nov 13, 2002
I think it goes without saying, but I'm going to say it anyway... best patch ever!!  Skill sell-back, specialization after creation, a new island with dungeons all over the place, tuskers everywhere, little funny coconut throwing Tuskies, new titles, fashion T's, nanner cream pies suitable for wacky slapstick throwing action, and refreshing beverages with tiny umbrellas!! And did I mention all the Tuskers?

Many many updates to The Iron Coast patch page. Thank you to everyone who sent in information and screenshots! If you'd still like to help, I'd love to get pics of the Tusker Horns, Tokens and Gems, etc. and any other miscellaneous nanner treats you might concoct. I'd also love to get more details on the Title quest dungeons. Thanks again! All together now: Best Patch Ever!!

Nov 12, 2002
The information is just pouring in! Unfortunately, I've got a busy morning here at work, but I'll try to get things posted to The Iron Coast patch page as I'm able. Town Crier rumors are up, and more to come! Keep it coming! :)

Nov 12, 2002
Wow! My vassal, Ned Cleversun, is totally famous! He's so famous that the Devs made a patch just for him! That whole teaser video was such a shout-out to my site. And if that wasn't enough... a Tusker Island. Get it? All Tuskers, all the time. A dead giveaway. Looks like an update to Ned Cleverusn's portal map may be coming soon! ;-)

That's right, it's patch day!! Kinda snuck up on us. Just a reminder to please use the jackcat@spacestar.net to send screenshots. I'll be updating The Iron Coast patch page throughout the day. As always, Teasers, Build Notes, Letter to the Players, etc. can be found here.

If you need instructions on taking screenshots, please visit the Mail and Screenshot Tips page.

Miscellaneous additions and corrections to the site, including a buff and fluff of the Evil Couch quest at Frest Greelving's Haunted Mansion.

Nov 11, 2002
Pies recipes updated in the Food section with Peppermint Pumpkin Pie, made by giving Ulgrim the Unpleasant a stick of Peppermint! Thanks to Sek the Wise of MT for this delicious (?) discovery!

Gaerlan's Citadel quest updated with the final message you receive after you've done the quest enough times to get all the rewards available to you. Thanks to Chupon of LC and Tabitha of MT!

Nov 10, 2002
Big update to the Fletching section! Fletching Items updated, Chorizite Arrowheads, Arrows, Quarrels and Darts added, Toothpick information added to the Whittling page, and the Arrows & Quarrels stats, as well as the Atlatl Darts, have been updated with the new damage and value stats in the Sports section. Special thanks to Narisah of HG for help!!

I'd like to get the entire Sports section updated with the new damage stats. If you want to help and own a quest weapon, please send a screen of the new stats to jackcat@spacestar.net. Thanks! Toothpick screens also needed!

Nov 7, 2002
It's the quest that keeps on giving! Tauraloi, a spear reward, has been added to the Gaerlan's Citadel quest. Thanks to Mattius the Studly of SC for pics and stats!

Nov 5, 2002
Many miscellaneous updates as the latest patch info gets sorted into its proper home. The Evil Couch added to the Interior Decorating page, Apparel updated with the new Masks, Tokens and Totems page updated, Jewelry page updated, and the stats on several Gaerlan's Citadel quest rewards have been updated with the newest changes.

Nov 4, 2002
The rewards for Gaerlan's Citadel quest keep coming in! Thanks to Tabitha of MT for the Circlet of Supremacy which increases your Rank by 1!! Linkmage of HG made another exciting discovery! You can turn Iasparailaun in to the Agent of the Arcanum for Gaerlan's Token which can be exchanged for the new title of Gaerlan Slayer! You'll also receive an MM note and a nice xp reward! (Thanks again to Tabitha of MT for tracking down a screen of the token for me!)

Nov 3, 2002
Yes, I know it's been days since I updated, but it was birthday week, and I was out having fun and getting... older! ;-) Letters section has been updated with a selection of letters from October.

Ned Cleversun's death total has also been updated. Congratulations on level 93! He's pictured showing off his new bow, a gift from Calli, with 142% damage mod imbued with Crippling Bow. He writes, "As Galfrida says, the new imbued bows make the Elari a wall ornament. With it I have brought a new level of hopelessness to BSD." Awww. That's so cute!

Thanks also to all the people who have contributed to the Tip Jar and have used my Amazon referral page to order items. I appreciate the support of the site!
