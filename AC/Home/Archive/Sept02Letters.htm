Sept  2002 - Elemental In-Game

Hey Maggie
I absolutely love your site. Anyway, I was reading the Verdict patch page and I noticed at the bottom someone said elementals are no longer in-game. Well, I'm LSed at AB and there seems to be a lot of acid elementals around: Caustics, Miasmas, etc. Was that person just talking about a certain place? Anyway... Love your site

James Arrowswift of SC
Level  55 Deadeye


Hi James Arrowswift -
You're right that there are still some elementals. Certain towns are under attack from special golems, accompanied by elementals. But these are special localized spawns. What people are referring to is that in the previous patch, normal spawns throughout the landscape were replaced by elemental spawns. Where you used to be able to find Mattekars, you would then find Elementals. Want to hunt Lugians outside? Elementals. And so on and so forth. These elemental spawns are gone and creatures have returned to their natural habitats. Hope that clears things up.

- Maggie the Jackcat

But, er ...when were you planning to make the new site available to every body out there? I mean, that's not what we did last time, remember?

So, I was wondering if, you know, we could perhaps do the same thing with the new site. Put it up for everyone some time next Spring, or early Summer. What do you think about shooting for a super-duper rollout maybe next Christmas? That is, Christmas 2003. Lots of press releases, lots of "buzz," the works! I think this project will set a new standard for wonderfulness, so let's take the time we need to get it right. Thanks!
