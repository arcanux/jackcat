May 22, 2009
It's been a while, hasn't it? What could lure Maggie from her self imposed exile? A curious fellow located in Zaikhal, the town my vassal and I grew up in, so many years ago. Goes by the name of Ned the Clever. There's just something about him... something familiar.... ;-)

A gracious and heartfelt thank you to Turbine for the tribute to my late vassal, Ned Cleversun. And a thank you for the companion lore piece from this month's patch: Secrets of the Apostates. I'm honored!

The story captures so many of my favorite traits: Ned's mapping skills, his mischievous smile, his blushes, his fondness for his matty coat and Olthoi Helm, his lust and his whoring!

Oh, wait. I read that wrong, you say? "Lust for adventure" and "haring off into the wilderness." Yes, yes. That's what I meant, of course.

I think this just might be the best patch ever!

January 2009 archived.


Ned the Clever, 13.4N, 0.9E (Zaikhal)
