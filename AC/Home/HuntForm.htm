Letters

 

 I get several letters a week asking questions like, "Where's a good place for a level 25 Axe fighter to hunt?" I hate to respond with, "um... don't have a clue!!", so they just sit in my Inbox, making me feel guilty!

I've long wanted to add a repository of suggested hunting areas for various level players. Where do you tell your newest vassal to hunt? Where's your favorite vitae removal spot? Where do you go when you need some quick cash? 

Please use this form to recommend your favorite hunting spots, either currently, or ones you enjoyed at earlier levels. Good spots could be dungeons, or areas of the terrain where certain creatures habitually spawn. Please also fill in the recommended level, and what sorts of creatures we might expect to find. The Comments area is for any pertinent information you wish to add, such as what type of character might enjoy the area the most, types of protections you'd recommend, or any other information that might be helpful.

As with the Letters section, certain ground rules will apply. Recommendations may or may not be published. Maggie the Jackcat will make all selections about which entries are chosen to be included. Duplicate suggestions may be eliminated. Suggestions may be edited for length, clarity or directness. Errors in grammar and spelling will be corrected, if I catch them. It is assumed that all recommendations may be published.

Your in-game name and server will be attached to each published suggestion. If you have more than one great hunting spot you'd like to share, please fill out the form a separate time for each location.
