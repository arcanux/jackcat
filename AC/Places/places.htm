Tip: To take screen shots, go to the Keyboard settings (Click the Keyboard button on your log-in screen--before your character enters the game) and make sure your * key is mapped to screen shot. When you press the * key during a game, a screen shot will automatically be saved into your Asheron's Call file on your computer. Subsequent screen shots will be numbered. Make sure you use an editing program to convert the .bmp file into a .jpg file before sending it by email, to a newsgroup, or posting it on the web. Delete the .bmp files after converting to save room on your hard drive.
