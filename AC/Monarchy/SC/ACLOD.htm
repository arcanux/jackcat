Monarch: Sharpy
Web Site: http://www.aclod.com/
Home Base: Villa at 29.0N 17.8E near Glenden Wood (Howling Wolf Villas)

Our allegiance's main goal is to have fun! In turn, we require that you respect others right to have fun as well. Simple as that. Once you are a member of our allegiance, you will be part of a big family and we sincerely hope you enjoy your time spent playing this fantastic game and chatting with friends!

Who can be a member?

1. We welcome all levels, genders, classes or races for membership in our allegiance.
2. All new members (AND any vassals they bring with them) must comply with this agreement to stay a member of this allegiance. We assume you agree with this agreement when you swear allegiance to any of our members. You decline this agreement by un-swearing from our members.

What guidelines will I have to follow while a part of this allegiance family?

1. Follow the golden rule in all of your dealings.
2. Since there is such diversity in our allegiance members, any kind of foul language, inappropriate comments or behavior will not be tolerated. Keep it a safe, family environment.
3. Patrons are responsible for their vassals.
4. Assist all allegiance members lower in level than yourself whenever possible.
5. All members must strive to be self-sufficient.
6. Anything not specifically mentioned, use your common sense.

We strive to have at least one buffbot online at all times at the villa.
