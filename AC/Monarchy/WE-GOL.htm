The Story Behind The Guardians of Light
Guardian of Light was the patron of my patron. He decided he should leave the "Reeve Monk" and break his allegiance to the man he was sworn to (he was a direct vassal of the monarch). I requested that my patron and other members under him meet with Guardian of Light. After some discussion I was pleased with what he had to say, although I was very torn between keeping my loyalty to "Reeve Monk" or keeping my loyalty to my patron. I so much wanted to be loyal to my patron and felt that Guardian of Light was an honest person with qualities that would make him a good Monarch of his own guild.

He possessed the same ideas I had of what a guild should be. At this time I still wasn't too sure I liked him, because he was very quiet and only answered when he was asked a question. He did not offer more or less information. But the answers he did give were to my liking. So I struggled with my decision, but decided it was the right thing and stayed with my patron as his patron became Monarch of his own guild.

The guild is now named Guardians of Light. It started small at first, but we have grown into family, all helping each other as it should be. I was made a General in our guild. I have since done all I can to help Guardian of Light and all the members with whatever I can. We have joined together to build this monarchy as friends, and we treat each other as friends. We have grown so much since the beginning, and did it all without hurting each other or stepping on each other's toes.

We are a non-griefing guild. A lot of our guild's code of conduct was taken from the guidelines of other guilds. Breaking the rules and our code of conduct can and will get you removed from the allegiance and/or banned from rejoining under any other members. We also have meetings and promotions for officers.

General Zolvera of Guardians of Light

Officers and Their Responsibilities

Officers lead quests, donate items to members, perform services to help members, and try to stay up-to-date on new events. The members have many outings together and work as a team. Sometimes the allegiance holds contents and gives out prizes to the members.

ALL OFFICERS:

1. Officers are here to help all members within their means.
2. Help to lighten the load of the General.
3. An officer is not here to become a slave to the members of the guild. They do need to make time to hunt and help vassals, but when they have time and the ability to help a guild member, they should volunteer the service.
4. Officer should follow the Code of Conduct.
5. Officers should list Events as the arise or plan them.
6. It is their job to correct a member when he is being out of line or breaking the CoC.
7. An officer is not here to power-level characters for guild members. If they do, it is their own decision to.
8. Officer should attend the meetings and bring up any issue or news and events.
9. Officers can suggest changes to rules to be voted on in officer meetings.
10. Officers have the ability to make change and add-ons to the website.
11. An officer leading an event is to make sure that the members are following the CoC and there is no line jumping in quests where others are present before them.
12. Officers are knowledgeable and strive to be up to date on new items and quests in the game.

GENERALS

1. Can make a final decision in order to resolve an issue on the spot.
2. Have access to mansion chest.
3. Looks for ways to make being in our guild more enjoyable for all.
4. Never chooses an enemy of the guild over a guild member.
5. Can promote 2 Commander of their choice who have demonstrated loyalty to the guild and helpfulness to all members.

COMMANDERS

1. Help the Generals and Monarch in making the guild events go smoothly.
2. Have access to certain areas of control on the website.
3. Looks for ways to make being in our guild more enjoyable for all.
4. Can bring issues that need to be resolved immediately to a General.

She was in Glenden Wood, and was about the same level as I was. We belonged to the same monarchy at the time, so we spoke to each other and hunted together. I talked with Guardian of Light in Qalaba'r, and listened to his words. He recognized me for who I am and was, just another gimp who was trying to make his way in a world that would bring death quicker than one could say Subway. He accepted me into his guild, and it has been a growing and thriving community even since. Some have gone their separate ways due to the style of play; others have joined knowing that they are now part of a greater good. In the end, Guardian of Light may be the leader, but he has made me feel as through my opinion matters, and that equality is not based on level or skill, but on trust, and devotion to all who call themselves the Guardians of Light.
