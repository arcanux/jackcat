The philosophy of our monarchy is to Have Fun. We are a small allegiance and we like it that way. This way it gives us the opportunity to get everyone involved. We are a family oriented allegiance. Everyone is treated equal. We set aside time both during the week and on weekends to fit everyone's schedule. If a quest is requested, we do all we can do make it happen. We have our own buffbot in the Villa Courtyard. We also have our own all tink, all trade, 3school, and is available for all tink and trade needs. She can also be used as a back up buffbot. Our Monarch, Torg Corathsun, is a hands on player and is available to all most of the time. When he is not he makes sure someone is. But most of all, He makes sure everyone has fun.
