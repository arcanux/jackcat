Monarch: Thee Rogue Knight
Web Site: http://www.geocities.com/guildoftherogueknights

The Rogue Knights---is the guild name, a character brandishes the name for a headpiece to the guild

The guild has a guild master and matron
Guild Master---Trantione, a rogue by nature, dagger melee
Guild Matron---Tasha the Legionaire, xbower, wife to Trantoine

Approximate members is about 50

This guild was built out of desire for people to have a place to belong after seeing the corruptness of most clans, most clans claim to have no inner circle, no rank/xp chains, or some basic ideal, but from past experience, all clans have them, Trantoine has been in many clans, saw the corruptness they claim doesn't exist, but clearly have, he built a guild with no high morals to put on the guild members other than those the Guild Inner Circle must maintain, The Rogue Knights can have rank chains, xp chains, and has an inner circle, but that inner circle is what holds The Rogue Knights together, maintain their rank and prestige, and take care of all finances and necessary needs of the guild, the lower members just belong and live their life as they see fit with no weight on their shoulders

The only requirements are those that the Guild Inner Circle {GIC} have to meet to main their GIC status.

Experience Chains are possible in The Rogue Knights

Focus Points: having fun, we do quests with friends and lead some ourselves, hunt in favorable hunting spots, PKing isn't frowned up on-it can be done but mostly friendly play

We are a self supportive guild, all key skills are covered, especially for archers/xbowers. We have at least 4 alchemists. We have to take care of guild legionaires; they are the back bone of the guild's kill power.

What is best about the Guild: We help out all, not for selfishness, but because friends are a strong asset. The Rogue Knights help out other clans, and friends just for the benefit of their friendship
