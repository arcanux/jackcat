Monarch: Greko
Web Site: http://cg.dereth.ac/
Home Base: 27.2S, 40.0E

My Ingame name is Greko and I am the monarch of the Crimson Guard. The Crimson Guard was conceived and birthed by Bishop Coop a couple years ago, and its primary focus at inception was PK. The monarchy was very small, but as it grew, select people were added, and a good majority of the Firebird people followed. As the allegiance grew, it took on a personality of its own, and pk became a smaller percentage of the followers, and replaced with a rich, diverse, mature set of toons and people. When Bishop Coop left, our biggest concern was carrying the torch he created forward in the manner and class with which he led us. Today, we enjoy the company of about 1900 followers with about 30-35 being active members. This monarchy has always insisted on respect from its followers, not only to our own people, but to the outside LC world as a whole.

We are governed by a council with two paths. Path 1 is the warring/pk headed up by a pk commander, and Path 2 is a domestic/human affairs path that takes care of the allegiance needs headed up by a Operations Commander. This structure is located at:

http://mywebpages.comcast.net/ssewell/cgorg3.htm

Our intention is to have fun. We have a published CoC, and conflict resolution process. We ask that anyone wanting to add a vassal to make sure they fit our standard of fun and respect before they do so. We also have what we are very excited about, and that is The Rescue Rangers. The Rescue Rangers give credit to individual people for making rescue runs for people in our clan. As people make more rescue runs, they attain a new title on our website, culminating at 100 runs being titled Rescue Ranger. This system works, and ensures no one is left behind!
