Monarch: Ricky Martin
Web Site: http://castledwellers.proboards15.com/
Home Base: outskirts of Danby's Outpost

We are an Anti guild that formed in the fall of 2004 after spending a year in both TFK and Gold Toof. We call ourselves the Castle Dwellers because of the 5 years we have been lifestoned at Neydissa Castle, where the name came up as a joke. It seemed almost appropriate to have the nickname become our guild name

--Membership Requirements: you must be an Anti who values his guild and is not just looking for someone to power level him or her. We always look for good people who want to help us fight the good fight, make some friends, and have some fun.
--Experience Chains: since the exp chain ended as far as passup we have a board on our website so that if anyone is interested, they can be assigned a patron and a vassl.
--Game Focus: Quests, Hunting, PK. We have members who are exceptionally talented in leading quests. We also love to group fellow, so we can get our lower levels up in levels. And in Darktide, who doesn't love to raid or defend?

As Ricky is our monarch, I should say he has been wonderful with us. He laughs at the jokes made about his name and admires anyone willing to wear his tag to fight PKs. He has a big heart and has always helped out anyone who needed him. He is the kind of monarch who will fight with his followers, even if it means dying with them. His two main allies are the guild Alzar, which is also a break from his old guild Gold Toof and also Powers Inc, who remain to this day, one of the oldest and proudest old school antis in the game...

I chose to become a Castle Dweller because I share the same feelings about the game as Ricky. I liked that Ricky wanted to have a guild where everyone mattered, even the level 5. One of the comments he made about a raid was what gained his respect to me. A level 20 mage wanted to raid and said that she would not be able to do much. Ricky told her, "Just by going you show how much u can do." He was right... that mage stood back and healed as we fought PKs, dying last in a raid where we all died.
