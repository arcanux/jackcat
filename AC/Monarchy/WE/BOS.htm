Bio of Dieter McBain

A story told by the Gods is usually heard by only those of either kingly heritage or upon their awaiting the arrival of Crom. My story is a story akin to this. I am Dieter McBain, of the clan McBain. My father was William Marcus McBain and his lineage dates back to the times of the Ahacine Wars. My mother was Frieda Teufal. Her family descended from the Northlands where their language is forgotten, yet the deeds of their ancestors shall never be.
I was born into a time of war. I have had to learn much thru self sacrifice and thru my own mistakes. I learned the art of sword fighting from a slave my father owned. Upon my father's murder, I freed my teacher only to witness his murder at the hands of a Shadow Lieutenant. I then learned the Black Arts of Magic from another friend. I have become a master of Life, Item, and Creature. I am now learning the art of Mana Conversion, knowing this will take time and I will endeavor to master it as well.

My story thru the ages has included bloodbaths and many lonely nights upon the plains of Dereth. In my travels I have begun to collect a lost rag tag band of followers. These Melee's, Mages, and Archers have become friends that I owe much too. I am thankful for their loyalty.
I have accepted my place at the head of this group. My responsibilities run great and I know I shall not fail. My parents taught me that a friend comes when called, a friend answers the alarms of war, and a friend must be loyal above all. I understand and live by these principals. I will be fair and just. I shall be honorable and loyal. I shall be firm and steadfast in my beliefs. I shall honor the names of those lost in battle. I shall forget the names of the weak along with those who cannot stand in battle and flee when one needs their help most.

My friends shall live by my side and die by my side. I shall never forget them as long as they are loyal to my cause. I shall bring together this band of rag tags, this brotherhood of lost souls, this Brotherhood of Steel! Let all know that my loyalty is Forever and For All Eternity!

~~Dieter McBain (Sword/Item/Critter/Life)~~
