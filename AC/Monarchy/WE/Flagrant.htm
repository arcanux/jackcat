Monarch: Hilunder
Web Site: http://www.hilunder.com (private board)
Home Base: 63.1 S, 21.6 E, NE of Qalaba'r

Approximate members: 600

A close knit guild of friends structured without politics or strife for our
own benefit.

We're a group of friends. I, Hilunder, serve as the figurehead Monarch purely for purposes of recognizing how we're all sworn together. We make all decisions as a group. When interacting with other guilds, no FLOD member speaks for us without knowing from previous discussion what our position is, or they wait until we discuss the issue before acting. Every FLOD member has a say in what we do and how we do it. The only rule set forth and ratified unanimously is that no FLOD member will engage in any public display of negativity towards any other guild, be it on the boards or be it in open chat in game.

Membership requirements: Is this someone I would hunt with? Would I be willing to visit the lifestone on more then one occasion for this person? Can I see myself spending time with this person? And will they see me the same way? Would I drop what I was doing to help them with a problem? Would they for me? Can I trust them? Can I mule around them? Can I give this prospective member a body permit? Are they here just for the free buffbot?
Weapons? Tools? I'm not. They shouldn't be. ~By St Evan of the FLOD.

We have several xp style minichains set up to push our tinkerers. We have no formal ban or allowances for xp chains.

We're a small group, we quest a lot with ally and friendly guilds, and go hunting regularly.
