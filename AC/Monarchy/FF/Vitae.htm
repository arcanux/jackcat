Monarch: Bennet of Swords
Web Site: http://www.odinsrealm.com/lifestone/
Home Base: Wilomine Valley Villas

We're a small clan that's been around since the end of beta, in one form or another. In the early days, the allegiance followed the great Decado, but due to his departure, Vitae Inc. found themselves without a leader. Deep from within the ranks, Bennet of Swords stepped forth to pick up the banner and carry them boldly into further (mis)adventure!

We're a close-knit family of friends who have adventured, quested, and died many, many times together. Our focus is mainly to uphold the virtues of honor, trust, and companionship that our friend and faithful leader Decado taught us so many years ago. We are a relaxed group who treat all who cross our paths the way we wish to be treated, further spreading goodwill amongst our fellow Frostfellians.

We currently have no membership requirements, but we believe that your actions reflect upon your person heavily. We ask that all who join us treat your brethren in arms with the same courtesy and respect that you'd like to be shown. Further, we also ask that you treat those who aren't under our flag to the same courtesies, if not more.

We do not have an experience chain of any sort, but have a few little "Mini-chains" to assist the leveling of tinker-mules, or low level characters to assist in their enjoyment of Dereth.

We focus most of all on having fun. Do not ever be afraid of asking the monarch for help with a quest, body recovery, or anything. He is here for his vassals... time and time again proven. Quests, hunting, even a few PK events have been a staple of Vitae Inc. since Decado was the Monarch. Bennet shows him great honor in keeping up with his traditions.
