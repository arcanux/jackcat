Monarch: Ninavie
Web Site: http://tid.zegeger.net/
Home Base: 28.8S, 3.8E

We are a close knit, mature, friendly, open for new ppl and new ideas guild, with something for everyone. We are allied with 4 other guilds, which means we always have enough ppl to do events with, while managing to keep the small family feeling with in the guild. We have support groups for basically every aspect of the game.

We do not have any xp chain. We do, however, have a open to all members rank 8 chain. The only thing we ask from new members is to read and sign up on our homepage and msg forum. And read and sign that you understand and agree to our Code of Conduct.

One by one, we left our home world Ispar heeding Asheron's call In Dereth. We joined together in a search for a new, higher purpose. Listening to the words of the wise men like Jojii, slaying monsters across the land. After many adventures we found what we had been searching for... now we are known as... The Champions of Dragons. Honor The Seekers of Dragons might and the Keepers of Dragons virtue. We wield the shield of Power and we kneel at the shrine of the Dragons. We are THE ISPARIAN DRAGONS!
