The KoNK Clan is Run as a family,

We have a few very basic rules.

Do not Swear or verbally abuse others

Try to treat others as you would have them treat you.

And most importantly Have Fun.



We have very little griefing and our reputation on Questing and adventuring is second to none, We hunt and Quest on a daily basis.

We have 24/7 buff bots and an In Clan Distribution policy meaning you can usually get anything you want/need from the clan without the need to collect tradables.

Our Vassal Patron Policy is very loose we believe acting as a family enables anyone at any level under any patron to contribute and benefit from the clan on an equal basis.

A large percentage of our followers are very old school and have a vast knowledge of the game and the way it runs.

Our player base is one if not the largest on HarvestGain, at any one time you can find up-to 20 regular players and some times more.

KotNK has a unique ruling system where the whole clan no matter what level or ability has a say in clan decisions, Knowledge and attitude mean a lot more to us than level.

Lastly, it has been proven on many occasions that there is No Clan in the game that is quite like us, over time we have had very few members leave and those that have, came back after a very short time. we play because we love it and for no other reason.
