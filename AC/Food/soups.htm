Recipes - Adv.

 

There's nothing like a nice bowl of soup on a chilly winter day! The following recipes explain how to cook soups, stews and dumplings.

Recipes
(See Difficulty for Recommended cooking skill. Actual Difficulty will give a 50/50 chance. A cooking skill that is higher than the required difficulty will increase your chances of success.)

Carol's Carrot Soup
1. Cooking Pot + Carrot = Carrot Stock
2. Carrot Stock + Milk = Rich Carrot Stock
3. Rich Carrot Stock + Cheese = Carol's Carrot Soup

Pumpkin Soup
1. Baking Pan + Pumpkin = Cooked Pumpkin
2. Cooked Pumpkin + Milk - Liquid Pumpkin
3. Honey + Liquid Pumpkin =  Sweetened Pumpkin
4. Cinnamon + Sweetened Pumpkin = Spiced Pumpkin
5. Cooking Pot + Spiced Pumpkin = Pumpkin Soup

Carrot Cake Soup
Carrot Cake Soup was modeled after a Penny-Arcade column and comic strip!

1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Egg = Batter
3. Flour + Batter = Cake Batter
4. Cake Batter + Carrot = Carrot Cake Batter
5. Baking Pan + Carrot Cake Batter =  Carrot Cake
6. Carving Knife + Carrot Cake =  Carrot Cake Cubes
7. Carrot Cake Cubes + Milk = Carrot Cake Soup!

Beef Stew
1. Carving Knife + Side of Beef =  3 Steaks
2. Cooking Pot + Steak = Beef Stew
or
1. Cooking Pot + Meat = Beef Stew

Chicken Stew
1. Carving Knife + Chicken = 3 Pieces of Chicken
2. Cooking Pot + Piece of Chicken =  Chicken Stew

Fish Stew
1. Carving Knife + Fish = 3 Fish Filets
2. Cooking Pot + Fish Filet = Fish Stew

Mushroom Stew
1. Cooking Pot + Mushroom =  Mushroom Stew

Rabbit Stew
1. Carving Knife + Rabbit Carcass = 2 Rabbit Pieces
2. Cooking Pot + Rabbit Piece = Rabbit Stew

Chicken Dumpling
1. Mortar & Pestle + Rice = Rice Flour
2. Rice Flour + Water = Rice Dough
3. Rice Dough + Chicken = Chicken Dumpling

Fish Dumpling
1. Mortar & Pestle + Rice = Rice Flour
2. Rice Flour + Water = Rice Dough
3. Rice Dough + Fish = Fish Dumpling
