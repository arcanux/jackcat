Recipes - Adv.

 

Everything tastes better when it's fried! The following recipes explain how to cook fried foods.

Recipes
(See Difficulty for Recommended cooking skill. Actual Difficulty will give a 50/50 chance. A cooking skill that is higher than the required difficulty will increase your chances of success.)

Fried Steak
1. Carving Knife + Side of Beef = 2 Steaks
2. Frying Pan + Steak = Fried Steak

Fried Chicken
1. Carving Knife + Chicken = 3 Chicken Pieces
2. Frying Pan + Chicken Piece = Fried Chicken

Fried Rabbit
1. Carving Knife + Rabbit Carcass = 3 Rabbit Pieces
2. Frying Pan + Rabbit Piece = Fried Rabbit

Fried Fish Filet
1. Carving Knife + Fish = 3 Fish Filet
2. Frying Pan + Fish Filet = Fried Fish Filet

Fried Egg
1. Frying Pan + Egg = Fried Egg

Fried Mushroom
1. Frying Pan + Mushroom = Fried Mushroom

Flat Bread
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Frying Pan + Dough = Flat Bread
