Recipes - Adv.

 

With the Throne of Destiny expansion, players may complete a quest to obtain a Brew Kettle. This kettle can be used to make any number of delicious, or not so delicious, beers! See the Brewmaster's Quest for details. Below are the recipes to make the latest and greatest AC libations!

Supplies can be purchased in Sanamar. In addition, Vibrant and Ultra Green Hops and high quality yeast can be found at the Eastwatch and Westwatch Outposts. Use a Baking Pan on Plain or Amber Barley for Roasted Barley.

Recipes
(See Difficulty for Recommended cooking skill. Actual Difficulty will give a 50/50 chance. A cooking skill that is higher than the required difficulty will increase your chances of success.)

Homemade Amber Ale

1. Brew Kettle + Water = Full Brew Kettle
2. Amber Barley + Full Brew Kettle = Amber Wort
3. Green Hops + Amber Wort = Finished Amber Wort
4. Liquid Yeast + Finished Amber Wort = Amber Fermented Brew
5. Keg + Amber Fermented Brew = Keg of Homemade Amber Ale
6. Empty Bottles + Keg of Homemade Amber Ale = 50 Bottles of Homemade Amber Ale

You fill your brewkettle with water!
You create a nice Amber Wort.
You add the Green Hops to the Wort, creating a Finished Amber Wort.
You add the Green Hops to the Wort, creating a Finished Amber Wort.
You create a Fermented Amber Brew.
You fill the keg with Homemade Amber Ale!
You fill 50 empty bottles with Homemade Amber Ale!
