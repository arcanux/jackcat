Recipes - Adv.

 

I like pie. But then, doesn't everyone?! The following recipes explain how to make various pies.

Recipes
(See Difficulty for Recommended cooking skill. Actual Difficulty will give a 50/50 chance. A cooking skill that is higher than the required difficulty will increase your chances of success.)

Apple Pie
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Apple = Apple Pie

Chicken Pie
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Carving Knife + Chicken = 3 Chicken Pieces
3. Dough + Chicken Piece = Chicken Pie

Meat Pie
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Meat = Meat Pie
or
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Carving Knife + Side of Beef = 3 Steaks
3. Dough + Steak = Meat Pie

Mushroom Pie
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Mushroom = Mushroom Pie

Spiced Apple Pie
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Apple = Apple Pie
3. Cinnamon + Apple Pie = Spiced Apple Pie

Fish Pie
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Carving Knife + Fish = 3 Fish Filet
3. Dough + Fish Filet = Fish Pie

Rabbit Pie
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Carving Knife + Rabbit Carcass = 3 Rabbit Pieces
3. Dough + Rabbit Piece = Rabbit Pie

Pumpkin Pie
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Baking Pan + Pumpkin = Cooked Pumpkin
3. Cooked Pumpkin + Milk = Liquid Pumpkin
4. Honey + Liquid Pumpkin = Sweetened Pumpkin
5. Cinnamon + Sweetened Pumkin = Spiced Pumpkin
6. Spiced Pumpkin + Egg = Pumpkin Pie Filling
7. Dough + Pumpkin Pie Filling = Pumpkin  Pie

Peppermint Pumpkin Pie
1. Hand Ulgrim the Unpleasant (found at the Xarabuydun Pub) a Peppermint Stick in exchange for the pie.

You give Ulgrim the Unpleasant Peppermint Stick.
Ulgrim the Unpleasant tells you, "Hey Peppermint! I recently made my own new recipe. Here try some of my new Peppermint Pumpkin Pie!"
Ulgrim the Unpleasant gives you Peppermint Pumpkin Pie.

Nanner Cream Pie
1. Heavy Grinder + Nanners = Mushed Nanners
 2. Flour + Water = Dough
3. Mushed Nanners + Dough = Nanner Cream Pie
