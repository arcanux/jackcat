Recipes - Adv.

 

Candy Bars, Ice Cream, Cakes & Cookies. Dereth is full of wonders! The following recipes provide the steps to make these wonderful tasty sweets.

Recipes
(See Difficulty for Recommended cooking skill. Actual Difficulty will give a 50/50 chance. A cooking skill that is higher than the required difficulty will increase your chances of success.)

Bar of Milk Chocolate
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roasted Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cocoa Powder + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Mixture
5. Cocoa Mixture + Milk = Milky Cocoa Mixture
6. Milky Cocoa Mixture + Honey = Bar of Milk Chocolate

Bar of Dark Chocolate
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roasted Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cocoa Powder + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Mixture
5. Cocoa Mixture + Honey = Bar of Dark Chocolate

Milk Chocolate Candy Bar
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roasted Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cocoa Powder + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Mixture
5. Cocoa Mixture + Milk = Milky Cocoa Mixture
6. Milky Cocoa Mixture + Honey = Bar of Milk Chocolate
7. Monougat + Bar of Milk Chocolate = Milk Chocolate Candy Bar

Dark Chocolate Candy Bar

3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder

5. Cocoa Mixture + Honey = Bar of Dark Chocolate

3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cocoa Powder + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Mixture
5. Cocoa Mixture + Milk = Milky Cocoa Mixture
6. Milky Cocoa Mixture + Honey = Bar of Milk Chocolate
7. Peppermint Stick + Bar of Milk Chocolate = Peppermint Chocolate Bar

Gingerbread

6. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
7. Cocoa Powder + Cake Batter = Chocolate Cake Batter

Carrot Cake
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Egg = Batter
3. Flour + Batter = Cake Batter
4. Cake Batter + Carrot = Carrot Cake Batter
5. Baking Pan + Carrot Cake Batter = Carrot Cake

Fruit Cake
1. Cinnamon + Brown Lump = Spicy Brown Lump
2. Flour + Spicy Brown Lump = Spicy Lumpy Flour
3. Spicy Lumpy Flour + Egg = Rich Spicy Lumpy Flour
4. Rich Spicy Lumpy Flour + Red Wine = Fruitcake Batter
5. Baking Pan + Fruitcake Batter = Fruitcake

Ice Cream

2. Frozen Cream + Honey = Ice Cream

Chocolate Ice Cream

Green Tea Ice Cream

Peppermint Ice Cream

Nanner Split
1. Noodle Cutter + Nanners = Nanner Halves
 2. Magic Iceball + Milk = Frozen Cream
3. Frozen Cream + Honey = Ice Cream
4. Nanner Halves + Ice Cream = Nanners & Ice Cream
5. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Beans
6. Heavy Grinder + Roasted Beans = Chocolate Liquer
7. Nanners & Ice Cream + Chocolate Liquor = Nanner Split

Chocolate Covered Nanners
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roasted Beans = Chocolate Liquer
3. Chocolate Liquor + Nanners =  Nanners Covered in Chocolate
4. Strange Stick + Nanners Covered in Chocolate = Chocolate Covered Nanners
