Recipes - Adv.

 

What could be better after a long chilly day than slipping on your bunny slippers and sitting on your couch in front the fire sipping a nice cup of Hot Chocolate? Use the recipes below for the steps to create many delicious beverages.

Recipes
(See Difficulty for Recommended cooking skill. Actual Difficulty will give a 50/50 chance. A cooking skill that is higher than the required difficulty will increase your chances of success.)

Apple Juice
1. Metal Press + Apple = Apple Juice

Filled Beer Stein
Use a keg of beer, available in most taverns on an Empty Beer Stein (purchase from barkeep). No cooking skill required.

Chocolate Milk
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roaster Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Chocolate Liquor + Milk = Bitter Milk
4. Bitter Milk + Honey = Chocolate Milk

Cold Milk
1. Heavy Grinder + Magic Iceball = Crushed Ice
2. Crushed Ice + Milk = Cold Milk

Mocha
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roaster Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cocoa Powder + Coffee = Mocha Base
5. Mocha Base + Milk = Mocha

Rich Mocha
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roaster Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cocoa Powder + Coffee = Mocha Base
5. Mocha Base + Milk = Mocha
6. Cinnamon + Mocha = Rich Mocha

Iced Mocha
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roaster Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cocoa Powder + Coffee = Mocha Base
5. Mocha Base + Milk = Mocha
6. Heavy Grinder + Magic Iceball = Crushed Ice
7. Crushed Ice + Mocha = Iced Mocha

Rich Iced Mocha
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roaster Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cocoa Powder + Coffee = Mocha Base
5. Mocha Base + Milk = Mocha
6. Cinnamon + Mocha = Rich Mocha
7. Heavy Grinder + Magic Iceball = Crushed Ice
8. Crushed Ice + Rich Mocha = Rich Iced Mocha

Hot Chocolate
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roaster Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cooking Pot + Milk = Hot Milk
5. Hot Milk + Honey = Sweetened Hot Milk
6. Sweetened Hot Milk + Cocoa Powder = Hot Chocolate

Peppermint Hot Chocolate
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roaster Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cooking Pot + Milk = Hot Milk
5. Hot Milk + Honey = Sweetened Hot Milk
6. Sweetened Hot Milk + Cocoa Powder = Hot Chocolate
7. Peppermint + Hot Chocolate = Peppermint Hot Chocolate

Hot Chocolate with Marshmallows
1. Baking Pan + Brown Beans = Roasted Brown Beans
2. Heavy Grinder + Roaster Brown Beans = Chocolate Liquor
3. Metal Press + Chocolate Liquor = Cocoa Powder
4. Cooking Pot + Milk = Hot Milk
5. Hot Milk + Honey = Sweetened Hot Milk
6. Sweetened Hot Milk + Cocoa Powder = Hot Chocolate
7. Carving Knife + Eep = Marshmallows
8. Marshmallows + Hot Chocolate =  Hot Chocolate with Marshmallows

Eggnog
1. Heavy Grinder + Nutmeg = Ground Nutmeg
2. Ground Nutmeg + Milk = Spiced Milk
3. Spiced Milk + Egg = Eggnog
