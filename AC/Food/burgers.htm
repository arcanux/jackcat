Recipes - Adv.

 

Junk food! Burgers and Pizza. Also, a delicious selection of sandwiches, made with sliced bread -- the greatest thing since bread! The following recipes explain how to make Burgers, Pizza and Sandwiches.

Recipes
(See Difficulty for Recommended cooking skill. Actual Difficulty will give a 50/50 chance. A cooking skill that is higher than the required difficulty will increase your chances of success.)

Holtburger
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Baking Pan + Dough = Bread
3. Carving Knife + Side of Beef = 3 Steaks
4. Heavy Grinder + Steak = Ground Meat
5. Ground Meat + Bread = Holtburger

Pizza
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Rennet + Milk = Cheese
3. Dough + Cheese = Pizza

Famous Pizza
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Rennet + Milk = Cheese
3. Dough + Cheese = Pizza
4. Oregano + Pizza = Famous Pizza

Egg Sandwich
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Baking Pan + Dough = Bread
3. Carving Knife + Bread = Sliced Bread
4. Sliced Bread + Egg = Egg Sandwich

Fish Sandwich
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Baking Pan + Dough = Bread
3. Carving Knife + Bread = Sliced Bread
4. Sliced Bread + Fish = Fish Sandwich

Chicken Sandwich
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Baking Pan + Dough = Bread
3. Carving Knife + Bread = Sliced Bread
4. Sliced Bread + Chicken = Chicken Sandwich

Cheese Sandwich
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Baking Pan + Dough = Bread
3. Carving Knife + Bread = Sliced Bread
4. Rennet + Milk = Cheese
5. Sliced Bread + Cheese = Cheese Sandwich

Grilled Cheese Sandwich
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Baking Pan + Dough = Bread
3. Carving Knife + Bread = Sliced Bread
4. Rennet + Milk = Cheese
5. Sliced Bread + Cheese = Cheese Sandwich
6. Frying Pan + Cheese Sandwich = Grilled Cheese Sandwich
