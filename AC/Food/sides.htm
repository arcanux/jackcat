Recipes - Adv.

 

Not hungry for the whole meal and just looking for a little something to nibble on? Try these tasty appetizers and side dishes! Step by step instructions provided below.

Recipes
(See Difficulty for Recommended cooking skill. Actual Difficulty will give a 50/50 chance. A cooking skill that is higher than the required difficulty will increase your chances of success.)

Stuffed Mushroom
1. Carving Knife + Mushroom = Stemless Mushroom
2. Rennet + Milk = Cheese
3. Stemless Mushroom + Cheese =  Cheese Stuffed Mushroom
4. Baking Pan + Cheese Stuffed Mushroom = Stuffed Mushroom

Viamont Toast
1. Flour + Water = Dough (2x)
2. Baking Pan + Dough = Bread
3. Dough + Egg = Batter
4. Batter + Bread = Viamont Toast

Coleslaw
1. Carving Knife + Cabbage = Coleslaw

Sushi
1. Carving Knife + Fish = 3 Fish Filets
2. Uncooked Rice + Fish Filet = Sushi

Stuffed Grape Leaf
1. Uncooked Rice + Grapes = Stuffed Grape Leaf

Pickled Egg
1. Brine + Egg = Pickled Egg

Kimchi
1. Brine + Cabbage = Kimchi

Hot Kimchi
1. Brine + Cabbage = Kimchi
2. Hot Sauce + Kimchi = Hot Kimchi

Flaming Kimchi
1. Brine + Cabbage = Kimchi
2. Hot Sauce + Kimchi = Hot Kimchi
3. Fire Oil + Hot Kimchi = Flaming Kimchi
Alchemy skill needed for Fire Oil

Apple Sauce
1. Heavy Grinder + Apple = Apple Sauce

Spiced Apple Sauce
1. Heavy Grinder + Apple = Apple Sauce
2. Cinnamon + Apple Sauce = Spiced Apple Sauce

Nanner Bread
1. Flour + Water = Dough
2. Dough + Nanners = Nanner Dough
3. Baking Pan + Nanner Dough = Nanner Bread

Nanner Chips
1. Carving Knife + Bunch of Nanners = Sliced Nanners
2. Baking Pan + Sliced Nanners = Nanner Chips

Simple Field Rations
1. Cooking Pot + Simple Dried Rations = 25 Simple Field Rationses

Elaborate Field Rations
1. Cooking Pot + Elaborate Dried Rations = 25 Elaborate Field Rationses

Advanced Recipes

Add Health, Mana or Victual Oils (made with the Alchemy skill) to increase the benefits of these foods. See the Advanced Recipes page for details. Note: Hot Kimchi, Applesauce and Spiced Applesauce are the only appetizers or sides that use oils.
