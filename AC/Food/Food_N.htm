Ned Cleversun's Favorite Foods!

  
Home



 		 Ned Cleversun enjoys hamburgers with pickles (spears, not those stupid chips) pizza, cheezy bread with marinara sauce, and Chinese pork fried rice.

Lately, Danier has introduced Ned to the pleasures of "pouches of dried meat," which restore 85 points of health and weigh only 50 burden units. It isn't clear exactly what kind of meat it is, or what sort of creature provided the meat, but Ned can always use a few extra health points!

Other favorites are green tea ice cream -- a specialty of Danier's alter-ego, Sugar -- and pistachio nuts.
