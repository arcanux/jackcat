From the Zone's Bestiary and the Dark Majesty Expansion CD:

Scarecrows were introduced in October, 2000's Hollow Victory patch.

Appearing during the autumn season, these manikins of sticks and straw seemed benign enough. . . at first. Believed to have come through the same portals that draw the Isparians to Dereth, many took these Scarecrows for a touching reminder of home.

Until, that is, suspicious grew about these pumpkin-headed figures. Were they indeed drawn from the world of Ispar, or simply Virindi-crafted puppets?
