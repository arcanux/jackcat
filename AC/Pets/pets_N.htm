Ned Cleversun's Favorite Pets!

  
Home

 Dereth has a wide variety of monsters to hunt, and Ned Cleversun revels in the choices available! His favorite creatures to hunt are Tuskers. He's also been known to fight the Drudges in BSD. Around Ayan, he can sometimes be seen hunting high level Banderlings and Umbris Shadows. He's heard that there are many other types of monsters also.

Ned has a RL (real life) character who has a male cat companion animal. Ned has trained the cat to become weak and slow when vulned with Frailty. When Ned pretends to shoot arrows, the cat runs and then leaps in the air and falls down, feigning death.

Lately, Ned has been asking the devs for a pack cat, one that is also trainable and wall-mountable.
