Samsur Area to Tufa & Al-Jalima
On the road between Samsur and Tufa, just after the mountain pass, you'll spot a group of 6 Villas. A wide variety of creatures now inhabit the desert, including Spiky Amoredillos; Old Bones, Skeletons & Skeleton Warriors; Rust Gromnies; Desert Rats; low level Drudges; Carrion, Blood and Vorous Shreth; Crude and Rough Monouga; Broken Fragments; Parfal and Listris Niffis; Tumerok Workers and Standard Bearers; Auroch Fire Bulls and Cows, and Lesser Mu-Miyas.

You can also find a group of 8 houses on the road between Samsur and Al-Jalima. Area creatures include Brown and Spiny Armoredillo; Outcast and Crude Monouga; Vorous and Gnawer Shreth; and Parfal Niffis. 

SW of Samsur you'll find more cottages, an interesting arrangement with three perched on top of a butte, with 3 more cottages near the base. You may encounter Sandy Armoredillos, Vorous Shreth, Rust Gromnies and Rough Monouga, and Mu-Miyas.
