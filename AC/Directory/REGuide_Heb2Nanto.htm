Hebian-To to Nanto
A group of 8 cottages can be found SW of Hebian-To in a wooded area. Creatures in the immediate vicinity include Wood Golems, Veteran Reedsharts, Jibrit Zephyrs, Jade Gromnies, and Serpent Clan Tumeroks (Standard Bearers & Workers).

For something a little more roomy, you'll also find a mansion just outside of Nanto. Surrounding the area are an occasional Mosswart Chief, Snowy and White Mattekars, Wood and Limestone Golems, Liagus Lugians, Spiny and Bronze Armoredillos, and Field Ursuin.
Another group of cottages can be found North of Nanto. You may encounter Tumerok Standard Bearer and Workers (Serpent Clan), Hunter Shreths and Spiney Armoredillos.

If you prefer a Villa, you can find a group of 4 on the coast NE of Nanto.
