Shoushi to Baishi
The road between Shoushi and Baishi is some of the prettiest countryside in Dereth. Just SW of Shoushi you can find a group of 5 Villas at the base of a mountainside, surrounded by Gnawer and Blood Shreth, low level Drudges, Young and Feeder Mosswarts, Field Ursuin and Adult Reedsharks.

Further down the road you'll come across a mansion. Area creatures include Scavenger Ursuin, Laigus Lugian, Barker Mosswarts, more Reedsharks (Adult, Elder and Veteran), and White Mattekars.

If you'd prefer a smaller cottage, try the group of 8 just North of Baishi. You may encounter some Mosswarts -- Feeders and an occasional Shaman, Blood Shreths, Broken Fragments, Wood Golems, Black Rats, and Snowy and White Mattekars.
