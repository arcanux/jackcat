Yaraq Area
Looking for some nice beach front property? Why not try the coast north of Yaraq! You'll find four charming villas near the sea or 8 cottages further up the coast.

Creatures in the area include Wild Monogua, low level Drudges, Undead, Spikey Armoredillos, Parfal and Listris Niffis, Vorous Shreth, Zombies, and Water & Muc Golems. Further South you'll also find Red Wasps, Shallow Sharks, Azure Gromnies and the occasional Shadow Child.

You can also find a Mansion SE of Yaraq, out in the desert surrounded by Rough Monouga, Undead and Drudge Skulkers.
