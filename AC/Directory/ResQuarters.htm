Real Estate Guide

 Residential Quarters were introduced in April 2002's Betrayal event to meet the housing demand.

Each apartment has 2 wall hooks, a floor hook and a storage chest. You must be level 20 and have Dark Majesty installed to purchase.

PRICE:
(subject to change without notice)

100,000 pyreal
1 Writ of Refuge

MAINTENANCE:

10,000 pyreal
Frequency -- every 3 months

In addition to the individual quarters, there are also larger common areas where you can socialize with friends.

Inside each group of Residential Halls, you'll also find portals back to the other two cities as well. It certainly makes travel easier! From the main courtyard, take one of the four hallways which leads to the exit portals (the other 3 corridors end in a "T".
