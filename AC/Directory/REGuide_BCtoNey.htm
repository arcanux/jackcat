Between Bandit Castle & Neydisa Castle
The area north of Bandit Castle is much more hazardous than it used to be. You'll find dangers comparable to the Direlands: Lich Lords; High Tumeroks; Othoi Nymphs, & Workers; Scrawed & Harrower Grievvers; Shadow Lieutenants; Banderling Strikers, Enforcers, Bezerkers; Ferocious, Wily & Brute Moungas; Drudger Raveners, Stalkers, Lurkers; Dual Fragments; Skeleton Bone Knigths & Lords; Ulu Sclavus; Ancient Mu-Miyah; Lithos & Gigas Lugians; Obsidain Golem & Dark Wisps.

The Villas on the road towards Neydisa Castle each have several levels and a house portal for easy access (not yet functional). You can also find a beautiful Mansion, with two fountains gracing the entrance. Further down the road you'll find a group of cottages. These are heavily guarded by Banderlings, Monougas and Lich Lords. It's too bad about the neighbors! ;-)
