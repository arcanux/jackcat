Al-Arqas and Xarabaydun Area
Just SW of Al-Arqas you'll find a settlement of 8 cottages near a portal to Tufa. The surrounding area can be quite dangerous, with Lesser & Greater Mu-Miyas, Rough Monougas, Spikey Armoredillos, Parfal & Listris Niffis, Rough Monouga, in addition to the less formidable Gnawer Shreth, Drudge Slinkers, Desert Rats and Undead. 

More cottages can be found both Northwest and North of town. Area creatures include Skeleton Warriors, Rough and Wild Monouga, Se Sclavus and Hunter Shreth.

If you continue further down the beach from Yaraq, just West of Al-Arqas, you'll find some more cottages on the shore. You may run across some Rust Gromnies, Desert Rats and Gnawer Shreths.

Even further south, the starter town of Xarabaydun has some lovely beach property to the west on the coast. Or you could head North a short distance for a cottage in the desert. Creatures include Se & Esse Sclavus; Wild and Outcast Monouga; Sandy Armoredillos, Auroch Bulls, Rust Gromnies and Drudge Sneakers.
