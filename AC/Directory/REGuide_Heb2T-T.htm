Hebian-To to Tou-Tou
Southeast of Hebian-To you can find 7 cottages. Creatures on the terrain include Veteran Reedsharks; Mudlurk, Creeper and Feeder Mosswarts; Drudge Prowlers, Laigus Lugian, and Ivory Gromnies.

As you follow the road to Tou-Tou, you'll begin to encounter White Mattekars; Limestone Golems; Bronze Armoredillos; Carrion, Blood, and Hunter Shreth; Jibrit & Kilif Zefirs; Jade Gromnies and Black Rats. You'll find another group of 6 cottages south of Tou-Tou.

In addition to cottages, you can also find a beautiful mansion, overlooking the water. Area creatures include Limestone and Wood Golems, plus the occasional Shadow Child.
