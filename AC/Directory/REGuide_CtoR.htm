Cragstone to Rithwic
The area around Cragstone and on the road to Rithwic has some extremely pretty housing locations. A mansion is just west of town, where the road splits, and has a view of the river nearby.  You may encounter Outcast Monouga, Veteran Reedsharks, Banderlings (Young, Scout and Guards), Scavenger Ursuin, Aurochs, Mite Matrons, Snowy Mattekars, and Broken Fragments.

As you travel SW of town, you'll come across the first of several groups of cottages. In addition to the creatures above, you may also encounter Tumerok Standard Bearers and Workers (Falcon Clan); Vorous Shreth; Banderling Breeders, Raiders and Ravers; and everyone's favorites: Zofrit Zefirs.

Halfway to Rithwic, you'll find more cottages, right on the River!! What a gorgeous location! Blood and Hunter Shreth spawn nearby, as well as Reedshark Elders, Wood & Water Golems, Field & Scavenger Ursuin, and Snowy Mattekars.

As you get closer to Rithwic and the last group of cottages before town, the beasts are less intimidating. You may find some Stoney Armoredillows, in addition to lower level Banderlings and Drudges.
