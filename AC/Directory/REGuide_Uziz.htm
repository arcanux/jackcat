Uziz Area 
The area around Uziz and the Southwest of Osteth has many groups of homes from which to choose. A group of 5 Villas can be found between Al-Arqas and Uziz, with Rough Monouga, Desert Rats, Parfal Niffis, Greater & Lesser Mu-Miyas and Aste Sclavus nearby.

Southwest of Uziz you'll find a group of 7 cottages. Area creatures include Sandy and Spikey Armoredillos; Hunter Shreth; Esse, Aste & Se Sclavus; Drudge Prowlers; Undead and Zombies; and Auroch Fire Bulls.

Another group of cottages lies East of Uziz, up on a plateau. Creatures you may encounter include Tumerok Workers & Standard Bearers (Falcon Clan), Crude Monouga, Se Sclavus, Skelton Captains, Amploth Lugians, and Ghost Wisps.

Just N of Uziz you can find another group of 8 cottages, surrounded by similar creatures listed above. Skeletons and Skeleton Warriors also roam nearby.

Heading NE down the road towards Yanshi, you'll find several more groups of cottages. Other creatures you may encounter and Brown Armoredillos, Rust Gromnies. As you approach Yanshi, you may also find, Reedsharks, White Mattekars, Scavenger & Field Ursuin, Ivory Gromnie, Serpent Clan Tumeroks, Mosswart Feeders and the occasional Shaman.

If you take the road North towards Samsur instead, you will discover Uziz's impressive Mansion.
