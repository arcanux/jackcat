Zaikhal Mansions
You'll find many cottage settlements on the road from Zaikhal to Glendon Wood. In addition to these, Zaikhal also has two beautiful mansions on the outskirts of town. The SW Zaikhal Mansion sits high on a hill overlooking the water. Surrounding creatures include Vorous Shreth, Snowy & White Mattekars, and Banderling Guards & Scouts. The East Mansion, perched on a plateau, also has a beautiful view. You may encounter Aste & Se Sclavus, and Lesser & Greater Mu-Miyah nearby.
