Between Zaikhal & Glenden Wood
The road between Zaikhal and Glenden Woods has several settlements of cottages. The road is a little more dangerous than it used to be. You'll still encounter Drudges, Gnawer Shreths and low level Skeletons, but now you may also see Se and Aste Sclavus, Lesser Mu-miyah, Blood Shreth, Zombies and Desert Rats. Further along the road, near the 3rd group of cottages below, you may also encounter Stoney Armoredillos, White Mattekars, and Mite Squibs. The fourth group listed below is nestled in the Woods, close to GW.
