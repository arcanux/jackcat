Glenden Wood Area
In addition to a series of cottages on the road from Ziakhal to Glenden Wood, you can also find housing surrounding the town itself. A group of 5 Villas lies South of Glenden Wood. The terrain is scattered with Amploth & Obeloth Lugian, Male & Female Tuskers, a Banderling Captain or two, and the occasional Zofrit Zefir. You can also find another Villa south of town, all by itself, atop a hill overlooking the countryside. More Villas are located SE of town. Nearby creatures include Vorous Shreth, Banderling Raiders, Wood Golems, Veteran & Elder Reedsharks and Snowy Mattekars.

As you approach the Glenden Wood Mansion, you may encounter Shadows, Sandstone Golems, White & Snowy Mattekars, and Toifor Ursuin.

Banderling Raiders, Wood Golems and Hunter Shreths are some creatures you may find on your way to a group of 4 cottages, perched on a hillside over-looking the road just NW of Glenden Wood.
