Ned Cleversun's Favorite Shopping Spot

  
Home Dereth is full of shops and vendors, and innumerable places to buy things; nevertheless, the emporium of first resort has always been the Hub. The Hub (or Subway) is the Mall of America of Dereth, the place where Adventurers and Warriors go to get the very best loot available. If it's not in the Hub, it's not in the game.
