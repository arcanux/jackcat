Sunflowers & Snowflowers
During the Betrayal event, the apprentices (see above) began to carry new pedestals and vases in their inventories. Spring has sprung! Not only have dye plants begun to appear more frequently, but Sunflowers are blooming as well! The crystal vase can hold up to 5 sunflowers, and it may be placed on a pedestal. The sunflower itself may also be hooked.

Sunflowers have been found in the NW of Dereth on the way to the Chorizite Refiner near the Beach Fort, near the Northreach Settlement at 74.0N, 35.0E, near the Caulnalain Vestibule, the mountains East of Qalaba'r, in the Sennon Valley area, near Stonehold, and north of Holtburg at 65.9N, 30.3E; 69.4N, 35.7E; 73.7N, 26.0E; and 73.9N, 12.8E. They have also been found east of Lady Maila Estates (outside Mayoi) at 74.9N, 40.1W; 73.2N, 43.3W; 76.3N, 41.2W; 78.0N, 42.1W; and 73.0N, 41.5W.

During The Hall of the Tusker King snowflowers dotted the landscape. They have been found in the N Direlands  and outside Ayan Baqur, as well as near Neydisa and the Crater. (Reported locations 72.2S, 39.2E and  87.9N, 5.1W -- not static spawns.) Snowflowers, like Sunflowers, may be added to vases (pics below by Wekar of LC).

Crafted or Quest Housing Items
The Head of the Olthoi Queen was one of the first housing decorations, introduced during the Dark Majesty. During The First Strike, Snowmen were also able to be hooked at your home. A suit of Thorsten's Armor was first seen in the Training Academy dungeon. During the Betrayal event, it was discovered to be a replica of the famous Thorsten Cragstone's armor. You can obtain a suit of armor to display in your own home by completing a quest.

A Horn of Vigilance and the Ursuin Rug became available during the Hidden Vein event. Learn more about the Horn of Vigilance quest here. The Ursuin Rug is made by giving Gillian the Poacher up at Bandit Castle (66.3N, 49.9E) a hide from a Slasher Ursuin.

During June 2002's Castling event, new quests were added for the Arcane Pedestal, the Map of Dereth and Flags! October of 2002 brought the stylish Evil Couch during the Festivus event! The Hall of the Tusker King event of Dec. 2002 introduced some festive holiday decorations: Holiday Lights and a Snowman Snowglobe!

The Coconut Tusker, usable with both wall and floor hooks, can be carved from the little coconuts dropped by Tusker Tossers by using a Whittling Knife! Introduced during The Hall of the Tusker King. Fishing Pole, Trunk of Bones, Ball of Gunk and Skeleton are all items pulling from a Fishing Hole (Discoveries event.) Treasure is found in Ulgrim's Basement. The Alloy Machine is part of the Undead Mechanic quest from Reign of Terror. The Living Tome is also from the Reign of Terror event. Olthoi Resurgent is from A Swelling Tide. Visit Frost Haven, the town introduced in Waking From the Abyss, to get an Ice Box to air condition your home! You can also decorate your home with a Snowflake or Honorary Snowman Token! The Homecoming Pennant & The Font of Jojii are from the Mirror, Mirror event. Candeth Keep Blueprints - Mired Hearts. Map to Burun Lair - Pillars Made of Sand. Blueprints of a Burun Fortess & Carved Mosswart Statue from From the Darkness Born. The Wallbound Niffis is from The Calm.

The Castling event brought changes to the Bronze Statues. Time and the elements did their work and all Bronze Statues became oxidized, appearing a blue-green in color.
