You can buy Mana Charges to give mana to magic items in order to use their spells (Use the table below for a list of places to buy Mana Charges and Stones). When you use a charge to "refill" your item, the charge is destroyed. If you have numerous items to refill, you can use the charge -- or several charges -- on your Paper Doll (the picture of your character in the inventory panel) and it will distribute the mana over the items needing refilled, starting with the item that is lowest on mana. You must be wearing or holding the the items you want to charge in this way. And you might want to put away your Focusing Stone first!! The Titan Mana Charge is found on the Hieromancer's Armor quest.

Use Mana Stones to drain  Mana from one Magical Item in order to transfer it to another item. The efficiency determines the % of mana drawn from the source object. If a magic gem contains 100 mana, you will absorb 25 mana using a Lesser Mana Stone, 50 using a Regular Mana Stone and 75 mana using a greater Mana Stone. Mana Stone may be destroyed upon use.
