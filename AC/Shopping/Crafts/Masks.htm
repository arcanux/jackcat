I just love Halloween! Masks were introduced during October 2000's Hollow Victory update. Original masks included the Mosswart, Drudge, Skeleton, Tusker, and Banderling Masks. A special Virindi Mask was also introduced which could made into striking heritage masks by three new mask makers (see tables to the right).

Alexander the Deft tells you, "Hello, I am Alexander the Deft. If you give me a creature's head or facial covering, I may be able to make it into a mask for you."

Janda Sulifa tells you, "Did you know there is a ceremonial mask for each culture? I so long for the day that I can make a ceremonial mask of my own!"

Tsua Kagemata tells you, "Greetings to you on this fine day. I am Tsua, mask maker of no small ability. If you provide me with a creature's head or facial covering, I will make it into a mask that will allow you to participate in Oseth's celebration."

During the Hollow Victory event, the Olthoi Helm was also updated with a new, scary look! To receive one of the new helms, simply hand your old one to a mask maker. Creature masks were seasonal -- available for that month only. The heritage masks (and the Skeleton Mask), however, remain permanently in the game and are available year round.

In the 2001 October event, The Widening Gyre, masks returned, even better than before! New creature drops were added: Ursuin, Zombie, Sclavus, and Pumpkin Heads, plus a Doll Mask which could be traded in for an Inviso Mask, sure to make you lose your head! A new set of beautiful heritage masks also became available, made from the previous month's Virindi Inquisitor's Mask. A final mask, the Virindi Director's Mask (a reward from the month's major quest), rounded out the newest headgear. Subsequent events have seen even more masks added to game!

To make a Creature Mask, take your trophy head to one of the Mask Makers. 

The Virindi Masks are made by Leopold in Ayan Baqur (59.2S, 88.3W).

To make the special Heritage Masks, give the Virindi Masks back to one of the mask makers. Alexander the Deft returns the Aluvian style mask; Janda Sulifiya returns the Gharu'ndim style mask; and Tsua Kagemate gives you a Sho style mask.

Other masks are available from quests. See the Quest Masks page for details!

Many players enjoy combining their masks with one of the Costumes available in game!
