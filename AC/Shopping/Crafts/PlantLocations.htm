Dyes

click pics to enlarge

Town Crier tells you, "News! Intrepid adventurers report that a wizened old sage in a swampy garden has access to rare and highly prized dye colors!"

Dye plants were introduced during the Twilight's Gleaming event.  Originally seasonal, specific colors were found at various times of the year in fixed locations. It was possible to camp known spawn spots by logging out every few minutes to reset the land block. During the Betrayal event plant spawns were updated and became randomized.

Currently, all dye plants (except Thananim which only comes from little green seeds) may be found growing on the landscape. Use the tables to the right for information on the regions where each dye plant may be found. The coordinates given are left over from the fixed spawn days and are no longer valid, but are provided as guides to direct you to the general areas of Dereth where the plants grow.

Note: During the That Which is Ours event, plant spawn changed. Colban and Relanim plant started being found near the South Dires and near the Aerlinthe D Note portal. Renalim seems to be spawning all over the Dires. Minalim has been seen near Teth and Colban, Argenory, Verdalim, and Lapyan were all found around Slaughterhouse!

During the Lost in the New Horizon event, a new method to obtain dye plants was introduced.  Dig around in the droppings of Shreth or certain Tuskers (Rampager and Assailer) and you can find some Little Green Seeds! (Shreth poop! Yucky!), Give the seeds to Terese, an Herbalist located inside the Swamp Garden, and she will give you dye plants in return. The Tusker Seeds are particularly desirable, as they yield the black dye plant Thananim.

The Swamp Garden is located outside of Yanshi at 22.5S, 45.8E - no level restriction.

--Map: Swamp Gardens
--Shortcut: Take the Yanshi portal from Shoushi at 35.0S, 74.4E. Take the Swamp Temple Place settlement portal at 10.3S, 45.7E.
--Creatures: Adult Reedshark, Reedshark Elder, Veteran Reedshark, Jungle Reaver

To reach Terese head east down the hallway and down the ramp. You'll have to fight past a few Adult and Elder Reedsharks. At the split, hug the right wall until you reach the drop off. Jump down into the pit (you might want to try killing a few of the Veterans below before jumping) and take the corridor to the south. At the four way split turn right heading west and you'll find Terese next to an exit portal.

Terese tells you, "If I really believed you cared, I might help you grow your own plants. But I'm sure you wouldn't find them to be terribly "elite"."

Terese tells you, "What do you want? Wasting my time, no doubt, wasting my time! Don't you have some grandiose quest to do, or innocent monkeys to kill? Leave me alone with my plants. Growing them isn't easy, you know."

Terese tells you, "Yes, the Shreth have been eating the plants. That's part of Dereth's new ecology. Go look it up. But, if you happen to go rooting through Shreth droppings, as I imagine you like to do, I'll take any seeds that you might find."

Terese added another saying after the dungeon was updated during A New Threat!

Terese tells you, "Do you see all these lovely new plants? I woke up one morning and their fronds had appeared out of nowhere! Even an animal-killer such as you cannot dampen my spirits."

Little Green Seeds can be found on any type of Shreth and may yield any color of plant except black. The seeds found on Rampager and Assailer Tuskers will produce the black plants.

When you hand your seed to Terese, the responses from Terese will vary. Sometimes you'll may not receive a plant at all, only a rude comment! Sometimes she will try to cast a spell on you. And at other times, you may get lucky and receive 5 plants instead of one!
