Creature Masks were introduced during October 2000's Hollow Victory update. The first masks available were the Mosswart, Drudge, Skeleton, Tusker, and Banderling Masks, plus an updated Olthoi Helm. Creature masks are made from trophies that drop from certain creatures (see tables to the right). Give a creature head to one of the mask makers in return for a mask!

Mask Makers

Alexander the Deft (Aluvian)
Eastham - 17.6N, 63.4E

Janda Sulifiya (Gharu'ndim)
Al-Jalima - 7.4N, 4.7E

Tsua Kagemata (Sho)
Sawato - 28.7S, 59.3E

Alexander the Deft tells you, "Hello, I am Alexander the Deft. If you give me a creature's head or facial covering, I may be able to make it into a mask for you."

Janda Sulifa tells you, "Did you know there is a ceremonial mask for each culture? I so long for the day that I can make a ceremonial mask of my own!"

Tsua Kagemata tells you, "Greetings to you on this fine day. I am Tsua, mask maker of no small ability. If you provide me with a creature's head or facial covering, I will make it into a mask that will allow you to participate in Oseth's celebration."

Some of the creature masks are seasonal -- available for the festival months only.

In the 2001 October event, The Widening Gyre, masks returned, even better than before! New creature drops were added: Ursuin, Zombie, Sclavus, and Pumpkin Heads, plus a Doll Mask which could be traded in for an Inviso Mask, sure to make you lose your head!

Banderling and Mosswart masks received an updated look during the Iron Coast event and Eviscerator Helms were also added!

You give Olthoi Hunter Eviscerator Head.
Olthoi Hunter gives you Olthoi Helm
Olthoi Hunter tells you, "An amazing specimen. I've read about the battle that Asheron witnessed on the Olthoi world between the Olthoi and the Grievvers. What? You didn't know that? Well in talking with Verdentine we put that together. The this is these Olthoi look to be bred to stand against that foe, who knows. We learn more every day."
Olthoi Hunter tells you, "Enjoy, and may it protect you well."

Visions in the Darkness saw even more masks being added, including a new Mutilator Helm, a Scary Minion Mask, a Moarsman Mask, Knath Heads and the ever popular Chicken Hat!

You give Olthoi Hunter Mutilator Head.
Olthoi Hunter tells you, "You're lucky you survived. All we can deduce about the smallest of the olthoi assault troops is that they serve the purpose of entering small places. They also are the fastest and strongest of their species. Wear this helmet with pride, you have earned the respect of many with this kill."
You've earned 10,000 experience.
Olthoi Hunter gives you Mutilator Helm.

The Scary Minion Mask is made by finding both a Hollow Minions Mask and a Pumpkin Head.

You give Alexander the Deft Hollow Minion's Face.
Alexander the Deft tells you, "I have a great idea! Go and find me a Scarecrow Head."

You give Janda Sulifiya Knath Husk.
Janda Sulifiya tells you, "These are hard to find. Here, nobody will know who you are in this mask!"
Janda Sulifiya gives you Knath Head.

You give Alexander the Deft Knath Husk.
Alexander the Deft tells you, "You seriously want a mask made from this
thing?"
Alexander the Deft tells you, "Well ok..."
Alexander the Deft gives you Knath Head.

You give Janda Sulifiya Whole Chicken.
Janda Sulifiya tells you, "I'm sorry, I'm not a cook."
Janda Sulifiya tells you, "You want a what? You should never put a ch... Okay... I won't judge you. Here."
Janda Sulifiya gives you Chicken Hat.

You give Alexander the Deft Whole Chicken.
Alexander the Deft tells you, "Hahaha, your [sic] kidding right?"
Alexander the Deft tells you, "Oh, I see you're a chicken lover. Well in
that case you'll love this hat."
Alexander the Deft gives you Chicken Hat.

The Festival Season in 2004 came later than usual. During December's The Calm event, four more masks were added: Burun, Chittick, Guruk and Mite.

Janda Sulifiya tells you, "They wait thousands of years to seek vengeance for who knows what ancient crime, and what happens when they finally arrive on Dereth? Some tiny Isparian lops off their head! I love it!"
Janda Sulifiya gives you Burun Guruk Mask.

Many players enjoy combining their masks with one of the Costumes available in game!
