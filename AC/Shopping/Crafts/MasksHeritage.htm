Heritage Masks were introduced during October 2000's Hollow Victory update. By giving a Virindi Mask to one of the three mask makers, you would receive a striking Heritage Mask in return! Each of the three heritages in Dereth -- Aluvian, Gharu'ndim, Sho -- has its own mask maker. For example, giving your Virinidi Mask to the Aluvian mask maker will yield the Aluvian heritage mask, the King's Helm.

Mask Makers

Alexander the Deft (Aluvian)
Eastham - 17.6N, 63.4E

Janda Sulifiya (Gharu'ndim)
Al-Jalima - 7.4N, 4.7E

Tsua Kagemata (Sho)
Sawato - 28.7S, 59.3E

To find out more about the Code of Pwyll read about the History and Factions of the Aluvians.

To learn more about the Shagar Zharala, read the Gharu'ndim heritage story, To Be A Shadow.

Read more about Koji in the Sho heritage stories, Koji's Sword and Koji and The Dragon of Power.

Note: stats for the heritage masks were updated during the Reign of Terror event. You can also upgrade the Shadow's Garb mask by adding tassels. See the Zharalim quest for details.
