In addition to creature masks, Virindi masks and heritage masks, certain masks are obtainable through quests.

The first quest mask introduced was the Obsidian Director's Mask during The Widening Gyre. To get the mask you must complete a quest in the Singularity Obsidian Repository - 55.9S, 64.8W (Levels 45-56) -- the third dungeon of the Repositories quests.

Note: The Obsidian Director's Mask cannot be used to receive heritage masks.
 

Obsidian Director's Mask

--thanks to Ghetto Booty

Another popular mask is Martine's Mask from the Verdict event. After you've taken the exit portal at the end of the Gaerlan's Citadel quest, you'll appear at the top of the citadel where you can receive your Martine's Robe and Mask.

When you first put on the mask, you might think you're going crazy! You'll hear voices in your head. The Martine's Mask quest page has a list of sayings the mask emits.
