Pumpkins

 

 

 

Coron Usgin tells you, "For no extra fee at all I will give thee an extremely useful carving knife which thou may use to craft this item! This knife alone has a thousand and one uses! It can carve arrowshafts, cut meat and chicken, chop cabbage, filet fish, slice mushrooms and carrots, cut bread and even carve a pumpkin! Is that not extraordinary?!"
 

Pumpkin Carving was introduced in October, 2000's Hollow Victory update as a seasonal event and became available once again during 2001's patch, The Widening Gyre. Pumpkins can be found scattered on the ground, usually at the feet of a Scarecrow (a permanent spawn can be found near Larry the Bunny Master's hut). Scarecrows may also drop pumpkins as loot when killed.

Use either a Whittling Knife or a Carving Knife to make a Jack O' Lantern. Anybody can make a reasonably carved Jack O'Lantern. To make a Superior Jack O'Lantern -- which doubles as a flaming thrown weapon! -- use the knife a 2nd time on the Jack O'Lantern. To be successful, you must be trained in the trade skill of the tool (if you fail, you will get a mangled pumpkin in return).

During the Festivus event, animated Scarecrows returned, and this time they were accompanied by a new treat: Great Pumpkins! Like regular pumpkins, Great Pumkins can also be carved into Jack O'Lanterns using a carving knife. Very BIG Jack O'Lanterns! ;-) Like the regular lanterns, Great Superior Jack O'Lanterns are made by carving your Great Jack O'Lantern a 2nd time. To find a Great Pumpkin, you must first locate one of the animated Scarecrows. Don't kill it; instead, pick up all the pumpkins spawning nearby. Eventually, Great Pumpkins will begin to spawn.
