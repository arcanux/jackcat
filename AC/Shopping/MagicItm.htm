You can buy Mana Charges to give mana to magic items. When you use a charge, it is destroyed. Use Mana Stones to drain  Mana from one Magical Item then to transfer it to another. The efficiency determines the % of mana drawn from the source object. If a magic gem contains 100 mana, you will absorb 25 mana using a Lesser Mana Stone, 50 using a Regular Mana Stone and 75 mana using a greater Mana Stone. Be careful not to overcharge your magic item, or you could destroy it. For example: to recharge magic shoes which hold 120 mana, and are down to 10, do not charge over 110 mana or you risk destroying the shoes. Mana Stones will sometimes have several uses before being destroyed.
