The Arts and Crafts section contains information on Mask Making, Tinkering, Pumpkin Carving, Dyes, and various Art pieces in Dereth.

The Stone, Ores and Tokens page list all the miscellaneous bits and pieces you may find in your travels.



To save time when selling your loot , put all your items into one container. Drag the whole container over to the merchant's sale panel and anything the merchant buys will automatically be placed into the slots to sell.

Tip: Use different colored packs to keep  your inventory organized. You are allowed a maximum of 7 containers.
