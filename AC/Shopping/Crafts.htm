The Gallery

The Arts & Crafts section give you plenty of fun ideas for those bits of rat tails or smelly hides lying around Dereth. Use the Crafters section to find out which Collectors and Crafters take which trophies and find out the various rewards. Marae Lassel Crafters are available on a separate page.

The Dyes section will teach you how to use the plants scattered around Dereth to alter the color of your armor or clothing. Beware! If you are not a skilled Cook, you could permanently damage your armor!

Tinkering allows you to improve your armor, items and weapons by using salvaged material found in Dereth.

Lockpicking identifies all the known keys in Dereth as well as instructions to carve special keys and lists the various types of available chests.

The Masks section has information on how to make all sorts of masks, including Virindi Masks, and the fabulous Heritage Masks.

The Gallery was something I had long planned, but it took a conversation with Rim the Dragonfly in Lytelthorpe to spur me to action. Visit some of the beautiful artwork scattered throughout Dereth.

And if you're in the Halloween spirit, try your hand at carving pumpkins!
