Store Bought Portal Gems
If you're a new player and don't have Item Magic (you are unable to cast portal spells), you may wish to carry a few of these portal gems for emergencies. When you use them, they will open a portal to the starter town indicated on the gem. In addition, portal gems also exist for the three Master Mages. Portal gems can be purchased at mage shops in starter towns or master mage shops. Invitations are sold by Wedding Planners.
